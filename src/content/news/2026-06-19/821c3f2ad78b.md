# Breaking the Solver Bottleneck: Training Task Generators at the Learnable Frontier
# 突破求解器瓶颈：在可学习前沿训练任务生成器

**Abstract:** The limiting resource for training agents via reinforcement learning (RL) is increasingly frontier task supply: valid, solvable tasks just difficult enough to train the current model. As reasoning and agentic models improve, fixed task distributions saturate, while naive synthetic generation yields tasks that are trivial, impossible, or ill-posed. 

**摘要：** 通过强化学习（RL）训练智能体时，限制性资源日益表现为前沿任务的供应：即那些既有效、可求解，又恰好具备足够难度以训练当前模型的任务。随着推理模型和智能体模型能力的提升，固定的任务分布逐渐饱和，而简单的合成生成往往会产生琐碎、无法实现或定义不当的任务。

Training a task generator with RL to optimize validity and learnability can address this bottleneck, but direct optimization requires repeated solver rollouts per candidate. For software-engineering (SWE) tasks, a single rollout can take tens of minutes; solver-in-the-loop generator training is intractable. 

利用强化学习训练任务生成器以优化任务的有效性和可学习性可以解决这一瓶颈，但直接优化需要对每个候选任务进行反复的求解器运行（rollout）。对于软件工程（SWE）任务，单次运行可能耗时数十分钟；因此，将求解器置于循环中的生成器训练在计算上是不可行的。

We introduce PROPEL, a solver-amortized framework for training task generators at the targeted solve rate. PROPEL trains a lightweight activation probe on a one-time labeled corpus of generated tasks and solver outcomes. The probe predicts target-solver pass rate from a frozen generator reference model and serves as a proxy for solve rate during generator optimization, reducing generator evaluation to a single forward pass. 

我们引入了 PROPEL，这是一个用于在目标求解率下训练任务生成器的求解器摊销框架。PROPEL 通过一次性标记的生成任务与求解结果语料库，训练了一个轻量级的激活探针（activation probe）。该探针能够根据冻结的生成器参考模型预测目标求解器的通过率，并在生成器优化过程中作为求解率的代理指标，从而将生成器评估简化为单次前向传播。

Across math, code, and software-engineering at multiple model scales, PROPEL shifts generation toward the targeted solve rate: for coding, tasks generated at the learnable frontier increase from $10.1\% \rightarrow 20.0\%$ for a Qwen2.5-3B-Instruct solver and from $5.3\% \rightarrow 12.6\%$ for a Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct solver. For SWE, PROPEL increases the share of generations at the targeted solve rate from $9.8\% \rightarrow 19.6\%$ for Qwen3.5-27B on repositories not seen during training of probe and generator.

在数学、代码和软件工程等多个模型规模的测试中，PROPEL 将生成任务向目标求解率偏移：在代码任务方面，对于 Qwen2.5-3B-Instruct 求解器，处于可学习前沿的任务生成比例从 $10.1\%$ 提升至 $20.0\%$；对于 Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct 求解器，该比例从 $5.3\%$ 提升至 $12.6\%$。在软件工程任务方面，对于 Qwen3.5-27B 模型，在探针和生成器训练期间未见过的代码库上，PROPEL 将目标求解率下的生成任务份额从 $9.8\%$ 提升至 $19.6\%$。
