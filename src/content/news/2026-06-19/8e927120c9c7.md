# Gaussian Mixture Attention: Linear-Time Sequence Mixing via Probabilistic Latent Routing
# 高斯混合注意力：通过概率潜在路由实现线性时间序列混合

**Abstract:** The dense token-to-token interaction pattern of standard dot-product attention remains a central bottleneck in scaling Transformer architectures to long contexts. We introduce **Gaussian Mixture Attention (GMA)**, a probabilistic attention-style sequence mixer that replaces explicit pairwise query--key comparison with routing through $K$ learned Gaussian mixture components.

**摘要：** 标准点积注意力机制中密集的词元间（token-to-token）交互模式，仍然是扩展 Transformer 架构以处理长上下文的核心瓶颈。我们引入了**高斯混合注意力（Gaussian Mixture Attention, GMA）**，这是一种概率注意力风格的序列混合器，它通过 $K$ 个学习到的高斯混合分量进行路由，取代了显式的成对查询-键（query-key）比较。

Queries and keys are mapped to posterior *responsibility* vectors over a shared latent routing space; their overlap defines an implicit responsibility-space affinity, while values are written into and read from a $K$-slot latent memory. By exploiting the associativity of matrix multiplication, GMA avoids materializing the induced $N\times N$ affinity matrix and instead uses two responsibility matrices whose dominant activation storage scales as $\mathcal{O}(NK)$ rather than $\mathcal{O}(N^2)$ for fixed $K$.

查询和键被映射到共享潜在路由空间上的后验*责任（responsibility）*向量；它们的重叠定义了一种隐式的责任空间亲和力，而值则被写入 $K$ 个槽位的潜在内存中并从中读取。通过利用矩阵乘法的结合律，GMA 避免了生成 $N\times N$ 的亲和力矩阵，转而使用两个责任矩阵，其主要激活存储在 $K$ 固定时，从 $\mathcal{O}(N^2)$ 扩展为 $\mathcal{O}(NK)$。

We formulate bidirectional and causal variants of GMA, provide an end-to-end differentiable parameterization of the Gaussian mixture components, and analyze its responsibility-modulated gradient structure, constrained non-negative low-rank affinity interpretation, and local routing stability.

我们制定了 GMA 的双向和因果变体，提供了高斯混合分量的端到端可微参数化，并分析了其责任调节的梯度结构、受约束的非负低秩亲和力解释以及局部路由稳定性。

Empirically, GMA exhibits the intended fixed-$K$ linear memory scaling and is competitive with attention-style baselines on long-context classification, while causal GMA improves over tested linear/random-feature attention variants on WikiText-103 but remains behind optimized causal SDPA and Mamba in the current implementation.

实验表明，GMA 表现出了预期的固定 $K$ 线性内存扩展特性，在长上下文分类任务上与注意力风格的基线模型具有竞争力；虽然因果 GMA 在 WikiText-103 上优于所测试的线性/随机特征注意力变体，但在当前实现中仍落后于优化的因果 SDPA 和 Mamba。

Analysis of learned responsibilities further shows broad component usage and moderate alignment with surface-form token categories, supporting GMA as a probabilistic, interpretable, fixed-$K$ linear-time attention-style alternative rather than a universal replacement for optimized softmax attention or state-space models.

对学习到的责任向量的分析进一步显示了广泛的分量使用情况，以及与表层词元类别（surface-form token categories）的中度对齐，这支持了 GMA 作为一种概率性、可解释、固定 $K$ 的线性时间注意力替代方案的定位，而非对优化后的 Softmax 注意力或状态空间模型的通用替代品。
