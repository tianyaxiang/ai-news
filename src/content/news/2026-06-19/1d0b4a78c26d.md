# Fisher Width: A Geometric Measure of Complexity on Statistical Manifolds
# Fisher Width：统计流形上的一种几何复杂度度量

**Abstract:** Gaussian width is a central geometric complexity measure in high-dimensional probability, compressed sensing, convex optimization, and learning theory. It quantifies the average extent of a set along random directions, thereby capturing the effective dimension of constraint sets, hypothesis classes, and descent cones.
**摘要：** 高斯宽度（Gaussian width）是高维概率、压缩感知、凸优化和学习理论中一种核心的几何复杂度度量。它量化了一个集合在随机方向上的平均延伸程度，从而捕捉了约束集、假设类和下降锥的有效维度。

However, this notion is intrinsically Euclidean. Statistical models instead carry a natural Riemannian geometry induced by the Fisher information metric, where directions are scaled according to statistical distinguishability rather than ambient Euclidean length.
然而，这一概念本质上是欧几里得的。相比之下，统计模型携带由费舍尔信息度量（Fisher information metric）诱导的自然黎曼几何，其中方向的缩放是根据统计可区分性而非环境欧几里得长度来确定的。

We introduce Fisher width, a Fisher-geometric analogue of Gaussian width for statistical manifolds. At a parameter point $\theta$, Fisher width replaces the Euclidean identity by the local metric tensor $G(\theta)^{1/2}$, measuring the Gaussian width of the Fisher-rescaled set. This makes the resulting quantity sensitive to local statistical curvature and invariant under smooth reparameterizations.
我们引入了 Fisher Width，这是统计流形上高斯宽度的一种费舍尔几何模拟。在参数点 $\theta$ 处，Fisher Width 用局部度量张量 $G(\theta)^{1/2}$ 替换了欧几里得恒等式，从而测量了经费舍尔重缩放后的集合的高斯宽度。这使得所得的量对局部统计曲率敏感，并在平滑重参数化下保持不变。

We develop the basic theory of Fisher width, showing that it retains key structural features of Gaussian width, including concentration, metric perturbation stability, and spectral comparison bounds with the Euclidean baseline, while also capturing anisotropic geometric effects invisible to Euclidean measures.
我们发展了 Fisher Width 的基础理论，证明它保留了高斯宽度的关键结构特征，包括集中性、度量扰动稳定性以及与欧几里得基准的谱比较界，同时还能捕捉到欧几里得度量无法察觉的各向异性几何效应。

As an application, we prove a generalization bound for Fisher-Lipschitz hypothesis classes and propose computable estimators, which we evaluate empirically on MNIST across three model classes. Fisher width is to statistical manifolds what Gaussian width is to Euclidean convex bodies. This work lays the foundation for studying complexity and learning on curved statistical manifolds.
作为应用，我们证明了 Fisher-Lipschitz 假设类的泛化界，并提出了可计算的估计量，并在 MNIST 数据集上对三种模型类进行了实证评估。Fisher Width 之于统计流形，正如高斯宽度之于欧几里得凸体。这项工作为研究弯曲统计流形上的复杂性和学习奠定了基础。
