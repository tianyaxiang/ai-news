### Breville Barista Express
### Breville Barista Express

Breville's Barista Express is a semiautomatic espresso machine, which means that the grinder and steaming wand are included in the machine—a one-stop shop for milk-frothy drinks. It's been the top-selling espresso maker of its kind for so long I don't even remember when this wasn't true.
Breville的Barista Express是一款半自动浓缩咖啡机，这意味着它内置了磨豆机和蒸汽棒，是制作奶泡咖啡的一站式解决方案。它作为同类产品中的销量冠军已经太久了，以至于我都不记得它何时曾跌落过榜首。

This $200 discount during Amazon Prime Day 2026 makes for the lowest price I've seen on the Barista Express in about a half-decade—a deal good enough I had to double- and then triple-check it to make sure it was true.
2026年亚马逊Prime Day期间的这200美元折扣，是我近五年来见过的Barista Express最低价格——这个优惠力度大到我不得不反复确认了好几次，以确保它是真实的。

WIRED has been recommending the Express as one of our favorite espresso makers for about a decade for its mix of value, reliability, precision, and ease of use.
《连线》（WIRED）杂志推荐这款Express作为我们最喜爱的浓缩咖啡机已有十年之久，因为它完美结合了性价比、可靠性、精准度和易用性。

Why's it so popular? It's a Goldilocks thing—a mix of accessible price, Breville's excellent reputation for customer service on high-ticket items, and beautiful ease of use. While the built-in grinder isn't as good as on Breville's top-tier Oracle Jet, the Express will make full-flavored, consistent, well-extracted espresso on medium and dark roasts.
它为什么这么受欢迎？因为它恰到好处——兼具亲民的价格、Breville在高端产品上卓越的客户服务口碑，以及极佳的易用性。虽然其内置磨豆机不如Breville顶级的Oracle Jet，但Express在处理中度及深度烘焙咖啡豆时，依然能制作出风味浓郁、品质稳定且萃取充分的浓缩咖啡。

Breville's excellent reputation for honoring warranties and offering excellent customer service doesn't hurt—a big deal on a device that'll hopefully be with you for years. Besides, we can attest to this longevity: WIRED senior gear editor Julian Chokkattu has been pulling shots from his Barista Express for seven years, and it's still going strong.
Breville在履行保修和提供优质售后服务方面的良好声誉也加分不少——对于一台希望陪伴你多年的设备来说，这非常重要。此外，我们可以证明其耐用性：WIRED资深装备编辑Julian Chokkattu使用他的Barista Express制作咖啡已经七年了，它至今依然表现强劲。

### Ninja Luxe Cafe Pro
### Ninja Luxe Cafe Pro

Ninja's flagship semiautomatic espresso machine is also on a great sale for Prime Day 2026, in a deal that already started this week. The Ninja Luxe Cafe Pro is on sale for $150 off, matching the lowest price I've seen it.
Ninja的旗舰级半自动浓缩咖啡机也参与了2026年Prime Day的促销活动，优惠已于本周开始。Ninja Luxe Cafe Pro目前直降150美元，达到了我所见过的最低价格。

When Ninja announced it was jumping into the semiautomatic espresso market in 2024, I was skeptical. But both the Pro and the slightly downmarket Luxe Cafe Premium have surprised me with their ability to make excellent espresso with minimal effort.
当Ninja在2024年宣布进军半自动浓缩咖啡机市场时，我曾持怀疑态度。但无论是Pro版本还是定位稍低的Luxe Cafe Premium，它们都能以极简的操作制作出优质的浓缩咖啡，这让我感到惊喜。

The best deal is on the Pro, which includes a useful bean assist function to help you dial in your beans—a rarity at this price point. It also offers a manual tamping lever and a hot water spout that was mysteriously lacking in Ninja's lower-tier device.
最值得入手的还是Pro版本，它包含了一项实用的“豆子辅助”（bean assist）功能，能帮助你精准调试咖啡豆——这在同价位产品中非常罕见。它还配备了手动压粉杆和热水出口，而这些功能在Ninja的低端机型中却莫名缺失。

Ninja's devices aren't as tried and tested as the Breville. And the “drip coffee” function is best left alone. But if you're a latte lover, you may still have reason to prefer Ninja's coffee maker. The reason is the milk frother, which is among the best on any semiautomatic espresso machine I've seen, courtesy of a magnetic metal whisk at the bottom of the milk carafe. It's some of the most integrated, well-frothed milk I've seen from an automatic frother.
Ninja的设备虽然不像Breville那样经过了时间的长期考验，且其“滴滤咖啡”功能最好忽略不计。但如果你是拿铁爱好者，你依然有理由偏爱Ninja的咖啡机。原因在于它的奶泡机——得益于奶罐底部的磁力金属搅拌器，这是我所见过的半自动浓缩咖啡机中表现最好的奶泡系统之一。它制作出的奶泡与咖啡融合度极高，是我见过自动奶泡机中效果最好的。
