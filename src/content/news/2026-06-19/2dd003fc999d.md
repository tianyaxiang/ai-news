# A year of Walmart Plus is half off ahead of Prime Day
# 沃尔玛会员 Walmart Plus 年费在 Prime Day 前夕半价优惠

Do you often find yourself shopping at the big blue, or perhaps you’re just looking for an alternative to Amazon? Either way, Walmart is currently offering a year of its Walmart Plus subscription for $49, which typically costs $98.
你是否经常在沃尔玛购物，或者正在寻找亚马逊的替代方案？无论哪种情况，沃尔玛目前正提供 Walmart Plus 年度会员订阅优惠，价格仅为 49 美元，而其原价通常为 98 美元。

The membership includes perks like free delivery on orders over $35, free next-day and two-day shipping from Walmart.com when available, and free home pickup for returns. Importantly, it also grants you early access to sales, including the upcoming Walmart Deals starting on June 22nd that coincide with Prime Day 2026.
该会员权益包括：订单满 35 美元即可享受免费配送、Walmart.com 上符合条件的商品可享免费次日达或两日达服务，以及免费的上门退货取件服务。更重要的是，它还能让你提前参与促销活动，包括将于 6 月 22 日开启、与 2026 年亚马逊 Prime Day 同期进行的“沃尔玛特惠”（Walmart Deals）活动。

In addition to the aforementioned shopping perks, the subscription bundles in a handful of other offers and memberships. For instance, you can choose from either ad-supported Paramount Plus or Peacock, with the option to switch every 90 days. You’ll also get a Pawp membership for remote veterinary care, discounts on gas at Walmart and Sam’s Club, and even a free Whopper with any purchase from Burger King every three months.
除了上述购物福利外，该订阅还捆绑了其他多项优惠和会员权益。例如，你可以选择含广告版的 Paramount Plus 或 Peacock 会员，并每 90 天可更换一次。此外，你还将获得用于远程兽医护理的 Pawp 会员资格、沃尔玛及山姆会员店的加油折扣，甚至每三个月在汉堡王（Burger King）进行任何消费即可免费获得一个皇堡（Whopper）。
