The search for dark matter has been blown wide open. For decades, physicists have hunted for weakly interacting massive particles (WIMPs), a leading candidate for dark matter. But their search has run into a new problem: neutrinos. These tiny particles from the sun and other stars can create a “neutrino fog” that drowns out any signal of dark matter. Hitting the neutrino fog does not, however, mean an end to the search. Researchers just have to shift the focus of their hunt. They’re now casting a much wider net. New proposals include quantum sensors, liquid-helium detectors, and even searches in Jupiter’s atmosphere. Find out how the search for dark matter has entered entirely new territory. —Dan Garisto
寻找暗物质的研究领域已被彻底打开。几十年来，物理学家一直在搜寻弱相互作用大质量粒子（WIMPs），这是暗物质的主要候选者。但他们的研究遇到了一个新问题：中微子。这些来自太阳和其他恒星的微小粒子会产生一种“中微子雾”，掩盖掉任何暗物质信号。然而，遇到“中微子雾”并不意味着研究的终结。研究人员只需调整搜寻重点。他们现在正撒下更大的网，新方案包括量子传感器、液氦探测器，甚至在木星大气层中进行搜寻。了解暗物质的搜寻工作如何进入了全新的领域。——Dan Garisto

Entrepreneurs in Nairobi are making the case for going solar. Shops with diesel-powered grain mills are common in Nairobi. Milcah Wanjiru’s is different: it runs on either solar energy or the grid. About a quarter of Kenya’s population still lacks centralized electricity, and off-grid solar is being promoted as a route to universal access by 2030. In Wanjiru’s case, it cuts operating costs and can improve profits once the upfront investment is recovered. Read the full story on the rise of solar milling systems across Kenya and beyond. —Geoffrey Kamadi
内罗毕的创业者们正在力推太阳能。在内罗毕，使用柴油驱动的磨坊很常见。但 Milcah Wanjiru 的磨坊与众不同：它既可以使用太阳能，也可以接入电网。肯尼亚约四分之一的人口仍缺乏集中供电，离网太阳能正被推广为 2030 年实现全民用电的途径。对 Wanjiru 而言，这不仅降低了运营成本，在收回前期投资后还能提高利润。阅读关于肯尼亚及其他地区太阳能磨坊系统兴起的完整报道。——Geoffrey Kamadi
