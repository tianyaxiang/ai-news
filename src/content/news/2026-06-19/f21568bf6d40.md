# Hulk, Punisher join Peter Parker in Spider-Man: Brand New Day trailer
# 绿巨人与惩罚者加入彼得·帕克，亮相《蜘蛛侠：全新的一天》预告片

We’re about six weeks out from the debut of Spider-Man: Brand New Day, the follow-up to 2021’s No Way Home. It’s been five years since Spidey graced the big screen, so naturally, Sony Pictures has released a new trailer to build audience anticipation. (Spoilers for No Way Home below.)

距离《蜘蛛侠：全新的一天》（Spider-Man: Brand New Day）上映还有大约六周时间，该片是2021年《蜘蛛侠：英雄无归》（No Way Home）的续作。距离蜘蛛侠上次登上大银幕已经过去了五年，因此索尼影业自然发布了一支新预告片来提升观众的期待值。（以下内容包含《英雄无归》剧透。）

No Way Home ended on a pretty bleak note, with Peter Parker (Tom Holland) asking Doctor Strange (Benedict Cumberbatch) to erase him from everyone’s memory to protect the multiverse, including MJ (Zendaya). Brand New Day is directed by Destin Daniel Cretton (Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings).

《英雄无归》的结局相当凄凉：彼得·帕克（汤姆·赫兰德饰）请求奇异博士（本尼迪克特·康伯巴奇饰）抹除所有人对他记忆，以保护多元宇宙，这也包括MJ（赞达亚饰）。《全新的一天》由德斯汀·丹尼尔·克雷顿（《尚气与十环传奇》导演）执导。

Per the official synopsis: Four years have passed since the events of No Way Home, and Peter is now an adult living entirely alone, having voluntarily erased himself from the lives and memories of those he loves. Crime-fighting in a New York that no longer knows his name, he’s devoted himself entirely to protecting his city—a full-time Spider-Man—but as the demands on him intensify, the pressure sparks a surprising physical evolution that threatens his existence, even as a strange new pattern of crimes gives rise to one of the most powerful threats he has ever faced.

根据官方剧情简介：距离《英雄无归》的事件已经过去了四年，彼得现在是一个完全独自生活的成年人，他自愿将自己从所爱之人的生活和记忆中抹去。在那个不再记得他名字的纽约市打击犯罪，他全身心地投入到保护城市的工作中——成为一名全职蜘蛛侠。但随着对他要求的不断增加，压力引发了一种惊人的身体进化，威胁到了他的生存，与此同时，一种奇怪的新型犯罪模式也带来了他所面临的最强大的威胁之一。

Naturally, Holland is reprising his role as Peter Parker/Spider-Man, along with Zendaya as MJ and Jacob Batalon as Peter’s former bestie Ned Leeds. The film also features Jon Bernthal as Frank Castle/Punisher, Mark Ruffalo as Bruce Banner/Hulk, Michael Mando as Mac Gargan/Scorpion (captured by Spider-Man in Homecoming), Marvin Jones III as albino crime lord Lonnie Lincoln/Tombstone, and Charlie Cox as Matt Murdock/Daredevil (briefly Peter’s lawyer in No Way Home). Sadie Sink, Liza Colon-Zayas, and Tramell Tillman join the case in as-yet-undisclosed roles.

理所当然，赫兰德将继续饰演彼得·帕克/蜘蛛侠，赞达亚饰演MJ，雅各布·巴特朗饰演彼得的前挚友内德·利兹。该片还包括乔·博恩瑟饰演的弗兰克·卡索/惩罚者、马克·鲁法洛饰演的布鲁斯·班纳/绿巨人、迈克尔·曼多饰演的麦克·加根/蝎子人（曾在《英雄归来》中被蜘蛛侠抓获）、马文·琼斯三世饰演的白化病犯罪头目朗尼·林肯/墓碑，以及查理·考克斯饰演的马特·默多克/夜魔侠（曾在《英雄无归》中短暂担任彼得的律师）。萨迪·辛克、丽莎·科隆-扎亚斯和特拉梅尔·蒂尔曼也加入了演员阵容，角色尚未公开。

We learned from the first trailer in March that something strange is happening to Peter physically; he woke up surrounded in webbing and sought the help of Banner. It’s possible that Peter’s DNA is mutating, which, Banner said, “would be enormously dangerous.” But if Peter can make it through this new cycle, he might just get a rebirth—and a fresh trilogy of Holland-starring Spider-Man films.

我们从三月份的首支预告片中得知，彼得的身体正在发生奇怪的变化；他醒来时被蛛网包围，并寻求班纳的帮助。彼得的DNA可能正在发生突变，班纳称这“将极其危险”。但如果彼得能度过这个新周期，他可能会获得重生——并开启由赫兰德主演的全新蜘蛛侠三部曲。

In the latest trailer, Peter specifically asks Banner if there is a way to get rid of the bad parts and keep the good parts of whatever is going on with his mutation. Banner’s response: “How would you decide what parts of nature are good or bad?” Meanwhile, our friendly neighborhood Spider-Man is faced with a new threat that nobody can see. Peter is the only person who seems to be immune to whatever strange power is affecting everyone else—and the “only one who can sense it.”

在最新的预告片中，彼得专门询问班纳，是否有一种方法可以去除他突变中坏的部分，保留好的部分。班纳回答道：“你如何定义自然界中哪些部分是好的，哪些是坏的？”与此同时，我们友好的邻居蜘蛛侠正面临一个无人能见的威胁。彼得似乎是唯一对这种影响其他人的奇怪力量免疫的人，也是“唯一能感知到它的人”。

He does have an ally in Banner, who naturally Hulks out when the situation calls for it. Peter gets a chance to save MJ, who still doesn’t remember their previous reality. And he gets a grumpy pep talk from Frank Castle: “If you’re gonna do something, you’d better do it now.” Perhaps those new powers will come in handy in the inevitable showdown.

他确实有一位盟友班纳，当情况需要时，班纳自然会变身为绿巨人。彼得有机会拯救MJ，而她仍然不记得他们之前的现实。他还得到了弗兰克·卡索暴躁的鼓励：“如果你要做什么，最好现在就做。”也许这些新力量在不可避免的决战中会派上用场。

Spider-Man: Brand New Day hits theaters on July 31, 2026.

《蜘蛛侠：全新的一天》将于2026年7月31日上映。
