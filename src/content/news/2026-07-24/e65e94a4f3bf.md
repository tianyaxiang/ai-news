### Cubby Clipboard

**Launching today**
今天正式发布

**Windows clipboard history that searches inside screenshots**
一款支持搜索截图内容的 Windows 剪贴板历史记录工具

**Cubby is the clipboard history Windows should have shipped. It keeps copied text, images, files, and screenshots searchable after restarts, while offline OCR lets you find a screenshot by the words inside it.**
Cubby 是 Windows 本应内置的剪贴板历史记录工具。它能让复制的文本、图像、文件和截图在重启后依然可供搜索；同时，离线 OCR（光学字符识别）功能让你能够通过截图内的文字来查找图片。

**Everything stays encrypted on your PC: no account, no cloud, no clipboard telemetry. Open it with a shortcut, search, and paste.**
所有数据均在你的电脑上加密存储：无需账户、无需云端、无剪贴板遥测。只需通过快捷键打开，搜索，然后粘贴即可。
