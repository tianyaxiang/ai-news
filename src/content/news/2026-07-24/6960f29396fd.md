# STN-TGAT: Top-K Portfolio Construction via Prior-Guided Graph Attention with Learnable Soft-Threshold Sparsification

**STN-TGAT：基于先验引导图注意力与可学习软阈值稀疏化的 Top-K 投资组合构建**

***

**Abstract:** This paper tackles the problem of stock ranking and portfolio construction under realistic investment settings by jointly modeling temporal dynamics and cross-sectional dependencies. 

**摘要：** 本文旨在解决现实投资环境下的股票排序与投资组合构建问题，通过联合建模时间动态特征与横截面依赖关系来应对这一挑战。

We propose the Soft-Threshold NMI-prior Transformer Graph Attention Network (STN-TGAT), which integrates a temporal Transformer with a Graph Attention Network to capture long-horizon sequential patterns and dynamic inter-stock relationships. 

我们提出了软阈值互信息（NMI）先验 Transformer 图注意力网络（STN-TGAT），该模型将时间序列 Transformer 与图注意力网络相结合，以捕捉长周期的序列模式及股票间的动态关联。

An NMI-based prior graph combined with a soft-threshold sparsification mechanism enhances structural robustness by mitigating noisy correlations while preserving informative connections. 

基于 NMI 的先验图结合软阈值稀疏化机制，通过抑制噪声相关性并保留关键信息连接，增强了模型的结构鲁棒性。

The portfolio formation process incorporates practical considerations, including Top-5 selection within the Top-50 S&P 500 constituents, explicit weight allocation, and transaction cost adjustment, thereby aligning the evaluation with real-world trading conditions. 

投资组合的构建过程纳入了多项实际考量，包括从标普 500 指数的前 50 只成分股中筛选前 5 只股票、明确的权重分配以及交易成本调整，从而使评估过程与真实交易环境保持一致。

Empirical results on real-world data demonstrate that STN-TGAT consistently outperforms benchmark models from predictive accuracy and investment profitability measured by portfolio returns. 

在真实数据集上的实证结果表明，无论是在预测准确性还是以投资组合收益衡量的投资盈利能力方面，STN-TGAT 均持续优于基准模型。

These findings suggest that combining decision-aligned training with adaptive relational modeling provides a coherent and practically effective framework for data-driven portfolio construction.

研究结果表明，将决策导向的训练与自适应关系建模相结合，为数据驱动的投资组合构建提供了一个连贯且具有实际应用价值的框架。
