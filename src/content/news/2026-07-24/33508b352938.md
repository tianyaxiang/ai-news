# Bayesian Wind Tunnels for Model Selection
# 贝叶斯风洞：用于模型选择

**Abstract:** Prior work has shown that transformers can perform exact Bayesian filtering within a fixed hypothesis class. Can they also perform Bayesian model selection -- identifying the correct hypothesis class from data? We introduce model-selection Bayesian wind tunnels: controlled environments where ground-truth posteriors over hypothesis classes are available in closed form.

**摘要：** 先前的研究表明，Transformer 可以在固定的假设空间内执行精确的贝叶斯滤波。那么，它们能否执行贝叶斯模型选择——即从数据中识别出正确的假设空间？我们引入了“模型选择贝叶斯风洞”：这是一种受控环境，其中关于假设空间的基准后验概率（ground-truth posteriors）可以以闭式解的形式获得。

Using fixed-point-free involutions -- whose defining property f(f(x))=x is purely relational -- a 2.8M-parameter transformer achieves 0.01-bit entropy agreement with the Bayesian optimum (3 seeds), with both integer tokens and opaque symbols whose meanings change every episode. This extends to non-nested comparisons: involutions vs. 3-cycles (where neither class is a subset of the other) achieve class-posterior MAE under 0.001, demonstrating genuine model selection beyond simplicity/subset bias.

通过使用无不动点的对合（fixed-point-free involutions，其定义属性 f(f(x))=x 是纯粹关系性的），一个 2.8M 参数的 Transformer 在整数 Token 和每个 Episode 含义都会改变的不透明符号上，均实现了与贝叶斯最优解 0.01 比特的熵一致性（3 个随机种子）。这扩展到了非嵌套比较：对合与 3-循环（其中任何一个类都不是另一个的子集）实现了低于 0.001 的类后验平均绝对误差（MAE），证明了超越简单性/子集偏差的真正模型选择能力。

We then identify a sharp perceptual access condition: when the discriminative statistic requires arithmetic -- modular addition (rotations) or multiplication (f(x)=cx mod p) -- model selection succeeds with integer tokens but fails completely with opaque symbols, and this boundary persists under 112x scaling (2.8M to 316M parameters). A stationarity control confirms the operative factor: opaque tokens with a fixed relabeling succeed (0.009-bit MAE), showing that stable semantics, not integer identity, enable circuit compilation.

随后，我们确定了一个明确的感知访问条件：当判别统计量需要算术运算（模加法或乘法）时，模型选择在使用整数 Token 时成功，但在使用不透明符号时完全失败，且这种边界在 112 倍规模扩展（从 2.8M 到 316M 参数）下依然存在。平稳性控制实验证实了关键因素：具有固定重标记的不透明 Token 可以成功（0.009 比特 MAE），这表明是稳定的语义而非整数身份本身，使得电路编译成为可能。

Header subtask diagnostics localize the failure to the composition of header inversion with arithmetic rather than header parsing itself. Probing frontier LLMs on the same tasks shows qualitative Bayesian behavior but a large calibration gap (~55x), measured through lossy probes and therefore directional rather than exact.

头部子任务诊断将失败定位在头部反转与算术运算的组合上，而非头部解析本身。在相同任务上对前沿大语言模型（LLM）进行探测显示出定性的贝叶斯行为，但存在较大的校准差距（约 55 倍），由于是通过有损探测器测量的，因此该结果仅具方向性而非精确性。
