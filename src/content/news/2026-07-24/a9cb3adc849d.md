# OpenAI makes ChatGPT Health available to all US users
# OpenAI 向所有美国用户开放 ChatGPT Health 功能

OpenAI said today it is making ChatGPT Health, a feature that helps users with health-related queries, available to all U.S.-based users over 18 across all plans. The announcement comes a day after a Florida-based pastor sued the company for giving a near-fatal suggestion not to consult a doctor.
OpenAI 今日宣布，其旨在帮助用户处理健康相关查询的功能“ChatGPT Health”现已向所有 18 岁以上的美国用户开放，涵盖所有订阅计划。此前一天，佛罗里达州的一位牧师起诉了该公司，称其提供的建议导致他差点丧命，因为该建议称他无需咨询医生。

The company started testing the feature through a dedicated hub earlier in January, allowing users to integrate data from other services and their personal information from services such as Apple Health, Function, and MyFitnessPal. At that time, OpenAI said users were asking 230 million health-related queries each week. That number has now gone up to 300 million.
该公司于今年 1 月通过一个专门的中心开始测试该功能，允许用户整合来自其他服务的数据，以及来自 Apple Health、Function 和 MyFitnessPal 等服务的个人信息。当时，OpenAI 表示用户每周会提出 2.3 亿次与健康相关的查询。这一数字目前已上升至 3 亿次。

Users can also integrate their medical records from hospital systems like Epic and Oracle Health, and health platforms like One Medical and Function Health. OpenAI said earlier that users needed to use the health hub for health-related queries. Now they can choose to draw insights from connected information in the health section in all queries. The company said during the testing it observed that 70% of health-related queries took place outside the dedicated hub.
用户还可以整合来自 Epic 和 Oracle Health 等医院系统，以及 One Medical 和 Function Health 等健康平台的医疗记录。OpenAI 此前表示，用户需要使用健康中心来进行健康相关的查询。现在，用户可以在所有查询中选择从健康板块的关联信息中获取见解。该公司表示，在测试期间观察到 70% 的健康相关查询发生在专门的中心之外。

Through this new feature, users can use their health information to get information on food or allergies in the general chat. The company noted that its models have made progress in answering health-related queries. The company noted that the smallest model from its latest release, GPT 5.6-Luna, outperforms GPT 5.5 on HealthBench evaluation, an open source benchmark developed by the company to evaluate large language models (LLMs) on health queries.
通过这一新功能，用户可以在常规聊天中使用其健康信息来获取有关食物或过敏的信息。该公司指出，其模型在回答健康相关查询方面已取得进展。公司还提到，其最新发布的最小模型 GPT 5.6-Luna 在 HealthBench 评估中表现优于 GPT 5.5；HealthBench 是该公司开发的一个开源基准测试，用于评估大语言模型（LLM）在健康查询方面的表现。

OpenAI said that it doesn’t use any user data to train its model, and it works with physicians to improve its models on health queries. Despite these performance gains, the company maintains in its terms that its services are “not intended for use in the diagnosis or treatment of any health condition.” The company cited these clauses in response to the above lawsuit, and also told The New York Times that it is working on making health- or medicine-related answers safer.
OpenAI 表示，它不会使用任何用户数据来训练其模型，并与医生合作以改进模型在健康查询方面的表现。尽管性能有所提升，但该公司在条款中坚持声明，其服务“并非旨在用于任何健康状况的诊断或治疗”。该公司在回应上述诉讼时引用了这些条款，并告诉《纽约时报》，他们正在努力使健康或医学相关的回答更加安全。

With the latest roll out, OpenAI said it wants people to verify information and take medical decisions based on professional advice. Several studies have highlighted that AI bots are not reliable for medical advice. However, this has not deterred companies like Anthropic and Google from launching health-related AI features.
随着此次最新功能的推出，OpenAI 表示希望人们能够核实信息，并基于专业建议做出医疗决策。多项研究强调，人工智能机器人提供的医疗建议并不可靠。然而，这并没有阻止 Anthropic 和 Google 等公司推出与健康相关的 AI 功能。

Health in ChatGPT is rolling out to logged-in U.S. users with free, Go, Plus, and Pro plans on the web and iOS this week.
本周，ChatGPT Health 功能将向已登录的美国用户推出，适用于网页版和 iOS 端的免费版、Go、Plus 和 Pro 计划。
