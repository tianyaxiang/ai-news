# Corsair’s PC case with a panoramic glass design is $70 off
# 海盗船（Corsair）全景玻璃机箱现降价 70 美元

On the hunt for a PC case that’s loaded with features and has a unique look? Amazon, Best Buy, and Corsair are selling the Corsair Frame 4500X RS for $119.99, a $70 discount from the usual price. 
正在寻找一款功能丰富且外观独特的电脑机箱吗？目前亚马逊（Amazon）、百思买（Best Buy）和海盗船官网正在以 119.99 美元的价格销售 Corsair Frame 4500X RS，较原价优惠了 70 美元。

The versatile case supports motherboards up to EATX, GPUs up to 460mm in length, and has a seamless panoramic glass window that wraps around its front and left side. The case includes three fans with reverse rotors mounted to its right side, letting the ARGB lighting shine into the case while they pull in air.
这款多功能机箱支持最大 EATX 规格的主板和长达 460 毫米的显卡，并配有环绕机箱前部和左侧的无缝全景玻璃窗。机箱右侧预装了三个反向扇叶风扇，在吸入空气的同时，能让 ARGB 灯效完美呈现于机箱内部。

While the panoramic window might draw more attention, one of this case’s standout functional features is Corsair’s Infinirail system. Rather than having individual holes for fan screws, there are long slots on sliding rails, giving you more flexibility with cooling components and configuration. As a result, you can fit radiators up to 280mm or 360mm on the top or side of the case, depending on whether you opt for 120mm or 140mm case fans.
虽然全景玻璃窗可能更引人注目，但该机箱最出色的功能之一是海盗船的 Infinirail 系统。它不再使用传统的独立风扇螺丝孔，而是采用了带有滑动导轨的长槽设计，为你安装散热组件和进行配置提供了更大的灵活性。因此，你可以根据选择 120 毫米还是 140 毫米的风扇，在机箱顶部或侧面安装 280 毫米或 360 毫米的冷排。

The case also supports rear connection motherboards like this Gigabyte X870 Aorus for cleaner cable runs, and is ready for vertical GPU mounting if you buy a riser cable. There are some other nice inclusions as well, like an anti-sag GPU support arm, and the screws come in a reusable box rather than a series of tiny plastic bags. The case includes a two year manufacturer’s warranty.
该机箱还支持像技嘉 X870 Aorus 这样的背插式主板，以实现更整洁的理线效果；如果购买转接线，还可以支持显卡竖装。此外，机箱还包含一些贴心的配件，例如防下垂显卡支架，且螺丝采用可重复使用的盒子包装，而非一堆零散的小塑料袋。该机箱提供两年制造商保修。
