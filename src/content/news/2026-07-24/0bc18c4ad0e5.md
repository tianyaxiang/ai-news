### What Is MagSafe?
### 什么是 MagSafe？

MagSafe is Apple’s magnetic technology built into the iPhone 12, iPhone 13, iPhone 14, iPhone 15, iPhone 16, and iPhone 17 ranges (excluding the iPhone 16e). It comprises of a ring of magnets on the back of the phone, and it was developed to create a faster, more precise wireless charging system. Those magnets on the back also connect to different accessories, such as external batteries, car mounts, and wallets.

MagSafe 是苹果公司内置于 iPhone 12、13、14、15、16 和 17 系列（iPhone 16e 除外）中的磁吸技术。它由手机背部的一圈磁铁组成，最初是为了实现更快、更精准的无线充电系统而开发的。手机背部的这些磁铁还可以连接各种配件，例如外接电池、车载支架和磁吸卡包。

If you use a case on your iPhone, you will need to make sure it’s also MagSafe (the case itself will have a ring of magnets inside it). This will ensure a proper connection with MagSafe accessories. If you use a non-MagSafe case, the connection will be very weak, and MagSafe products may just slide off your iPhone.

如果你给 iPhone 套了手机壳，必须确保该手机壳也支持 MagSafe（壳体内部会内置一圈磁铁）。这将确保与 MagSafe 配件的稳固连接。如果你使用的是不支持 MagSafe 的手机壳，磁吸连接会非常微弱，MagSafe 配件可能会从你的 iPhone 上滑落。

### Which iPhones Support MagSafe?
### 哪些 iPhone 支持 MagSafe？

Apple first introduced MagSafe for its iPhones in 2020. The following models support the technology: iPhone 12, 12 Mini, 12 Pro, 12 Pro Max, iPhone 13, 13 Mini, 13 Pro, 13 Pro Max, iPhone 14, 14 Mini, 14 Pro, 14 Pro Max, iPhone 15, 15 Plus, 15 Pro, 15 Pro Max, iPhone 16, 16 Plus, 16 Pro, 16 Pro Max, iPhone 17, 17e, 17 Pro, 17 Pro Max, and iPhone Air.

苹果于 2020 年首次在 iPhone 上引入 MagSafe 技术。以下机型支持该技术：iPhone 12、12 Mini、12 Pro、12 Pro Max、iPhone 13、13 Mini、13 Pro、13 Pro Max、iPhone 14、14 Mini、14 Pro、14 Pro Max、iPhone 15、15 Plus、15 Pro、15 Pro Max、iPhone 16、16 Plus、16 Pro、16 Pro Max、iPhone 17、17e、17 Pro、17 Pro Max 以及 iPhone Air。

### Do MagSafe Wallets Work With Android Phones?
### MagSafe 卡包适用于安卓手机吗？

To use a MagSafe wallet with an Android phone (or the iPhone 16e), you’ll need to use a MagSafe case. Several case manufacturers have started adding a ring of magnets to Android phone cases in order to port over the same magnetic experience native to iPhones. We have guides to cases designed for top Android phone models featuring magnetic options that should play nicely with third-party MagSafe wallets.

要在安卓手机（或 iPhone 16e）上使用 MagSafe 卡包，你需要搭配一个 MagSafe 手机壳。许多手机壳制造商已经开始在安卓手机壳中加入磁吸环，以便将 iPhone 原生的磁吸体验移植过来。我们有针对主流安卓机型设计的磁吸手机壳推荐指南，这些手机壳通常能与第三方 MagSafe 卡包完美兼容。

### Will a MagSafe Wallet Demagnetize My Credit Card?
### MagSafe 卡包会消磁我的信用卡吗？

No, MagSafe will not demagnetize your credit cards. Credit cards have magnetic stripe encoding that’s strong enough to keep them from being demagnetized, and MagSafe wallets in this guide come with a built-in shield designed to keep your cards safe. However, you may run into issues with hotel key cards and some gift cards. Keep those away as a precaution.

不会，MagSafe 不会使你的信用卡消磁。信用卡的磁条编码强度足以防止被消磁，且本指南中推荐的 MagSafe 卡包均内置了屏蔽层，旨在保护你的卡片安全。不过，酒店房卡和某些礼品卡可能会遇到消磁问题。为保险起见，请将这些卡片远离磁吸区域。
