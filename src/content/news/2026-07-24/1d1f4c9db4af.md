# OpenAI is making big claims as it rolls out ChatGPT Health to everyone
# OpenAI 在向所有人推出 ChatGPT Health 之际发表了重大声明

OpenAI is rolling out ChatGPT Health to everyone in the US on Thursday, allowing more people to connect their medical records and health-tracking information to the chatbot. During a briefing, Ashley Alexander, OpenAI’s vice president of health product, says the company’s models “are now capable of reasoning at levels that are better than clinician level.”

OpenAI 将于周四向美国所有用户推出 ChatGPT Health，允许更多人将他们的医疗记录和健康追踪信息连接到该聊天机器人。在一次简报会上，OpenAI 健康产品副总裁 Ashley Alexander 表示，该公司的模型“现在的推理能力已经达到了优于临床医生的水平。”

When asked for more information about how the performance of OpenAI’s models stacks up against human clinicians, OpenAI health lead Karan Singhal says that he would “temper” the claim that they are reasoning at better levels, but that “there have been individual studies that have been pointing in that direction,” like this one from Harvard and Stanford. On Wednesday, a Florida pastor sued OpenAI over claims ChatGPT provided him with “extremely dangerous medical recommendations,” allegedly leading him to put off medical care for a pulmonary embolism.

当被问及 OpenAI 模型的表现与人类临床医生相比如何时，OpenAI 健康业务负责人 Karan Singhal 表示，他会“缓和”关于模型推理能力优于人类的说法，但他补充道，“确实有一些个别研究指向了这一方向”，例如哈佛大学和斯坦福大学的这项研究。周三，佛罗里达州的一位牧师起诉了 OpenAI，声称 ChatGPT 向他提供了“极其危险的医疗建议”，据称导致他推迟了肺栓塞的治疗。

The broader release of ChatGPT Health comes just weeks after OpenAI launched its new GPT-5.6 Sol, which it claims is its “strongest model yet for health.” ChatGPT Health will run on the company’s latest models.

ChatGPT Health 的大范围发布距离 OpenAI 推出其新款 GPT-5.6 Sol 仅过去了几周，该公司称这是其“迄今为止最强大的健康模型”。ChatGPT Health 将运行在该公司最新的模型之上。

With this update, OpenAI will now allow users to ask questions about their connected medical information in the main chat experience, rather than having them navigate to the dedicated ChatGPT Health tab. ChatGPT will ask for permission before using your health information to personalize its response, adding that “all conversations are encrypted at rest and at transit,” while information connected in Health “receives additional encryption protection.”

通过此次更新，OpenAI 现在允许用户在主聊天界面中询问有关其已连接医疗信息的问题，而无需跳转到专门的 ChatGPT Health 标签页。ChatGPT 在使用您的健康信息来个性化回复之前会征求您的许可，并补充称“所有对话在存储和传输过程中均已加密”，而连接到 Health 的信息则“获得了额外的加密保护”。

Users can also review and change the kinds of health data they connect to ChatGPT, including post-visit notes, notes from their care team, lab results, and medications. Currently, users can connect their data from health providers, along with services like Apple Health, Weight Watchers, MyFitnessPal, and Function. OpenAI notes that the ChatGPT Health “supports, not replaces, professional care.” It’s rolling out to all logged-in ChatGPT users aged 18 and older on the web and iOS across free, Go, Plus, and Pro plans.

用户还可以查看和更改他们连接到 ChatGPT 的健康数据类型，包括就诊后记录、护理团队的笔记、实验室检查结果和药物信息。目前，用户可以连接来自医疗服务提供商的数据，以及 Apple Health、Weight Watchers、MyFitnessPal 和 Function 等服务的数据。OpenAI 指出，ChatGPT Health 是“支持而非取代专业医疗护理”。该功能正向所有 18 岁及以上、已登录网页版和 iOS 版 ChatGPT 的用户推出，涵盖免费版、Go 版、Plus 版和 Pro 版计划。
