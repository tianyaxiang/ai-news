# Crowd4D: Scene-Aware Monocular 4D Crowd Reconstruction
# Crowd4D：场景感知的单目 4D 人群重建

**Abstract:** Recovering scene-consistent 4D crowd motion from monocular video in large-scale scenes remains challenging due to severe depth ambiguity and complex scene geometry.
**摘要：** 在大规模场景中，从单目视频恢复与场景一致的 4D 人群运动仍然具有挑战性，这主要是由于严重的深度模糊和复杂的场景几何结构所致。

Existing monocular crowd reconstruction methods typically rely on single-plane assumptions, leading to unreliable metric scale and spatial drift under complex terrain.
现有的单目人群重建方法通常依赖于单平面假设，这导致在复杂地形下会出现度量尺度不可靠和空间漂移的问题。

We propose Crowd4D, the first scene-aware 4D crowd reconstruction framework that jointly optimizes the crowd and scene from a monocular RGB video in large-scale scenes.
我们提出了 Crowd4D，这是首个场景感知的 4D 人群重建框架，能够从大规模场景的单目 RGB 视频中联合优化人群与场景。

Crowd4D explicitly incorporates scene geometry and ensures consistency across image and scene spaces via a multi-stage optimization strategy.
Crowd4D 明确地整合了场景几何信息，并通过多阶段优化策略确保了图像空间与场景空间之间的一致性。

A key bottleneck of this task lies in accurate human-scene alignment, particularly in scale and position. However, human and scene reconstructions are typically decoupled.
该任务的一个关键瓶颈在于实现精确的人与场景对齐，特别是在尺度和位置方面。然而，目前的人群重建与场景重建通常是解耦的。

To address this, we introduce the Human-Scene Interaction Proxy, abbreviated as HSIP, as an intermediate representation derived from Scene Interaction Point Clouds and a Scene Interaction Surface, abbreviated as SIPC and SIS.
为了解决这一问题，我们引入了“人-场景交互代理”（Human-Scene Interaction Proxy，简称 HSIP），作为一种由“场景交互点云”（SIPC）和“场景交互表面”（SIS）导出的中间表示。

These representations encode explicit scene-aware geometric priors and redefine the optimization space for large-scale monocular 4D crowd reconstruction.
这些表示编码了明确的场景感知几何先验，并重新定义了大规模单目 4D 人群重建的优化空间。

To further improve temporal stability under occlusions, we introduce Crowd Structural Coherence Regularization, abbreviated as CSCR, which leverages HSIP-based spatial priors to impose soft temporal consistency on pairwise relative displacements and directions within local crowd neighborhoods.
为了进一步提高遮挡情况下的时间稳定性，我们引入了“人群结构一致性正则化”（Crowd Structural Coherence Regularization，简称 CSCR），它利用基于 HSIP 的空间先验，对局部人群邻域内的成对相对位移和方向施加软时间一致性约束。

Extensive experiments demonstrate that Crowd4D consistently outperforms existing state-of-the-art methods and enables robust monocular 4D crowd reconstruction in complex, large-scale real-world scenes.
大量实验表明，Crowd4D 在性能上始终优于现有的最先进方法，并能够在复杂的大规模真实场景中实现稳健的单目 4D 人群重建。
