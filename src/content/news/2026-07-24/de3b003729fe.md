# The Best Vacuum for Pet Hair—We Tested Many to Find Which Ones Work Best (2026)
# 宠物毛发吸尘器推荐：我们测试了多款产品，找出表现最佳的型号（2026年）

The best vacuum for pet hair is one that actually sucks up the fur—which is a more difficult ask than you may imagine. Pet fur is particularly tough to clean up. If it’s short and coarse, like my cat Basil’s, it gets easily embedded in fabrics and carpet; if it’s long and fluffy like my cat Clover’s, it flies away to accumulate in hard-to-reach corners. But whatever your situation, there’s a vac for that!
最好的宠物毛发吸尘器必须能够真正吸走毛发——这比你想象的要困难得多。宠物毛发特别难以清理。如果是像我家猫 Basil 那样短而粗的毛，很容易嵌入织物和地毯中；如果是像我家猫 Clover 那样长而蓬松的毛，它们会四处飘散，堆积在难以触及的角落。但无论你面临什么情况，总有一款吸尘器适合你！

A few of my pet-owning colleagues and I have tested over a dozen vacuums—from traditional corded models to cordless, handheld, robotic, and even 2-in-1 vac/mop combos—to find out which is actually the best vacuum for pet hair. My consistent favorite cordless option for pet hair has been the Dyson Gen5 Detect ($800), because of its illuminating light that lets you see hairs normally naked to the human eye. I'm not usually the biggest fan of corded vacuums, because of their limitations, but I was also seriously impressed with the amount of deep dirt, dander, and embedded fur the cheap Bissell Pet Hair Eraser Allergen Lift-Off Vacuum ($200) got out of my high-pile carpet.
我和几位养宠物的同事测试了十几款吸尘器——从传统的有线型号到无线、手持、扫地机器人，甚至是吸拖一体机——以找出哪款才是真正的宠物毛发清理神器。我一直以来最喜欢的无线吸尘器是 Dyson Gen5 Detect（800美元），因为它配备的照明灯能让你看到肉眼通常无法察觉的毛发。我通常不太喜欢有线吸尘器，因为它们有局限性，但平价的 Bissell Pet Hair Eraser Allergen Lift-Off Vacuum（200美元）从我的长绒地毯中清理出的深层污垢、皮屑和嵌入毛发的数量，确实让我印象深刻。

Updated July 2025: I've added the Eufy Omni C28, 3i P10 Ultra, Riccar R65 Cordless Stick Vacuum, and Smoture VAC01 Cordless Vacuum Cleaner. I've also updated links and prices throughout.

### Best Cordless Vacuum for Pet Hair: Dyson Gen5 Detect
### 最佳无线宠物毛发吸尘器：Dyson Gen5 Detect

I am completely obsessed with this cordless vacuum. Dyson’s Gen5Detect packs all the punch you’d get from a corded vac while also being lightweight and convenient. It also doubles as a handheld vac with a crevice tool and smaller hair screw tool when you want to do a quick or tight job like stairs or corners. Dyson claims it has the most powerful HEPA filtration of any cordless vac on the market. It even has a digital screen that shows just how much gross stuff you’re vacuuming up, tracking particle size—microscopic, like allergens; fine, like skin flakes; medium, like dust mites; and coarse, like sugar.
我对这款无线吸尘器完全着迷。Dyson Gen5Detect 拥有不输有线吸尘器的强劲吸力，同时又轻便易用。它还可以变身为手持吸尘器，配备缝隙吸头和较小的防缠绕螺旋吸头，方便你处理楼梯或角落等狭窄区域。戴森声称它拥有市面上无线吸尘器中最强大的 HEPA 过滤系统。它甚至配有一个数字屏幕，能实时显示你吸入了多少脏东西，并追踪颗粒大小——包括微观的过敏原、细小的皮屑、中等的尘螨以及粗大的颗粒（如糖粒）。

There’s a Digital Motorbar cleaner head for carpets—with 56 detangling vanes, hair never gets stuck in the bar—and pet hair gets sucked straight into the back for a mess-free clean. There’s also a Fluffy Optic cleaner head for hard floors. It has a super-soft rotating material and an angled green blade of light to illuminate invisible pet hair and dust invisible to the human eye. With 70 minutes of run time and excellent attachments that work for pet hair (and dust and dander) on a myriad of surfaces, this versatile vac is one of my favorite products I’ve ever tested.
它配有一个用于地毯的 Digital Motorbar 清洁吸头，带有 56 个防缠绕叶片，毛发绝不会卡在滚刷上，宠物毛发会被直接吸入后部，清理过程非常干净。此外，还有一个用于硬地板的 Fluffy Optic 清洁吸头，它采用超柔软的旋转材质，并配有倾斜的绿色光束，可以照亮肉眼看不见的宠物毛发和灰尘。凭借 70 分钟的续航时间和适用于多种表面清理宠物毛发（及灰尘、皮屑）的出色配件，这款多功能吸尘器是我测试过的最喜欢的产品之一。

### Best Cheap Cordless Vacuum for Pet Hair: Bissell PowerClean FurFinder
### 最佳平价无线宠物毛发吸尘器：Bissell PowerClean FurFinder

It has three power levels and a big headlight on the front to make it easy to spot fur and cat litter, and it performs well on hardwood, carpet, rugs, and tile. And it turned my cat tree from hairball hell into nearly brand-new. All you need to do is push a button to switch between suction modes; a light indicates the level. It's easy to pop the dust cup off and empty it, plus the canister sits on the outside of the bar, which makes it less likely to lean while cleaning.
它有三个档位，前端配有大灯，可以轻松发现毛发和猫砂，在硬木地板、地毯、垫子和瓷砖上表现出色。它把我那满是毛球的猫爬架清理得几乎焕然一新。你只需按下一个按钮即可切换吸力模式，指示灯会显示当前档位。集尘杯拆卸和清空都很方便，而且集尘桶位于杆身外部，这使得它在清洁时更不容易倾倒。

WIRED reviewer Nena Farrell's favorite part of the vac was the FurFinder Tool, which looks like a mini vacuum head. It's 6 inches wide, about half the size of a regular cordless vacuum head, but it's a fantastic size to vacuum upholstery and platforms on a cat tree. There's also a crevice tool with a brush extension included, and she was impressed with the suction power to easily pull up all types of fur and dust.
WIRED 评测员 Nena Farrell 最喜欢的部分是 FurFinder 吸头，它看起来像一个迷你吸尘头。它宽 6 英寸，大约是普通无线吸尘头的一半大小，但用来清理家具软垫和猫爬架平台非常合适。包装内还附带了一个带刷毛延伸的缝隙吸头，她对这款吸尘器能轻松吸起各种毛发和灰尘的强劲吸力印象深刻。

### Best Handheld Vacuum for Pet Hair: Shark UltraCyclone Pro
### 最佳手持宠物毛发吸尘器：Shark UltraCyclone Pro

This lightweight (just under 3 pounds) handheld vac from Shark is specifically designed to tackle pet hair: It has powerful suction with two cyclonic airstreams, and a motorized self-cleaning power brush sucks the hair back so it doesn't get wrapped around the brush (my biggest complaint with vacuuming pet hair).
这款来自 Shark 的轻量级（不到 3 磅）手持吸尘器专为处理宠物毛发而设计：它拥有双气旋强劲吸力，且配备电动自清洁动力刷，能将毛发吸入，避免其缠绕在刷头上（这是我清理宠物毛发时最大的痛点）。
