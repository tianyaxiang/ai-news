# Final Coyote vs. Acme trailer drops at SDCC
# 《歪心狼大战ACME》终极预告片在圣地亚哥漫展（SDCC）发布

San Diego Comic-Con has kicked off strong, and we’ve got a couple of new trailers to show for it: the final trailer for *Coyote vs. Acme*, and the full trailer for director Zach Cregger’s fresh take on *Resident Evil*. Some lucky folks were also treated to a screening of *Resident Evil*’s first 18 minutes.

圣地亚哥国际动漫展（SDCC）已强势开幕，我们迎来了两部重磅预告片：《歪心狼大战ACME》（Coyote vs. Acme）的终极预告，以及导演扎克·克雷格（Zach Cregger）执导的《生化危机》（Resident Evil）全新力作的完整预告。一些幸运的观众还提前观看了《生化危机》的前18分钟片段。

### Coyote vs. Acme
### 《歪心狼大战ACME》

We were thrilled when the first trailer for *Coyote vs. Acme* dropped in April. The film had been shelved for years and is finally getting the broad theatrical release it deserves thanks to Ketchup Entertainment, which acquired it from Warner Bros. The concept alone is sheer brilliance: Wile E. Coyote, after decades of ACME equipment failing him in his efforts to catch that darned Road Runner, decides to sue the corporation. It’s based on a well-known satirical piece by Ian Frazier (also titled “Coyote vs. Acme”) published in *The New Yorker* in 1990.

今年四月，《歪心狼大战ACME》首支预告片发布时，我们感到非常兴奋。这部电影曾被雪藏多年，多亏了从华纳兄弟手中购得版权的 Ketchup Entertainment，它终于获得了应有的广泛院线发行机会。仅从构思来看，这简直是天才之作：歪心狼（Wile E. Coyote）在几十年来使用 ACME 产品试图捕捉那只讨厌的“哔哔鸟”（Road Runner）却屡屡失败后，决定起诉这家公司。该片改编自伊恩·弗雷泽（Ian Frazier）1990 年发表在《纽约客》上的同名讽刺短文。

Per the official premise: After decades of being blown to bits by bombs, demolished by dynamite, mangled by magnets, battered by boulders, trampled by trains, tricked by tunnels, sprung by springs, steamrolled by steamrollers, maligned by misfires, bedeviled by bungees, rattled by rockets, backstabbed by bat suits, rocked by rocket skates, upended by unicycles, quaked by quake pills, rubberized by rogue bands, and hurled headlong off every cliff in the Southwest, Wile E. Coyote (Genius) finally fights back. Teaming up with billboard accident lawyer Kevin Avery (Will Forte), he takes on slick corporate counsel Buddy Crane (John Cena) and ACME, Inc., the profit-obsessed conglomerate behind every one of the Coyote’s chaotic catastrophes.

根据官方剧情简介：在经历了数十年的被炸弹炸碎、被炸药摧毁、被磁铁夹伤、被巨石撞击、被火车碾压、被隧道戏弄、被弹簧弹飞、被压路机压扁、被哑火折磨、被蹦极绳缠绕、被火箭震动、被蝙蝠装背刺、被火箭溜冰鞋折腾、被独轮车掀翻、被地震药丸震慑、被失控皮筋弹射，以及从西南地区的每一座悬崖上坠落之后，歪心狼（天才）终于决定反击。他与广告牌事故律师凯文·艾弗里（威尔·福特饰）联手，对抗精明的企业律师巴迪·克雷恩（约翰·塞纳饰）以及 ACME 公司——那个制造了歪心狼所有混乱灾难的唯利是图的巨头。

In addition to Forte and Cena, the cast includes Lana Condor as Kevin’s niece, Paige Avery; P.J. Byrne as ACME lawyer Bill Pellicano; and Luis Guzman as the judge presiding over the trial. Director Dave Green even tapped longtime voice actor Eric Bauza—a longtime fixture for Looney Tunes—to voice several classic characters, including Tweety Bird, Daffy Duck, Bugs Bunny, and Foghorn Leghorn.

除了福特和塞纳，演员阵容还包括饰演凯文侄女佩奇·艾弗里的拉娜·康多（Lana Condor）、饰演 ACME 律师比尔·佩利卡诺的 P.J. 伯恩（P.J. Byrne），以及饰演审判法官的路易斯·古兹曼（Luis Guzman）。导演戴夫·格林（Dave Green）甚至邀请了长期为《乐一通》（Looney Tunes）配音的资深配音演员埃里克·鲍扎（Eric Bauza），为崔弟（Tweety Bird）、达菲鸭（Daffy Duck）、兔八哥（Bugs Bunny）和大嘴怪（Foghorn Leghorn）等多个经典角色配音。

We loved the first trailer, and the second is even funnier. There’s not a ton of new footage, but we get Porky Pig showing up for a “pants-less modeling” audition, and Cena’s slick lawyer interrogating Tweety Bird (“I don’t pay you to ‘tink you taw,’ I pay you to ‘taw'”). Good trailers don’t automatically translate into good movies, but it’s still hard to believe that Warner Bros. shelved this movie as a tax write-off. *Coyote vs. Acme* hits theaters on August 28, 2026.

我们很喜欢第一支预告片，而第二支则更加搞笑。虽然新镜头不多，但我们看到了猪小弟（Porky Pig）参加“不穿裤子模特”试镜的片段，以及塞纳饰演的精明律师审问崔弟的场景（“我付钱不是让你‘想说’，我是让你‘说’出来”）。好的预告片并不等同于好的电影，但华纳兄弟竟然为了抵税而雪藏这部电影，实在令人难以置信。《歪心狼大战ACME》将于 2026 年 8 月 28 日在影院上映。

### Resident Evil
### 《生化危机》

The *Resident Evil* film franchise has grossed over $1.2 billion worldwide since the first film debuted in 2002, but an attempt to reboot it a few years ago floundered. Sony Pictures is trying again, this time tapping Zach Cregger—who wrote, produced, and directed last year’s Oscar-winning horror hit *Weapons*—for the project. The studio showed the first teaser for Cregger’s *Resident Evil* during CinemaCon in April and dropped the full trailer today at SDCC.

自 2002 年首部电影上映以来，《生化危机》系列电影在全球已获得超过 12 亿美元的票房，但几年前的重启尝试却以失败告终。索尼影业决定再次尝试，这次他们邀请了去年凭借恐怖片《武器》（Weapons）获得奥斯卡奖的编剧、制片兼导演扎克·克雷格（Zach Cregger）操刀。索尼在四月的 CinemaCon 上展示了克雷格版《生化危机》的首支预告片，并于今日在 SDCC 发布了完整预告。

Per the official logline: “*Resident Evil* follows Bryan (Austin Abrams), a medical courier who unwittingly finds himself in a nonstop race for survival as one fateful, horrifying night collapses around him in chaos.” Joining Abrams (who also appeared in *Weapons*) in the main cast are Paul Walter Hauser as Carl, Zach Cherry as Dave, Kali Reis as Pauline, and Johnno Wilson as Max.

根据官方剧情梗概：“《生化危机》讲述了医疗快递员布莱恩（奥斯汀·艾布拉姆斯饰）的故事，他在一个注定恐怖的夜晚，无意中陷入了一场永无止境的生存竞赛，周围的一切都在混乱中崩塌。”除了艾布拉姆斯（他也出演了《武器》），主要演员还包括饰演卡尔的保罗·沃尔特·豪泽（Paul Walter Hauser）、饰演戴夫的扎克·切里（Zach Cherry）、饰演宝琳的卡莉·瑞斯（Kali Reis）以及饰演马克斯的约翰诺·威尔逊（Johnno Wilson）。

According to Cregger, this new film is not a direct game adaptation, but an original story with original characters set in the same fictional universe. Nor will it be like *Weapons*; Cregger’s *Resident Evil* vision, he said, is closer to *Evil Dead II*. But it would, he said, be “true to the spirit of the games.”

据克雷格透露，这部新片并非直接改编自游戏，而是一个设定在同一虚构宇宙中的原创故事，角色也是全新的。它也不会像《武器》那样；克雷格表示，他心目中的《生化危机》更接近《鬼玩人2》（Evil Dead II）。但他强调，这部电影将“忠于游戏精神”。

The new trailer firmly establishes our protagonist, Bryan, as a likable everyman working as a medical delivery driver to bring transplant organs to hospitals. It’s a cold winter night, with heavy snow falling, when he agrees to make an urgent delivery outside “the zone”—for double the pay—to Raccoon City General. While Bryan is driving and arguing with his pregnant girlfriend on the phone, a woman walks into the road, and he hits her. We know she’s a zombie, but Bryan does not—although he’ll figure it out pretty quickly. We see him searching frantically for ammo in a nearby house where something very bad has clearly happened. The rest of the town is just as overrun, and according to Carl, “Everything that has happened in this town transpired in a matter of hours.” So much for Bryan’s extra run being “no sweat.” *Resident Evil* opens in theaters on September 18, 2026.

新预告片确立了主角布莱恩的形象：一个讨人喜欢的普通人，职业是医疗快递员，负责将移植器官送往医院。在一个大雪纷飞的寒冷冬夜，他为了双倍报酬，同意前往“隔离区”外的浣熊市总医院进行紧急配送。当布莱恩一边开车一边与怀孕的女友通电话争吵时，一名女子走上马路，他撞倒了她。我们知道那是丧尸，但布莱恩不知道——尽管他很快就会发现。我们看到他在一栋显然发生了惨剧的房子里疯狂寻找弹药。镇上的其他地方也同样被丧尸占领，正如卡尔所说：“这个镇上发生的一切，仅仅是在几个小时内发生的。”布莱恩原本以为这趟额外的工作会“轻而易举”，结果却事与愿违。《生化危机》将于 2026 年 9 月 18 日在影院上映。
