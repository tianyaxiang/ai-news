# 22 Back-to-School College Dorm Room Essentials (2026)

### A Great Paper Planner
### 出色的纸质日程本

Even though basically everything is digital these days, a paper planner like this one—my favorite overall—is great for jotting down important schedule updates. Plus, putting pen to paper can be quite therapeutic. What I love about this planner is that it’s inexpensive, has monthly and weekly spreads, and includes two open pages for notes each week. Because it's undated, students can use it however they choose. It's flexible enough to work for basically anybody.

尽管如今几乎一切都已数字化，但像这款我最钟爱的纸质日程本，依然是记录重要日程更新的绝佳工具。此外，笔尖在纸上书写的感觉非常治愈。我喜欢这款日程本的原因在于它价格亲民，拥有月度和周度视图，且每周都包含两页空白笔记页。由于它没有预设日期，学生可以根据自己的需求自由使用，其灵活性足以满足任何人的习惯。

### A Solid Backpack
### 结实的背包

You don't need to spend a ton to get a well-made backpack for class. Herschel's Heritage Backpack was my college bag of choice, and it's still holding up, years following graduation. It has a 15-inch laptop sleeve and enough room for bulky textbooks in the main compartment. My one gripe is its lack of a water bottle pocket. The bag is made from durable 600-denier polyester with a faux-leather bottom and handles.

你不需要花大价钱就能买到一个做工精良的上课背包。Herschel Heritage 背包曾是我大学时的首选，毕业多年后它依然完好如初。它配有一个15英寸的笔记本电脑隔层，主仓空间足以容纳厚重的教科书。我唯一的不满是它缺少水杯侧袋。这款背包采用耐用的600D聚酯纤维制成，并配有仿皮底座和提手。

### 营造香氛环境

You can also change the color of the light and rotate the direction of the plug.

It's a whopping 4 inches thick, so it'll make your bed extra-comfortable. It's also cooling and antimicrobial thanks to its copper-infused memory foam construction and GlacioTex cooling covers. It's dense and deep, and it’ll ensure you won't feel like you're sleeping on a folding table.

别买普通的床垫保护套了，买这个吧。这款床垫罩是我们推荐的宿舍首选，提供各种尺寸。它足足有4英寸厚，能让你的床变得格外舒适。得益于注入铜离子的记忆棉结构和 GlacioTex 凉感面料，它还具备降温和抗菌功能。它质地厚实，能确保你不会有睡在折叠桌上的感觉。

### 可重复使用的笔记本

Rocketbook makes a variety of notebooks and styles, but WIRED reviewer Nena Farrell recommends the Fusion Plus, since it gives you a nice variety of page styles, including classic monthly and weekly pages and project-management layouts.
