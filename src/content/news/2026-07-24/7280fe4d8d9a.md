# Runway launches AI model router as generative media gets crowded
# 随着生成式媒体领域竞争加剧，Runway 推出 AI 模型路由工具

Runway no longer wants to be just another AI model company. It wants to become the infrastructure layer for generative media. On Thursday, the startup launched Runway Media Router through Runway Dev, its developer platform, released earlier this month, that provides API access to a growing roster of third-party image, video, and audio models alongside Runway’s own.
Runway 不再只想做一家普通的 AI 模型公司，它希望成为生成式媒体的基础设施层。本周四，这家初创公司通过本月初发布的开发者平台 Runway Dev 推出了“Runway 媒体路由”（Runway Media Router）。该平台不仅提供 Runway 自家的模型，还通过 API 接入了越来越多的第三方图像、视频和音频模型。

The Media Router is a tool that automatically selects the best image, video, or audio generation model for a request based on whether a developer prioritizes quality, speed, or cost. While model routers have become increasingly common in the world of large language models, Runway says this is the first built specifically for generative media.
“媒体路由”工具可以根据开发者对质量、速度或成本的优先级，自动为请求选择最佳的图像、视频或音频生成模型。虽然模型路由在大型语言模型领域已变得越来越普遍，但 Runway 表示，这是首个专门为生成式媒体构建的路由工具。

“The routing really fits into that overall promise of being the easiest one-stop shop for developers to integrate with any type of generative media model,” Anthony Maggio, Runway’s chief product officer, told TechCrunch. The launch, shared exclusively with TechCrunch, marks another step in Runway’s evolution from an AI video startup into infrastructure for companies building with generative media.
Runway 首席产品官 Anthony Maggio 在接受 TechCrunch 采访时表示：“路由功能非常符合我们的整体愿景，即成为开发者集成任何类型生成式媒体模型时最简单的一站式商店。”此次发布是与 TechCrunch 独家分享的，标志着 Runway 从一家 AI 视频初创公司向生成式媒体基础设施提供商转型的又一重要步骤。

Through Runway Dev, customers including Adobe, Cloudflare, ElevenLabs, Expedia, Shutterstock, and Quora can build media generation directly into their own products using Runway’s API rather than sending their users to Runway’s own app or site. The launch of the router comes as the number of generative media models has exploded, making it increasingly difficult and time-consuming for developers to evaluate new releases.
通过 Runway Dev，包括 Adobe、Cloudflare、ElevenLabs、Expedia、Shutterstock 和 Quora 在内的客户，可以直接利用 Runway 的 API 将媒体生成功能集成到自己的产品中，而无需将用户引导至 Runway 的应用程序或网站。此次路由工具的发布正值生成式媒体模型数量激增之际，这使得开发者评估新模型变得越来越困难且耗时。

Through the Runway Dev platform, developers can access the latest media models when they’re released. “Most developers are not spending the time to really understand the capabilities of each of these models and where they excel or differ based on various types of outputs across video, image, and audio,” Maggio said. “The unique proposition we’re bringing to the table is all of that intelligence around what the best model is for each different use case, and meshing that with preference you apply around the context of your business.”
通过 Runway Dev 平台，开发者可以在最新媒体模型发布时第一时间获取使用。Maggio 说：“大多数开发者并没有花时间去深入了解这些模型的具体能力，以及它们在视频、图像和音频等不同输出类型上的优劣差异。我们带来的独特价值在于，我们整合了关于‘哪种模型最适合特定用例’的智能，并将其与开发者根据自身业务背景设定的偏好相结合。”

Maggio noted that Chinese generative media models are becoming increasingly popular. However, many businesses building their own products might not be comfortable working with models that come out of China, so they could, he said, potentially set a preference for American model providers — a preference that may become more common as the Trump administration explores bans and sanctions against Chinese open AI models.
Maggio 指出，中国的生成式媒体模型正变得越来越受欢迎。然而，许多开发自有产品的企业可能并不习惯使用来自中国的模型，因此他们可能会倾向于选择美国模型提供商。随着特朗普政府探索对中国开源 AI 模型实施禁令和制裁，这种偏好可能会变得更加普遍。

That’s just one example of preferences that developers can set, though. Maggio says customers are mainly interested in routing the model to account for token pricing and quality. Token pricing has become a hot topic in 2026 as enterprises that went all-in on agentic AI felt the sting of high token bills. In the world of LLMs, model routing for token pricing has become common, so it only makes sense that routing for generative media would follow.
不过，这只是开发者可以设置的偏好示例之一。Maggio 表示，客户主要关注的是基于 Token 定价和质量进行模型路由。随着全面投入智能体（Agentic AI）的企业感受到高额 Token 账单的压力，Token 定价在 2026 年已成为热门话题。在大型语言模型（LLM）领域，基于 Token 定价的模型路由已成常态，因此生成式媒体领域跟进这一做法也是顺理成章。

The Media Router launch also comes weeks after Runway replaced its unlimited subscription plans with token-based pricing, a move that drew criticism from some users. On the quality front, deciding which models provide the best quality for any given task isn’t as easy for generative media as it is with language models, Maggio says. That’s where the router’s intelligence layer kicks in.
在推出媒体路由工具的几周前，Runway 刚刚用基于 Token 的定价取代了无限订阅计划，此举引发了一些用户的批评。Maggio 表示，在质量方面，对于生成式媒体而言，判断哪些模型在特定任务中表现最佳，并不像语言模型那样简单。这正是路由工具的智能层发挥作用的地方。

It’s based on the expertise Runway’s in-house creative team has developed in evaluating output across every media type — things like how video models handle motion, how image models handle composition, or how voice models handle lip syncing. Runway had already done a lot of the work building that intelligence layer for its agent product, a conversational AI creative partner that Runway launched in May to help turn text prompts into fully edited multi-shot videos and marketing campaigns.
该智能层基于 Runway 内部创意团队在评估各类媒体输出时积累的专业知识，例如视频模型如何处理运动、图像模型如何处理构图，或语音模型如何处理口型同步。Runway 此前已为其智能体产品构建了大量此类智能层工作，该智能体是 Runway 在 5 月推出的对话式 AI 创意伙伴，旨在帮助将文本提示转化为经过完整剪辑的多镜头视频和营销活动。

The Runway Media Router, Maggio says, takes the same routing technology Runway built for its own products and packages it for outside developers to use. Runway’s strategy today reflects how fragmented and competitive the current generative media landscape is, and how much the startup needs to expand and pivot to stay competitive.
Maggio 表示，Runway 媒体路由工具采用了 Runway 为自家产品构建的相同路由技术，并将其封装供外部开发者使用。Runway 今天的战略反映了当前生成式媒体领域的碎片化和竞争激烈程度，也体现了这家初创公司为了保持竞争力，必须不断扩张和转型。

Runway’s last AI video model release — Gen 4.5 — was in December. At the time, the model topped leaderboards, outperforming similar models from incumbents like Google. In the same month, Runway released its first world model. Aside from an upgrade to its video editing model, Aleph 2.0, in May, Runway hasn’t dropped a new dedicated frontier video model in months. (TechCrunch has asked when the startup plans to release Gen 5.)
Runway 上一次发布 AI 视频模型 Gen 4.5 是在去年 12 月。当时，该模型在排行榜上名列前茅，表现优于谷歌等老牌厂商的同类模型。同月，Runway 发布了其首个世界模型。除了 5 月份对其视频编辑模型 Aleph 2.0 的升级外，Runway 已经好几个月没有发布新的前沿视频模型了。（TechCrunch 已询问该公司计划何时发布 Gen 5。）

Today, while Aleph 2.0 ranks among the leading video editing models according to Artificial Analysis, the company’s text-to-video and image-to-video models no longer lead the rankings. In the top 20 spots are models from heavy hitters like Google and China’s ByteDance and Alibaba. Rather than asking developers to bet on a single model staying ahead, Media Router assumes that the best model will continue to change — and it keeps Runway in the game so that it can continue to build on the frontier. If not as the best new AI model, then as the best orchestration layer.
如今，尽管根据 Artificial Analysis 的数据，Aleph 2.0 仍位居领先的视频编辑模型之列，但该公司的文生视频和图生视频模型已不再处于榜首。排名前 20 的模型中，充斥着谷歌、中国字节跳动和阿里巴巴等巨头的产品。媒体路由工具不再要求开发者押注于单一模型，而是预设了“最佳模型将不断更迭”的前提——这让 Runway 能够继续留在赛场上，在技术前沿持续深耕。即便不是作为最强 AI 模型，也要成为最强的编排层。

Anastasis Germanidis, Runway’s co-founder and co-CEO, acknowledged that the startup has been known for a long time primarily for “that end user piece,” but it had to build a full stack to get there, one that includes a developer platform, a creative tool suite, and an inference layer underneath it all. He says the company has seen increasing interest from companies for Runway to live across every part of that stack.
Runway 联合创始人兼联席 CEO Anastasis Germanidis 承认，长期以来，这家初创公司主要以“终端用户产品”而闻名，但为了达到这一目标，它必须构建一个完整的技术栈，包括开发者平台、创意工具套件以及底层的推理层。他说，公司发现越来越多的企业希望 Runway 能覆盖这一技术栈的每一个环节。

“You need great models underneath, but the orchestration increasingly matters a lot because people are building entire campaigns with those models, or they’re building entire finished multi-scene generations out of those models,” Germanidis told TechCrunch. “It’s something that we increasingly had to build — that intelligence layer that comes on top of the pure pixel models. The router is one way in which the benefits...”
“你需要底层的优秀模型，但编排工作正变得越来越重要，因为人们正在利用这些模型构建整个营销活动，或者利用它们生成完整的、多场景的成品，”Germanidis 对 TechCrunch 说。“这是我们越来越需要构建的东西——即在纯像素模型之上增加的智能层。路由工具就是我们实现这一目标的方式之一……”
