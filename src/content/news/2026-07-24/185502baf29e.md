# VQ-Transplant: Efficient VQ-Module Integration for Pre-trained Visual Tokenizers
# VQ-Transplant：面向预训练视觉分词器的高效 VQ 模块集成方案

Vector Quantization (VQ) underpins modern discrete visual tokenization. However, training quantization modules for state-of-the-art VQ-based models requires significant computational resources which, in practice, all but prevents the development of novel, cutting-edge VQ techniques under resource constraints.
向量量化（VQ）是现代离散视觉分词技术的基础。然而，训练最先进的 VQ 模型所需的量化模块需要消耗巨大的计算资源，这在实际操作中几乎阻碍了在资源受限条件下开发新型、前沿 VQ 技术的能力。

To address this limitation, we propose **VQ-Transplant**, a simple framework that enables plug-and-play integration of new VQ modules into frozen, pre-trained tokenizers by replacing their native VQ modules. Crucially, the proposed transplantation process preserves all encoder-decoder parameters, obviating the need for costly end-to-end retraining when modifying the quantization method.
为了解决这一局限性，我们提出了 **VQ-Transplant**。这是一个简单的框架，通过替换预训练分词器原有的 VQ 模块，实现了新 VQ 模块的即插即用式集成。至关重要的是，该移植过程保留了所有的编码器-解码器参数，从而在修改量化方法时无需进行昂贵的端到端重训练。

To mitigate decoder-quantization mismatch, we introduce a lightweight decoder adaptation strategy (trained for only 5 epochs on ImageNet-1k) to align feature priors with the new quantization space.
为了缓解解码器与量化器之间的不匹配问题，我们引入了一种轻量级的解码器适配策略（在 ImageNet-1k 上仅训练 5 个 epoch），旨在将特征先验与新的量化空间对齐。

In our empirical evaluation, we find that VQ-Transplant allows obtaining near state-of-the-art reconstruction fidelity for industry-level models like VAR while reducing the training cost by 95%. VQ-Transplant democratizes quantization research by enabling resource-efficient integration of novel VQ techniques while matching industry-level reconstruction performance.
在实证评估中，我们发现 VQ-Transplant 能够使 VAR 等工业级模型获得接近最先进水平的重建保真度，同时将训练成本降低了 95%。VQ-Transplant 通过实现新型 VQ 技术的高效资源集成，同时保持工业级的重建性能，从而让量化研究变得更加普及。
