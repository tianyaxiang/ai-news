### The power line that could reshape New York’s grid is hitting snags
### 可能重塑纽约电网的输电线路遭遇阻碍

During a heat wave on July 3, New York State’s grid imported enough electricity from Canada to meet about 9% of its total demand that day. Some of that power shuttled in on a 339-mile power line stretching from Quebec to Queens. It opened in May and is officially the longest underground transmission line in North America. It could provide up to 20% of New York City’s electricity demand, largely with abundant hydropower from Quebec. One wrinkle: The line has been down for most of this month, and some experts are concerned about how drought will affect the power supply feeding it. Still, the line could help shape the future of our grid, if it can overcome these sorts of snags. Read our story to understand how. —Casey Crownhart
在7月3日的热浪期间，纽约州电网从加拿大进口的电力足以满足当天约9%的总需求。其中部分电力通过一条从魁北克延伸至皇后区、全长339英里的输电线路输送。该线路于5月开通，是北美正式最长的地下输电线路。它最多可提供纽约市20%的电力需求，主要依靠魁北克丰富的水力发电。但有一个问题：该线路本月大部分时间处于停运状态，一些专家担心干旱会影响其电力供应。尽管如此，如果能克服这些障碍，这条线路仍有助于塑造我们电网的未来。阅读我们的报道以了解详情。——Casey Crownhart

**Welcome to the dark side of crypto’s permissionless dream**
**欢迎来到加密货币“无需许可”梦想的阴暗面**

Jean-Paul Thorbjornsen is a founder of THORChain, a blockchain through which users can swap one cryptocurrency for another and earn fees from making those swaps. But is he responsible for what it’s used for? It’s a question that matters because in January last year, its users lost more than $200 million in cryptocurrency after THORChain transactions and accounts were frozen by an admin override, which users believed was not supposed to be possible given the decentralized structure. It’s also been used by North Korean hackers to move $1.2 billion of stolen ethereum. Thorbjornsen explains this all away as a function of THORChain’s decentralized and permissionless nature. Read our story exploring whether we should believe him or not. —Jessica Klein
Jean-Paul Thorbjornsen是THORChain的创始人，这是一个区块链，用户可以通过它交换加密货币并从中赚取手续费。但他是否应对其用途负责？这是一个重要的问题，因为去年1月，在THORChain的交易和账户被管理员权限冻结后，用户损失了超过2亿美元的加密货币，而用户认为鉴于其去中心化结构，这本不该发生。它还被朝鲜黑客用于转移12亿美元被盗的以太坊。Thorbjornsen将这一切解释为THORChain去中心化和无需许可性质的必然结果。阅读我们的报道，探讨我们是否应该相信他。——Jessica Klein
