# The DJI Osmo Pocket 3 lookalike is down to $329
# DJI Osmo Pocket 3 的“平替”产品降至 329 美元

Xtra’s Muse is $50 less than DJI’s version. You can also save on Apple’s iPad Air (M3) and the black Magic Mouse.
Xtra Muse 的价格比 DJI 的同类产品便宜 50 美元。此外，你还可以以优惠价格入手苹果 iPad Air (M3) 和黑色版妙控鼠标 (Magic Mouse)。

Initially, I was going to tell you about a good deal happening on the DJI Osmo Pocket 3, which is down from the $500 it’s sold at most of the year to $378 at multiple retailers. But there’s a better deal happening on a nearly identical portable camera, the Xtra Muse, which is $329 (roughly $100 off what it sold for three months ago). It’s not just some copycat; my colleague Sean Hollister’s report on the company is linked below, wherein he said the Muse “appears to be the same product in almost every palpable way,” though DJI won’t comment on its involvement with Xtra.
起初，我打算向大家介绍 DJI Osmo Pocket 3 的促销活动，该产品今年大部分时间售价为 500 美元，目前在多家零售商处已降至 378 美元。但现在有一款几乎一模一样的便携式摄像机——Xtra Muse，它的价格更具吸引力，仅为 329 美元（比三个月前的售价便宜了约 100 美元）。它不仅仅是简单的山寨品；我的同事 Sean Hollister 在下文链接的报道中提到，Muse “在几乎所有可感知的方面都与 DJI 的产品如出一辙”，尽管 DJI 方面拒绝就其与 Xtra 的关系置评。

Regardless, you can save a good bit of change on the Muse right now at Amazon. It seems great for vlogging (Sean uses the DJI Osmo Pocket 3 for recording Today I’m Toying with videos), or for any scenario where you want better footage than your smartphone can likely capture.
无论如何，你现在可以在亚马逊上以相当划算的价格买到 Muse。它非常适合视频博主（Sean 使用 DJI Osmo Pocket 3 来录制《Today I’m Toying》系列视频），或者适用于任何你想要获得比智能手机拍摄效果更好的场景。

At launch, the Muse couldn’t connect to Bluetooth mics, which meant that DJI’s own wireless mics were off the table. But as of a February firmware update, it can wirelessly connect to various mics, including DJI’s Mic 2, Mini Mini and the Mic 3, among others.
在发布之初，Muse 无法连接蓝牙麦克风，这意味着 DJI 自家的无线麦克风无法使用。但在二月份的固件更新后，它已经可以无线连接多种麦克风，包括 DJI Mic 2、Mini Mini 以及 Mic 3 等。

Best Buy still has the lowest prices on Apple’s last-gen (but still great) iPad Air with the M3 processor. While retailers like Amazon and Walmart are either out of stock or selling refurbished tablets around $450 and up, Best Buy has the 128GB version with Wi-Fi and 5G support for $499. Sure, you could opt just for the version that offers just Wi-Fi connectivity at the same price, but it’s a better value to get the cellular option.
