### Best Overall Keyboard: Keychron K3 Ultra 8K
### 最佳综合键盘：Keychron K3 Ultra 8K

One of the highlights of Keychron’s current lineup is its selection of mid-century inspired keyboards with clean, simple designs and wood highlights. Instead of the standard gamer-y aesthetic, these keyboards offer a more refined style that looks at home on a professional’s desk. The K3 is the first low-profile keyboard in this series, offering a slimmer and more portable typing experience with the same fantastic design language. While this isn’t necessarily the best keyboard on this list by any one metric, I think it’s the most practical option for the largest number of users.
Keychron目前产品线的一大亮点是其一系列受中世纪风格启发的键盘，它们拥有简洁的设计和木质装饰。与常见的“电竞风”不同，这些键盘呈现出一种更精致的风格，非常适合放在专业人士的办公桌上。K3是该系列中首款轻薄型键盘，在保持出色设计语言的同时，提供了更纤薄、更便携的打字体验。虽然它在某些单一指标上未必是榜单中最强的，但我认为它是绝大多数用户最实用的选择。

There are two versions of the K3: The typing-focused Ultra, and the gaming-focused HE. The Ultra uses standard low-profile mechanical switches, with options for either linear Red switches, or tactile Brown or Banana switches. These switches can be swapped out without any soldering thanks to the hot-swap sockets on the PCB. The switches and stabilizers come lubed from the factory, resulting in typing that feels smooth and offers a consistent, rattle-free sound when typing.
K3有两个版本：侧重打字的Ultra版和侧重游戏的HE版。Ultra版使用标准的轻薄机械轴体，提供线性红轴，或段落感的茶轴/香蕉轴可选。得益于PCB上的热插拔轴座，这些轴体无需焊接即可更换。轴体和卫星轴在出厂时已预润滑，打字手感顺滑，且敲击声音一致、无杂音。

The HE offers only linear Hall Effect switches, but these allow for more advanced software customization, such as adjustable actuation distances (how far you press the switch before it registers an input) and Rapid Trigger (allows for more rapid keypresses by detecting the direction of the switch’s movement). While the enhanced customization of a Hall Effect switch is great for gaming, the adjustability won’t be worth the added cost and loss of switch customization for the average user.
HE版仅提供线性磁轴，但它们支持更高级的软件定制，例如可调触发距离（按压轴体到触发输入所需的距离）和快速触发（Rapid Trigger，通过检测轴体运动方向实现更快速的按键响应）。虽然磁轴的增强定制功能对游戏玩家很有用，但对于普通用户来说，为了这些功能而增加成本并牺牲轴体定制性并不划算。

### Best Keyboard for Work: Keychron B2 Pro
### 最佳办公键盘：Keychron B2 Pro

Keychron’s B2 Pro is one of the best scissor-switch keyboards available, mainly because of the customization it allows. This keyboard uses Keychron’s Launcher, an in-browser software that provides extensive and intuitive customization options for its keyboards, with everything from remapping and macros to multiple function layers. For any type of productivity work, this level of customization can be a great way to optimize an already-good keyboard to one that's perfect for your regular tasks.
Keychron B2 Pro是目前市面上最好的剪刀脚键盘之一，主要原因在于其强大的定制能力。该键盘支持Keychron Launcher（一款基于浏览器的软件），为键盘提供了广泛且直观的定制选项，包括按键重映射、宏命令以及多层功能层。对于任何类型的生产力工作而言，这种程度的定制化能将一款优秀的键盘优化为完美契合你日常任务的工具。

This keyboard offers a good typing experience for the price, using low-profile scissor switches with a short and snappy key press without being particularly loud or disruptive. The case is made entirely of plastic, and the keycaps are subtly sculpted with a dish (similar to Logitech’s MX Keys line), which makes finding the center of a keycap easier when touch-typing.
这款键盘在同价位中提供了良好的打字体验，采用轻薄的剪刀脚轴体，按键行程短且反馈干脆，同时不会产生过大的噪音。外壳完全由塑料制成，键帽带有微妙的凹陷设计（类似于罗技的MX Keys系列），这使得盲打时更容易定位键帽中心。

### Best Portable Keyboard: Razer Joro Portable Wireless Gaming Keyboard
### 最佳便携键盘：Razer Joro 便携无线游戏键盘

Slim, durable, and highly customizable, Razer’s Joro is pretty much the most compact gaming keyboard you can find today. It’s thinner than a laptop and just barely thicker than an iPhone, and has the same footprint as a standard 60 percent keyboard, meaning it can slip easily into a backpack, tote bag, or even a large purse. The Joro uses Razer’s Synapse software, an incredibly powerful and intuitive customization app that lets you control the keyboard’s RGB lighting, remap any of its keys, and program macros with ease.
轻薄、耐用且高度可定制，Razer Joro几乎是你目前能找到的最紧凑的游戏键盘。它比笔记本电脑更薄，厚度仅略高于iPhone，且占地面积与标准的60%键盘相同，这意味着它可以轻松塞进背包、手提袋甚至大号钱包里。Joro使用Razer Synapse软件，这是一个功能极其强大且直观的定制应用，让你能轻松控制键盘的RGB灯效、重映射任意按键并编写宏命令。

The only significant downside of this keyboard is the lack of a 2.4-GHz dongle. This means the keyboard can connect only to devices via Bluetooth or a wired connection. While this isn’t a deal-breaker for me, since the device still has multi-device Bluetooth connectivity, it does slightly hinder the keyboard's performance.
这款键盘唯一明显的缺点是缺少2.4GHz接收器。这意味着键盘只能通过蓝牙或有线方式连接设备。虽然对我来说这并非不可接受，因为该设备仍支持多设备蓝牙连接，但这确实在一定程度上限制了键盘的性能表现。
