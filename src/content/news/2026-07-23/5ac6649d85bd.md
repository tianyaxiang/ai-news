# Surprise Forcing: What to Remember, When to Skip in Long Video Generation
# Surprise Forcing：长视频生成中“记什么”与“跳过什么”的决策机制

**Abstract:** Streaming autoregressive diffusion makes minute-scale video synthesis practical, but its bounded context and fixed denoising schedule allocate resources uniformly across a highly non-stationary sequence. A rolling key-value cache forgets distant visual evidence even when that evidence remains important, while every generated chunk receives the same number of denoising passes irrespective of its actual difficulty.

**摘要：** 流式自回归扩散模型使得分钟级的视频合成成为可能，但其有限的上下文窗口和固定的去噪调度导致资源在高度非平稳的序列中被均匀分配。滚动键值缓存（Rolling key-value cache）往往会遗忘远处的视觉证据，即便这些证据依然重要；同时，无论生成片段的实际难度如何，每个片段都会被分配相同次数的去噪过程。

We introduce Surprise Forcing, a training-free framework that treats both limitations as online resource-allocation problems. A Surprise-Gated Memory Bank summarizes evicted frames with value-token descriptors, evaluates them using complementary global-deviation and nearest-neighbor novelty signals, and regulates admission through a feedback-controlled budget in normalized score space. Priority-based replacement and relevance-aware routing then keep the external memory compact and useful.

我们引入了“Surprise Forcing”，这是一个无需训练的框架，将上述两个局限性视为在线资源分配问题来解决。其中，“惊喜门控记忆库”（Surprise-Gated Memory Bank）利用价值标记描述符（value-token descriptors）总结被剔除的帧，通过互补的全局偏差和最近邻新颖性信号进行评估，并利用归一化分数空间中的反馈控制预算来调节准入。基于优先级的替换机制和相关性感知路由，确保了外部存储的紧凑性和有效性。

In parallel, Surprise-Aware Denoising estimates chunk difficulty from the maximum adjacent-frame cosine distance after the first denoising pass and uses a local percentile scheduler to skip intermediate steps for comparatively easy chunks. Experiments on VBench, VBench-Long, and VBench-2.0 show that the proposed allocation strategy improves long-horizon consistency and visual quality while retaining real-time streaming throughput.

与此同时，“惊喜感知去噪”（Surprise-Aware Denoising）通过首次去噪后的最大相邻帧余弦距离来评估片段难度，并利用局部百分位调度器（local percentile scheduler）跳过相对简单片段的中间步骤。在 VBench、VBench-Long 和 VBench-2.0 上的实验表明，该分配策略在保持实时流式传输吞吐量的同时，显著提升了长序列的一致性和视觉质量。
