# Dyson V10 Konical Review (2026): Affordable and Solid
# 戴森 V10 Konical 评测（2026）：性价比之选，表现稳健

I’ve spent the past week living with Dyson’s newest vacuums, and a clear favorite has emerged. But it’s not the one I expected.
过去一周，我一直在使用戴森最新的几款吸尘器，其中一款脱颖而出，成为了我的最爱。但它并不是我预想中的那一款。

I was ready to love the V16 Piston Animal for all the capabilities and suction power it offers, but as I’ve been testing, it’s the Dyson V10 Konical that has left me most impressed for its performance at this price. Funny enough, when thinking about Dyson’s new lineup, it’s the least flashy offering, and the one I always forget about. The V8 Cyclone revives a longtime favorite, while the V16 packs the highest suction we’ve seen yet, making both easy to remember and exciting to try.
我本以为自己会爱上 V16 Piston Animal，因为它功能全面且吸力强劲，但在测试过程中，V10 Konical 凭借其在该价位段的表现给我留下了最深刻的印象。有趣的是，在戴森的新品阵容中，它显得最不起眼，也是我最容易忽略的一款。V8 Cyclone 复刻了经典，而 V16 则拥有我们目前见过的最强吸力，这两款产品都让人印象深刻且充满期待。

It was easy to let the V10 Konical fade into the background. It has the same power level as the V8 but a slightly higher price tag thanks to the new All Floor Cones cleaner head instead of the older Motorbar style.
V10 Konical 很容易被淹没在众多产品中。它的功率与 V8 相当，但由于配备了全新的 All Floor Cones 地刷而非老款的 Motorbar 地刷，价格略高一些。

When placed side by side, though, the V10 Konical easily justifies its $100 higher price over the V8 Cyclone. It did better than the V8 in almost every test I ran when comparing Dyson’s vacuums side by side, and it did the best when vacuuming up sand on a rug (even better than more expensive Dyson vacuums like the V15 Detect and Gen5Detect). If you’re looking for a good Dyson vacuum that doesn’t cost the better part of a grand, this is the one to get.
然而，将两者放在一起对比时，V10 Konical 完全对得起它比 V8 Cyclone 高出的 100 美元差价。在几乎所有的对比测试中，它的表现都优于 V8，尤其是在清理地毯上的沙子时，表现甚至超过了 V15 Detect 和 Gen5Detect 等更昂贵的型号。如果你想买一台性能出色且价格不到一千美元的戴森吸尘器，选这一款准没错。

### Stat Block
### 数据概览

The Dyson V10 Konical packs a solid range of features for its price. It packs 150 air watts of power, which is above average for lightweight stick vacs, though it is weaker than most of Dyson’s own stick vacs. Our favored V15 Detect has 240 air watts, for example, but the V10 Konical felt similar on carpet to the V15 Detect, even with the change in suction power. It even felt like it suctioned better when I used it in handheld mode.
Dyson V10 Konical 以其价格提供了相当扎实的功能。它拥有 150 空气瓦（AW）的吸力，对于轻量级手持吸尘器来说高于平均水平，尽管比戴森大多数其他型号要弱。例如，我们青睐的 V15 Detect 拥有 240 AW 的吸力，但 V10 Konical 在地毯上的实际体感与 V15 Detect 差异不大。甚至在手持模式下，我觉得它的吸力表现更好。

That’s because just suction power alone doesn’t make a vacuum great at sucking up dirt and debris. A great motor and vacuum head design are equally important, if not more so. Dyson says the V10 Konical’s engine has 15 cyclone chambers built into it for air to flow through and create efficient suction. So while the power is lower than the V15, the two vacuums feel much more similar than I expected, since it still has impressive suction for its power and price point.
这是因为单纯的吸力并不代表吸尘器清理污垢和碎屑的效果就好。优秀的电机和地刷设计同样重要，甚至更为关键。戴森表示，V10 Konical 的引擎内置了 15 个气旋室，通过空气流动产生高效吸力。因此，尽管功率低于 V15，但两者的实际使用感受比我预期的要接近得多，毕竟以它的功率和价位来看，其吸力表现依然令人印象深刻。

The V10 also has a seven-cell lithium battery that lasts for 60 minutes, like the more expensive V15 before it. There are three power modes, but there’s no screen on this device—just a built-in light on the mode button that shows you which setting you’re on. There’s green for eco mode, blue for medium mode, and red for max mode. The mode changes the suction level, so eco is great for lighter cleans while max is best for bigger messes; each higher mode uses more battery. The lack of a screen also means you’ll fly blind on battery life, so beware how long you spend on more powerful modes.
V10 配备了七芯锂电池，续航时间长达 60 分钟，与更昂贵的 V15 一样。它有三种功率模式，但机身上没有屏幕，只有模式按钮上的内置灯光来显示当前设置：绿色代表节能模式，蓝色代表中档模式，红色代表强力模式。不同模式对应不同的吸力水平，节能模式适合轻度清洁，强力模式则适合处理大面积污垢；档位越高，耗电越快。由于没有屏幕，你无法直观看到剩余电量，因此在使用强力模式时需要留意使用时间。

The V10 comes with the new All Floor Cones cleaner head, which is similar to the head that comes with the new V16 Piston Animal (though that version can sense messes). The All Floor Cones head has cones on either side to suck up hair and debris; the cone design promises to detangle long hair instead of letting it get stuck around the vacuum head’s bar. So far, I haven’t seen any hair tangled around the cones, so it seems to be working as intended. There’s also a built-in illuminating light to spot dust and debris when you’re vacuuming hard floors, which is a nice bonus.
V10 配备了全新的 All Floor Cones 地刷，与新款 V16 Piston Animal 附带的地刷类似（尽管 V16 版本具备污垢感应功能）。All Floor Cones 地刷两侧设有锥形结构，用于吸入毛发和碎屑；这种设计旨在防止长发缠绕在刷条上。到目前为止，我还没有发现任何毛发缠绕在锥形结构上，看来效果确实如预期。此外，它还内置了照明灯，方便在清理硬质地板时发现灰尘和碎屑，这是一个非常实用的加分项。

This vacuum comes with two attachments: a crevice tool and a mini motorized tool that’s designed for cleaning upholstery. Plus, unlike many older Dyson models, including the more expensive V15 or the cheaper (but equally new) V8, you’ll also get fully sealed, five-stage HEPA filters to keep allergens from circulating out of the vacuum and back into the air.
这款吸尘器附带两个配件：缝隙吸头和专为清洁软装家具设计的迷你电动吸头。此外，与许多老款戴森机型（包括更贵的 V15 或更便宜但同样是新款的 V8）不同，它还配备了全密封的五重 HEPA 过滤系统，防止过敏原从吸尘器中排出并重新进入空气中。

It’ll also be compatible with Dyson’s new docking station, the Auto-empty Dok, hopefully out later this year. Only the V10 Konical and the V16 Piston Animal ($980) are currently slated to work with it.
它还将兼容戴森即将推出的新款自动清空底座（Auto-empty Dok），预计将于今年晚些时候发布。目前仅有 V10 Konical 和 V16 Piston Animal（售价 980 美元）计划支持该底座。

### Comparison Game
### 对比测试

What made me like the Dyson V10 Konical so much was how well it did in my testing rounds. I tested it with sand and cereal on rugs, followed by litter on hardwood, and then carpeted stairs and bedrooms. It did better than many Dysons in its overall score, beating the V15 Detect, V16 Piston Animal, and V8 Cyclone with its score.
让我如此喜爱 Dyson V10 Konical 的原因在于它在测试中的出色表现。我用它清理了地毯上的沙子和麦片，随后是硬质地板上的猫砂，以及铺设地毯的楼梯和卧室。在综合评分中，它的表现优于许多戴森机型，得分超过了 V15 Detect、V16 Piston Animal 和 V8 Cyclone。

My scoring criteria ran 1 through 4 on five tests—sand, cat litter, Cheerios, general vacuuming, and handheld mode—with 4 representing the best performance and 1 being the lowest. The V10 Konical walked away with a 14 out of 20, while the V15 came behind it with a 12 out of 20. The V10 came in second overall out of five Dyson vacuums, with only the Gen5Detect beating it with a score of 17. The V10 pulls ahead of the Gen5Detect if you’re looking for add-ons, though, as the V10 will be compatible with Dyson’s self-emptying docking station that will release later this year.
我的评分标准涵盖了五项测试（沙子、猫砂、麦片、日常吸尘和手持模式），每项 1 到 4 分，4 分为最佳，1 分为最差。V10 Konical 最终获得了 20 分中的 14 分，而 V15 以 12 分紧随其后。在五款戴森吸尘器中，V10 综合排名第二，仅次于获得 17 分的 Gen5Detect。不过，如果你看重扩展配件，V10 则优于 Gen5Detect，因为它将兼容戴森今年晚些时候推出的自动清空底座。

While testing, I was particularly impressed with how well it picked up sand for its lower suction power, and it did a great job sucking up an intentional mess of Cheerios I created. I was less impressed with the hard floor results, but since the V10 doesn’t work with the Fluffy Optic head like Dyson’s high-end vacuums, it was never going to be as effective as those that can switch to a head made for that surface. Still, it’s not bad—it was still able to vacuum up the litter I scattered on hard floors as a test. It just needed a couple of passes instead of a single pass to get it all.
在测试过程中，它在较低吸力下清理沙子的能力令我印象深刻，清理我故意撒下的麦片时也表现出色。虽然它在硬质地板上的表现稍逊一筹，但考虑到 V10 无法像戴森高端机型那样使用 Fluffy Optic 地刷，它的效果自然无法与那些配备专用硬质地板刷头的机型相比。不过，它的表现依然不错——它依然能清理掉我撒在硬质地板上的猫砂，只是需要多推几次，而不是一次就能清理干净。
