# FALCON-Discover: Discovering Concentrated False-Confidence Regions for Calibration

**Abstract:** Calibration is usually evaluated in aggregate, but the most dangerous failures are often local: predictions that remain highly confident despite being wrong. We study this failure mode as false-confidence concentration, the extent to which confident errors occupy compact, discoverable regions of prediction space.

**摘要：** 模型校准通常在总体层面进行评估，但最危险的失效往往发生在局部：即模型在预测错误时依然保持高度自信。我们将这种失效模式研究为“错误自信集中度”（false-confidence concentration），即自信的错误在预测空间中占据紧凑且可被发现区域的程度。

We introduce FALCON-Discover, a post-hoc, model-agnostic framework that ranks predictions using discrepancy signals from confidence, local support, neighborhood agreement, and perturbation stability.

我们引入了 FALCON-Discover，这是一个事后（post-hoc）、与模型无关的框架。它通过利用来自置信度、局部支持度、邻域一致性和扰动稳定性等方面的差异信号，对预测结果进行排序。

Across seven binary tabular datasets, four seeds, five-fold cross-fitting, and strong learners including XGBoost and CatBoost, we find that false-confidence concentration is recurrent but regime-dependent.

通过在七个二分类表格数据集、四个随机种子、五折交叉拟合以及包括 XGBoost 和 CatBoost 在内的强学习器上进行测试，我们发现“错误自信集中”现象是普遍存在的，但具有明显的区域依赖性。

At the main confidence threshold, discrepancy-based ranking substantially outperforms the strongest validation-selected calibration or trust-scoring baseline in the strongest regimes, while raw confidence recovers little dangerous-error mass.

在主要的置信度阈值下，基于差异的排序方法在最强区域的表现显著优于目前最强的验证集校准或信任评分基准，而原始置信度指标几乎无法捕捉到这些危险的错误。

The best detector varies across datasets: learned discrepancy is strongest when multiple cues must be combined, whereas stability-centered ranking works best when local decisional fragility dominates.

最佳检测器因数据集而异：当需要结合多种线索时，学习到的差异性指标表现最强；而当局部决策脆弱性占主导地位时，以稳定性为中心的排序方法效果最好。

These results show that dangerous overconfidence is better treated as a family-level discovery problem than as a single-score calibration problem, and motivate calibration strategies that explicitly target regions where confidence, support, and stability diverge.

这些结果表明，将危险的过度自信视为一个“族群级发现问题”（family-level discovery problem），比将其视为单一分数的校准问题更为有效。这也促使我们开发出能够明确针对置信度、支持度和稳定性出现分歧区域的校准策略。
