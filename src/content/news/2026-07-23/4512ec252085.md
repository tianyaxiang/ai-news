# Price-hiked iPads are a little cheaper right now
# 涨价后的 iPad 目前稍微便宜了一些

A number of Apple products got more expensive last month, so we’re happy to find deals wherever and whenever we can. If you’re searching for a high-end iPad, one of the more notable deals currently happening is on the 13-inch iPad Pro M5 with 512GB of storage. Normally $1,699, it’s $150 off at Amazon, costing $1,549.99. It’s $100 off at Best Buy. This is the most premium iPad that Apple sells, with impressive performance from its M5 processor, a gorgeous OLED screen, up to 60W of fast charging, and support for the Apple Pencil Pro and Magic Keyboard.

上个月许多苹果产品价格上涨，因此我们很高兴能随时随地找到优惠。如果您正在寻找一款高端 iPad，目前最值得关注的优惠之一是 13 英寸、512GB 存储容量的 M5 iPad Pro。其原价为 1,699 美元，目前在亚马逊上优惠 150 美元，售价为 1,549.99 美元；在百思买（Best Buy）则优惠 100 美元。这是苹果目前在售的最顶级 iPad，拥有 M5 处理器带来的强劲性能、绚丽的 OLED 屏幕、最高 60W 的快充，并支持 Apple Pencil Pro 和妙控键盘。

There’s also a deep discount on some configurations of the 11-inch iPad Air M3. This model sports 1TB of storage, an optional 5G cellular connection, and it’s marked down to $749, a $500 discount from the suggested retail price. While the M3 chip is on the older side, you get 1TB of storage for just $40 more than the base M4 version with 128GB. The discount is available in all four colors: blue, purple, space gray, and starlight.

11 英寸 M3 iPad Air 的部分配置也有大幅折扣。该型号配备 1TB 存储空间，可选 5G 蜂窝网络连接，价格已降至 749 美元，较建议零售价优惠了 500 美元。虽然 M3 芯片相对较旧，但您只需比 128GB 的基础版 M4 机型多花 40 美元，就能获得 1TB 的存储空间。此次折扣适用于所有四种颜色：蓝色、紫色、深空灰色和星光色。

Sony is selling refurbished Playstation 5 Slim consoles for $499, a $100 break from the usual price. They may have some minor cosmetic imperfections, but are tested and certified by Sony to perform just as well as a new console. These slightly older digital-only models have the full 1TB of storage, as opposed to the newer 825GB models, and have a one-year warranty in case there are any issues.

索尼目前正在以 499 美元的价格出售翻新的 PlayStation 5 Slim 主机，比常规价格便宜了 100 美元。这些主机可能存在一些轻微的外观瑕疵，但均经过索尼测试和认证，性能与全新主机无异。这些稍旧的数字版机型拥有完整的 1TB 存储空间（区别于较新的 825GB 机型），并提供一年保修，以防出现任何问题。

Normally $99.99, a four-pack of the second-generation Apple AirTags are on sale for $89.99 at Amazon, matching their lowest price ever. The updated version of Apple’s popular Bluetooth tracker boosts the speaker volume, making it easier to find your stuff, and has more precise tracking. They’re still easy to setup, with one-touch pairing on iOS devices, and will run for over a year on a single replaceable battery.

第二代苹果 AirTags 四件装在亚马逊上的售价从通常的 99.99 美元降至 89.99 美元，追平了历史最低价。这款苹果热门蓝牙追踪器的升级版提升了扬声器音量，让寻找物品变得更容易，且追踪精度更高。它们依然易于设置，支持 iOS 设备一键配对，单节可更换电池的使用寿命可超过一年。

The symmetrical mouse features an 8K polling rate and 35K DPI sensor, specs that will let you customize it to your play style, and eight programmable buttons to put every action right where you need it. At just 54 grams, the Viper V3 Pro is impressively lightweight, yet it still manages to last 95 hours of battery life on a single charge.
