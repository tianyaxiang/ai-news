# Beyond Output-Space Calibration: Spectral Evidence Bundling for Selective Reliability Estimation in Time-Series Classification

**超越输出空间校准：用于时间序列分类中选择性可靠性估计的频谱证据捆绑**

---

Post-hoc calibration for time-series classification usually remaps output scores, but deployment decisions such as trust, abstention, and review depend on whether a confident prediction is supported by the current temporal signal.

针对时间序列分类的后验校准通常会对输出分数进行重映射，但诸如信任、弃权和审查等部署决策，取决于当前的预测置信度是否得到了时间信号的支持。

We address three time-series reliability gaps: identical confidence values can hide different temporal support, average calibration can miss false high-confidence errors, and output-space recalibration offers limited input-linked auditability.

我们解决了时间序列可靠性方面的三个缺口：相同的置信度值可能掩盖了不同的时间支持度；平均校准可能会漏掉错误的“高置信度”预测；以及输出空间的重校准在与输入相关的可审计性方面能力有限。

We introduce a validation-gated fixed-label reliability policy that keeps the backbone prediction unchanged while estimating whether it should be trusted. The method combines output-side cues with whole-sample spectral descriptors, including band energy, entropy, peak dominance, period support, and phase stability, to form a scalar reliability estimate and diagnostic band-level evidence.

我们引入了一种基于验证门控（validation-gated）的固定标签可靠性策略，该策略在保持主干模型预测不变的同时，评估其是否值得信任。该方法将输出端的线索与全样本频谱描述符（包括频带能量、熵、峰值优势、周期支持和相位稳定性）相结合，从而形成标量可靠性估计和诊断性的频带级证据。

A validation gate enables spectral conditioning only when correctness ranking improves without breaching FalseConf@0.9 or AURC tolerances; otherwise it reverts to the safer output-space baseline.

验证门控机制仅在正确性排序有所提升且不违反 FalseConf@0.9 或 AURC 容差的情况下，才会启用频谱调节；否则，它将回退到更安全的输出空间基准。

Across eight heterogeneous UCR/UEA datasets, eight time-series backbone families, and standard recalibrators, the unconstrained method improves fixed-label selective-reliability metrics on the matched evaluation subset, raising Corr-AURC from 0.693 to 0.779. The validation-gated policy further improves Corr-AURC to 0.786 and reduces FalseConf@0.9 to 0.094.

在八个异构的 UCR/UEA 数据集、八个时间序列主干模型系列以及标准重校准器上，该无约束方法在匹配的评估子集上提升了固定标签的选择性可靠性指标，将 Corr-AURC 从 0.693 提高到了 0.779。而验证门控策略进一步将 Corr-AURC 提升至 0.786，并将 FalseConf@0.9 降低至 0.094。

These results suggest that reliability estimation for time-series classifiers benefits from bundling output confidence with spectral evidence, while validation gating prevents unsupported spectral conditioning.

这些结果表明，时间序列分类器的可靠性估计通过将输出置信度与频谱证据捆绑而获益，同时验证门控机制有效防止了缺乏支持的频谱调节。
