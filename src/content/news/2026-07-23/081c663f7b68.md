# Philips’ new smart toothbrush shows you where you didn’t properly brush
# 飞利浦新款智能牙刷：精准识别刷牙盲区

The latest addition to Philips’ Sonicare line of smart electric toothbrushes could take the guesswork out of your brushing routine. The Next-Generation DiamondClean 9900 Prestige uses a third-generation AI model to provide real-time feedback on how you’re brushing through a glowing ring on its base, while a touchscreen highlights areas of your mouth that may need more attention when you think you’re done.

飞利浦 Sonicare 系列智能电动牙刷迎来最新成员，它能让你的刷牙过程不再“盲目”。这款新一代 DiamondClean 9900 Prestige 采用了第三代人工智能模型，通过底座上的发光环提供实时刷牙反馈；当你以为刷完时，触摸屏还会高亮显示口腔中可能需要额外清洁的区域。

The Sonicare Next-Generation DiamondClean 9900 Prestige is expected to launch in Europe and the US this fall, with a wider global rollout coming in 2027. Pricing hasn’t been announced, but previous versions sell for $430, and you can expect this new one to be even more expensive.

Sonicare 新一代 DiamondClean 9900 Prestige 预计将于今年秋季在欧洲和美国上市，并于 2027 年在全球范围内推广。目前官方尚未公布价格，但考虑到前代产品售价为 430 美元，预计这款新品的价格将会更高。

The toothbrush’s touchscreen lets you swipe through eight cleaning modes including gum health, max clean, and sensitive options. To help ensure you’re not pressing too hard while you brush, causing damage, the glowing ring on the base of the brush changes color to indicate when you should be a little gentler. But the 9900 Prestige also uses pressure sensors to adjust its intensity when you’re pressing too hard so the bristles go easier on your gums.

这款牙刷的触摸屏支持滑动切换八种清洁模式，包括牙龈健康、深度清洁和敏感模式等。为了防止刷牙力度过大造成损伤，底座上的发光环会通过变色来提醒你减轻力度。此外，9900 Prestige 还配备了压力传感器，当你用力过猛时，它会自动调节震动强度，从而减少刷毛对牙龈的刺激。

The touchscreen also displays what Philips calls a mouth map representing your teeth and gums in 12 different segments that are highlighted in blue or orange indicating how effective your brushing was. The 9900 Prestige tracks its movements in your mouth while you brush and areas it deems in need of a little more attention glow orange so you know exactly what areas you need to target during a second pass.

触摸屏还会显示飞利浦所谓的“口腔地图”，将牙齿和牙龈分为 12 个区域，并以蓝色或橙色高亮显示，以反映你的刷牙效果。9900 Prestige 会在你刷牙时追踪其在口腔内的运动轨迹，被系统判定为需要额外清洁的区域会显示为橙色，让你清楚地知道在补刷时应该重点照顾哪些部位。
