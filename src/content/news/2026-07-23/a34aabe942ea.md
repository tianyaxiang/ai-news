# Making

**Beej's Bit Bucket ⚡ Tech and Programming Fun 2026-03-12 On Making**
Beej's Bit Bucket ⚡ 科技与编程趣谈 2026-03-12 关于“创造”

**I made this! TLDR: I gain a lot of fulfillment by making things. I don't consider things built by others at my request to be made by me, and are therefore much less fulfilling. And then I feel sad. This article starts strong and then heads off into the weeds.**
我做到了！简而言之：我从创造事物中获得极大的满足感。我不认为别人应我要求而构建的东西是我自己做的，因此它们带来的满足感要少得多。这让我感到难过。这篇文章开头很有力，但随后便有些偏离主题了。

**There have been a lot of pieces written about what I'll call "the AI dev schism" And I think there's a lot of truth to those:**
关于我所称的“AI 开发分裂”现象，已经有很多文章进行了探讨，我认为其中不乏真知灼见：

*   **Loss of the craft, coding things by hand** / 技艺的丧失，手动编写代码
*   **Loss of low-level problem-solving** / 底层问题解决能力的丧失
*   **Loss of fun** / 乐趣的丧失
*   **Gain of high-level problem-solving** / 高层问题解决能力的提升
*   **Getting through back-burnered projects** / 完成积压已久的项目
*   **Gain of fun** / 乐趣的增加

**We'll just grant those as being correct for various developers. But there's something else that troubles me.**
我们姑且承认这些观点对不同的开发者来说都是正确的。但还有另一件事困扰着我。

**Backstory before we get going, so you can get a better idea of my perspective: I'm a Gen-X hacker; I cut my teeth 80s microcomputer era. I hold a BS and MS in CS. I have 20 years industry experience, (Hewlett-Packard, startups, cofounder, Activision, etc.). CS instructor for the last 9 years, now at Oregon State University-Cascades. I'm 65% Doom on the AI-Utopia/Doom scale. I'm a Claude Code user sometimes. I code by hand sometimes. My father taught philosophy at a community college for 35 years. This might help explain the latter part of this blog entry.**
在开始之前先交代一下背景，以便你更好地理解我的视角：我是 X 世代的黑客；我在 80 年代的微机时代初露锋芒。我拥有计算机科学学士和硕士学位。我有 20 年的行业经验（惠普、初创公司、联合创始人、动视等）。过去 9 年担任计算机科学讲师，目前在俄勒冈州立大学卡斯卡德分校任教。在“AI 乌托邦/末日”量表上，我倾向于 65% 的末日论。我有时会使用 Claude Code，有时也会手动写代码。我父亲在社区大学教了 35 年哲学。这或许能解释这篇博文的后半部分。

**I Am Uncomfortable. I don't like lying. And yet I feel, dear reader, I have misled you. Yes, all that has been created (including my deck) and I was the initiator of all that creation. But I don't really feel like I made any of it. I'm uncomfortable claiming that I did so.**

**Since you are certainly aware by now that all of the above is AI-generated (except my deck, which was created by skilled, paid craftsmen), perhaps you feel a little bit of discomfort with me claiming credit for doing those things, too.**
既然你现在肯定已经意识到上述所有内容都是 AI 生成的（除了我的露台，那是由技术娴熟的付费工匠建造的），也许你对我声称自己完成了这些工作也感到一丝不安。

**However, I don't think everyone feels this way. I know many people who ask contractors to build things and they phrase it like they built it. "I put in a new front deck," they'd say, even though other people did all the work. Personally, I feel that's misleading. I'm more of a "I had a new front deck put in" kind of person. And when I do have Claude create something for me, I just can't say that I made it. Other people can, but I just can't. Again, I'm more prone to say, "I had this code built for me."**
然而，我不认为每个人都有这种感觉。我认识许多人，他们请承包商建造东西，却说得好像是自己建的一样。他们会说“我装了一个新前露台”，尽管实际上是别人完成了所有工作。就我个人而言，我觉得这具有误导性。我更倾向于说“我让人装了一个新前露台”。当我让 Claude 为我创作某些东西时，我无法说那是我做的。别人或许可以，但我做不到。同样，我更倾向于说“我让人为我写了这段代码”。
