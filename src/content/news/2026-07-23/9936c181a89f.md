# Compressing What Matters: Neuron Importance Meets Data-Aware Low Rank Approximation for Language Model Compression
# 压缩关键信息：神经元重要性与数据感知低秩近似在语言模型压缩中的结合

**Abstract:** To excel at their domain large language models are comprised of billions of parameters. Yet this comes at the cost of huge memory requirements restricting their applicability in resource-constrained environments. 

**摘要：** 为了在各自领域表现出色，大型语言模型通常包含数十亿个参数。然而，这带来了巨大的内存需求，限制了它们在资源受限环境中的应用。

To address the problem of neural network (NN) compression Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) has played a key role as a fundamental component for matrix compression through decomposition. To minimize compression error and to maximize the efficacy of the compressed model on the downstream tasks previous works focused on low-rank approximation of the NN's weight matrices either from the perspective of parameter importance or per-layer functional equivalence. 

为了解决神经网络（NN）压缩问题，奇异值分解（SVD）作为通过分解进行矩阵压缩的基础组件，发挥了关键作用。为了最小化压缩误差并最大化压缩模型在下游任务中的效能，以往的研究主要集中在神经网络权重矩阵的低秩近似上，通常从参数重要性或层级功能等效性的角度出发。

While previous works studied the aforementioned perspectives in isolation in this work we are investigating the effectiveness of an approach that combines ideas from these two perspectives in a single objective. 

虽然以往的研究是孤立地探讨上述视角，但在本研究中，我们探讨了一种将这两个视角的思想结合在单一目标函数中的方法的有效性。

In parallel to this an important aspect that affects the compression quality is the distribution of the compression rate across layers and NN parameters. Earlier works mostly considered distributing the compression rate uniformly across layers and network weights or relied on computationally expensive heuristic search. 

与此同时，影响压缩质量的一个重要方面是压缩率在各层和神经网络参数间的分配。早期的研究大多考虑在各层和网络权重之间均匀分配压缩率，或者依赖于计算成本高昂的启发式搜索。

Contrary to them in this work we propose an enhanced and computationally efficient algorithm for dynamic compression rate allocation. Experimental results support the efficacy of the proposed approach which performs on par or substantially better than the previous state-of-the-art especially under high compression ratios.

与这些方法相反，我们在本研究中提出了一种增强且计算高效的动态压缩率分配算法。实验结果支持了所提方法的有效性，该方法在性能上与现有的最先进技术相当，甚至在某些情况下显著优于后者，特别是在高压缩比的情况下。
