# 3 Google updates from Galaxy Unpacked 2026
# 2026年 Galaxy Unpacked 发布会上的 3 项谷歌更新

At Galaxy Unpacked, we shared how Samsung users can boost productivity and get time back on new foldables, watches, and glasses coming soon.
在 Galaxy Unpacked 发布会上，我们分享了三星用户如何通过即将推出的新款折叠屏手机、手表和眼镜来提高生产力并节省时间。

Google launched the new Samsung Galaxy Z Fold8 and Flip8 series featuring Gemini Intelligence to automate daily tasks across 40 popular apps. You can now use Gemini Notebook on these devices to synthesize complex research and create custom resources like slide decks or podcasts. To get started, take advantage of the included six-month trial of Google AI Pro and explore new Gemini integrations for your Galaxy Watch and upcoming intelligent eyewear.
谷歌发布了全新的三星 Galaxy Z Fold8 和 Flip8 系列，搭载 Gemini Intelligence，可自动处理 40 多款热门应用中的日常任务。现在，你可以在这些设备上使用 Gemini Notebook 来整合复杂的研究资料，并创建幻灯片或播客等定制资源。若要开始体验，请利用随附的 6 个月 Google AI Pro 试用期，并探索 Gemini 与 Galaxy Watch 及即将推出的智能眼镜之间的新集成功能。

Today at Galaxy Unpacked in London, we celebrated the launch of the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra, Fold8, and Flip8. With these devices, we are making it easier than ever to get started on Android while also rolling out the first capabilities of Gemini Intelligence — our vision for technology that works proactively for you. Whether you choose to unfold your phone to maximize your productivity on a bigger screen or keep it folded so you can focus on your day, Gemini Intelligence is there to help with the heavy lifting.
今天在伦敦举行的 Galaxy Unpacked 发布会上，我们庆祝了三星 Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra、Fold8 和 Flip8 的发布。通过这些设备，我们让用户比以往任何时候都更容易上手 Android 系统，同时也推出了 Gemini Intelligence 的首批功能——这是我们对“主动为你服务”的技术愿景。无论你是选择展开手机以在大屏幕上最大化生产力，还是保持折叠状态以专注于日常事务，Gemini Intelligence 都能为你分担繁重的工作。

**1. Get next-generation task automation with Gemini Intelligence.**
**1. 通过 Gemini Intelligence 实现下一代任务自动化。**

Gemini Intelligence can help you offload digital chores so you can spend less time behind a screen. Back in February, we introduced a beta for task automation, helping you delegate simple everyday tasks to Gemini, such as ordering a rideshare or getting your favorite takeout meal for delivery. Now task automation is expanding support from a handful of apps to more than 40 popular apps. This means Gemini can now help you handle more of your daily "life admin" — from shopping and making dinner reservations to booking travel experiences and buying event tickets.
Gemini Intelligence 可以帮你分担数字琐事，让你减少盯着屏幕的时间。早在二月份，我们就推出了任务自动化的测试版，帮助你将简单的日常任务委托给 Gemini，例如叫车或订购你最喜欢的外卖。现在，任务自动化的支持范围已从少数几个应用扩展到 40 多个热门应用。这意味着 Gemini 现在可以帮你处理更多的日常“生活行政”事务——从购物、预订晚餐到预订旅行体验和购买活动门票。

**2. Raise the bar on your projects with Gemini Notebook.**
**2. 用 Gemini Notebook 提升你的项目水平。**

To help you manage deep projects and synthesize complex information, we are bringing the power of Gemini Notebook (formerly NotebookLM) directly to your device. The app comes preinstalled on Samsung's new foldables. Gemini Notebook is your thinking and research partner powered by our advanced multimodal models. On the Galaxy Z Fold8 series' expansive display, you can drag and drop photos, documents, whiteboard images, voice recordings, and more directly into a side-by-side workspace.
为了帮助你管理深度项目并整合复杂信息，我们将 Gemini Notebook（前身为 NotebookLM）的功能直接带到了你的设备上。该应用已预装在三星新款折叠屏手机中。Gemini Notebook 是由我们先进的多模态模型驱动的思维和研究伙伴。在 Galaxy Z Fold8 系列的宽大屏幕上，你可以将照片、文档、白板图像、录音等内容直接拖放到并排的工作区中。

**3. Take Gemini on the go with watches and glasses.**
**3. 通过手表和眼镜随时随地使用 Gemini。**

Our collaboration with Samsung extends beyond smartphones. On the Galaxy Watch 9, you have more convenient access to Gemini on the go. Simply raise your wrist and Gemini will activate so you can ask it questions, no wake word required. We’re also bringing Gemini to glasses. At Google I/O 2026, we announced intelligent eyewear — created in partnership with Samsung and launching this fall — and shared two designs from our partners, eyewear brands Gentle Monster and Warby Parker.
我们与三星的合作不仅限于智能手机。在 Galaxy Watch 9 上，你可以更方便地随时随地访问 Gemini。只需抬起手腕，Gemini 就会激活，无需唤醒词即可提问。我们还将 Gemini 带到了眼镜上。在 2026 年的 Google I/O 大会上，我们宣布了与三星合作开发的智能眼镜，并将于今年秋季推出，同时分享了合作伙伴 Gentle Monster 和 Warby Parker 的两款设计。
