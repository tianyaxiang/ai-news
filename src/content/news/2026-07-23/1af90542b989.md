# Preorders for Samsung’s new Z Fold and Flip 8 come with up to $350 in gift cards
# 三星新款 Z Fold 和 Flip 8 开启预购，最高可获 350 美元礼品卡

Samsung’s newest foldables are here. At Galaxy Unpacked, the company announced the Galaxy Z Flip 8, Galaxy Z Fold 8, and Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra. All three devices will be available on August 7th, but you can preorder them now starting at $1,199.99, $1,899.99, and $2,099.99, respectively.
三星最新的折叠屏手机正式亮相。在 Galaxy Unpacked 发布会上，该公司发布了 Galaxy Z Flip 8、Galaxy Z Fold 8 和 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra。这三款设备将于 8 月 7 日正式上市，但目前已开启预购，起售价分别为 1,199.99 美元、1,899.99 美元和 2,099.99 美元。

You probably noticed that Samsung’s foldable lineup looks a little different this year. Rather than simply updating the Galaxy Z Fold 7, Samsung now has two book-style foldables: the productivity-focused Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra and the shorter, wider (when folded) Galaxy Z Fold 8 that’s geared more toward entertainment. The Galaxy Z Flip 8, meanwhile, is a touch thinner and lighter than its predecessor, but otherwise retains the same design as the Flip 7.
你可能已经注意到，三星今年的折叠屏产品线有所不同。三星没有仅仅对 Galaxy Z Fold 7 进行常规升级，而是推出了两款书本式折叠屏手机：主打生产力的 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra，以及折叠后更短、更宽，更偏向娱乐体验的 Galaxy Z Fold 8。与此同时，Galaxy Z Flip 8 比前代产品更轻薄，但在设计上基本延续了 Flip 7 的风格。

All three phones also feature Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy processor and ship with Android 17 and One UI 9, which add new AI features like Gemini Intelligence task automation and Now Nudge, which proactively surfaces helpful suggestions like an event to your calendar.
这三款手机均搭载了高通骁龙 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy 处理器，并预装 Android 17 和 One UI 9 系统。新系统增加了多项 AI 功能，例如 Gemini Intelligence 任务自动化，以及能够主动提供日历事件等实用建议的“Now Nudge”功能。

### Where to preorder the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra
### 如何预购三星 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra

Samsung’s Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra starts at $2,099.99 when you buy the base model with 12GB of RAM and 256GB of storage. If you want 512GB of storage, it costs $2,299.99, while 1TB of storage will set you back $2,699.99. The phone is available in violet shadow, graphite, cream, and shadow green, with the latter exclusive to Samsung’s online store.
三星 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra 的基础款（12GB 内存 + 256GB 存储）起售价为 2,099.99 美元。512GB 版本售价为 2,299.99 美元，1TB 版本则为 2,699.99 美元。该机提供紫影、石墨、奶油和影绿四种配色，其中影绿为三星官网独占。

In terms of deals, Amazon is including a $350 gift card with preorders of the Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra, while Best Buy is offering a $200 gift card. Samsung, meanwhile, is throwing in $200 in Samsung credit, plus the $30 reservation credit for those who reserved, and you can also save $100 with code PAYPAL100. The company’s additionally offering up to $100 off on select Samsung products.
在优惠方面，亚马逊为预购 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra 的用户提供 350 美元的礼品卡，百思买（Best Buy）则提供 200 美元礼品卡。三星官网则提供 200 美元的三星商城积分，加上此前预约用户的 30 美元预约金，使用代码 PAYPAL100 还可再减 100 美元。此外，三星还针对部分精选产品提供最高 100 美元的折扣。
