# Microsoft is bringing original Xbox games to PC
# 微软将初代 Xbox 游戏引入 PC 平台

Microsoft is expanding its Xbox backward compatibility efforts today by bringing original Xbox games to PC. An early preview release will see four classic Xbox games available on PC today, as part of a bigger effort to bring more Xbox console games to PC in the future.

微软今日扩大了其 Xbox 向下兼容计划，正式将初代 Xbox 游戏引入 PC 平台。作为未来将更多 Xbox 主机游戏带到 PC 这一宏大计划的一部分，首批四款经典 Xbox 游戏已于今日在 PC 上开启抢先体验。

The first four games are Blinx: The Time Sweeper, Conker: Live and Reloaded, Crimson Skies: High Road to Revenge, and Fuzion Frenzy. You’ll be able to purchase and download each of these games on PC, and they’ll also be included with all Xbox Game Pass plans. If you already own a digital copy of these games, you can now play them on PC or handhelds like the Xbox Ally and Xbox Ally X.

首批四款游戏分别是《Blinx: The Time Sweeper》、《Conker: Live and Reloaded》、《Crimson Skies: High Road to Revenge》以及《Fuzion Frenzy》。你现在可以在 PC 上购买并下载这些游戏，它们也将包含在所有 Xbox Game Pass 订阅计划中。如果你已经拥有这些游戏的数字版，现在就可以在 PC 或 Xbox Ally 和 Xbox Ally X 等掌机上游玩。

Microsoft has also added a bunch of PC features to these games to make it easier to customize graphics settings while maintaining the original gameplay. VSync support has been added, alongside up to 4x resolution upscaling, anisotropic filtering, enhanced anti-aliasing, fullscreen and windowed modes, and more.

微软还为这些游戏增加了一系列 PC 特性，在保留原始游戏体验的同时，让玩家能更轻松地自定义图形设置。新增功能包括垂直同步（VSync）支持、最高 4 倍分辨率缩放、各向异性过滤、增强型抗锯齿、全屏与窗口模式切换等。

Achievements are also coming to these four classic Xbox games in the coming months, a highly requested addition for both console and PC. All four titles will also include customizable language and audio settings.

未来几个月内，这四款经典 Xbox 游戏还将加入成就系统，这是主机和 PC 玩家呼声最高的功能之一。此外，这四款游戏都将支持自定义语言和音频设置。

Most modern PCs will be able to run these Xbox games comfortably, and Microsoft’s minimum system requirements include:
GPU: Nvidia GTX 950 or AMD Radeon RX 550 or Intel UHD 770 or Intel Arc A310
CPU: Intel Core i3-8100 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200 or AMD Ryzen Z2 A Processor
RAM: 8GB

大多数现代 PC 都能流畅运行这些 Xbox 游戏，微软公布的最低系统要求如下：
显卡：Nvidia GTX 950 或 AMD Radeon RX 550 或 Intel UHD 770 或 Intel Arc A310
处理器：Intel Core i3-8100 或 AMD Ryzen 3 1200 或 AMD Ryzen Z2 A 处理器
内存：8GB

You will also need Windows 11 to run these original Xbox games, and Microsoft recommends a GTX 1070 Ti or equivalent on the GPU side and a six-core CPU like the Intel Core i5-10400 or AMD Ryzen 5 3600 to get the best out of these games.

运行这些初代 Xbox 游戏需要 Windows 11 系统。为了获得最佳游戏体验，微软建议显卡配置达到 GTX 1070 Ti 或同等水平，处理器建议使用 Intel Core i5-10400 或 AMD Ryzen 5 3600 等六核 CPU。

“This marks the beginning of a broader effort to preserve Xbox games from the past and bring them to PC over time,” says Jason Ronald, VP of next generation at Xbox. “Xbox Backward Compatibility on PC is another step toward preserving the games from the past and making it easier for you to enjoy and play more of the games you already own across the devices you use every day.”

“这标志着我们保护过往 Xbox 游戏并逐步将其引入 PC 这一宏大计划的开端，”Xbox 下一代平台副总裁 Jason Ronald 表示，“PC 端的 Xbox 向下兼容功能是我们保护经典游戏的又一步，旨在让你更轻松地在日常使用的设备上享受并游玩你已拥有的更多游戏。”

Ronald first teased that classic Xbox games were coming to PC earlier this year, in a discussion about the next-generation Project Helix Xbox console. Microsoft’s next Xbox will also play PC games, and the company has been working on a disc-to-digital feature that will allow Xbox owners to digitize their existing physical game collections in preparation for future Xbox consoles.

Ronald 今年早些时候在讨论下一代 Project Helix Xbox 主机时，首次透露了经典 Xbox 游戏即将登陆 PC 的消息。微软的下一代 Xbox 主机也将支持运行 PC 游戏，该公司目前正在开发一项“光盘转数字版”功能，允许 Xbox 用户将现有的实体游戏收藏数字化，为未来的 Xbox 主机做好准备。
