### Slash Pages

Slash pages are common pages you can add to your website, usually with a standard, root-level slug like /now, /about, or /uses.
“Slash 页面”是指你可以添加到网站上的常见页面，通常带有像 `/now`、`/about` 或 `/uses` 这样标准的根目录路径。

They tend to describe the individual behind the site and are distinguishing characteristics of the IndieWeb.
它们往往用于描述网站背后的个人，是“独立网络”（IndieWeb）的显著特征。

The name was coined independently by both Caleb Hearth and Shellsharks. This page was made by Robb Knight.
这个名称由 Caleb Hearth 和 Shellsharks 分别独立提出。本页面由 Robb Knight 创建。
