### Passkeys were invented by engineers with zero understanding of consumer brain
### 密钥（Passkeys）是由完全不懂消费者心理的工程师发明的

Nikita Bier@nikitabierReplying to @iamjasonlevin
Nikita Bier@nikitabier 回复 @iamjasonlevin

Passkeys were invented by security engineers with zero understanding of the consumer brain. We had no way to argue its merits since no one understood what they are. 
密钥（Passkeys）是由那些完全不懂消费者心理的安全工程师发明的。我们根本无法去争论它的优劣，因为压根没人搞得懂这到底是什么。

As a result, we are now all asked to produce the magic fairy dust to login to apps. It may be on your phone, in your browser, in your operating system, in your body, who knows. But now you’re safe.
结果就是，现在我们所有人都被要求去寻找那种“魔法仙尘”来登录应用。它可能在你的手机里、浏览器里、操作系统里，甚至在你的身体里，谁知道呢？但反正现在你“安全”了。
