### The Similarities / 共同点

Let’s take a quick peek at what the eighth-generation Samsung Galaxy Z foldables have in common this year.

让我们快速浏览一下今年第八代三星 Galaxy Z 系列折叠屏手机的共同之处。

#### The Display / 屏幕

Although Samsung’s foldables all feature exterior and foldout screens in completely different sizes, they all boast Samsung’s Dynamic AMOLED 2X technology, so you get a 120-Hz refresh rate, support for HDR, and higher peak brightness than ever before (up to 3,000 nits), making it easy to read your screen, even in direct sunlight. They also have features designed to filter blue light and reduce eye strain.

尽管三星折叠屏手机的外屏和内屏尺寸各不相同，但它们都采用了三星的 Dynamic AMOLED 2X 技术，因此都具备 120Hz 刷新率、HDR 支持以及比以往更高的峰值亮度（最高可达 3,000 尼特），即使在阳光直射下也能轻松阅读屏幕。它们还具备过滤蓝光和减轻视疲劳的功能。

The Fold8 Ultra and Fold8 have a special Flex Titanium layer that reduces the visible crease and provides extra strength, flexibility, and resilience. The titanium-alloy film should help the displays last longer, but also means they are flatter and smoother than before, with an anti-reflective finish that reduces glare.

Fold8 Ultra 和 Fold8 拥有一层特殊的 Flex Titanium（柔性钛）涂层，可减少可见折痕，并提供额外的强度、柔韧性和耐用性。这种钛合金薄膜不仅有助于延长屏幕寿命，还使屏幕比以往更平整、更顺滑，并配有防反射涂层以减少眩光。

#### The Usual Specs / 常规规格

All three phones have Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor inside, with 12 GB of RAM and 256 GB or 512 GB of storage. You can also get the Fold8 Ultra and Fold8 with 16 GB of RAM and 1 TB of storage. They are all IP48 water- and dust-resistant and feature Samsung’s Advanced Armor Aluminum frame, which is scratch-resistant and durable enough to survive minor drops.

这三款手机均搭载高通骁龙 8 Elite Gen 5 处理器，配备 12GB 运行内存以及 256GB 或 512GB 存储空间。Fold8 Ultra 和 Fold8 还提供 16GB 内存和 1TB 存储版本。它们均支持 IP48 级防水防尘，并采用三星的“高级装甲铝”边框，具备抗刮擦能力，足以应对轻微跌落。

The Fold8 Ultra and Fold8 support fast charging at up to 45 watts and fast wireless charging at 20 watts; the Flip8 is slightly slower for both. All three support Wireless PowerShare to top up wireless earbuds and other devices. Connectivity includes Wi-Fi 7 and Ultra Wideband (UWB).

Fold8 Ultra 和 Fold8 支持最高 45W 有线快充和 20W 无线快充；Flip8 在这两方面的速度稍慢。三款手机均支持无线反向充电（Wireless PowerShare），可为无线耳机及其他设备充电。连接功能包括 Wi-Fi 7 和超宽带（UWB）。

### Samsung Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra

The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra is the biggest and best foldable that Samsung offers. If you crave the largest screen available but want to fold it away and have something that feels close to a regular smartphone when closed, this is it. The 6.5-inch cover display and slimmed-down design are slick but won’t turn heads. Open it up and you get an expansive 8-inch screen that feels like two 6.5-inch displays side by side.

三星 Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra 是三星目前提供的大尺寸、高性能折叠屏手机。如果你渴望拥有最大的屏幕，同时又希望在折叠后能获得接近普通智能手机的手感，那么它就是你的不二之选。6.5 英寸的外屏和纤薄的设计非常精致，但不会显得过于张扬。展开后，你将获得一块宽阔的 8 英寸屏幕，感觉就像两块 6.5 英寸屏幕并排在一起。

Everything is smoother and stronger, with Samsung's usual raft of annual refinements. Both displays boast Samsung’s latest tech and a silky smooth adaptive refresh rate that also saves power. Speaking of which, the battery is significantly bigger than the Fold7 at 5,000 mAh, and it supports faster wired and wireless charging than its predecessor. The only slight disappointment is the lack of magnetic Qi2, which is such a handy feature for easy docking and perfect wireless charging alignment.

一切都变得更流畅、更坚固，并延续了三星一贯的年度改进。两块屏幕都采用了三星的最新技术，并具备丝滑的自适应刷新率，同时也更加省电。说到电池，其容量达到了 5,000 mAh，比 Fold7 有显著提升，且支持比前代更快的有线和无线充电。唯一的小遗憾是缺少了磁吸 Qi2 标准，这本是一项方便对齐和无线充电的实用功能。

What truly makes the Fold8 Ultra “ultra” is the camera system. It’s a top-tier combo of a 200-megapixel main shooter, 50-megapixel ultrawide, and a 10-megapixel telephoto lens that supports 3X optical zoom. Both the main and ultrawide can shoot 8K video, and you’ll find plenty of AI assistance in your photo and video editing options.

让 Fold8 Ultra 真正称得上“Ultra”的是它的摄像头系统。它采用了顶级组合：2 亿像素主摄、5,000 万像素超广角镜头以及支持 3 倍光学变焦的 1,000 万像素长焦镜头。主摄和超广角镜头均可拍摄 8K 视频，你还将在照片和视频编辑选项中发现丰富的 AI 辅助功能。

Who it’s for: The flagship fan who wants the best of the best and doesn’t mind spending to get it.

适合人群：追求极致体验、且不介意为此投入预算的旗舰手机爱好者。
