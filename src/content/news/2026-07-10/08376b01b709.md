### Deep Reinforcement Learning for Reliability Based Bi-Objective Portfolio Optimization
### 基于可靠性的双目标投资组合优化深度强化学习研究

**Abstract:** Portfolio optimization under uncertainty is inherently a multi-objective decision problem involving complex interactions among return, risk, market dynamics, and practical investment constraints.
**摘要：** 在不确定性下的投资组合优化本质上是一个多目标决策问题，涉及收益、风险、市场动态和实际投资约束之间的复杂相互作用。

Existing reliability based portfolio optimization approaches primarily rely on static optimization frameworks and often fail to capture sequential decision making, tail risk, and market frictions such as transaction costs.
现有的基于可靠性的投资组合优化方法主要依赖于静态优化框架，往往无法捕捉序列决策、尾部风险以及交易成本等市场摩擦。

To address these limitations, we propose a deep reinforcement learning framework for multi-objective reliability based portfolio optimization (MORP-DRL).
为了解决这些局限性，我们提出了一种用于多目标可靠性投资组合优化的深度强化学习框架（MORP-DRL）。

The proposed framework jointly optimizes expected return and downside risk using three complementary risk measures: variance, Conditional Value-at-Risk (CVaR), and Entropic Value-at-Risk (EVaR).
该框架利用三种互补的风险度量指标——方差、条件风险价值（CVaR）和熵风险价值（EVaR）——对预期收益和下行风险进行联合优化。

To model uncertainty and heavy-tailed market behavior, asset returns are represented using GARCH(1,1), Extreme Value Theory, and a t-copula dependence structure, while realistic scenarios are generated through quasi-Monte Carlo simulation.
为了模拟不确定性和市场厚尾行为，资产收益率通过 GARCH(1,1) 模型、极值理论和 t-copula 依赖结构进行表示，同时通过准蒙特卡洛模拟生成现实场景。

A Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) based strategy is developed under practical constraints including transaction costs and portfolio bounds, and is benchmarked against NSGA-II.
我们在包含交易成本和投资组合边界等实际约束条件下，开发了一种基于近端策略优化（PPO）的策略，并将其与 NSGA-II 算法进行了基准测试对比。

Experiments on ten global equity indices across pre-COVID, COVID, and post-COVID market regimes demonstrate that MORP-DRL achieves competitive risk-return performance, reduced downside risk during periods of market stress, and scalability to high-dimensional portfolio settings.
在涵盖新冠疫情前、疫情期间及疫情后市场制度的十个全球股票指数上的实验表明，MORP-DRL 实现了具有竞争力的风险收益表现，在市场压力期间降低了下行风险，并具备在高维投资组合环境下的可扩展性。
