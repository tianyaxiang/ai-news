# Epilogue SN Operator Review: Super Nintendo Fun
# Epilogue SN Operator 评测：超级任天堂的乐趣

$60 at Epilogue | Rating: 9/10
售价 60 美元（Epilogue 官网） | 评分：9/10

**TIRED:** Multiregion cartridge support. Supports Super Scope and SNES Mouse games. Finally allows virtual save states. Exhaustive list of filters, tweaks, cheats, and emulation tools to experiment with. Bargain device price.
**优点：** 支持多区域卡带。支持 Super Scope 光枪和 SNES 鼠标游戏。终于支持虚拟存档功能。提供详尽的滤镜、调整选项、作弊码及各种模拟工具供玩家尝试。设备价格极具性价比。

**TIRED:** Full-screen view still doesn’t actually fill the screen. Can’t run translation packs (yet). Collecting the best SNES games is a pricey treasure hunt.
**缺点：** 全屏模式仍无法真正填满屏幕。暂不支持运行汉化/翻译补丁。收集顶级的 SNES 游戏是一场昂贵的寻宝之旅。

With Sony cutting disc production for PlayStation consoles by 2028, the future looks bleak for anyone who likes actually owning their video games. The past, though, well, that’s never looked brighter, with the retro gaming sector offering more opportunities than ever to enjoy the games of yesteryear.
随着索尼计划在 2028 年前停止生产 PlayStation 主机光盘，对于那些喜欢真正“拥有”游戏的人来说，未来显得有些黯淡。然而，过去却从未如此闪耀，复古游戏领域提供了比以往任何时候都多的机会，让玩家能够重温往昔的经典。

There are now heaps of field-programmable gate array (FPGA) consoles that effectively remake original consoles, supporting authentic game cartridges on modern displays—like Analogue’s excellent N64 mimic, the Analogue3D. But Romania-based Epilogue has been going a slightly different route. Its Operator line is a burgeoning series of plug-in cart slots that let you play original cartridges on your computer, running the games through emulation.
目前市面上涌现了大量现场可编程门阵列（FPGA）主机，它们有效地重现了原版主机，支持在现代显示器上使用正版游戏卡带——例如 Analogue 出色的 N64 复刻机 Analogue3D。但总部位于罗马尼亚的 Epilogue 走了一条略有不同的路线。其 Operator 系列是一系列新兴的插件式卡槽设备，让你能在电脑上游玩原版卡带，并通过模拟器运行游戏。

The delightful GB Operator was first out of the gate earlier in 2026, supporting carts from three generations of Game Boy hardware. Epilogue is now following up with the SN Operator, which does the same for Super Nintendo Entertainment System and Super Famicom carts—and does so rather brilliantly.
令人惊喜的 GB Operator 于 2026 年初率先发布，支持三代 Game Boy 硬件的卡带。现在，Epilogue 推出了后续产品 SN Operator，它为超级任天堂（SNES）和超级红白机（Super Famicom）卡带提供了同样的功能，而且表现相当出色。

Like its predecessor, the SN Operator is a simple perspex cuboid, albeit bigger, measuring 185 × 70 × 37 millimeters to accommodate the much larger SNES game cartridges. The cart slot now boasts guard flaps to protect the inner circuitry from dust and debris—much like Nintendo’s original consoles did—and the whole unit benefits from a rubber base that keeps it stationary on any desk, despite its mere 230-gram base weight.
与前代产品一样，SN Operator 是一个简单的有机玻璃长方体，只是体积更大，尺寸为 185 × 70 × 37 毫米，以容纳更大的 SNES 游戏卡带。卡槽现在配有防尘盖，可以保护内部电路免受灰尘和碎屑的侵害——就像任天堂的原版主机一样。整个设备底部配有橡胶垫，尽管重量仅为 230 克，但仍能稳固地放置在任何桌面上。

The greater size means it’s not as portable as the GB Operator (I certainly wouldn’t want to lug it and a bunch of full-size carts around to play via my laptop on the go), but the original hardware was always a home console, so it’s no real loss.
更大的体积意味着它不如 GB Operator 便携（我肯定不想带着它和一堆全尺寸卡带到处跑，只为了在笔记本电脑上玩游戏），但原版硬件本身就是家用主机，所以这并不算什么真正的损失。

Setup remains simple. Connect the device to your computer (Windows, macOS, Linux, and even Raspberry Pi are supported) via the included 1-meter braided USB-C to USB-C cord—which also provides power—install Epilogue’s Playback software, slot in your chosen game, and you’re good to go.
设置依然简单。通过附带的 1 米长编织 USB-C 转 USB-C 线缆（同时提供电源）将设备连接到电脑（支持 Windows、macOS、Linux 甚至树莓派），安装 Epilogue 的 Playback 软件，插入你选择的游戏，即可开始游玩。

The SN Operator continues to offer a host of visual options and filters for how games run, with screen modes ranging from pixel-perfect representations to CRT replicas—oddly, some imitating the grayscale appearance of the Game Boy—and modifiers to how fast or slow a game runs, including fast-forward and rewind features.
SN Operator 继续提供丰富的视觉选项和滤镜来调整游戏运行效果，屏幕模式从像素级完美呈现到 CRT 模拟效果应有尽有——奇怪的是，有些滤镜甚至模仿了 Game Boy 的灰度外观。此外，它还提供游戏运行速度调节功能，包括快进和倒带功能。

Somewhat annoyingly, it doesn’t seem to offer a “true” full-screen view of your games—expand Playback to fill your monitor, and although you’ll get a much larger view of the game, it’s one with black borders on all four sides. If there’s a way to get the original 4:3 aspect-ratio image to fill the middle of the screen with only pillar-box borders, I’ve not found it in any of the settings.
令人烦恼的是，它似乎无法提供“真正”的游戏全屏视图——将 Playback 窗口放大到填满显示器后，虽然游戏画面变大了，但四周仍会有黑边。如果有什么方法能让原始的 4:3 比例图像填满屏幕中间且仅保留左右黑边，我在设置中并没有找到。

Image scaling aside, if you don’t like how Playback initially handles the emulation, you can tweak it. The popular BSNES emulator is enabled by default, with five versions of Snes9X included, and any other emulator core you prefer can be added manually. That’s getting deep into the weeds, though, since for most players, the SN Operator’s plug-and-play approach will more than suffice. It’s worth noting that not all cores support achievement tracking through Retro Achievements, another returning feature that looks to be a mainstay of the Operator line.
撇开图像缩放不谈，如果你不喜欢 Playback 默认的模拟效果，可以进行调整。它默认启用流行的 BSNES 模拟器，并内置了五个版本的 Snes9X，你也可以手动添加任何你喜欢的模拟器核心。不过这属于进阶玩法，对于大多数玩家来说，SN Operator 的即插即用方式已经绰绰有余。值得注意的是，并非所有核心都支持通过 Retro Achievements 进行成就追踪，这是 Operator 系列回归的另一项功能，看起来已成为该系列的标配。

The SN Operator continues to allow you to make legal copies of cartridges you own, backing up a cart’s image to your computer in seconds. This is purely for your own archival purposes—you’ll still need an actual cartridge inserted to play.
SN Operator 继续允许你为拥有的卡带制作合法备份，在几秒钟内将卡带镜像备份到电脑上。这纯粹是为了你个人的存档目的——游玩时仍需插入真实的卡带。

At this point, you’re probably thinking the SN Operator sounds like it’s just the GB Operator for SNES carts. Honestly? It kind of is, and that would probably be good enough for retro fans—it’s a fantastic tool for viable SNES gaming in 2026. However, Epilogue hasn’t rested on any laurels, instead tailoring its latest gadget to the unique needs and features of the hardware it emulates.
看到这里，你可能觉得 SN Operator 听起来就像是 SNES 版的 GB Operator。老实说？确实如此，这对复古游戏粉丝来说已经足够好了——它是 2026 年游玩 SNES 游戏的绝佳工具。然而，Epilogue 并没有止步不前，而是针对其模拟硬件的独特需求和特性，对这款最新设备进行了量身定制。

One of the biggest wins is its universality. Unlike the various generations of Game Boy consoles, which always allowed you to play games from anywhere in the world, the SNES had plenty of region restrictions in place. Carts were physically incompatible—the blocky North American carts won’t fit the rounded slots of the Japanese Super Famicom or the European Super Nintendo consoles, and vice versa—and featured lockout chips.
最大的亮点之一是其通用性。与始终允许游玩全球游戏的 Game Boy 系列不同，SNES 存在许多区域限制。卡带在物理上不兼容——方正的北美卡带无法插入日本 Super Famicom 或欧洲 Super Nintendo 的圆形卡槽，反之亦然——并且还设有锁定芯片。

There were workarounds—European or Australian gamers will likely remember dual-boot adapters that used a local cart to spoof the console into accepting an imported American game—but with different TV standards to consider, most players were effectively limited to only the games released in their home territory.
虽然曾有过变通方法——欧洲或澳大利亚玩家可能还记得那种使用本地卡带欺骗主机以运行进口美版游戏的双引导适配器——但考虑到不同的电视制式，大多数玩家实际上只能游玩本地区发行的游戏。

With the SN Operator, that’s all consigned to the past. All cart sizes fit, and lockout chips are ignored—insert a game from anywhere in the world and, so long as it’s still in good working order, it’ll load. Modern displays mean old hurdles like different NTSC or PAL display standards are irrelevant.
有了 SN Operator，这一切都成为了过去。所有尺寸的卡带都能插入，锁定芯片也被忽略——插入来自世界任何地方的游戏，只要卡带本身完好，就能直接运行。现代显示器意味着 NTSC 或 PAL 等不同的显示制式障碍已不再重要。

That brings a few material benefits for purists. When the NTSC versus PAL distinction was an issue, it often meant PAL games ran more slowly due to the standard’s lower 50-Hz refresh rate compared to NTSC’s 60 Hz. Being in the UK, I can finally play Street Fighter II Turbo at its original speed, or the classic action platformer Spider-Man and the X-Men in Arcade's Revenge without Spidey feeling like he’s web-swinging through mud. 这为纯粹主义者带来了一些实质性的好处。在 NTSC 和 PAL 制式存在差异的年代，PAL 版游戏往往因为 50Hz 的刷新率低于 NTSC 的 60Hz 而运行得更慢。身处英国，我终于可以以原始速度游玩《街头霸王 II Turbo》，或者游玩经典的动作平台游戏《蜘蛛侠与 X 战警：街机复仇》，而不必再觉得蜘蛛侠像是在泥潭里荡蛛丝。无区域限制也让北美玩家受益，使他们能够游玩此前不兼容的其他地区独占游戏。
