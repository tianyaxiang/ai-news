# WHERE to Generate Matters: Budget-Aware Synthetic Augmentation for Label Skewed Federated Learning

**Title:** WHERE to Generate Matters: Budget-Aware Synthetic Augmentation for Label Skewed Federated Learning
**标题：** 生成位置至关重要：针对标签偏斜联邦学习的预算感知合成增强

**Abstract:** Label skew in federated learning (FL) causes client drift and degrades global accuracy. Synthetic data augmentation can reduce this imbalance; however, full class balancing requires substantial computation cost.
**摘要：** 联邦学习（FL）中的标签偏斜会导致客户端漂移并降低全局准确率。合成数据增强可以减少这种不平衡；然而，完全的类别平衡需要巨大的计算成本。

We propose FedEAS, a policy that assigns each client an entropy-adaptive per-class generation budget computed from its local label distribution. The budget jointly decides \emph{how much} each client generates and \emph{WHERE} the samples go.
我们提出了 FedEAS，这是一种根据客户端本地标签分布计算出的熵自适应每类生成预算的策略。该预算共同决定了每个客户端生成样本的“数量”以及样本的“去向”（即生成位置）。

Accordingly, the total generation budget follows from the per-client budgets rather than being fixed in advance. FedEAS recovers most of the accuracy gain of full class balancing while reducing the generation budget by 94.1\%.
因此，总生成预算是由各客户端的预算汇总而成的，而不是预先固定的。FedEAS 在将生成预算降低 94.1% 的同时，恢复了完全类别平衡所带来的大部分准确率提升。

At the same total generation budget, it outperforms Uniform allocation by up to 18.82\% across CIFAR-10 and CIFAR-100.
在相同的总生成预算下，该方法在 CIFAR-10 和 CIFAR-100 数据集上的表现比均匀分配策略高出 18.82%。
