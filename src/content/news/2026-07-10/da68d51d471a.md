# OpenAI launches its new family of models with GPT-5.6
# OpenAI 发布 GPT-5.6 系列模型

OpenAI unveiled its newest family of models on Thursday, introducing a new set of heavyweight programs into an increasingly crowded field of AI offerings. GPT-5.6 comes in three variants: Sol (considered its workhorse), Terra (a more intermediate option), and Luna (its budget friendly option). These models expand what users can do across a variety of fields — with the company promising powerful capabilities in enterprise work, coding, and even scientific research.

OpenAI 于周四揭晓了其最新的模型系列，为日益拥挤的 AI 市场引入了一套重量级程序。GPT-5.6 包含三个版本：Sol（被视为主力机型）、Terra（中端选项）和 Luna（经济实惠型选项）。这些模型扩展了用户在多个领域的应用能力，公司承诺其在企业办公、编程甚至科学研究方面都具备强大的性能。

CEO Sam Altman has promised that his company’s newest models are orders of magnitude more efficient and cost-effective than previous versions, recently telling CNBC that Sol is 54% more token efficient when it comes to AI coding tasks. Most notably, the company calls 5.6 its “strongest cybersecurity model yet, achieving frontier performance with significantly fewer tokens.” Indeed, much hubbub has been made about the model’s cyber capabilities, as the Trump administration previously sought to restrict its rollout, ostensibly due to fears of how the model could be misused.

首席执行官 Sam Altman 承诺，公司最新的模型在效率和成本效益上比以往版本有了数量级的提升。他最近在接受 CNBC 采访时表示，Sol 在处理 AI 编程任务时，Token 使用效率提高了 54%。最值得注意的是，该公司称 5.6 是其“迄今为止最强大的网络安全模型，以显著更少的 Token 实现了前沿性能。”事实上，该模型的网络安全能力引发了广泛讨论，此前特朗普政府曾试图限制其发布，表面上是担心该模型可能被滥用。

GPT-5.6 supports defensive activities, including threat modeling, code review and patching, and blue teaming (simulating an attack on your own systems to find weaknesses before real hackers do). OpenAI also released a new tool called ChatGPT Work, which — just as it sounds — is designed as a workplace companion for enterprise teams, running on desktop, web, and mobile, that can help with daily clerical tasks, like drafting documents, spreadsheets, and presentations.

GPT-5.6 支持防御性活动，包括威胁建模、代码审查与修补，以及蓝队演练（在真正的黑客发现漏洞之前，模拟对自身系统进行攻击以寻找弱点）。OpenAI 还发布了一款名为 ChatGPT Work 的新工具，顾名思义，它旨在作为企业团队的办公助手，可在桌面端、网页端和移动端运行，协助处理日常文书工作，如起草文档、电子表格和演示文稿。

OpenAI’s newly announced family of models follows on the heels of similar releases this week from competitors SpaceXAI and Meta. However, GPT-5.6 and its attendant marketing seems most designed to take aim at OpenAI’s primary opponent, Anthropic. Anthropic has managed to make itself the likable underdog of the AI race, focusing fixedly on enterprise customers and winning a growing share of support as a result.

OpenAI 新发布的模型系列紧随竞争对手 SpaceXAI 和 Meta 本周的类似发布之后。然而，GPT-5.6 及其配套的营销策略似乎主要是为了针对 OpenAI 的主要竞争对手 Anthropic。Anthropic 已成功将自己打造为 AI 竞赛中备受青睐的“挑战者”，专注于企业客户，并因此赢得了越来越多的支持。

Not to be outdone, OpenAI cites the Artificial Analysis Coding Agent Index, a notable benchmarking metric, to claim that its latest family of models outshines Anthropic’s models at every turn. OpenAI calls Sol its “best coding model yet,” and has explicitly compared it to Anthropic’s recently released (and much hyped) Fable. Using the Coding Agent Index, OpenAI claims that Sol “sets a new state of the art at 80, 2.8 points above Fable 5, while using less than half the output tokens, taking less than half the time, and costing about one-third less.” It adds: “That advantage extends across the family: Terra performs just above Fable 5, while Luna outperforms Opus 4.8.”

不甘示弱的 OpenAI 引用了著名的基准测试指标“人工智能分析编码代理指数”（Artificial Analysis Coding Agent Index），声称其最新的模型系列在各方面都超越了 Anthropic 的模型。OpenAI 称 Sol 为其“迄今为止最好的编码模型”，并明确将其与 Anthropic 最近发布（且备受炒作）的 Fable 进行了对比。利用该编码代理指数，OpenAI 声称 Sol “以 80 分创下了新的行业领先水平，比 Fable 5 高出 2.8 分，同时输出 Token 消耗不到前者的一半，耗时不到一半，成本也降低了约三分之一。”并补充道：“这种优势延伸到了整个系列：Terra 的表现略高于 Fable 5，而 Luna 则优于 Opus 4.8。”

The company says that 5.6 is now available across ChatGPT, Codex, and the OpenAI API. Availability per million tokens is priced as follows: Sol is $5 input / $30 output, Terra is $2.50 input / $15 output, and Luna is $1 input / $6 output.

该公司表示，5.6 版本现已在 ChatGPT、Codex 和 OpenAI API 中可用。每百万 Token 的定价如下：Sol 为输入 5 美元/输出 30 美元，Terra 为输入 2.50 美元/输出 15 美元，Luna 为输入 1 美元/输出 6 美元。
