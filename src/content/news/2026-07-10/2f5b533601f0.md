# Conspiracies and regrets abound in Dune: Part Three trailer
# 《沙丘3》预告片：阴谋与悔恨交织

We haven’t seen much footage to date for Denis Villeneuve’s Dune: Part Three, other than the broody and haunting extended teaser Warner Bros. dropped in March. But now we’ve got a shiny new trailer jam-packed with tantalizing hints of what to expect, and plenty of Easter eggs to delight avid book fans. (Spoilers for first two films in the franchise below.)

除了华纳兄弟三月份发布的那段忧郁而令人难忘的加长预告片外，我们至今还没看到太多关于丹尼斯·维伦纽瓦执导的《沙丘3》的影像资料。但现在，我们迎来了一支全新的预告片，其中塞满了令人期待的线索，以及足以让原著书迷们大饱眼福的彩蛋。（下文包含前两部电影的剧透。）

As previously reported, in 2021’s Dune, we first met Frank Herbert’s iconic anti-hero, Paul Atreides (Timothée Chalamet). That film culminated in the brutal defeat of House Atreides by rival House Harkonnen, with Paul and his mother, Lady Jessica (Rebecca Ferguson), fleeing to the desert and taking refuge among the Fremen. Among them is Chani (Zendaya), whom Paul has been seeing in visions all along.

正如之前报道的那样，在2021年的《沙丘》中，我们首次见到了弗兰克·赫伯特笔下标志性的反英雄人物——保罗·厄崔迪（提莫西·查拉梅饰）。那部电影以厄崔迪家族被宿敌哈克南家族残酷击败而告终，保罗和他的母亲杰西卡夫人（丽贝卡·弗格森饰）逃往沙漠，并在弗雷曼人中寻求庇护。其中就包括保罗在幻象中一直看到的契妮（赞达亚饰）。

In Dune: Part Two (2024), Paul ingratiated himself with the Fremen, learning to ride a sandworm and winning Chani’s love. A pregnant Lady Jessica had her own plans for Paul as a messiah figure, in order to fulfill a Bene Gesserit prophecy. Ultimately, Paul challenges the emperor, defeats his champion, and demands the Princess Irulan (Florence Pugh) as his wife. The other Great Houses reject Paul as ruler, so he launches a holy war against them. And Chani rides off on a sandworm after refusing to bow down to Paul.

在《沙丘2》（2024）中，保罗赢得了弗雷曼人的信任，学会了骑沙虫，并赢得了契妮的芳心。怀孕的杰西卡夫人为了实现贝尼·杰瑟里特姐妹会的预言，对保罗这位“救世主”有着自己的打算。最终，保罗挑战了皇帝，击败了他的冠军勇士，并要求迎娶伊勒琅公主（弗洛伦斯·皮尤饰）。其他大贵族拒绝承认保罗的统治地位，于是他发动了一场圣战。而契妮在拒绝向保罗屈服后，骑着沙虫离去。

Dune: Part Three draws on Herbert’s novel Dune: Messiah and will take place roughly 17 years after the end of Part Two, according to Villeneuve, as Paul faces the consequences of his earlier actions. Per the official premise: Dune Messiah continues the story of Paul Atreides, better known—and feared—as the man christened Muad’Dib. As Emperor of the known universe, he possesses more power than a single man was ever meant to wield. Worshipped as a religious icon by the fanatical Fremen, Paul faces the enmity of the political houses he displaced when he assumed the throne—and a conspiracy conducted within his own sphere of influence. And even as House Atreides begins to crumble around him from the machinations of his enemies, the true threat to Paul comes to his lover, Chani, and the unborn heir to his family’s dynasty…

《沙丘3》取材于赫伯特的小说《沙丘救世主》。据维伦纽瓦透露，故事将发生在《沙丘2》结束约17年后，保罗将直面他此前行为所带来的后果。根据官方剧情简介：《沙丘救世主》延续了保罗·厄崔迪的故事，他更为人所知——也更令人恐惧——的身份是“穆阿迪布”。作为已知宇宙的皇帝，他拥有的权力远超凡人所能掌控的极限。他被狂热的弗雷曼人奉为宗教偶像，同时还要面对他在登基时所取代的各政治家族的敌意，以及在他势力范围内暗中进行的阴谋。正当厄崔迪家族因敌人的诡计而开始分崩离析时，保罗真正的威胁却降临到了他的爱人契妮，以及他尚未出世的家族继承人身上……

Chalamet is back as Paul Atreides, along with Zendaya, Pugh, and Ferguson. Josh Brolin reprises his role as weapons master Gurney Halleck; Javier Bardem is back as Stilgar, leader of the Fremen tribe at Sietch Tabr; and Jason Momoa returns as Duncan Idaho/Hayt. Joining the cast are Anya Taylor-Joy as Paul’s younger sister, Alia; Robert Pattinson as the villainous Scytale, who plots to dethrone Paul; Isaach de Bankolé as Paul’s former Fedaykin, Farok; Nakoa-Wolf Momoa (son of Jason) as Paul and Chani’s son, Leto II Atreides; and Ida Brooke as Paul and Chani’s daughter, Ghanima Atreides.

查拉梅回归饰演保罗·厄崔迪，赞达亚、皮尤和弗格森也将回归。乔什·布洛林继续饰演武器大师哥尼·哈莱克；哈维尔·巴登回归饰演塔布尔穴地弗雷曼部落的首领斯第尔格；杰森·莫玛回归饰演邓肯·艾达荷/海特。新加入的演员包括：安雅·泰勒-乔伊饰演保罗的妹妹阿丽亚；罗伯特·帕丁森饰演企图推翻保罗的邪恶反派斯凯特尔；伊萨赫·德·班克尔饰演保罗的前费代金战士法洛克；纳科亚-沃尔夫·莫玛（杰森之子）饰演保罗与契妮的儿子雷托二世；艾达·布鲁克饰演保罗与契妮的女儿加尼玛。

### War and peace
### 战争与和平

The trailer opens with an argument between Paul and Chani, interspersed with flashbacks to happier times and scenes of a brutal battle. “I trusted you!” she angrily tells Paul. “You promised me that you would never take power in your name. You convinced me that this was your home, that I was your home.” Instead, Paul waged galactic war and “destroyed thousands of worlds” to become emperor, alienating the woman he loved.

预告片以保罗和契妮的争吵开场，穿插着过去幸福时光的闪回和残酷战斗的场景。“我信任过你！”她愤怒地对保罗说，“你答应过我，绝不会以你的名义夺取权力。你曾让我相信这里是你的家，而我是你的家。”然而，保罗却发动了银河战争，“摧毁了数千个世界”以成为皇帝，疏远了他所爱的女人。

Cut to the reappearance of Duncan Idaho, or rather, a reanimated ghola named Hayt who retains Duncan’s memories. He thinks Paul is “way beyond redemption” after all the atrocities he has committed. Hayt says he’s there to deliver a peace proposal but warns Paul that “it’s meant to destroy you.”

镜头切换到邓肯·艾达荷的再次出现，或者更准确地说，是一个保留了邓肯记忆的复活人偶“海特”。在目睹了保罗犯下的所有暴行后，他认为保罗已经“无可救药”。海特声称他是来递交一份和平提案的，但警告保罗说：“这东西是为了摧毁你而设计的。”

The conspirators seem to include Princess Irulan and Scytale, but she doesn’t seem keen on the fact that Scytale intends to kill Paul to achieve “regime change.” She calls him an idiot: “You’ve just signed our death warrants.” (Paul does have prescient powers as a result of the events in Dune: Part Two, which makes conspiring against him rather challenging.)

阴谋者似乎包括伊勒琅公主和斯凯特尔，但她似乎并不赞同斯凯特尔试图通过杀死保罗来实现“政权更迭”的做法。她骂他是白痴：“你刚刚签下了我们的死刑判决书。”（由于《沙丘2》中的事件，保罗确实拥有预知能力，这使得针对他的阴谋变得相当棘手。）

Meanwhile, the film version of Chani just might be conspiring against Paul in her own way. We see her on the dunes of Arrakis, seemingly summoning a sandworm, which charges before stopping in front of her. The final shot is a remorseful Paul asking for forgiveness for all that he has done.

与此同时，电影版的契妮可能正在以她自己的方式密谋对抗保罗。我们看到她在厄拉科斯的沙丘上，似乎在召唤一只沙虫，沙虫冲向她，但在她面前停了下来。最后一个镜头是充满悔恨的保罗，请求人们原谅他所做的一切。

Dune: Part Three is slated to hit theaters on December 18, 2026.
《沙丘3》定于2026年12月18日在影院上映。
