# LipSSD: Lipschitz-Constrained Single-Shot Detection for Adversarially Robust Object Detection
# LipSSD：用于对抗鲁棒目标检测的 Lipschitz 约束单次检测器

**Abstract:** Object detectors have many applications in safety-critical systems, but they are known to be sensitive to worst-case perturbations such as adversarial attacks, which limits their applicability in real-world scenarios. Compared with classification, adversarial robustness for object detection has received less attention, and existing methods are often tied to adversarial training, whose performance may not transfer across attacks, perturbation budgets, or architectures.

**摘要：** 目标检测器在安全关键系统中有着广泛的应用，但众所周知，它们对对抗攻击等最坏情况下的扰动非常敏感，这限制了它们在现实场景中的适用性。与分类任务相比，目标检测的对抗鲁棒性受到的关注较少，且现有方法往往依赖于对抗训练，而对抗训练的性能在不同的攻击方式、扰动预算或架构之间可能无法有效迁移。

In this work, we introduce Lipschitz-constrained variants of object detection architectures as robust-by-design alternatives to standard detectors. We validate this approach with LipSSD, a Lipschitz-constrained Single Shot MultiBox Detector (SSD), and provide a comprehensive study of its adversarial robustness using multiple white-box adversarial attacks and datasets.

在这项工作中，我们引入了 Lipschitz 约束的目标检测架构变体，作为标准检测器的一种“设计即鲁棒”（robust-by-design）的替代方案。我们通过 LipSSD（一种 Lipschitz 约束的单次多框检测器，SSD）验证了该方法，并利用多种白盒对抗攻击和数据集对其对抗鲁棒性进行了全面研究。

We first analyze the accuracy-robustness trade-off induced by Lipschitz constraints and show that it can be controlled through a single training hyperparameter. We then demonstrate that Lipschitz-constrained detectors are complementary to adversarial training: under the same training setup on the Pascal VOC dataset, adversarially trained LipSSD improves mAP@50 on unseen attacks by up to 15 points over classical adversarially trained SSD.

我们首先分析了由 Lipschitz 约束引起的准确率与鲁棒性之间的权衡，并证明可以通过单一的训练超参数对其进行控制。随后，我们证明了 Lipschitz 约束检测器与对抗训练具有互补性：在 Pascal VOC 数据集的相同训练设置下，经过对抗训练的 LipSSD 在面对未见过的攻击时，其 mAP@50 指标比传统的对抗训练 SSD 提升了高达 15 个百分点。

Finally, we use more specific safety-critical datasets such as LARD and KITTI, and show that Lipschitz-constrained detectors can improve robustness while largely preserving clean performance. These results suggest that architectural Lipschitz control is a practical and attack-agnostic direction for improving the robustness of object detectors.

最后，我们使用 LARD 和 KITTI 等更具体的安全关键数据集进行了测试，结果表明 Lipschitz 约束检测器可以在很大程度上保持原始性能的同时提高鲁棒性。这些结果表明，架构级的 Lipschitz 控制是提高目标检测器鲁棒性的一种实用且与攻击类型无关的方向。
