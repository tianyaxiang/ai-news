# Overview of the NLPCC 2026 Shared Task 1: Difficulty-Aware Multilingual and Multimodal Medical Instructional Video Understanding Evaluation

**NLPCC 2026 共享任务 1 概览：难度感知型多语言多模态医学教学视频理解评估**

***

Following the CMIVQA, MMI-VQA, and M4IVQA challenges in NLPCC 2023--2025, we introduce the Difficulty-Aware Medical Instructional Video Question Answering (DA-MIVQA) shared task for NLPCC 2026. 

继 NLPCC 2023-2025 年举办的 CMIVQA、MMI-VQA 和 M4IVQA 挑战赛之后，我们在 NLPCC 2026 中推出了难度感知型医学教学视频问答（DA-MIVQA）共享任务。

DA-MIVQA extends previous multilingual and multimodal medical video benchmarks by explicitly distinguishing questions according to the type and complexity of evidence required for answering. 

DA-MIVQA 通过根据回答问题所需的证据类型和复杂程度对问题进行明确区分，扩展了以往的多语言和多模态医学视频基准。

Specifically, simple questions can often be answered from subtitle-based textual cues, whereas complex questions require visual grounding, procedural understanding, and cross-modal evidence integration. 

具体而言，简单问题通常可以通过基于字幕的文本线索来回答，而复杂问题则需要视觉定位、程序性理解以及跨模态证据整合。

The challenge contains three tracks: Difficulty-Aware Temporal Answer Grounding in Single Video (DA-TAGSV), Difficulty-Aware Video Corpus Retrieval (DA-VCR), and Difficulty-Aware Temporal Answer Grounding in Video Corpus (DA-TAGVC). 

该挑战赛包含三个赛道：单视频难度感知时间答案定位（DA-TAGSV）、难度感知视频语料库检索（DA-VCR）以及视频语料库中的难度感知时间答案定位（DA-TAGVC）。

The dataset is collected from public medical instructional channels, covers diverse scenarios such as first aid, emergency response, rehabilitation, nursing, and general medical education, and is manually verified with difficulty annotations. 

该数据集收集自公共医学教学频道，涵盖了急救、应急响应、康复、护理和通用医学教育等多种场景，并经过人工验证及难度标注。

This paper presents the task motivation, dataset construction, evaluation protocol, participation overview, competition results, and representative systems of DA-MIVQA. 

本文介绍了 DA-MIVQA 的任务动机、数据集构建、评估协议、参赛概况、竞赛结果以及代表性系统。

DA-MIVQA provides a practical benchmark for evaluating medical instructional video question answering systems under varying textual, visual, temporal, and procedural reasoning requirements.

DA-MIVQA 为评估在不同文本、视觉、时间及程序性推理要求下的医学教学视频问答系统提供了一个实用的基准。
