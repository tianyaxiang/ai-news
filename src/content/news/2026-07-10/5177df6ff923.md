# CoFINN: Conservation Flux Informed Neural Networks for Physics Problems Governed by Conservation Laws
# CoFINN：针对守恒定律物理问题的守恒通量信息神经网络

We present CoFINN (Conservation Flux Informed Neural Networks), a physics-informed deep learning framework for predicting compressible flow fields governed by conservation laws. 
我们提出了 CoFINN（守恒通量信息神经网络），这是一个用于预测受守恒定律支配的可压缩流场的物理信息深度学习框架。

Unlike conventional data-driven convolutional neural networks (CNNs), which optimize only pixel-wise similarity metrics, CoFINN embeds finite-volume conservation physics directly into the training process. 
与仅优化像素级相似度指标的传统数据驱动卷积神经网络（CNN）不同，CoFINN 将有限体积守恒物理直接嵌入到训练过程中。

Unlike classical physics-informed methods which enforce differential-equation residuals at collocation points through automatic differentiation, CoFINN adopts a finite-volume perspective consistent with modern CFD methodology. 
与通过自动微分在配置点强制执行微分方程残差的经典物理信息方法不同，CoFINN 采用了与现代计算流体力学（CFD）方法论一致的有限体积视角。

CoFINN interprets CNN output fields as structured computational grids, where each pixel represents a finite-volume cell, and enforces conservation consistency through sophisticated numerical flux calculations. 
CoFINN 将 CNN 输出场解释为结构化计算网格，其中每个像素代表一个有限体积单元，并通过复杂的数值通量计算来强制执行守恒一致性。

The framework is evaluated on transonic flow prediction around airfoils at (M=0.7, Re=6 * 10^6), including challenging conditions involving shock waves and high angles of attack. 
该框架在 (M=0.7, Re=6 * 10^6) 条件下的翼型跨音速流预测中进行了评估，其中包括涉及激波和高攻角的挑战性工况。

Results show that CoFINN improves aerodynamic force prediction accuracy, reducing drag prediction error by up to 34% at extreme angles of attack and by approximately 15% on average across the test set. 
结果表明，CoFINN 提高了气动力预测的准确性，在极端攻角下阻力预测误差降低了高达 34%，在整个测试集上的平均误差降低了约 15%。

Improvements are particularly significant in limited-data regimes, demonstrating that the conservation-based loss acts as an effective physical regularizer. 
这种改进在数据受限的情况下尤为显著，证明了基于守恒的损失函数可以作为一种有效的物理正则化手段。

The proposed approach maintains the computational efficiency advantages of CNN surrogates while significantly improving physical consistency and conservation behavior. 
所提出的方法在保持 CNN 代理模型计算效率优势的同时，显著提高了物理一致性和守恒特性。

The framework is architecture-agnostic and extensible to broader classes of conservation-law-governed physical systems. 
该框架与架构无关，并可扩展至更广泛的受守恒定律支配的物理系统。
