# A Quiet Failure in Calibrated Virtual Screening: Marginal Conformal Prediction Under-Covers the Minority Class, and a Class-Conditional Fix Recovers It

**校准虚拟筛选中的隐性失效：边缘共形预测对少数类别的覆盖不足及其基于类条件的修复方案**

***

Conformal prediction is being adopted in drug discovery to put an honest number on model reliability: pick an error rate alpha, and the method returns prediction sets containing the true label with probability at least 1 - alpha. 

共形预测（Conformal prediction）正被引入药物发现领域，旨在为模型可靠性提供一个客观的量化指标：设定一个错误率 alpha，该方法会返回包含真实标签的预测集，且其置信度至少为 1 - alpha。

We show this guarantee can be dangerous on imbalanced datasets. Across four datasets, standard (marginal) conformal prediction hits its global 90% coverage target while leaving the minority class badly exposed: realized minority coverage falls to 64.8% on blood-brain-barrier penetration and to 4.2% on clinical-trial toxicity, where the rare class is nearly abandoned. 

我们指出，在不平衡数据集上，这种保证可能存在风险。在四个数据集的测试中，标准（边缘）共形预测虽然达到了 90% 的全局覆盖目标，却导致少数类别处于极度缺乏保护的状态：在血脑屏障渗透性任务中，少数类别的实际覆盖率降至 64.8%；而在临床试验毒性任务中，该覆盖率仅为 4.2%，几乎完全忽略了稀有类别。

The failure is not tied to one model: a random forest, a graph network, and a frozen chemical language model all reproduce it (p < 0.001 in every case), with severity tracking baseline calibration on rare labels rather than architecture. 

这种失效并非特定于某一种模型：随机森林、图神经网络以及冻结参数的化学语言模型均复现了这一现象（所有情况下的 p < 0.001），且失效的严重程度取决于稀有标签的基准校准情况，而非模型架构本身。

A conservation identity explains the effect: the minority's shortfall equals the majority's surplus amplified by the imbalance ratio, predicting the measured gap to within one point and ordering severity across datasets. 

一个守恒恒等式解释了这一效应：少数类别的覆盖缺口等于多数类别的覆盖盈余乘以不平衡比率。该公式能将测量出的差距预测在 1 个百分点以内，并准确反映不同数据集间的严重程度排序。

The failure survives realistic scaffold splits and a second conformal score, while aggregate accuracy and overall coverage stay reassuringly high, which is exactly why it is easy to miss. 

这种失效在现实的骨架拆分（scaffold splits）和第二种共形评分下依然存在，而由于总体准确率和整体覆盖率依然保持在令人放心的水平，这正是该问题容易被忽视的原因。

Class-conditional (Mondrian) conformal prediction closes the gap on every dataset, restoring minority coverage to target for a modest increase in prediction-set size. 

基于类条件（Mondrian）的共形预测弥补了所有数据集上的这一差距，在仅略微增加预测集大小的前提下，将少数类别的覆盖率恢复到了目标水平。

We localize the failures to generic molecular scaffolds - plain benzene and pyridine cores occurring in both classes - propose a one-number diagnostic, and show with a cost model that abstaining on affected compounds flips a screening campaign from net-negative to net-positive utility. 

我们将失效定位在通用的分子骨架上——即同时出现在两个类别中的普通苯环和吡啶核心。我们提出了一种单数值诊断指标，并通过成本模型证明，对受影响的化合物采取弃权策略，可以将筛选工作的效用从净负值转变为净正值。

Our contribution is demonstrating on real chemistry how severe and invisible this known conformal-theory gap becomes under imbalance, and laying out a practical protocol restoring per-class reliability.

我们的贡献在于通过真实的化学数据展示了在数据不平衡的情况下，这一已知的共形理论缺陷会变得多么严重且隐蔽，并制定了一套恢复各类别可靠性的实用方案。
