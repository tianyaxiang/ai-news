# MiLSD: A Micro Line-Segment Detector for Resource-Constrained Devices
# MiLSD：面向资源受限设备的微型线段检测器

**Abstract:** Line segment detection is a key building block in visual SLAM, 3D reconstruction, and industrial inspection. Recent deep learning methods have greatly improved accuracy, yet even the smallest models require several megabytes of memory, exceeding low-cost MCU capacity. 

**摘要：** 线段检测是视觉 SLAM、三维重建和工业检测中的关键基础模块。尽管近期的深度学习方法显著提高了检测精度，但即使是最小的模型也需要数兆字节的内存，这超出了低成本微控制器（MCU）的容量限制。

This work investigates the maximum achievable accuracy under a sub-megabyte budget. We propose MiLSD, a detector tailored for MCU-level constraints, and systematically compare three output representations within a compact fully-convolutional backbone. 

本研究探讨了在小于 1MB 内存预算下所能达到的最高精度。我们提出了 MiLSD，这是一款专为 MCU 级约束而设计的检测器，并在紧凑的全卷积骨干网络中系统地比较了三种输出表示形式。

Our study shows that the proposed F-Clip center-with-length-and-angle formulation learns most effectively at small model sizes. We find that 8-bit quantization preserves full-precision performance, while 4-bit quantization causes significant degradation, particularly in angle regression, with quantization-aware training recovering only part of the loss. 

研究表明，所提出的 F-Clip（中心点+长度+角度）公式在小模型尺寸下学习效果最为显著。我们发现，8 位量化可以保持全精度性能，而 4 位量化会导致显著的性能下降，特别是在角度回归方面，且量化感知训练只能恢复部分损失。

With a one-megabyte activation budget and inference enhancements including sub-pixel decoding, test-time augmentation, and a lightweight verifier, MiLSD improves sAP10 on ShanghaiTech Wireframe from 10.6 (25k parameters, 0.25 MB) to 24.1 within 1 MB. 

在 1MB 激活内存预算以及包括亚像素解码、测试时增强和轻量级验证器在内的推理增强技术的支持下，MiLSD 将 ShanghaiTech Wireframe 数据集上的 sAP10 指标从 10.6（25k 参数，0.25 MB）提升至 24.1（1 MB 以内）。

Rather than competing with GPU-scale parsers, we map the accuracy memory trade-off across representations, bit-widths, capacities, and post-processing strategies for embedded vision systems.

我们并未试图与 GPU 规模的解析器竞争，而是为嵌入式视觉系统绘制了涵盖不同表示形式、位宽、容量和后处理策略的精度-内存权衡图谱。
