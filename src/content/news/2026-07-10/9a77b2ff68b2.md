# Pixel-Precise Explainable Stress Indexing: A Semantic Segmentation Framework for Disease Severity Quantification in Field Crops
# 像素级可解释压力指数：一种用于大田作物病害严重程度量化的语义分割框架

**Abstract:** Plant diseases, resulting from both biotic and abiotic stresses, cause an estimated 20-40% loss in global agricultural yield annually, resulting in economic damages exceeding USD 220 billion. Accurate and scalable stress quantification is essential for precision agriculture, yet traditional manual assessments are labour-intensive and subjective.

**摘要：** 由生物和非生物胁迫引起的植物病害每年导致全球农业产量损失约 20-40%，造成超过 2200 亿美元的经济损失。精确且可扩展的压力（病害）量化对于精准农业至关重要，然而传统的评估方式不仅耗费人力，且具有主观性。

This paper proposes a unified deep learning pipeline integrating semantic segmentation, regression-based severity estimation, and disease classification. Stress severity is categorised into four levels (Low to Very High) based on the proportion of infected leaf area.

本文提出了一种统一的深度学习流水线，集成了语义分割、基于回归的严重程度估计以及病害分类功能。根据受感染叶片面积的比例，压力严重程度被划分为四个等级（从低到极高）。

Experiments on the Apple Tree Leaf Disease Segmentation dataset (1,641 samples, six classes) evaluate four models: U-Net (MobileNetV2), SegFormer, FCN, and PSPNet. U-Net with MobileNetV2 achieves the best performance with 98.20% pixel accuracy, 0.70 mIoU, and 99.41% detection accuracy at 14.7 ms per image, making it suitable for real-time use. SegFormer performs competitively (mIoU 0.66), while FCN and PSPNet show lower spatial accuracy (approximately 0.49 mIoU).

在苹果树叶片病害分割数据集（1,641 个样本，六个类别）上的实验评估了四种模型：U-Net (MobileNetV2)、SegFormer、FCN 和 PSPNet。其中，搭载 MobileNetV2 的 U-Net 表现最佳，像素准确率达到 98.20%，mIoU 为 0.70，检测准确率为 99.41%，且单张图像处理速度仅需 14.7 毫秒，非常适合实时应用。SegFormer 表现具有竞争力（mIoU 0.66），而 FCN 和 PSPNet 的空间准确率较低（mIoU 约为 0.49）。

The computed severity index strongly correlates with expert annotations (r = 0.968, R^2 = 0.937), demonstrating the system's reliability for automated crop monitoring and decision support.

计算出的严重程度指数与专家标注高度相关（r = 0.968, R^2 = 0.937），证明了该系统在自动化作物监测和决策支持方面的可靠性。
