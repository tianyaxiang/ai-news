# A Randomized PDE Energy driven Iterative Framework for Efficient and Stable PDE Solutions
# 一种用于高效稳定求解偏微分方程的随机 PDE 能量驱动迭代框架

Efficient and stable solution of partial differential equations (PDEs) is central to scientific and engineering applications, yet existing numerical solvers rely heavily on matrix based discretizations, while learning based methods require costly training and often suffer from limited generalization.

偏微分方程（PDEs）的高效稳定求解是科学与工程应用的核心，然而现有的数值求解器严重依赖于基于矩阵的离散化方法，而基于学习的方法则需要昂贵的训练成本，且往往面临泛化能力有限的问题。

In this work, we propose a PDE energy driven framework that solves PDEs through physically constrained diffusion iterations, without relying on classical matrix based finite element assembly or data driven neural network training.

在这项工作中，我们提出了一种 PDE 能量驱动框架，通过物理约束的扩散迭代来求解偏微分方程，无需依赖传统的基于矩阵的有限元组装或数据驱动的神经网络训练。

The proposed method evolves arbitrary random initial fields through PDE energy driven implicit iterations combined with Gaussian smoothing, while strictly enforcing boundary conditions at each iteration.

该方法通过 PDE 能量驱动的隐式迭代结合高斯平滑，对任意随机初始场进行演化，并在每次迭代中严格执行边界条件。

The proposed formulation is applied to representative one dimensional Poisson, Heat, and viscous Burgers equations, covering both steady state and transient problems.

该公式被应用于具有代表性的一维泊松方程、热传导方程和粘性伯格斯方程，涵盖了稳态和瞬态问题。

Numerical results demonstrate stable convergence to the unique physical solution from random initializations, with accurate resolution of sharp gradients and controlled Mean Squared Error (MSE) across a wide range of discretization parameters.

数值结果表明，从随机初始化出发，该方法能够稳定收敛至唯一的物理真解，并能精确解析尖锐梯度，在广泛的离散化参数范围内保持受控的均方误差（MSE）。

Detailed comparisons with analytical solutions indicate that the framework achieves competitive accuracy and stability. Overall, the proposed framework provides a fast, flexible, and physically consistent alternative to traditional numerical solvers, offering a potential pathway for scalable PDE solutions in both research and engineering applications.

与解析解的详细对比表明，该框架实现了具有竞争力的精度和稳定性。总体而言，所提出的框架为传统数值求解器提供了一种快速、灵活且物理一致的替代方案，为研究和工程应用中可扩展的 PDE 求解提供了一条潜在途径。
