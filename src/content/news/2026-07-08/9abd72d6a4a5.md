# Double-Helix Active Geometry: LiDAR-Anchored Multi-View Depth with Selective Abstention
# 双螺旋主动几何：基于激光雷达锚定与选择性弃权的视差深度估计

**Abstract:** Consumer depth sensors such as the LiDAR scanner on recent iPhones provide metric range, but their useful range is short and their returns are sparse. We present DH-Active, a lightweight, training-free geometry back-end that treats the sensor as a metric ruler rather than the sole source of depth. 

**摘要：** 近期 iPhone 上搭载的激光雷达（LiDAR）扫描仪等消费级深度传感器虽然能提供公制测距，但其有效范围较短且返回的数据点稀疏。我们提出了 DH-Active，这是一个轻量级、无需训练的几何后端，它将传感器视为一种公制标尺，而非深度的唯一来源。

Near-field returns anchor the metric relative pose of two views through PnP; visually trackable samples without a valid depth return are then triangulated under that pose. A parallax/reprojection gate abstains wherever the geometry is ill-conditioned, leaving an explicit hole and a selective score instead of forcing an estimate. 

近场返回的数据通过 PnP（透视 n 点问题）锚定两个视图的公制相对位姿；随后，对于没有有效深度返回但视觉上可追踪的样本，在该位姿下进行三角测量。视差/重投影门控机制会在几何条件不佳的情况下选择“弃权”，留下明确的空洞和选择性评分，而不是强行进行估计。

The measured core front end, including spiral sampling, sparse back-projection, and hole taxonomy but excluding preprocessing and multi-view recovery, runs at 1.11 ms median latency on CPU (OpenCV using 14 threads), about 38 times faster than a DINOv2-L visual branch on GPU in our timing setup. 

经测量的核心前端（包括螺旋采样、稀疏反向投影和空洞分类，但不包括预处理和多视图恢复）在 CPU 上（使用 14 线程的 OpenCV）的中位延迟为 1.11 毫秒，在我们设定的测试环境下，比 GPU 上的 DINOv2-L 视觉分支快约 38 倍。

Across two iPhone captures and the public TUM RGB-D and ARKitScenes benchmarks, held-out depth is recovered at 1.4 to 6.7 percent median relative error. In a controlled ARKitScenes protocol that uses only returns within 2 m to set scale and an independent laser scan as ground truth, DH-Active achieves 64.2 percent scene-median coverage of evaluable far-field candidates at 13.4 percent scene-median relative error; direct triangulation from the device trajectory is not usable. 

在两次 iPhone 采集的数据以及公开的 TUM RGB-D 和 ARKitScenes 基准测试中，留存深度数据的恢复中位相对误差在 1.4% 到 6.7% 之间。在受控的 ARKitScenes 协议中（仅使用 2 米以内的返回数据来设定比例，并以独立的激光扫描作为真值），DH-Active 对可评估远场候选点的场景中位覆盖率达到了 64.2%，场景中位相对误差为 13.4%；而直接利用设备轨迹进行三角测量则无法使用。

We also report the alternatives that failed in our tests: single-frame defocus, classical focus-stack depth, defocus-LiDAR fusion, point-to-point ICP over a good visual-inertial track, and attention-to-holes resampling. A 1.26 B learned model remains more accurate after oracle scale alignment. The contribution here is narrower: metric sparse depth, explicit abstention, zero learned parameters, and near-millisecond CPU cost.

我们还报告了在测试中失败的替代方案：单帧散焦、经典对焦堆叠深度、散焦-激光雷达融合、基于良好视觉惯性轨迹的点对点 ICP，以及针对空洞的注意力重采样。在经过预言机（Oracle）尺度对齐后，一个 12.6 亿参数的学习模型仍然更准确。本文的贡献点更为聚焦：公制稀疏深度、显式弃权、零学习参数以及近毫秒级的 CPU 开销。
