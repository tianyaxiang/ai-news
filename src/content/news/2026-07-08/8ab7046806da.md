# The Best iPhone 17 Cases (2026): Our Picks After Testing 100+
# 2026 年最佳 iPhone 17 手机壳：百余款测试后的精选推荐

Even the latest iPhones need a case. The Ceramic Shield 2 glass on the front of the iPhone 17 may be three times as scratch-resistant as its predecessors, but that hasn't prevented us from experiencing scratches on our iPhone Air and iPhone 17. Glass is still glass, folks.
即使是最新款的 iPhone 也需要手机壳。iPhone 17 正面采用的 Ceramic Shield 2 玻璃虽然比前代产品的耐刮擦性能提升了三倍，但这并没有阻止我们的 iPhone Air 和 iPhone 17 出现划痕。各位，玻璃终究还是玻璃。

The best iPhone 17 cases and screen protectors can work together to keep your phone in pristine condition for as long as possible, aka when you decide to sell your iPhone to buy a new one. We have tested more than 100 cases and screen protectors to find the very best. Whether you have an iPhone 17 Pro Max or a new iPhone 17e, we've got something for you. We're continuously testing new cases and accessories—if there's something you want us to check out, drop a comment.
最好的 iPhone 17 手机壳和屏幕保护膜可以协同工作，尽可能长时间地保持手机处于原始状态，也就是当你决定卖掉旧手机换新机时，它依然能保持良好的成色。我们测试了 100 多款手机壳和屏幕保护膜，只为找出其中的佼佼者。无论你使用的是 iPhone 17 Pro Max 还是新款 iPhone 17e，我们都有适合你的选择。我们一直在测试新的手机壳和配件——如果你有想让我们评测的产品，欢迎在评论区留言。

### Best Case Overall: Native Union Active Case
### 最佳综合手机壳：Native Union Active Case

Native Union's Active case for the iPhone 17 models is fantastic. I love the dot pattern design on the back, and there are a handful of colors to choose from. I previously had Mous' Limitless case as the absolute best iPhone 17 case—it's still excellent—but I've quickly come to prefer Native Union's option. It's slightly grippier and employs a softer microfiber inner lining, the edges around the display and cameras are nicely raised, and the rear texture is lovely. The buttons are snappy, and the Camera Control button is covered up by glass, so you can still slide your finger on it to change camera modes. Better yet, considering the aluminum on the Pro models tends to show scratches easily, especially around the Camera Plateau, the Active case covers it up for maximum protection.
Native Union 为 iPhone 17 系列推出的 Active 手机壳非常出色。我喜欢它背面的圆点图案设计，而且有多种颜色可供选择。我之前认为 Mous 的 Limitless 手机壳是 iPhone 17 的最佳选择（它依然非常优秀），但我很快就更倾向于 Native Union 的这款产品。它的抓握感稍好，内部采用了更柔软的超细纤维衬里，显示屏和摄像头周围的边缘有很好的加高处理，背部的质感也非常棒。按键反馈清脆，且“相机控制”按钮被玻璃覆盖，因此你仍然可以通过滑动手指来切换拍摄模式。更棒的是，考虑到 Pro 机型的铝合金边框容易产生划痕（尤其是在摄像头模组周围），Active 手机壳将其完全覆盖，提供了最大程度的保护。

### Runner-Up: Mous Limitless Phone Case
### 亚军：Mous Limitless Phone Case

Mous makes some of my favorite cases, and the Limitless series is great because you can choose from various designs, from a wood back to speckled fabric. I tried the Marine, which has a lovely, soft fabric-like texture on the back. The thermoplastic polyurethane bumper is rigid and nicely raised to protect the screen and cameras, and I especially love that the Camera Control button isn't a cutout, but a real button embedded in the case. It makes the case more symmetrical. MagSafe works excellently, and the buttons are snappy. I like the design of the Native Union Active case a little more, but the Limitless is otherwise flawless.
Mous 生产的一些手机壳是我的最爱，Limitless 系列非常棒，因为你可以选择各种设计，从木质背板到斑点织物应有尽有。我试用了 Marine 款，它的背面有一种可爱、柔软的类织物纹理。热塑性聚氨酯（TPU）边框坚固且加高设计合理，能很好地保护屏幕和摄像头。我特别喜欢它的“相机控制”按钮不是镂空设计，而是嵌入手机壳中的真实按键，这让手机壳看起来更加对称。MagSafe 功能表现出色，按键反馈也很清脆。虽然我个人更喜欢 Native Union Active 的设计，但除此之外，Limitless 几乎无可挑剔。

### Best Clear Case: dbrand Ghost Case 2.0
### 最佳透明手机壳：dbrand Ghost Case 2.0

Dbrand company promises its Ghost 2.0 case won't yellow over time, and if it does, you'll get your money back. You can see your iPhone's color through the clear back, but the edges are wrapped in a thicker material to absorb shocks. It has a ribbed design for better grip, and the textured buttons are very clicky. I've also found Dbrand's magnets to be a little stronger than those in most other cases.
Dbrand 公司承诺其 Ghost 2.0 手机壳不会随时间推移而发黄，如果发黄，他们将退款。你可以透过透明背板看到 iPhone 的颜色，而边缘则包裹着较厚的材料以吸收冲击。它采用了棱纹设计以增强抓握感，带纹理的按键点击感非常清脆。我还发现 Dbrand 的磁铁比大多数其他手机壳中的磁铁稍微强一些。

### Best Cheap Case: Spigen Ultra Hybrid MagFit Case
### 最佳平价手机壳：Spigen Ultra Hybrid MagFit Case

I tested several Spigen cases, and the Ultra Hybrid MagFit is my favorite, specifically the model with the teardown skin. Spigen has been making cases with these designs for a few years now, but this iteration is my favorite. It's surprisingly elegant without being too much. The clear case also lets your iPhone's color shine through. I had no trouble with MagSafe, and the buttons are very clicky. The edges around the screen and cameras rise well enough to keep them off surfaces. It's hard to beat the value here at just $20—even the Camera Control is covered up and isn't a silly cutout!
我测试了几款 Spigen 手机壳，Ultra Hybrid MagFit 是我的最爱，尤其是带有“拆解（teardown）”皮肤的那款。Spigen 几年来一直采用这种设计，但这一代是我最喜欢的。它出奇地优雅，又不会显得过于浮夸。透明的背板让你的 iPhone 原色得以展现。MagSafe 使用起来毫无问题，按键点击感非常清脆。屏幕和摄像头周围的边缘加高程度足以防止它们接触桌面。仅需 20 美元，其性价比极高——甚至连“相机控制”按钮都被覆盖保护，而不是那种愚蠢的镂空设计！
