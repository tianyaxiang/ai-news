# Learning 3D Affordances for Blade Insertion in Cluttered Stowing
# 学习用于杂乱仓储中刀片插入的 3D 可供性 (Affordance)

Many manipulation tasks require reasoning about free-space affordances: discovering volumes where an extended rigid tool can safely navigate, complementary to surface contact affordances for grasping. Robotic stowing is a canonical instance, where a blade must sweep items aside inside cluttered fabric bins to create insertion space.
许多操作任务需要对自由空间可供性进行推理：即发现扩展刚性工具可以安全导航的体积，这与用于抓取的表面接触可供性相辅相成。机器人仓储就是一个典型的例子，其中刀片必须在杂乱的织物箱内将物品扫开，以创造插入空间。

Production stow systems generate millions of such episodes, but standard approaches with unimodal data infer affordances as SE(3) pose distributions, a geometric question asked in the wrong domain. VulcanVoxel keeps inference spatial: a masked autoencoder over 3D occupancy fields reconstructs blade occupancy conditioned on scene geometry, computing feasibility locally at each voxel and recovering multi-modal predictions from unimodal data.
生产仓储系统会产生数百万次此类操作片段，但使用单模态数据的标准方法将可供性推断为 SE(3) 位姿分布，这相当于在错误的领域提出了几何问题。VulcanVoxel 将推断保持在空间层面：通过对 3D 占用场进行掩码自动编码，根据场景几何结构重建刀片占用情况，在每个体素处局部计算可行性，并从单模态数据中恢复多模态预测。

Blade affordances are spatial objects, subsets of 3D space defined by geometric feasibility. Pose parameters carry no structure for reasoning whether unobserved placements are feasible, and standard generative objectives including flow matching faithfully learn the unimodal distribution produced by execution policies and cannot recover geometric alternatives.
刀片可供性是空间对象，是由几何可行性定义的 3D 空间子集。位姿参数不具备推理未观察到的放置位置是否可行的结构，而包括流匹配（flow matching）在内的标准生成目标只能忠实地学习执行策略产生的单模态分布，无法恢复几何替代方案。

Trained on 10,000 real warehouse stow episodes without human annotation, VulcanVoxel achieves top-5 coverage of 0.89 versus 0.71 for the best pose-based baseline, with a distilled student providing RGB-to-voxel inference in 30 ms, compared to 1.4 s for voxel-to-voxel. We have released a dataset of real blade insertion cycles with RGB-D observations and pose trajectories at this URL.
VulcanVoxel 在无需人工标注的情况下，通过 10,000 次真实仓库仓储片段进行训练，其 Top-5 覆盖率达到 0.89，而最佳的基于位姿的基准模型仅为 0.71。此外，经过蒸馏的学生模型可在 30 毫秒内提供 RGB 到体素的推断，相比之下，体素到体素的推断需要 1.4 秒。我们已在该网址发布了包含 RGB-D 观测和位姿轨迹的真实刀片插入周期数据集。
