# Final extension: Startup Battlefield Australia applications now close July 20
# 最后延期：Startup Battlefield Australia 申请截止日期延长至 7 月 20 日

One last chance to apply. Due to overwhelming interest, we’ve extended applications for Startup Battlefield Australia to July 20. If you’ve been thinking about applying, do it now. There won’t be another extension.
最后一次申请机会。由于报名热情高涨，我们将 Startup Battlefield Australia 的申请截止日期延长至 7 月 20 日。如果你一直在考虑申请，现在就行动吧。不会再有延期了。

One application could change everything. Since the first Startup Battlefield Australia in 2017, there have been 26 alumni companies that have collectively raised over $147 million, with three successful acquisitions. They’ve been backed by some of the world’s most respected investors — including Y Combinator, Blackbird Ventures, Square Peg Capital, Khosla Ventures, Microsoft, AirTree Ventures, Startmate, Techstars, and SOSV. It all started with one decision: They applied.
一份申请可能会改变一切。自 2017 年首届 Startup Battlefield Australia 举办以来，已有 26 家校友企业累计融资超过 1.47 亿美元，并完成了三起成功收购。它们获得了全球一些最受尊敬的投资者的支持，包括 Y Combinator、Blackbird Ventures、Square Peg Capital、Khosla Ventures、Microsoft、AirTree Ventures、Startmate、Techstars 和 SOSV。这一切都始于一个决定：他们提交了申请。

Why apply now? If you’re building something ambitious, this is a fast track to the people who can move your startup forward. Selected founders will pitch live to: Top-tier investors, global media, Australia’s leading founders and operators, and potential partners, customers, and hires. This is more than a pitch competition. It’s a chance to earn visibility, credibility, and connections that can take years to build.
为什么要现在申请？如果你正在打造雄心勃勃的项目，这是一条通往能推动你初创公司发展的关键人物的快车道。入选的创始人将向以下群体进行现场路演：顶级投资者、全球媒体、澳大利亚领先的创始人和运营者，以及潜在的合作伙伴、客户和招聘对象。这不仅仅是一场路演比赛，更是一个获得知名度、信誉和人脉的机会，而这些通常需要数年时间才能建立。

What’s at stake? On August 19, 2026, eight startups will pitch live at Stripe Tour Sydney. The top three will receive up to $15,000 in Stripe fee credits. The grand prize is even bigger: Automatic entry into Startup Battlefield 200 at TechCrunch Disrupt in San Francisco this October. No second application. No extra round. Just a direct path to one of the world’s biggest startup stages.
奖项设置如何？2026 年 8 月 19 日，八家初创公司将在 Stripe Tour Sydney 进行现场路演。前三名将获得最高 15,000 美元的 Stripe 手续费抵扣额度。大奖更具吸引力：自动入选今年 10 月在旧金山举行的 TechCrunch Disrupt 大会上的 Startup Battlefield 200。无需二次申请，无需额外轮次，直接通往全球最大的初创公司舞台之一。

Who should apply? We’re looking for early-stage startups across Australia and New Zealand that are: Pre-seed to Series B, building a real product or showing strong traction, ready to scale, and ready to tell their story. You don’t need to be a household name. We’re looking for the next one.
谁应该申请？我们正在寻找澳大利亚和新西兰的早期初创公司，要求如下：处于种子轮前至 B 轮阶段，正在打造真实产品或展现出强劲的发展势头，准备好扩大规模，并准备好讲述他们的故事。你不需要家喻户晓，我们正在寻找下一个明星企业。

The deadline has moved — the opportunity hasn’t. This extension gives you more time, but not much. Applications now close July 20. If you’ve been waiting, this is the moment. Submit your application before July 20. Free to apply. No equity taken. One opportunity that could change everything.
截止日期变了，但机会没变。这次延期给了你更多时间，但时间不多了。申请现于 7 月 20 日截止。如果你一直在等待，现在就是时刻。请在 7 月 20 日之前提交申请。免费申请，不占股份。这是一个可能改变一切的机会。
