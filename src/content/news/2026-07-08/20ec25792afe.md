# Proxy-Pointer RAG: Temporal Reasoning Without Semantic Precompilation
# Proxy-Pointer RAG：无需语义预编译的时序推理

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) has evolved rapidly in recent times. The original RAG paradigm was designed to be straightforward: retrieve the most relevant chunks from a corpus and use them to answer a question.
检索增强生成（RAG）近年来发展迅速。最初的 RAG 范式设计非常直观：从语料库中检索最相关的片段，并利用它们来回答问题。

While effective for many document-centric queries, this approach begins to struggle when questions extend beyond the boundaries of a single document. Or when questions are temporal in nature, such as tracking the history of an entity over years.
虽然这种方法对于许多以文档为中心的问题非常有效，但当问题超出单个文档的范畴，或者涉及时间维度（例如追踪某个实体多年来的历史）时，它就开始显得力不从心。

A few examples would be the following: Which companies did we acquire over the last decade? How has our AI strategy evolved since 2018? Which sustainability commitments announced in previous annual reports were eventually fulfilled? How has management’s capital allocation philosophy changed over the years?
以下是一些例子：过去十年我们收购了哪些公司？自 2018 年以来，我们的 AI 战略是如何演变的？之前年度报告中宣布的可持续发展承诺最终实现了哪些？管理层的资本配置理念多年来发生了怎样的变化？

Recently, a new architectural pattern, LLM-Wiki, has proposed an interesting solution to this challenge. Rather than repeatedly searching raw documents during retrieval, an extensive semantic processing step during ingestion builds a persistent knowledge base consisting of canonical pages linked through an index. Future queries are answered primarily from this compiled knowledge rather than the original documents.
最近，一种新的架构模式“LLM-Wiki”为这一挑战提供了一个有趣的解决方案。它不再在检索过程中反复搜索原始文档，而是在摄入阶段通过广泛的语义处理步骤，构建一个由索引链接的规范页面组成的持久知识库。未来的查询主要基于这些已编译的知识来回答，而不是直接查询原始文档。

While an elegant idea, this raises important architectural questions such as: Should enterprise knowledge be semantically compiled before anyone asks for it? Is there a way to avoid paying this ingestion cost over an entire corpus of large documents (e.g. voluminous annual report, contracts etc), most of whose content may never actually be queried about?
虽然这是一个优雅的构想，但它引发了重要的架构问题，例如：企业知识是否应该在有人查询之前就进行语义编译？有没有办法避免为整个大型文档语料库（如冗长的年度报告、合同等）支付这种摄入成本，毕竟其中大部分内容可能永远不会被查询到？

What should be present in the canonical pages such that almost every future query can be answered without referring to the raw source? An over-engineered canonical page may itself be a very long one, and over time grow significantly larger than the source documents themselves, resulting in higher retrieval latency and cost.
规范页面中应该包含什么，才能确保几乎所有未来的查询都无需参考原始来源即可回答？过度设计的规范页面本身可能非常冗长，并且随着时间的推移，其体积可能会显著超过原始文档，从而导致更高的检索延迟和成本。

In this article, I shall compare LLM-Wiki with the Proxy-Pointer (PP) architecture, a structure-aware retrieval framework that approaches the same problem from a fundamentally different perspective. Rather than eagerly compiling semantic knowledge during ingestion, Proxy-Pointer preserves the structural organization of documents and performs semantic synthesis only when a query requires it.
在本文中，我将把 LLM-Wiki 与 Proxy-Pointer (PP) 架构进行比较。Proxy-Pointer 是一种结构感知检索框架，它从根本上不同的角度处理相同的问题。Proxy-Pointer 不会在摄入时急切地编译语义知识，而是保留文档的结构组织，仅在查询需要时才进行语义合成。

This kind of lazy semantic analysis of content is consistent with the premise of Proxy-Pointer to deliver precision without the ingestion tax, which I have detailed in my articles Proxy-Pointer RAG: Achieving Vectorless Accuracy at Vector RAG Scale and Cost and Proxy-Pointer RAG: Structure Meets Scale — 100% Accuracy with Smarter Retrieval. Let’s see the comparison with the help of a real-life scenario.
这种对内容的“惰性”语义分析符合 Proxy-Pointer 的前提，即在无需支付“摄入税”的情况下提供精确度。我在之前的文章《Proxy-Pointer RAG：以向量 RAG 的规模和成本实现无向量精度》和《Proxy-Pointer RAG：结构与规模的结合——通过更智能的检索实现 100% 准确率》中对此进行了详细阐述。让我们通过一个现实场景来看看对比。

### The LLM-Wiki Approach
### LLM-Wiki 方法

LLM-Wiki records the temporal context by moving much of the reasoning to ingestion time. Instead of treating documents as isolated artifacts, each incoming document is processed by an LLM that extracts concepts, entities, relationships, and facts. These are merged into a collection of canonical pages representing persistent knowledge about the organization.
LLM-Wiki 通过将大部分推理工作转移到摄入阶段来记录时间上下文。它不再将文档视为孤立的制品，而是由 LLM 处理每个传入的文档，提取概念、实体、关系和事实。这些信息被合并成一系列代表组织持久知识的规范页面。

For example, multiple annual reports may collectively update canonical pages such as “Acquisitions”, “Artificial Intelligence”, “Cloud Strategy”, “Sustainability”, “Product Portfolio”, “Supply chain” etc. Each new annual report contributes additional knowledge to these pages. Alongside these canonical pages, the system maintains an index that helps locate the appropriate pages for future queries.
例如，多份年度报告可以共同更新诸如“收购”、“人工智能”、“云战略”、“可持续发展”、“产品组合”、“供应链”等规范页面。每一份新的年度报告都会为这些页面贡献额外的知识。除了这些规范页面外，系统还维护一个索引，帮助定位未来查询所需的适当页面。

Conceptually, the architecture resembles the following: When a user asks, “Which companies did we acquire during the last decade?“, the system primarily searches the index, locates the acquisition-related canonical page, and answers directly from the compiled knowledge. The original documents remain available as an immutable data store, but ideally they are no longer part of the normal query path.
从概念上讲，该架构类似于：当用户询问“过去十年我们收购了哪些公司？”时，系统主要搜索索引，定位与收购相关的规范页面，并直接从已编译的知识中回答。原始文档作为不可变数据存储保留，但理想情况下，它们不再是正常查询路径的一部分。

From an architectural perspective, this is an example of knowledge compilation. How do we build this Wiki? Let’s say the input is a 200 page annual report. In order to optimise on the compute cost, we would like to extract information related to all the canonical pages in one pass and update the relevant pages.
从架构角度来看，这是知识编译的一个例子。我们如何构建这个 Wiki？假设输入是一份 200 页的年度报告。为了优化计算成本，我们希望一次性提取与所有规范页面相关的信息，并更新相关页面。

This would mean asking the LLM to run dozens of extraction tasks simultaneously: Detect: acquisitions, products, AI initiatives, cloud strategy, sustainability, geopolitical risks, partnership, supply chain, regulatory changes, customers, litigation, investments, subsidiaries, organizational restructuring, technology trends, …..
这意味着要求 LLM 同时运行数十个提取任务：检测：收购、产品、AI 计划、云战略、可持续发展、地缘政治风险、合作伙伴关系、供应链、监管变化、客户、诉讼、投资、子公司、组织重组、技术趋势……

As the number of semantic objectives grows, the model’s attention is divided across them. The model must continuously decide which pieces of information belong to which canonical pages, whether new pages should be created, and how newly discovered facts relate to existing knowledge.
随着语义目标数量的增加，模型对它们的注意力被分散了。模型必须不断决定哪些信息属于哪些规范页面，是否应该创建新页面，以及新发现的事实如何与现有知识相关联。

As prompt complexity increases, asking the model to optimize for many objectives simultaneously, LLMs exhibit high-dimensional information extraction degradation. Especially for long and dense documents, this can reduce recall, increase omissions and miss less salient information.
随着提示词复杂性的增加，要求模型同时优化多个目标，LLM 会表现出高维信息提取能力的退化。特别是在处理长且密集的文档时，这可能会降低召回率，增加遗漏，并错过不太显著的信息。

More fundamentally, the system faces a difficult prediction problem. It must decide during ingestion which information is worth materializing into the canonical knowledge base while anticipating every possible question that users will ask in the future.
更根本的是，系统面临一个困难的预测问题。它必须在摄入时决定哪些信息值得固化到规范知识库中，同时还要预判用户未来可能提出的每一个问题。

For instance, “How has the revenue contribution of Subsidiary X evolved since its acquisition?“ The relevant evidence for the above query may be scattered across multiple annual reports, embedded within detailed financial statements, footnotes, or segment reporting tables. Materializing every potentially relevant financial line item into canonical pages would significantly increase the size and maintenance cost of the knowledge base.
例如，“子公司 X 自被收购以来的收入贡献是如何演变的？”上述查询的相关证据可能分散在多份年度报告中，嵌入在详细的财务报表、脚注或分部报告表中。将每一个潜在相关的财务明细项固化到规范页面中，将显著增加知识库的规模和维护成本。
