# Google’s Pixel event is set for August 12
# 谷歌 Pixel 发布会定档 8 月 12 日

Google is preparing for its Made by Google launch event, which is scheduled for August 12 in New York City, as announced on Tuesday. We’re hoping this event will be less cringeworthy than last year’s, which featured Jimmy Fallon and other celebrities, including Stephen Curry and the Jonas Brothers.
谷歌周二宣布，正筹备于 8 月 12 日在纽约市举行“Made by Google”新品发布会。我们希望今年的活动能比去年少一些尴尬——去年的发布会邀请了吉米·法伦（Jimmy Fallon）以及斯蒂芬·库里（Stephen Curry）和乔纳斯兄弟（Jonas Brothers）等名人助阵。

There are numerous rumors circulating about the upcoming Pixel 11 lineup. According to email invites shared by outlets like The Verge and Bloomberg, the new devices are expected to feature design upgrades, particularly a new gold color variant for the Pixel 11.
关于即将推出的 Pixel 11 系列，目前已有大量传闻流出。据 The Verge 和彭博社等媒体分享的邮件邀请函显示，新设备预计将迎来设计升级，尤其是 Pixel 11 将新增一款金色版本。

Other leaks suggest that the base Pixel 11 may come with slimmer bezels and a sleek black camera bar, while the Pixel 11 Pro is rumored to be slightly thinner than its predecessor. Additionally, there is speculation about the Pixel 11 Pro Fold, which might have a redesigned camera bump and a lighter profile compared to the previous model.
其他泄露信息显示，基础款 Pixel 11 可能会采用更窄的边框和时尚的黑色摄像头条，而传闻称 Pixel 11 Pro 将比前代产品更薄。此外，还有关于 Pixel 11 Pro Fold 的猜测，该机型可能会采用重新设计的摄像头凸起，并比上一代产品更轻薄。

On a negative note, one report suggests that Google may skip the 128GB option for the new models, starting instead with 256GB, which could lead to a higher price tag.
从负面消息来看，有报道称谷歌可能会取消新机型的 128GB 版本，直接从 256GB 起步，这可能会导致售价上涨。

Last year, the event took place on August 20, where Google announced the Pixel 10 series, AI-powered upgrades, and other devices, including a new foldable, the Pixel Watch 4, and the second generation of its budget-friendly A-Series earbuds.
去年的发布会于 8 月 20 日举行，谷歌当时发布了 Pixel 10 系列、AI 功能升级以及其他设备，包括一款新的折叠屏手机、Pixel Watch 4 以及第二代高性价比 A 系列耳机。
