# Reliability-Aware Monocular Depth Supervision for Sparse-View Neural Reconstruction
# 面向稀疏视角神经重建的可靠性感知单目深度监督

Sparse-view neural reconstruction is challenging in outdoor driving scenes, where cameras usually move along a narrow forward-facing trajectory and provide limited multi-view overlap. Although monocular depth estimators can provide dense geometric priors, their predictions are noisy, and not uniformly reliable across image regions.

在户外驾驶场景中，稀疏视角神经重建面临巨大挑战，因为摄像机通常沿狭窄的前向轨迹移动，且提供的多视角重叠有限。尽管单目深度估计器可以提供密集的几何先验，但其预测结果往往存在噪声，且在图像不同区域的可靠性并不均匀。

In this work, we study monocular depth supervision for sparse-view neural reconstruction. We use Depth Anything V2 as a dense monocular depth prior, align its predictions to metric depth using scale-shift fitting, and apply depth supervision selectively through photometric masks generated from an RGB-only baseline model.

在这项工作中，我们研究了用于稀疏视角神经重建的单目深度监督方法。我们使用 Depth Anything V2 作为密集单目深度先验，通过尺度-平移拟合（scale-shift fitting）将其预测结果对齐到度量深度，并利用仅基于 RGB 的基准模型生成的测光掩码（photometric masks）来选择性地应用深度监督。

We evaluate this strategy on two representative scene representations: Mip-NeRF-360 and Splatfacto. On KITTISeq02 under an every2 sparse-view setting, masked monocular depth supervision gives only marginal rendering gains for Mip-NeRF-360 and does not improve metric geometry. In contrast, Splatfacto benefits more clearly, improving PSNR from 14.903 to 15.932 and reducing RMSE from 0.542 to 0.100.

我们在两种代表性的场景表示方法上评估了该策略：Mip-NeRF-360 和 Splatfacto。在 KITTISeq02 数据集及 every2 稀疏视角设置下，掩码单目深度监督对 Mip-NeRF-360 的渲染效果提升微乎其微，且未能改善度量几何精度。相比之下，Splatfacto 受益更为显著，其 PSNR 从 14.903 提升至 15.932，RMSE 从 0.542 降低至 0.100。

Additional KITTISeq05 experiments and matched-ratio mask ablations further show that the gains for Splatfacto come from selecting reliable low-error regions rather than simply reducing the number of depth-supervised pixels. Additional experiments on the Bicycle scene show that depth supervision can improve geometry while hurting RGB rendering quality when multi-view coverage is already strong.

额外的 KITTISeq05 实验和匹配比例掩码消融研究进一步表明，Splatfacto 的性能提升源于对可靠的低误差区域的选择，而非仅仅减少了深度监督像素的数量。在 Bicycle 场景上的补充实验显示，当多视角覆盖已经很充分时，深度监督虽然能改善几何结构，但可能会损害 RGB 渲染质量。

Overall, our results suggest that monocular depth priors are useful for under-constrained sparse-view reconstruction, but should be applied selectively and with moderate weighting.

总的来说，我们的研究结果表明，单目深度先验对于欠约束的稀疏视角重建非常有用，但应采取选择性应用并配合适度的权重。
