# How many labels do you need? A decision framework for cross-habitat marine species recognition
# 你需要多少标签？跨栖息地海洋物种识别的决策框架

**Abstract:** Automated image recognition is increasingly used to scale ecological monitoring beyond manual annotation, yet ecologists lack evidence-based guidance on how much labelling effort reliable deployment at new sites requires. We present a decision framework quantifying the trade-off between labelling effort and recognition accuracy when transferring vision systems across marine habitats.

**摘要：** 自动图像识别正越来越多地被用于扩展生态监测，以超越人工标注的局限，但生态学家目前缺乏基于证据的指导，无法确定在新的地点进行可靠部署需要多少标注工作量。我们提出了一个决策框架，量化了在不同海洋栖息地之间迁移视觉系统时，标注工作量与识别准确率之间的权衡。

The benchmark spans five datasets, three oceans, and three taxonomic groups (fish, corals, invertebrates), from tropical reefs in the Great Barrier Reef and French Polynesia to a temperate Danish fjord. We evaluated four recognition models (DINOv2, CLIP, ResNet-50, EfficientNet-B4) under four adaptation strategies (linear probing, LoRA, Visual Prompt Tuning, full fine-tuning) across three protocols: within-habitat transfer across 20 reef sites (240 runs), cross-dataset geographic transfer along a difficulty gradient (40 runs), and few-shot adaptation curves with 0-100 labelled samples per class (648 runs).

该基准测试涵盖了五个数据集、三大洋和三个分类群（鱼类、珊瑚、无脊椎动物），范围从大堡礁和法属波利尼西亚的热带珊瑚礁到丹麦的温带峡湾。我们评估了四种识别模型（DINOv2、CLIP、ResNet-50、EfficientNet-B4）在四种适应策略（线性探测、LoRA、视觉提示微调、全参数微调）下的表现，并采用了三种协议：跨 20 个珊瑚礁地点的栖息地内迁移（240 次运行）、沿难度梯度的跨数据集地理迁移（40 次运行），以及每类 0-100 个标注样本的小样本适应曲线（648 次运行）。

Frozen self-supervised foundation features (DINOv2 + linear classifier, 1,538 trainable parameters) generalised to unseen reef sites at least as well as fully fine-tuned convolutional baselines four orders of magnitude larger; they learned species-diagnostic, habitat-invariant representations, whereas baselines encoded habitat-specific shortcuts that fail at new sites. As few as 10-20 labelled images per species sufficed to deploy reliable recognition at a new site, cutting annotation effort by roughly an order of magnitude.

冻结的自监督基础特征（DINOv2 + 线性分类器，1,538 个可训练参数）在未见过的珊瑚礁地点的泛化能力，至少与规模大四个数量级的全微调卷积基准模型相当；它们学习到了物种诊断性且与栖息地无关的表征，而基准模型则编码了在特定栖息地有效的“捷径”，这些捷径在新的地点往往会失效。每个物种仅需 10-20 张标注图像就足以在新的地点部署可靠的识别系统，从而将标注工作量减少了约一个数量级。

**Solution:** Programmes expanding to new sites can deploy reliable recognition by pairing a frozen, open foundation model (DINOv2) with a simple linear classifier and annotating only 10-20 images per species - roughly 1-4 hours per site. The framework lets programmes budget labelling effort against expected accuracy across sites, ecosystems, and platforms.

**解决方案：** 扩展到新地点的项目可以通过将冻结的开源基础模型（DINOv2）与简单的线性分类器配对，并仅为每个物种标注 10-20 张图像（每个地点大约耗时 1-4 小时）来部署可靠的识别系统。该框架使项目能够根据不同地点、生态系统和平台，在标注工作量与预期准确率之间进行预算规划。
