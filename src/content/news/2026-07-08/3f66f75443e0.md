# A Granularity-Aware EEG Feature Framework for Psychopathology Dimension Prediction
# 一种用于精神病理学维度预测的粒度感知脑电图（EEG）特征框架

**Abstract:** Electroencephalography (EEG) offers a noninvasive approach for examining neurophysiological correlates of dimensional psychopathology, yet systematic evidence across EEG paradigms and feature granularities remains limited.
**摘要：** 脑电图（EEG）为研究维度精神病理学的神经生理学相关性提供了一种非侵入性方法，然而，跨 EEG 范式和特征粒度的系统性证据仍然有限。

Here, we develop a granularity-aware EEG feature pipeline that organizes multi-scale descriptors into global, regional, and channel levels. Using the Healthy Brain Network (HBN) cohort, we evaluate the prediction of four psychopathology dimensions: p-factor, internalizing, externalizing, and attention problems, across four EEG paradigms.
在此，我们开发了一种粒度感知的 EEG 特征流水线，将多尺度描述符组织为全局、区域和通道三个层级。利用“健康大脑网络”（HBN）队列，我们评估了在四种 EEG 范式下对四种精神病理学维度的预测效果：p 因子（p-factor）、内化问题、外化问题以及注意力问题。

Given the heterogeneity of pediatric psychopathology and the moderate reliability of questionnaire-derived scores, this setting represents a challenging feasibility test rather than a clinical screening scenario. Tree-based models and granularity-balanced feature selection showed promising improvements over conventional approaches in selected conditions, although effect sizes remained modest.
鉴于儿童精神病理学的异质性以及问卷得分的中等可靠性，该设置代表了一项具有挑战性的可行性测试，而非临床筛查场景。在特定条件下，基于树的模型和粒度平衡特征选择方法相比传统方法显示出显著的改进，尽管效应量仍然较为温和。

Visualization of selected markers revealed dimension-specific spatial and spectral patterns that were broadly aligned with existing neurophysiological knowledge. An exploratory cross-dataset sanity check on the independent PEARL cohort suggested that the proposed selection principle remains technically feasible under protocol shifts, without claiming cross-dataset generalizability.
对所选标记物的可视化揭示了维度特异性的空间和频谱模式，这些模式与现有的神经生理学知识大体一致。在独立的 PEARL 队列上进行的探索性跨数据集一致性检查表明，所提出的选择原则在协议发生变化时在技术上仍然可行，但该研究并未声称具备跨数据集的通用性。

Overall, multi-scale EEG features contain weak but detectable signals related to dimensional psychopathology, and granularity-aware selection may serve as a useful feature-reduction strategy for future EEG-based phenotyping studies.
总而言之，多尺度 EEG 特征包含与维度精神病理学相关的微弱但可检测的信号，粒度感知选择可作为未来基于 EEG 的表型研究中一种有用的特征降维策略。
