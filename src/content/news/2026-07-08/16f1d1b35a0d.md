# Evaluating Time Series Foundation Models for Electricity Price Forecasting: Contamination Risk, Distributional Shifts, and Covariate Dependence
# 评估用于电力价格预测的时间序列基础模型：污染风险、分布偏移与协变量依赖

**Abstract:** Time series foundation models (TSFMs) have shown strong zero-shot forecasting performance, but their generalization in covariate-driven, non-stationary settings is underexplored.
**摘要：** 时间序列基础模型（TSFMs）在零样本预测任务中展现出了强大的性能，但它们在以协变量驱动的非平稳环境下的泛化能力尚未得到充分研究。

Electricity price forecasting (EPF) presents a challenging testbed due to complex temporal dependencies, distributional shifts, and strong reliance on structural and contextual information.
电力价格预测（EPF）由于其复杂的时间依赖性、分布偏移以及对结构化和上下文信息的强依赖性，构成了一个极具挑战性的测试平台。

We propose a two-dataset-benchmarking framework for EPF to mitigate contamination risk and enable fair evaluation of TSFMs.
我们为 EPF 提出了一种双数据集基准测试框架，旨在降低数据污染风险，并实现对 TSFMs 的公平评估。

We examine key aspects of EPF including point and probabilistic forecasting performance, tail behavior, price spikes, and comparisons against domain-specific methods.
我们考察了 EPF 的关键方面，包括点预测和概率预测性能、尾部行为、价格峰值，以及与领域特定方法的对比。

We find that TSFMs are highly competitive and often outperform general-purpose baselines.
研究发现，TSFMs 具有极强的竞争力，且往往优于通用基准模型。

Yet, their performance depends critically on covariate support, and they do not consistently surpass domain-specific methods tailored to EPF.
然而，它们的性能在很大程度上取决于协变量的支持，且并不总是能超越专为 EPF 设计的领域特定方法。

Interestingly, simple ensembles of TSFMs and domain-specific methods appear to have significant potential, suggesting that the two approaches capture complementary predictive information.
有趣的是，将 TSFMs 与领域特定方法进行简单集成似乎具有巨大的潜力，这表明这两种方法能够捕获互补的预测信息。
