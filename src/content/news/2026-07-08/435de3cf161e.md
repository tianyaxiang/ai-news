# Google announces Pixel 11 launch event in August
# 谷歌宣布将于 8 月举办 Pixel 11 发布会

Google is hosting its next Made by Google launch event for Pixel hardware on August 12th in New York City, according to an invitation sent by Google to The Verge. Unusually, the event is taking place in the evening: It’ll kick off at 6PM ET that day.
根据谷歌发给《The Verge》的邀请函，谷歌将于 8 月 12 日在纽约市举办下一场“Made by Google”硬件发布会。不同寻常的是，此次活动定于傍晚举行：发布会将于当天美东时间下午 6 点正式开始。

The email also includes a brief animation teasing what appears to be a phone in the Pixel 11 family that’s gold. Pixel 11 leaks that have popped up so far show that the base phone could have slimmer bezels than the Pixel 10 and a solid black camera bar. Leaks of Pixel 11 Pro indicate the phone could be a touch thinner than the Pixel 10 Pro. And the Pixel 11 Pro Fold could also be thinner than the previous model and might get a redesigned camera bump.
邮件中还包含一段简短的动画，预告了一款看起来属于 Pixel 11 系列的金色手机。目前出现的 Pixel 11 泄露信息显示，标准版手机的边框可能比 Pixel 10 更窄，并配有纯黑色的摄像头条。关于 Pixel 11 Pro 的泄露信息则表明，该机型可能比 Pixel 10 Pro 略薄。此外，Pixel 11 Pro Fold 也可能比前代产品更薄，并可能采用重新设计的摄像头凸起。

Last year’s Pixel 10 launch event — which featured Jimmy Fallon and a live studio audience — took place on August 20th, with the base and Pro model phones releasing shortly after on August 28th.
去年的 Pixel 10 发布会于 8 月 20 日举行，当时吉米·法伦（Jimmy Fallon）亲临现场并有演播室观众参与，标准版和 Pro 版手机随后于 8 月 28 日正式发售。
