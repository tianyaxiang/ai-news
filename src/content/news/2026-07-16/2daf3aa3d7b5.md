# 4 Best Personal Safety Alarms: Garmin, Sabre, & More (2026)
# 2026年四款最佳个人安全警报器：Garmin、Sabre 等

Not everyone feels at ease with self-defense tools that rely on physical confrontation, and I'm no exception. For many, myself included, personal safety alarms offer a straightforward alternative. These devices are especially helpful for families, whether it's a child signaling for help after getting separated in a crowded space or a parent needing a quick way to alert others in an emergency. But personal safety devices are useful for everyone, from solo joggers to commuters and hitchhikers.

并非每个人都能自在地使用依赖肢体冲突的自卫工具，我也不例外。对于许多人（包括我自己）来说，个人安全警报器提供了一种简单直接的替代方案。这些设备对家庭特别有帮助，无论是孩子在拥挤场所走散后发出求救信号，还是家长在紧急情况下需要快速提醒他人，都能派上用场。事实上，个人安全设备对每个人都有用，从独自慢跑者到通勤者和搭便车的人，皆是如此。

In this guide, I've rounded up some of the best personal safety alarms that I and WIRED staffers have tested. We also have a guide for the Best Personal Safety Apps, as well as a guide for apps that help people with disabilities travel more easily.

在本指南中，我汇总了我和《连线》（WIRED）员工测试过的一些最佳个人安全警报器。我们还提供了“最佳个人安全应用程序”指南，以及帮助残障人士更轻松出行的应用程序指南。

### How I Test Personal Safety Alarms
### 我如何测试个人安全警报器

I personally trigger every alarm and, when applicable, speak to the emergency responders. Here's how I evaluate these devices:
*   **Portability:** I look at the size and weight of each device, prioritizing discreet devices that are easy to carry.
*   **Sound:** Every portable alarm I choose produces between 120 and 140 decibels, which is loud enough to startle would-be assailants and alert bystanders in a crisis.
*   **Price:** I factor in up-front costs and recurring expenses, such as subscription fees or replacement batteries. I also test battery life against manufacturer's claims.
*   **Ease of use:** Safety alarms should be simple to operate in high-stress situations. I assess the user experience during setup and the accessibility of customer support.
*   **The extras:** I take stock of included features and test how well they perform compared to competitors. Some alarms include these extras at no additional charge, while other companies required paid upgrades; I keep this in mind.

我会亲自触发每一个警报器，并在适用时与紧急响应人员进行沟通。以下是我评估这些设备的方法：
*   **便携性：** 我会查看每台设备的尺寸和重量，优先选择易于携带且隐蔽的设备。
*   **声音：** 我选择的每一款便携式警报器都能产生 120 到 140 分贝的声音，这足以惊吓潜在的袭击者并在危机时刻提醒旁人。
*   **价格：** 我会将前期成本和经常性支出（如订阅费或更换电池的费用）纳入考量。我还会根据制造商的声明测试电池寿命。
*   **易用性：** 安全警报器在压力巨大的情况下应易于操作。我会评估设置过程中的用户体验以及客户支持的可及性。
*   **附加功能：** 我会盘点设备包含的功能，并测试其与竞争对手相比的表现。有些警报器免费提供这些附加功能，而另一些公司则需要付费升级；我会将这一点考虑在内。

### Best Overall: She’s Birdie (The Original)
### 最佳综合奖：She’s Birdie (初代款)

I used the original Birdie for over a year, and just knowing it was clipped to my keys gave me peace of mind when I walked home at night. This gadget is like carrying a fire alarm in your pocket. If you ever feel threatened, you simply pull the pin to activate a 130-decibel siren, accompanied by a flashing strobe light; keep the speaker uncovered and directed toward your target. It’s designed to create a diversion and attract the attention of anyone within earshot.

我使用初代 Birdie 超过一年，只要知道它挂在我的钥匙上，我在夜间步行回家时就会感到安心。这个小工具就像在口袋里装了一个火灾警报器。如果你感到受到威胁，只需拔出插销，它就会发出 130 分贝的警报声，并伴有闪烁的频闪灯；请确保扬声器未被遮挡并对准目标。它的设计目的是制造干扰并吸引周围人的注意。

### Best for Discreet Situations: InvisaWear Safety Wearables
### 最佳隐蔽场景奖：InvisaWear 安全穿戴设备

Backed by home security company ADT, InvisaWear offers a range of accessories, including keychains, bracelets, and necklaces. The functionalities require a $20 monthly subscription (iOS, Android). This includes 24/7 ADT access, in-app chat (clutch for situations when you can't make a phone call), a voice-activation option if you can't reach the button, and even virtual self-defense classes.

在家庭安防公司 ADT 的支持下，InvisaWear 提供了一系列配饰，包括钥匙扣、手链和项链。其功能需要每月 20 美元的订阅费（支持 iOS 和 Android）。这包括 24/7 全天候 ADT 接入、应用内聊天（在无法拨打电话的情况下非常关键）、无法触及按钮时的语音激活选项，甚至还有虚拟自卫课程。

### Best for Runners and Cyclists: Sabre 2-in-1 Clip-On Personal Alarm
### 最佳跑步者与骑行者奖：Sabre 二合一夹式个人警报器

Sabre is a trusted brand in personal safety, most notable for its line of pepper sprays and gels. The 2-in-1 clip-on stands out for outdoor runners and cyclists. It's weatherproof, and you can clip it onto your clothes, backpack, or bike. The LED light feature includes three modes (always on, slow flash, and fast flash) to keep you visible during nighttime walks, runs, and rides. If needed, you can press the button to trigger the 120-decibel siren that is audible up to 1,300 feet.

Sabre 是个人安全领域值得信赖的品牌，以其胡椒喷雾和凝胶系列而闻名。这款二合一夹式警报器在户外跑步者和骑行者中脱颖而出。它具有防风雨功能，你可以将其夹在衣服、背包或自行车上。LED 灯功能包括三种模式（常亮、慢闪和快闪），确保你在夜间步行、跑步和骑行时保持可见。如有需要，按下按钮即可触发 120 分贝的警报声，在 1,300 英尺（约 396 米）范围内均可听到。

### 最佳野外探险奖：Garmin inReach Mini 2

Most personal safety gadgets can't reach emergency services if you're out of cell range, which is a problem if you’re a frequent camper or hiker, or perhaps just someone who spends time off the grid. For a reliable connection wherever you are—even in remote wilderness or on the open sea—WIRED loves the Garmin inReach Mini 2.

大多数个人安全小工具在超出手机信号覆盖范围时无法联系紧急服务，如果你经常露营、徒步，或者只是喜欢远离网络生活，这会是个问题。无论你身在何处——即使是在偏远的荒野或公海上——为了获得可靠的连接，《连线》杂志都非常推崇 Garmin inReach Mini 2。
