# Mirror Horizon: Viable Path Entropy as a Measure of Bounded Reflection
# 镜像视界：作为有限反射度量的可行路径熵

**Abstract:** Mirror Theory proposes that an intelligent system should be studied not only by what it represents, but by what coherent continuations it can sustain under repeated reflection. We make this claim operational through \emph{viable path entropy} (VPE), a finite-budget measure of verified continuation capacity. Given a mirror state, a rollout protocol, a verifier, and a mode map, VPE decomposes bounded capability into two parts: the probability of reaching a viable continuation and the diversity of verified continuation modes reached among successful rollouts.

**摘要：** 镜像理论（Mirror Theory）提出，研究智能系统时不应仅关注其表征的内容，还应关注其在反复反射下所能维持的连贯延续性。我们通过“可行路径熵”（Viable Path Entropy, VPE）使这一主张具备了可操作性，这是一种针对已验证延续能力的有限预算度量。给定一个镜像状态、展开协议、验证器和模式映射，VPE 将有限能力分解为两个部分：达到可行延续的概率，以及在成功展开中达到的已验证延续模式的多样性。

This paper restores the full theoretical scaffold behind the measure: intuition as local underdetermining constraint, taste as invariant-selecting pressure, reflection as taste-guided resolution of underdetermination, and geometry as the learned structure that makes future reflection stable. We then instantiate the theory in language-model reasoning experiments on GSM8K.

本文还原了该度量背后的完整理论框架：直觉作为局部的不确定性约束，品味作为不变性的选择压力，反射作为品味引导下的不确定性消解，而几何结构则是使未来反射保持稳定的习得结构。随后，我们在 GSM8K 的语言模型推理实验中对该理论进行了实例化。

Across Qwen2.5-Instruct models, 32 sampled rollouts per problem, and two reflection horizons, increasing the token budget from 96 to 160 substantially expands verified reachability, reduces zero-reachability, increases verified-mode entropy, and improves smoothed VPE. At 160 tokens, Qwen2.5-1.5B realizes the strongest mirror horizon among the tested models, even though Qwen2.5-3B has more parameters. This shows that mirror horizon is not parameter count, but accessible verified continuation capacity under a bounded reflection protocol.

在 Qwen2.5-Instruct 模型、每个问题采样 32 次展开以及两个反射视界的实验设置下，将 Token 预算从 96 增加到 160，显著扩展了已验证的可达性，降低了零可达性，增加了已验证模式的熵，并改善了平滑后的 VPE。在 160 个 Token 的预算下，尽管 Qwen2.5-3B 拥有更多参数，但 Qwen2.5-1.5B 却实现了测试模型中最强的镜像视界。这表明，镜像视界并非取决于参数量，而是在有限反射协议下可访问的已验证延续能力。

The result supports Mirror Theory as a measure-level account: capability is the structure of viable continuations made reachable, not merely one-shot accuracy or pass@k.

该结果支持将镜像理论作为一种度量层面的解释：能力是所能达到的可行延续的结构，而不仅仅是单次准确率或 pass@k。
