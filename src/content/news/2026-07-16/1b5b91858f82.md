# Samsung shows off ‘brand new shape’ for Z Fold 8 in Spider-Man teaser
# 三星在《蜘蛛侠》预告片中展示 Galaxy Z Fold 8 的“全新外形”

Samsung gave a sneak peek of the “brand new shape” for its upcoming Galaxy Z Fold 8 in a new teaser for *Spider-Man: Brand New Day*. The video includes a few shots of Spidey taking a foldable phone off a 3D printer and opening it book-style, but each shot is heavily obscured by lens flares. This is likely the redesigned Galaxy Z Fold 8, which is rumored to have a wider, passport-like shape.

三星在《蜘蛛侠：全新的一天》（Spider-Man: Brand New Day）的最新预告片中，抢先展示了其即将推出的 Galaxy Z Fold 8 的“全新外形”。视频中出现了几个镜头，展示了蜘蛛侠从 3D 打印机中取出一款折叠手机并像书本一样将其打开，但每个镜头都被强烈的镜头光晕所遮挡。这很可能就是经过重新设计的 Galaxy Z Fold 8，传闻称其拥有更宽、类似护照的机身比例。

Apple is also rumored to be readying a foldable iPhone with a similar design, which could launch later this year. Samsung’s expected to announce the wider Galaxy Z Fold 8, and the rest of its new lineup of devices, at a Galaxy Unpacked event on July 22nd.

据传苹果也在准备一款采用类似设计的折叠屏 iPhone，可能会在今年晚些时候发布。三星预计将于 7 月 22 日举行的 Galaxy Unpacked 发布会上正式公布这款更宽的 Galaxy Z Fold 8 以及其他系列新品。

As 9to5Google reports, the Spider-Man teaser also appears to include the Galaxy Watch Ultra 2, Galaxy Z Flip 8, and Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra. These are all shown clearly without any of the cinematic lens flares, but they’re difficult to tell apart from Samsung’s current slate of devices — the flip phone shown in the teaser appears identical to the current Z Flip 7.

据 9to5Google 报道，这段《蜘蛛侠》预告片似乎还展示了 Galaxy Watch Ultra 2、Galaxy Z Flip 8 和 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra。这些设备在镜头中清晰可见，没有受到电影级光晕的影响，但它们与三星现有的设备系列很难区分——预告片中出现的翻盖手机看起来与目前的 Z Flip 7 完全一致。
