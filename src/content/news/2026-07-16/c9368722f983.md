# How Query Visibility Changes KV-Cache Compression Rankings: A Matched-Budget Audit
# 查询可见性如何改变 KV-Cache 压缩排名：一项匹配预算审计

**Abstract:** KV-cache compression methods are predominantly evaluated with the query appended to the context before compression -- a query-aware protocol. Yet the economic case for a compressed KV cache is reuse: compress a document once, answer many future questions against it. In that deployment, compression must happen query-agnostic -- before any question is seen.

**摘要：** KV-Cache 压缩方法通常在压缩前将查询（Query）附加到上下文中进行评估，这是一种“查询感知”（query-aware）协议。然而，压缩 KV-Cache 的经济价值在于复用：即对文档进行一次压缩，随后针对该文档回答多个未来问题。在这种部署场景下，压缩必须在“查询不可知”（query-agnostic）的情况下进行，即在看到任何问题之前就完成压缩。

We present a matched-budget audit of six published compression methods against three trivial baselines on three open 7-9B models (144,300 paired evaluations on RULER-8192; 40,800 on LongBench; 50,000-resample paired bootstrap throughout). Everything is held fixed -- model, compression ratio, instances, decoding -- except the scoring rule.

我们对六种已发表的压缩方法与三种基础基准进行了匹配预算审计，测试对象为三种开源的 7-9B 模型（在 RULER-8192 上进行了 144,300 次配对评估，在 LongBench 上进行了 40,800 次评估，并全程进行了 50,000 次重采样配对自助法分析）。除了评分规则外，所有变量（模型、压缩比、实例、解码方式）均保持不变。

Three findings. (1) Query visibility changes the rankings: under the agnostic protocol, of the five audited methods that share a common attention backend, only KeyDiff beats a best-of-3 trivial baseline consistently (31 of 36 cells), and the most widely deployed method, SnapKV, loses to "keep the start and the recent window" on average (-0.066).

研究得出三项发现。（1）查询可见性会改变排名：在“查询不可知”协议下，在五个共享相同注意力后端的审计方法中，只有 KeyDiff 能持续优于“三选一”的基础基准（36 个测试单元中胜出 31 个）；而应用最广泛的 SnapKV 方法，其平均表现甚至不如“保留开头和最近窗口”的简单策略（-0.066）。

(2) The per-method drop between the two protocols is ordered consistently with how visible the question is to each method's scoring signal, legible in its source code: from Delta=+0.198 for SnapKV (the question sits inside its 64-token observation window) down to Delta=+0.011 for KeyDiff (its score contains no query term at all).

（2）两种协议之间各方法的性能下降幅度，与其评分信号对问题的可见程度呈一致性排序，这一点在其源代码中清晰可见：SnapKV 的性能降幅（Delta）高达 +0.198（因为问题位于其 64-token 的观察窗口内），而 KeyDiff 的降幅仅为 +0.011（因为其评分机制完全不包含查询项）。
