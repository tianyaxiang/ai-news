# Contrastive Joint-Embedding Prediction for Representation Learning in Structural MRI
# 用于结构性核磁共振成像表征学习的对比联合嵌入预测

**Abstract:** Self-supervised learning offers a compelling approach for medical imaging, where labeled data are scarce and acquisition costs are high. We present COJEPA, a self-supervised framework for volumetric brain MRI that combines a joint-embedding predictive architecture (JEPA) with a contrastive loss (CO), targeting two complementary properties: local predictivity and global discriminability.

**摘要：** 自监督学习为医学影像领域提供了一种极具吸引力的方法，因为该领域往往面临标注数据稀缺且获取成本高昂的问题。我们提出了 COJEPA，这是一个针对体积脑部核磁共振成像（MRI）的自监督框架。该框架结合了联合嵌入预测架构（JEPA）与对比损失（CO），旨在实现两个互补的特性：局部预测能力和全局判别能力。

The model is trained without labels on T1-weighted structural MRI from two cohorts (HCP-YA and AABC, $N{=}2286$, ages 22 to 90), extending I-JEPA to 3D with foreground-aware block masking, a hierarchical convolutional patch embedding, and world-space sinusoidal positional encodings.

该模型在无需标签的情况下，利用来自两个队列（HCP-YA 和 AABC，共 2286 例，年龄跨度 22 至 90 岁）的 T1 加权结构性 MRI 数据进行训练。我们将 I-JEPA 扩展至 3D 领域，并引入了前景感知块掩码（foreground-aware block masking）、分层卷积补丁嵌入（hierarchical convolutional patch embedding）以及世界空间正弦位置编码（world-space sinusoidal positional encodings）。

We evaluate all three objectives across zero-shot twin retrieval, brain tumor segmentation (BraTS 2024), and age regression (OpenBHB). COJEPA achieves the best monozygotic twin recall at rank@1 (0.84), the best finetuning age MAE (2.55 years on OpenBHB 3.0T), and matches CO on BraTS whole-tumor Dice, demonstrating that the combined objective yields representations that are simultaneously discriminative and locally structured.

我们在零样本同卵双胞胎检索、脑肿瘤分割（BraTS 2024）和年龄回归（OpenBHB）三个任务上评估了所有目标函数。COJEPA 在 rank@1 的同卵双胞胎召回率上达到了最优（0.84），在年龄回归微调任务中实现了最佳的平均绝对误差（MAE 为 2.55 岁，基于 OpenBHB 3.0T 数据集），并在 BraTS 全肿瘤 Dice 系数上与对比学习（CO）表现持平。这证明了结合后的目标函数能够产生兼具判别性和局部结构性的表征。
