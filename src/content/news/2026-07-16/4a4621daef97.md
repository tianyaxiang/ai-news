# Anomalous Frame Detection Using VLM-Based Description Comparison for Extracting Expert-Specific Actions and Contextual Decision-Making Scenes with Intra-Video Self-Similarity
# 基于视觉语言模型描述比对的异常帧检测：利用视频内自相似性提取专家特定动作与情境决策场景

**Abstract:** Maintenance of critical infrastructures, such as railways and power plants, is essential for ensuring operational safety and reliability. However, the declining number of skilled maintenance workers highlights the need to transfer expert know-how to less experienced workers.
**摘要：** 铁路和发电厂等关键基础设施的维护对于确保运营安全和可靠性至关重要。然而，熟练维护人员数量的减少凸显了将专家技术诀窍传授给经验不足的工人的必要性。

Previous studies have attempted to extract candidates of expert knowledge by comparing videos of manual-based work with those of expert workers, mainly focusing on differences in observable actions. However, expert know-how is often embedded not only in actions but also in contextual decision-making during task execution.
以往的研究尝试通过对比人工操作视频与专家操作视频来提取专家知识候选，主要关注可观察动作的差异。然而，专家技术诀窍往往不仅体现在动作中，还体现在任务执行过程中的情境决策中。

This paper proposes a method that detects anomalous frames between two task videos to automatically extract candidate scenes containing expert-specific actions and contextual decision-making scenes. The method generates frame-wise visual descriptions using a vision-language model (VLM).
本文提出了一种检测两个任务视频间异常帧的方法，以自动提取包含专家特定动作和情境决策场景的候选片段。该方法利用视觉语言模型（VLM）生成逐帧的视觉描述。

Expert-specific actions are extracted based on frame similarities computed from description comparisons between two videos, while contextual decision-making scenes are extracted using segment similarities derived from intra-video self-similarity of the descriptions.
专家特定动作基于两个视频描述比对计算出的帧相似度进行提取，而情境决策场景则利用从描述的视频内自相似性导出的片段相似度进行提取。

In simulated distribution board maintenance experiments involving 27 task scenarios, the proposed method achieved extraction rates of 65% for action candidates and 61% for decision-scene candidates, improving over conventional methods that achieved 59% and 33%, respectively. These results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed approach in discovering candidate scenes containing expert know-how.
在涉及 27 个任务场景的模拟配电盘维护实验中，该方法在动作候选提取率上达到了 65%，在决策场景候选提取率上达到了 61%，优于传统方法（分别为 59% 和 33%）。这些结果证明了所提方法在发现包含专家技术诀窍的候选场景方面的有效性。
