# OpenAI's first branded hardware is... a light-up keyboard?
# OpenAI 的首款品牌硬件竟然是……一个发光键盘？

As rumors continue to swirl about OpenAI’s work on a personalized smart speaker and other hardware, the company is today rolling out its first branded device. The $230 Codex Micro is a specialized, RGB-lit mini-keyboard designed to let users monitor and quickly interact with multiple Codex agents with a glance and a few clicks.
尽管关于 OpenAI 正在研发个性化智能音箱及其他硬件的传闻不断，但该公司今天正式推出了其首款品牌设备。这款售价 230 美元的 Codex Micro 是一款专用的 RGB 背光迷你键盘，旨在让用户通过一眼扫视和几次点击，即可监控并快速与多个 Codex 智能体进行交互。

The device is described as a “limited-run collaboration” with Work Louder, which already sells a very similar-looking Creator Micro line of customizable square keyboards targeted at creative professionals. The Codex Micro differentiates itself from those mainly through six frosted keys in the top two rows, which offer color-coded live feedback on up to six Codex threads, even when they are not in focus on-screen.
该设备被描述为与 Work Louder 的“限量合作款”。Work Louder 此前已销售外观非常相似的 Creator Micro 系列定制化方形键盘，主要面向创意专业人士。Codex Micro 与这些产品的主要区别在于顶部两排的六个磨砂按键，它们能为多达六个 Codex 线程提供颜色编码的实时反馈，即使这些线程在屏幕上未处于激活状态。

Ideally, those colored keys will cycle from white when a thread is idle to blue when Codex is thinking to green when a task is complete. But the keys can also flash amber when Codex requires feedback or a decision from a human operator and red when a thread encounters an error, letting users know at a glance which of their Codex tasks needs immediate attention. A quick tap on the lit-up button brings up the applicable Codex window on-screen.
理想情况下，这些彩色按键会根据状态循环变色：线程空闲时为白色，Codex 思考时为蓝色，任务完成时为绿色。当 Codex 需要人工反馈或决策时，按键会闪烁琥珀色；当线程遇到错误时则会闪烁红色，让用户一眼就能看出哪个 Codex 任务需要立即处理。轻点亮起的按钮，即可在屏幕上调出相应的 Codex 窗口。

That kind of simplified, always-available thread monitoring could be handy for users who keep a half-open laptop to monitor their continually running AI agents. But this kind of physical keyboard attachment is, of course, primarily useful in a desktop setting, while ChatGPT’s mobile app can offer more detailed monitoring away from the desk.
这种简化且随时可用的线程监控功能，对于那些需要半开笔记本电脑来监控持续运行的 AI 智能体的用户来说非常方便。当然，这种物理键盘附件主要适用于桌面环境，而 ChatGPT 的移动应用程序则可以在离开办公桌时提供更详细的监控。

Below those frosted light-up buttons, the Codex Micro offers six buttons mapped by default to common Codex tasks like accepting and rejecting changes, branching threads, and a “push to talk” button for audio prompts. But those functions can be remapped through software and altered physically with one of 32 included keycaps. And while the first “layer” of customized functions is reserved for Codex, users can program and cycle through five other function sets for general computing shortcuts.
在这些磨砂发光按钮下方，Codex Micro 提供了六个默认映射到常见 Codex 任务的按钮，例如接受或拒绝更改、分支线程，以及用于语音提示的“一键通”按钮。这些功能可以通过软件重新映射，并使用随附的 32 个键帽进行物理更改。虽然第一层自定义功能是为 Codex 保留的，但用户还可以编程并循环切换另外五组功能，用于常规的电脑快捷操作。

While the Codex Micro is at heart simply a slightly modified version of existing third-party hardware, it features prominent OpenAI branding on the box and on the face of the device itself (along with a “You can just build things” tagline referencing OpenAI’s Super Bowl ad). The “limited run” partnership is also being described as accepting orders only “while supplies last,” suggesting that long-term availability is still up in the air.
虽然 Codex Micro 本质上只是现有第三方硬件的微调版本，但它在包装盒和设备正面都印有醒目的 OpenAI 品牌标识（以及引用 OpenAI 超级碗广告的标语“You can just build things”）。此次“限量”合作也被描述为“售完即止”，这表明其长期供应情况尚不确定。

Still, the launch of the first OpenAI-branded hardware highlights the company’s ambitions to expand beyond LLM-based software. Those ambitions have been apparent since at least 2023, when OpenAI reportedly started collaborating with former Apple design chief Jony Ive. Last May, OpenAI acquired Ive’s design firm LoveFrom, a partnership reportedly focused on a handheld, screenless device that accepts audio and visual input from around the user.
尽管如此，首款 OpenAI 品牌硬件的发布凸显了该公司超越大语言模型软件、向外扩张的雄心。这些雄心至少从 2023 年起就已显现，当时据报道 OpenAI 开始与前苹果设计主管 Jony Ive 合作。去年 5 月，OpenAI 收购了 Ive 的设计公司 LoveFrom，据称该合作专注于开发一款手持式、无屏幕设备，能够接收用户周围的音频和视觉输入。

Last October, the Financial Times reported that the collaborative design effort had run into unexpected technical and design hurdles that could delay the device’s reported plans for a 2026 launch. And just last week, Apple threw a potential wrench into OpenAI’s hardware plans with a lawsuit alleging the theft by an ex-Apple employee of trade secrets related to hardware manufacturing. The Codex Micro is taking orders now and expects to ship to users “shortly after purchase,” according to a spokesperson.
去年 10 月，《金融时报》报道称，这一合作设计工作遇到了意想不到的技术和设计障碍，可能会推迟该设备原定于 2026 年的发布计划。就在上周，苹果公司提起诉讼，指控一名前苹果员工窃取了与硬件制造相关的商业机密，这可能给 OpenAI 的硬件计划带来变数。据发言人称，Codex Micro 目前已开放订购，预计将在“购买后不久”发货。
