# Semidirect Fourier Delta Attention: Phase-Controlled Delta Memory with Constructive Chunk-WY Kernels
# 半直傅里叶 Delta 注意力：基于构造性 Chunk-WY 核的相位控制 Delta 记忆

Linear attention replaces softmax attention's growing KV cache with a fixed recurrent state, but this compression limits exact state tracking and long-context memory. We introduce \emph{Semidirect Fourier Delta Attention} (SFDA), a phase-controlled generalization of Kimi Delta Attention that replaces real diagonal decay with block-rotational Fourier control:
线性注意力机制用固定的循环状态取代了 Softmax 注意力中不断增长的 KV 缓存，但这种压缩限制了精确的状态跟踪和长上下文记忆能力。我们引入了“半直傅里叶 Delta 注意力”（Semidirect Fourier Delta Attention, SFDA），这是一种 Kimi Delta Attention 的相位控制推广版本，它用块旋转傅里叶控制取代了实数对角衰减：

\[ S_t=(I-\beta_t k_tk_t^*)\Lambda_tS_{t-1}+\beta_tk_tv_t^*, \qquad \Lambda_t=\diag(\alpha_t\odot e^{i\theta_t}). \]

Our main result is a constructive chunk-WY factorization for products \(A_t=\Lambda_t-u_tr_t^*\), giving
我们的主要成果是针对乘积 \(A_t=\Lambda_t-u_tr_t^*\) 的构造性 Chunk-WY 分解，从而得到：

\[ A_t\cdots A_1=\Gamma_t-Y_tM_tW_t^* \]

with rank growth bounded inside fixed chunks. This yields an exact affine chunk transfer, formal stability and complexity bounds, and a compact characterization of phase-plus-low-rank memory.
其中秩的增长被限制在固定的块（chunk）内。这实现了精确的仿射块传递、形式化的稳定性和复杂度界限，并对“相位+低秩”记忆给出了紧凑的表征。

We verify the algebra numerically and show in toy state-tracking experiments that SFDA learns cyclic memory where the phase-disabled KDA baseline remains near chance. Fused kernels and large-scale language-model comparisons are left to future work.
我们通过数值方法验证了该代数结构，并在简单的状态跟踪实验中表明，SFDA 能够学习循环记忆，而禁用了相位功能的 KDA 基准模型表现仅接近随机水平。融合核（Fused kernels）的实现及大规模语言模型对比留待未来工作完成。
