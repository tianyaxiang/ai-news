# SpikeDS: Dual Sparsity Spikformer for Perineural Invasion Prediction in 3D MRI
# SpikeDS：用于 3D MRI 神经周围浸润预测的双稀疏脉冲 Transformer

**Abstract:** Perineural invasion (PNI) is associated with poor prognosis in cholangiocarcinoma (CCA). However, its detection from 3D MRI remains challenging due to the subtle and spatially heterogeneous imaging signatures at the tumor periphery. Capturing such spatially sparse cues necessitates volumetric analysis of 3D MRI, but existing deep learning approaches incur prohibitive computational costs on volumetric medical images, limiting their clinical deployment.

**摘要：** 神经周围浸润 (PNI) 与胆管癌 (CCA) 的不良预后相关。然而，由于肿瘤外周成像特征的细微性和空间异质性，通过 3D MRI 检测 PNI 仍然具有挑战性。捕捉这些空间稀疏的线索需要对 3D MRI 进行体积分析，但现有的深度学习方法在处理体积医学图像时会产生高昂的计算成本，从而限制了其临床部署。

We propose Dual Sparsity Spikformer (SpikeDS), a spiking neural network architecture that jointly exploits activation sparsity from binary spike communication and spatial sparsity from window pruning based on firing rates. SpikeDS introduces Dual Sparsity Spiking Attention (DSSA), which combines two complementary mechanisms.

我们提出了双稀疏脉冲 Transformer (SpikeDS)，这是一种脉冲神经网络架构，它结合利用了来自二进制脉冲通信的激活稀疏性和基于发放率的窗口剪枝所带来的空间稀疏性。SpikeDS 引入了双稀疏脉冲注意力机制 (DSSA)，该机制结合了两种互补的机制。

The first is Window-based Expert Mixture Spiking Attention (W-EMSA), which selectively applies attention only to salient windows identified by their firing rates. The second is Cross-Window Spiking Self-Attention (CW-SSA), which enables global context exchange through an asymmetric scheme in which pruned windows still contribute as key-value sources.

第一种是基于窗口的专家混合脉冲注意力 (W-EMSA)，它仅选择性地对通过发放率识别出的显著窗口应用注意力。第二种是跨窗口脉冲自注意力 (CW-SSA)，它通过一种非对称方案实现全局上下文交换，其中被剪枝的窗口仍可作为键值 (key-value) 源做出贡献。

Evaluated on a clinical cohort of 139 CCA patients via 5-fold cross-validation, SpikeDS achieves an AUC of 0.753 while consuming only 14.4 mJ, surpassing the best baseline in both AUC and energy efficiency. These results suggest that dual sparsity provides an effective hardware-aware strategy for improving the efficiency of 3D spiking transformers without compromising diagnostic performance.

通过对 139 名 CCA 患者的临床队列进行 5 折交叉验证评估，SpikeDS 的 AUC 达到了 0.753，同时仅消耗 14.4 mJ 的能量，在 AUC 和能效方面均超过了最佳基准模型。这些结果表明，双稀疏性提供了一种有效的硬件感知策略，可以在不影响诊断性能的前提下提高 3D 脉冲 Transformer 的效率。
