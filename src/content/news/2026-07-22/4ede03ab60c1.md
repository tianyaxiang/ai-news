# Fully-sensorized smart-eyewear platform for on-device Machine Learning
# 用于端侧机器学习的全传感器智能眼镜平台

**Abstract:** This paper presents ARGO, a smart eyewear platform designed to bridge ergonomic comfort, high computational throughput, and energy efficiency. Unlike cloud-dependent solutions, ARGO leverages the STM32N6 microcontroller and its integrated Neural Processing Unit (NPU) to enable on-device machine learning, minimizing latency and preserving user privacy through local data processing.

**摘要：** 本文介绍了 ARGO，这是一个旨在兼顾人体工程学舒适度、高计算吞吐量和能源效率的智能眼镜平台。与依赖云端的解决方案不同，ARGO 利用 STM32N6 微控制器及其集成的神经网络处理单元（NPU）实现端侧机器学习，通过本地数据处理最大限度地减少延迟并保护用户隐私。

The primary contribution lies in the holistic co-design of hardware, firmware, and artificial intelligence, centered on the deployment of an optimized YOLOv11 model for real-time urban obstacle recognition. To ensure compatibility with the target NPU, we introduce Head-wise Parallel Attention (HPA), an architectural refinement that enables efficient accelerator execution while preserving the original computational logic.

其主要贡献在于硬件、固件和人工智能的整体协同设计，核心在于部署了一个经过优化的 YOLOv11 模型，用于实时城市障碍物识别。为了确保与目标 NPU 的兼容性，我们引入了“头部并行注意力机制”（Head-wise Parallel Attention, HPA），这是一种架构改进，能够在保持原始计算逻辑的同时实现高效的加速器执行。

The model is trained on the Walking On The Road (WOTR) dataset, and the final deployed configuration achieves an mAP50-95 of 24 under strict memory constraints, with a memory footprint of only 2.483 MB. The platform integrates a multimodal sensor suite, RGB cameras, Time-of-Flight sensors, microphones, and ambient sensors, and delivers 10 FPS at a continuous autonomy of ~113 minutes on a 200 mAh battery.

该模型在“行走在路上”（WOTR）数据集上进行训练，最终部署的配置在严格的内存限制下实现了 24 的 mAP50-95，内存占用仅为 2.483 MB。该平台集成了多模态传感器套件，包括 RGB 摄像头、飞行时间（ToF）传感器、麦克风和环境传感器，在 200 mAh 电池供电下可提供 10 FPS 的帧率，并实现约 113 分钟的持续续航。

These results demonstrate the feasibility of a high-performance, privacy-preserving, and socially acceptable assistive device, and highlight how competitive edge AI solutions increasingly demand tightly integrated, multidisciplinary co-design approaches.

这些结果证明了开发高性能、保护隐私且符合社会接受度的辅助设备的可行性，并强调了具有竞争力的边缘 AI 解决方案正日益需要紧密集成、多学科协同的设计方法。
