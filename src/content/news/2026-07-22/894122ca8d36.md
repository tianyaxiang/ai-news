# Nearly every Kindle is steeply discounted at Best Buy
# 百思买（Best Buy）几乎全线 Kindle 产品迎来大幅折扣

If you’ve been thinking about picking up a Kindle before school starts, or for your next vacation, now’s a good time to do it. As part of its Black Friday in July sale, Best Buy has brought the Kindle Paperwhite back down to its all-time low of $124.99 ($35 off). It’s the Kindle we recommend for most people because it has a bunch of features. It’s waterproof and features a sharp 300ppi, 7-inch display, it offers an adjustable warm light for reading at night, and it has great battery life that can last for more than a month on a single charge.

如果你正考虑在开学前或下一次度假前入手一台 Kindle，现在正是好时机。作为“七月黑色星期五”促销活动的一部分，百思买已将 Kindle Paperwhite 的价格降至 124.99 美元的历史最低点（优惠 35 美元）。这是我们向大多数人推荐的 Kindle 机型，因为它功能全面：它具备防水性能，配备清晰的 300ppi 7 英寸显示屏，提供可调节暖光以供夜间阅读，并且拥有出色的电池续航能力，单次充电可使用超过一个月。

Best Buy has also discounted nearly every other Kindle model. The Kindle Kids is a good deal even for adults. It’s down to $99.99 ($30 off), and includes the same hardware as the standard Kindle, but with some extras: a kid-friendly cover, no ads, a two-year worry-free guarantee, and six months of Amazon Kids Plus. If you read comics, the Kindle Colorsoft is also on sale starting at $159.99 ($90 off). It shares many of the Paperwhite’s strengths but adds a color E Ink display that displays color at up to 150ppi. The Kindle Colorsoft Signature Edition, which has 32GB of storage and wireless charging, is on sale for $219.99 ($60 off).

百思买还对几乎所有其他 Kindle 型号进行了打折。Kindle Kids 即便对成年人来说也是一笔划算的交易。它降价至 99.99 美元（优惠 30 美元），硬件配置与标准版 Kindle 相同，但额外附赠了儿童友好型保护套、无广告体验、两年无忧保修以及六个月的 Amazon Kids Plus 会员。如果你喜欢看漫画，Kindle Colorsoft 起售价也降至 159.99 美元（优惠 90 美元）。它保留了 Paperwhite 的许多优点，并增加了彩色电子墨水屏，色彩显示分辨率高达 150ppi。拥有 32GB 存储空间和无线充电功能的 Kindle Colorsoft 签名版（Signature Edition）现售价为 219.99 美元（优惠 60 美元）。

If you want an e-reader that doubles as a digital notebook, the Kindle Scribe and Kindle Scribe Colorsoft are worth considering. They’re on sale starting at $379.99 ($120 off) and $479.99 ($150 off), respectively. Each offers an 11-inch display with an included stylus for writing notes, annotating Kindle books, and marking documents. They also include notebook templates and AI-powered tools that can summarize notes or clean up messy handwriting. The biggest difference is that the Scribe Colorsoft adds a color E Ink display, making it a better choice for reviewing color documents or organizing notes with different colors.

如果你想要一台兼作数字笔记本的电子阅读器，Kindle Scribe 和 Kindle Scribe Colorsoft 值得考虑。它们分别降价至 379.99 美元（优惠 120 美元）和 479.99 美元（优惠 150 美元）起。每款设备都配备 11 英寸显示屏，并附带一支手写笔，用于记录笔记、标注 Kindle 书籍和标记文档。它们还内置了笔记本模板和人工智能工具，可以总结笔记或整理潦草的字迹。最大的区别在于 Scribe Colorsoft 增加了彩色电子墨水屏，使其成为查看彩色文档或用不同颜色整理笔记的更好选择。
