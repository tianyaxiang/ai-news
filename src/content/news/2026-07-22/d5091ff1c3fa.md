# GenSyn10: A Multi-Generative AI Dataset For Benchmarking Image Classification
# GenSyn10：用于图像分类基准测试的多生成式 AI 数据集

**Abstract:** The rapid advancement of generative AI has outpaced our ability to reliably detect its outputs, particularly when detectors encounter generators they have not seen before. We introduce GenSyn10, a CIFAR-10-aligned synthetic image dataset of 60,000 images (10 classes, 32$\times$32, 50k/10k split) generated using three architecturally diverse state-of-the-art models: FLUX.2-dev (Rectified Flow Transformer), HunyuanImage-3.0 (MoE Transformer), and Qwen-Image-2512 (Multimodal Diffusion Transformer), to advance research in AI-generated image detection.

**摘要：** 生成式 AI 的飞速发展已经超过了我们可靠检测其输出的能力，特别是在检测器遇到未曾见过的生成器时。我们推出了 GenSyn10，这是一个与 CIFAR-10 对齐的合成图像数据集，包含 60,000 张图像（10 个类别，32$\times$32 分辨率，50k/10k 划分）。该数据集使用三种架构各异的顶尖模型生成：FLUX.2-dev（Rectified Flow Transformer）、HunyuanImage-3.0（MoE Transformer）和 Qwen-Image-2512（多模态扩散 Transformer），旨在推动 AI 生成图像检测领域的研究。

A central challenge in this domain is that detectors perform well on known generators but degrade on unseen ones. GenSyn10 addresses this limitation by curating data from multiple contemporary architectures under a standardized generation protocol, enabling controlled and systematic evaluation of out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization to novel generators. Images are generated using a template-based prompt engine and downsampled to ensure consistency.

该领域的一个核心挑战在于，检测器在已知生成器上表现良好，但在未见过的生成器上性能会下降。GenSyn10 通过在标准化生成协议下从多种当代架构中整理数据，解决了这一局限性，从而能够对针对新型生成器的分布外（OOD）泛化能力进行受控且系统的评估。图像使用基于模板的提示引擎生成，并经过下采样以确保一致性。

We evaluate 17 image classification models under a four-stage protocol: real-data baseline, zero-shot transfer, fine-tuning, and retention. Despite a measurable domain gap, CIFAR-10-trained models achieve up to 96.86\% zero-shot accuracy on GenSyn10, increasing to 99.88\% after fine-tuning. In binary real-vs-synthetic classification, fine-tuned models achieve 97-99.9\% accuracy on seen generators but drop to 79-96\% on images from an unseen generator, highlighting persistent limitations in OOD generalization. These results establish GenSyn10 as a controlled benchmark for studying synthetic image detection beyond single-generator settings, supporting research on robustness, domain adaptation, and cross-generator generalization.

我们通过四个阶段的协议评估了 17 个图像分类模型：真实数据基准、零样本迁移、微调和保持。尽管存在可测量的领域差异，但在 CIFAR-10 上训练的模型在 GenSyn10 上实现了高达 96.86% 的零样本准确率，微调后提升至 99.88%。在二元“真实 vs 合成”分类任务中，微调后的模型在已知生成器上达到了 97-99.9% 的准确率，但在未见过的生成器图像上降至 79-96%，这凸显了 OOD 泛化中持续存在的局限性。这些结果确立了 GenSyn10 作为研究单生成器设置之外合成图像检测的受控基准，为鲁棒性、领域自适应和跨生成器泛化的研究提供了支持。
