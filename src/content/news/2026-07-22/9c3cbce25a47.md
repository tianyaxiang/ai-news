# 3D FaceShell: Attribute Transfer in 3D Face Avatars as a VLM Defense Mechanism
# 3D FaceShell：作为视觉语言模型（VLM）防御机制的 3D 面部头像属性迁移

Photorealistic 3D face avatars are increasingly deployed as reusable digital assets across applications such as telepresence, animation, and personalized media. At the same time, vision-language models (VLMs) can infer sensitive attributes from rendered images with open-ended semantic reasoning without any fine-tuning. This creates a new privacy challenge: once a 3D face avatar is shared, any of its renderings can be analyzed to extract high-level facial attributes.

逼真的 3D 面部头像正越来越多地作为可重用数字资产，部署在远程呈现、动画和个性化媒体等应用中。与此同时，视觉语言模型（VLM）无需任何微调，即可通过开放式语义推理从渲染图像中推断出敏感属性。这带来了一个新的隐私挑战：一旦 3D 面部头像被共享，其任何渲染图都可能被分析并提取出高层级的面部属性。

Existing defenses largely operate in 2D image space and do not address identity-preserving semantic manipulation of 3D facial representations. We propose 3D FaceShell, a framework for steering VLM interpretations of faces rendered from 3D models while preserving geometric fidelity and facial identity.

现有的防御措施大多在 2D 图像空间中运行，并未解决 3D 面部表征在保持身份特征前提下的语义操纵问题。我们提出了 3D FaceShell，这是一个旨在引导 VLM 对 3D 模型渲染面部进行解读的框架，同时能够保持几何保真度和面部身份特征。

3D FaceShell augments the original 3D representation with a learnable Gaussian shell that produces subtle, spatially distributed perturbations optimized through multi-view embedding alignment. The perturbations are designed to be visually inconspicuous yet sufficient to redirect VLM-based attribute inference in a view-consistent manner.

3D FaceShell 通过一个可学习的高斯外壳（Gaussian shell）增强了原始的 3D 表征，该外壳通过多视图嵌入对齐优化，产生细微且空间分布的扰动。这些扰动在设计上力求视觉不可见，但足以以视图一致的方式重定向基于 VLM 的属性推断。

Extensive experiments on reconstructed celebrity face avatars and multiple black-box VLMs demonstrate that 3D FaceShell significantly increases attribute injection and mismatch rates while maintaining high perceptual similarity and identity consistency. Our results show that it is possible to manipulate VLM-level semantic interpretation of 3D faces without compromising their human-recognizable appearance.

在重建的名人面部头像和多个黑盒 VLM 上进行的广泛实验表明，3D FaceShell 在保持高感知相似度和身份一致性的同时，显著提高了属性注入和失配率。我们的研究结果表明，在不损害人类可识别外观的前提下，操纵 3D 面部的 VLM 级语义解读是可行的。
