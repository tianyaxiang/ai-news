# Moving Like a Human: Ego-Motion-Normalized Temporal Signatures for Real-Time Aerial Person Tracking on Milliwatt-Class Hardware
# 像人类一样移动：用于毫瓦级硬件实时空中人员跟踪的自我运动归一化时间特征

**Abstract:** Follow-me person tracking must run on the drone itself, where affordable companion computers offer only a few effective int8 GFLOP/s. At typical follow distances a person spans 10-60 pixels, indistinguishable from clutter and beyond the reach of single-frame appearance detectors. The missing evidence is temporal and belongs in the input representation, computed analytically, rather than in learned temporal machinery.

**摘要：** “跟随模式”的人员跟踪必须在无人机本身上运行，而经济实惠的配套计算机仅能提供极少数有效的 int8 GFLOP/s（每秒十亿次浮点运算）。在典型的跟随距离下，一个人仅占 10-60 个像素，难以与背景杂波区分，超出了单帧外观检测器的能力范围。缺失的证据在于时间维度，且应属于输入表示的一部分，通过解析计算得出，而非依赖于学习型的时间处理机制。

EMTS-Det is a five-stage system that estimates ego-motion, converts each frame into ego-motion-normalized residual-motion channels, detects person centers with a 22k-parameter, 7.6-MFLOP network, tracks a locked target with a Kalman filter in stabilized coordinates, and verifies tracks with a 1-D convolutional classifier of human motion (ROC AUC 0.941). Training uses a synthetic-motion curriculum with motion channels generated by the deployed ego-motion code.

EMTS-Det 是一个五阶段系统，它能够估计自我运动（ego-motion），将每一帧转换为自我运动归一化的残差运动通道，利用一个 22k 参数、7.6-MFLOP 的网络检测人员中心，在稳定坐标系中使用卡尔曼滤波器跟踪锁定目标，并利用一维卷积人类运动分类器（ROC AUC 0.941）验证跟踪结果。训练过程采用了合成运动课程学习，其中运动通道由部署的自我运动代码生成。

Multi-seed ablations locate the value in generalization: on held-out VisDrone-DET a luminance-only variant collapses to 0.051 AP25 versus 0.415, as does YOLOv8n fine-tuned identically despite 1,100 times the compute, while the deployed int8 detector reaches 0.694 AP25 in-domain and 0.444 on this split. Temporal-shift modules lower accuracy, so the deployed detector is stateless. Silent int8 calibration failures are documented; min-max calibration with propagated caches matches float within 0.008 AP.

多种子消融实验验证了其泛化价值：在留出的 VisDrone-DET 数据集上，仅使用亮度信息的变体性能下降至 0.051 AP25（对比 0.415），同样经过微调的 YOLOv8n 在计算量为其 1100 倍的情况下也表现类似，而部署的 int8 检测器在域内达到 0.694 AP25，在该分割集上达到 0.444。时间偏移模块会降低准确率，因此部署的检测器是无状态的。文中记录了静默 int8 校准失败的情况；使用传播缓存的最小-最大值校准与浮点数精度差距在 0.008 AP 以内。

On a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W the pipeline runs at 31.85 FPS with 0.462 AP25 and 0.714 recall over 1,000 real-world UAV videos, versus 1.95 FPS and 0.172 AP25 for YOLOv8n. A 57-second field sequence shows auto-lock at 1.3 s, 97.9% lock recall, and recovery from all nine occlusions with zero false re-locks.

在 Raspberry Pi Zero 2W 上，该流水线在 1000 段真实无人机视频中以 31.85 FPS 的速度运行，AP25 为 0.462，召回率为 0.714；相比之下，YOLOv8n 仅为 1.95 FPS 和 0.172 AP25。一段 57 秒的实地测试序列显示，系统在 1.3 秒内实现自动锁定，锁定召回率为 97.9%，并成功从全部 9 次遮挡中恢复，且零误重锁。
