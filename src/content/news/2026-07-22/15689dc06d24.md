# 8 Best Smartwatches (2026): Apple, Google, and Hybrid Watches
# 2026年八款最佳智能手表：Apple、Google 及混合动力手表

A great timepiece does not just display the time of day. It can also elevate your outfit and make you feel good. The best smartwatches can do even more, from tracking your workouts and heart rate to serving as a notification hub and accessing voice assistants. While wearing a connected watch, you can leave your phone in your pocket and use your wrist for simple tasks.
一款出色的时计不仅能显示时间，还能提升你的穿搭品味，让你心情愉悦。而最好的智能手表功能远不止于此，从追踪锻炼和心率，到充当通知中心并调用语音助手，应有尽有。佩戴智能手表时，你可以将手机留在口袋里，直接通过手腕完成简单的任务。

Like many consumer gadgets today, smartwatches have gotten pricier, driven by several factors, including rising component costs and supply chain pressures. That said, the Apple Watch Series 11 remains my favorite for iPhone owners, but Google’s Pixel Watch 4 is the best smartwatch for Android users. I also like several other options—in various styles and with different levels of smarts.
与当今许多消费电子产品一样，受零部件成本上升和供应链压力等多种因素影响，智能手表的价格也水涨船高。尽管如此，Apple Watch Series 11 依然是我心目中 iPhone 用户的首选，而 Google Pixel Watch 4 则是 Android 用户的最佳选择。此外，我还看好其他几款风格各异、智能化程度不同的产品。

### Best Smartwatch for iPhone Users: Apple Watch Series 11
### iPhone 用户最佳智能手表：Apple Watch Series 11

The Series 11 is the top smartwatch for iPhone owners, mixing a nice blend of features for a reasonable price. That includes arguably the most important upgrade: all-day battery life; Apple has finally upgraded the 18-hour run time to a full 24 hours, and that's a game-changer. Now you can wear your watch to bed and track your sleep knowing it won't die in the middle of the night.
Series 11 是 iPhone 用户的首选智能手表，它以合理的价格提供了出色的功能组合。其中最重要的升级莫过于全天候电池续航：Apple 终于将 18 小时的续航时间提升到了完整的 24 小时，这彻底改变了使用体验。现在，你可以佩戴手表入睡并追踪睡眠，不必担心它会在半夜没电。

The second most notable addition is hypertension notifications. The CDC says nearly half the US adult population has high blood pressure, which increases the risk of a stroke or heart disease. It's FDA-cleared, and you'll need to wear the watch for 14 days before it starts delivering alerts. (You shouldn't use the Series 11 to diagnose hypertension, but you can double-check your results with your physician if there are trouble signs.) Apple has also added a Sleep Score that rates your sleep, and blood oxygen is officially back.
第二个值得注意的新增功能是高血压通知。据美国疾控中心（CDC）称，近一半的美国成年人患有高血压，这会增加中风或心脏病的风险。该功能已获得 FDA 认证，你需要佩戴手表 14 天后，它才会开始发送警报。（你不应使用 Series 11 来诊断高血压，但如果出现异常迹象，可以咨询医生进行复查。）Apple 还增加了用于评估睡眠质量的“睡眠评分”功能，血氧监测功能也正式回归。

### Best Smartwatch for Android Users: Google Pixel Watch 4
### Android 用户最佳智能手表：Google Pixel Watch 4

The Pixel Watch 4 is the first repairable smartwatch from Google, and that alone makes it easy to recommend. You can swap the battery and display when the time comes, and parts are available at iFixit. It also helps that this is one of the prettiest smartwatches on the market, aided by a new display that sits under the domed glass; it's gorgeous.
Pixel Watch 4 是 Google 首款可维修的智能手表，仅凭这一点就足以推荐。当需要时，你可以更换电池和显示屏，相关零件可在 iFixit 上购买。此外，它是目前市面上最漂亮的智能手表之一，得益于圆顶玻璃下方的全新显示屏，外观非常华丽。

Performance is slick with the new Qualcomm Snapdragon Wear W5 Gen 2 chipset powering the experience, and Google's Material 3 Expressive redesign really gives life to the interface and makes the smartwatch fun to use. The screen gets bright, the health and fitness features are impeccably accurate—especially the revamped sleep tracking—and there's even support for satellite messaging during emergencies (you need the cellular version of the watch for this).
得益于全新的高通骁龙 Wear W5 Gen 2 芯片组，其性能表现流畅。Google 的 Material 3 Expressive 设计语言为界面注入了活力，让这款手表用起来充满乐趣。屏幕亮度很高，健康和健身功能精准无误——尤其是改进后的睡眠追踪功能——甚至还支持紧急情况下的卫星通讯（需使用蜂窝网络版手表）。

The best new feature is Raise to Talk with Gemini. Just lift your wrist, and you can say anything to the voice assistant. That includes asking the weather or asking a question in the middle of a conversation with a friend, having the assistant start a workout, or even turning on the lights in your living room if you have a smart-home setup.
最棒的新功能是“抬腕即说”（Raise to Talk）的 Gemini 语音助手。只需抬起手腕，你就可以对语音助手下达任何指令。无论是询问天气、在与朋友交谈时提问、让助手开启锻炼模式，还是在拥有智能家居设备的情况下打开客厅灯光，都能轻松实现。
