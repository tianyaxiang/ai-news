### A Discreet Smart Lock / 一款低调的智能锁
**Level Lock Pro**

I am obsessed with this smart lock. The Level Lock Pro is a full-lock replacement, but it looks like you kept the original front of your lock and only swapped out the backing (which is also an option! More on invisible swaps below.) The inside even looks normal, unlike the massive, bulky backing most smart locks have. The only one who will know it's smart is you.
我非常迷恋这款智能锁。Level Lock Pro 是一款完整的锁具替代品，但它看起来就像你保留了锁具原本的正面，只更换了背面（这也是一种选择！下文会详细介绍这种隐形更换方式）。它的内部看起来也很正常，不像大多数智能锁那样拥有巨大且笨重的后盖。只有你自己知道它是智能的。

Even with such a small form, it's not lacking in features. You'll get lots of unlocking options, including NFC and Apple Home Key, plus Matter compatibility and a long battery life. You'll want to add Level's Connect Wi-Fi Bridge ($79) if you want to control it from afar.
尽管外形如此小巧，但它功能齐全。你将获得多种解锁选项，包括 NFC 和 Apple Home Key，此外还支持 Matter 协议，并拥有超长电池寿命。如果你想远程控制它，建议添加 Level 的 Connect Wi-Fi 网桥（79美元）。

### Fresh Bulbs / 焕新的灯泡
**Cync Smart LED Light Bulb, PAR38**

If you have light fixtures you want to remote-control, add an outdoor smart bulb. There are tons to choose from, and you can usually find one from any brand you already have at home. The only downside is that outdoor-rated smart bulbs are usually 4.75-inch-diameter PAR38-style bulbs, so they're best for downward-facing floodlights on your porch or balcony. They'll likely be too big to fit in a wall fixture as a replacement for a normal-sized bulb. Don't just grab any smart bulb—not all are outdoor-rated. Check for mentions of outdoor use and waterproof ratings to make sure they're safe to use.
如果你有想要远程控制的灯具，可以添加一个户外智能灯泡。市面上有海量选择，你通常可以从家里已有的品牌中找到一款。唯一的缺点是，户外级智能灯泡通常是 4.75 英寸直径的 PAR38 型灯泡，因此它们最适合用于门廊或阳台上的向下投射泛光灯。它们可能太大，无法放入壁灯中作为普通尺寸灯泡的替代品。不要随便买一个智能灯泡——并非所有灯泡都适合户外使用。请检查是否有关于户外使用和防水等级的说明，以确保使用安全。
