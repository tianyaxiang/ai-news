# What Makes Linguistic Representations Good Models of High-Level Visual Perception in the Human Brain?
### 是什么让语言表征成为人类大脑高层视觉感知的良好模型？

**Abstract:** Image descriptions represented with language models (LMs) predict human brain responses to naturalistic images in high-level visual regions, but the factors driving this predictivity remain unclear. To investigate this, we systematically studied how images are described and which language models are used to embed those descriptions.

**摘要：** 由语言模型（LM）表征的图像描述能够预测人类大脑在高层视觉区域对自然图像的反应，但驱动这种预测能力的因素尚不明确。为了探究这一点，我们系统地研究了图像是如何被描述的，以及使用了哪些语言模型来对这些描述进行嵌入（Embedding）。

For a common set of images, we considered six caption types -- including human-annotated and multiple machine-generated captions -- differing along several dimensions. Each caption was represented with five LMs, spanning autoregressive LMs trained to predict upcoming words and text embedders, i.e., LMs fine-tuned on semantic tasks requiring sentence/document-level representations.

针对一组通用图像，我们考虑了六种标题类型——包括人工标注和多种机器生成的标题——它们在多个维度上存在差异。每个标题由五个语言模型进行表征，涵盖了旨在预测后续词汇的自回归语言模型，以及文本嵌入器（即在需要句子/文档级表征的语义任务上进行微调的语言模型）。

Machine-generated captions yielded significant brain predictivity and alignment, often surpassing human-annotated captions used in previous work. Across caption types, text embedders consistently outperformed autoregressive LMs, a pattern replicated when measuring behavioural alignment with image-similarity judgments.

机器生成的标题产生了显著的大脑预测能力和一致性，往往超过了以往研究中使用的人工标注标题。在各种标题类型中，文本嵌入器的表现始终优于自回归语言模型；在测量与图像相似度判断的行为一致性时，也重复出现了这一模式。

Analyses of caption representations from different model layers further revealed that both brain predictivity and behavioural alignment peak at intermediate network depth, shortly after a point thought to mark the emergence of syntactic and semantic structure.

对不同模型层级的标题表征分析进一步揭示，大脑预测能力和行为一致性均在网络中间深度达到峰值，这一位置恰好出现在被认为标志着句法和语义结构出现的节点之后。

Altogether, our results demonstrate that both the content of image captions and the LM used to represent them influence brain- and behaviour-modelling performance, establishing caption embeddings as a useful tool for studying high-level visual perception.

总而言之，我们的研究结果表明，图像标题的内容以及用于表征它们的语言模型都会影响大脑和行为建模的性能，这确立了标题嵌入作为研究高层视觉感知的有效工具的地位。
