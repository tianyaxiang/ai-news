# The JEPA Predictor: A Transferable Operator for Occluded Feature Completion
# JEPA 预测器：一种用于遮挡特征补全的可迁移算子

**Abstract:** Joint-Embedding Predictive Architectures (JEPAs) train a predictor jointly with their encoder, but downstream deployment discards the predictor and reads features from the encoder alone. The predictor is, by construction, a learned operator from visible-context features to features at masked positions, the structure a partial-view classifier needs.

**摘要：** 联合嵌入预测架构（JEPAs）在训练时将预测器与编码器联合训练，但在下游部署时通常会丢弃预测器，仅从编码器读取特征。从结构上讲，该预测器是一个从可见上下文特征到掩码位置特征的学习算子，这正是局部视图分类器所需要的结构。

We show that this operator is portable across encoder families. We first establish that, at heavy mask, retaining the frozen predictor on a JEPA encoder substantially closes the accuracy gap against the strongest non-JEPA discriminative baselines.

我们证明了该算子可以在不同的编码器系列之间进行迁移。我们首先确定，在重度掩码情况下，在 JEPA 编码器上保留冻结的预测器可以显著缩小与最强非 JEPA 判别式基准模型之间的准确率差距。

We then bolt the frozen predictors of I-JEPA and V-JEPA 2 onto four non-JEPA hosts (CLIP, DINOv3, DINOv2, MAE) through a single linear projection between feature spaces, fit in closed form on 500 ImageNet-1k images. Across both ImageNet-9 and Stanford Dogs and across three mask fractions, the lift over each host's masked-encoder baseline grows monotonically with the mask fraction K in every host-donor pair.

随后，我们通过特征空间之间的单一线性投影，将 I-JEPA 和 V-JEPA 2 的冻结预测器“挂载”到四个非 JEPA 主机模型（CLIP、DINOv3、DINOv2、MAE）上，并在 500 张 ImageNet-1k 图像上以闭式解进行拟合。在 ImageNet-9 和 Stanford Dogs 数据集以及三种掩码比例下，每个主机-供体对在主机掩码编码器基准上的性能提升，均随掩码比例 K 的增加而单调增长。

CLIP paired with the I-JEPA predictor recovers most of the accuracy that masking removed on ImageNet-9 at heavy occlusion, and lifts fine-grained Stanford Dogs from 15.9% to 52.1% (+36 pp). The mechanism is identifiable: the projection pays a fixed cost on visible patches and the predictor provides a growing benefit on masked patches; the benefit dominates the heavy-occlusion regime.

与 I-JEPA 预测器配对的 CLIP 模型在 ImageNet-9 的重度遮挡下恢复了大部分因掩码而损失的准确率，并将细粒度 Stanford Dogs 数据集的准确率从 15.9% 提升至 52.1%（+36 个百分点）。其机制是明确的：投影在可见补丁上产生固定成本，而预测器在掩码补丁上提供不断增长的收益；这种收益在重度遮挡环境下占据主导地位。

At low K on fine-grained classification the projection cost exceeds the benefit, defining the boundary where the linear bridge breaks down. The frozen JEPA predictor functions as a portable operator for occluded feature completion across encoder families, requiring no retraining of either model while fitting matched linear probes per mask fraction.

在细粒度分类的低 K 值情况下，投影成本超过了收益，这定义了线性桥接失效的边界。冻结的 JEPA 预测器可作为一种跨编码器系列进行遮挡特征补全的可迁移算子，无需对任何模型进行重新训练，只需针对每个掩码比例拟合匹配的线性探针即可。
