China’s AI models have Trump’s AI world at war with itself. Last weekend, several current and former advisers to President Donald Trump on AI publicly lobbed insults at the country’s leading AI companies. David Sacks branded Anthropic’s models “lobotomized” and “woke.” Emil Michael, a top Pentagon official, called OpenAI’s new head of strategic futures a “supreme village idiot.” It began because no one can agree on what to do about Kimi, a free, open-source model that Chinese AI company Moonshot launched last week. Every time a new smart, free model from China gets released, US companies see less reason to fork out money for models from Anthropic or OpenAI.
