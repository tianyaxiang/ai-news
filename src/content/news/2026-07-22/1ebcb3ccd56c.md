# PCjs Machines

Welcome to PCjs. PCjs uses JavaScript to emulate a small collection of hardware and software that I grew up with in the 1970s and 1980s, allowing you to experience their slow CPUs, low-resolution displays, and primitive sound effects, all in the comfort and safety of your desktop or mobile web browser.
欢迎来到 PCjs。PCjs 使用 JavaScript 模拟了我成长于 20 世纪 70 年代和 80 年代时所接触的一系列硬件和软件，让你能够在桌面端或移动端网页浏览器中，舒适且安全地体验那些缓慢的 CPU、低分辨率显示器以及原始的音效。

Over time, PCjs emulations have expanded to include selected IBM PC Compatibles and more classic machines, such as Minicomputers, Programmable Calculators, Terminals, and Arcade Games. To learn more or contribute to these very modest preservation efforts, visit the PCjs open-source project on GitHub.
随着时间的推移，PCjs 的模拟范围已扩展至精选的 IBM PC 兼容机以及更多经典设备，例如小型机、可编程计算器、终端和街机游戏。若想了解更多信息或为这些微薄的保护工作做出贡献，请访问 GitHub 上的 PCjs 开源项目。
