# The Best NAS Devices for Your Home After Months of Testing
# 经过数月测试，这些是目前最适合家庭使用的 NAS 设备

Whether you want to back up all your precious files, make it easier to share and collaborate, or create your own entertainment cloud, network-attached storage (NAS) devices are the way. These versatile servers can boost your home network and help you offload tasks from your computer and other devices.

无论你是想备份所有珍贵的文件、更轻松地进行共享与协作，还是想创建属于自己的娱乐云，网络附属存储（NAS）设备都是最佳选择。这些多功能的服务器可以增强你的家庭网络，并帮助你分担电脑及其他设备的存储压力。

I’ve spent the last few months testing NAS devices from some of the top brands with a focus on families looking to create backups and maybe spin up an entertainment server to cut back on streaming subscriptions. These are the NAS devices I recommend.

在过去的几个月里，我测试了来自各大顶级品牌的 NAS 设备，重点关注那些希望创建备份，并可能通过搭建娱乐服务器来减少流媒体订阅费用的家庭用户。以下是我推荐的 NAS 设备。

***

### Synology DiskStation DS225+
### 群晖 DiskStation DS225+

While no longer undisputed, Synology is still the heavyweight champion of the NAS market, and this two-bay NAS is ideal for the average home. It is extremely fast, either topping the charts or coming close in my tests, and consistently hitting around 300 MB/s read and 250 MB/s write, though that drops to a little over 100 MB/s if your network is limited to 1 Gbps anywhere (router, port, cable, or switch).

虽然群晖（Synology）不再是唯一的霸主，但它依然是 NAS 市场的重量级冠军，而这款双盘位 NAS 非常适合普通家庭。它的速度极快，在我的测试中始终名列前茅或接近榜首，读取速度稳定在 300 MB/s 左右，写入速度约为 250 MB/s。不过，如果你的网络环境（路由器、端口、线缆或交换机）限制在 1 Gbps，速度会降至 100 MB/s 出头。

It has a generous array of ports (2.5-Gbps and 1-Gbps Ethernet ports and USB ports front and back). The excellent hardware includes a speedy Intel Celeron J4125 quad-core processor, 2 GB of RAM that can be upgraded to 6 GB, and space for two drives (up to 40 TB). It is compact and relatively quiet, with some minor fan noise when it’s busy. The screwless clip-on tray design makes it quick to insert drives.

它配备了丰富的接口（包括 2.5 Gbps 和 1 Gbps 以太网口，以及前后的 USB 接口）。其出色的硬件配置包括一颗快速的英特尔赛扬 J4125 四核处理器、可升级至 6 GB 的 2 GB 内存，以及两个硬盘位（最高支持 40 TB）。机身紧凑且相对安静，仅在满载时会有轻微的风扇声。免螺丝的卡扣式硬盘托架设计使得安装硬盘非常快捷。

Setup and configuration are simple and accessible. Synology’s software, through its web interface and mobile apps, is the most polished and user-friendly I’ve tested, and Synology offers the largest selection of apps (both its own polished offerings and third-party apps like Plex and Jellyfin). This NAS offers tons of flexibility for backups, works great as a media server, and offers a lot of extras, including VPN server and security camera support.

设置和配置简单易上手。群晖的软件（通过网页界面和移动应用）是我测试过最精致、最人性化的系统，且群晖提供了最丰富的应用选择（既有其自研的精品应用，也有 Plex 和 Jellyfin 等第三方应用）。这款 NAS 在备份方面提供了极大的灵活性，作为媒体服务器表现出色，并提供了许多额外功能，包括 VPN 服务器和安防摄像头支持。

***

### Synology BeeStation Plus
### 群晖 BeeStation Plus

For folks seeking a simple network backup that doesn’t require much thought or configuration, I also tested the all-in-one Synology BeeStation Plus, which comes with storage included, but no apps or additional features. It’s quick and easy to set up, works fine for automatic backups, and has a decent photo app that can recognize faces, but the performance is middling.

对于那些寻求简单网络备份、不想花费太多心思进行配置的用户，我还测试了群晖的一体化 BeeStation Plus。它自带存储空间，但没有应用商店或额外的功能。它的设置快速简单，自动备份功能运行良好，且拥有一个能识别面孔的不错照片应用，但整体性能表现平平。

***

### Ugreen NAS DH4300 Plus
### 绿联 NAS DH4300 Plus

A relative newcomer to the NAS market, Ugreen is better known for its charging gear and mobile storage, but this four-bay NAS is an impressive debut. For most people, the Ugreen NASync DH4300 Plus is a very affordable way to jump to four bays, potentially giving you up to 128 TB, though you must provide your own drives.

作为 NAS 市场的新晋选手，绿联（Ugreen）以充电设备和移动存储闻名，但这款四盘位 NAS 是一个令人印象深刻的开端。对于大多数人来说，绿联 NASync DH4300 Plus 是一种非常经济实惠的四盘位升级方案，最高可提供 128 TB 的存储空间，不过你需要自行购买硬盘。

It wasn’t especially fast in my tests, but after your initial setup, this is unlikely to be a big issue for most people, and it wasn’t too slow either. You can expect around 100 MB/s read/write speeds for a 1-Gbps network, but can push up to between 200 and 300 MB/s if your network gear supports it. It has a single 2.5-Gbps Ethernet port, but also two USB-A ports, one USB-C port, and an HDMI port.

在我的测试中，它的速度不算特别快，但对于大多数人来说，初始设置完成后，这通常不会成为大问题，而且它的速度也不算慢。在 1 Gbps 网络环境下，你可以获得约 100 MB/s 的读写速度；如果你的网络设备支持，速度可提升至 200 到 300 MB/s。它配有一个 2.5 Gbps 以太网口，以及两个 USB-A 接口、一个 USB-C 接口和一个 HDMI 接口。

***

### Asustor FS6706T
### 华芸 (Asustor) FS6706T

Asustor is a spin-off from Asus (short for Asus-storage), which also makes some of the best routers and mesh systems you can buy. This NAS could be an ideal choice for anyone seeking something for the TV. Resembling a set-top box, this svelte, six-bay NAS takes M.2 NVMe solid-state drives (SSDs) up to 24 TB. It is very fast.

华芸（Asustor）是华硕（Asus）旗下的存储品牌（Asus-storage 的缩写），华硕同时也生产市面上一些最好的路由器和 Mesh 系统。这款 NAS 对于那些想将其连接电视使用的用户来说是理想之选。它外观酷似机顶盒，这款纤薄的六盘位 NAS 支持 M.2 NVMe 固态硬盘（SSD），最高容量可达 24 TB。它的速度非常快。
