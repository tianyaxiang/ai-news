# IVR-R1: Refining Trajectories through Iterative Visual-Grounded Reasoning in Reinforcement Learning
# IVR-R1：通过强化学习中的迭代视觉基础推理优化轨迹

**Abstract:** Multimodal large language models via reinforcement learning (RL) have demonstrated remarkable capabilities in complex visual reasoning tasks, yet they remain limited in long-horizon multimodal scenarios, often suffering from visual hallucination and logical error.
**摘要：** 基于强化学习（RL）的多模态大语言模型在复杂的视觉推理任务中展现出了卓越的能力，但在长程多模态场景中仍存在局限性，经常出现视觉幻觉和逻辑错误。

Current methods typically pre-encode high-dimensional visual scenes into discrete textual proxies to facilitate downstream reasoning. As the reasoning chain unfolds, however, the inherent information asymmetry between text and visual scenes tends to erode visual grounding, resulting in misguided reasoning and erroneous outputs.
当前的方法通常将高维视觉场景预编码为离散的文本代理，以促进后续的推理。然而，随着推理链的展开，文本与视觉场景之间固有的信息不对称往往会削弱视觉基础（visual grounding），导致推理偏差和错误输出。

To address this issue, we introduce IVR-R1 (Iterative Visual-grounded Reasoning), a novel RL training framework that facilitates dynamic visual re-alignment that actively rectifies reasoning trajectories to guide policy optimization.
为了解决这一问题，我们引入了 IVR-R1（迭代视觉基础推理），这是一种新型的强化学习训练框架，它通过动态视觉重对齐来主动纠正推理轨迹，从而引导策略优化。

Specifically, by leveraging a reward-driven screening mechanism to identify flawed rollouts, IVR-R1 executes a fine-grained, step-level error attribution within the multimodal context. By iteratively cross-referencing intermediate reasoning states against pristine visual priors, a Re-Reasoning Loop enables automated trajectory rectification, effectively synthesizing expert-level demonstrations that serve as high-fidelity reasoning templates for the policy model.
具体而言，IVR-R1 利用奖励驱动的筛选机制来识别有缺陷的执行过程（rollouts），并在多模态上下文中执行细粒度的步骤级错误归因。通过将中间推理状态与原始视觉先验进行迭代交叉比对，重推理循环（Re-Reasoning Loop）实现了自动化的轨迹修正，有效地合成了专家级的演示，为策略模型提供了高保真的推理模板。

Our experiments across diverse multimodal benchmarks demonstrate that IVR-R1 consistently outperforms existing reinforcement learning methods, establishing a superior paradigm for maintaining logical and visual consistency in complex multimodal reasoning.
我们在多个多模态基准测试上的实验表明，IVR-R1 的表现始终优于现有的强化学习方法，为在复杂多模态推理中保持逻辑和视觉一致性建立了一种更优越的范式。
