# I've Tested Dozens of Packing Cubes. Here's What's Worth Taking on Your Next Trip
# 我测试了数十款收纳袋，以下是值得带上你下一次旅程的产品

Even though I'm a frequent traveler, I lived well over four decades of my life without thinking I needed packing cubes. I know how to pack, I thought. I can roll my things up tightly to fit in any carry-on suitcase or backpack. Are packing cubes really worth it? Why would I spend money on something simply to separate my workout pants and pajamas from my daily outfits?

尽管我经常旅行，但在过去四十多年里，我从未觉得需要使用收纳袋。我曾认为自己很会打包，可以将衣物紧紧卷起，塞进任何登机箱或背包中。收纳袋真的值得买吗？为什么要花钱买这种仅仅用来把运动裤和睡衣与日常服装隔开的东西呢？

Then I bought a set, and I will never go back. Packing cubes are often scoffed at by r/onebag minimalists and those who can live three weeks out of a backpack with four outfits and a toothbrush. To their point, regular packing cubes do add weight (usually less than a pound), but compressible styles, like the Eagle Creek Pack-It Reveal Carry-On Set ($104), Thule's Compression Packing Cube Set ($54), or Bagsmart Compression Cubes ($43), are a game changer for overpackers. Even noncompression versions like Beis' Packing Cubes ($68) have labels that will help you take your packing organization to the next level.

后来我买了一套，从此就再也离不开它了。Reddit“一包走天下”（r/onebag）的极简主义者，以及那些靠四套衣服和一把牙刷就能背着背包生活三周的人，往往对收纳袋嗤之以鼻。他们的观点有一定道理，普通的收纳袋确实会增加重量（通常不到一磅），但像 Eagle Creek Pack-It Reveal 登机套装（104美元）、Thule 压缩收纳袋套装（54美元）或 Bagsmart 压缩收纳袋（43美元）这类可压缩款式，对于行李较多的人来说简直是革命性的。即使是像 Beis 收纳袋（68美元）这样的非压缩版本，也带有标签，能帮助你将打包整理提升到一个新的水平。

Packing cubes are also critical for duffel bags and travel backpacks, where it’s harder to keep clothes folded and organized. They protect your things from dirt, damage, and sand. And they get your clothes to your destination with fewer wrinkles. They also provide privacy: If TSA decides to go through your stuff publicly right next to the security line (as happened to me one year when I packed an apparently suspicious-looking 20-ounce bottle of soda), they will only go through the offending cube instead of rifling through everything. You can also pack multiple family members’ clothes in the same suitcase using color-coordinated cubes. If you find you've overpacked your checked bag, it makes it easier to remove items to get it back to a proper weight.

收纳袋对于行李袋和旅行背包也至关重要，因为在这些包里，衣物很难保持折叠整齐。它们能保护你的物品免受污垢、损坏和沙子的侵扰，并能让衣物在到达目的地时减少褶皱。它们还能提供隐私保护：如果 TSA（美国运输安全管理局）决定在安检口旁公开检查你的行李（我有一年就遇到过，因为我带了一瓶看起来很可疑的 20 盎司汽水），他们只会检查那个有问题的收纳袋，而不是翻遍你所有的东西。你还可以通过颜色区分收纳袋，将多位家庭成员的衣物装在同一个行李箱中。如果你发现托运行李超重，使用收纳袋也能让你更轻松地取出物品，将重量控制在合理范围内。

Unpacking is also much quicker and easier with a set of cubes. You don’t even have to take your clothes out—you can put the packing cubes right in the hotel drawers. If you're ready to give the packing cube life a try, or upgrade a current set, check out my list of road-tested favorites for every situation.

使用收纳袋后，拆包也会变得更快、更简单。你甚至不需要把衣服拿出来，直接把收纳袋放进酒店抽屉即可。如果你准备尝试使用收纳袋，或者想升级现有的套装，请查看我这份针对各种情况、经过实地测试的推荐清单。

### Best Compression Packing Cubes: Eagle Creek Pack-It Reveal Carry-On Set
### 最佳压缩收纳袋：Eagle Creek Pack-It Reveal 登机套装

Eagle Creek produces a wide range of excellent travel accessories, but these packing cubes are particularly well done. They're made of thick, sturdy fabric; they are thoughtfully designed, with good compression and high-quality zipper pulls. I also like that there’s just enough mesh for breathability, but the bottom and back of the cube is still opaque for privacy. (If you want even more privacy, there’s a solid-color “isolate" set, of which we've also tested a small cube, and it's just as sturdy.) There are also separate sets for extended stays or slim shapes, or you can buy cubes individually. The large cube in this set is split for putting dirty clothes (or clean clothes) on one side—or you can buy it alone for $35. As a bonus, they're also water-resistant and come with a nice carry handle at the top.

Eagle Creek 生产各种出色的旅行配件，但这些收纳袋做得尤为出色。它们由厚实耐用的面料制成，设计周到，具有良好的压缩性能和高质量的拉链头。我特别喜欢它们适度的网眼设计，既保证了透气性，底部和背部又保持不透明以保护隐私。（如果你需要更高的隐私性，还有纯色的“Isolate”系列，我们也测试过其中的小号收纳袋，同样非常结实。）此外，还有针对长途旅行或细长形状的独立套装，你也可以单独购买。套装中的大号收纳袋被分隔开，可以一边放脏衣服（或干净衣服），你也可以花 35 美元单独购买。额外的好处是，它们具有防水功能，顶部还配有一个方便的提手。

### Best Packing Cubes for Carry-On Luggage: Thule Compression Packing Cube Set
### 最佳登机箱收纳袋：Thule 压缩收纳袋套装

These cubes, made by Swedish car rack stalwart Thule (pronounced TOO-lee, in case you were wondering), were a very, very close second to Eagle Creek's for the best compression cube set. The Thule cubes' rigid construction and small sizes meant they couldn't be used for longer trips or too many bulky items like sweaters, but they still provided a shocking amount of compression, making them ideal for carry-ons.

这些收纳袋由瑞典汽车行李架巨头 Thule（发音为 TOO-lee，以防你好奇）制造，在最佳压缩收纳袋评选中，它以极微弱的差距位居 Eagle Creek 之后。Thule 收纳袋坚固的结构和较小的尺寸意味着它们不适合长途旅行或装载太多像毛衣这样的大件物品，但它们依然提供了惊人的压缩效果，非常适合登机箱使用。

I wasn't sure the medium size would even fit one pair of pants, but it not only fit my bulkiest pair of sweats with ease, but compressed them down to a tidy little package. The Bluesign-approved 100-denier ripstop nylon, which is translucent enough to identify the cubes' contents but not see them in their entirety, was also a welcome touch, as was the fabric's water repellence.

我原本不确定中号尺寸是否能装下一条裤子，但它不仅轻松装下了我最厚的一条运动裤，还将其压缩成了一个整洁的小包裹。经 Bluesign 认证的 100 旦尼尔防撕裂尼龙材质既半透明，能让你识别袋内物品，又不会完全看清内部，这种设计很贴心，其面料的防水性能也同样出色。
