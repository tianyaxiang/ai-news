# A Large-Scale Dataset and Benchmark: Do Protein-Ligand Models Learn Binding Sites or Just Binding Likelihood?
# 大规模数据集与基准测试：蛋白质-配体模型究竟是学会了识别结合位点，还是仅仅学会了结合概率？

**Abstract:** Protein-ligand modeling underpins computational drug discovery and molecular design. Existing protein-ligand benchmarks typically evaluate whether a protein and ligand interact and how strongly they bind, through tasks such as binary binding prediction and affinity regression. However, these evaluations provide limited evidence of whether models can localize binding sites or identify the non-covalent interactions underlying molecular recognition.

**摘要：** 蛋白质-配体建模是计算药物发现和分子设计的基石。现有的蛋白质-配体基准测试通常通过二元结合预测和亲和力回归等任务，来评估蛋白质与配体之间是否存在相互作用及其结合强度。然而，这些评估方法提供的证据有限，无法证明模型是否真正能够定位结合位点，或识别分子识别背后的非共价相互作用。

To address this gap, we introduce InteractBind, a large-scale protein-ligand dataset comprising approximately 100k protein-ligand pairs, together with a benchmark for fine-grained evaluation. The core fine-grained task is that of binding-site localization, which uses protein-residue and ligand-atom interaction maps spanning six major types of non-covalent interactions to assess whether model-derived interaction maps localize binding sites.

为了填补这一空白，我们引入了 InteractBind，这是一个包含约 10 万个蛋白质-配体对的大规模数据集，并配套了一个用于细粒度评估的基准测试。其核心细粒度任务是结合位点定位，该任务利用涵盖六大类非共价相互作用的蛋白质残基与配体原子相互作用图，来评估模型生成的相互作用图是否能够准确锁定结合位点。

InteractBind further includes binding affinity and protein similarity-controlled splits to support realistic generalization assessment. Using InteractBind, we evaluate eight existing sequence-based and interaction-aware models, assessing binary binding prediction and binding-site localization. Results reveal limited binding-site localization despite strong binary binding prediction, with marked variation across non-covalent interaction types.

InteractBind 还包含了结合亲和力和蛋白质相似度受控的划分，以支持对模型泛化能力的真实评估。利用 InteractBind，我们评估了八种现有的基于序列和基于相互作用感知的模型，并对其二元结合预测和结合位点定位能力进行了测试。结果显示，尽管这些模型在二元结合预测方面表现出色，但在结合位点定位方面的能力却十分有限，且在不同类型的非共价相互作用中表现出显著差异。

Overall, InteractBind establishes a benchmark paradigm that encourages the development of more interpretable and physically grounded protein-ligand models.

总而言之，InteractBind 建立了一种基准测试范式，旨在鼓励开发更具可解释性和物理基础的蛋白质-配体模型。
