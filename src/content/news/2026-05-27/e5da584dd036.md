# A World Model of Radiologist Reading for Medical Image Representation Learning
# 用于医学图像表征学习的放射科医生阅片世界模型

**Abstract:** Radiologist eye-tracking data provide a rich record of how experts search, compare, and accumulate evidence during image reading; yet, existing methods exploit this signal only partially, either as a static spatial prior or as an auxiliary prediction target decoupled from diagnosis. 

**摘要：** 放射科医生的眼动追踪数据记录了专家在阅片过程中如何进行搜索、比较和积累证据的丰富信息；然而，现有方法仅部分利用了这一信号，要么将其作为静态的空间先验，要么将其作为与诊断脱钩的辅助预测目标。

We propose GazeWorld, a medical imaging world model that treats the image as the world and the radiologist's fixation sequence as a trajectory through it. GazeWorld autoregressively predicts the latent representation of the next fixated patch from all previously visited ones, while a spatial-completion branch covers unvisited regions. 

我们提出了 GazeWorld，这是一个医学影像世界模型，它将图像视为“世界”，将放射科医生的注视序列视为穿过该世界的轨迹。GazeWorld 通过自回归方式，根据之前访问过的所有图像块来预测下一个注视块的潜在表征，同时通过一个空间补全分支来覆盖未访问的区域。

At inference, GazeWorld generates a sequence of patch representations from the image alone without requiring real gaze data. Frozen GazeWorld features achieve state-of-the-art diagnostic accuracy across all nine supervised settings on CheXpert, RSNA Pneumonia, and SIIM-ACR Pneumothorax, as well as the highest zero-shot accuracy on all three benchmarks. 

在推理阶段，GazeWorld 仅凭图像本身即可生成一系列图像块表征，无需真实的眼动数据。冻结后的 GazeWorld 特征在 CheXpert、RSNA Pneumonia 和 SIIM-ACR Pneumothorax 的全部九项监督学习设置中均达到了最先进的诊断准确率，并在所有三个基准测试中实现了最高的零样本（zero-shot）准确率。

On the GazeSearch benchmark, a generic decoder trained on the same frozen features outperforms the purpose-built LogitGaze-Med by over 16% in ScanMatch and 22% in SED, despite not being explicitly trained to predict gaze. GazeWorld demonstrates that modeling how experts read, not just what they conclude, offers a promising pretraining paradigm for medical imaging AI.

在 GazeSearch 基准测试中，基于相同冻结特征训练的通用解码器在 ScanMatch 指标上超过了专门设计的 LogitGaze-Med 16% 以上，在 SED 指标上超过了 22%，尽管它并未经过明确的眼动预测训练。GazeWorld 表明，对专家“如何阅读”而非仅仅是“得出什么结论”进行建模，为医学影像 AI 提供了一种极具前景的预训练范式。
