# Stop advertising in your commits
# 停止在你的提交信息中打广告

Stop advertising in your commits! Posted on 2026-05-26 by Akseli Lahtinen Co-authored-by: ur mom Co-authored-by: ur mom
停止在你的提交信息（commits）中打广告！发布于 2026 年 5 月 26 日，作者：Akseli Lahtinen，合著者：你妈，合著者：你妈。

I don't get why people just gleefully add these ads for companies to their open-source projects that do not pay them a penny (but actively take money from them in subscription fees).
我实在不明白，为什么人们会乐此不疲地在那些不仅没给他们一分钱（反而还通过订阅费从他们身上赚取利润）的开源项目中，为这些公司添加广告。

"Assisted by blabot", "co-authored-by: slopgpt", "sent from my fartphone" Why? It's a fucking ad.
“由 blabot 辅助”、“合著者：slopgpt”、“从我的屁手机发送”。为什么？这他妈就是广告。

I bet you use ad blockers, yet you add ads to your commits. Stop that.
我敢打赌你平时肯定会用广告拦截器，结果转头却在自己的提交信息里加广告。别再这么做了。

Disclose your "AI" tools in a merge request if needed but leave them out of the damn commits, those are for technical information and not for advertising.
如果有必要，你可以在合并请求（merge request）中披露你使用的“AI”工具，但请把它们从该死的提交信息里拿开。提交信息是用来记录技术细节的，而不是用来打广告的。

Or just add "generated by an LLM" but do not give those companies free advertising space. Or just.. Do not use these tools, which is fairly easy.
或者你只需写上“由大语言模型生成”，但不要给那些公司提供免费的广告位。又或者……干脆别用这些工具，这其实挺简单的。

Any tool that adds advertising to commits or emails or whatever messages is a really bad one. Fuck ads.
任何会在提交信息、电子邮件或任何消息中植入广告的工具都是垃圾。去他妈的广告。

Also before someone gets their nipples in a twist about something, here is my "AI" stance: https://akselmo.dev/posts/why-i-will-likely-never-use-ai-programming-tools/
另外，在某些人因为这件事感到抓狂之前，先看看我对“AI”的立场：https://akselmo.dev/posts/why-i-will-likely-never-use-ai-programming-tools/
