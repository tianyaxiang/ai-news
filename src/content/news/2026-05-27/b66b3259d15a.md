# Task-Aligned Self-Supervised Learning for Medical Image Analysis: A Systematic Review and Practical Design Guidelines
# 面向医学图像分析的任务对齐自监督学习：系统综述与实用设计指南

**Abstract:** Self-supervised learning (SSL) has emerged as a promising paradigm for addressing the annotation bottleneck in medical imaging by learning representations from unlabeled data. However, its effectiveness depends heavily on the design of the pretext task and its alignment with the downstream clinical objective. 

**摘要：** 自监督学习（SSL）已成为解决医学影像标注瓶颈的一种极具前景的范式，它通过从未标注数据中学习表征来发挥作用。然而，其有效性在很大程度上取决于预训练任务（pretext task）的设计及其与下游临床目标的一致性。

We present a systematic, task-oriented review of SSL in medical imaging, examining how different pretext-task formulations influence performance across classification, segmentation, detection, and other tasks. Following PRISMA guidelines, we analyze 75 studies published between 2017 and 2025 and organize them into four paradigms: contrastive, non-contrastive and predictive, generative and reconstruction-based, and hybrid learning. 

我们对医学影像中的 SSL 进行了一项系统性的、面向任务的综述，探讨了不同的预训练任务形式如何影响分类、分割、检测及其他任务的性能。遵循 PRISMA 指南，我们分析了 2017 年至 2025 年间发表的 75 项研究，并将它们归纳为四种范式：对比学习、非对比与预测学习、生成与重构学习，以及混合学习。

Rather than cataloguing methods by architecture, we map each paradigm to the downstream objectives it best supports. Our analysis shows there is no universally optimal SSL strategy; instead, performance is governed by the alignment between the pretext task, the imaging modality, and the target task. 

我们没有按架构对方法进行分类，而是将每种范式映射到其最支持的下游目标上。我们的分析表明，不存在通用的最优 SSL 策略；相反，性能取决于预训练任务、成像模态与目标任务之间的对齐程度。

Contrastive methods learn global discriminative features and align well with classification, but may overlook subtle pathological patterns. Generative and spatial prediction-based approaches better preserve local anatomical structure, making them more suitable for segmentation and other dense prediction tasks, while hybrid methods offer the most balanced performance. 

对比学习方法能够学习全局判别特征，与分类任务高度契合，但可能会忽略细微的病理模式。生成式和基于空间预测的方法能更好地保留局部解剖结构，使其更适合分割及其他密集预测任务，而混合方法则提供了最均衡的性能。

We further show that modality-specific design is critical and that SSL provides its greatest benefit in low-label and few-shot regimes. Finally, we distill these findings into practical design guidelines and outline open challenges, including pathology-aware pretext task design, resource-efficient training for high-dimensional data, and standardized evaluation protocols. This work offers practical guidance for designing more effective and clinically relevant SSL frameworks in medical imaging.

我们进一步指出，针对特定模态的设计至关重要，且 SSL 在低标注和少样本场景下能发挥最大效益。最后，我们将这些发现提炼为实用的设计指南，并概述了当前面临的挑战，包括病理感知预训练任务设计、高维数据的资源高效训练，以及标准化的评估协议。本研究为设计更有效且具有临床意义的医学影像 SSL 框架提供了实践指导。
