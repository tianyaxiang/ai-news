# Algometrics: Forecasting Under Algorithmic Feedback
# 算法度量学：算法反馈下的预测

**Abstract:** In algorithmic markets, predictive models become part of the data-generating process they aim to forecast. Once their outputs are converted into trades, allocations, execution schedules, or risk controls, they change the future data on which they are evaluated. I introduce algometrics, a framework for time series whose evolution depends on the predictive algorithms forecasting them.

**摘要：** 在算法市场中，预测模型成为了其旨在预测的数据生成过程的一部分。一旦模型的输出被转化为交易、分配、执行计划或风险控制措施，它们就会改变用于评估自身的未来数据。我引入了“算法度量学”（algometrics），这是一个针对时间序列的框架，其演变过程取决于对其进行预测的预测算法。

The framework distinguishes historical risk, measured under passive forecasting, from deployment risk, measured when forecasts drive actions. I prove three results. First, deployment risk is not identifiable from passive historical data alone: even in a one-step linear feedback model, infinitely many algorithm-mediated environments induce the same historical law while implying different deployment risks for the same forecaster.

该框架区分了“历史风险”（在被动预测下测量）和“部署风险”（在预测驱动行动时测量）。我证明了三个结论。首先，仅凭被动的历史数据无法识别部署风险：即使在单步线性反馈模型中，无数种算法中介环境也可能诱导出相同的历史规律，但对于同一个预测者而言，却意味着不同的部署风险。

Second, historical model rankings can invert under crowding, so a predictor with lower passive error can have higher deployment error once similar algorithms are adopted. Third, randomized or instrumented actions identify short-horizon linear feedback, and I derive a finite-sample bound for deployment-risk estimation. These results suggest that time-series benchmarks in algorithmic markets should report feedback sensitivity alongside predictive accuracy.

其次，在拥挤效应下，历史模型排名可能会发生逆转，因此一旦采用了类似的算法，被动误差较低的预测器可能会产生更高的部署误差。第三，随机化或工具变量操作可以识别短期的线性反馈，我推导出了部署风险估计的有限样本界。这些结果表明，算法市场中的时间序列基准测试不仅应报告预测准确性，还应报告反馈敏感性。
