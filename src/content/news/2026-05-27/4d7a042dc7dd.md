### Best Outdoor Security Camera: Arlo Pro 6
### 最佳户外安防摄像头：Arlo Pro 6

Crystal-clear footage day or night, a speedy-loading live feed, and a smart notification system make the Arlo Pro 6 my favorite outdoor security camera. It connects directly to Wi-Fi, has a wide 160-degree field of view, and records at up to 2K resolution with HDR. (Your feed won't look blown out when the sun is out or there’s another bright light source in the frame.)
无论昼夜，清晰的画面、快速加载的实时视频流以及智能通知系统，使 Arlo Pro 6 成为我最喜爱的户外安防摄像头。它可直接连接 Wi-Fi，拥有 160 度的广角视野，并支持最高 2K 分辨率及 HDR 录制。（即使在阳光直射或画面中有强光源时，视频也不会出现过曝。）

The Arlo Pro 6 is consistent and reliable, and replaces the previous version (Arlo Pro 5) here, adding USB-C charging instead of the proprietary magnetic cable, Bluetooth for easier setup, and slightly improved battery life. Arlo claims up to eight months, but this depends entirely on how busy it is. After a month of testing, I’m on course to get around four months of battery.
Arlo Pro 6 表现稳定可靠，取代了前代产品（Arlo Pro 5）。它改用了 USB-C 充电接口而非专有的磁吸线，增加了蓝牙功能以简化设置，电池续航也有所提升。Arlo 声称续航可达八个月，但这完全取决于使用频率。经过一个月的测试，预计其续航时间约为四个月。

But there’s a big, expensive catch. You need an Arlo Secure plan ($10 per month or $96 a year for a single camera, $20 per month or $216 a year for unlimited cameras) to unlock the best features and get 60 days of cloud video history. For a mix of cameras and a doorbell, this might be worth it, but it’s too expensive for a single camera.
但它有一个昂贵的代价：你需要订阅 Arlo Secure 计划（单台摄像头每月 10 美元或每年 96 美元；多台摄像头每月 20 美元或每年 216 美元）才能解锁核心功能并获得 60 天的云端视频历史记录。如果你同时拥有多台摄像头和门铃，这或许物有所值，但对于单台设备来说太贵了。

### Best Value: TP-Link Tapo Wire-Free MagCam (Battery)
### 性价比之选：TP-Link Tapo 无线磁吸摄像头（电池版）

Considering the lower cost and the option to record locally on a microSD card, the Tapo Wire-Free MagCam (C425) comes surprisingly close to matching our top pick on features. It connects directly to Wi-Fi, has a 150-degree field of view, and can record video at up to 2K and 30 frames per second.
考虑到较低的成本以及支持 microSD 卡本地录制的功能，Tapo 无线磁吸摄像头 (C425) 在功能上与我们的首选产品非常接近。它可直接连接 Wi-Fi，拥有 150 度视野，并支持最高 2K 分辨率和 30fps 的视频录制。

Even at the highest quality setting, it doesn’t match our top pick, and the lack of HDR is noticeable when the sun shines, as bright areas can become blown out. Higher-quality footage impacts battery life, too, and my MagCam needs a charge every three months or so. But you can get it bundled with a solar panel for $130. The magnetic base makes it easy to fit and remove to recharge.
即使在最高画质设置下，它也无法与我们的首选产品相比。由于缺乏 HDR，在阳光照射下，高亮区域容易出现过曝。高质量视频也会影响电池续航，我的 MagCam 大约每三个月需要充电一次。不过，你可以以 130 美元的价格购买包含太阳能板的套装。磁吸底座使其安装和拆卸充电非常方便。

The Tapo app is solid, with the ability to categorize motion (person, pet, vehicle), activity zones you can filter by detection type, and privacy zones. The two-way audio is passable, and there’s a built-in alarm. Notifications came through swiftly, and the live feed usually loaded within three or four seconds.
Tapo 的应用程序表现稳健，能够对运动进行分类（人、宠物、车辆），设置可按检测类型过滤的活动区域以及隐私区域。双向语音效果尚可，并内置了警报器。通知推送迅速，实时视频通常在三到四秒内即可加载完成。
