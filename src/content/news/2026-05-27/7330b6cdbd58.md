# RAW: Robust Avatar Watermarking -- Benchmarking and Baseline

**Abstract:** Digital avatar watermarking presents unique challenges: avatars are routinely post-processed with background replacement, reframing, and format conversion before deployment. 

**摘要：** 数字虚拟人（Avatar）水印技术面临着独特的挑战：在部署之前，虚拟人通常会经过背景替换、重构图（reframing）和格式转换等后期处理。

We introduce **RAW** (Robust Avatar Watermarking), a benchmark comprising 50 synthetic avatar videos from 5 commercial providers and 6 attacks simulating real-world avatar workflows. 

我们推出了 **RAW** (Robust Avatar Watermarking)，这是一个包含来自 5 家商业供应商的 50 段合成虚拟人视频的基准测试集，并模拟了 6 种真实世界虚拟人工作流中的攻击方式。

Evaluating 7 existing methods reveals that avatar-specific attacks such as background removal significantly degrade watermark recovery. 

通过对 7 种现有方法的评估发现，诸如背景移除等针对虚拟人的攻击会显著降低水印的恢复效果。

We propose **WALT** (Watermarking Avatars with Learned Textures), which embeds watermarks in UV texture space via 3D face reconstruction. 

我们提出了 **WALT** (Watermarking Avatars with Learned Textures)，该方法通过 3D 人脸重建技术，将水印嵌入到 UV 纹理空间中。

WALT achieves the highest robustness to zoom attacks (92.4%) while maintaining strong performance on background removal (95.6%). 

WALT 在应对缩放攻击时表现出最高的鲁棒性（92.4%），同时在背景移除任务中也保持了出色的性能（95.6%）。

We release our benchmark to facilitate research into avatar-specific watermarking.

我们发布了该基准测试集，旨在促进针对虚拟人水印技术的相关研究。
