# Iterative Refinement Neural Operators are Learned Fixed-Point Solvers: A Principled Approach to Spectral Bias Mitigation
# 迭代细化神经算子即学习型不动点求解器：一种缓解谱偏差的原则性方法

**Abstract:** Neural operators serve as fast, data-driven surrogates for scientific modeling but typically rely on a monolithic, single-pass inference procedure that struggles to resolve high-frequency details, a limitation known as spectral bias.
**摘要：** 神经算子作为科学建模中快速、数据驱动的替代模型，通常依赖于单一的、一次性的推理过程，这使得它们难以解析高频细节，这种局限性被称为“谱偏差”（spectral bias）。

We introduce the Iterative Refinement Neural Operator (IRNO), which augments pre-trained operators with a learned refinement module iteratively applied via fixed-point iteration.
我们引入了迭代细化神经算子（Iterative Refinement Neural Operator, IRNO），它通过一个学习型细化模块增强了预训练算子，并利用不动点迭代进行反复应用。

IRNO decomposes the prediction into a coarse initialization followed by successive residual corrections, paralleling classical numerical solvers.
IRNO 将预测过程分解为粗略初始化和随后的连续残差校正，这与经典的数值求解器原理一致。

Under local assumptions, we establish contraction of the induced operator, ensuring convergence to a unique fixed point.
在局部假设下，我们建立了诱导算子的收缩性，从而确保了其收敛到一个唯一的不动点。

To explicitly target high-frequency errors, we propose a progressive spectral loss that adaptively increases penalty on high-frequency components over refinement steps during training.
为了明确针对高频误差，我们提出了一种渐进式谱损失函数，该函数在训练过程的细化步骤中，能够自适应地增加对高频分量的惩罚。

Across physical systems, IRNO consistently lowers error, with up to 56.05% improvement on turbulent flow.
在各类物理系统中，IRNO 始终能降低误差，在湍流模拟中性能提升高达 56.05%。

On Active Matter, spectral analysis reveals that, relative to base operator, the normalized error ratios decrease to 27.72-36.10% in low-, 5.07-6.68% in mid-, and 1.48-2.04% in high-frequencies, remaining stable beyond the trained iteration count.
在活性物质（Active Matter）研究中，谱分析显示，与基础算子相比，归一化误差比率在低频段降至 27.72-36.10%，中频段降至 5.07-6.68%，高频段降至 1.48-2.04%，且在超过训练迭代次数后仍保持稳定。
