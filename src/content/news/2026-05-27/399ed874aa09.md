### Ajar

**Launching today: Lid Angle Sync & Keep Awake for AI Agents on Mac**

今天发布：适用于 Mac 上 AI 代理的“屏幕开合角度同步”与“保持唤醒”功能

**Ajar makes your Mac respond to what you're doing, not what you remember to press.**

Ajar 让你的 Mac 能够根据你的实际操作做出响应，而不是依赖于你是否记得去手动按键。

**Lid Angle Sync maps your MacBook's lid angle to screen brightness, keyboard backlight, and system volume. Multiple displays are supported, with per-display offsets.**

“屏幕开合角度同步”（Lid Angle Sync）功能可以将 MacBook 的屏幕开合角度映射到屏幕亮度、键盘背光和系统音量上。该功能支持多显示器，并可针对每个显示器设置偏移量。

**Awake keeps the Mac awake when you're actually working. Built-in rules detect Claude Code, Codex, and Gemini CLI sessions. Add your own for apps, Wi-Fi, disks, power, or a closed-lid clamshell.**

“保持唤醒”（Awake）功能可以在你实际工作时让 Mac 保持唤醒状态。内置规则可自动检测 Claude Code、Codex 和 Gemini CLI 会话。你还可以针对特定应用程序、Wi-Fi、磁盘、电源状态或合盖模式（Clamshell）添加自定义规则。
