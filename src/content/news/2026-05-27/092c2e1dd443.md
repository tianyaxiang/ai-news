# 15 Best Travel Toiletry Bags, Tested Over Many Miles (2026)

Consider many people's idea of a toiletry bag. Even the best toiletry bags can be a rudimentary pouch or cube with a single zippered compartment—or, perhaps more primitively, a ziplock bag—with just enough space to hold travel shampoo and conditioner, a toothbrush, maybe a razor. But the items probably roll around and spill, or squish out of hastily screwed-on lids. Next thing you know, you’re rummaging through what's basically a junk drawer anytime you need to find something. There is a better way.

很多人对洗漱包的印象可能还停留在最基础的层面。即便是市面上最好的洗漱包，有时也不过是一个带拉链的简易收纳袋或方块包——甚至更原始一点，就是一个密封塑料袋——里面勉强塞得下旅行装的洗发水、护发素、牙刷，或许还有一把剃须刀。但这些物品很可能会在包里滚来滚去、发生泄漏，或者因为瓶盖没拧紧而被挤压出来。结果就是，每次你需要找东西时，都得像翻垃圾抽屉一样手忙脚乱。其实，有更好的解决方案。

As someone who lives far from family, I have been traveling every few months for the past 18 years and have tried all kinds of toiletry bags, including our favorite hanging option, the LL Bean Personal Organizer ($40), and our pick for the best men's toiletry kit, from Wandrd ($44). As any seasoned traveler knows, the best toiletry bags are largely a matter of personal taste. Some people like to travel as light as possible with just a razor and some shampoo. Others have elaborate skin- and hair-care routines that practically require a tackle box. There are clear bags, bags with many compartments or elastic organizers, and even bags made out of sailcloth. The only firm principle is that a “good” toiletry bag should make your travel life easier, not more difficult.

作为一名远离家乡生活的人，过去18年来我每隔几个月就要出差一次，尝试过各种各样的洗漱包，包括我们最推荐的悬挂式洗漱包 L.L. Bean Personal Organizer（40美元），以及我们评选出的最佳男士洗漱包 Wandrd（44美元）。任何经验丰富的旅行者都知道，最好的洗漱包很大程度上取决于个人喜好。有些人喜欢轻装上阵，只带一把剃须刀和一点洗发水；而另一些人则有复杂的护肤和护发流程，几乎需要一个工具箱来装。市面上有透明包、多隔层或带弹性收纳带的包，甚至还有用帆布制成的包。唯一的准则是：一个“好”的洗漱包应该让你的旅行生活更轻松，而不是更麻烦。

### Best Toiletry Bag for Men: Wandrd Toiletry Bag
### 最佳男士洗漱包：Wandrd Toiletry Bag

Wandrd's Toiletry Bag comes in two sizes: medium and large. Get the large, because it has more storage and organization and doesn't cost much more. The medium, which is also available on Amazon, is a little too simple for the asking price (though if you buy both, you can merge them together). It's made from Robic nylon with a thermoplastic urethane coating, and it's weather-proof (not submersible), thanks to the zippers and coating. That should help keep liquid in if your cologne bottle breaks when baggage handlers are a little too carefree with your luggage.

Wandrd 洗漱包有两种尺寸：中号和大号。建议购买大号，因为它拥有更多的存储空间和更合理的布局，且价格相差不大。中号虽然在亚马逊上有售，但对于其定价来说显得过于简单（不过如果你两个都买，可以将它们组合在一起使用）。它采用 Robic 尼龙材质，并涂有热塑性聚氨酯（TPU）涂层，具备防风雨性能（不可浸没在水中）。如果行李搬运工在处理你的行李时过于粗鲁导致香水瓶破裂，这种涂层和拉链设计有助于防止液体渗出。

### Best Toiletry Bag for Women: Parallelle Traveller
### 最佳女士洗漱包：Parallelle Traveller

Though not a Dopp kit in the strictest sense, the Parallelle Traveller is such a thoughtfully designed bag that it works for just about any purpose. Bag designer Annie Fan went through 60 iterations to get the details just right, and it shows. Unzip the sides, peel it down, and it becomes a caddy with up to 20 pockets, six pouches, and three dividers, keeping all your products—even some full-sized ones—upright and easy to grab. Zip it back up, and your stuff is secure enough to toss into a bag or suitcase.

虽然严格来说它不属于传统的男士洗漱包（Dopp kit），但 Parallelle Traveller 的设计极其周到，几乎适用于任何用途。包袋设计师 Annie Fan 经过了60次迭代才将细节打磨得恰到好处，这一点显而易见。拉开侧面拉链并展开，它就变成了一个拥有多达20个口袋、6个收纳袋和3个隔板的收纳架，能让你的所有产品——甚至是部分正装产品——保持直立，方便拿取。拉上拉链后，它足够稳固，可以直接丢进包里或行李箱中。
