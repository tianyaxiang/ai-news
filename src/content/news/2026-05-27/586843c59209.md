# Remote sensing data imputation using deep learning for multispectral imagery
# 基于深度学习的多光谱遥感影像数据插补研究

**Abstract:** Remote sensing techniques have been increasingly utilised in aquatic applications in recent years. A common challenge in using optical satellite data is the presence of missing observations due to cloud cover. These data gaps can lead to missed detection of critical events, such as algal blooms, in lakes of high interest to water authorities. As a result, enhancing the completeness of optical satellite datasets is crucial for improving the monitoring and prediction of algal blooms.

**摘要：** 近年来，遥感技术在水域应用中得到了日益广泛的使用。在使用光学卫星数据时，一个常见的挑战是由于云层覆盖导致的观测数据缺失。这些数据缺口可能导致水务部门高度关注的湖泊中关键事件（如藻类爆发）的漏检。因此，提高光学卫星数据集的完整性对于改善藻类爆发的监测和预测至关重要。

In this study, we compared a traditional data imputation method (i.e., linear interpolation) with deep learning models for reconstructing missing spectral bands across four lakes with historical records of algal blooms. The deep learning models adopted include CNN-based architectures (i.e., CNN, Inception Resnet, and Autoencoder) and CNN-LSTM-based architectures (i.e., CNN-LSTM, Resnet-LSTM, and Autoencoder-LSTM).

在本研究中，我们对比了传统数据插补方法（即线性插值）与深度学习模型，用于重建四个具有藻类爆发历史记录的湖泊中缺失的光谱波段。所采用的深度学习模型包括基于 CNN 的架构（即 CNN、Inception Resnet 和自动编码器）以及基于 CNN-LSTM 的架构（即 CNN-LSTM、Resnet-LSTM 和 Autoencoder-LSTM）。

Our results demonstrated that deep learning models substantially outperformed the baseline linear interpolation method in imputing spectral band values within artificially masked regions. Among these models, CNN delivered the best performance across most lakes. Furthermore, we evaluated the performance of algal bloom indices (i.e., Green/Red and NDCI) derived from the imputed imagery by comparing them with the observed data.

研究结果表明，在对人工遮罩区域内的光谱波段值进行插补时，深度学习模型的表现显著优于基准线性插值法。在这些模型中，CNN 在大多数湖泊中表现最佳。此外，我们通过将插补影像导出的藻类爆发指数（即 Green/Red 和 NDCI）与观测数据进行对比，评估了其性能。

Our results demonstrate that deep learning models are effective for imputing missing data in PlanetScope SuperDove imagery, enabling more reliable applications in water monitoring.

我们的研究结果证明，深度学习模型能够有效插补 PlanetScope SuperDove 影像中的缺失数据，从而为水质监测提供更可靠的应用支持。
