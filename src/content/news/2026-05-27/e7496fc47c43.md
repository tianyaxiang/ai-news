### Audio / 音频

**FXSound** - Boost sound quality, volume, and bass. Has a beautiful modern UI. 🪟 🟢 ⭐
**FXSound** - 提升音质、音量和低音。拥有美观的现代 UI。🪟 🟢 ⭐

**JACK Audio** - Audio server for routing and mixing sound between programs. 🪟 🍎 🐧
**JACK Audio** - 用于在程序间路由和混合声音的音频服务器。🪟 🍎 🐧

**EarTrumpet** - Advanced volume control for Windows, offering app-specific audio management. 🪟
**EarTrumpet** - Windows 的高级音量控制工具，提供针对特定应用程序的音频管理。🪟

**Ambie** - Use white noise, nature sounds, & more to boost your productivity. 🪟 🟢
**Ambie** - 使用白噪音、自然之声等来提高你的工作效率。🪟 🟢

**BlackHole** - Virtual audio loopback driver for routing audio between macOS applications. 🍎 🟢
**BlackHole** - 用于在 macOS 应用程序之间路由音频的虚拟音频回环驱动程序。🍎 🟢

### Audio Players / 音频播放器

**Foobar2000** - Lightweight and highly customizable audio player with support for many formats. 🪟 🍎 ⭐
**Foobar2000** - 轻量级且高度可定制的音频播放器，支持多种格式。🪟 🍎 ⭐

**Audacious** - Lightweight audio player for simple music playback. 🪟 🐧 🟢
**Audacious** - 用于简单音乐播放的轻量级音频播放器。🪟 🐧 🟢

**Karafun** - Collection of karaoke songs across multiple genres. Play your own CDG files or use downloads from YouTube. 🪟
**Karafun** - 涵盖多种流派的卡拉 OK 歌曲合集。可播放你自己的 CDG 文件或使用从 YouTube 下载的内容。🪟

**MusicBee** - Feature-rich music player and manager. 🪟
**MusicBee** - 功能丰富的音乐播放器和管理器。🪟

**Strawberry Music Player** - Music player for organizing and playing your audio collection. 🪟 🍎 🐧
**Strawberry Music Player** - 用于整理和播放音频收藏的音乐播放器。🪟 🍎 🐧

**Tor Browser** - Privacy browser routing traffic over the Tor network. 🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢 ⭐
**Tor Browser** - 通过 Tor 网络路由流量的隐私浏览器。🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢 ⭐

**Arc** - Vertical tab browser for modern productivity. 🪟 🍎
**Arc** - 专为现代生产力设计的垂直标签页浏览器。🪟 🍎

**Brave** - Privacy-focused and fast browser. 🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢
**Brave** - 注重隐私且快速的浏览器。🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢

**Chrome** - Fast browser with integrated Google services. 🪟 🍎 🐧
**Chrome** - 集成 Google 服务的快速浏览器。🪟 🍎 🐧

**Chromium** - Browser project behind Chrome. 🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢
**Chromium** - Chrome 背后的浏览器开源项目。🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢

**ungoogled-chromium** - Privacy-focused Chromium with Google services removed. 🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢
**ungoogled-chromium** - 移除了 Google 服务的注重隐私的 Chromium 版本。🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢

**Firefox** - Browser with privacy and customization. 🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢
**Firefox** - 具备隐私保护和高度定制化的浏览器。🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢

**LibreWolf** - Privacy-focused Firefox fork with enhanced security. 🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢
**LibreWolf** - 增强了安全性的注重隐私的 Firefox 分支。🪟 🍎 🐧 🟢

### Communication Messaging / 通讯与即时通讯

**Discord** - Communication platform for text, voice, and video, popular with gamers. 🪟 🍎 🐧 ⭐
**Discord** - 受游戏玩家欢迎的文字、语音和视频通讯平台。🪟 🍎 🐧 ⭐

**Telegram** - Cloud-based messaging app known for speed, security, and group chats. 🪟 🍎 🐧
**Telegram** - 以速度、安全性和群聊功能著称的云端即时通讯应用。🪟 🍎 🐧

**WhatsApp** - Free messaging app with text, voice, video calls, and multimedia sharing. 🪟 🍎
**WhatsApp** - 提供文字、语音、视频通话和多媒体共享的免费通讯应用。🪟 🍎

**Signal** - Secure, private messaging app with end-to-end encryption. 🪟 🍎 🐧
**Signal** - 具备端到端加密的安全隐私通讯应用。🪟 🍎 🐧

**Slack** - Collaboration hub for teams with messaging, file sharing, and integrations. 🪟 🍎 🐧
**Slack** - 团队协作中心，支持消息传递、文件共享和集成功能。🪟 🍎 🐧
