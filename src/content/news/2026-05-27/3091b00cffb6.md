# CAFD: Concept-Aware DNN Fault Detection using VLMs
# CAFD：基于视觉语言模型（VLM）的深度神经网络概念感知故障检测

**Abstract:** Fault detection for Deep Neural Networks (DNNs) has received increasing attention in recent years. While more advanced hybrid approaches have been proposed to combine multiple sources of information and outperform earlier techniques, they often incur substantial computational overhead, limiting scalability and practicality in real-world settings.

**摘要：** 近年来，深度神经网络（DNN）的故障检测受到了越来越多的关注。尽管目前已提出多种先进的混合方法来整合多源信息并超越早期技术，但这些方法往往伴随着巨大的计算开销，限制了其在现实场景中的可扩展性和实用性。

In this paper, we introduce Concept-Aware Fault Detection (CAFD), a learning-based approach that achieves superior fault detection performance by effectively integrating multiple information sources while maintaining practical efficiency. Specifically, CAFD is trained using a carefully selected set of informative features, including model-based signals derived from the DNN's outputs, distance-based features, and a novel concept-based feature, called Concept Failure Ratio (CFR).

在本文中，我们引入了概念感知故障检测（CAFD），这是一种基于学习的方法。它通过有效整合多种信息源，在保持实际效率的同时实现了卓越的故障检测性能。具体而言，CAFD 使用一组精心挑选的信息特征进行训练，包括源自 DNN 输出的基于模型的信号、基于距离的特征，以及一种称为“概念故障率”（CFR）的新型概念特征。

CFR leverages Vision-Language Models (VLMs) to extract textual concepts from images and quantify the likelihood that their presence is associated with DNN failures. By incorporating this feature, CAFD benefits from complementary semantic information, enabling more effective fault detection.

CFR 利用视觉语言模型（VLM）从图像中提取文本概念，并量化这些概念的存在与 DNN 故障之间的关联可能性。通过引入这一特征，CAFD 受益于互补的语义信息，从而实现了更有效的故障检测。

Our results demonstrate that CFR serves as an effective indicator for DNN fault detection. We conduct an extensive empirical evaluation of CAFD, comparing it against five state-of-the-art baselines across three subject DNN models and datasets, including ImageNet. Across a wide range of constrained selection budgets, CAFD consistently outperforms all baselines in Fault Detection Rate (FDR), achieving average FDR improvements of 18.3% across all investigated subjects and budget sizes.

我们的研究结果表明，CFR 是 DNN 故障检测的有效指标。我们对 CAFD 进行了广泛的实证评估，将其与三个目标 DNN 模型和数据集（包括 ImageNet）上的五个最先进基准进行了比较。在广泛的受限选择预算范围内，CAFD 在故障检测率（FDR）方面始终优于所有基准，在所有研究对象和预算规模下，平均 FDR 提升了 18.3%。
