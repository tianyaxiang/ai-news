# Parameter Efficient Multi-Class Intelligent Scheduling for Multimodal Online Distributed Industrial Anomaly Detection
# 面向多模态在线分布式工业异常检测的参数高效多类智能调度

**Abstract:** Industrial anomaly detection has attracted significant attention as a fundamental challenge in industrial systems. The rapid advancement of heterogeneous industrial sensors has driven industrial anomaly detection from unimodal to multimodal paradigms. However, existing methods are primarily designed for centralized and offline settings, overlooking the distributed and continuously generated data characteristic of real-world industrial environments.

**摘要：** 工业异常检测作为工业系统中的一项基础性挑战，已引起了广泛关注。异构工业传感器的快速发展，推动了工业异常检测从单模态向多模态范式的转变。然而，现有的方法主要针对集中式和离线环境设计，忽略了现实工业环境中数据分布式且持续生成的特性。

With the advancement of edge intelligence, modern edge devices are increasingly capable of not only data acquisition but also distributed model training, enabling collaborative intelligence across the system. Industrial anomaly detection represents a critical application in this context. Motivated by these challenges, we propose a novel framework termed Multimodal Online Distributed Industrial Anomaly Detection (MODIAD).

随着边缘智能的进步，现代边缘设备不仅具备数据采集能力，还日益具备分布式模型训练能力，从而实现了跨系统的协同智能。工业异常检测正是这一背景下的关键应用。受此挑战驱动，我们提出了一种名为“多模态在线分布式工业异常检测”（MODIAD）的新型框架。

We first present a comprehensive workflow for MODIAD and then formulate a Multi-class Intelligent Scheduling (MIS) problem to coordinate cross class model updates by balancing data sufficiency and class update frequency. To efficiently solve this problem, we design a Sequential Marginal Gain Greedy (SMG) algorithm that enables effective multi-class training under resource constraints.

我们首先提出了 MODIAD 的综合工作流程，随后构建了一个多类智能调度（MIS）问题，通过平衡数据充足性和类别更新频率来协调跨类模型更新。为了高效解决该问题，我们设计了一种顺序边际增益贪婪（SMG）算法，能够在资源受限的情况下实现有效的多类训练。

Furthermore, to improve the computational and communication efficiency during training, we propose an Resource Efficient Class-Wise Low Rank Adaptation (REC-LoRA) strategy, which significantly reduces system overhead while preserving detection performance. Extensive experiments on two representative multimodal industrial anomaly detection datasets, MVTec 3D-AD and Eyecandies demonstrate that the proposed approach achieves superior performance and efficiency under the MODIAD scenario.

此外，为了提高训练过程中的计算和通信效率，我们提出了一种资源高效的类级低秩自适应（REC-LoRA）策略，该策略在保持检测性能的同时，显著降低了系统开销。在两个具有代表性的多模态工业异常检测数据集（MVTec 3D-AD 和 Eyecandies）上进行的广泛实验表明，所提出的方法在 MODIAD 场景下实现了卓越的性能和效率。
