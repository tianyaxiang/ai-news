# LLM-AutoSciLab: Closed-Loop Scientific Discovery via Active Experimentation with LLMs
# LLM-AutoSciLab：基于大语言模型主动实验的闭环科学发现

**Abstract:** Scientific discovery is a closed-loop process in which hypotheses guide data acquisition and observations refine the hypothesis space. Yet most approaches reduce discovery to supervised learning over fixed datasets, where limited observations can support multiple plausible mechanisms that fit locally but fail to generalize. Thus, the key challenge is selecting informative observations to resolve uncertainty, shifting the focus from static inference to adaptive data acquisition.

**摘要：** 科学发现是一个闭环过程，其中假设指导数据获取，而观测结果则用于优化假设空间。然而，大多数方法将科学发现简化为在固定数据集上的监督学习，在这种情况下，有限的观测结果可能支持多种在局部拟合良好但无法泛化的合理机制。因此，核心挑战在于如何选择具有信息量的观测结果以消除不确定性，从而将研究重点从静态推理转向自适应数据获取。

To address this, we propose LLM-AutoSciLab, a closed-loop framework that couples hypothesis generation with hypothesis-conditioned experiment selection and mechanism refinement. Rather than fitting models to passively collected data, LLM-AutoSciLab iteratively proposes plausible hypotheses, selects informative experiments to distinguish or refine them, and updates its state using the resulting evidence.

为了解决这一问题，我们提出了 LLM-AutoSciLab，这是一个将假设生成与基于假设的实验选择及机制优化相结合的闭环框架。LLM-AutoSciLab 不再仅仅将模型拟合到被动收集的数据上，而是迭代地提出合理的假设，选择具有信息量的实验来区分或优化这些假设，并利用所得证据更新其状态。

To evaluate dynamic, closed-loop scientific discovery with active data acquisition, we introduce ActiveSciBench, comprising two datasets: ActiveSciBench-Chem with 57 enzyme-kinetics tasks and ActiveSciBench-GRN with 45 gene-regulatory-network tasks. These datasets model discovery as a budget-constrained process requiring adaptive experiment design, variable selection, and recovery of true mechanisms.

为了评估具有主动数据获取功能的动态闭环科学发现，我们引入了 ActiveSciBench，它包含两个数据集：包含 57 个酶动力学任务的 ActiveSciBench-Chem 和包含 45 个基因调控网络任务的 ActiveSciBench-GRN。这些数据集将科学发现建模为一个受预算约束的过程，要求具备自适应实验设计、变量选择以及恢复真实机制的能力。

Across NewtonBench, ActiveSciBench-Chem, and ActiveSciBench-GRN, LLM-AutoSciLab outperforms prior methods, achieving 67.6% and 35.1% symbolic accuracy on NewtonBench and ActiveSciBench-Chem, respectively, and 31.1% exact graph recovery on ActiveSciBench-GRN. Moreover, hypothesis-guided experimentation is 2-5x more sample-efficient than the strongest competing baselines.

在 NewtonBench、ActiveSciBench-Chem 和 ActiveSciBench-GRN 的测试中，LLM-AutoSciLab 的表现优于现有方法，在 NewtonBench 和 ActiveSciBench-Chem 上分别达到了 67.6% 和 35.1% 的符号准确率，并在 ActiveSciBench-GRN 上实现了 31.1% 的精确图恢复率。此外，与最强的对比基准相比，假设引导的实验在样本效率上提高了 2-5 倍。
