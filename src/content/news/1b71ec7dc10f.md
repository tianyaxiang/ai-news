# Hackers are mass-exploiting the cPanel bug to gain control of thousands of websites
# 黑客正大规模利用 cPanel 漏洞以获取数千个网站的控制权

Nearly a week after the makers of the popular web server management software cPanel and WebHost Manager (WHM) alerted users of a critical flaw in its software, hackers are now mass-compromising thousands of websites that rely on the vulnerable software.
在热门网络服务器管理软件 cPanel 和 WebHost Manager (WHM) 的开发商提醒用户其软件存在严重漏洞近一周后，黑客目前正大规模入侵数千个依赖该易受攻击软件的网站。

As of Monday, there are more than 550,000 potentially vulnerable servers running cPanel, a number that has remained stable for days. And there are now around 2,000 cPanel instances likely compromised, down from around 44,000 on Thursday. These statistics are published by Shadowserver, a nonprofit organization that scans and monitors the internet for cyberattacks.
截至周一，有超过 55 万台运行 cPanel 的服务器存在潜在风险，这一数字已连续数日保持稳定。目前约有 2,000 个 cPanel 实例可能已被入侵，低于周四的约 44,000 个。这些统计数据由非营利组织 Shadowserver 发布，该组织负责扫描和监控互联网上的网络攻击。

On Thursday, security researchers alerted that hackers started compromising servers running cPanel and WHM, taking advantage of a bug that allowed the attackers to take full control of and hijack the vulnerable servers via their control panels. As Bleeping Computer reported, some of the extent of damage so far is visible by the fact that Google has indexed dozens of websites that at some point displayed a message from a group of hackers that claimed to have encrypted the victim’s files in an apparent ransomware attack. Some of those sites now load normally.
周四，安全研究人员发出警告称，黑客开始利用一个漏洞入侵运行 cPanel 和 WHM 的服务器，该漏洞允许攻击者通过控制面板完全控制并劫持这些易受攻击的服务器。据 Bleeping Computer 报道，目前部分受损情况已显现：谷歌索引了数十个网站，这些网站曾一度显示来自黑客组织的信息，声称已在一次明显的勒索软件攻击中加密了受害者的文件。其中一些网站目前已恢复正常加载。

The ransom note included a chat ID for the victims to contact the hackers, who did not immediately respond to TechCrunch’s request for comment. U.S. Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) warned on Thursday that the vulnerability — tracked as CVE-2026-41940 — was being exploited in the wild, and added it to its Known Exploited Vulnerabilities (KEV) catalog. CISA asked government agencies to patch by Sunday. CISA did not immediately respond to a request for comment, asking whether it could confirm that government agencies have patched their servers.
勒索信中包含一个聊天 ID，供受害者联系黑客，但黑客未立即回应 TechCrunch 的置评请求。美国网络安全与基础设施安全局 (CISA) 周四警告称，该漏洞（编号为 CVE-2026-41940）正被广泛利用，并将其列入“已知被利用漏洞”(KEV) 目录。CISA 要求政府机构在周日前完成补丁修复。CISA 未能立即回应关于其是否能确认政府机构已修复服务器的置评请求。

The attacks against web servers running cPanel and WHM have likely been ongoing since much earlier than the vulnerability was disclosed. According to KnownHost CEO Daniel Pearson, his company detected attacks as far back as February 23. An unnamed spokesperson for cPanel acknowledged receipt of TechCrunch’s request for comment, but did not provide a response.
针对运行 cPanel 和 WHM 的网络服务器的攻击可能早在漏洞披露之前就已经开始了。据 KnownHost 首席执行官 Daniel Pearson 称，他的公司早在 2 月 23 日就检测到了攻击。cPanel 的一位匿名发言人确认收到了 TechCrunch 的置评请求，但未提供回应。
