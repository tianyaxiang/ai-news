### A Spectral Phase Diagram for Binary Few-Shot Classification: Intrinsic Dimensionality, Geometric Saturation, and Representational Diagnosis
### 二元少样本分类的谱相图：内在维度、几何饱和度与表征诊断

**Abstract:** Deciding when to stop collecting labeled examples is a fundamental but undertheorized problem in applied machine learning. The saturation index $S(K) = \operatorname{erank}(\widehat{\Sigma}_W^{(K)}) / K$ measures the ratio of the effective rank of the pooled within-class sample covariance to the shot count; we prove it falls below a threshold precisely when the covariance estimator is well-concentrated around the population covariance and the linear discriminant has stabilized.

**摘要：** 决定何时停止收集标注样本是应用机器学习中一个基础但理论研究不足的问题。饱和度指标 $S(K) = \operatorname{erank}(\widehat{\Sigma}_W^{(K)}) / K$ 衡量了池化类内样本协方差的有效秩与样本数（shot count）之比；我们证明，当协方差估计量在总体协方差周围良好集中且线性判别式趋于稳定时，该指标会精确地降至某个阈值以下。

The index is computable in $O(d^3)$ time from support features alone, requiring no test labels or trained classifier. Evaluated across $N = 246$ doubling-pair observations from seventeen binary tasks and six datasets, sixteen of seventeen tasks have a positive within-task Spearman correlation between $S(K)$ and marginal accuracy gain (median $\rho = 0.811$). The pooled Spearman correlation is $\rho = 0.548$ ($p = 1.1 \times 10^{-20}$, $N = 246$).

该指标仅需支持特征即可在 $O(d^3)$ 时间内计算得出，无需测试标签或已训练的分类器。通过对来自十七个二元任务和六个数据集的 $N = 246$ 组倍增对观测值进行评估，十七个任务中有十六个在任务内表现出 $S(K)$ 与边际准确率增益之间的正斯皮尔曼相关性（中位数 $\rho = 0.811$）。汇总后的斯皮尔曼相关系数为 $\rho = 0.548$ ($p = 1.1 \times 10^{-20}$, $N = 246$)。

A three-phase diagram (exploration, transition, saturation) with mean marginal gains of $3.48\%$, $2.40\%$, and $0.82\%$ is supported by all pairwise significance tests ($p \leq 0.008$). As a binary stopping rule, the index achieves AUC $= 0.752$, providing meaningful probabilistic guidance for annotation decisions. Asymptotic effective rank and peak accuracy show no significant monotone relationship across tasks (Spearman $r_s = 0.380$, $p = 0.133$, $N = 17$). A small saturation index paired with low accuracy diagnoses representational inadequacy. All results are for binary classification with a fixed linear classifier; extensions to $N$-way settings and pretrained backbone representations are discussed as future work.

一个包含三个阶段（探索、过渡、饱和）的相图，其平均边际增益分别为 $3.48\%$、$2.40\%$ 和 $0.82\%$，并得到了所有成对显著性检验的支持 ($p \leq 0.008$)。作为一种二元停止规则，该指标达到了 AUC $= 0.752$，为标注决策提供了有意义的概率指导。渐近有效秩与峰值准确率在各任务间未显示出显著的单调关系（斯皮尔曼 $r_s = 0.380$, $p = 0.133$, $N = 17$）。较小的饱和度指标配合低准确率，可诊断出表征能力的不足。所有结果均针对使用固定线性分类器的二元分类；针对 $N$ 路设置和预训练骨干表征的扩展将在未来工作中讨论。
