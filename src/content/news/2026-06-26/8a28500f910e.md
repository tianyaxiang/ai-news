# LLM Evolution as an Industry-Scale Ecosystem: A Lifecycle Perspective on Continual Learning
# 大语言模型演进作为工业级生态系统：持续学习的生命周期视角

**Abstract:** Continual learning capability is critical for Industrial LLMs, as deployed models must be continuously updated to meet evolving requirements and environments, rather than repeatedly retrained from scratch. However, most existing research focuses on improvements on static benchmarks, failing to capture real industrial needs.

**摘要：** 持续学习能力对于工业级大语言模型（LLM）至关重要，因为已部署的模型必须不断更新以满足不断变化的需求和环境，而不是每次都从头开始重新训练。然而，目前大多数研究集中在静态基准测试的改进上，未能捕捉到真实的工业需求。

In this survey, we reformulate Industrial Continual Learning (ICL) for LLMs as a closed-loop update-and-release problem in a versioned ecosystem, where updates propagate hierarchically to industrial, application-specific models and LLM-powered applications, with capability inheritance and transfer across versions and model families.

在本综述中，我们将大语言模型的工业持续学习（ICL）重新定义为一个版本化生态系统中的闭环更新与发布问题。在该系统中，更新会分层传播至工业模型、特定应用模型以及基于大模型的应用中，并实现跨版本和跨模型家族的能力继承与迁移。

From this ecosystem perspective, we identify three core challenges: repeated adaptation erodes model plasticity, foundation-model upgrades break capability inheritance, and long-term sustainability is constrained by deployment requirements.

从这一生态系统视角出发，我们确定了三个核心挑战：重复的适应过程会侵蚀模型的塑性（plasticity），基础模型的升级会破坏能力继承，且长期可持续性受到部署需求的制约。

We then organize the technical landscape of ICL around five lifecycle design principles: preserving plasticity headroom, treating upgrades as capability transfer, enabling trustworthy continual reinforcement learning, making training recipes self-optimizing, and building accountability as a base layer for long-term iteration. For each principle, we synthesize representative technical directions.

随后，我们围绕五个生命周期设计原则梳理了 ICL 的技术图谱：保留塑性空间、将升级视为能力迁移、实现可信的持续强化学习、使训练方案具备自优化能力，以及构建作为长期迭代基础层的问责机制。针对每一项原则，我们综合了具有代表性的技术方向。

Finally, we evaluate the maturity of each principle and its technical components via an evidence-based lens, identify key gaps hindering real-world deployment, and outline a practical ICL deployment blueprint and a pathway for feeding industrial realities back into academic research.

最后，我们通过循证视角评估了每一项原则及其技术组件的成熟度，识别了阻碍实际部署的关键差距，并勾勒出一份实用的 ICL 部署蓝图，以及将工业实践反馈回学术研究的路径。
