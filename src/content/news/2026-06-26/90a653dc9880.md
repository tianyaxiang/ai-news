# Noise-Aware Boundary-Enhanced Generative Learning for Ultrasound Speckle Reduction
# 用于超声散斑去噪的噪声感知边界增强生成学习

**Abstract:** Ultrasound is a non-invasive, real-time, and cost-effective imaging technique widely used in clinical diagnosis. However, its diagnostic efficacy is often compromised by inherent speckle noise that degrades image quality and obscures underlying anatomical structures.
**摘要：** 超声是一种非侵入性、实时且经济高效的成像技术，广泛应用于临床诊断。然而，其诊断效能常因固有的散斑噪声而受损，这些噪声会降低图像质量并掩盖潜在的解剖结构。

Existing speckle reduction methods tend to over-smooth tissue boundaries and generalize poorly to heterogeneous noise levels. To address these limitations, we propose a Noise-Aware Boundary-Enhanced Generative Learning (NBGL) framework for ultrasound speckle reduction, which simultaneously preserves annotated anatomical boundaries and adapts to varying noise levels.
现有的散斑去噪方法往往会过度平滑组织边界，且对异质噪声水平的泛化能力较差。为了解决这些局限性，我们提出了一种用于超声散斑去噪的噪声感知边界增强生成学习（NBGL）框架，该框架能够同时保留标注的解剖边界并适应不同的噪声水平。

The NBGL framework consists of a speckle reduction branch and a boundary enhancement branch. The former leverages generative learning to suppress speckle noise, while the latter learns boundary-sensitive representations to preserve target anatomical structures.
NBGL 框架由散斑去噪分支和边界增强分支组成。前者利用生成学习来抑制散斑噪声，而后者则学习边界敏感的表征以保留目标解剖结构。

Furthermore, a noise-aware interaction weight generation (NIWG) module estimates the speckle noise level via 3D Laplacian filtering and a median absolute deviation estimator, and translates it into an adaptive interaction weight. This weight is incorporated into a weighted feature-wise linear modulation (wFiLM) module to adaptively modulate cross-branch feature coupling, thereby improving robustness to varying noise levels.
此外，噪声感知交互权重生成（NIWG）模块通过 3D 拉普拉斯滤波和中位数绝对偏差估计器来估计散斑噪声水平，并将其转化为自适应交互权重。该权重被整合到加权特征线性调制（wFiLM）模块中，以自适应地调节跨分支特征耦合，从而提高对不同噪声水平的鲁棒性。

Extensive evaluations on 141 3D transvaginal ultrasound volumes demonstrate that NBGL consistently outperforms state-of-the-art methods in speckle reduction and structural preservation across six noise levels, while maintaining consistency with annotated anatomical boundaries.
在 141 例 3D 经阴道超声体积数据上的广泛评估表明，NBGL 在六种噪声水平下的散斑去噪和结构保留方面均持续优于现有最先进的方法，同时保持了与标注解剖边界的一致性。
