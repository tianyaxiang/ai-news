# When Do Conservation Laws Survive Learned Representations? Certified Horizons for Latent World Models
# 守恒定律何时能在学习到的表征中留存？潜在世界模型的认证视界

**Abstract:** We ask a representation-learning question about physical world models: when does a conservation law remain certifiable after a model learns a latent representation? A certified horizon bounds -- in advance, from measurable model defects -- how many steps a rollout provably stays on a physical invariant's level set. The key design choice is what is certified: not a learned latent Hamiltonian or a learned scalar witness (a model can conserve either while drifting in true energy), but the decoded physical invariant obtained by decoding the latent state and evaluating the known invariant.

**摘要：** 我们针对物理世界模型提出了一个表征学习问题：当模型学习到潜在表征后，守恒定律在何时仍是可认证的？“认证视界”（certified horizon）通过可测量的模型缺陷，预先界定了模型在推演过程中能够保持在物理不变量水平集上的步数。设计的关键在于认证的对象：并非学习到的潜在哈密顿量或标量见证函数（模型可能在保持这两者的同时，却在真实能量上发生漂移），而是通过解码潜在状态并评估已知不变量所得到的“解码物理不变量”。

Around this object we derive shell-horizon certificates whose budget decomposes into representation, readout, and latent-dynamics defects, with a monotone alignment bridge through which a soft learned witness yields a certified horizon for the decoded invariant, and test them across state, learned-lift, and pixel observations on conservative systems.

围绕这一对象，我们推导出了壳层视界证书（shell-horizon certificates），其误差预算分解为表征缺陷、读出缺陷和潜在动力学缺陷。通过一个单调对齐桥梁，软学习见证函数可以为解码后的不变量提供认证视界。我们在保守系统的状态观测、学习提升（learned-lift）观测以及像素观测中对这些证书进行了测试。

Conservation certificates can survive learned representation, but not all geometric priors survive equally: hard canonical symplectic structure yields the longest horizons in known phase coordinates yet does not cross a learned chart, whereas a controlled-Lipschitz-aligned soft invariant survives in the learned-representation settings we test; pixel certification is recovered on a readout-stable sub-tube; and the Kepler problem exposes a geometric boundary. The central object is therefore not a latent Hamiltonian, but a decoded physical invariant whose robustness to representation learning can be measured, certified, and falsified.

守恒证书可以在学习到的表征中留存，但并非所有几何先验的留存效果都相同：硬正则辛结构在已知相坐标中能产生最长的视界，却无法跨越学习到的图表（learned chart）；而受控的 Lipschitz 对齐软不变量则能在我们测试的学习表征设置中留存；像素认证在读出稳定的子管（sub-tube）上得以恢复；开普勒问题则揭示了一个几何边界。因此，核心对象并非潜在哈密顿量，而是解码后的物理不变量，其对表征学习的鲁棒性是可以被测量、认证和证伪的。
