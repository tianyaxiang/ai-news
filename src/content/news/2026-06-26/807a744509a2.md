苹果公司最近上调了 iPad 的价格，但如果你知道去哪里寻找，依然可以省下数百美元。

The good news is that many retailers are still selling their inventory at the old prices or far less, which means you can still score some of the best iPad deals we may see in awhile — if ever again. So if you’ve been thinking about picking up a new iPad, now is probably the time to do it.

The M4-powered iPad Air offers 12GB of RAM, Apple Intelligence features, Apple Pencil Pro support, and Wi-Fi 7 and Bluetooth 6, making it a great middle ground between the entry-level iPad and iPad Pro.
搭载 M4 芯片的 iPad Air 提供 12GB 内存、Apple Intelligence 功能、支持 Apple Pencil Pro，并配备 Wi-Fi 7 和蓝牙 6，使其成为入门级 iPad 和 iPad Pro 之间极佳的折中选择。

If the iPad Air isn’t what you’re after, though, don’t worry. There are still plenty of other iPad deals worth considering, whether you’re shopping for Apple’s affordable entry-level model or its high-end iPad Pro. Here are the best discounts we could find.
不过，如果 iPad Air 不是你的目标，也不必担心。还有许多其他值得考虑的 iPad 优惠，无论你是想购买苹果经济实惠的入门级机型，还是高端的 iPad Pro。以下是我们能找到的最优惠折扣。

### iPad (2025)
Apple’s entry-level iPad is an excellent tablet for casual use, even if it is getting a little long in the tooth. Its high-res screen is great for watching video, and its A16 processor is still fast enough to handle light games and productivity apps.
苹果的入门级 iPad 是一款非常适合日常使用的平板电脑，尽管它已经推出有一段时间了。它的高分辨率屏幕非常适合观看视频，其 A16 处理器处理轻度游戏和生产力应用程序依然绰绰有余。

### iPad Mini (2024)
The iPad Mini’s smaller size is tailor-made for travel, with a light, sturdy design that’s easy to slip into a carry-on. Its compact 8.3-inch display is great for reading, watching movies, and playing games, and it even supports the Apple Pencil Pro, making it an excellent tool for doodling. The battery holds up well, too, and you can easily pair it with a Bluetooth keyboard to tackle some light work.
iPad Mini 的小巧尺寸专为旅行打造，轻便坚固的设计使其易于放入随身行李中。其紧凑的 8.3 英寸显示屏非常适合阅读、看电影和玩游戏，它甚至支持 Apple Pencil Pro，使其成为涂鸦的绝佳工具。电池续航表现也很出色，你还可以轻松地将其与蓝牙键盘配对，以处理一些轻量级工作。

### iPad Pro (2025)
Apple’s newest iPad Pros have an M5 processor, an N1 chip, and a C1X chip for cellular versions. They’re faster, support Wi-Fi 7, charge more quickly, and start with 12GB of RAM.
苹果最新的 iPad Pro 搭载了 M5 处理器、N1 芯片，蜂窝网络版本还配备了 C1X 芯片。它们运行速度更快，支持 Wi-Fi 7，充电速度更快，且起步内存即为 12GB。
