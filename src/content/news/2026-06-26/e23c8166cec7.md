### 1. The LLM arbiter: the single LLM call at the end

This is the LLM call that Article 7B (anchor detection) placed at stage 3. That article produced candidates from each detector; this section is what happens to them. A candidate is a single passage a detector returned with its anchor (where it was matched), its aggregated unit (section, page, or chunk), and a snippet of surrounding context. The arbiter sees them all in one call, ranks them with reasons, and outputs the final list.
这就是 7B 文章（锚点检测）中提到的第三阶段大语言模型调用。上一篇文章由各个检测器生成了候选内容；本节将探讨如何处理这些内容。候选内容是指检测器返回的单个段落，包含其锚点（匹配位置）、聚合单元（章节、页面或块）以及一段周围的上下文。仲裁者在一次调用中查看所有内容，根据理由进行排序，并输出最终列表。

### 1.1 Score fusion is the wrong instinct
### 1.1 分数融合是错误的直觉

When multiple methods return candidates, the reflex is to combine their scores. The methods return numbers on different scales: cosine similarity, BM25 unbounded, and co-occurrence integer counts. Adding them is meaningless. Normalizing each method’s score does not help either, because a 0.9 cosine and a 0.9 normalized BM25 do not mean the same thing about the same candidate.
当多种方法返回候选内容时，人们的本能反应是合并它们的分数。但这些方法返回的数值量纲不同：余弦相似度、无界的 BM25 分数以及共现整数计数。将它们相加毫无意义。对每种方法的分数进行归一化也无济于事，因为 0.9 的余弦相似度和 0.9 的归一化 BM25 分数对于同一个候选内容并不代表相同的含义。

The classical answer is Reciprocal Rank Fusion (RRF). It sidesteps the calibration problem by ignoring scores and using ranks. Candidates ranked by several methods accumulate contributions; candidates seen by only one method get a single small term. RRF is the default in many vector DBs (Pinecone, Weaviate, Elastic) and works on common cases without tuning. But RRF leaves real signal on the floor.
经典的解决方案是倒数排名融合（RRF）。它通过忽略分数而仅使用排名来规避校准问题。被多种方法排名的候选内容会累积贡献值；仅被一种方法发现的候选内容则获得一个较小的分值。RRF 是许多向量数据库（如 Pinecone、Weaviate、Elastic）的默认设置，在常见场景下无需调优即可工作。但 RRF 会丢失真正的有效信息。

Why a method ranked a candidate matters. TOC ranked it because a section title matched. Keywords ranked it because premium and € co-occurred on the same line. Embeddings ranked it because something near the page was vaguely close in vector space. RRF compresses all of that to a rank index. The agreement across methods becomes a number, and the reason for the agreement disappears.
方法为何给候选内容排序至关重要。目录（TOC）排序是因为章节标题匹配；关键词排序是因为“premium”和“€”在同一行共现；向量排序是因为页面附近的内容在向量空间中较为接近。RRF 将所有这些信息压缩为一个排名索引。不同方法之间的一致性变成了一个数字，而达成一致的原因却消失了。

### 1.2 Hand the LLM a structured brief
### 1.2 为大语言模型提供结构化简报

The LLM is the layer that ranks. Its input is not “here are five passages, pick the best”. It is a structured brief, one row per candidate, listing what each retrieval method found out:
大语言模型是负责排序的层。它的输入不是“这里有五个段落，选出最好的”，而是一份结构化的简报，每个候选内容占一行，列出每种检索方法发现的信息：

*   **candidate_id**: stable reference (page + line range, or section + line offset).
*   **candidate_id**: 稳定引用（页面 + 行范围，或章节 + 行偏移量）。
*   **methods**: which retrieval methods showed this candidate (TOC, keywords, embeddings).
*   **methods**: 发现该候选内容的检索方法（目录、关键词、向量）。
*   **section**: the TOC section the candidate sits in, lifted from toc_df.
*   **section**: 候选内容所属的目录章节（从 `toc_df` 中提取）。
*   **matched_keywords**: the keywords from the parsed question that landed on this candidate.
*   **matched_keywords**: 解析后的问题中命中该候选内容的关键词。
*   **snippet**: three to five lines of surrounding context, lifted from line_df.
*   **snippet**: 三到五行的周围上下文（从 `line_df` 中提取）。

The brief is what the LLM reads. It looks like what an expert sees on their screen: a section heading on top, the matched keywords in the body, the lines around the match. Much closer to that than to a ranked list of cosine.
这就是大语言模型读取的内容。它看起来就像专家在屏幕上看到的一样：顶部是章节标题，正文中是匹配的关键词，以及匹配内容周围的行。这比单纯的余弦相似度排名列表要直观得多。
