# YouTube updates Shorts to make it even more like TikTok
# YouTube 更新 Shorts 功能，使其更像 TikTok

Similar to TikTok’s ‘clear mode,’ a new ‘clear screen’ feature will remove the clutter from the video you’re watching.
类似于 TikTok 的“清晰模式”（clear mode），一项全新的“清屏”（clear screen）功能将移除你正在观看的视频上的杂乱元素。

YouTube is adding even more TikTok-like features to Shorts, including a new “clear screen” mode that removes the icons and text from the video you’re watching. In a blog post on Thursday, YouTube says it’s also replacing the “thumbs-up” button with a “heart” icon, and is introducing a new option that lets you watch Shorts at 2x speed.
YouTube 正在为 Shorts 添加更多类似 TikTok 的功能，其中包括一项全新的“清屏”模式，可以移除视频画面上的图标和文字。YouTube 在周四的一篇博客文章中表示，他们还将把“点赞”（thumbs-up）按钮替换为“爱心”（heart）图标，并引入了一个新选项，允许用户以 2 倍速观看 Shorts。

You can now speed up a short by swiping down on your screen.
现在，你可以通过在屏幕上向下滑动来加速播放短视频。

As noted by YouTube, you can speed up a video by holding down the edge of your screen and lifting your finger when you want it to return to normal speed. You can lock the speed at 2x by pressing the player and swiping down. Another change allows you to mute watching by tapping the video and selecting the “mute” button.
正如 YouTube 所述，你可以通过按住屏幕边缘来加速视频，松开手指即可恢复正常速度。你也可以通过按住播放器并向下滑动来锁定 2 倍速播放。另一项改动是，用户现在可以通过点击视频并选择“静音”（mute）按钮来关闭声音。

These new additions make Shorts even more like TikTok, which has had the similarly-named “clear mode” for years — a feature that also removes captions, icons, and usernames from your screen. TikTok already offers a way to speed up videos as well, and lets users “like” a video by pressing a “heart” button.
这些新功能的加入使 Shorts 变得更像 TikTok。TikTok 多年来一直拥有同名的“清晰模式”，该功能同样可以从屏幕上移除字幕、图标和用户名。此外，TikTok 也早已提供了视频加速功能，并允许用户通过点击“爱心”按钮来点赞视频。

Along with these updates, YouTube is removing the “dislike” button. It’s encouraging users to share their feedback with the platform by selecting the “Not interested” and “Don’t recommend this channel” options from the three-dots menu instead.
伴随这些更新，YouTube 正在移除“不喜欢”（dislike）按钮。平台鼓励用户通过点击三点菜单中的“不感兴趣”（Not interested）和“不推荐此频道”（Don’t recommend this channel）选项来反馈意见。
