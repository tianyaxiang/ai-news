# SEMIR: Topology-Preserving Graph Minors for Thin-Structure Segmentation

**Abstract:** Thin-structure segmentation--power lines, cracks, lane markings at 1-3 pixel width--requires preserving connectivity that standard representations preclude: patching severs continuous structures and conventional superpixels merge thin targets into background before classification.

**摘要：** 细长结构分割（如 1-3 像素宽的电力线、裂缝、车道线）需要保持连通性，但标准表示方法往往无法做到这一点：分块处理（patching）会切断连续结构，而传统的超像素方法在分类前会将细长目标合并到背景中。

Topology-aware losses penalize connectivity breaks at the objective level but cannot recover what the representation has already destroyed. We propose SEMIR, a framework that replaces the pixel lattice with a parameterized graph minor whose contraction map preserves thin-structure connectivity under the contraction criterion.

拓扑感知损失函数虽然能在目标层面惩罚连通性中断，但无法恢复已被表示方法破坏的结构。我们提出了 SEMIR 框架，它用参数化的图子式（graph minor）取代了像素网格，其收缩映射在收缩准则下能够保持细长结构的连通性。

The minor collapses millions of pixels into tens or hundreds of boundary-aligned supernodes, enabling full-resolution inference without patching at scales demonstrated up to 21 MP in this paper; a lightweight GNN classifies the reduced graph and an exact map lifts predictions to pixel resolution.

该图子式将数百万像素压缩为数十或数百个与边界对齐的超节点，从而实现了无需分块的全分辨率推理，本文展示了其在高达 2100 万像素（21 MP）规模下的应用；轻量级图神经网络（GNN）对简化后的图进行分类，并通过精确映射将预测结果提升至像素分辨率。

One pipeline--identical architecture, features, loss, and GNN hyperparameters across all dataset--matches or exceeds domain-specific baselines on TTPLA (power lines), CrackSeg9k (pavement cracks), and SkyScapes Lane (aerial markings) on Dice, IoU, and Boundary F1 while reducing mask fragmentation by at least 4.6x relative to SLIC at matched inference.

该流水线在所有数据集上使用相同的架构、特征、损失函数和 GNN 超参数，在 TTPLA（电力线）、CrackSeg9k（路面裂缝）和 SkyScapes Lane（航拍标记）数据集上的 Dice、IoU 和 Boundary F1 指标均达到或超过了特定领域的基准模型，同时在同等推理条件下，掩码碎片化程度比 SLIC 降低了至少 4.6 倍。
