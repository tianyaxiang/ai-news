### Polygraph

**Launching today**
今天正式发布

**Let AI agents see cross repo and maintain session memory.**
让 AI 智能体实现跨仓库可见性并保持会话记忆。

**AI Coding Agents**
AI 编程智能体

**AI coding agents are limited to how autonomously they can work because they have no model of the codebase as a whole. Polygraph is a meta-harness that gives agents what they're missing: Visibility across every repo boundary and memory that survives the session.**
目前的 AI 编程智能体在自主工作能力上受到限制，因为它们缺乏对整个代码库的全局模型。Polygraph 是一个元框架（meta-harness），它为智能体补齐了缺失的关键能力：跨越所有仓库边界的可见性，以及能够跨会话持久保存的记忆。

**Connect all your repos, private and public, into a unified dependency graph without moving any code. Resume, reference or build on any session created by any developer, on another machine, even on different agents.**
无需迁移任何代码，即可将你所有的私有和公共仓库连接成一个统一的依赖关系图。你可以随时恢复、引用或基于任何开发者在其他机器上、甚至在不同智能体上创建的会话进行开发。
