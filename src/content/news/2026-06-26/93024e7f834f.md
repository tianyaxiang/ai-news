# FreeStory: Training-Free Character Consistency for Free-Form Visual Storytelling
# FreeStory：面向自由形式视觉叙事的免训练角色一致性框架

Visual storytelling aims to generate image sequences that are both aligned with narrative prompts and consistent in character appearance across images. Recent training-free methods improve character consistency by reusing attention features, but rely on structured prompts where full character descriptions are repeated in every prompt.
视觉叙事旨在生成既符合叙事提示词，又能保持跨图像角色外观一致性的图像序列。近期的免训练方法通过重用注意力特征来提升角色一致性，但这些方法依赖于结构化的提示词，即要求在每个提示词中重复完整的角色描述。

This assumption simplifies the task but deviates from natural storytelling, where characters are typically introduced once and later referred to using pronouns or type-based expressions. We propose **FreeStory**, a training-free framework that reformulates character consistency under free-form prompts as entity-grounded feature reuse.
这种假设简化了任务，但偏离了自然叙事的方式——在自然叙事中，角色通常只在首次出现时进行介绍，后续则通过代词或基于类型的表达来指代。我们提出了 **FreeStory**，这是一个免训练框架，它将自由形式提示词下的角色一致性问题重新定义为“基于实体的特征重用”。

Our method associates reference mentions with their corresponding character descriptions and combines dynamic character masks, correspondence-aware feature matching, key-value injection, and query blending to preserve identity while retaining generation diversity.
我们的方法将引用提及与相应的角色描述相关联，并结合了动态角色掩码、对应感知特征匹配、键值注入以及查询融合技术，在保持角色身份一致性的同时，保留了生成的丰富多样性。

We also introduce **FreeStoryBench**, a benchmark for this setting that includes both single- and multi-character stories. Experiments show that FreeStory achieves state-of-the-art performance among training-free methods on structured benchmarks and stronger overall consistency over baselines under free-form prompts.
我们还推出了 **FreeStoryBench**，这是一个针对该场景的基准测试集，涵盖了单角色和多角色叙事。实验表明，FreeStory 在结构化基准测试中达到了免训练方法中的领先水平，并在自由形式提示词下展现出比基准模型更强的一致性。
