# OpenSEO: An Open Source Alternative to Semrush and Ahrefs

OpenSEO is an SEO tool for the people. If tools like Semrush or Ahrefs are too expensive or bloated, OpenSEO is a pay-as-you-go alternative that you actually control. All-in-one SEO tool for you and your AI agent. Connect with any agent like Claude Code, OpenClaw, or Hermes. We have pre-built skills, but you can build your own to tailor OpenSEO to your needs.

OpenSEO 是一款面向大众的 SEO 工具。如果觉得 Semrush 或 Ahrefs 等工具过于昂贵或臃肿，OpenSEO 提供了一个按需付费且完全由你掌控的替代方案。这是一个为你和你的 AI 智能体准备的一体化 SEO 工具，可以连接 Claude Code、OpenClaw 或 Hermes 等任何智能体。我们提供了预设技能，你也可以构建自己的技能，根据需求定制 OpenSEO。

### Main SEO Workflows
*   **Keyword research:** Find topics worth targeting, estimate demand, and prioritize what to write next.
*   **Rank tracking:** Monitor keyword positions across desktop and mobile over time, with SERP feature detection.
*   **Domain insights:** Understand where your domain is gaining or losing visibility so you can focus on the pages that move revenue.
*   **Backlinks:** See who links to your site, which pages attract links, and where links are newly won or lost.
*   **Site Audits:** Catch technical issues early so your site is easier for search engines to crawl and rank.
*   **AI brand visibility:** See how your brand appears in AI answers, including competitor mentions and source coverage.
*   **AI search prompt explorer:** Track and explore the prompts people might use when they ask AI tools for recommendations in your market.

### 主要 SEO 工作流
*   **关键词研究：** 寻找值得定位的主题，预估需求，并确定优先撰写的内容。
*   **排名追踪：** 监控关键词在桌面端和移动端的长期排名变化，并支持 SERP 特性检测。
*   **域名洞察：** 了解域名的可见性变化，从而专注于那些能带来收益的页面。
*   **反向链接：** 查看谁链接到了你的网站，哪些页面吸引了链接，以及链接的获取与流失情况。
*   **网站审计：** 尽早发现技术问题，使你的网站更易于被搜索引擎抓取和排名。
*   **AI 品牌可见度：** 查看你的品牌在 AI 回答中的呈现方式，包括竞争对手提及和来源覆盖情况。
*   **AI 搜索提示词探索：** 追踪并探索人们在向 AI 工具寻求市场建议时可能使用的提示词。

### OpenSEO MCP
OpenSEO exposes an MCP server so AI agents can use your SEO data directly. Connect Codex, Claude Code, Claude Desktop, or another MCP client to run keyword research, inspect SERPs, compare domains, or review backlinks directly from your editor or chat.

### OpenSEO MCP
OpenSEO 提供了一个 MCP 服务器，使 AI 智能体能够直接使用你的 SEO 数据。你可以连接 Codex、Claude Code、Claude Desktop 或其他 MCP 客户端，直接在编辑器或聊天窗口中进行关键词研究、查看 SERP、对比域名或审查反向链接。

OpenSEO Agent Skills are reusable workflows for Codex and Claude Code. They guide your agent through SEO tasks and can use the OpenSEO MCP for live keyword, SERP, backlink, and domain data.

### OpenSEO 智能体技能

### Pricing / Costs
OpenSEO is totally free to use. It works by using DataForSEO's APIs, which is a paid third-party service unaffiliated with OpenSEO. There are two separate things:
1.  **OpenSEO app cost:** $0, you host it yourself.
2.  **DataForSEO API:** pay-as-you-go based on usage.

### 定价与成本
OpenSEO 完全免费。它通过调用 DataForSEO 的 API 运行，这是一家与 OpenSEO 无关联的第三方付费服务商。这里涉及两部分费用：
1.  **OpenSEO 应用成本：** 0 美元，由你自行托管。
2.  **DataForSEO API：** 按实际使用量付费。

### Self-hosting
OpenSEO supports two self-hosting paths:
*   **Docker:** For personal use and testing (Recommended for local use).
*   **Cloudflare:** For internet-facing self-hosting across multiple devices or for your team.

### 自行托管
OpenSEO 支持两种托管方式：
*   **Docker：** 适用于个人使用和测试（推荐本地使用）。
*   **Cloudflare：** 适用于面向互联网的托管，方便多设备访问或团队协作。
