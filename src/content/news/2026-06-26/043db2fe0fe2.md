# Google finally releases a Finance Android app, promises iOS version later in 2026
# 谷歌终于发布了 Google Finance 安卓版应用，并承诺将于 2026 年晚些时候推出 iOS 版本

Google Finance is not a new product—it has been around for 20 years, long enough that it initially relied on Flash to display charts and graphs. The website has gotten a few major updates over the years, but it has never had a mobile app until now.
Google Finance 并不是一个新产品——它已经存在了 20 年，时间久到它最初还需要依赖 Flash 来显示图表和图形。多年来，该网站经历了几次重大更新，但直到现在才拥有移动应用程序。

Google has released the first standalone app for Google Finance, which is currently exclusive to Android, with iOS planned for later this year. The app is available globally in the Play Store, but that’s not the only update to Google’s financial tracker.
谷歌发布了首款 Google Finance 独立应用程序，目前仅限安卓平台使用，iOS 版本计划于今年晚些时候推出。该应用已在全球 Play 商店上架，但这并不是谷歌金融追踪器的唯一更新。

The AI-powered makeover for the Finance website is also leaving beta, making Google’s chatbot a core part of the experience. Naturally, the mobile app includes a heaping helping of generative AI that aims to make sense of irrational financial markets.
Google Finance 网站的 AI 升级版也已结束测试阶段，谷歌的聊天机器人现已成为该体验的核心部分。理所当然地，这款移动应用也集成了大量的生成式 AI 功能，旨在帮助用户解读非理性的金融市场。

If you’ve checked out the new Finance web experience, you’ll see a lot of familiar features in the app. You can create watchlists, monitor real-time market data, and keep up with financial news in one place. While perusing graphs of stock performance, Finance will use AI to generate “key moments” that can explain why the numbers changed. This feature initially launched in the Finance web interface in May.
如果你体验过全新的 Finance 网页版，你会发现应用中有很多熟悉的功能。你可以在一个地方创建自选股列表、监控实时市场数据并跟进财经新闻。在查看股票表现图表时，Finance 会利用 AI 生成“关键时刻”，解释数据变化的原因。该功能最初于 5 月在 Finance 网页界面中推出。

The mobile app also gets Google’s new AI research tool, accessible via the “Ask” button floating at the bottom of the UI. This allows users to converse with Google’s money-tuned bot about stocks. The bottom bar also includes a History section where you can easily access your past chats.
这款移动应用还配备了谷歌新的 AI 研究工具，用户可以通过界面底部悬浮的“Ask”（提问）按钮进行访问。这允许用户与谷歌专门针对金融领域调优的机器人讨论股票。底部栏还包含一个“历史记录”部分，方便用户查看过往的对话。

Google says that the current Android app is just a starting point. It plans to adapt more features from the new website to the app over time. Consequently, the updated website has a few features you won’t find in the app. While you can build a watchlist in the app by searching for stock symbols, the website has an expansive portfolio feature.
谷歌表示，目前的安卓应用只是一个起点。它计划随着时间的推移，将网站上的更多功能适配到应用中。因此，更新后的网站目前拥有一些你在应用中找不到的功能。虽然你可以在应用中通过搜索股票代码来建立自选股列表，但网站拥有更强大的投资组合功能。

Portfolios from the old Finance will port over, gaining new AI insights and suggestions. You can also upload a CSV or PDF to build a trackable portfolio in Google Finance. The chatbot has access to your portfolio data and can answer questions taking that into account.
旧版 Finance 中的投资组合将被迁移过来，并获得新的 AI 洞察和建议。你还可以上传 CSV 或 PDF 文件，在 Google Finance 中建立可追踪的投资组合。聊天机器人可以访问你的投资组合数据，并据此回答问题。

The Finance website is also gaining a new AI-powered research tool that can send you periodic updates. Google suggests something like: “Send me a daily pre-market briefing analyzing significant overnight moves across major cryptocurrencies.” Whatever you’re interested in, be that crypto or something marginally less sketchy, you’ll get notifications through the mobile app when your research reports are ready. They will also be viewable in the web version’s research panel.
Finance 网站还增加了一个新的 AI 研究工具，可以定期向你发送更新。谷歌建议的用法如：“每天给我发送一份盘前简报，分析主要加密货币隔夜的重要波动。”无论你对什么感兴趣，无论是加密货币还是其他相对稳健的资产，当研究报告准备好时，你都会通过移动应用收到通知。这些报告也可以在网页版的研究面板中查看。

Few industries have adopted generative AI as readily as finance. Many of the investments and market trends that lead to numbers going up or down are driven by AI models. So maybe a hallucinatory robot is what you need to make sense of the nonsense. Google has you covered there.
很少有行业像金融业这样迅速地采用生成式 AI。许多导致市场数据涨跌的投资和趋势都是由 AI 模型驱动的。所以，也许你需要一个会产生“幻觉”的机器人来帮你理清这些荒谬的市场逻辑。谷歌已经为你准备好了。
