# OpenAI will delay GPT-5.6 after Trump administration request
# 应特朗普政府要求，OpenAI 将推迟发布 GPT-5.6

The Trump administration, apprehensive of potential security issues, has reportedly asked OpenAI to stagger the release of its next big-ticket model, GPT-5.6.
由于担心潜在的安全问题，据报道，特朗普政府已要求 OpenAI 分阶段发布其下一款重磅模型 GPT-5.6。

The Information reported that OpenAI CEO Sam Altman told employees Wednesday in a company Q&A that it would release GPT-5.6 in limited preview form — granting access only to a small group of enterprise customers — in compliance with a request from the federal government. During that preview period, the Trump administration itself would reportedly approve access for customers on a case-by-case basis.
据《The Information》报道，OpenAI 首席执行官山姆·奥特曼（Sam Altman）周三在公司问答环节中告诉员工，为响应联邦政府的要求，公司将以有限预览的形式发布 GPT-5.6，仅向少数企业客户开放访问权限。在预览期间，据称特朗普政府将对客户的访问申请进行逐一审批。

It’s a more favorable deal than the Trump administration gave OpenAI rival Anthropic, which earlier this month received an ultimatum requiring it to suspend access to its Mythos 5 and Fable 5 models. The administration issued an export control directive that prohibited “foreign nationals” from accessing the technology (including any of Anthropic’s own employees who were not US citizens).
相比之下，这一待遇要优于 OpenAI 的竞争对手 Anthropic。本月初，Anthropic 接到了最后通牒，被要求暂停其 Mythos 5 和 Fable 5 模型的访问权限。当时，政府发布了一项出口管制指令，禁止“外国国民”访问该技术（包括 Anthropic 内部非美国公民的员工）。

The seemingly heavy-handed approach to AI regulation, after promises from the Trump administration that it would take a “speed wins” approach to the technology and encourage an American AI exports program, raised alarm bells across the tech industry.
此前，特朗普政府曾承诺将对人工智能技术采取“速度取胜”的策略，并鼓励美国的 AI 出口计划。然而，此次看似强硬的监管手段在科技行业引发了警觉。

Now, some of those concerns seem to be coming to pass — and in a decidedly uneven way, depending on the company.
如今，这些担忧似乎正在成为现实，而且根据公司的不同，其受到的影响也明显不尽相同。
