# Yuvion VL: A Multimodal Foundation Model for Adversarial Content and AI Safety
# Yuvion VL：用于对抗性内容与人工智能安全的多模态基础模型

**Abstract:** General-purpose models often struggle to reliably identify and understand real-world multimodal risks, largely due to the inherent multimodal adversarial nature of content and AI safety. We present Yuvion VL, a family of multimodal large language models purpose-built for content and AI safety, with both instruction-tuned and reasoning-oriented variants.

**摘要：** 通用模型往往难以可靠地识别和理解现实世界中的多模态风险，这在很大程度上归因于内容和人工智能安全中固有的多模态对抗性。我们推出了 Yuvion VL，这是一个专为内容和人工智能安全而构建的多模态大语言模型系列，包含指令微调版和推理导向版。

Yuvion VL addresses this gap by treating safety as an inherently adversarial and multimodal problem and designing the entire pipeline around adversarial robustness. For data construction, we develop an automated pipeline integrating adversarial-aware data synthesis with multi-stage quality control, producing large-scale, high-quality multimodal samples augmented with domain knowledge and reasoning annotations.

Yuvion VL 通过将安全性视为一个本质上的对抗性和多模态问题，并围绕对抗性鲁棒性设计整个流程，从而填补了这一空白。在数据构建方面，我们开发了一个自动化流程，将对抗感知数据合成与多阶段质量控制相结合，生成了大规模、高质量的多模态样本，并辅以领域知识和推理注释。

For training, we adopt a three-stage pipeline that includes continued pretraining for risk-concept cross-modal alignment, instruct post-training for production-grade safety tasks, and reasoning post-training for enhanced interpretability and performance in complex tasks. We further introduce Confuse-then-Contrast Fine-Tuning, a contrastive framework that mines model-specific confusions and constructs multi-image contrastive groups to enforce explicit discrimination of fine-grained visual-semantic elements, enabling the model to distinguish between visually similar cases with different safety implications in adversarial safety tasks.

在训练方面，我们采用了三阶段流程，包括用于风险概念跨模态对齐的持续预训练、用于生产级安全任务的指令后训练，以及用于增强复杂任务中可解释性和性能的推理后训练。我们进一步引入了“混淆后对比”（Confuse-then-Contrast）微调方法，这是一种对比框架，通过挖掘模型特定的混淆点并构建多图像对比组，强制模型对细粒度的视觉语义元素进行明确区分，使模型能够在对抗性安全任务中区分视觉相似但安全含义不同的案例。

To support rigorous evaluation, we further introduce Yuvion VL RiskEval (YVRE), a collection of benchmarks covering diverse open and internal evaluations, with a focus on content and AI safety, adversarial robustness, and real-world capability requirements. Experiments show that Yuvion VL-32B achieves industry-leading safety performance, surpassing comparably sized open-source models and best closed-source commercial models, while maintaining comparable general capabilities.

为了支持严格的评估，我们进一步推出了 Yuvion VL RiskEval (YVRE)，这是一套涵盖多种公开和内部评估的基准测试集，重点关注内容与人工智能安全、对抗性鲁棒性以及现实世界的能力需求。实验表明，Yuvion VL-32B 实现了行业领先的安全性能，超越了同等规模的开源模型和最优秀的闭源商业模型，同时保持了相当的通用能力。
