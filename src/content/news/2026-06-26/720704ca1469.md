# From Meta Idea to Advanced Mathematical Discovery -- Human-AI Co-Discovery of Sign-Embedding Quantum Algorithms
# 从元构思到高等数学发现——人机协同探索符号嵌入量子算法

**Abstract:** AI-assisted mathematics is often evaluated on solving predefined problems. In practice, however, many important advances begin earlier, when a vague research intuition is transformed into a concrete problem, a promising route, and a theorem family worth proving. This report studies that stage through a case study that led to sign-embedding quantum algorithms for matrix equations and matrix functions, foundational primitives in quantum linear algebra and operator-output quantum algorithms.

**摘要：** 人工智能辅助数学研究通常以解决预定义问题作为评估标准。然而在实践中，许多重要的进展往往始于更早的阶段——即当模糊的研究直觉被转化为具体问题、有前景的研究路径以及值得证明的定理族时。本报告通过一个案例研究探讨了这一阶段，该研究最终导出了用于矩阵方程和矩阵函数的“符号嵌入量子算法”（sign-embedding quantum algorithms），这是量子线性代数和算子输出量子算法中的基础原语。

The project began with a human-originated intuition that rational approximation is especially effective for jump-type functions such as the sign function, and might therefore serve as a design principle for quantum algorithms. Rather than merely assisting after the problem was fixed, AI-assisted exploration, including workflows later integrated into the agentic AI-mathematician system AIM, played a key role in expanding this intuition into a route map, comparing candidate formulations, and converging toward sign embedding as the central framework.

该项目始于人类研究者的一种直觉：有理逼近对于符号函数（sign function）等跳跃型函数特别有效，因此可能作为量子算法的一种设计原则。AI 辅助探索并未仅仅在问题确定后提供帮助，而是发挥了关键作用——包括后来整合进智能 AI 数学家系统 AIM 中的工作流——将这一直觉扩展为路线图，比较了候选公式，并最终收敛于以“符号嵌入”为核心的框架。

AIM then helped connect a known matrix-sign identity to wider classes of matrix equations and matrix functions, and drafted proof and complexity calculations. The decisive scientific judgments remained human: selecting which human-AI-expanded routes were worth pursuing, rejecting a Cayley-trapezoidal approximation when its validity required a hidden condition, and refining the Sylvester implementation from a coarse quadratic-gap query route to the final factorized and scaled analysis.

随后，AIM 协助将已知的矩阵符号恒等式与更广泛的矩阵方程和矩阵函数类别联系起来，并起草了证明过程和复杂度计算。决定性的科学判断仍由人类做出：选择哪些经人机扩展后的路径值得深入研究，在 Cayley-梯形逼近法的有效性需要隐含条件时将其否决，并将 Sylvester 实现从粗略的二次间隙查询路径优化为最终的因子分解与缩放分析。

The report argues that human-AI co-discovery workflows, with systems such as AIM as important components, are most valuable not as standalone theorem provers, but as research partners for problem formation, connection discovery, derivation, and skeptical review inside a human-gated research loop.

本报告认为，以 AIM 等系统为重要组成部分的人机协同发现工作流，其最大价值不在于作为独立的定理证明器，而在于作为研究伙伴，在人类把关的研究闭环内，协助进行问题形成、关联发现、推导以及批判性审查。
