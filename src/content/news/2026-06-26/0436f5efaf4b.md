# Title: I built an AI project manager for dev teams because Jira was too much and Trello was too little — meet Rahnuma.io 🚀
# 标题：我为开发团队打造了一款 AI 项目经理，因为 Jira 太繁琐而 Trello 又太简单——隆重介绍 Rahnuma.io 🚀

After months of building in public, Rahnuma.io is live on Product Hunt today! 🎉
经过数月的公开构建，Rahnuma.io 今天正式在 Product Hunt 上线了！🎉

The problem: Every dev team I've worked with ends up in the same trap: Jira is too heavy and slows everyone down with ceremony, while Trello/Notion are too light and can't actually tell you if your sprint is on track. Nobody had a tool that combined real project management with AI that actually understands developer workflows. So I built one.
问题所在：我合作过的每一个开发团队最终都会陷入同样的困境：Jira 太过沉重，繁琐的流程拖慢了每个人的进度；而 Trello/Notion 又太轻量，无法真正告诉你冲刺（Sprint）是否按计划进行。市面上没有一款工具能将真正的项目管理与真正理解开发者工作流的 AI 结合起来。所以我自己做了一个。

What is Rahnuma.io? Rahnuma.io is an AI-powered DevOps platform that sits where project management meets your actual engineering workflow:
什么是 Rahnuma.io？Rahnuma.io 是一个 AI 驱动的 DevOps 平台，它位于项目管理与实际工程工作流的交汇点：

*   🧠 AI task generation — describe a feature in plain English, get a structured task with subtasks
*   🧠 AI 任务生成 — 用简单的英语描述一个功能，即可获得包含子任务的结构化任务列表
*   📊 Deadline risk forecasting — a live risk score (0–100) built from time risk, blockers, and completion rate, so you know a sprint is in trouble before it blows up
*   📊 截止日期风险预测 — 基于时间风险、阻塞因素和完成率生成的实时风险评分（0-100），让你在冲刺出现问题前就能提前预知
*   🗂️ Kanban + sprints — drag-and-drop boards, WIP limits, burndown charts, story points
*   🗂️ 看板 + 冲刺 — 支持拖拽式看板、在制品（WIP）限制、燃尽图和故事点
*   🤖 AI sprint retros — auto-generated "what went well / what didn't / action items"
*   🤖 AI 冲刺回顾 — 自动生成“做得好的地方 / 不足之处 / 行动项”
*   🔗 GitHub & Bitbucket integration — see commits next to the tasks they belong to
*   🔗 GitHub 和 Bitbucket 集成 — 在任务旁边直接查看对应的代码提交记录
*   📈 Reports that don't require a translator — including an "Explain to My Boss" button that turns your sprint into a plain-English executive summary
*   📈 无需翻译的报告 — 包含一个“向老板解释”按钮，可将你的冲刺进度转化为通俗易懂的执行摘要
*   🔔 Slack notifications for task creation, assignment, and comments
*   🔔 针对任务创建、分配和评论的 Slack 通知
*   🧾 Client portals — shareable, printable progress reports for non-technical stakeholders
*   🧾 客户门户 — 为非技术利益相关者提供可分享、可打印的进度报告

Why it's different: Most "AI project management" tools just bolt a chatbot onto a Trello clone. Rahnuma.io's AI is wired directly into the data model — it knows your sprint history, your velocity, your blockers — so the forecasts and summaries are grounded in your actual project state, not a generic prompt.
它有何不同：大多数“AI 项目管理”工具只是在 Trello 的克隆版上强行加了一个聊天机器人。而 Rahnuma.io 的 AI 直接接入了数据模型——它了解你的冲刺历史、开发速度和阻塞因素——因此预测和摘要是基于你项目的实际状态，而非通用的提示词。

Tech under the hood: Next.js 15 (Turbopack) · TypeScript · Prisma + PostgreSQL · Clerk auth · Polar billing · Redis · xAI Grok with Groq fallback for AI · Server-Sent Events for realtime sync.
技术栈：Next.js 15 (Turbopack) · TypeScript · Prisma + PostgreSQL · Clerk 身份验证 · Polar 计费 · Redis · xAI Grok（AI 备用方案为 Groq） · 用于实时同步的服务器发送事件（SSE）。
