# Dense Supervision Is Not Enough: The Readout Blind Spot in Looped Language Models
# 密集监督并不足够：循环语言模型中的读出盲点

**Abstract:** Looped language models turn hidden states into runtime state: each state is decoded for prediction and fed back into future computation. This creates a basic supervision question: which state variables does cross-entropy actually control? We show that dense per-loop cross-entropy controls the variables exposed by the readout, not every variable active in the recurrent transition.

**摘要：** 循环语言模型（Looped language models）将隐藏状态转化为运行时状态：每个状态都被解码用于预测，并反馈到后续的计算中。这引发了一个基本的监督问题：交叉熵实际上控制了哪些状态变量？我们证明，密集的逐循环交叉熵仅控制读出（readout）所暴露的变量，而非循环转换中活跃的所有变量。

Hidden-state scale gives a concrete failure mode. Scale-invariant readouts such as RMSNorm and LayerNorm hide radial scale from the immediate cross-entropy loss, while pre-norm residual recurrence continues to carry and update that same scale. Thus per-loop loss can make early exits usable without controlling recurrent scale.

隐藏状态的尺度（Scale）提供了一个具体的失效模式。诸如 RMSNorm 和 LayerNorm 等尺度不变的读出机制，将径向尺度从即时的交叉熵损失中隐藏起来，而预归一化（pre-norm）的残差循环却持续携带并更新该尺度。因此，逐循环损失可以在不控制循环尺度的情况下，使早期退出（early exits）变得可用。

In 44M and 129M looped transformers without inter-loop normalization, per-loop cross-entropy through RMSNorm readouts still drives final hidden-state norms into the thousands or tens of thousands. Scale-visible readouts and explicit norm penalties keep norms in the tens, and scale-removing recurrence is the complementary architectural fix.

在没有循环间归一化的 44M 和 129M 参数循环 Transformer 中，通过 RMSNorm 读出的逐循环交叉熵，仍会将最终隐藏状态的范数（norms）推高至数千甚至数万。而尺度可见的读出机制和显式的范数惩罚则能将范数保持在几十的水平，消除尺度的循环机制则是互补的架构修复方案。

The resulting design rule is simple: dense supervision trains exits; recurrent scale control requires either making scale visible to a loss or removing it from the loop. Consistent with this rule, scale-controlled variants achieve lower perplexity at matched inference-depth operating points in our variable-depth benchmarks.

由此得出的设计规则很简单：密集监督用于训练退出机制；而循环尺度控制则需要要么让尺度对损失函数可见，要么将其从循环中移除。与该规则一致，在我们的可变深度基准测试中，尺度受控的变体在匹配的推理深度操作点上实现了更低的困惑度（perplexity）。
