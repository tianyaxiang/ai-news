# Are We There Yet? Exploring the Capabilities of MLLMs in Assistive AI Applications
# 我们到了吗？探索多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）在辅助人工智能应用中的能力

**Abstract:** Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) have redefined visual understanding by combining vision encoders with large-scale language models. This unified architecture enables strong performance on tasks like image captioning, visual question answering, and multimodal dialogue, often in zero- and few-shot settings. Their general-purpose capabilities and flexible interfaces make MLLMs a promising foundation for real-world vision-language applications.

**摘要：** 多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）通过将视觉编码器与大规模语言模型相结合，重新定义了视觉理解。这种统一的架构使得模型在图像描述、视觉问答和多模态对话等任务中表现出色，且通常无需训练或仅需少量样本（zero- and few-shot）。其通用能力和灵活的接口使 MLLMs 成为现实世界视觉-语言应用的有前途的基础。

Assistive AI aims to help users interact with their environments through natural language. These scenarios demand robust visual recognition, contextual reasoning, and multilingual comprehension—capabilities that MLLMs are believed to offer. However, their effectiveness in assistive settings remains to be fully understood.

辅助人工智能（Assistive AI）旨在帮助用户通过自然语言与环境进行交互。这些场景要求模型具备强大的视觉识别、上下文推理和多语言理解能力——而这些正是人们认为 MLLMs 所具备的。然而，它们在辅助场景中的实际有效性仍有待全面了解。

In this work, we explore whether MLLMs can support Assistive AI by evaluating state-of-the-art models on real-world tasks: recognizing everyday objects like currency, answering questions based on scene text, and reading visually presented content across multiple languages. To this end, we developed a system, NetraLink, using a head-mounted GoPro to capture real-world egocentric data, and collected a benchmark covering these assistive scenarios.

在这项工作中，我们通过评估最先进的模型在现实任务中的表现，探讨了 MLLMs 是否能够支持辅助人工智能。这些任务包括：识别货币等日常物品、基于场景文本回答问题，以及阅读多种语言的视觉呈现内容。为此，我们开发了一个名为 NetraLink 的系统，利用头戴式 GoPro 捕捉现实世界的自我中心（egocentric）数据，并收集了一个涵盖这些辅助场景的基准测试集。

Our findings provide a comprehensive diagnostic of current MLLMs, highlighting their strengths and limitations in enabling assistive technologies grounded in visual perception and language interaction.

我们的研究结果为当前的 MLLMs 提供了全面的诊断，突显了它们在实现基于视觉感知和语言交互的辅助技术方面的优势与局限性。
