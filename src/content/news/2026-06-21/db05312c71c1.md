# WorkClaw

**Meet the AI team for your team. WorkClaws are collaborative, proactive AI coworkers who work in Slack and Microsoft Teams just like every other colleague.**
为您团队打造的 AI 团队。WorkClaw 是协作型、主动型的 AI 同事，它们像其他同事一样在 Slack 和 Microsoft Teams 中工作。

**They're fully customizable to get work done your way by learning skills and routines.**
它们完全可定制，通过学习各种技能和工作流程，以您想要的方式完成任务。

**Unlike most AI products that pair one person with one assistant, WorkClaws can collaborate 24/7 with your whole team.**
与大多数将“一人配一助手”的 AI 产品不同，WorkClaw 可以与您的整个团队进行 24/7 全天候协作。

**Each Claw has a job title, a manager in your org chart, and a cloud-hosted ClawOS computer with the ability to access more than 3,000 apps.**
每个“Claw”（AI 同事）都有自己的职位头衔、组织架构中的直属经理，以及一台托管在云端的 ClawOS 计算机，能够访问超过 3,000 款应用程序。
