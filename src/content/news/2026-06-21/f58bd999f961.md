### Pixlie

**Pixlie**

Launching today, Pixlie is an AI video studio for creators who want control—not a black-box generator. Browse your library, queue jobs in the cloud, and track renders in the app. Same account on pixlys.com and iOS.
Pixlie 于今日正式发布。这是一个专为追求创作掌控力而非“黑盒”生成器的创作者所打造的 AI 视频工作室。用户可以在应用内浏览素材库、在云端排队任务并追踪渲染进度。pixlys.com 网页端与 iOS 端账号互通。

*   Granular creative workflow not just “type and pray”
*   提供精细化的创作工作流，拒绝“输入提示词后祈祷结果”的盲目模式。

*   Live on iOS App Store; Google play web at pixlys.com
*   现已上线 iOS App Store；Google Play 及网页版请访问 pixlys.com。
