# FedTR: Federated Learning Framework with Transfer Learning for Industrial Visual Inspection
# FedTR：用于工业视觉检测的迁移学习联邦学习框架

**Abstract:** Federated learning (FL) is a collaborative learning scheme to train deep learning models, where collaborating parties can consolidate their models without sharing local data with other parties, hence preserving data privacy.
**摘要：** 联邦学习（FL）是一种用于训练深度学习模型的协作学习方案，协作方可以在不与其他方共享本地数据的情况下整合各自的模型，从而保护数据隐私。

Nevertheless, when implementing FL in Industrial visual inspection (IVI), the constraints posed by limited data availability and the intricate nature of the inspection tasks significantly impact the performance of the resulting model.
然而，在工业视觉检测（IVI）中实施联邦学习时，数据可用性有限以及检测任务的复杂性所带来的限制，会显著影响最终模型的性能。

This paper introduces FedTR, a novel FL framework incorporating transfer learning designed for Autonomous IVI, focusing on the challenging task of identifying label defects through end-to-end text recognition.
本文介绍了 FedTR，这是一个为自主工业视觉检测（Autonomous IVI）设计的、结合了迁移学习的新型联邦学习框架，专注于通过端到端文本识别来识别标签缺陷这一具有挑战性的任务。

Transfer learning is a method that leverages the knowledge of a pre-trained model to adapt to a different dataset.
迁移学习是一种利用预训练模型知识来适应不同数据集的方法。

FedTR initially trains the model using a publicly available dataset, after which performs the essential federated learning process with model fine-tuning on the distributed and limited private data.
FedTR 首先使用公开数据集对模型进行训练，随后在分布式且有限的私有数据上进行必要的联邦学习过程，并对模型进行微调。

Extensive experiment results demonstrate the effectiveness and feasibility of FedTR on private ink cartridge datasets for label defect identification.
大量的实验结果证明了 FedTR 在用于标签缺陷识别的私有墨盒数据集上的有效性和可行性。

FedTR achieves an end-to-end text recognition word-level accuracy of 95.5% and 94.2% on homogeneous and heterogeneous data respectively.
FedTR 在同构和异构数据上的端到端文本识别词级准确率分别达到了 95.5% 和 94.2%。

Additionally, it attains performance levels that are on par with those achieved through centralized training.
此外，它所达到的性能水平与集中式训练相当。
