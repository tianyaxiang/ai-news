# LOGOS: Language-guided Oriented Object Detection in Aerial Scenes
# LOGOS：航空场景中语言引导的定向目标检测

**Abstract:** Object detection in geospatial scenes, such as satellite and aerial imagery, poses significant challenges due to the varying orientations and densities of objects, as well as the complex backgrounds inherent to remote sensing imagery.
**摘要：** 在卫星和航空影像等地理空间场景中，由于目标的方向和密度各异，以及遥感影像固有的复杂背景，目标检测面临着巨大的挑战。

Traditional methods for oriented object detection have struggled to address issues such as angular discontinuity, fixed query sizes, and inefficiencies in handling sparse or cluttered scenes.
传统的定向目标检测方法在解决角度不连续性、固定查询尺寸以及处理稀疏或杂乱场景效率低下等问题时，一直面临困难。

In this paper, we propose LOGOS, a novel transformer-based approach that leverages textual prompts to guide the detection of oriented objects in aerial scenes.
在本文中，我们提出了 LOGOS，这是一种基于 Transformer 的新颖方法，利用文本提示（textual prompts）来引导航空场景中定向目标的检测。

In particular, our proposed approach incorporates prompt-modulated content queries to dynamically adjust the model's focus based on the provided text, thereby improving object detection accuracy in complex environments.
具体而言，我们提出的方法结合了提示调制的内容查询（prompt-modulated content queries），能够根据提供的文本动态调整模型的关注点，从而提高复杂环境下的目标检测精度。

Empirically, extensive experiments on the DOTA dataset demonstrate that LOGOS outperforms existing state-of-the-art methods, particularly in densely packed and rotated object scenarios.
实验结果表明，在 DOTA 数据集上的大量实验证明，LOGOS 的性能优于现有的最先进方法，特别是在密集排列和旋转目标场景中表现突出。

Our approach offers a significant step forward in improving the robustness and scalability of oriented object detection in remote sensing applications.
我们的方法在提升遥感应用中定向目标检测的鲁棒性和可扩展性方面迈出了重要的一步。
