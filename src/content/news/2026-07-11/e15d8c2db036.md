### Adversarial Decoys: Misdirecting Attention-Based Defenses in ViT
### 对抗性诱饵：误导视觉 Transformer (ViT) 中基于注意力的防御机制

**Abstract:** Vision Transformers (ViTs) remain vulnerable to localized adversarial attacks, e.g., adversarial patches, while recent test-time defenses mitigate them by suppressing image tokens with abnormally high attention scores. These defenses exploit a strong coupling between attention and adversarial effectiveness: adversarial tokens often need to attract substantial attention to influence the prediction.

**摘要：** 视觉 Transformer (ViT) 仍然容易受到局部对抗性攻击（如对抗性补丁）的影响，而近期的测试时防御机制通过抑制具有异常高注意力分数的图像 Token 来缓解这一问题。这些防御手段利用了注意力与对抗有效性之间的强耦合关系：对抗性 Token 通常需要吸引大量的注意力才能影响预测结果。

We introduce adversarial decoys, independently optimized image patches that redirect the attention, and therefore related defenses, toward selected target tokens. Rather than jointly optimizing misclassifications and defense evasion, our approach decouples the two objectives: the original adversarial region induces the incorrect prediction, while a separate decoy manipulates the attention ranking used by the defense.

我们引入了“对抗性诱饵”（Adversarial Decoys），这是一种经过独立优化的图像补丁，旨在将注意力（以及相关的防御机制）重定向到选定的目标 Token 上。我们的方法没有将错误分类和规避防御进行联合优化，而是将这两个目标解耦：原始的对抗区域负责诱导错误预测，而独立的诱饵则操纵防御机制所使用的注意力排名。

A layer-wise objective increases target-token attention and promotes these tokens above competing non-target ones. Since the decoy is optimized independently of the underlying attack, the method is attack-agnostic and can be easily integrated with any existing adversarial patch attack.

通过逐层目标函数，该方法增加了目标 Token 的注意力，并使其排名高于竞争性的非目标 Token。由于诱饵的优化独立于底层攻击，该方法具有攻击无关性，可以轻松集成到任何现有的对抗性补丁攻击中。

Experiments on ImageNet across multiple ViT architectures and attacks show that decoys can redirect high attention scores away from the true adversarial region while preserving much of the attack effectiveness. These results reveal a fundamental limitation of using attention magnitude as an indicator of adversarial relevance.

在 ImageNet 上针对多种 ViT 架构和攻击进行的实验表明，诱饵可以将高注意力分数从真实的对抗区域转移开，同时保留大部分攻击效果。这些结果揭示了将注意力幅度作为对抗相关性指标所存在的根本局限性。
