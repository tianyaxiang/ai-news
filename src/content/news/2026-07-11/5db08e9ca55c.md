# Jet-Long: Efficient Long-Context Extension with Dynamic Bifocal RoPE
# Jet-Long：基于动态双焦点 RoPE 的高效长上下文扩展

**Abstract:** Modern LLMs are increasingly deployed in long-context applications such as retrieval-augmented generation, repository-level coding, and agentic workflows whose accumulated reasoning and tool traces routinely push the input an order of magnitude past the pretraining window, making zero-shot context extension the dominant deployment path for open-weight checkpoints.

**摘要：** 现代大语言模型（LLM）越来越多地部署在长上下文应用中，例如检索增强生成（RAG）、代码库级编程以及智能体工作流。这些应用中积累的推理过程和工具调用轨迹通常会将输入长度推向超出预训练窗口一个数量级，这使得零样本（zero-shot）上下文扩展成为开源权重模型部署的主流路径。

Most existing zero-shot methods fix a single rescaling factor up front, so an aggressive factor sacrifices short-context fidelity while a conservative one breaks down at long contexts.

大多数现有的零样本方法预先固定单一的重缩放因子，因此激进的因子会牺牲短上下文的保真度，而保守的因子则会在长上下文中失效。

We propose Jet-Long, a tuning-free zero-shot method that pairs a local RoPE-faithful window with a long-range window whose rescaling factor adapts dynamically to the current sequence length, recovering the base model exactly at short inputs while extrapolating cleanly at long ones.

我们提出了 Jet-Long，这是一种无需微调的零样本方法。它将局部 RoPE 保真窗口与长程窗口相结合，后者的重缩放因子可根据当前序列长度动态调整，从而在短输入时精确恢复基础模型，并在长输入时实现平滑外推。

An inclusion-exclusion attention merge and an on-the-fly RoPE correction rotation make the bifocal construction essentially free at inference; fused into a single CuTe kernel, long-context prefill reaches up to $1.39\times$ FA2 throughput on H100 (approaching the Hopper-only FA4), and single-batch generation incurs $\le 4\%$ overhead at every length.

通过包含-排除注意力合并（inclusion-exclusion attention merge）和即时 RoPE 校正旋转，这种双焦点结构在推理时几乎没有额外开销；将其融合进单个 CuTe 内核后，长上下文预填充（prefill）在 H100 上的吞吐量最高可达 FA2 的 1.39 倍（接近仅限 Hopper 架构的 FA4），且单批次生成在任何长度下的开销均不超过 4%。

On Qwen3-1.7B/4B/8B up to 128K context, Jet-Long leads RULER by $+4.79$/$+2.18$/$+2.03$~pp over the strongest baseline at 1.7B/4B/8B, achieves the best overall accuracy on HELMET-RAG (a benchmark identified by HELMET as the most efficient predictor of downstream long-context performance) and attains the lowest PG-19 perplexity.

在 Qwen3-1.7B/4B/8B 模型高达 128K 的上下文中，Jet-Long 在 1.7B/4B/8B 规模上分别比最强基线在 RULER 评测中高出 +4.79/+2.18/+2.03 个百分点，在 HELMET-RAG（被 HELMET 认定为下游长上下文性能最有效预测指标的基准测试）上实现了最佳综合准确率，并获得了最低的 PG-19 困惑度（perplexity）。

Jet-Long also generalizes to hybrid attention architectures such as Jet-Nemotron for further long-context improvement without retraining, and remains hyperparameter-resilient for ease of deployment.

Jet-Long 还可以推广到混合注意力架构（如 Jet-Nemotron），在无需重新训练的情况下进一步提升长上下文性能，并保持了超参数的鲁棒性，便于部署。
