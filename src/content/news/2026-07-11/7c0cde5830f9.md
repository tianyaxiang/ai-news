# Who Gets Missed in the Tail? Thresholded Subgroup Underdiagnosis in Long-Tailed Chest X-ray Classification
# 谁在长尾中被遗漏了？胸部 X 光分类中基于阈值的子群体诊断不足问题

**Abstract:** In chest X-ray (CXR) classification, acceptable ranking performance can still leave rare-positive patients below threshold, even within subgroups. We study this pre-deployment fairness problem as an audit question: after a long-tailed multi-label CXR model is converted from scores into decisions, who is missed?

**摘要：** 在胸部 X 光（CXR）分类中，即使排序性能表现尚可，仍可能导致罕见阳性患者的得分低于阈值，尤其是在特定子群体中。我们将这一部署前的公平性问题视为一项审计课题进行研究：当长尾多标签 CXR 模型从得分转换为决策结果后，究竟是谁被遗漏了？

Across VinDr-CXR and MIMIC-CXR/CXR-LT, we use a diagnostic ladder to separate class-level long-tail losses, subgroup-aware weighting, group robustness, and threshold selection. On VinDr-CXR, group-tail weighting followed by tail-aware thresholding reduces tail FNR from 0.665 to 0.269, sex worst-group FNR from 0.705 to 0.157, and age worst-group FNR from 0.822 to 0.133, while macro-mAP increases from 0.611 to 0.635.

在 VinDr-CXR 和 MIMIC-CXR/CXR-LT 数据集上，我们使用一种“诊断阶梯”方法，将类级长尾损失、子群体感知加权、群体鲁棒性和阈值选择分离开来。在 VinDr-CXR 上，采用群体尾部加权并结合尾部感知阈值处理后，尾部假阴性率（FNR）从 0.665 降至 0.269，性别最差群体的 FNR 从 0.705 降至 0.157，年龄最差群体的 FNR 从 0.822 降至 0.133，同时宏观平均精度（macro-mAP）从 0.611 提升至 0.635。

On MIMIC-CXR/CXR-LT, the same score-to-threshold comparison reduces tail FNR from 0.866 to 0.741 and lowers worst-group FNR across sex, age, race, and insurance; residual missed-positive rates nevertheless remain high. Paired bootstrap contrasts on VinDr support the thresholded FNR reductions, and GroupDRO reference runs indicate that aggregate group robustness alone does not remove rare subgroup misses in this setting.

在 MIMIC-CXR/CXR-LT 上，同样的得分转阈值比较将尾部 FNR 从 0.866 降至 0.741，并降低了性别、年龄、种族和保险等维度的最差群体 FNR；然而，残留的漏诊阳性率依然较高。在 VinDr 上进行的配对自助法（bootstrap）对比支持了基于阈值的 FNR 降低效果，而 GroupDRO 的参考运行结果表明，仅靠聚合的群体鲁棒性并不能消除该场景下罕见子群体的漏诊问题。

The study supports a narrow audit claim: rare-label fairness in CXR depends jointly on the finding, subgroup, and operating threshold, not on label frequency or ranking metrics alone.

本研究支持一个具体的审计结论：CXR 中的罕见标签公平性取决于诊断结果、子群体和操作阈值的共同作用，而非仅仅取决于标签频率或排序指标。
