# Spotify will let you fine-tune your weekly Release Radar playlist
# Spotify 将允许你微调每周的“新歌雷达”播放列表

Spotify is giving listeners control to fine-tune what gets surfaced for them in Release Radar — one of its most popular weekly playlists. The new options allow you to narrow the playlist to a specific genre, focus on artists that are new to you, and more.
Spotify 正在赋予听众更多控制权，以便微调其最受欢迎的每周播放列表之一——“新歌雷达”（Release Radar）中呈现的内容。这些新选项允许你将播放列表缩小到特定流派，专注于你从未听过的艺人，等等。

Listeners can choose from up to five options like “Discover new artists,” “Editors’ picks,” and “Pop.” They’re rolling out now across mobile and desktop apps and will appear at the top of your Release Radar playlist once they’re available.
听众最多可以选择五个选项，例如“发现新艺人”、“编辑精选”和“流行音乐”。这些功能目前正在移动端和桌面端应用中陆续推出，一旦可用，它们将出现在你“新歌雷达”播放列表的顶部。

Spotify also says it’s making tweaks to the algorithm to serve up “more personalized recommendations,” along with a new look featuring updated cover and header art.
Spotify 还表示，正在对算法进行调整以提供“更个性化的推荐”，同时还带来了更新了封面和标题艺术的新外观。

This is just the latest in a number of recent changes Spotify has made to its flagship playlists and music discovery features. That includes launching new release recommendations chosen by the editorial staff, which seemed to be a response to a growing revolt against our increasingly algorithmically governed lives.
这只是 Spotify 近期对其旗舰播放列表和音乐发现功能所做的一系列调整中的最新举措。其中包括推出由编辑团队精选的新歌推荐，这似乎是对人们日益反抗被算法主导生活的一种回应。
