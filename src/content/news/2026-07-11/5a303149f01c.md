# LightCrafter: PBR-Conditioned Video Diffusion Refinement for Controllable and Consistent Relighting
# LightCrafter：用于可控且一致重光照的 PBR 条件视频扩散细化模型

**Abstract:** Video relighting requires balancing long-form temporal consistency with a physically grounded understanding of light transport, which depends on accurate estimation of intrinsic scene properties such as materials, geometry, and illumination.
**摘要：** 视频重光照需要在长时序一致性与对光传输的物理基础理解之间取得平衡，而这取决于对场景内在属性（如材质、几何形状和光照）的准确估计。

Existing methods follow two paradigms: (1) reconstruct a video's photometric properties via inverse rendering and relight them to a target illumination via forward rendering, using physically-based rendering (PBR) or a neural renderer; these suffer from noisy reconstructions and struggle with hard-to-model effects such as global illumination.
现有方法遵循两种范式：（1）通过逆向渲染重建视频的光度属性，并利用基于物理的渲染（PBR）或神经渲染器通过正向渲染将其重光照至目标光照；这些方法往往受限于重建噪声，且难以处理全局光照等复杂效果。

(2) Frame the task as generative video-to-video translation conditioned on relighting targets (a target environment map or text); this limits relighting control and temporal stability, since diffusion models struggle to translate long-form videos, and is constrained by the availability of input/relit training pairs.
（2）将该任务建模为以重光照目标（目标环境贴图或文本）为条件的生成式视频到视频翻译；这种方法限制了重光照的控制能力和时间稳定性，因为扩散模型在处理长视频翻译时表现不佳，且受限于输入/重光照训练对的可用性。

We propose LightCrafter, a hybrid pipeline that reformulates video relighting as video translation of a proxy video: rather than translating the input video directly to the target, we translate a PBR rendering of the input under the target illumination to the final target.
我们提出了 LightCrafter，这是一种混合流水线，它将视频重光照重新定义为代理视频的视频翻译：我们不再将输入视频直接翻译为目标视频，而是将输入视频在目标光照下的 PBR 渲染结果翻译为最终目标。

This bakes illumination targets into the PBR proxy, removing the need to teach the diffusion model illumination concepts like environment maps, and enables more intricate lighting control while naturally providing long-form temporal consistency.
这种方法将光照目标嵌入到 PBR 代理中，无需让扩散模型学习环境贴图等光照概念，从而实现了更精细的光照控制，同时自然地保证了长时序的一致性。

We show PBR renders alone already outperform some prior art but struggle with effects like global illumination; to capture these, we leverage photometric priors in video generation models by post-training CogVideoX on synthetic video pairs and real-world unpaired videos.
我们证明，仅使用 PBR 渲染就已经优于部分现有技术，但在处理全局光照等效果时仍显吃力；为了捕捉这些效果，我们通过在合成视频对和真实世界非配对视频上对 CogVideoX 进行后训练，利用了视频生成模型中的光度先验。

We outperform prior state-of-the-art on existing real-world relighting benchmarks and contribute a synthetic benchmark for further analysis. We will release our dataset, benchmark, metrics, and code.
我们在现有的真实世界重光照基准测试中超越了此前的最先进水平，并贡献了一个合成基准测试以供进一步分析。我们将发布我们的数据集、基准测试、指标和代码。
