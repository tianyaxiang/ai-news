# Report of the 5th PVUW Challenge: Towards More Diverse Modalities in Pixel-Level Understanding
# 第五届 PVUW 挑战赛报告：迈向像素级理解中更多样化的模态

**Abstract:** This report summarizes the objectives, datasets, and top-performing methodologies of the 2026 Pixel-level Video Understanding in the Wild (PVUW) Challenge, hosted at CVPR 2026, which evaluates state-of-the-art models under highly unconstrained conditions.
**摘要：** 本报告总结了 2026 年在 CVPR 2026 上举办的“野外像素级视频理解”（PVUW）挑战赛的目标、数据集及表现最佳的方法。该挑战赛旨在评估高度非受限条件下的最先进模型。

To provide a comprehensive assessment, the 2026 edition features three specialized tracks: the MOSE track for tracking objects within densely cluttered and severely occluded scenarios; the MeViS-Text track for localizing targets via motion-focused linguistic expressions; and the newly inaugurated MeViS-Audio track, which pioneers acoustic-driven object segmentation.
为了提供全面的评估，2026 年的赛事设有三个专业赛道：用于在密集杂乱和严重遮挡场景中跟踪物体的 MOSE 赛道；通过聚焦运动的语言表达来定位目标的 MeViS-Text 赛道；以及新设立的 MeViS-Audio 赛道，该赛道开创了声学驱动的物体分割技术。

By introducing previously unreleased challenging data and analyzing the cutting-edge, multimodal solutions submitted by participants, this report highlights the community's latest technical advancements and charts promising future directions for robust video scene comprehension.
通过引入此前未公开的挑战性数据，并分析参赛者提交的前沿多模态解决方案，本报告突显了该领域最新的技术进展，并为稳健的视频场景理解规划了有前景的未来方向。
