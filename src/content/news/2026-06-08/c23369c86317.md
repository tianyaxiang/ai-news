# Writing in the Age of AI
# AI 时代的写作

I haven't written articles in quite a while and I recently decided to come back to it. At work I use AI daily, so a big part of my coding tasks are delegated to agents.
我已经很久没有写文章了，最近决定重新开始。在工作中，我每天都会使用人工智能，因此我大部分的编程任务都交给了智能体（agents）去完成。

I try not to do the same when it comes to writing text but I don't have a clear reason for that (or maybe I do and I don't want to admit it).
在撰写文本时，我尽量避免这样做，但我并没有明确的理由（或者说，也许我有理由，只是不想承认）。

I am sure people can take advantage of generating text with the help of AI but at the moment I feel like prompting would not save me any time and writing the text myself would be faster.
我相信人们可以利用人工智能来生成文本，但目前我觉得编写提示词（prompting）并不能节省我的时间，自己动手写反而更快。

What about you? Are you using AI to write your articles?
你呢？你正在使用人工智能来撰写文章吗？
