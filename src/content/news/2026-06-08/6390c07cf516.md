# Major P2P issues in Israel and possibly other Middle East countries
# 以色列及中东地区可能存在的重大 P2P 网络问题

I am not sure i am in the right place but we are all out of options here since we couldn't get any help from the game or steam support. Since around the 13/03, there is a major systemic problem in every game that uses Steam Networking for P2P games.
我不确定是否找对了反馈渠道，但由于无法从游戏方或 Steam 客服那里获得任何帮助，我们已经束手无策了。自 3 月 13 日左右起，所有使用 Steam Networking 进行 P2P 联机的游戏都出现了严重的系统性问题。

For example, in the game Street Fighter 6, when playing with one Israeli or another with PC to PC the ping between players are ~120ms. when playing with European players the ping is around 60-80ms which means it works well, it currently affects only Israeli players when playing PC to PC.
以《街头霸王 6》为例，当以色列玩家之间进行 PC 对 PC 联机时，玩家间的延迟高达 120ms 左右。而与欧洲玩家联机时，延迟仅在 60-80ms 之间，说明网络连接是正常的。目前该问题仅影响以色列玩家之间的 PC 对 PC 联机。

Since Street Fighter 6 supports cross-play we have tried playing PC to PS5 and the ping is a flawless 5-10ms. It currently affects all players in Israel, we have a few dozens in our community with several ISPs, we have of course attempted speaking to our ISPs and port forwarding and we have found no network issues on their part.
由于《街头霸王 6》支持跨平台联机，我们尝试了 PC 与 PS5 之间的联机，延迟表现完美，仅为 5-10ms。目前该问题影响了以色列的所有玩家。我们的社区有几十名玩家，使用了不同的互联网服务提供商（ISP），我们当然也尝试过联系 ISP 并进行端口转发，但并未发现他们那边存在网络问题。

in other P2P games that don't use steam networking the issue is not existent(for example, Tekken 8). We are currently don't know what we can do with this issue since nothing we have done have helped and 120ms is too high for it to be playable in any P2P game.
在其他不使用 Steam Networking 的 P2P 游戏中，该问题并不存在（例如《铁拳 8》）。我们目前不知道还能做些什么，因为我们尝试的所有方法都无效，而 120ms 的延迟对于任何 P2P 游戏来说都太高，根本无法进行正常对战。

While I can't confirm but heard reports from players in other middle eastern countries like Egypt that they also report on this issue so it could be region-wide.
虽然我无法证实，但有来自埃及等其他中东国家玩家的报告称他们也遇到了同样的问题，因此这可能是一个区域性的故障。
