# Xbox Games Showcase 2026: All the news and trailers
# Xbox Games Showcase 2026：所有新闻与预告片

The console industry is in a weird place, and both Xbox and PlayStation have a chance to change the narrative a bit with their showcases at Summer Game Fest. Sony did that by focusing on the single-player titles it’s known for, and then it was Microsoft’s turn.
主机游戏行业正处于一个微妙的时期，Xbox 和 PlayStation 都有机会通过夏季游戏节（Summer Game Fest）的展示会来扭转舆论。索尼通过专注于其标志性的单机游戏做到了这一点，接下来轮到了微软。

The Xbox Games Showcase was focused mainly on games we already know, but there was also some confused messaging about the company’s exclusivity strategy. Gears of War: E-Day won’t be launching on the PS5, which was a big surprise, but other major first-party titles like Fable and Halo: Combat Evolved are still coming to Sony’s console. That makes it hard to tell just how focused Microsoft really is on exclusives.
Xbox 游戏展示会主要集中在我们已知的内容上，但关于该公司独占策略的信息却显得有些混乱。《战争机器：E-Day》（Gears of War: E-Day）将不会登陆 PS5，这令人大感意外；然而，《神鬼寓言》（Fable）和《光环：战斗进化》（Halo: Combat Evolved）等其他大型第一方游戏仍将登陆索尼的主机。这让人很难判断微软对独占策略的专注程度究竟如何。

In a blog post recapping the event, the company said that “Games already announced for multiplatform releases will stick to that plan — we’re committed to investing in and growing Xbox both on console and beyond,” suggesting that future first-party titles will be only for Xbox consoles.
在回顾此次活动的博客文章中，微软表示：“已经宣布跨平台发布的游戏将继续按计划进行——我们致力于在主机及其他领域投资并发展 Xbox。”这暗示未来的第一方游戏将仅限于 Xbox 主机平台。

The event came at an interesting time for Xbox. Microsoft’s gaming division went through an executive shake-up in April, and new boss Asha Sharma has been making some smaller but welcome changes, while also promising what she calls the “return of Xbox.” Given how confused the Xbox brand has been of late, it seems likely bigger changes are on the way.
此次活动对 Xbox 而言处于一个有趣的节点。微软游戏部门在四月份经历了高层人事变动，新任负责人 Asha Sharma 正在进行一些小规模但受欢迎的改革，并承诺实现她所谓的“Xbox 的回归”。鉴于 Xbox 品牌近期的混乱状态，看来更大的变革即将来临。

There were at least a few surprises, though, including a first look at Sony’s Crazy Taxi revival and a very brief teaser for Persona 6.
不过，活动中至少还是有几个惊喜，包括索尼《疯狂出租车》（Crazy Taxi）重启版的首次亮相，以及《女神异闻录 6》（Persona 6）的简短预告。

### Fable will be getting a story expansion sometime after launch.
### 《神鬼寓言》将在发售后推出剧情扩展包。

According to the Fable website: Fable: Order of the Hero features a brand-new region that brims with danger, wonder, and whispers of long buried power. As you explore, your deeds earn the cultist’s admiration, and you rise to become their latest (and greatest) celebrity overseer.
根据《神鬼寓言》官网介绍：《神鬼寓言：英雄秩序》（Fable: Order of the Hero）包含一个全新的区域，那里充满了危险、奇迹以及被长期埋藏的力量的低语。在探索过程中，你的事迹将赢得邪教徒的崇拜，你将崛起成为他们最新（也是最伟大）的名人监督者。

With your newfound authority, you’ll guide their rituals and shape the cult into your own heroic vision, whether noble, nasty or just plain weird. But becoming the new boss does beg a darker question. If the cult has a vacancy for a new Heroic leader……what happened to the last one? You’ll get it as part of the Premium Edition.
凭借新获得的权力，你将引导他们的仪式，并将该邪教塑造成你心目中的英雄愿景，无论是高尚、邪恶还是纯粹的怪异。但成为新老板引出了一个更黑暗的问题：如果邪教需要一位新的英雄领袖……上一任去哪了？该扩展包将作为高级版的一部分提供。

### Sega used generative AI as part of making the new Crazy Taxi, apparently.
### 显然，世嘉在制作新版《疯狂出租车》时使用了生成式 AI。

As spotted by Kotaku, the Steam page for the newly announced World Tour says that: At SEGA Corporation, we utilize generative AI as a support tool for developers, aiming to provide better content to our users and enable developers to focus more on creative tasks. We have used such generative AI support tools during development of Crazy Taxi: World Tour. No AI was used in reference to the performers in the game.
正如 Kotaku 所发现的，新公布的《疯狂出租车：世界巡回赛》（Crazy Taxi: World Tour）Steam 页面显示：在世嘉公司，我们利用生成式 AI 作为开发者的辅助工具，旨在为用户提供更好的内容，并让开发者能更专注于创意任务。我们在《疯狂出租车：世界巡回赛》的开发过程中使用了此类生成式 AI 辅助工具。游戏中未涉及任何针对表演者的 AI 使用。

I’m not exactly sure what this all means, though, and I’m glad it at least wasn’t used “in reference to the performers in the game.”
虽然我不确定这一切具体意味着什么，但我很高兴它至少没有被用于“游戏中表演者的相关内容”。

### A return of Xbox console exclusives.
### Xbox 主机独占游戏的回归。

Both Gears of War: E-Day and Clockwork Revolution are Xbox console exclusive titles, a phrase we haven’t heard for quite some time given Microsoft’s multiplatform push. “These are not timed exclusives,” says Joe Skrebels, Xbox Wire editor-in-chief. “Games already announced for multiplatform releases will stick to that plan – we’re committed to investing in and growing XBOX both on console and beyond.”
《战争机器：E-Day》和《发条革命》（Clockwork Revolution）都是 Xbox 主机独占游戏，考虑到微软近期的跨平台策略，我们已经很久没听到这个词了。“这些不是限时独占，”Xbox Wire 主编 Joe Skrebels 表示，“已经宣布跨平台发布的游戏将继续按计划进行——我们致力于在主机及其他领域投资并发展 Xbox。”

### Clockwork Revolution is also an Xbox console exclusive.
### 《发条革命》同样是 Xbox 主机独占游戏。

We’ve been waiting to hear more about inXile’s new steampunk RPG, and during the Xbox Games Showcase today we finally got a rough release window: 2027. Clockwork Revolution will also be an Xbox console exclusive, just like Gears of War: E-Day.
我们一直在等待了解更多关于 inXile 工作室这款蒸汽朋克 RPG 的消息，在今天的 Xbox 游戏展示会上，我们终于得到了一个大致的发布窗口：2027 年。《发条革命》将和《战争机器：E-Day》一样，成为 Xbox 主机独占游戏。

### Persona 6 exists, and that’s all we know
### 《女神异闻录 6》确实存在，但我们只知道这些

This year’s Summer Game Fest turned out to be a good one for fans of Japanese RPGs. First, the conclusion of the Final Fantasy VII remake trilogy was announced at SGF Live, and now we have the long-awaited return of the Persona series.
今年的夏季游戏节对日式 RPG 粉丝来说非常友好。首先，在 SGF 直播中宣布了《最终幻想 VII》重制版三部曲的终章，现在我们又迎来了期待已久的《女神异闻录》系列的回归。

Atlus confirmed Persona 6 exists with a brief teaser, but aside from implying a very dark tone, that’s about all the video revealed. There’s no release date or window, though the game will be coming to the PS5, Xbox, and PC whenever it does launch. Atlus describes the game as “a bold, new standalone story blending heartfelt daily life and new characters with pulse-pounding, supernatural adventure,” which could also describe most Persona titles.
Atlus 通过一段简短的预告片确认了《女神异闻录 6》的存在，但除了暗示其基调非常黑暗之外，视频中几乎没有透露其他信息。目前没有发布日期或窗口，但该游戏在发布时将登陆 PS5、Xbox 和 PC 平台。Atlus 将这款游戏描述为“一个大胆的全新独立故事，融合了感人的日常生活、新角色以及令人心跳加速的超自然冒险”，这几乎可以用来描述大多数《女神异闻录》作品。

### Spyro’s back.
### 小龙斯派罗（Spyro）回归。

Activision’s new Spyro game is Spyro: A Realm Beyond, and it’s launching in spring 2027. It’ll be available on Xbox, PS5, PC, and Nintendo Switch 2.
动视的新款《小龙斯派罗》游戏名为《小龙斯派罗：超越领域》（Spyro: A Realm Beyond），将于 2027 年春季发布。它将登陆 Xbox、PS5、PC 和任天堂 Switch 2 平台。

### A spooky new Castlevania for October.
### 十月将迎来一部令人毛骨悚然的新《恶魔城》。

Castlevania: Belmont’s Curse, made by teams who worked on Dead Cells, will be released on October 15th. Despite the pedigree of its developers, it’s not a roguelike.
由《死亡细胞》（Dead Cells）开发团队制作的《恶魔城：贝尔蒙特的诅咒》（Castlevania: Belmont’s Curse）将于 10 月 15 日发布。尽管开发商背景深厚，但它并非一款 Roguelike 游戏。

### Fly over some national parks.
### 飞越国家公园。

The next big update for Flight Simulator gives you a chance to explore over 30 American landmarks and national parks without bothering with a road trip. It launches, appropriately, on July 4th. Also you can fly the Goodyear blimp.
《模拟飞行》（Flight Simulator）的下一个重大更新让你有机会探索 30 多个美国地标和国家公园，无需亲自驾车旅行。该更新恰如其分地于 7 月 4 日发布。此外，你还可以驾驶固特异飞艇。

### Microsoft’s Xbox 25th anniversary console comes in translucent green
### 微软 Xbox 25 周年纪念主机采用半透明绿色设计

Microsoft has created a special edition Xbox Series X to celebrate 25 years of the console. The Xbox 25th-anniversary console takes design cues from the original Xbox console, with both the console and controller featuring a translucent green.
微软打造了一款特别版 Xbox Series X 来庆祝该主机诞生 25 周年。这款 Xbox 25 周年纪念主机采用了初代 Xbox 的设计元素，主机和手柄均采用了半透明绿色。
