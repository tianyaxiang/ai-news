### Wave

**Launching today**
今天正式发布

**Turn your voice into text — local or cloud, your choice**
将您的语音转换为文本——本地或云端，任您选择

**Wave lets you invoke an AI model anywhere on macOS using just your voice. Hold a hotkey, speak, and release—your speech is transcribed, processed, and the result appears exactly where you need it.**
Wave 让您只需通过语音即可在 macOS 的任何位置调用 AI 模型。按住快捷键，说话，然后松开——您的语音会被转录、处理，结果会直接出现在您需要的地方。

**If you're typing, it replaces or inserts text. If you're reading, it shows a floating answer. Works across all apps with selected text as context.**
如果您正在打字，它可以替换或插入文本；如果您正在阅读，它会显示一个浮动答案。该功能适用于所有应用程序，并能以选中的文本作为上下文进行处理。
