# Halo: Campaign Evolved arrives July 28th
# 《光环：战役进化》将于 7 月 28 日发售

Master Chief is back.
士官长回归了。

As part of its Xbox Games Showcase on Sunday, Microsoft revealed new details about Halo: Campaign Evolved, the upcoming remake of Halo: Combat Evolved’s campaign mode. The remake will debut on Xbox Series S / X, PC, and PS5 on July 28th.
在周日的 Xbox 游戏展示会上，微软公布了关于《光环：战役进化》（Halo: Campaign Evolved）的新细节，这是《光环：战斗进化》战役模式的重制版。该重制版将于 7 月 28 日在 Xbox Series S / X、PC 和 PS5 平台上推出。

Today’s mission trailer includes a first look at Operation: Meteorite, a new three-mission story arc in Halo: Campaign Evolved. It takes place a year before the events of the first game and features Master Chief and Sgt. Johnson.
今日发布的任务预告片首次展示了“陨石行动”（Operation: Meteorite），这是《光环：战役进化》中新增的一个包含三个任务的剧情篇章。故事发生在初代游戏事件的一年前，主角为士官长和约翰逊中士。

Microsoft announced Campaign Evolved last year, calling it a “modern evolution” of the original story. The game is built in Unreal Engine 5 and features “stunning new 4K visuals, updated animations, remastered music, and re-recorded voice lines.” There are also three brand-new “prequel” missions. Halo Studios is adding a few weapons from later titles to the game, like the energy sword and the battle rifle. And you’ll also be able to play two-player local split-screen co-op and up to four-player co-op online.
微软去年宣布了《战役进化》，称其为原版故事的“现代进化”。该游戏基于虚幻引擎 5 构建，拥有“令人惊叹的全新 4K 视觉效果、更新的动画、重制的音乐以及重新录制的语音”。此外，游戏还包含三个全新的“前传”任务。Halo Studios 在游戏中加入了一些后续作品中的武器，例如能量剑和战斗步枪。玩家还可以进行双人本地分屏合作，以及最多四人的在线合作模式。

My colleague Tom Warren got to play a demo of Campaign Evolved, and while he said that it “looks and feels distinctively Halo,” he noted, “I realized there’s a lot going on in Campaign Evolved beyond its Unreal Engine 5-powered visuals.”
我的同事 Tom Warren 试玩了《战役进化》的演示版，虽然他表示游戏“看起来和玩起来都非常有《光环》的味道”，但他同时也指出：“我意识到《战役进化》不仅仅只有虚幻引擎 5 带来的视觉升级，其内涵远不止于此。”

Microsoft is starting preorders for Halo: Campaign Evolved today, with a $69.99 premium edition providing early access to the game on July 23rd. There’s even a $199.99 collector’s edition that includes a 12-inch “Dark Horse” Master Chief statue, a light-up LED Cortana chip, and more.
微软今日开启了《光环：战役进化》的预购，售价 69.99 美元的豪华版可让玩家在 7 月 23 日提前进入游戏。此外还有售价 199.99 美元的收藏版，内含一个 12 英寸的“黑马”（Dark Horse）士官长雕像、一个可发光的 LED 科塔娜芯片等周边。

Halo: Campaign Evolved will debut on Xbox Series S / X, PC, and PS5 on July 28th.
《光环：战役进化》将于 7 月 28 日在 Xbox Series S / X、PC 和 PS5 平台上正式发售。
