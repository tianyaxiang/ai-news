# How to Watch Apple’s WWDC 2026 Keynote—and What to Expect
# 如何观看苹果 WWDC 2026 主题演讲——以及有哪些看点

Apple CEO Tim Cook’s final time hosting the company’s annual Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) is just about here. The outgoing Apple leader will shepherd a keynote address announcing new software features for Siri, iOS, and Apple’s artificial intelligence efforts. It’s likely to be a final look at the company’s vision from the leader who focused on turning Apple into a dominant subscription business.

苹果公司首席执行官蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）最后一次主持公司年度全球开发者大会（WWDC）的时间即将到来。这位即将离任的苹果领导人将主持主题演讲，宣布 Siri、iOS 以及苹果人工智能领域的新软件功能。这很可能是这位致力于将苹果转型为订阅服务巨头的领导者，最后一次展示其对公司的愿景。

The WWDC keynote kicks off Monday, June 8, at 10 am PT (1 pm Eastern). You can watch the livestream right here on this page, or on Apple’s WWDC website. It will also play live on YouTube and the Apple TV app.

WWDC 主题演讲将于太平洋时间 6 月 8 日周一上午 10 点（东部时间下午 1 点）开始。您可以在本页面直接观看直播，或前往苹果 WWDC 官网观看。此外，YouTube 和 Apple TV 应用也将同步直播。

WIRED will be running a live blog, so you can follow along as it happens for a drip feed of the announcements. We’ll also have roundups of the changes coming to iOS, MacOS, and everything else Apple puts out there during the show. As always for these events, the rumor mill has been churning. Here’s what to expect from Apple’s announcements at WWDC.

《连线》（WIRED）将开设实时博客，您可以随时关注，获取源源不断的公告更新。我们还将汇总 iOS、macOS 以及苹果在发布会上推出的所有其他新变化。与往常的此类活动一样，传闻早已满天飞。以下是苹果在 WWDC 上可能发布的看点。

### AI OS
### AI 操作系统

As it does just about every WWDC, Apple is bound to announce new software operating systems for its products. These should all have the OS 27 moniker, as Apple recently adopted the car industry naming strategy of labeling its operating systems for the year ahead. That means iOS 27, macOS 27, watchOS 27, and so on.

正如每届 WWDC 一样，苹果注定会为其产品发布新的操作系统。这些系统都将冠以“27”的后缀，因为苹果最近采用了汽车行业的命名策略，即以未来年份来标记其操作系统。这意味着我们将看到 iOS 27、macOS 27、watchOS 27 等等。

Apple seems keen to boost performance across its operating systems, prioritizing fixes and stability over big new features. There are bound to be a bundle of AI infusions, branded as Apple’s Intelligence features. According to Bloomberg’s Mark Gurman, Apple is likely to introduce some new AI-powered features that let you create your own digital passes for events and split bills by taking a picture of receipts.

苹果似乎热衷于提升其操作系统的整体性能，将修复漏洞和稳定性置于重大新功能之上。当然，届时肯定会有一系列注入 AI 的功能，统称为“Apple Intelligence”。据彭博社的马克·古尔曼（Mark Gurman）称，苹果可能会引入一些新的 AI 功能，让用户能够为活动创建数字通行证，并通过拍摄收据照片来分摊账单。

### Siri Supersized
### Siri 全面升级

Everybody’s waiting for the big Siri glow-up. Apple’s virtual assistant has been the company’s problem child for a while now, frequently more frustrating than actually useful. It was supposed to be revamped with Apple Intelligence a few years ago, but was delayed. Now, at long last, Apple is expected to announce its big Siri update, boosted by a partnership with Google to infuse Siri with its Gemini AI.

每个人都在等待 Siri 的大变身。苹果的这位虚拟助手长期以来一直是公司的“问题儿童”，往往让人感到沮丧多过实用。它本应在几年前通过 Apple Intelligence 进行重塑，但被推迟了。现在，苹果终于有望宣布 Siri 的重大更新，通过与谷歌的合作，将 Gemini AI 注入 Siri。

Apple and Google’s partnership has been in the works since 2024, but neither company confirmed the details until a joint statement in January that spelled out a “multi-year collaboration” to boost Apple Intelligence features with Google Gemini. That statement also promised “a more personalized Siri coming this year.” WWDC seems like a good time for such news to come out.

苹果与谷歌的合作自 2024 年以来一直在进行中，但直到 1 月份双方发表联合声明，才确认了细节，明确了这是一项“多年期合作”，旨在利用谷歌 Gemini 增强 Apple Intelligence 功能。该声明还承诺“今年将推出更个性化的 Siri”。WWDC 似乎是发布此类消息的好时机。

AI boosts in Siri have already been very confrontational, like in May when Apple agreed to pay $250 million to iPhone 15 and 16 owners who had never gotten Siri’s long-promised AI smarts. Last week, Gurman again leaked some details about Apple’s potential changes to Siri. These include a darker interface in a dedicated Siri app, a dedicated spot in Apple’s Dynamic Island at the top of iPhone screens, and the chatbot-like capabilities enhanced by Google’s Gemini AI. It sounds like Apple is doubling down on Siri and putting it front and center in the iPhone interface.

Siri 的 AI 升级之路一直充满争议，例如 5 月份苹果同意向那些从未获得 Siri 承诺已久的 AI 智能功能的 iPhone 15 和 16 用户支付 2.5 亿美元赔偿。上周，古尔曼再次泄露了关于 Siri 可能发生变化的细节。其中包括一个深色界面的独立 Siri 应用、在 iPhone 屏幕顶部“灵动岛”（Dynamic Island）中的专属位置，以及由谷歌 Gemini AI 增强的类似聊天机器人的功能。听起来苹果正在加倍投入 Siri，并将其置于 iPhone 界面的核心位置。

### Apple Hardware
### 苹果硬件

After its expensive misstep with the Apple Vision Pro, it’s possible Apple may tease something about its upcoming smart glasses, which have not been officially confirmed to be in production, but are expected to arrive sometime next year. Apple doesn’t usually show off new hardware at its developer conference, but it has happened before, as with the Vision Pro reveal in 2023.

在 Apple Vision Pro 遭遇昂贵的挫折后，苹果可能会预告一些关于其即将推出的智能眼镜的消息。虽然官方尚未确认该产品已投入生产，但预计将于明年某个时候面世。苹果通常不会在开发者大会上展示新硬件，但这种情况以前也发生过，比如 2023 年 Vision Pro 的发布。

Apple may also announce a split-screen feature on its iPhones, which is neat, but perhaps more importantly, there’s a sliver of evidence that an Apple foldable device is coming later this year. Looking at Apple’s software tinkering is a decent way to divine what’s coming next, as its mobile operating system already has software features that could support a folding device.

苹果还可能宣布 iPhone 的分屏功能，这很不错，但更重要的是，有零星证据表明苹果的可折叠设备将于今年晚些时候推出。观察苹果在软件上的调整是推测未来动向的好方法，因为其移动操作系统已经具备了支持折叠设备的功能。

There don’t seem to be any new Mac computer announcements on the way, but those are more likely to come at Apple’s fall events anyway. There have also been rumors about Apple putting cameras into its AirPods. There might be a tease for that, but it’s unlikely.

目前似乎没有新的 Mac 电脑发布计划，但这些产品更有可能在苹果的秋季发布会上亮相。此外，还有传言称苹果正在将摄像头植入 AirPods。可能会有相关的预告，但可能性不大。
