# Notion restores access to Anthropic after service disruption
# Notion 在服务中断后恢复了对 Anthropic 的访问

Notion’s integration with Anthropic apparently had a hiccup this weekend. Early Sunday morning, the company posted, “Anthropic’s Opus 4.7 and 4.8 models are experiencing degraded performance, which is causing a higher rate of failures for users selecting these models in Notion AI.”
Notion 与 Anthropic 的集成显然在本周末出现了一些小故障。周日清晨，该公司发布公告称：“Anthropic 的 Opus 4.7 和 4.8 模型性能出现下降，导致用户在 Notion AI 中选择这些模型时失败率升高。”

As a result, Notion said it was disabling use of “all Anthropic models” in its automated productivity tool. Twelve hours later, Notion’s head of product Max Schoening wrote that he was “astonished” at “the amount of people RT-ing this because they want a story around model quality to be the reason.” (According to the public stats on X, Notion’s post has been reposted around 1,200 times.)
因此，Notion 表示将暂时禁用其自动化生产力工具中“所有 Anthropic 模型”的使用。十二小时后，Notion 产品负责人 Max Schoening 发文称，他对“如此多人转发此消息，仅仅是因为他们希望将模型质量作为（故障的）原因”感到“震惊”。（根据 X 平台上的公开数据，Notion 的该条推文已被转发约 1,200 次。）

“The degraded performance was a temporary service disruption,” Schoening said. “This happens. It happens to Notion, GitHub, AWS, your OpenClaw, and everything in between.” He added that Notion has restored access to Anthropic’s models.
Schoening 表示：“性能下降只是暂时的服务中断。这种情况时有发生。Notion、GitHub、AWS、OpenClaw 以及其他所有平台都曾遇到过类似问题。”他补充说，Notion 现已恢复对 Anthropic 模型的访问。

Meanwhile, an Anthropic spokesperson said in a statement, “A brief infrastructure issue caused elevated errors on multiple Claude models for a short period of time. The issue has since been resolved. We’re grateful to our users for their patience while we worked to restore service.”
与此同时，Anthropic 发言人在一份声明中表示：“一个短暂的基础设施问题导致多个 Claude 模型在短时间内错误率升高。该问题现已解决。感谢用户在我们恢复服务期间给予的耐心。”
