### Moonshot AI suspends new subscriptions due to Kimi K3 demand
### 月之暗面因 Kimi K3 需求激增暂停新订阅

Kimi.ai@Kimi_MoonshotKimi K3 has received far more love than we expected, and our GPUs are feeling it. Over the past 48 hours, demand has pushed close to the limits of our current capacity. To protect the experience of existing subscribers, we're temporarily pausing new subscriptions and prioritizing compute for current members. Existing subscribed users are not affected.

Kimi.ai@Kimi_Moonshot Kimi K3 收到的喜爱远超我们的预期，我们的 GPU 已经感受到了压力。在过去的 48 小时内，需求已接近我们目前的算力极限。为了保障现有订阅用户的体验，我们暂时暂停了新订阅的开通，并将算力优先分配给现有会员。已订阅用户不受影响。

We're adding capacity as fast as we can and will reopen new subscription spots in batches. Going forward, we'll also split membership into two more focused plans: Kimi Membership for Kimi Web, App, and Work; and Kimi Code Membership for coding workflows. This will help us match compute more precisely and keep the experience stable. Thank you for your patience and understanding!

我们正在以最快速度扩充算力，并将分批重新开放新订阅名额。未来，我们将把会员服务拆分为两个更具针对性的方案：面向 Kimi 网页版、App 及工作平台的“Kimi 会员”，以及面向编程工作流的“Kimi 代码会员”。这将有助于我们更精准地匹配算力，并保持体验的稳定性。感谢您的耐心与理解！
