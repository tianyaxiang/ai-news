# US Marshals arrest the Tate brothers in Miami
# 美国法警在迈阿密逮捕泰特兄弟

The two are facing new charges of rape, sex trafficking, and ‘extreme pornography’ in the UK.
两人在英国面临强奸、性贩运和“极端色情”等新指控。

Andrew and Tristan Tate were arrested Saturday in Miami by US Marshals in relation to new rape and sex trafficking charges in England. According to the Associated Press, British authorities are seeking the brothers’ extradition.
安德鲁·泰特（Andrew Tate）和特里斯坦·泰特（Tristan Tate）周六在迈阿密被美国法警逮捕，原因涉及他们在英国面临的强奸和性贩运新指控。据美联社报道，英国当局正在寻求将这对兄弟引渡回国。

The new charges facing Andrew Tate include seven counts of rape, three of sex trafficking, three counts of assault, and “19 charges relating to sexual images of children and extreme ‘pornography,’” according to the New York Times. Tristan Tate faces two new charges of rape, one of sexual assault, and three of sex trafficking. This is in addition to the 21 charges already announced in 2025 by British authorities, plus ongoing investigations in Romania and the US.
据《纽约时报》报道，安德鲁·泰特面临的新指控包括七项强奸罪、三项性贩运罪、三项袭击罪，以及“19项涉及儿童性图像和极端‘色情’的指控”。特里斯坦·泰特则面临两项新的强奸指控、一项性侵犯指控和三项性贩运指控。这还不包括英国当局在2025年已经宣布的21项指控，以及目前在罗马尼亚和美国正在进行的调查。

The brothers had previously been indicted in Romania on trafficking and rape charges, but a judge threw the indictment out over procedural issues. Many believe the lifting of travel restrictions on the two and the judges’ decision may have been related to a pressure campaign by the Trump administration on the Romanian government. The White House was also accused of intervening on Andrew Tate’s behalf last year when customs officials seized his devices.
这对兄弟此前曾在罗马尼亚因贩运和强奸罪被起诉，但法官因程序问题驳回了起诉。许多人认为，对两人旅行限制的解除以及法官的决定，可能与特朗普政府对罗马尼亚政府施加的压力有关。去年，当海关官员扣押安德鲁·泰特的设备时，白宫也被指控曾代表他进行干预。

The two are self-proclaimed misogynists who have amassed a following online despite being banned from most platforms for hate speech, including YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. The two ran an online school that claimed to teach men how to free themselves from “socially induced incarceration” and how to recruit women into “sexual slavery.” They’ve also led an online school on how to make money in crypto, drop shipping, and “scam-adjacent activities,” according to Vox, with a pyramid scheme-like structure.
两人自称是厌女症者，尽管因仇恨言论被YouTube、TikTok和Instagram等大多数平台封禁，但仍在网上积累了大量追随者。他们曾经营一所号称教导男性如何从“社会诱导的监禁”中解放出来，以及如何招募女性进行“性奴役”的在线学校。据Vox报道，他们还曾开设过一所教授如何通过加密货币、代发货和“类诈骗活动”赚钱的在线学校，其运作模式类似于金字塔传销。

The two have repeatedly faced arrest and criminal investigations for their activities dating back to 2015.
自2015年以来，两人因其活动多次面临逮捕和刑事调查。
