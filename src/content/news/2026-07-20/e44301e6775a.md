# Netflix paid $587M for Ben Affleck’s AI filmmaking startup
# Netflix 以 5.87 亿美元收购本·阿弗莱克的 AI 电影制作初创公司

In a new regulatory filing, Netflix revealed that it paid $587 million in cash for InterPositive, a startup co-founded by actor and director Ben Affleck.
在一份新的监管文件中，Netflix 披露其以 5.87 亿美元现金收购了由演员兼导演本·阿弗莱克（Ben Affleck）共同创立的初创公司 InterPositive。

The streaming company announced the acquisition in March, with a statement from Affleck saying he wanted to “protect the power of human creativity.”
这家流媒体公司于今年 3 月宣布了此次收购，阿弗莱克在声明中表示，他希望“保护人类创造力的力量”。

According to Affleck, InterPositive’s AI tools help filmmakers improve their footage in post-production, particularly when it comes to making up for “real-world production challenges such as missing shots, background replacements or incorrect lighting.”
据阿弗莱克介绍，InterPositive 的 AI 工具旨在帮助电影制作人在后期制作中优化素材，特别是在弥补“现实拍摄中的挑战，如镜头缺失、背景替换或灯光错误”方面。

At the time, Netflix announced that the entire InterPositive team would be joining the company, with Affleck joining as a senior advisor, but it didn’t disclose the financial terms of the deal.
当时，Netflix 宣布 InterPositive 的整个团队将加入公司，阿弗莱克将担任高级顾问，但并未披露交易的财务条款。

A subsequent report in Bloomberg suggested that the deal could be worth up to $600 million.
彭博社随后的一篇报道曾指出，这笔交易的价值可能高达 6 亿美元。

In its most recent earnings report, Netflix said that around 300 of its titles have already used generative AI.
在最近的财报中，Netflix 表示其已有约 300 部作品使用了生成式 AI 技术。
