# Kodak EC35 is a dirt-cheap point-and-shoot film camera
# 柯达 EC35：一款价格极其亲民的胶片傻瓜相机

The film photography renaissance continues and gets more affordable.
胶片摄影的复兴仍在持续，且变得愈发平价。

Following the success of its $99 Kodak-branded Snapic A1, Reto Project is releasing the Kodak EC35, an even more affordable 35mm film camera for just $34.99. The EC35 certainly isn’t fancy. Its 25mm acrylic lens with a fixed f/10 aperture and 1/100 shutter speed basically put it on par with a drugstore disposable. The fact that it has a manual wind and rewind only emphasizes that. And you can really lean into that disposable aesthetic thanks to its built-in automatic flash.
继 99 美元的柯达品牌 Snapic A1 取得成功后，Reto Project 即将推出柯达 EC35，这是一款售价仅为 34.99 美元、更加实惠的 35mm 胶片相机。EC35 显然并不高端，其 25mm 亚克力镜头配有固定的 f/10 光圈和 1/100 秒快门速度，性能基本与药店出售的一次性相机相当。手动过片和回卷的设计进一步印证了这一点，而内置的自动闪光灯则让你能充分体验那种“一次性相机”的复古美学。

Unlike the A1, there’s no double exposure feature here or zone focusing. The EC35 is designed to require almost no thought to use. Its one special feature is the sliding lens cover, reminiscent of the Olympus Stylus / Mju lineup. It also comes in a bunch of fun colors, including butter yellow, lavender, powder blue, blush pink, and avocado green. The lens covers are a slightly darker shade than the rest of the camera body, giving them some nice subtle contrast. Even the midnight black and vanilla white feature different colored accents, making them slightly more stylish than a disposable camera.
与 A1 不同，这款相机没有多重曝光功能，也不支持区域对焦。EC35 的设计初衷就是让用户无需思考即可拍摄。它唯一的一个特色功能是滑动式镜头盖，让人联想到奥林巴斯 Stylus / Mju 系列。它还提供多种有趣的配色，包括奶油黄、薰衣草紫、粉蓝色、腮红粉和牛油果绿。镜头盖的颜色比机身略深，形成了微妙的色彩对比。即便是午夜黑和香草白版本，也配有不同颜色的点缀，使其看起来比普通的一次性相机更具时尚感。
