# A Unified Approach to Interpreting Knowledge Distillation for Large Language Models via Interactions
# 通过交互作用统一解释大语言模型知识蒸馏的方法

**Abstract:** Despite the success of knowledge distillation (KD) in Large Language Models (LLMs), the underlying mechanism behind its efficacy remains unclear. In this paper, we propose a unified approach to explore the common mechanism of various KD methods using interactions.
**摘要：** 尽管知识蒸馏（KD）在大语言模型（LLM）中取得了成功，但其背后的有效机制尚不明确。在本文中，我们提出了一种统一的方法，利用交互作用来探索各种知识蒸馏方法的共同机制。

Specifically, we decompose the output score of the LLM into the sum of numerous interactions. Each interaction represents a nonlinear relationship involving a set of input variables (e.g., words). Based on the decomposed interactions, we discover that the common mechanism underlying various KD methods is the sparsification of interactions, i.e., student models retain fewer interactions for inference while suppressing other interactions to zero effects.
具体而言，我们将大语言模型的输出分数分解为多个交互作用的总和。每个交互作用代表涉及一组输入变量（例如单词）的非线性关系。基于这些分解出的交互作用，我们发现各种知识蒸馏方法背后的共同机制是交互作用的稀疏化，即学生模型在推理时保留较少的交互作用，同时将其他交互作用抑制为零影响。

Furthermore, we discover that the performance variance across different KD methods arises from their capabilities in handling complex interactions. A KD method typically yields better performance if it enables the student model to achieve higher sparsity of complex interactions.
此外，我们发现不同知识蒸馏方法之间的性能差异源于它们处理复杂交互作用的能力。如果一种知识蒸馏方法能够使学生模型实现更高程度的复杂交互作用稀疏化，它通常会产生更好的性能。

Motivated by these insights, we propose a plug-and-play loss function called Complex Interaction Penalty (CIP) to explicitly enforce the sparsity of complex interactions during the distillation process. Extensive experiments demonstrate that integrating CIP consistently improves the performance of diverse KD methods on both in-domain and out-of-distribution benchmarks.
受这些见解的启发，我们提出了一种名为“复杂交互惩罚”（Complex Interaction Penalty, CIP）的即插即用损失函数，旨在蒸馏过程中显式地强制执行复杂交互作用的稀疏性。大量实验表明，集成 CIP 可以持续提升多种知识蒸馏方法在域内和分布外基准测试中的性能。
