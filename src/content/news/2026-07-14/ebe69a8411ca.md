Have you dreamed about having a cyber living being (cyber waifu, digital pet) or digital companion that could play with and talk to you? With the power of modern large language models like ChatGPT and famous Claude, asking a virtual being to roleplay and chat with us is already easy enough for everyone. Platforms like Character.ai (a.k.a. c.ai) and JanitorAI as well as local playgrounds like SillyTavern are already good-enough solutions for a chat based or visual adventure game like experience. But, what about the abilities to play games? And see what you are coding at? Chatting while playing games, watching videos, and is capable of doing many other things.

Some also call this kind of being "digital human." Sadly, as it's not open sourced, you cannot interact with her after her live streams go offline. Therefore, this project, AIRI, offers another possibility here: let you own your digital life, cyber living, easily, anywhere, anytime.
也许你已经知道 Neuro-sama 了。她是目前最优秀的虚拟主播，能够玩游戏、聊天并与你及观众互动。有些人称这种存在为“数字人”。遗憾的是，由于它不是开源的，当她直播下线后，你就无法与她互动了。因此，本项目 AIRI 提供了另一种可能：让你轻松地在任何时间、任何地点拥有属于自己的数字生命和赛博生活。

### What's So Special About This Project?
### 这个项目有什么特别之处？

Unlike the other AI driven VTuber open source projects, アイリ was built with support of many Web technologies such as WebGPU, WebAudio, Web Workers, WebAssembly, WebSocket, etc. from the first day.
与其他 AI 驱动的 VTuber 开源项目不同，アイリ（AIRI）从第一天起就构建在对 WebGPU、WebAudio、Web Workers、WebAssembly、WebSocket 等多种 Web 技术支持的基础上。

Tip: Worrying about the performance drop since we are using Web related technologies? Don't worry, while the Web browser version is meant to give an insight into how much we can push and do inside browsers and webviews, we will never fully rely on this. The desktop version of AIRI is capable of using native NVIDIA CUDA and Apple Metal by default (thanks to HuggingFace & the beloved candle project), without any complex dependency management.
提示：担心使用 Web 相关技术会导致性能下降？别担心，虽然 Web 浏览器版本旨在展示我们在浏览器和 webview 中能达到的极限，但我们绝不会完全依赖它。AIRI 的桌面版本默认支持使用原生的 NVIDIA CUDA 和 Apple Metal（感谢 HuggingFace 和备受喜爱的 candle 项目），且无需复杂的依赖管理。

Considering the tradeoff, it is partially powered by Web technologies for graphics, layouts, animations, and the WIP plugin system for everyone to integrate things. This means that アイリ is capable of running on modern browsers and devices and even on mobile devices (already done with PWA support). This brings a lot of possibilities for us (the developers) to build and extend the power of アイリ VTuber to the next level, while still leaving the flexibility for users to enable features that require TCP connections or other non-Web technologies such as connecting to a Discord voice channel or playing Minecraft and Factorio with friends.
考虑到权衡，它部分由 Web 技术驱动，用于图形、布局、动画以及供大家集成功能的插件系统（开发中）。这意味着 アイリ 能够在现代浏览器、设备甚至移动设备上运行（已通过 PWA 支持实现）。这为我们（开发者）提供了无限可能，可以将 アイリ VTuber 的能力提升到新的高度，同时保留了灵活性，让用户能够启用需要 TCP 连接或其他非 Web 技术的功能，例如连接到 Discord 语音频道或与朋友一起玩《Minecraft》和《Factorio》。

Note: We are still in the early stage of development where we are seeking out talented developers to join us and help us to make アイリ a reality. It's ok if you are not familiar with Vue.js, TypeScript, and devtools required for this project, you can join us as an artist, designer, or even help us to launch our first live stream. Even if you are a big fan of React, Svelte or even Solid, we welcome you. You can open a sub-directory to add features that you want to see in アイリ, or would like to experiment with.
注意：我们仍处于开发早期阶段，正在寻找有才华的开发者加入我们，帮助我们将 アイリ 变为现实。即使你不熟悉 Vue.js、TypeScript 和本项目所需的开发工具也没关系，你可以以艺术家、设计师的身份加入，甚至帮助我们开启第一次直播。即使你是 React、Svelte 甚至 Solid 的忠实粉丝，我们也欢迎你。你可以创建一个子目录来添加你希望在 アイリ 中看到的功能，或者进行实验。

### Current Progress & Roadmap
### 当前进展与路线图

*   **Brain**: Play Minecraft, Play Factorio (WIP, but PoC and demo available), Play Kerbal Space Program (announcement TBD), Co-play Helldivers 2 (WIP), Chat in Telegram, Chat in Discord
*   **大脑**：玩《Minecraft》、玩《Factorio》（开发中，但已有 PoC 和演示）、玩《坎巴拉太空计划》（公告待定）、联机《绝地潜兵 2》（开发中）、Telegram 聊天、Discord 聊天
*   **Memory**: Pure in-browser database support (DuckDB WASM | pglite), Memory Alaya (WIP), Pure in-browser local (WebGPU) inference
*   **记忆**：纯浏览器内数据库支持 (DuckDB WASM | pglite)、Memory Alaya（开发中）、纯浏览器内本地 (WebGPU) 推理
*   **Ears**: Audio input from browser, Audio input from Discord, Client side speech recognition, Client side talking detection
*   **耳朵**：浏览器音频输入、Discord 音频输入、客户端语音识别、客户端说话检测
*   **Mouth**: Multi-provider voice synthesis, including ElevenLabs, Microsoft/Azure Speech, OpenAI-compatible TTS, Alibaba Cloud Model Studio, and local Kokoro TTS
*   **嘴巴**：多供应商语音合成，包括 ElevenLabs、Microsoft/Azure Speech、OpenAI 兼容 TTS、阿里云百炼模型服务以及本地 Kokoro TTS
*   **Body**: VRM support (Control VRM model, VRM model animations, Auto blink, Auto look at, Idle eye movement), Live2D support (Control Live2D model, Live2D model animations)
*   **身体**：VRM 支持（控制 VRM 模型、VRM 模型动画、自动眨眼、自动注视、闲置眼部运动）、Live2D 支持（控制 Live2D 模型、Live2D 模型动画）
