# LieBN: Batch Normalization over Lie Groups
# LieBN：李群上的批归一化

**Abstract:** Manifold-valued measurements are prevalent in various machine learning tasks. Recent advances have extended Deep Neural Networks (DNNs) to operate on manifolds, accompanied by normalization techniques tailored to different geometries, collectively referred to as Riemannian normalization. However, most existing Riemannian normalization methods are either designed for specific manifolds or fail to effectively normalize manifold-valued sample distributions.

**摘要：** 流形值测量在各种机器学习任务中非常普遍。近期的研究进展已将深度神经网络（DNNs）扩展到流形上运行，并辅以针对不同几何结构定制的归一化技术，统称为黎曼归一化（Riemannian normalization）。然而，大多数现有的黎曼归一化方法要么是针对特定流形设计的，要么无法有效地对流形值样本分布进行归一化。

To address these limitations, we propose LieBN, a framework for Riemannian Batch Normalization (RBN) over Lie groups. Our approach leverages the theoretically convenient left- and right-invariant metrics, which naturally exist in every Lie group, and provides theoretical guarantees for controlling the Riemannian mean and variance.

为了解决这些局限性，我们提出了 LieBN，这是一个用于李群上黎曼批归一化（RBN）的框架。我们的方法利用了每个李群中自然存在的、理论上非常方便的左不变和右不变度量，并为控制黎曼均值和方差提供了理论保证。

We instantiate LieBN across nine distinct geometries: four on the Symmetric Positive Definite (SPD) manifold, one on the group of rotation matrices, and four on the manifold of full-rank correlation matrices. Notably, among the SPD metrics, we introduce a novel right-invariant metric and extend three existing Lie group structures via matrix power deformation. Extensive experiments on different manifolds validate the effectiveness of our framework. The code is available at this https URL.

我们将 LieBN 实例化于九种不同的几何结构中：四种在对称正定（SPD）流形上，一种在旋转矩阵群上，四种在满秩相关矩阵流形上。值得注意的是，在 SPD 度量中，我们引入了一种新颖的右不变度量，并通过矩阵幂变形扩展了三种现有的李群结构。在不同流形上的大量实验验证了我们框架的有效性。代码可在该链接获取。
