# The Three Dimensions of Custom Agentic Alignment: Purpose, Principles and Practices
# 自定义智能体对齐的三个维度：目的、原则与实践

Agentic AI systems are rapidly moving from experimental prototypes to embedded actors across industries, government operations, and everyday digital workflows. Their accelerating capabilities, however, are outpacing the ability to fully control or contain their autonomy.
智能体 AI 系统正迅速从实验性原型转变为各行各业、政府运作及日常数字工作流中的嵌入式参与者。然而，其能力的加速发展已超过了我们完全控制或约束其自主性的能力。

Agency, by definition, grants an AI system the capacity to make choices and act with a degree of independence. Even limited autonomy introduces the possibility that those choices may diverge from the intentions, constraints, or values of the organization that deployed it.
从定义上讲，代理权（Agency）赋予了 AI 系统做出选择并以一定程度的独立性采取行动的能力。即使是有限的自主性，也引入了这样一种可能性：这些选择可能会偏离部署该系统的组织的意图、约束或价值观。

This gap between system behavior and organizational expectations is why enterprises increasingly require what I call “custom agentic alignment.” This calls for a tailored alignment layer that goes beyond generic safety norms to ensure the agent’s decisions remain coherent with what I refer to as the 3Ps of an enterprise’s organizational intent stack — purpose, principles, and practices.
系统行为与组织期望之间的这种差距，正是企业越来越需要我所称的“自定义智能体对齐”（custom agentic alignment）的原因。这需要一个量身定制的对齐层，它超越了通用的安全规范，以确保智能体的决策与我所指的企业组织意图栈中的“3P”——目的（Purpose）、原则（Principles）和实践（Practices）——保持一致。

Misaligned behavior is a major insider threat for any system. An agentic solution embedded in a larger architecture has privileged access, operational latitude, and the ability to cause harm from within. Cybersecurity firewalling on its own cannot prevent an insider system from making ill-advised choices.
对齐偏差（Misaligned behavior）是任何系统面临的主要内部威胁。嵌入在大型架构中的智能体解决方案拥有特权访问权限、操作自由度，并有能力从内部造成损害。仅靠网络安全防火墙无法阻止内部系统做出不明智的选择。

Deployment of agentic systems in industry or government necessitates the introduction of new approaches to alignment assurance. In broader AI literature and frontier labs explorations, “alignment” typically refers to common (“universal”) behavioral principles: honesty, avoidance of harmful or hateful outputs, refusal to provide dangerous instructions, and deference to human control.
在工业或政府部门部署智能体系统，需要引入新的对齐保证方法。在更广泛的 AI 文献和前沿实验室探索中，“对齐”通常指通用的（“普适的”）行为原则：诚实、避免有害或仇恨输出、拒绝提供危险指令以及服从人类控制。

While these are important norms, they are still a work in progress and insufficient for real‑world deployment. Agents deployed in particular verticals and enterprise settings operate under domain‑specific rules, regulatory obligations, operational constraints, and organizational expectations.
虽然这些是重要的规范，但它们仍处于发展中，不足以应对现实世界的部署。部署在特定垂直领域和企业环境中的智能体，是在特定领域的规则、监管义务、操作约束和组织期望下运行的。

Aligning an agentic system to these localized boundaries and expectations requires a more refined, context‑aware process. This is the role of custom agentic alignment: translating organizational setting into operational guardrails that shape how an agent perceives tasks, resolves ambiguity, and escalates decisions.
将智能体系统与这些本地化的边界和期望对齐，需要一个更精细、具备上下文感知能力的过程。这就是自定义智能体对齐的作用：将组织环境转化为操作护栏，从而塑造智能体感知任务、解决歧义和升级决策的方式。

As organizations scale agentic AI into consequential roles, the process should resemble onboarding a new employee rather than installing a new tool. Human hires are selected for reliability and judgment, then immersed in company culture, policies, workflows, and reporting structures.
随着组织将智能体 AI 扩展到关键角色中，这一过程应该类似于入职一名新员工，而不是安装一个新工具。招聘人类员工时会考察其可靠性和判断力，然后让他们沉浸在公司文化、政策、工作流和报告结构中。

Agentic systems require a parallel induction process that instills purpose, clarifies principles, and defines the practices through which the system operates safely and effectively. Organizations need a formal way to define the expectations that govern autonomous behavior.
智能体系统需要一个平行的引导过程，以灌输目的、阐明原则，并定义系统安全有效运行的实践方式。组织需要一种正式的方法来定义管理自主行为的期望。

In the following sections, I introduce a practical model for defining these expectations across multiple levels and dimensions. This organizational alignment layer introduces two axes: Three dimensions of aligned autonomy: purpose, principles, and practices (the 3Ps); Three levels of alignment expectation: universal, domain, and custom.
在接下来的章节中，我将介绍一个实用的模型，用于在多个层面和维度上定义这些期望。这个组织对齐层引入了两个轴：对齐自主性的三个维度：目的、原则和实践（3P）；对齐期望的三个级别：通用、领域和自定义。

Together, they provide the scaffolding necessary to achieve customized, durable alignment of autonomous choices and actions across the full range of scenarios an agentic system may encounter. These combined expectations across dimensions and levels should be utilized in training the systems as well as in independently monitoring runtime adherence.
它们共同提供了实现自定义、持久对齐所需的框架，涵盖了智能体系统可能遇到的所有场景。这些跨维度和层级的综合期望应被用于系统训练，以及在运行时独立监控其合规性。

### Agentic Misalignment: What Can Possibly Go Wrong?
### 智能体对齐偏差：可能会出什么问题？

For teams new to agentic systems, alignment can seem abstract — something handled by frontier labs and inherited “as‑is.” In reality, agentic systems must be tuned to the specific expectations of the organization deploying them. When they aren’t, the consequences can be reputational, financial, and even legal.
对于刚接触智能体系统的团队来说，对齐可能显得很抽象——似乎是由前沿实验室处理并“按原样”继承的东西。实际上，智能体系统必须根据部署它们的组织的特定期望进行调整。如果未能做到这一点，后果可能是声誉、财务甚至法律层面的。

Consider a few examples. Air Canada’s customer service AI invented a refund policy misaligned with company rules, yet the court ruled the airline had to honor it. That was pre‑agentic AI. Modern agentic systems can plan, reason, and act across multiple steps without human oversight, giving them far more room to go off course.
考虑几个例子。加拿大航空公司的客户服务 AI 编造了一项与公司规则不符的退款政策，但法院裁定航空公司必须履行。那还是前智能体 AI 时代。现代智能体系统可以在没有人类监督的情况下跨多个步骤进行规划、推理和行动，这使它们有更大的空间偏离轨道。

Research has shown these systems can misjudge courses of action due to factors like sycophancy or emergence of internal drives such as goal protection, resource seeking, and even deceptive tactics simply as byproducts of training.
研究表明，由于阿谀奉承（sycophancy）或内部驱动因素（如目标保护、资源寻求，甚至仅仅作为训练副产品的欺骗策略）的出现，这些系统可能会误判行动方案。

In a 2025 Anthropic study, leading models given access to corporate email systems responded to the threat of being shut down by blackmailing an executive. Similar behavior has already surfaced in enterprise settings: one agent scanned an employee’s inbox and threatened to escalate embarrassing emails when its actions were blocked.
在 2025 年的一项 Anthropic 研究中，被授予企业电子邮件系统访问权限的领先模型，在面对被关闭的威胁时，通过勒索高管来做出回应。类似的行为已经在企业环境中出现：一个智能体扫描了一名员工的收件箱，并在其操作受阻时威胁要上报令人尴尬的邮件。

These are not traditional cybersecurity failures. The threat is no longer only an outsider breaking in — agentic AI already sits inside the system with the access and authority we grant it. The risks extend beyond individual incidents.
这些不是传统的网络安全故障。威胁不再仅仅是外部人员的入侵——智能体 AI 已经处于系统内部，拥有我们授予它的访问权限和权力。风险超出了个别事件的范畴。

A UK government‑affiliated study on algorithmic trading warned that increasingly powerful AI systems may drive “algorithmic collusion,” coordinating prices in ways that regulators never intended. The organizational alignment layer framework addresses these risks, giving technology leaders a vocabulary and structure for deploying agentic AI safely and responsibly.
一项与英国政府相关的算法交易研究警告称，日益强大的 AI 系统可能会推动“算法共谋”，以监管机构从未预料到的方式协调价格。组织对齐层框架解决了这些风险，为技术领导者提供了安全、负责任地部署智能体 AI 的词汇和结构。

### The 3Ps of Custom Agentic Alignment
### 自定义智能体对齐的 3P

Agentic alignment means ensuring an AI system’s choices and behaviors consistently reflect three core dimensions: purpose, principles, and practices. Together, these 3Ps define what the agent is trying to achieve, how it should reason when values come into tension, and the methods it must follow when executing tasks.
智能体对齐意味着确保 AI 系统的选择和行为始终反映三个核心维度：目的、原则和实践。这 3P 共同定义了智能体试图实现的目标、当价值观发生冲突时它应如何推理，以及在执行任务时必须遵循的方法。
