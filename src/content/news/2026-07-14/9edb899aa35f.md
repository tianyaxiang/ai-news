# Vision Transformers Learn Gestalt-Like Figure-Ground Cues from Natural Images
# 视觉 Transformer 从自然图像中学习类似格式塔的图底线索

**Abstract:** Figure-ground organization in the human visual system relies on several shape-based cues, including surroundedness, convexity, and symmetry. While these cues have been extensively studied using abstract stimuli, little is known about how they operate under natural conditions or how they arise from the statistics of natural scenes.

**摘要：** 人类视觉系统中的图底组织（Figure-ground organization）依赖于多种基于形状的线索，包括包围性（surroundedness）、凸性（convexity）和对称性（symmetry）。尽管这些线索已在抽象刺激下得到了广泛研究，但人们对于它们如何在自然条件下运作，或如何从自然场景的统计规律中产生，仍知之甚少。

Deep neural networks offer a promising path forward: a model that relies on the same figure-ground cues as humans would provide tractable experimental access to the underlying mechanisms. In this study, we evaluate shape-based figure-ground organization in Vision Transformers (ViTs), for which prior work has demonstrated the emergence of object-based grouping.

深度神经网络提供了一条有前景的研究路径：如果一个模型能够像人类一样依赖相同的图底线索，那么它将为研究其潜在机制提供可行的实验途径。在本研究中，我们评估了视觉 Transformer（ViTs）中基于形状的图底组织能力，此前已有研究证明了 ViTs 中会出现基于对象的组合（object-based grouping）现象。

We test 25 ViTs spanning supervised and self-supervised training objectives, by fitting linear probes to predict figure-ground assignment from intermediate patch representations using both natural images and controlled artificial stimuli that isolate individual cues. Our results show that ViTs robustly encode surroundedness and convexity, and that probes trained on natural images generalize zero-shot to artificial stimuli across several models.

我们测试了 25 个涵盖监督学习和自监督学习目标的 ViT 模型，通过拟合线性探针（linear probes），利用自然图像和隔离了单一线索的受控人工刺激，从中间补丁（patch）表示中预测图底分配。结果表明，ViTs 能够稳健地编码包围性和凸性，并且在多个模型中，基于自然图像训练的探针能够零样本（zero-shot）泛化到人工刺激上。

For symmetry we observe mixed results: the cue is encoded for uniformly colored but not for textured regions. Taken together, our findings demonstrate that Gestalt-like figure-ground cues can be learned from natural scene statistics and position ViTs as a compelling model system for studying the computational mechanisms of perceptual organization.

对于对称性，我们观察到的结果喜忧参半：该线索在颜色均匀的区域被编码，但在有纹理的区域则不然。综上所述，我们的研究结果表明，类似格式塔（Gestalt-like）的图底线索可以从自然场景的统计规律中习得，这使得 ViTs 成为研究感知组织计算机制的一个极具吸引力的模型系统。
