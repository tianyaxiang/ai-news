# Director: Accelerating Distributed MoE Serving via Online Proactive Expert Placement
# Director：通过在线主动专家放置加速分布式 MoE 推理服务

Expert parallelism has become the prevailing paradigm to serve Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) models. Its efficiency depends on the communication and computation latencies of the GPUs, which are linked to the placement of experts in the GPUs.
专家并行已成为服务混合专家（MoE）模型的主流范式。其效率取决于 GPU 的通信和计算延迟，而这些延迟与专家在 GPU 上的放置位置密切相关。

Existing works for optimizing expert placement focus on leveraging past requests' expert activation patterns. However, they demonstrate deficiencies facing diverse and rapidly changing request patterns, calling for an online, proactive approach.
现有的专家放置优化工作主要侧重于利用历史请求的专家激活模式。然而，在面对多样化且快速变化的请求模式时，这些方法表现出不足，因此需要一种在线、主动的解决方案。

Implementing such an approach requires addressing several challenges: the uncertainty associated with incoming requests' expert activation, the cost of expert migration, and the NP-hard complexity in optimization.
实现这种方法需要解决几个挑战：与传入请求的专家激活相关的不确定性、专家迁移的成本，以及优化过程中 NP-hard 复杂性带来的难题。

Therefore, we present Director, a new distributed MoE serving system that minimizes end-to-end latency via prediction-driven, online expert placement. Director uses either a lightweight cascaded predictor or a low-bit quantized replica for expert activation patterns of incoming requests.
因此，我们提出了 Director，这是一个全新的分布式 MoE 服务系统，通过预测驱动的在线专家放置来最小化端到端延迟。Director 使用轻量级级联预测器或低位量化副本，来预测传入请求的专家激活模式。

An online migration module then enacts the changes with near-zero downtime by executing migrations in compute-bound phases, keeping disruption bounded. At its core, a relaxation-based expert placement optimizer operates under capacity constraints, runs in polynomial time, and achieves a $(1+\epsilon)$ approximation ratio.
随后，在线迁移模块通过在计算密集阶段执行迁移，以近乎零停机的方式实现调整，从而将干扰控制在有限范围内。其核心是一个基于松弛法的专家放置优化器，它在容量约束下运行，能够在多项式时间内完成计算，并达到 $(1+\epsilon)$ 的近似比。

Finally, we implement a prototype and demonstrate, through extensive experiments, a reduction in end-to-end latency of $11\sim55\%$ for popular MoE models (e.g., Mistral, DeepSeek and Qwen) compared to existing work.
最后，我们实现了一个原型系统，并通过大量实验证明，与现有工作相比，该系统在主流 MoE 模型（如 Mistral、DeepSeek 和 Qwen）上的端到端延迟降低了 $11\sim55\%$。
