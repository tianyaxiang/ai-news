### Reward Transport: Property Control in Flow Matching via Noise-Space Alignment
**奖励传输：通过噪声空间对齐实现流匹配中的属性控制**

**Abstract:** The coupling in flow matching -- the rule pairing noise vectors with data points -- is typically treated as a computational choice. We show that this coupling can instead serve as an alignment interface: by matching noise and data according to a target molecular property, it embeds controllable structure directly into the learned flow field.
**摘要：** 流匹配中的耦合（即噪声向量与数据点配对的规则）通常被视为一种计算选择。我们证明，这种耦合实际上可以作为一种对齐接口：通过根据目标分子属性匹配噪声和数据，它可以将可控结构直接嵌入到学习到的流场中。

Building on this view, we introduce Reward Transport, which uses optimal transport coupling at training time to align a scalar noise-space coordinate with molecular rewards; at inference, varying this coordinate steers the generated distribution without requiring an oracle, reward model, gradient guidance, or additional computation.
基于这一观点，我们引入了“奖励传输”（Reward Transport），它在训练时利用最优传输耦合将标量噪声空间坐标与分子奖励对齐；在推理阶段，改变该坐标即可引导生成的分布，而无需预言机（oracle）、奖励模型、梯度引导或额外的计算。

In the coupling-preserving limit, thresholding this coordinate recovers the Cross-Entropy Method's truncated reward distribution, providing a principled, continuously adjustable distribution-level control knob.
在保持耦合的极限情况下，对该坐标进行阈值处理可以恢复交叉熵方法（Cross-Entropy Method）的截断奖励分布，从而提供了一种有原则的、可连续调节的分布级控制旋钮。

Empirically, on ZINC-250K and GuacaMol, sweeping the scalar induces monotone control of logP and consistent QED control over its operating range; most tellingly, the same knob produces opposite structural responses for different targets, growing molecules for logP but shrinking them for QED, which rules out a generic size bias.
实验表明，在 ZINC-250K 和 GuacaMol 数据集上，扫描该标量可以实现对 logP 的单调控制以及在其工作范围内对 QED 的一致性控制；最显著的是，同一个旋钮针对不同目标产生了相反的结构响应——在优化 logP 时分子变大，而在优化 QED 时分子变小，这排除了通用尺寸偏差的可能性。

The interface is complementary to classifier-free guidance and conditional flow matching, while a negative result under epsilon-prediction diffusion clarifies where coupling-level alignment is structurally absent.
该接口与无分类器引导（classifier-free guidance）和条件流匹配互补，而 epsilon 预测扩散下的负面结果则阐明了耦合级对齐在何处结构性缺失。
