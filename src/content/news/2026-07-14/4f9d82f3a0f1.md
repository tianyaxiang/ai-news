# HAT Super-Resolution and a PARSeq+CLIP4STR Voting Ensemble for Extreme In-the-Wild License Plate Recognition
# HAT 超分辨率与 PARSeq+CLIP4STR 投票集成模型：应对极端野外场景车牌识别

**Abstract:** We describe our entry to the ICIP 2026 Grand Challenge on Extreme In-the-Wild License Plate Super-Resolution (XLPSR), which scored 9.73 wECR on the public validation leaderboard. The system pairs a Hybrid Attention Transformer super-resolution (HAT) front-end with an ensemble of two scene-text recognisers (PARSeq-S and CLIP4STR-B) and a confidence-weighted character-voting scheme that abstains on uncertain positions.

**摘要：** 我们介绍了参与 ICIP 2026“极端野外场景车牌超分辨率”（XLPSR）挑战赛的方案，该方案在公开验证排行榜上获得了 9.73 的 wECR 分数。该系统将混合注意力 Transformer 超分辨率（HAT）前端与两个场景文本识别器（PARSeq-S 和 CLIP4STR-B）的集成模型相结合，并采用了一种置信度加权的字符投票机制，能够在不确定的位置选择弃权。

We treat XLPSR as a recognition task gated by image legibility: the SR step exists to lift characters out of sub-pixel territory, and the asymmetric scoring rule (+2 / -1 / 0) is exploited explicitly through abstention. Our pipeline runs in 1.7 s per sequence on RTX 3090 (max 2.7 s, p99 2.4 s), well under the 60 s/sequence Docker budget.

我们将 XLPSR 视为一项受图像清晰度制约的识别任务：超分辨率（SR）步骤旨在将字符从亚像素区域中提取出来，并利用非对称评分规则（+2 / -1 / 0）通过弃权机制进行显式优化。我们的流水线在 RTX 3090 上处理每个序列平均耗时 1.7 秒（最大 2.7 秒，p99 为 2.4 秒），远低于 Docker 规定的每序列 60 秒的预算。
