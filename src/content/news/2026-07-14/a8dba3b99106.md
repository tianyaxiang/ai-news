# Apple’s public betas for iOS 27 and more are out now
# 苹果 iOS 27 等系统的公测版现已发布

Apple has just released public betas for iOS 27 and other major OS updates that are set to publicly launch this fall. The big new feature this year is Siri AI, the delayed AI-powered revamp to Siri. It actually works — which is big praise! — though it keeps things brief.

苹果刚刚发布了 iOS 27 及其他主要操作系统的公测版，这些系统预计将于今年秋季正式推出。今年最大的新功能是 Siri AI，这是 Siri 经过人工智能驱动后的升级版本，此前曾有所推迟。它确实能正常工作——这已经是很高的评价了！——尽管它的回答依然保持简洁。

Other betas available now include iPadOS 27, watchOS 27, and macOS 27 Golden Gate. If you want to test out Apple’s upcoming updates, fair warning that you may run into issues like unexpected glitches or a battery that drains faster than you’re used to. Use your best judgment on whether you should actually install the beta or wait to install an update until it’s officially released. But if you’re willing to roll the dice and live on the edge, installing public betas gives you a chance to peek into Apple’s future. (Though I strongly recommend backing up your device before installing a beta, just in case you need to roll things back.)

目前可用的其他公测版还包括 iPadOS 27、watchOS 27 和 macOS 27 Golden Gate。如果你想抢先体验苹果即将推出的更新，请注意，你可能会遇到一些问题，例如意外的故障或电池耗电速度比平时更快。请自行判断是否应该安装测试版，还是等待正式版发布后再更新。但如果你愿意冒险尝试，安装公测版将让你有机会一窥苹果的未来。（不过，我强烈建议在安装测试版之前备份你的设备，以防需要回退系统。）

To join the iOS 27 public beta program, visit this page on Apple’s website and click the button to sign up. Once you’ve done that — and backed up your device, which you did, right? — open the Settings app, then go to General > Software Update. At the top of your screen, you should see a section for “Beta Updates.” Tap that, and then select the iOS 27 public beta.

要加入 iOS 27 公测计划，请访问苹果官网的相关页面并点击注册按钮。完成注册后——并且备份了你的设备（你备份了吧？）——打开“设置”应用，进入“通用” > “软件更新”。在屏幕顶部，你应该能看到“Beta 版更新”选项。点击它，然后选择 iOS 27 公测版即可。

For other Apple products, the steps to get set up on the beta are generally similar.

对于其他苹果产品，安装测试版的步骤大致相同。
