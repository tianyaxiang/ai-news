# The Pixel colors might rule this year
# 今年 Pixel 的配色可能会大放异彩

If this hot pink Pixel is real, that would be awesome.
如果这款亮粉色的 Pixel 是真的，那一定会非常酷。

This year’s Google Pixel 11 lineup might come in a bunch of funky colors. A series of now-deleted Amazon listings spotted by 9to5Google show what appear to be placeholders for Google’s upcoming Pixel 11 in hot pink Fuchsia (Hibiscus), vibrant green Moss (Pistachio), and Midnight (Obsidian) black.
今年的 Google Pixel 11 系列可能会推出一系列时髦的配色。据 9to5Google 发现，亚马逊上曾出现过一系列现已被删除的商品列表，其中似乎包含了 Google 即将推出的 Pixel 11 的占位信息，配色包括亮粉色“紫红色”（芙蓉色）、充满活力的绿色“苔藓色”（开心果色）以及“午夜黑”（黑曜石色）。

We’ve seen two sets of names for the Pixel 11’s rumored colorways so far, and, confusingly, both variations are included in the early Amazon listings, according to 9to5Google. The Pixel 11 Pro listings spotted by 9to5Google reportedly showed the device in a dusky green Pine (Olive), a frosty blue Light Fog (Fog), pastel purple Sterling (Sterling/Frost), and orange-y Dune (Canyon). Meanwhile, the Pixel 11 Pro Fold may just come in two colors: Pine (Olive) and Midnight (Obsidian).
据 9to5Google 报道，目前我们已经看到了两套关于 Pixel 11 传闻配色的命名，令人困惑的是，这两套命名都出现在了早期的亚马逊列表中。据报道，9to5Google 发现的 Pixel 11 Pro 列表显示该设备有暗绿色“松木色”（橄榄色）、冰蓝色“轻雾色”（雾霾色）、淡紫色“纯银色”（霜色）以及橙色调的“沙丘色”（峡谷色）。与此同时，Pixel 11 Pro Fold 可能仅提供两种颜色：松木色（橄榄色）和午夜黑（黑曜石色）。

The purported listings also contained some other details about the upcoming devices, such as an $899 price tag for the Pixel 11, which will supposedly come with a 6.3-inch, 1080 x 2424 display, 256GB of storage, and 12GB of RAM, 9to5Google reports. That lines up with rumors that Google could raise the price of the base Pixel 11 by dropping the lowest 128GB storage configuration. The listings reportedly mentioned a not-yet-announced “Google Pixel Tag” as well, which sounds like some kind of tracking device.
据 9to5Google 报道，这些所谓的列表还包含了一些关于即将发布设备的其他细节，例如 Pixel 11 的定价为 899 美元，据称将配备 6.3 英寸、1080 x 2424 分辨率的显示屏、256GB 存储空间和 12GB 内存。这与此前关于 Google 可能通过取消最低 128GB 存储配置来提高基础版 Pixel 11 价格的传闻相吻合。据报道，列表中还提到了尚未公布的“Google Pixel Tag”，听起来像是一种追踪设备。

But before you get too excited, 9to5Google notes that these listings should be taken with a “big grain of salt,” as there were some strange inclusions. Some of the listings mentioned Android 16, even though Android 17 launched last month, and a SIM ejector tool, which most of the Pixel 10 lineup doesn’t need. Google is getting ready to take the wraps off its new Pixel phones during its hardware event on August 12th, and it looks like the upcoming devices may have just surfaced in the Federal Communications Commission database as well.
但在你过于兴奋之前，9to5Google 指出，这些列表应“持保留态度”，因为其中包含了一些奇怪的内容。例如，部分列表提到了 Android 16，尽管 Android 17 上个月就已经发布了；此外还提到了 SIM 卡弹出工具，而大多数 Pixel 10 系列机型并不需要该工具。Google 正准备在 8 月 12 日的硬件发布会上揭开新款 Pixel 手机的面纱，而且看起来这些即将推出的设备似乎也刚刚出现在美国联邦通信委员会（FCC）的数据库中。
