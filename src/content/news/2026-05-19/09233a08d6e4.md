# Deep Pre-Alignment for VLMs

**Abstract:** Most Vision Language Models (VLMs) directly map outputs from ViT encoders to the LLM via a lightweight projector. While effective, recent analysis suggests this architecture suffers from an alignment challenge: visual features remain distant from the text space in the initial layers of the LLM, forcing the model to waste critical depth on superficial modality alignment rather than deep understanding and complex reasoning.

**摘要：** 大多数视觉语言模型（VLM）通过轻量级投影器将 ViT 编码器的输出直接映射到大语言模型（LLM）。尽管这种架构行之有效，但近期分析表明它面临着对齐挑战：在 LLM 的初始层中，视觉特征与文本空间仍存在较大距离，迫使模型将宝贵的深度资源浪费在表层的模态对齐上，而非用于深层理解和复杂推理。

In this work, we propose Deep Pre-Alignment (DPA), a novel architecture that replaces the standard ViT encoder with a small VLM as perceiver, ensuring visual features are deeply aligned with the text space of the target large language model.

在这项工作中，我们提出了深度预对齐（Deep Pre-Alignment, DPA）架构。这是一种新型架构，它将标准的 ViT 编码器替换为一个小型 VLM 作为感知器（perceiver），从而确保视觉特征与目标大语言模型的文本空间实现深度对齐。

Comprehensive experiments demonstrate the effectiveness of DPA. On the 4B parameter scale, DPA outperforms baselines by 1.9 points across 8 multimodal benchmarks, with gains widening to 3.0 points at the 32B scale. Moreover, by offloading alignment to the perceiver, DPA achieves a 32.9% reduction in language capability forgetting over 3 text benchmarks.

全面的实验证明了 DPA 的有效性。在 4B 参数规模下，DPA 在 8 个多模态基准测试中的表现比基准模型高出 1.9 个百分点；在 32B 规模下，这一优势进一步扩大至 3.0 个百分点。此外，通过将对齐任务卸载给感知器，DPA 在 3 个文本基准测试中将语言能力的遗忘率降低了 32.9%。

We further demonstrate that these gains are consistent across different LLM families including Qwen3 and LLaMA 3.2, highlighting the generality of our approach. Beyond performance, DPA also offers a seamless upgrade path for current VLM development, requiring only a modular replacement for the visual encoder with marginal computation overhead.

我们进一步证明，这些增益在包括 Qwen3 和 LLaMA 3.2 在内的不同 LLM 系列中均保持一致，凸显了我们方法的通用性。除了性能提升外，DPA 还为当前的 VLM 开发提供了一条无缝升级路径，仅需对视觉编码器进行模块化替换，且计算开销极小。
