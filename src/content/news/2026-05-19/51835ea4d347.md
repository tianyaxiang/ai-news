# ReactiveGWM: Steering NPC in Reactive Game World Models
# ReactiveGWM：在响应式游戏世界模型中操控 NPC

Current game world models simulate environments from a subjective, player-centric perspective. However, by treating the Non-Player Character (NPC) merely as background pixels, these models cannot capture interactions between the player and NPC. In that sense, they act as passive video renderers rather than real simulation engines, lacking the physical understanding needed to model action-induced NPC reactivities.

当前的游戏世界模型大多从主观的、以玩家为中心的视角来模拟环境。然而，由于这些模型仅将非玩家角色（NPC）视为背景像素，它们无法捕捉玩家与 NPC 之间的交互。从这个意义上讲，它们更像是被动的视频渲染器，而非真正的模拟引擎，缺乏建模“由动作引发的 NPC 反应”所需的物理理解能力。

We introduce ReactiveGWM, a reactive game world model that synthesizes dynamic interactions between the player and NPC. Instead of entangling all interaction dynamics, ReactiveGWM explicitly decouples player controls from NPC behaviors. Player actions are injected into the diffusion backbone via a lightweight additive bias, while high-level NPC responses (e.g., Offense, Control, Defense) are grounded through cross-attention modules.

我们引入了 ReactiveGWM，这是一种能够合成玩家与 NPC 之间动态交互的响应式游戏世界模型。ReactiveGWM 没有将所有交互动力学混为一谈，而是明确地将玩家控制与 NPC 行为解耦。玩家动作通过轻量级的加性偏置（additive bias）注入到扩散骨干网络中，而高层级的 NPC 响应（如进攻、控制、防御）则通过交叉注意力模块进行锚定。

Crucially, these modules learn a game-agnostic representation of interactive logic. This enables zero-shot strategy transfer: our learned modules can be plugged directly into off-the-shelf, unannotated world models of different games. This instantly unlocks steerable NPC interactions without any domain-specific retraining.

至关重要的是，这些模块学习到了一种与具体游戏无关的交互逻辑表示。这实现了零样本策略迁移：我们学习到的模块可以直接插入到不同游戏现成的、未经标注的世界模型中。无需任何特定领域的重新训练，即可立即解锁可操控的 NPC 交互功能。

Evaluated on two Street Fighter games, ReactiveGWM maintains fine-grain player controllability while achieving robust, prompt-aligned NPC strategy adherence, paving the way for scalable, strategy-rich interaction with the NPC.

在两款《街头霸王》游戏上的评估表明，ReactiveGWM 在保持精细玩家可控性的同时，实现了稳健且符合提示词要求的 NPC 策略执行，为实现可扩展、策略丰富的 NPC 交互铺平了道路。
