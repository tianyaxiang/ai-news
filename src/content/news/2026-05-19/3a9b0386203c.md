# Amazon’s new Alexa+ powered feature can generate podcast episodes
# 亚马逊推出由 Alexa+ 驱动的新功能，可按需生成播客节目

Amazon announced the latest update to Alexa+ on Monday: the ability to generate podcast episodes on demand. The new feature, called “Alexa Podcasts,” is rolling out to customers in the U.S. today.
亚马逊周一宣布了 Alexa+ 的最新更新：支持按需生成播客节目。这项名为“Alexa Podcasts”的新功能已于今日向美国用户推出。

Amazon describes the capability as a way to “turn any topic you’re curious about into a podcast episode, ready in minutes.” To use the feature, all users have to do is ask Alexa+ to create a podcast about a topic they’re interested in. Users don’t need to upload documents, write scripts, or plan anything ahead of time.
亚马逊将此功能描述为一种“将你好奇的任何主题转化为播客节目，并在几分钟内准备就绪”的方式。用户只需让 Alexa+ 创建一个他们感兴趣的主题播客即可使用该功能。用户无需上传文档、编写脚本或提前进行任何规划。

Instead, Alexa+ researches the request, gathers information, and generates a quick overview of what the episode will cover. From there, users can tweak things like the length, tone, and focus of the episode. Once finalized, Alexa+ uses AI-generated host voices to narrate the podcast.
相反，Alexa+ 会对请求进行研究、收集信息，并生成节目内容的快速概览。在此基础上，用户可以调整节目的时长、语调和重点。确定后，Alexa+ 会使用 AI 生成的主持人声音来讲述播客。

When the episode is ready, users get a notification through their Echo Show device and inside the Alexa app. Episodes are also saved in the app’s “Music” and “More” sections so they can be replayed later.
当节目准备就绪时，用户会通过 Echo Show 设备和 Alexa 应用程序收到通知。节目还会保存在应用程序的“音乐”和“更多”部分，以便日后回放。

The feature is another example of how Amazon is trying to turn Alexa+ into more than just a voice assistant. Instead of only answering questions or controlling smart home devices, Alexa+ is starting to act more like a personalized AI content creator.
这项功能是亚马逊试图将 Alexa+ 打造成不仅仅是语音助手的又一例证。Alexa+ 不再仅仅局限于回答问题或控制智能家居设备，而是开始扮演更像个性化 AI 内容创作者的角色。

At the same time, the launch is likely to spark some debate. AI-generated voices and automated content continue to raise questions around ethics, accuracy, and the future of traditional creators. There are also concerns about how reliable AI-generated podcasts will be, especially when covering news or complex topics.
与此同时，此次发布可能会引发一些争议。AI 生成的声音和自动化内容不断引发关于伦理、准确性以及传统创作者未来的质疑。人们还担心 AI 生成的播客在报道新闻或复杂主题时的可靠性。

Amazon emphasized its partnerships with major news organizations to improve content accuracy and reliability. The company says Alexa+ can access real-time information through agreements with outlets, including the Associated Press, Reuters, The Washington Post, Time, Forbes, Business Insider, Politico, USA Today, Condé Nast, Hearst, and Vox Media, alongside more than 200 local newspapers across the U.S.
亚马逊强调了其与各大新闻机构的合作，以提高内容的准确性和可靠性。该公司表示，通过与美联社、路透社、《华盛顿邮报》、《时代》周刊、《福布斯》、Business Insider、Politico、《今日美国》、康泰纳仕、赫斯特集团、Vox Media 以及全美 200 多家地方报纸达成协议，Alexa+ 可以获取实时信息。

Beyond podcasts, Amazon says it is exploring additional forms of personalized AI audio, including custom news briefings and content generated from users’ own documents and shared information.
除了播客之外，亚马逊表示正在探索其他形式的个性化 AI 音频，包括定制新闻简报以及根据用户自身文档和共享信息生成的内容。
