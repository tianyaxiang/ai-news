# These 11 Automatic Cat Feeders Were the Best We Tested in 2026
# 2026年我们测试出的11款最佳自动喂猫器

An automatic cat feeder just may change your life. No more screeching at 4 am. Plus, it can give some peace of mind while you're away from your fur baby. As seen with the rise of automatic litter boxes and pet tracking, technology has made it so we can closely monitor our pets without having to do a lot of the manual labor normally involved.
自动喂猫器可能会改变你的生活。你再也不用在凌晨4点被猫咪的尖叫声吵醒。此外，当你不在毛孩子身边时，它也能让你倍感安心。正如自动猫砂盆和宠物追踪器的兴起一样，科技让我们能够密切关注宠物，而无需进行大量繁琐的人工劳动。

Gravity-fed automatic dry-food dispensers were invented nearly a century ago, but today most automatic cat feeders utilize Wi-Fi and are connected to an app on your phone that allows you to make a schedule or feed on demand from afar, all while monitoring your pet’s eating habits. Newer models have taken this tech further, like the Petkit YumShare Dual-Hopper 2 Automatic Feeder With Camera ($170) which has a built-in, AI-enabled camera to analyze the remaining food in the bowl and will stop dispensing to prevent overfeeding.
重力式自动干粮喂食器早在近一个世纪前就被发明了，但如今大多数自动喂猫器都利用Wi-Fi连接手机App，让你能够远程制定喂食计划或按需喂食，同时还能监测宠物的饮食习惯。较新的型号将这项技术推向了更高水平，例如售价170美元的Petkit YumShare Dual-Hopper 2带摄像头自动喂食器，它内置了AI摄像头，可以分析碗中剩余的食物量，并停止出粮以防止过度喂食。

Automatic wet-food dispensers—our top pick in this category is the Petlibro Polar Smart Wet Feeder ($170)—are relatively new, often using ice packs or mini-refrigeration cooling systems to ensure the food stays as fresh as possible.
自动湿粮喂食器（我们在此类别中的首选是售价170美元的Petlibro Polar智能湿粮喂食器）相对较新，通常使用冰袋或微型制冷系统来确保食物尽可能保持新鲜。

Automatic feeders are also helpful for pet parents who want to monitor their pets’ health, have specialized schedules, or want to foster healthier eating habits. (A customizable automatic cat feeder can help with the endless gorging-vomiting cycle many cats struggle with.) Nearly all of the automatic cat feeders on this list are marketed toward cats, but some can be used with smaller dogs, and I’ve noted these below.
对于那些想要监测宠物健康、有特殊喂食时间表或希望培养更健康饮食习惯的宠物主人来说，自动喂食器也非常有用。（可定制的自动喂猫器可以帮助解决许多猫咪面临的“暴饮暴食-呕吐”循环问题。）此列表中的几乎所有自动喂猫器都是针对猫咪销售的，但有些也可以用于小型犬，我在下文中对此进行了标注。

### Best AI-Enabled Dual Feeder With Camera: Petkit YumShare Dual-Hopper 2
### 最佳AI赋能双仓带摄像头喂食器：Petkit YumShare Dual-Hopper 2

This model is an upgrade on one of my favorite models below, the Petkit YumShare Dual-Hopper Automatic Feeder With Camera, but with added AI features. It has a crystal-clear video feed, night vision, customizable meal calls, and a dual-hopper for two different types of food. Through the connected app, you can set feeding schedules or feed on demand from both tanks, use two-way audio, and watch the feeder from your phone. Unlike most other brands, the AI-enabled camera doesn't require a subscription and can identify up to 20 pets and log when they've eaten individually. The camera also uses AI to analyze the remaining food in the bowl and will stop dispensing to prevent overfeeding.
该型号是我下文中最喜欢的型号之一——Petkit YumShare双仓带摄像头自动喂食器的升级版，增加了AI功能。它拥有清晰的视频画面、夜视功能、可自定义的喂食语音，以及用于存放两种不同食物的双料仓。通过配套的App，你可以设置喂食计划或从两个料仓按需喂食，使用双向语音，并通过手机观看喂食情况。与其他大多数品牌不同，其AI摄像头无需订阅即可识别多达20只宠物，并分别记录它们的进食时间。摄像头还利用AI分析碗中剩余的食物，并停止出粮以防止过度喂食。

### Best Automatic Wet-Food Feeder: Petlibro Polar Wet Food Feeder
### 最佳自动湿粮喂食器：Petlibro Polar Wet Food Feeder

This updated Petlibro Polar Wet Food Feeder solves pretty much every gripe I had with other automatic feeders aimed at storing wet food—it’s essentially a mini-fridge guaranteed to keep food fresh, and it’s connected to an app where you can schedule and customize feedings and keep a feeding log. Food is given in three stainless steel wide, shallow bowls, which means less chance for whisker fatigue with wide bowls or feline acne with bacteria-harboring plastic. When it’s time to feed, the cover slides back from the hole and reveals the food beneath.
这款升级版的Petlibro Polar湿粮喂食器解决了我在其他湿粮自动喂食器上遇到的几乎所有痛点——它本质上是一个能确保食物新鲜的迷你冰箱，并连接到一个App，你可以在上面安排和自定义喂食时间，并保存喂食记录。食物通过三个宽而浅的不锈钢碗提供，这意味着宽碗减少了猫咪胡须疲劳的可能性，也避免了塑料材质滋生细菌导致的猫咪黑下巴问题。到了喂食时间，盖子会从孔洞滑开，露出下方的食物。

The refrigeration uses thermoelectric cooling and stays at 50 degrees Fahrenheit or below, and cooling is paused 30 minutes before scheduled feeding time to make sure it’s at the ideal temperature. The bowls are now numbered, so you can easily correlate feedings to specific bowls in-app. Through the app, you can alter schedules, manually feed on demand, and control how long it's open—there's even a sensor to make sure it doesn’t close while your kitty is feeding.
其制冷系统采用热电制冷，温度保持在华氏50度（约10摄氏度）或以下，并在预定喂食时间前30分钟暂停制冷，以确保食物处于理想温度。碗现在有了编号，因此你可以在App中轻松将喂食与特定碗对应起来。通过App，你可以更改时间表、手动按需喂食并控制盖子打开的时长——甚至还有一个传感器，确保在猫咪进食时盖子不会关闭。
