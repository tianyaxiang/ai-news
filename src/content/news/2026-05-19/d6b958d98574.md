# DiscoExplorer: An Open Interface for the Study of Multilingual Discourse Relations
# DiscoExplorer：用于研究多语言篇章关系的开放接口

**Abstract:** The relations connecting propositions in discourse such as cause (A because B) or concession (A although B) are a subject of intense interest in Computational Linguistics and Pragmatics, but challenging to study and compare across languages. 

**摘要：** 篇章中连接命题的关系，例如因果（A 因为 B）或让步（A 虽然 B），是计算语言学和语用学中备受关注的课题，但在跨语言研究和比较方面仍面临挑战。

Recent progress in standardizing discourse relation inventories across datasets offers the potential to facilitate such studies, but is hindered by the complexity of relevant data and the lack of easily accessible interfaces to analyze it. 

近期在跨数据集标准化篇章关系清单方面取得的进展，为促进此类研究提供了潜力，但受限于相关数据的复杂性以及缺乏易于使用的分析接口，研究进展依然受阻。

In this paper we present DiscoExplorer, a new open source web interface, capable of running on local computers, which we use to make datasets from the DISRPT Shared Task on discourse relation classification publicly available, covering 16 different languages. 

在本文中，我们介绍了 DiscoExplorer，这是一个全新的开源 Web 接口，可以在本地计算机上运行。我们利用该工具将 DISRPT 篇章关系分类共享任务中的数据集公开，涵盖了 16 种不同的语言。

We present the query language, search and visualization facilities for relations and signaling devices such as connectives, as well as some example studies.

我们展示了用于关系和连接词等信号装置的查询语言、搜索及可视化功能，并提供了一些研究案例。
