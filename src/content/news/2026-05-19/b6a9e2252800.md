# Quantization Undoes Alignment: Bias Emergence in Compressed LLMs Across Models and Precision Levels
# 量化会破坏对齐：压缩大语言模型在不同模型与精度下的偏见涌现

Large Language Models are routinely compressed via post-training quantization to reduce inference costs and memory footprint for cloud and edge deployment, yet the impact of this compression on model quality remains poorly understood.
大语言模型通常通过训练后量化进行压缩，以降低云端和边缘部署的推理成本及内存占用，但这种压缩对模型质量的影响目前尚不明确。

Existing studies typically compare only two conditions (full-precision vs. a single quantized variant), rely on aggregate bias metrics, and evaluate a single model family, making it impossible to distinguish gradual degradation from threshold-dependent safety failures.
现有研究通常仅比较两种情况（全精度与单一量化变体），依赖汇总的偏见指标，且仅评估单一模型系列，这使得研究人员无法区分渐进式的性能退化与阈值相关的安全性失效。

We conduct a controlled empirical study of three instruction-tuned models (Qwen2.5-7B, Mistral-7B, Phi-3.5-mini) at five precision levels (BF16 through 3-bit) on 12,148 BBQ bias benchmark items across 5 random seeds, totaling 911,100 inference records.
我们针对三种指令微调模型（Qwen2.5-7B、Mistral-7B、Phi-3.5-mini）进行了受控实证研究，涵盖五个精度级别（从 BF16 到 3-bit），并在 5 个随机种子下对 12,148 个 BBQ 偏见基准测试项进行了评估，总计产生 911,100 条推理记录。

Our results reveal that 3-bit quantization causes 6-21% of previously unbiased items to develop new stereotypical behaviors, following a clear dose-response pattern confirmed via logistic regression, while models' willingness to select "unknown" answers declines by 17.4%.
研究结果显示，3-bit 量化导致 6% 到 21% 原本无偏见的测试项产生了新的刻板印象行为，且通过逻辑回归证实了其遵循明确的剂量反应模式；同时，模型选择“未知”答案的意愿下降了 17.4%。

Crucially, these item-level changes are invisible to standard quality metrics: perplexity increases by less than 0.5% at 8-bit and under 3% at 4-bit across all three models, yet 2.5-5.6% of items already develop new biases at 4-bit.
至关重要的是，这些项目层面的变化在标准质量指标中是不可见的：在所有三个模型中，8-bit 下的困惑度（perplexity）增加不到 0.5%，4-bit 下增加不到 3%，但 4-bit 时已有 2.5% 到 5.6% 的测试项产生了新的偏见。

These findings demonstrate that aggregate evaluation metrics systematically miss fairness-critical degradation, underscoring the need for quality-aware compression protocols that explicitly test for bias emergence before deployment.
这些发现表明，汇总评估指标系统性地忽略了对公平性至关重要的性能退化，这强调了建立“质量感知”压缩协议的必要性，即在部署前必须明确测试偏见的涌现情况。
