# Walmart launches new budget-friendly Android tablets starting at $97
# 沃尔玛推出全新经济型安卓平板电脑，起售价 97 美元

Walmart’s Onn brand just launched a whole line of budget-friendly Android tablets that, all together, cost less in total than a single iPad Pro. As spotted by 9to5Google, the six new tablets ship with Android 16 and all but one cost less than $200.
沃尔玛旗下的 Onn 品牌刚刚推出了一系列经济型安卓平板电脑，所有这些平板电脑的总价加起来甚至比一台 iPad Pro 还要便宜。据 9to5Google 报道，这六款新平板电脑均预装 Android 16 系统，除一款外，其余机型的售价均低于 200 美元。

The little Onn Core 7 is the most affordable of the bunch at just $97, which gets you a 7-inch 1040 x 600 IPS LCD display, 4GB of RAM, 64GB of storage that’s expandable with a microSD card, and, according to Walmart, up to 10 hours of battery life.
小巧的 Onn Core 7 是该系列中最实惠的一款，仅售 97 美元。它配备了 7 英寸 1040 x 600 分辨率的 IPS LCD 显示屏、4GB 运行内存、64GB 存储空间（可通过 microSD 卡扩展），据沃尔玛称，其电池续航时间可达 10 小时。

A step above that are two larger Core tablets. The Core 8.1 costs $138 and includes an 8.1-inch 1524 x 1000 IPS LCD display, 6GB of RAM, 64GB of storage (also expandable with microSD), and up to 15 hours of battery life. The Core 11 for $167 is a bit bigger, similar to a base iPad, featuring an 11-inch 1840 x 1280 IPS LCD display, 6GB of RAM, 128GB of storage that’s expandable with a microSD slot, and up to 17 hours of battery life. There are also two affordable kids’ tablets available in 8-inch and 11-inch sizes for $118 and $136, respectively.
在此之上还有两款更大的 Core 平板电脑。Core 8.1 售价 138 美元，配备 8.1 英寸 1524 x 1000 分辨率的 IPS LCD 显示屏、6GB 运行内存、64GB 存储空间（同样支持 microSD 扩展），续航时间长达 15 小时。售价 167 美元的 Core 11 尺寸稍大，与基础款 iPad 相当，配备 11 英寸 1840 x 1280 分辨率的 IPS LCD 显示屏、6GB 运行内存、128GB 存储空间（支持 microSD 扩展），续航时间长达 17 小时。此外，还有两款经济型儿童平板电脑，分别为 8 英寸和 11 英寸，售价分别为 118 美元和 136 美元。

The priciest of the new tablets is the Onn Pro, which costs $288 and includes a 13.2-inch 2400 x 1600 IPS LCD display, 8GB of RAM, 256GB of storage, and an unnamed 2.6GHz MediaTek processor. Unlike the iPad Pro it’s imitating, it also includes a folio case and a stylus.
该系列中最昂贵的是 Onn Pro，售价 288 美元，配备 13.2 英寸 2400 x 1600 分辨率的 IPS LCD 显示屏、8GB 运行内存、256GB 存储空间以及一款未具名的 2.6GHz 联发科处理器。与它所模仿的 iPad Pro 不同，它还附赠了保护套和手写笔。
