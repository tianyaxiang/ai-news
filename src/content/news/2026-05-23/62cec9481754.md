# Look-Closer-Then-Diagnose: Confidence-Aware Ultrasound VQA via Active Zooming
# 深入观察再诊断：基于主动缩放的置信度感知超声视觉问答

**Abstract:** Vision-Language Models (VLMs) have significantly advanced medical visual question answering, yet their performance in ultrasound remains suboptimal. In clinical practice, sonographers explicitly focus on lesion regions to formulate reports, though diagnostic interpretations sometimes vary due to inherent subjectivity. 

**摘要：** 视觉语言模型（VLMs）在医学视觉问答领域取得了显著进展，但在超声影像中的表现仍不理想。在临床实践中，超声医师通常会明确聚焦于病灶区域以撰写报告，尽管由于固有的主观性，诊断解读有时会存在差异。

However, existing VLMs are not explicitly structured to interactively zoom into lesions prior to diagnosis; moreover, they typically treat annotations as unbiased ground truths, failing to account for their inherent subjectivity and ambiguity. 

然而，现有的视觉语言模型在诊断前并未被明确设计为能够交互式地放大病灶；此外，它们通常将标注视为无偏见的真值，未能考虑到标注本身固有的主观性和模糊性。

In this paper, we propose a framework specifically designed to consider the sonographer's cognitive workflow. We first introduce a structured Zoom-then-Diagnose paradigm, which replicates the interactive search process to enable lesion-focused reasoning. 

在本文中，我们提出了一个专门考虑超声医师认知工作流程的框架。我们首先引入了一种结构化的“先缩放、后诊断”（Zoom-then-Diagnose）范式，该范式复制了交互式搜索过程，从而实现了以病灶为中心的推理。

Furthermore, within the Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) framework, we introduce an uncertainty-aware reward derived from stochastic group-wise rollouts to estimate prediction consistency as a proxy for model confidence. Together, these two components encourage the model to reinforce accurate predictions on clear cases while remaining cautious under ambiguity. 

此外，在组相对策略优化（GRPO）框架内，我们引入了一种源自随机组级展开（stochastic group-wise rollouts）的不确定性感知奖励，用以评估预测一致性，并将其作为模型置信度的代理指标。这两个组件共同促使模型在面对清晰案例时强化准确预测，而在面对模糊情况时保持谨慎。

Experiments across liver, breast, and thyroid datasets show that our framework improves lesion localization by 39.3%, demonstrating that our model has learned the ability to actively look closer and diagnose.

在肝脏、乳腺和甲状腺数据集上的实验表明，我们的框架将病灶定位能力提高了 39.3%，证明了我们的模型已经学会了主动“深入观察并进行诊断”的能力。
