# Teaching Language Models to Forecast Research Success Through Comparative Idea Evaluation
# 教导语言模型通过比较性构思评估来预测研究成功率

**Abstract:** As language models accelerate scientific research by automating hypothesis generation and implementation, a new bottleneck emerges: evaluating and filtering hundreds of AI-generated ideas without exhaustive experimentation. We ask whether LMs can learn to forecast the empirical success of research ideas before any experiments are run.

**摘要：** 随着语言模型通过自动化假设生成和实施来加速科学研究，一个新的瓶颈随之出现：如何在不进行详尽实验的情况下，评估并筛选数百个由人工智能生成的构思。我们探讨了语言模型（LM）是否可以在进行任何实验之前，学习预测研究构思的实证成功率。

We study comparative empirical forecasting: given a benchmark-specific research goal and two candidate ideas, predict which will achieve better benchmark performance. We construct a dataset of 11,488 idea pairs grounded in objective outcomes from PapersWithCode. While off-the-shelf 8B-parameter models struggle (30% acc.), SFT dramatically boosts performance to 77.1%, outperforming GPT-5 (61.1%).

我们研究了比较性实证预测：给定一个特定基准的研究目标和两个候选构思，预测哪一个能获得更好的基准性能。我们构建了一个包含 11,488 个构思对的数据集，这些构思均基于 PapersWithCode 的客观结果。虽然现成的 8B 参数模型表现不佳（准确率仅为 30%），但经过监督微调（SFT）后，性能显著提升至 77.1%，超过了 GPT-5（61.1%）。

By framing evaluation as a reasoning task via Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR), we train models to discover latent reasoning paths, achieving 71.35% acc. with interpretable justifications. Through additional ablations and out-of-distribution tests, we show robustness to surface-level heuristics and transfer to both a cross-domain time-split test set and an independently constructed test set.

通过利用带有可验证奖励的强化学习（RLVR）将评估过程构建为推理任务，我们训练模型去发现潜在的推理路径，从而在提供可解释依据的同时达到了 71.35% 的准确率。通过额外的消融实验和分布外测试，我们证明了该模型对表面启发式方法的鲁棒性，并验证了其在跨领域时间分割测试集和独立构建测试集上的迁移能力。

Our results demonstrate that compute-efficient small language models can serve as effective, objective verifiers, offering a scalable path for autonomous scientific discovery.

我们的研究结果表明，计算高效的小型语言模型可以作为有效且客观的验证器，为自主科学发现提供了一条可扩展的路径。
