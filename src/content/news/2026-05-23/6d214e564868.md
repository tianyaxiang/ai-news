# Double descent for least-squares interpolation on contaminated data: A simulation study
# 受污染数据下最小二乘插值的双重下降：一项模拟研究

Overparametrized models can exhibit an excellent generalization performance, although they should be prone to overfitting according to classical statistical theory. The discovery of the "double descent", indicating that the generalization error decreases after a certain model complexity has been reached, opened a new line of research.

尽管根据经典统计理论，过参数化模型容易出现过拟合，但它们却能表现出极佳的泛化性能。“双重下降”（double descent）现象的发现，即模型在达到一定复杂度后泛化误差反而会下降，开启了一个全新的研究方向。

Robust statistics considers statistical estimation on contaminated data, which, due to assumptions that do not hold on real data, let data points appear as outliers w.r.t. the assumed "ideal" distribution, potentially severely distorting any classical estimator.

稳健统计学（Robust statistics）研究的是受污染数据上的统计估计问题。由于某些假设在真实数据上并不成立，导致数据点相对于假设的“理想”分布表现为异常值，这可能会严重扭曲任何经典估计器。

We address the question whether a double descent phenomenon can be observed in a linear regression setting with contaminated training data. We compare the performance of the highly non-robust least-squares interpolation estimator with several robust alternatives.

我们探讨了在受污染的训练数据下，线性回归设置中是否能观察到双重下降现象。我们将非稳健性极强的最小二乘插值估计器与几种稳健的替代方案进行了性能对比。

It turns out that large overparametrization indeed allows for a double descent phenomenon, resulting in a very good generalization performance of the least-squares interpolator, surpassing that of the robust alternatives.

研究结果表明，大规模的过参数化确实能够产生双重下降现象，使得最小二乘插值器获得非常好的泛化性能，甚至超过了那些稳健的替代方案。
