# The 13 Best Fans to Buy Before It Gets Hot Again (2026)
# 2026年夏季来临前，最值得购买的13款风扇

If you think you don't need a new fan this year, you might want to think again. Even if the dusty old pedestal fan you've been carting around for 25 years is still kicking, fans these days can do a lot more than just blow air. They're smarter, quieter, and far more versatile than they've ever been, with new tech features that would have seemed like science fiction even five years ago.
如果你认为今年不需要买新风扇，那可能得再考虑一下。即便你那台用了25年、满是灰尘的老式落地扇还能转动，如今的风扇功能早已远不止吹风那么简单。它们变得更智能、更安静、功能也更全面，许多新技术在五年前简直就像科幻小说一样。

The Dreo TurboPoly 707S, for instance, has radar that can track where someone is in the room and redirect airflow as they move about, while the company's 508S model can provide mood lighting in more than 1,000 color shades. There are bladeless fans, fans just for fitness purposes, and ones that can mist or cool your skin with a cryo-inspired metal plate.
例如，Dreo TurboPoly 707S 配备了雷达，可以追踪房间内人员的位置并随其移动调整风向；而该公司的 508S 型号则能提供超过1000种颜色的氛围灯。此外，市面上还有无叶风扇、健身专用风扇，以及能通过冷感金属板喷雾或冷却皮肤的风扇。

I've been covering fans at WIRED for almost two years, and I'm always on the lookout for the latest and most interesting fan technology. If you know of a cool fan you want me to check out, let me know in the comments.
我在《连线》(WIRED) 报道风扇产品已有近两年时间，我一直在关注最新、最有趣的风扇技术。如果你知道什么酷炫的风扇想让我测评，欢迎在评论区告诉我。

### Best Air Circulator: Vornado Vora 8 Whole Room Air Circulator
### 最佳空气循环扇：Vornado Vora 8 全屋空气循环扇

Vornado's bare-bones 660 air circulator was my top pick in this category for the past two years. It could sit on either a table or on the floor, ensuring bulk air movement through Vornado's proprietary Vortex Action. There was no remote, no oscillation, and three speeds activated by buttons on the top. It got the job done with a minimum of fuss, but darn if it wouldn't have been nice to have a remote.
Vornado 基础款 660 空气循环扇是我过去两年在该类别中的首选。它可以放在桌上或地上，通过 Vornado 专利的“涡旋技术”(Vortex Action) 确保大量空气流动。它没有遥控器，不支持摆头，只有顶部按钮控制的三档风速。它能简单高效地完成任务，但如果能有个遥控器就更好了。

From my mouth to Vornado's ear, apparently, as earlier this year the brand released the Vora 8, which is basically an upgraded version of the 660. It's stronger (1,100 ft/m max wind speed to the 660's 1,083 ft/m), with 10 speeds, three airflow modes, an aromatherapy pad for the center button, and—blessedly—a remote. It also oscillates vertically as well as horizontally, and it's got a backlit touch display on the base.
看来 Vornado 听到了我的心声，今年早些时候该品牌发布了 Vora 8，这基本上是 660 的升级版。它风力更强（最大风速 1,100 英尺/分钟，而 660 为 1,083 英尺/分钟），拥有 10 档风速、三种送风模式、中心按钮处的香薰垫，以及——谢天谢地——终于有了遥控器。它还支持垂直和水平摆头，底座上配有背光触摸显示屏。

### Best Ultra-Quiet Fan for Bedrooms: Dreo TurboPoly Fan 508S
### 卧室最佳超静音风扇：Dreo TurboPoly 508S

Dreo’s flagship PolyFan 704S has been the brand’s most popular pedestal fan for years, but I didn't have the best luck with my test unit. Plus, I found the fan to be heavy, unwieldy, and difficult to move around compared to competing models. Thankfully, last year saw the release of Dreo's improvement on the PolyFan, the TurboPoly. It's not only slightly smaller and lighter than the original PolyFan, but it's also billed as Dreo's quietest fan of all time.
Dreo 的旗舰款 PolyFan 704S 多年来一直是该品牌最受欢迎的落地扇，但我的测试机表现一般。此外，与竞品相比，我觉得这款风扇笨重且难以移动。好在去年 Dreo 推出了 PolyFan 的改进版——TurboPoly。它不仅比原版 PolyFan 更小、更轻，还被誉为 Dreo 史上最安静的风扇。

It comes in white, gold, or silver, sports nine speeds, can oscillate vertically or horizontally, and has smart capabilities (Google Assistant and Amazon's Alexa). It's controllable through the remote, the button on the fan itself, or via the Dreo app, which also includes a 12-hour timer and the ability to set a schedule. It's also got a cool RGB ring light on the back.
它有白色、金色和银色可选，具备 9 档风速，支持垂直或水平摆头，并拥有智能功能（支持 Google Assistant 和 Amazon Alexa）。你可以通过遥控器、机身按钮或 Dreo App 进行控制，App 还包含 12 小时定时和日程设置功能。机身背面还有一个很酷的 RGB 环形灯。

### Best Space-Saving Fan for Apartments: Dreo Nomad One (307) Tower Fan
### 公寓最佳省空间风扇：Dreo Nomad One (307) 塔扇

Though tower fans aren't always easy to clean and are prone to tipping over if you have active children or pets, they are less top-heavy than pedestal fans, and the slim profile and small footprint make them easy to store when you're short on space. In the tower fan category, Dreo's 36-inch Nomad One (otherwise known as the 307) is arguably the best bang for your buck, offering superior build quality and performance for this price point.
虽然塔扇通常不太容易清洁，且如果家里有活泼的孩子或宠物容易倾倒，但它们比落地扇重心更稳，且纤细的外形和较小的占地面积使其在空间有限时易于收纳。在塔扇类别中，Dreo 36 英寸的 Nomad One（即 307 型号）可以说是性价比之选，在该价位提供了出色的制造质量和性能。
