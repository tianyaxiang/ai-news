### Best Cordless Stick Vacuum: Shark PowerDetect Cordless Stick Vacuum
### 最佳无线手持吸尘器：Shark PowerDetect 无线手持吸尘器

In my latest round of cordless vacuum testing, Shark's PowerDetect has risen to the top with a great mix of features at an accessible price. It has your classic range of three power levels and comes with only two accessories, but the stick of the vacuum actually has a bendable point, so you can click to bend it and easily vacuum underneath surfaces without getting down on the floor to do it. It has impressive power levels and did the best job vacuuming up debris like sand and cereal on carpet, and it did a solid job getting dust out of carpet-covered stairs.

在我最近一轮的无线吸尘器测试中，Shark PowerDetect 凭借出色的功能组合和亲民的价格脱颖而出。它具备经典的三档吸力调节，虽然只配备了两个附件，但其吸尘杆设计有一个可弯曲的关节，只需轻轻一按即可弯折，让你无需趴在地上就能轻松清理家具底部。它的吸力表现令人印象深刻，在清理地毯上的沙子和麦片碎屑方面表现最佳，在清理铺设地毯的楼梯时也表现出色。

I think this is especially a great cordless stick vacuum if you have a mix of hard floors, rugs, and carpet in your home. If your home is primarily carpet, I'd recommend a Dyson for a deeper clean, but otherwise this Shark model will do fantastic. There's a version with a self-emptying docking station, too.

如果你家里既有硬地板，又有地毯和垫子，我认为这是一款非常棒的无线手持吸尘器。如果你的家中以地毯为主，我会推荐戴森以获得更深层的清洁效果，但除此之外，这款 Shark 型号的表现绝对出色。此外，它还有一个带自动集尘底座的版本。

### Best Dyson Stick Vacuum: Dyson V15 Detect
### 最佳戴森手持吸尘器：Dyson V15 Detect

There are a lot of Dyson vacuum models, but the V15 Detect is the best in Dyson's lineup with a reasonable price—for a Dyson, that is—and a good mix of features. Time and time again, though, the power of a Dyson vacuum can't be beat, especially with the V15 Detect's power and attachments.

戴森的吸尘器型号繁多，但 V15 Detect 是戴森产品线中表现最好的一款，价格也相对合理（当然，这是指在戴森的产品范围内），且功能组合非常均衡。事实证明，戴森吸尘器的动力始终难以被超越，尤其是 V15 Detect 的强劲吸力和丰富的附件。

It gets a deeper clean on my carpet than cheaper vacuums, and the sensor can tell you how many particles it's picking up if you want to dig into the data. There's a nice range of attachments, including the Fluffy Optic cleaner head that has a green laser to find dust and debris on hard floors. It has a trigger-style power button, so you'll have to hold it down the entire time you use it. That can be annoying if you know you'll be using it on a lot of carpet or hard floors, but it's handy for target-cleaning areas like staircases.

它在地毯上的清洁深度远超廉价吸尘器，如果你喜欢研究数据，它的传感器甚至能告诉你吸入了多少颗粒物。它配备了一系列实用的附件，包括带有绿色激光的 Fluffy Optic 清洁头，可以照亮硬地板上的灰尘和碎屑。它采用扳机式电源开关，这意味着使用时必须全程按住。如果你需要长时间清理大面积地毯或硬地板，这可能会有些麻烦，但对于楼梯等局部区域的清洁来说，这种设计非常方便。

### Best Affordable Cordless Vacuum: Bissell PowerClean Cordless Stick Vacuum
### 最佳平价无线吸尘器：Bissell PowerClean 无线手持吸尘器

If you're looking for a great affordable stick vacuum, my favorites have come from Bissell. You'll get everything you want in a good cordless: solid power, a HEPA-sealed filter, and a couple attachments. I've loved the Bissell's FurFinder ($200) for my house, but the Bissell PowerClean is an even cheaper version that skips the FurFinder attachment and leaves a few lights off of the headlight.

如果你正在寻找一款性价比极高的手持吸尘器，我最推荐 Bissell 的产品。它具备了一款优秀无线吸尘器所需的一切：强劲的动力、HEPA 密封过滤系统以及几个实用的附件。我个人很喜欢 Bissell 的 FurFinder（200美元），但 Bissell PowerClean 是一个更便宜的版本，它去掉了 FurFinder 附件，并减少了前置照明灯的数量。

WIRED reviewer Kat Merck found the headlight plenty bright enough to spot litter and dust on her floor. It has a 40-minute run time that she found to be just enough to clean all rugs, bare floors, and upholstery in a 3,000-square-foot house. It feels heavier than she expected, but it's a great cordless vacuum for the price, especially since it has a HEPA sealed allergen system to keep allergens from escaping the vacuum.

WIRED 的评测员 Kat Merck 发现，其前置灯的亮度足以看清地板上的垃圾和灰尘。它拥有 40 分钟的续航时间，据她测试，这足以清洁一栋 3000 平方英尺房屋内的所有地毯、硬地板和软装家具。虽然手感比预想中重，但考虑到价格，这是一款非常出色的无线吸尘器，尤其是它配备了 HEPA 密封过敏原系统，能有效防止过敏原从吸尘器中逸出。
