# Lost in Translation: How AI Exposes the Rift Between Law and Logic
# 迷失在翻译中：人工智能如何揭示法律与逻辑之间的鸿沟

The tension between Legal and IT has always been frustrating but AI is about to make it worse, at scale. The answer is observable compliance: encoding legal intent directly into architecture.
法律部门与IT部门之间的紧张关系一直令人沮丧，而人工智能即将大规模加剧这一矛盾。解决之道在于“可观测合规”（observable compliance）：将法律意图直接编码到架构中。

Over the last few years, I have sat in multiple meetings with IT and legal teams where the intent and motivation is visibly misaligned. It often feels like two completely different worlds trying to reach agreement under pressure. As one colleague once described it to me: “Legal writes for humans, IT builds for machines.”
在过去的几年里，我参加过多次IT团队与法律团队的会议，双方的意图和动机明显不一致。这常常让人感觉像是两个完全不同的世界在压力下试图达成共识。正如一位同事曾对我描述的那样：“法律是为人类编写的，而IT是为机器构建的。”

Law allows interpretation, context, and mitigation, while IT depends on logic and deterministic workflows. The result is that even small ambiguities can lead to weeks of wasted effort building solutions that were never legally viable in the first place.
法律允许解释、语境和缓解措施，而IT则依赖于逻辑和确定性的工作流。结果是，即使是微小的歧义也可能导致数周的努力付诸东流，构建出从一开始就不具备法律可行性的解决方案。

The problem, solution and predicted outcomes I outline here is aimed at all three foundations: the business leaders, IT professionals (more specifically in Data & AI) as well as legal professionals trying to navigate the growing disconnect in implementing compliant data solutions.
我在此概述的问题、解决方案和预期结果，旨在面向三个基础群体：企业领导者、IT专业人士（特别是数据与人工智能领域），以及试图在实施合规数据解决方案时应对日益严重的脱节问题的法律专业人士。

Rather than treating the problem as a communication issue alone, it proposes a practical framework for translating legal intent into machine-readable and architecture-aware controls that can scale with modern data and AI ecosystems. For years this tension was manageable, but it has been exposed since the introduction of GDPR rules in 2016, and the influx of AI requests from all corners of the business is about to expose the gap at scale.
与其将此问题仅仅视为沟通问题，本文提出了一种实用的框架，旨在将法律意图转化为机器可读且具备架构感知能力的控制措施，从而适应现代数据和人工智能生态系统的扩展。多年来，这种紧张关系尚可控，但自2016年GDPR规则出台以来，这种矛盾已逐渐暴露，而来自企业各个角落的人工智能需求浪潮，即将大规模地揭开这一鸿沟。

### Setting the scene
### 背景设定

**Business: outcomes over everything**
**业务部门：结果至上**

The business prioritises growth, revenue, optimisation and competitive advantage. Their language is KPIs, margins and performance. Compliance, from their perspective, is a constraint to navigate and not a mission in itself. They’re not trying to be reckless, they simply operate in a world measured by results.
业务部门优先考虑增长、收入、优化和竞争优势。他们的语言是KPI、利润率和绩效。从他们的角度来看，合规性是一种需要应对的约束，而不是一项使命本身。他们并非试图鲁莽行事，只是在一个以结果为衡量标准的世界中运作。

**Legal: risk, mitigation and defensibility**
**法律部门：风险、缓解与可辩护性**

Legal does not operate in absolutes. Zero risk is rarely demanded or realistic, they operate in acceptable risk. They do want any risk taken to be defensible, so if they are challenged on something, they can demonstrate they acted responsibly.
法律部门并不追求绝对。零风险既不被要求也不现实，他们是在“可接受的风险”范围内运作。他们确实希望所承担的任何风险都是可辩护的，这样如果受到质疑，他们可以证明自己是负责任地行事。

Legislation is written in narrative form and is intentionally principle-based. It leaves room for interpretation and legal professionals are trained to interpret that narrative. They are not trained to design database schemas, configure access controls or define system-level enforcement mechanisms.
法律条文以叙述形式编写，且刻意基于原则。它留下了诠释空间，而法律专业人士受过解读这种叙述的训练。他们并未受过设计数据库模式、配置访问控制或定义系统级执行机制的训练。

**IT: Deterministic Controls**
**IT部门：确定性控制**

IT needs specificity and cannot work in narratives. “Reasonable safeguards” cannot be implemented. When legal says, “It depends,” IT thinks, “I cannot build this.”
IT部门需要具体性，无法在叙述中工作。“合理的保障措施”是无法直接实施的。当法律部门说“视情况而定”时，IT部门想的是“我无法构建这个”。

So, the business wants to create value that leads to profitability, while Legal’s motivation is to ensure compliance. Meanwhile, IT must support both by building reliable systems that deliver value while remaining compliant. This creates an uneven compliance accountability burden and, as a result, makes discussions and agreements between these departments painstakingly slow.
因此，业务部门希望创造价值以实现盈利，而法律部门的动机是确保合规。与此同时，IT部门必须通过构建既能交付价值又能保持合规的可靠系统来支持双方。这造成了不均衡的合规问责负担，导致这些部门之间的讨论和达成协议的过程极其缓慢。

### How AI widens the gap
### 人工智能如何扩大鸿沟

Previously this was manageable because the pace of data usage was slower. Manual oversight was still feasible and legal teams could review major initiatives one-by-one. That era will end when the volume and velocity of AI-driven data usage overwhelms traditional compliance models.
此前这种情况尚可控，因为数据使用的节奏较慢。人工监督仍然可行，法律团队可以逐一审查重大举措。当人工智能驱动的数据使用量和速度压倒传统合规模型时，那个时代将宣告终结。

Data is no longer analysed linearly, it is continuously processed, combined, enriched, repurposed and modelled. Autonomous agents can even trigger workflows, generate insights and make decisions without human review. At such scale, traditional legal oversight breaks down.
数据不再是线性分析的，而是被持续处理、组合、丰富、重新利用和建模。自主智能体甚至可以在没有人工审查的情况下触发工作流、生成洞察并做出决策。在如此规模下，传统的法律监督机制将失效。

### From legal text to architecture-aware compliance
### 从法律文本到架构感知合规

Legal intent is rarely encoded in a way that systems can validate. Instead, compliance lives in PDFs, policies, meeting minutes and emails. That works to a point, but fails at scale. What is missing is a shared interface in a structured, machine-readable form, using concepts like structured metadata, policy-as-code and data contracts to add translation layers.
法律意图很少以系统可以验证的方式进行编码。相反，合规性存在于PDF、政策、会议纪要和电子邮件中。这在一定程度上有效，但在大规模应用时会失效。缺失的是一个结构化、机器可读的共享接口，利用结构化元数据、代码即策略（policy-as-code）和数据契约等概念来增加翻译层。

We need to move from theoretical compliance to observable compliance.
我们需要从理论合规转向可观测合规。

### The proposal
### 提案

**The Core Idea: Eradicate human error in handoffs between Legal and IT**
**核心理念：消除法律与IT部门交接过程中的人为错误**

Unstructured human conversation is a poor mechanism for transferring precise, technical, legally-consequential decisions between people who do not share a common language. The goal is not to eliminate human judgment, but to replace the parts of the process where human interaction adds friction and error rather than value.
非结构化的人类对话对于在不共享共同语言的人员之间传递精确、技术性、具有法律后果的决策来说，是一种糟糕的机制。目标不是消除人类判断，而是取代流程中那些人类互动只会增加摩擦和错误而非价值的部分。

我提出一个简单的组织原则：用结构化的、人工智能辅助的流程取代非结构化的人工交接。传统合规的失败点不在于有人类参与，而在于动机不同的人被要求达成精确的……
