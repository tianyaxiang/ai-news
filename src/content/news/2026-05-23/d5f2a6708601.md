# The Attribution Impossibility: No Feature Ranking Is Faithful, Stable, and Complete Under Collinearity
# 归因的不可能性：在共线性条件下，不存在既忠实、稳定又完备的特征排序方法

**Abstract:** No feature ranking can be simultaneously faithful, stable, and complete when features are collinear. For collinear pairs, ranking reduces to a coin flip. We prove this impossibility, quantify it for four model classes, resolve it via ensemble averaging (DASH), and machine-verify it with 305 Lean 4 theorems.

**摘要：** 当特征存在共线性时，没有任何特征排序方法能够同时做到忠实（faithful）、稳定（stable）和完备（complete）。对于共线性特征对，排序结果等同于抛硬币。我们证明了这一不可能性，针对四类模型进行了量化分析，通过集成平均法（DASH）解决了该问题，并利用 305 条 Lean 4 定理对结论进行了机器验证。

We characterize the complete attribution design space: exactly two families of methods exist -- faithful-complete methods (unstable, with rankings that flip up to 50% of the time) and ensemble methods like DASH (stable, reporting ties for symmetric features) -- and no method lies outside this dichotomy. The impossibility is quantitative: the attribution ratio diverges as 1/(1-rho^2) for gradient boosting, is infinite for Lasso, and converges for random forests.

我们刻画了完整的归因设计空间：目前仅存在两类方法——忠实-完备方法（不稳定，排序结果有高达 50% 的翻转概率）和集成方法（如 DASH，稳定，对对称特征报告平局）——没有任何方法能跳出这一二分法。这种不可能性是可量化的：对于梯度提升模型，归因比率随 1/(1-rho^2) 发散；对于 Lasso 回归，该比率为无穷大；而对于随机森林，该比率则趋于收敛。

DASH (Diversified Aggregation of SHAP) is provably Pareto-optimal among unbiased aggregations, achieving the Cramer-Rao variance bound with a tight ensemble size formula. In a survey of 77 public datasets, 68% exhibit attribution instability. Switching to conditional SHAP does not escape the impossibility when features have equal causal effects.

DASH（SHAP 的多样化聚合）在无偏聚合方法中被证明是帕累托最优的，它通过精确的集成规模公式达到了 Cramer-Rao 方差界。在对 77 个公开数据集的调查中，68% 的数据集表现出归因不稳定性。当特征具有相同的因果效应时，切换到条件 SHAP（conditional SHAP）也无法规避这一不可能性。

The framework includes practical diagnostics -- a Z-test workflow and single-model screening tool -- and has direct consequences for fairness auditing: SHAP-based proxy discrimination audits are provably unreliable under collinearity. The design space theorem, diagnostics, and impossibility are mechanically verified in Lean 4 (305 theorems from 16 axioms, 0 sorry) -- to our knowledge, the first formally verified impossibility in explainable AI.

该框架包含实用的诊断工具——Z 检验工作流和单模型筛选工具——并对公平性审计具有直接影响：在共线性条件下，基于 SHAP 的代理歧视审计被证明是不可靠的。设计空间定理、诊断方法和不可能性结论均已在 Lean 4 中通过机器验证（由 16 条公理推导出的 305 条定理，无任何遗漏），据我们所知，这是可解释人工智能（XAI）领域首个经过形式化验证的不可能性结论。
