They say that Memorial Day marks the unofficial start of summer. I have my doubts given the less-than-balmy weather in many parts of the US this weekend, though that doesn’t mean it’s not an appropriate time to pick up some summer essentials — including a portable speaker / smart lamp like Govee’s Table Lamp 2 Pro. Right now, it’s matching its all-time low of $134.99 ($45 off) at Amazon, Best Buy, and Govee’s online storefront ahead of the holiday on May 25th.

人们常说阵亡将士纪念日（Memorial Day）标志着夏季的非正式开始。考虑到本周末美国许多地区天气并不算温暖，我对此持保留意见，但这并不妨碍现在是入手一些夏季必备品的好时机——比如像 Govee Table Lamp 2 Pro 这样的便携式音箱/智能台灯。目前，在 5 月 25 日假期到来之前，它在亚马逊、百思买（Best Buy）和 Govee 在线商店的售价已降至 134.99 美元（优惠 45 美元），追平了历史最低价。

Govee currently has several color-changing table lamps in its stable, with the Lamp 2 Pro being the most capable of the bunch. It essentially combines a color-changing smart lamp with a capable JBL Bluetooth speaker, resulting in a device that can blanket your home with glitzy RGB lighting effects while playing your favorite T-Swift or Bad Bunny cuts (or whatever the kids are into these days). It’s not waterproof, sadly, but it is relatively portable at just 5 pounds; it also features a 5,200mAh battery, which delivers around 4.5 hours of runtime per charge.

Govee 目前拥有多款变色台灯，其中 Lamp 2 Pro 是功能最强大的一款。它本质上将变色智能灯与性能出色的 JBL 蓝牙音箱结合在一起，让你的房间既能沉浸在炫目的 RGB 灯光效果中，又能播放你最爱的 Taylor Swift 或 Bad Bunny 的热门单曲（或者当下年轻人喜欢的任何音乐）。遗憾的是它并不防水，但仅 5 磅的重量使其相对便携；它还配备了 5,200mAh 电池，单次充电可提供约 4.5 小时的续航时间。

For its 360-degree lighting array, the Lamp 2 Pro uses 210 LED beads, each individually controllable and capable of displaying both color and up to 600 lumens of tunable white light. There are a staggering 100 presets to choose from — all of which are strikingly vivid, 16 of which sync with music — and you can quickly swap between them using the Govee app over Wi-Fi or Bluetooth (it also works with Amazon Alexa and Google Home). The Matter-equipped lamp also offers a slew of ambient sounds, from chirping birds and waves to a crackling fireplace, just in case you’d rather wind down than rave at the end of the night.

在 360 度照明阵列方面，Lamp 2 Pro 使用了 210 颗 LED 灯珠，每一颗都可以独立控制，既能显示色彩，也能提供高达 600 流明的可调白光。它拥有多达 100 种预设模式可供选择，所有模式都非常生动，其中 16 种可以与音乐同步。你可以通过 Wi-Fi 或蓝牙使用 Govee App 快速切换（它也支持 Amazon Alexa 和 Google Home）。这款支持 Matter 协议的台灯还提供了多种环境音效，从鸟鸣、海浪到噼啪作响的壁炉声，以备你在深夜不想狂欢而只想放松时使用。

As for the integrated JBL speaker, it’s good enough. It produces clear highs and decent mids, making it well-suited for listening to the radio or your favorite podcast. The bass response is pretty limited, though it does get loud enough to use for your next backyard shindig.

至于内置的 JBL 音箱，表现中规中矩。它能产生清晰的高音和不错的重音，非常适合收听广播或你最喜欢的播客。低音表现相当有限，但音量足够大，足以应付你的下一次后院聚会。
