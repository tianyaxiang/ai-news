# Ablate-to-Validate: Are Vision-Language Models Really Using Continuous Thought Tokens?
# 消融以验证：视觉语言模型真的在使用连续思维标记吗？

**Abstract:** Vision-language models (VLMs) are increasingly augmented with continuous or latent non-textual tokens intended to support "visual thinking." Despite improved task accuracy, this alone does not show that models actually use these tokens for reasoning -- gains may arise from confounds such as added context length, special-token anchoring, or training-time regularization.

**摘要：** 视觉语言模型（VLM）越来越多地被增强以包含连续或潜在的非文本标记，旨在支持“视觉思维”。尽管任务准确率有所提高，但这本身并不能证明模型确实在使用这些标记进行推理——性能提升可能源于混杂因素，例如增加的上下文长度、特殊标记锚定或训练时的正则化。

We formalize a diagnostic principle, Ablate-to-Validate, for testing whether latent-token content is genuinely utilized, and instantiate it as the Token Replacement Test (TRT), a standardized suite of content-replacement ablations. TRT holds the prompt, image, token budget, and decoding fixed while replacing intermediate tokens with zero, random, first-repeat, or oracle alternatives, isolating whether performance depends on token content or merely on token presence.

我们形式化了一种诊断原则“消融以验证”（Ablate-to-Validate），用于测试潜在标记的内容是否被真正利用，并将其实例化为“标记替换测试”（TRT），这是一套标准化的内容替换消融实验。TRT 在保持提示词、图像、标记预算和解码过程不变的同时，将中间标记替换为零、随机、首次重复或预言机（oracle）替代方案，从而隔离出性能究竟是依赖于标记内容，还是仅仅依赖于标记的存在。

As a controlled testbed, we study relative depth reasoning with LLaVA-13B and Qwen2.5-VL-3B, training models to predict and consume continuous or discrete depth spans across multiple frozen encoders (SigLIP2, CLIP, DINOv2) and token budgets. We additionally apply TRT to three off-the-shelf visual-thinking systems (Mirage, Mull-Tokens, CoVT) on BLINK, VSP, and CV-Bench.

作为一个受控测试平台，我们研究了 LLaVA-13B 和 Qwen2.5-VL-3B 的相对深度推理，训练模型在多个冻结编码器（SigLIP2、CLIP、DINOv2）和标记预算下预测并消耗连续或离散的深度跨度。此外，我们将 TRT 应用于 BLINK、VSP 和 CV-Bench 上的三种现成视觉思维系统（Mirage、Mull-Tokens、CoVT）。

Across all settings, accuracy gains are a misleading proxy for latent-token reasoning: VLMs retain most improvement even when token content is corrupted or replaced, revealing a persistent gap between having a latent channel and using it as an information bottleneck. We recommend TRT as a standard diagnostic alongside accuracy for any method introducing continuous thought tokens.

在所有设置中，准确率的提升是衡量潜在标记推理的一个误导性指标：即使在标记内容被破坏或替换时，VLM 仍保留了大部分性能提升，这揭示了“拥有潜在通道”与“将其作为信息瓶颈使用”之间存在持续的差距。我们建议将 TRT 作为一种标准诊断方法，与准确率一起用于评估任何引入连续思维标记的方法。
