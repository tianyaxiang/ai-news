# PhysX-Omni: Unified Simulation-Ready Physical 3D Generation for Rigid, Deformable, and Articulated Objects

**PhysX-Omni：面向刚体、可变形及关节物体的统一仿真就绪型物理 3D 生成框架**

***

### Abstract
Simulation-ready physical 3D assets have emerged as a promising direction owing to their broad applicability in downstream tasks. However, most existing 3D generation methods either neglect physical properties or are limited to a single asset category, e.g., rigid, deformable, or articulated objects. To address these limitations, we introduce PhysX-Omni, a unified framework for simulation-ready physical 3D generation across diverse asset types.

**摘要：**
由于在下游任务中具有广泛的适用性，仿真就绪型（Simulation-ready）物理 3D 资产已成为一个极具前景的研究方向。然而，大多数现有的 3D 生成方法要么忽略了物理属性，要么仅局限于单一资产类别（如刚体、可变形物体或关节物体）。为了解决这些局限性，我们推出了 PhysX-Omni，这是一个用于跨多种资产类型进行仿真就绪型物理 3D 生成的统一框架。

***

Specifically, we develop a novel and efficient geometry representation tailored for Vision-Language Models, which directly encodes high-resolution 3D structures without compression, significantly improving generation performance. In addition, we construct the first general simulation-ready 3D dataset, PhysXVerse, covering diverse indoor and outdoor categories.

具体而言，我们开发了一种专为视觉-语言模型（Vision-Language Models）量身定制的新型高效几何表示方法，该方法无需压缩即可直接编码高分辨率 3D 结构，从而显著提升了生成性能。此外，我们构建了首个通用的仿真就绪型 3D 数据集 PhysXVerse，涵盖了多种室内和室外类别。

***

Furthermore, to comprehensively and flexibly evaluate both generative and understanding capabilities in the wild, we propose PhysX-Bench, which encompasses six key attributes: geometry, absolute scale, material, affordance, kinematics, and function description. Extensive experiments with conventional metrics and PhysX-Bench show that PhysX-Omni performs strongly in both generation and understanding.

此外，为了在真实场景中全面且灵活地评估生成和理解能力，我们提出了 PhysX-Bench，它包含六个关键属性：几何形状、绝对尺度、材质、可供性（affordance）、运动学和功能描述。通过传统指标和 PhysX-Bench 进行的大量实验表明，PhysX-Omni 在生成和理解方面均表现出色。

***

Moreover, additional studies further validate the potential of PhysX-Omni for applications in simulation-ready scene generation and robotic policy learning. We believe PhysX-Omni can significantly advance a wide range of downstream applications, particularly in embodied AI and physics-based simulation.

此外，进一步的研究验证了 PhysX-Omni 在仿真就绪型场景生成和机器人策略学习应用中的潜力。我们相信，PhysX-Omni 将能够显著推动广泛的下游应用，特别是在具身智能（Embodied AI）和基于物理的仿真领域。
