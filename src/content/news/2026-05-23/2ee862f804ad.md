# Flat-Pack Bench: Evaluating Spatio-Temporal Understanding in Large Vision-Language Models through Furniture Assembly
# Flat-Pack Bench：通过家具组装评估大型视觉语言模型的时空理解能力

**Abstract:** The emergence of Large Vision-Language Models (LVLMs) has significantly advanced video understanding capabilities. However, existing benchmarks focus predominantly on coarse-grained tasks such as action segmentation, classification, captioning, and retrieval. Furthermore, these benchmarks often rely on entities that can be easily identified verbally, like household objects, animals, human subjects, etc., limiting their applicability to complex, in-the-wild video scenarios.

**摘要：** 大型视觉语言模型（LVLMs）的出现显著提升了视频理解能力。然而，现有的基准测试主要集中在粗粒度任务上，例如动作分割、分类、字幕生成和检索。此外，这些基准测试通常依赖于可以通过语言轻松识别的实体，如家居用品、动物、人类主体等，这限制了它们在复杂的现实场景视频中的适用性。

But, many applications such as furniture assembly, cooking, etc., require step-by-step fine-grained spatio-temporal understanding of the video, which is not sufficiently evaluated in current benchmarks. To address this gap, we introduce Flat-Pack Bench, a novel benchmark centered on furniture assembly tasks.

然而，家具组装、烹饪等许多应用场景需要对视频进行逐步的细粒度时空理解，而当前的基准测试对此评估不足。为了填补这一空白，我们推出了 Flat-Pack Bench，这是一个以家具组装任务为核心的新型基准测试。

Our benchmark evaluates LVLMs on nuanced tasks, including temporal ordering of assembly actions, temporal localization of assembly state, understanding part mating, and tracking, using multiple-choice questions paired with visual prompts highlighting relevant parts as references for fine-grained questions.

我们的基准测试通过多项选择题评估 LVLMs 在细微任务上的表现，包括组装动作的时间排序、组装状态的时间定位、零件配合的理解以及追踪。题目配有视觉提示，通过高亮相关零件作为细粒度问题的参考。

Our experiments reveal that state-of-the-art LVLMs struggle significantly with fine-grained spatio-temporal reasoning, highlighting their limitations in effectively leveraging temporal information from videos, limited tracking ability, and understanding of spatial interactions like physical contact.

我们的实验表明，最先进的 LVLMs 在细粒度时空推理方面表现吃力，这凸显了它们在有效利用视频时序信息、追踪能力以及理解物理接触等空间交互方面的局限性。
