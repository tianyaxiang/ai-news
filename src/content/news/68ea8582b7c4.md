# Open Problems in Frontier AI Risk Management
# 前沿人工智能风险管理中的开放性问题

**Abstract:** Frontier AI both amplifies existing risks and introduces qualitatively novel challenges. Not only is there a notable lack of stable scientific consensus resulting from the rapid pace of technological change, but emerging frontier AI safety practices are often misaligned with, or may undermine, established risk management frameworks.

**摘要：** 前沿人工智能（Frontier AI）既放大了现有风险，也带来了性质上全新的挑战。由于技术变革的飞速发展，不仅科学界缺乏稳定的共识，而且新兴的前沿人工智能安全实践往往与既定的风险管理框架不一致，甚至可能对其造成破坏。

To address these challenges, we systematically surface open problems in frontier AI risk management. Adopting a problem-oriented approach, we examine each stage of the risk management process - risk planning, identification, analysis, evaluation, and mitigation - through a structured review of the literature, identifying unresolved challenges and the actors best positioned to address them.

为了应对这些挑战，我们系统地梳理了前沿人工智能风险管理中的开放性问题。我们采用以问题为导向的方法，通过对文献的结构化回顾，审视了风险管理过程的每一个阶段——包括风险规划、识别、分析、评估和缓解——并确定了尚未解决的挑战以及最适合解决这些问题的相关主体。

Recognising that different types of open problems call for different responses, we classify open problems according to whether they reflect (a) a lack of scientific or technical consensus, (b) misalignment with, or challenges to, established risk management frameworks, or (c) shortcomings in implementation despite apparent consensus and alignment.

鉴于不同类型的开放性问题需要不同的应对措施，我们根据以下标准对这些问题进行了分类：(a) 缺乏科学或技术共识；(b) 与既定风险管理框架不一致或对其构成挑战；(c) 尽管存在明显的共识和一致性，但在执行层面仍存在缺陷。

By mapping these open problems and identifying the actors best positioned to address them - including developers, deployers, regulators, standards bodies, researchers, and third-party evaluators - this work aims to clarify where progress is needed to enable robust and meaningful consensus on frontier AI risk.

通过梳理这些开放性问题并确定最适合解决它们的主体（包括开发者、部署者、监管机构、标准制定机构、研究人员和第三方评估机构），本研究旨在明确在哪些方面需要取得进展，从而就前沿人工智能风险达成稳健且有意义的共识。

This paper does not propose specific solutions; instead, it provides a problem-oriented, agenda-setting reference document, complemented by a living online repository, intended to support coordination, reduce duplication, and guide future research and governance efforts.

本文并未提出具体的解决方案；相反，它提供了一份以问题为导向、旨在设定议程的参考文件，并辅以一个动态更新的在线存储库，旨在支持协调工作、减少重复劳动，并为未来的研究和治理工作提供指导。
