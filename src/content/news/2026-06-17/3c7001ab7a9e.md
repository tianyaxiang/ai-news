### RAMS: Resource-Adaptive and Detection-Conditioned Model Switching for Embedded Edge Perception
### RAMS：面向嵌入式边缘感知的资源自适应与检测条件模型切换

**Abstract:** Edge object detection on embedded hardware requires balancing inference latency and detection quality under changing resource pressure. We present RAMS, a lightweight runtime controller that monitors device pressure, calibrates switching thresholds from idle behavior, and dynamically selects among three resident YOLOv8 tiers (NANO/SMALL/MEDIUM at 320/416/640 px) without model-reload latency.

**摘要：** 在嵌入式硬件上进行边缘目标检测时，需要在不断变化的资源压力下平衡推理延迟与检测质量。我们提出了 RAMS，这是一种轻量级运行时控制器，它能够监控设备压力，根据空闲状态下的行为校准切换阈值，并在无需模型重载延迟的情况下，在三个常驻 YOLOv8 层级（NANO/SMALL/MEDIUM，分辨率分别为 320/416/640 px）之间进行动态选择。

RAMS defines five switching policies, including two detection-conditioned variants that prevent aggressive downgrades after recent vulnerable-road-user (VRU) detections. We further introduce the VRU-Weighted Accuracy Score (SWAS), a scalar metric for offline policy comparison without ground-truth annotations, together with an oracle-bounded variant that separates detector circularity from genuine tier-retention benefit.

RAMS 定义了五种切换策略，其中包括两种检测条件变体，旨在防止在检测到弱势道路使用者（VRU）后出现激进的降级行为。此外，我们引入了 VRU 加权准确率评分（SWAS），这是一种无需地面真值标注即可进行离线策略比较的标量指标，并结合了一种预言机界定变体，用以区分检测器的循环依赖与模型层级保留带来的实际收益。

Across Raspberry Pi 5, x86 laptops, and Jetson Orin ONNX/TensorRT deployments, the same controller equations operate over a 37x latency range. On Jetson Orin TensorRT under heavy load, the safety2 policy achieves 3.41 ms mean latency, 5.6x faster than fixed-MEDIUM inference, while retaining 74% of its proxy accuracy through near-NANO operation with selective SMALL and MEDIUM locks during VRU-positive windows.

在 Raspberry Pi 5、x86 笔记本电脑以及 Jetson Orin ONNX/TensorRT 等部署环境下，同一套控制器方程可在 37 倍的延迟范围内有效运行。在 Jetson Orin TensorRT 的高负载测试中，safety2 策略实现了 3.41 毫秒的平均延迟，比固定使用 MEDIUM 模型推理快 5.6 倍；同时，通过在 VRU 出现窗口期选择性锁定 SMALL 和 MEDIUM 模型，并在其余时间以接近 NANO 的模式运行，该策略保留了 74% 的代理准确率。

Detection-conditioned switching improves SWAS by 25.4% under oracle scoring and 47.3% under detector-derived scoring relative to threshold-only policies under heavy load. Live KITTI evaluation reports per-tier VRU recall of 24.2%, 41.2%, and 59.0%, showing that reactive overrides are fundamentally limited by baseline detector recall.

在高负载环境下，与仅基于阈值的策略相比，检测条件切换策略在预言机评分下将 SWAS 提升了 25.4%，在基于检测器的评分下提升了 47.3%。实时 KITTI 评估报告显示，各层级的 VRU 召回率分别为 24.2%、41.2% 和 59.0%，这表明响应式覆盖机制在根本上受限于基准检测器的召回能力。
