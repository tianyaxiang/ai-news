# The Google / Xreal Aura XR glasses are now available to preorder
# Google / Xreal Aura XR 眼镜现已开启预购

The Project Aura glasses collaboration between Xreal and Google is now one step closer to being something you can buy. Reservations for the second Android XR device, now dubbed the Xreal Aura, are available for $99 starting today, with a full launch in the US, UK, Japan, Canada, and South Korea expected sometime this Fall.

Xreal 与 Google 合作的 Project Aura 眼镜距离正式发售又近了一步。这款被称为 Xreal Aura 的第二款 Android XR 设备从今天起开放预订，订金为 99 美元。预计该产品将于今年秋季在美国、英国、日本、加拿大和韩国正式上市。

The Xreal Aura follows Google’s first Android-powered XR device, the Samsung Galaxy XR headset, which launched at $1,799 in October 2025. We still don’t have a full price for the Aura — which Google has previously described as “a headset masquerading as glasses” — but Best Buy will be the first in-store retail partner when it does become available. Xreal also says that those who reserve within a two-week launch offer will receive $199 credit towards the launch purchase later this year, “saving you $100 on the final price.”

Xreal Aura 是继 Google 首款搭载 Android 系统的 XR 设备——三星 Galaxy XR 头显（于 2025 年 10 月以 1,799 美元的价格发布）之后的又一力作。目前我们尚不清楚 Aura 的最终售价——Google 此前将其描述为“伪装成眼镜的头显”——但百思买（Best Buy）已确定成为其首个线下零售合作伙伴。Xreal 同时表示，在两周预售期内完成预订的用户，将在今年晚些时候正式购买时获得 199 美元的抵扣额度，相当于“在最终价格上节省 100 美元”。

As part of this deposit scheme announcement, we know also know that the Xreal Aura weighs under 95 grams, and is powered by the new Snapdragon Reality Elite chip, Qualcomm’s latest XR platform. The chip, in combination with Google’s Android XR, aims to help the Aura glasses to meet demands for “better immersive experiences, higher performance, greater intelligence, and improved power optimization,” according to a statement from Qualcomm’s XR head, Ziad Asghar.

随着此次预订计划的公布，我们还了解到 Xreal Aura 的重量不到 95 克，并搭载了高通最新的 XR 平台——骁龙 Reality Elite 芯片。据高通 XR 部门负责人 Ziad Asghar 表示，该芯片结合 Google 的 Android XR 系统，旨在帮助 Aura 眼镜满足“更好的沉浸式体验、更高的性能、更强的智能化以及更优的功耗表现”等需求。

We might get some new details about the upcoming launch after Xreal showcases the Aura glasses at Augmented World Expo, which is running between June 15th-18th in Long Beach, California.

在 6 月 15 日至 18 日于加州长滩举行的增强现实世界博览会（Augmented World Expo）上，Xreal 将展示 Aura 眼镜，届时我们可能会获得关于此次发布会的更多细节。
