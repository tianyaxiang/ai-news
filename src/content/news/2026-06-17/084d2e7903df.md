# α-Fair Insurance Pricing: A Fairness Continuum
# α-公平保险定价：公平性连续统

**Abstract:** Fairness in insurance pricing remains a long-standing and deeply debated puzzle. On one hand, insurers, driven by profitability considerations, set premiums that differentiate across individual risks to achieve actuarial fairness. On the other hand, insurance serves a critical societal function by pooling risks across a population, motivating cross-subsidization among groups to promote solidarity fairness. The tension between these two competing notions of fairness makes insurance pricing inherently complex, particularly in modern settings where granular data allow for increasingly fine risk differentiation and regulators face growing pressure to protect vulnerable groups.

**摘要：** 保险定价中的公平性问题一直是一个长期存在且备受争议的难题。一方面，保险公司出于盈利考虑，通过区分个人风险来设定保费，以实现精算公平；另一方面，保险通过在人群中分摊风险发挥着重要的社会功能，这促使群体间进行交叉补贴，以促进团结公平。这两种相互竞争的公平观念之间的张力，使得保险定价变得极其复杂，尤其是在现代环境下，细粒度数据使得风险区分日益精细，而监管机构在保护弱势群体方面也面临着越来越大的压力。

To address this challenge, we propose an $\alpha$-**F**air **I**ndividual **S**olvent **P**remium ($\alpha$-FISP) framework for insurance pricing that explicitly captures the trade-off between actuarial and solidarity fairness while guaranteeing solvency, a fundamental requirement in insurance operations. We formulate the pricing problem as a constrained optimization task, where actuarially fair premiums are adjusted subject to budget constraints on cross-subsidization within each risk class.

为了应对这一挑战，我们提出了一种用于保险定价的 $\alpha$-公平个人偿付能力保费（$\alpha$-FISP）框架。该框架在保证保险运营基本要求——偿付能力的前提下，明确捕捉了精算公平与团结公平之间的权衡。我们将定价问题表述为一个约束优化任务，在每个风险类别内部的交叉补贴预算约束下，对精算公平保费进行调整。

This formulation naturally yields a family of solutions parameterized by $\alpha$, tracing a continuum between purely actuarial and purely solidarity-based pricing and enabling decision-makers to select an operating point along this fairness spectrum. We derive theoretical guarantees for the proposed framework. Numerical experiments show that $\alpha$-FISP is computationally tractable and aligns well with the U.S. regulatory regimes featuring heterogeneous state-level fairness requirements.

这一公式自然地产生了一系列以 $\alpha$ 为参数的解，描绘了从纯精算定价到纯团结定价之间的连续统，使决策者能够沿着这一公平性谱系选择一个运营点。我们为所提出的框架推导了理论保证。数值实验表明，$\alpha$-FISP 在计算上是可行的，并且与美国各州具有差异化公平性要求的监管制度高度契合。
