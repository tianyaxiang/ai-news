# Hierarchical GRU with Input-Conditioned Slot Queries for Ball Action Anticipation
# 基于输入条件槽查询的分层 GRU 模型：足球动作预测

We present a hierarchical model for ball action anticipation in football broadcast video. Given a 30-second observation window, the system predicts actions occurring in the subsequent 5-second window across 10 classes.
我们提出了一种用于足球转播视频中球类动作预测的分层模型。该系统在给定 30 秒观察窗口的情况下，能够预测随后 5 秒窗口内发生的 10 类动作。

A shared local Transformer encodes clip-level features within each 5-second sub-window; a GRU then aggregates temporal context across all sub-windows; finally, a Transformer decoder with K input-conditioned event slots decodes the anticipation target via three decoupled heads (objectness, class, temporal offset).
模型利用共享的局部 Transformer 对每个 5 秒子窗口内的片段级特征进行编码；随后通过 GRU 聚合所有子窗口的时间上下文；最后，一个带有 K 个输入条件事件槽的 Transformer 解码器通过三个解耦头（目标性、类别、时间偏移）解码出预测目标。

We introduce frequency-reweighted Hungarian matching that systematically favours rare action classes, and Gaussian soft targets for temporal bin supervision. On the SoccerNet Ball Action Anticipation benchmark, our method achieves 17.91% mAP on the test server.
我们引入了频率重加权的匈牙利匹配算法，该算法系统性地偏向于稀有动作类别，并采用高斯软目标进行时间箱监督。在 SoccerNet 球类动作预测基准测试中，我们的方法在测试服务器上达到了 17.91% 的 mAP。
