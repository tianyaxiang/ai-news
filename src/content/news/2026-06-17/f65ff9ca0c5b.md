### What’s a Robot Vacuum?
### 什么是扫地机器人？

A robot vacuum is an automated vacuum, usually shaped like a round disc, that moves around your house to vacuum (and mop, as many models these days include mopping) on your behalf. You might've heard some call these devices a "Roomba," which is the brand that made the concept popular, but today just about every cleaning brand—from high-end Dyson to affordable Bissell—makes some kind of robot vacuum. They come with either a charging station or a larger docking station that can self-empty.

扫地机器人是一种自动化吸尘器，通常呈圆盘状，可以在家中移动，代你完成吸尘（以及拖地，因为如今许多型号都具备拖地功能）工作。你可能听过有人把这些设备称为“Roomba”，这是让这一概念普及的品牌，但如今几乎每个清洁品牌——从高端的戴森到平价的必胜（Bissell）——都在生产扫地机器人。它们通常配有充电座或可自动集尘的大型基站。

### Can You Use Robot Vacuums on All Kinds of Floors?
### 扫地机器人适用于所有类型的地板吗？

You can, especially since there are so many mopping models now to better clean hard flooring. You can use these devices on anything from hardwood to LVP and tile, though I wouldn't recommend leaving smaller rugs out for these autonomous gadgets to get stuck on. Large, set rugs are fine, but bathroom and kitchen mats or any thin, easily moved rugs should be cleared out of the way and put in the wash instead of vacuumed by your robot vac.

可以，尤其是现在有许多具备拖地功能的型号，能更好地清洁硬质地板。你可以将这些设备用于从实木地板到塑胶地板（LVP）和瓷砖的各种地面。不过，我不建议在地上放置小型地毯，以免这些自动设备被卡住。大型固定地毯没问题，但浴室和厨房的脚垫或任何轻薄、易移动的地毯，最好先移开并清洗，而不是让扫地机器人去吸。

### What Will Your Robot Vacuum Need?
### 扫地机器人需要什么条件？

There are a few things most robot vacuums will need to work properly:
大多数扫地机器人正常工作需要满足以下几点：

*   **Space around the base station.** Many robot vacuum manuals recommend at least a foot of clearance on three of the four sides of the vacuum's base station to ensure it has room to return home.
    **基站周围的空间。** 许多扫地机器人说明书建议在基站的三个侧面至少留出一英尺（约30厘米）的空隙，以确保它有足够的空间返回基站。
*   **A good Wi-Fi signal.** If your robot vacuum connects to Wi-Fi, as many do these days for app control and software updates, it'll need a strong connection wherever it's placed. Most of them can only connect to the 2.4 GHz wireless band, so make sure you have that (and choose only that band when you're setting up if your Wi-Fi shows those bands separately).
    **良好的 Wi-Fi 信号。** 如果你的扫地机器人需要连接 Wi-Fi（如今大多数设备为了实现 App 控制和软件更新都需要联网），那么它所在的位置必须有强劲的信号。大多数机器人只能连接 2.4 GHz 频段，所以请确保你的网络支持该频段（如果你的 Wi-Fi 分别显示不同频段，设置时请务必只选择 2.4 GHz）。
*   **Regular maintenance.** It might be a robot, but it can't take care of itself. See below for my tips for basic robot vacuum maintenance, and make sure to read your vacuum's manual to know what your specific model requires.
    **定期维护。** 它虽然是机器人，但无法自我照料。请参阅下文了解基本的维护建议，并务必阅读说明书，了解你所用型号的具体要求。

### How Often to Maintain Your Robot Vacuum
### 扫地机器人需要多久维护一次？

Taking good care of your robot vacuum will help it last. Here's the debris-removal process you should follow for each robot vacuum style:
妥善保养扫地机器人有助于延长其使用寿命。以下是针对不同类型扫地机器人的垃圾清理流程：

*   **If it has a charger-only base:** This means the debris is stored inside the vacuum. You'll likely want to empty it after each use.
    **如果只有充电底座：** 这意味着垃圾存储在机器人内部。建议每次使用后都进行清理。
*   **If it has a self-emptying docking station:** Each manufacturer will have different advice, but it's usually between 30 and 60 days. If you run your vacuum daily and have a lot of debris, like pet hair, you might need to aim for 30 days or less.
    **如果配有自动集尘基站：** 每个厂家的建议不同，通常在 30 到 60 天之间。如果你每天使用且垃圾较多（如宠物毛发），可能需要缩短至 30 天或更短。
*   **If it has water tanks:** Dirty-water tanks should be cleaned after each mopping run. Clean water can stay filled in the station.
    **如果配有水箱：** 每次拖地后都应清理污水箱。清水箱则可以保持注水状态。

Make sure to review your robot vacuum's manual for any other steps the manufacturer recommends. Many brands also have replacement parts, so you can swap out individual pieces like brushes without needing an entirely new vacuum.
请务必查阅说明书，了解厂家建议的其他维护步骤。许多品牌提供替换零件，因此你可以更换刷子等单个部件，而无需购买全新的机器。
