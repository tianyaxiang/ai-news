# SpaceX to acquire AI coding platform Cursor for $60 billion
# SpaceX 将以 600 亿美元收购 AI 编程平台 Cursor

SpaceX will acquire AI coding tool Cursor for $60 billion in an all-stock transaction, the companies announced today. The deal is expected to close in the third quarter. It comes just two days after SpaceX’s unprecedented IPO and a few months after the merger of SpaceX and xAI, which brought a significant restructuring of xAI.

SpaceX 和 Cursor 今日宣布，SpaceX 将以全股票交易方式收购 AI 编程工具 Cursor，交易金额为 600 亿美元。该交易预计将于第三季度完成。此前两天，SpaceX 刚刚完成了史无前例的首次公开募股（IPO）；而在几个月前，SpaceX 与 xAI 完成了合并，并对 xAI 进行了大规模重组。

Cursor was one of the first tools to fully bake features that leverage large language models into an IDE. It’s a branch of Visual Studio Code with heavy AI integration. However, incumbent platforms and bigger AI companies have since rolled out comparable features. Cursor has seen considerable revenue growth over the past year, but its market share has also slipped as Anthropic’s Claude Code has achieved dominance in the space. TechCrunch reported that Cursor was struggling to break even.

Cursor 是首批将大语言模型功能深度集成到集成开发环境（IDE）中的工具之一。它是 Visual Studio Code 的一个分支，具有深度的 AI 集成。然而，随着现有平台和大型 AI 公司纷纷推出类似功能，Cursor 的市场份额有所下滑，尽管其过去一年营收增长显著，但 Anthropic 的 Claude Code 已在该领域占据主导地位。据 TechCrunch 报道，Cursor 此前一直难以实现盈亏平衡。

Early this year, the Cursor team said its future growth was bottlenecked on compute. This spring, xAI struck a deal to give Cursor access to its compute infrastructure, foreshadowing similar, larger deals with Anthropic and Google in the future. xAI and Cursor also began training models together at that time, including Grok Build, xAI’s coding and knowledge work model.

今年年初，Cursor 团队曾表示其未来增长受到算力瓶颈的制约。今年春天，xAI 与 Cursor 达成协议，向其开放算力基础设施，这为未来与 Anthropic 和 Google 达成类似的大规模交易埋下了伏笔。当时，xAI 和 Cursor 还开始共同训练模型，包括 xAI 的编程与知识工作模型 Grok Build。

Those deals with Anthropic and Google have relatively favorable termination clauses for SpaceX, so if SpaceX’s enterprise AI efforts take off and see high demand, it will theoretically be possible to reallocate compute from competitors directly to SpaceX and the Cursor team.

这些与 Anthropic 和 Google 的交易中包含对 SpaceX 相对有利的终止条款。因此，如果 SpaceX 的企业级 AI 业务起飞并出现高需求，理论上可以将算力从竞争对手处直接重新分配给 SpaceX 和 Cursor 团队。

This is a marriage between two companies that have arguably been falling behind in the AI race. xAI-turned-SpaceX’s Grok chatbot has been riddled with controversies, but its lack of a competitive coding model or harness has also been a strategic weakness. The tool has largely been stuck in an older, chatbot-centric paradigm, compared to offerings from Anthropic, Google, and OpenAI.

这是一场两家在 AI 竞赛中被认为已逐渐掉队的公司之间的联姻。并入 SpaceX 的 xAI 旗下的 Grok 聊天机器人一直争议不断，而缺乏具有竞争力的编程模型或开发工具也是其战略上的弱点。与 Anthropic、Google 和 OpenAI 的产品相比，该工具在很大程度上仍停留在以聊天机器人为中心的旧范式中。

Cursor had good talent and a strong product, but it couldn’t compete with larger companies on compute. SpaceX had the capacity but lacked the product and models to be competitive, even though much of its more than $2 trillion IPO’s promise hinged on providing AI services to enterprise customers. This acquisition is a direct response to both of their problems, though it still does not guarantee success in such a competitive field.

Cursor 拥有优秀的人才和强大的产品，但在算力上无法与大公司竞争。SpaceX 拥有算力资源，却缺乏具有竞争力的产品和模型，尽管其超过 2 万亿美元的 IPO 估值很大程度上依赖于为企业客户提供 AI 服务。此次收购是对双方问题的直接回应，尽管这并不能保证在如此激烈的竞争领域中一定能取得成功。
