### KDE Plasma 6.7 released
### KDE Plasma 6.7 正式发布

**Live Images**
**Live 镜像**

The easiest way to try it out is with a live image booted off a USB disk. Docker images also provide a quick and easy way to test Plasma.
体验该版本最简单的方法是通过 USB 启动盘运行 Live 镜像。此外，Docker 镜像也提供了一种快速简便的 Plasma 测试方式。

Download live images with Plasma | Download Docker images with Plasma
[下载包含 Plasma 的 Live 镜像](https://kde.org/plasma-desktop/) | [下载包含 Plasma 的 Docker 镜像](https://hub.docker.com/)

**Package Downloads**
**软件包下载**

Distributions have created, or are in the process of creating, packages listed on our wiki page. Note that packages of this release might not be available on all distributions at the time of this announcement.
各大发行版已经或正在制作相关软件包，具体列表请参阅我们的 Wiki 页面。请注意，在本文发布之时，并非所有发行版都已提供该版本的软件包。

Package download wiki page
[软件包下载 Wiki 页面](https://community.kde.org/Get_KDE_Software)
