# A Comparative Study of Graph Neural Network Layer Selection for Interaction Modelling in Driving Trajectory Prediction
# 驾驶轨迹预测中交互建模的图神经网络层选择对比研究

**Abstract:** Autonomous driving systems rely on precise trajectory prediction to plan safe and efficient movement. Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) have become a promising approach for modelling spatiotemporal interactions among road agents. However, designing GNN architectures for trajectory prediction remains non-standardized, with little guidance on which graph layers effectively capture spatial interactions and temporal dynamics.

**摘要：** 自动驾驶系统依赖于精确的轨迹预测来规划安全高效的行驶路径。图神经网络（GNNs）已成为建模道路参与者之间时空交互的一种极具前景的方法。然而，用于轨迹预测的 GNN 架构设计目前尚未标准化，且缺乏关于哪些图层能有效捕捉空间交互和时间动态的指导。

This paper offers a detailed comparative study of 19 graph layer types, focusing on their spatial and temporal processing capabilities to discover the most effective architectures for trajectory prediction. Within the explored hyperparameter setting, we highlight five standout layer combinations, with ARMA, Chebyshev, and topology-aware layers consistently performing better than others.

本文对 19 种图层类型进行了详细的对比研究，重点关注它们在空间和时间处理上的能力，旨在发现用于轨迹预测的最有效架构。在所探索的超参数设置范围内，我们重点介绍了五种表现突出的层组合，其中 ARMA、Chebyshev 和拓扑感知（topology-aware）层的表现始终优于其他层。

Beyond performance metrics, our findings yield practical design principles: sum-based aggregation is more effective than mean-based methods, multi-head attention mechanisms enable richer interactions, and assigning different weights to different hop distances significantly improves prediction accuracy. These findings offer useful guidance for designing more interpretable and effective trajectory prediction models.

除了性能指标外，我们的研究结果还总结出了一些实用的设计原则：基于求和（sum-based）的聚合方式比基于均值（mean-based）的方法更有效；多头注意力机制能够实现更丰富的交互；为不同的跳数（hop distances）分配不同的权重可以显著提高预测精度。这些发现为设计更具可解释性和更有效的轨迹预测模型提供了有益的指导。
