# GRAPE: Guided Parameter-Space Evolution for Compact Adversarial Robustness
# GRAPE：用于紧凑对抗鲁棒性的引导式参数空间演化

**Abstract:** Adversarial Training (AT) improves neural network robustness, but most methods train a fixed parameter space from the start. This paper asks whether the order in which parameters become optimizable can affect the final robust solution, even when the final architecture or computation budget is controlled.

**摘要：** 对抗训练（AT）能够提升神经网络的鲁棒性，但大多数方法从一开始就训练一个固定的参数空间。本文探讨了在最终架构或计算预算受控的情况下，参数变得可优化的顺序是否会影响最终的鲁棒解。

We propose GRAPE, Guided Parameter-Space Evolution, a training framework for compact adversarial robustness. GRAPE combines parameter-space stabilization with progressive hidden expansion: it stabilizes robust optimization in the currently exposed space, gradually releases new optimizable dimensions, and uses an adversarial spectral utilization score to guide newly released capacity toward high-pressure modules.

我们提出了 GRAPE（引导式参数空间演化），这是一个用于实现紧凑对抗鲁棒性的训练框架。GRAPE 将参数空间稳定化与渐进式隐藏扩展相结合：它在当前暴露的空间中稳定鲁棒优化，逐步释放新的可优化维度，并利用对抗性谱利用率评分（adversarial spectral utilization score）将新释放的容量引导至高压力模块。

In contrast to fixed-structure AT, GRAPE treats robust model learning as a process of progressive parameter-space exposure and evolution. Under the standard $\ell_\infty$ threat model on CIFAR-10, with fixed-structure ResNet-18 AT as a controlled reference, GRAPE improves PGD-20 robust accuracy from 51.70% to 56.94% at a nearly matched computation budget with a FLOPs ratio of 1.009x, while reducing parameter count by about 21.4%.

与固定结构的对抗训练（AT）不同，GRAPE 将鲁棒模型学习视为一个参数空间逐步暴露和演化的过程。在 CIFAR-10 的标准 $\ell_\infty$ 威胁模型下，以固定结构的 ResNet-18 AT 作为对照参考，GRAPE 在计算预算几乎持平（FLOPs 比率为 1.009x）的情况下，将 PGD-20 的鲁棒准确率从 51.70% 提升至 56.94%，同时将参数量减少了约 21.4%。

A sequential grow variant with the same final ResNet-18 architecture reaches 56.52% PGD-20 robust accuracy, indicating that the gain is not only due to final architecture differences but also to the parameter-space exposure path. These results suggest that guided parameter-space evolution can yield compact and robust parameter configurations under matched computation.

一种采用相同最终 ResNet-18 架构的顺序增长变体达到了 56.52% 的 PGD-20 鲁棒准确率，这表明性能提升不仅归功于最终架构的差异，还归功于参数空间的暴露路径。这些结果表明，引导式参数空间演化可以在匹配的计算条件下产生紧凑且鲁棒的参数配置。
