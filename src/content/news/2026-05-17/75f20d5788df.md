# PwC is deploying Claude to build technology, execute deals, and reinvent enterprise functions for clients
# 普华永道（PwC）部署 Claude 以构建技术、执行交易并重塑客户的企业职能

May 14, 2026
2026年5月14日

Anthropic and PwC today announced an expansion of their strategic alliance, deepening how PwC uses Claude to build technology, execute deals, and reinvent enterprise functions for clients across every industry it serves. Most enterprises are still running on systems and processes built for a pre-AI world—a drag that is estimated to be more than $2 trillion. Together, Anthropic and PwC are helping companies replace that weight and rebuild around AI.
Anthropic 与普华永道今日宣布扩大战略联盟，深化普华永道利用 Claude 为其服务的所有行业客户构建技术、执行交易及重塑企业职能的方式。目前，大多数企业仍在使用为“前人工智能时代”构建的系统和流程，这造成的效率拖累估计超过 2 万亿美元。Anthropic 与普华永道正携手帮助企业摆脱这一负担，并围绕人工智能进行重建。

Key elements of the announcement:
本次公告的关键要素：

*   PwC will roll out Claude Code and Cowork starting with U.S. teams and expanding toward a global workforce of hundreds of thousands of professionals.
    普华永道将率先在美国团队中推广 Claude Code 和 Cowork，并逐步扩展至全球数十万名专业人员。
*   The companies are establishing a joint Center of Excellence and a program to train and certify 30,000 PwC professionals on Claude.
    双方将建立一个联合卓越中心，并启动一项培训计划，旨在对 3 万名普华永道专业人员进行 Claude 相关的培训与认证。
*   The collaboration focuses on three areas of highest leverage: agentic technology build, AI-native deal-making, and reinvention of the enterprise function.
    此次合作聚焦于三个最具杠杆效应的领域：智能体（Agentic）技术构建、AI 原生交易撮合，以及企业职能的重塑。
*   PwC is launching a new finance business group (Office of the CFO) built on Claude as the first standalone business unit anchored in Anthropic's technology.
    普华永道正在推出一个新的财务业务组（CFO 办公室），这是首个基于 Anthropic 技术构建的独立业务单元。
*   Claude is already running in production across professional sports operations, insurance underwriting, mainframe modernization, HR transformation, and cybersecurity—cutting delivery times by up to 70%.
    Claude 已在职业体育运营、保险承保、大型机现代化、人力资源转型和网络安全等领域投入生产应用，交付时间缩短了高达 70%。

"PwC has been leading AI's expansion into the parts of the economy where accuracy and reliability are non-negotiable—financial services, healthcare, life sciences, cybersecurity—and the results are clear. Insurance underwriting that took 10 weeks now takes 10 days. Security work that took hours now takes minutes. We're excited to put Claude in the hands of hundreds of thousands of people across PwC's workforce," said Dario Amodei, Co-founder and CEO, Anthropic.
“普华永道一直引领着人工智能向那些对准确性和可靠性要求极高的经济领域扩展，如金融服务、医疗保健、生命科学和网络安全，且成果显著。原本需要 10 周的保险承保工作现在仅需 10 天，原本需要数小时的安全工作现在只需几分钟。我们很高兴能将 Claude 交到普华永道全球数十万员工手中，”Anthropic 联合创始人兼首席执行官 Dario Amodei 表示。

Clients are looking for ways to apply AI that are secure, responsible, and capable of delivering measurable outcomes in complex business environments. Our collaboration with Anthropic brings together advanced AI capabilities and PwC's industry experience to help organizations move from exploration to enterprise-wide impact with greater confidence," said Paul Griggs, US Senior Partner and CEO, PwC.
“关于人工智能的讨论已从‘可能性’转向‘执行力’。客户正在寻找安全、负责任且能在复杂商业环境中交付可衡量成果的人工智能应用方式。我们与 Anthropic 的合作将先进的 AI 能力与普华永道的行业经验相结合，帮助企业更有信心地从探索阶段迈向全企业范围的影响力，”普华永道美国高级合伙人兼首席执行官 Paul Griggs 表示。

### Areas of focus
### 重点领域

The biggest gains for enterprises will come from AI systems that complete tasks end-to-end, run continuously, and let the most experienced professionals operate at a scale not previously possible. PwC and Anthropic are building them in production today, across PwC's global network. The expansion centers on three areas:
企业获得的最大收益将来自那些能够端到端完成任务、持续运行，并让最有经验的专业人员以以往无法企及的规模进行操作的 AI 系统。普华永道和 Anthropic 目前正在普华永道的全球网络中进行生产部署。此次扩展聚焦于三个领域：

*   **Agentic technology build.** Engineering teams are using Claude Code to ship production software for major companies in weeks, not quarters. This includes a growing portfolio of agentic builds across financial services, pharma and life sciences, healthcare, and consumer markets.
    **智能体技术构建。** 工程团队正在使用 Claude Code 为大型企业交付生产级软件，周期从季度缩短至几周。这包括在金融服务、制药与生命科学、医疗保健和消费市场中不断增长的智能体构建组合。
*   **AI-native deal-making.** PwC is reinventing how it executes deals end-to-end — diligence, value creation, integration — with agents working alongside deal teams. For private equity sponsors and corporate acquirers, this compresses the path from thesis to value capture and changes the economics of what's worth doing.
    **AI 原生交易撮合。** 普华永道正在重塑其端到端的交易执行方式（包括尽职调查、价值创造和整合），让智能体与交易团队并肩工作。对于私募股权赞助商和企业收购方而言，这缩短了从投资论点到价值获取的路径，并改变了项目可行性的经济逻辑。
*   **Reinvention of the enterprise function.** Where many are running pilots, PwC is running production—including building scalable AI-native operating models for finance, supply chain, HR, and the engineering function itself.
    **企业职能重塑。** 在许多企业仍处于试点阶段时，普华永道已进入生产应用阶段，包括为财务、供应链、人力资源及工程职能本身构建可扩展的 AI 原生运营模式。

### Office of the CFO
### CFO 办公室

PwC is launching a new business group focused on transforming client finance organizations with Claude. The practice will pair PwC's finance expertise with Anthropic's full product set: Claude, Claude Cowork, and Claude Code. It's starting with regulated industries—banking, insurance, healthcare—where accuracy and auditability matter most. Engagements range from targeted help with specific finance tasks to top-to-bottom redesigns of the whole function.
普华永道正在成立一个新的业务组，专注于利用 Claude 转型客户的财务组织。该业务将结合普华永道的财务专业知识与 Anthropic 的全套产品：Claude、Claude Cowork 和 Claude Code。业务将从受监管行业（如银行、保险、医疗保健）开始，因为这些领域对准确性和可审计性要求最高。服务范围涵盖从针对特定财务任务的辅助，到对整个职能部门的彻底重构。

PwC started as "Customer Zero," putting Claude to work inside its own firm before bringing it to clients. Teams used it for journal entries, variance analysis, and RFPs, and built on Claude Code to optimize annual planning. In parallel, PwC has been helping Anthropic's own CFO office scale operations, controls, and international payroll. Both firms put the technology to work inside their own walls before bringing it to clients.
普华永道以“零号客户”的身份先行，在将 Claude 推向客户之前，先在公司内部进行了应用。团队将其用于日记账分录、差异分析和 RFP（建议书邀请），并利用 Claude Code 优化年度规划。与此同时，普华永道一直在帮助 Anthropic 自身的 CFO 办公室扩展运营、内控和国际薪酬管理。两家公司在将技术推向客户之前，都先在各自内部进行了实践。
