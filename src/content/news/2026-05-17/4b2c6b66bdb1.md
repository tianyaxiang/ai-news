### Smartwatches and fitness trackers
### 智能手表与健身追踪器

**Garmin Fenix 8**
The Garmin Fenix 8 is a premium multisport watch that features a speaker, mic, and on-device assistant for calls, timers, and voice commands. It also offers elite battery life, dual-frequency GPS, and robust sensors.
Garmin Fenix 8 是一款高端多功能运动手表，配备了扬声器、麦克风和设备端助手，可用于通话、设置计时器和语音指令。它还拥有顶级的电池续航、双频 GPS 以及强大的传感器。

**Amazfit Active 2**
The Amazfit Active 2 delivers outsized value for the price. It looks spiffy and has a wide array of health tracking features, plus built-in GPS and AI chatbots to provide extra context to your data.
Amazfit Active 2 提供了超乎价格的性价比。它外观时尚，具备多种健康追踪功能，并内置 GPS 和 AI 聊天机器人，能为你的健康数据提供更多参考信息。

Garmin’s Venu 4 is geared toward the average athlete, and it’s on sale at REI, Amazon, and Best Buy in both size configurations for $499.99 ($50 off), matching its best price to date. The wearable offers many of the same health and fitness tracking features as its predecessor, the Venu 3S, including abnormal heart rate alerts, detailed sleep insights, audio-guided meditation sessions, and a wheelchair mode.
Garmin Venu 4 专为普通运动爱好者设计，目前在 REI、亚马逊和百思买（Best Buy）均有促销，两种尺寸版本售价均为 499.99 美元（直降 50 美元），平了历史最低价。这款可穿戴设备提供了与其前代产品 Venu 3S 相同的许多健康和健身追踪功能，包括异常心率警报、详细的睡眠分析、语音引导冥想课程以及轮椅模式。

REI, Amazon, and Best Buy are also selling the Garmin Lily 2 Active for $249.99 ($50 off), which is its lowest price to date. The stylish smartwatch is a great budget-friendly Garmin, especially for people with smaller wrists. Like the standard Lily 2, it offers basic fitness tracking, notifications, and up to five days of battery life; however, the so-called “Active” model adds a physical button, built-in GPS, and support for more sports tracking.
REI、亚马逊和百思买目前也将 Garmin Lily 2 Active 的售价降至 249.99 美元（直降 50 美元），创下历史新低。这款时尚的智能手表是 Garmin 旗下极具性价比的选择，特别适合手腕较细的用户。与标准版 Lily 2 一样，它提供基础的健身追踪、通知提醒和长达五天的续航；不过，“Active”型号增加了物理按键、内置 GPS，并支持更多的运动追踪功能。

### Communication and safety gear
### 通讯与安全装备

**Garmin inReach Mini 2**
Garmin’s palm-sized inReach Mini 2 satellite communicator offers off-the-grid two-way messaging, built-in navigation capabilities, and powerful mapping features, including the ability to set waypoints when paired with Garmin’s Explore app. A subscription plan is required to access the Iridium network, though, with prices starting at $7.99 a month.
Garmin 手掌大小的 inReach Mini 2 卫星通讯器提供离线双向短信功能、内置导航能力以及强大的地图功能，包括与 Garmin Explore 应用程序配对时设置航点。不过，使用 Iridium 网络需要订阅服务，起价为每月 7.99 美元。

Garmin’s InReach Messenger Plus is on sale at REI, Amazon, and direct from Garmin for $299.99 ($100 off), its lowest prices to date. The subscription-based Plus model provides satellite connectivity and access to emergency services like the aforementioned InReach Mini 2, except it can also send photos and audio clips in addition to standard text messages.
Garmin InReach Messenger Plus 目前在 REI、亚马逊和 Garmin 官网均有促销，售价 299.99 美元（直降 100 美元），创下历史新低。这款基于订阅的 Plus 型号除了像上述的 InReach Mini 2 一样提供卫星连接和紧急服务访问外，还能在发送标准短信的基础上发送照片和音频片段。

Garmin’s newer InReach Mini 3 is also available from REI, Amazon, and Garmin for $399.99 ($100 off), its best price to date. Like the Mini 2, the rugged, palm-sized satellite communicator lets you send texts, share your location, check the weather, and trigger SOS alerts when you’re off the grid, all while offering battery life that can outlast your average smartphone. Unlike its predecessor, though, it features a color touchscreen, which makes it much easier to scroll through menus and bang out messages.
Garmin 新款 InReach Mini 3 目前在 REI、亚马逊和 Garmin 官网售价 399.99 美元（直降 100 美元），为历史最低价。与 Mini 2 一样，这款坚固耐用的掌上卫星通讯器让你在离线状态下也能发送短信、分享位置、查看天气并触发 SOS 警报，同时其续航能力远超普通智能手机。与前代产品不同的是，它配备了彩色触摸屏，这使得浏览菜单和输入信息变得更加轻松。
