# OpenAI co-founder Greg Brockman takes charge of product strategy
# OpenAI 联合创始人 Greg Brockman 接管产品战略

OpenAI co-founder and president Greg Brockman is officially taking the reins of the company’s product strategy, according to Wired. This seems to solidify an already-existing change, with Brockman overseeing OpenAI’s products on an interim basis while the company’s CEO of AGI deployment Fidji Simo is out on medical leave.

据《连线》（Wired）报道，OpenAI 联合创始人兼总裁 Greg Brockman 已正式接管公司的产品战略。这一举措似乎巩固了此前已经发生的变化——在公司 AGI 部署首席执行官 Fidji Simo 休病假期间，Brockman 一直在临时负责 OpenAI 的产品业务。

Wired also reports that in a staff memo, Brockman described plans to combine ChatGPT and its programming product Codex into a single unified experience. “We’re consolidating our product efforts to execute with maximum focus toward the agentic future, to win across both consumer and enterprise,” Brockman reportedly said.

《连线》还报道称，Brockman 在一份员工备忘录中描述了将 ChatGPT 及其编程产品 Codex 合并为单一统一体验的计划。据报道，Brockman 表示：“我们正在整合产品工作，以最大的专注力迈向智能体（agentic）未来，从而在消费者和企业市场双双获胜。”

OpenAI told TechCrunch that although Simo remains on medical leave she worked with Brockman on these changes. It also noted that OpenAI has already been talking about plans to combine ChatGPT, Codex, and its API into a single platform, with one core product team.

OpenAI 向 TechCrunch 表示，尽管 Simo 目前仍在休病假，但她曾与 Brockman 共同制定了这些变革。公司还指出，OpenAI 此前已经在讨论将 ChatGPT、Codex 和其 API 合并为一个单一平台，并由一个核心产品团队进行管理的计划。

At the end of last year, CEO Sam Altman declared a “code red” and said the company needs to refocus on the core ChatGPT experience. Since then, OpenAI has halted “side quests” including video generator Sora and OpenAI for Science.

去年年底，首席执行官 Sam Altman 发布了“红色警报”，并表示公司需要重新聚焦于 ChatGPT 的核心体验。自那时起，OpenAI 已经暂停了包括视频生成器 Sora 和“OpenAI for Science”在内的“支线任务”。
