# Stochastic Reset Pathfinding: Path-Level Regret for Cascading Bandits over Graph Paths
# 随机重置寻路：图路径上级联多臂老虎机的路径级遗憾分析

We introduce Stochastic Reset Pathfinding (SRP), an episodic learning problem on a known directed graph with unknown stationary edge success probabilities. In each episode, the agent commits to a source-to-goal path, and any edge failure during execution resets it to the source. SRP captures settings such as entanglement distribution in quantum repeater networks, payment routing on the Lightning Network, and delivery in unreliable mesh networks.

我们引入了随机重置寻路（Stochastic Reset Pathfinding, SRP），这是一个在已知有向图上进行的片段式学习问题，其中边的成功概率是未知的平稳分布。在每个片段中，智能体选择一条从起点到终点的路径；如果在执行过程中发生任何边故障，智能体将被重置回起点。SRP 涵盖了诸如量子中继网络中的纠缠分发、闪电网络中的支付路由以及不可靠网状网络中的数据传输等应用场景。

We show that the global-reset structure makes the optimal policy open-loop, placing SRP within the combinatorial cascading bandit (CCB) framework. We propose a Log-Dijkstra meta-algorithm with UCB (PathUCB) and Thompson Sampling (PathTS) instantiations. Our main technical result is a path-level regret bound for PathUCB that decomposes regret over suboptimal paths via a per-path complexity C(pi) combining each edge's prefix and suffix reliability.

我们证明了全局重置结构使得最优策略表现为开环策略，从而将 SRP 置于组合级联多臂老虎机（CCB）框架内。我们提出了一种 Log-Dijkstra 元算法，并给出了基于置信上限（PathUCB）和汤普森采样（PathTS）的具体实现。我们的主要技术成果是 PathUCB 的路径级遗憾界，该界通过结合每条边前缀和后缀可靠性的路径复杂度 C(pi)，将遗憾分解到各个次优路径上。

The bound is complementary to the edge-level CCB bound and more informative on structured graphs with polynomially many source-to-goal paths. Experiments on quantum-network, layered-DAG, grid-world, and Erdos-Renyi domains support the theory and show that PathTS typically achieves the best empirical performance among the algorithms tested.

该界限与边级 CCB 界限互补，在具有多项式数量起点到终点路径的结构化图上更具信息量。在量子网络、分层有向无环图（DAG）、网格世界和 Erdos-Renyi 图等领域的实验支持了这一理论，并表明 PathTS 在所测试的算法中通常能达到最佳的经验性能。

We then exhibit an adversarial instance on which PathTS fails to converge, consistent with a known exponential obstruction for combinatorial Thompson Sampling on multiplicative-reward problems. We recommend PathTS as the practical default while cautioning that adversarial instances exist.

随后，我们展示了一个 PathTS 无法收敛的对抗性实例，这与组合汤普森采样在乘性奖励问题上已知的指数级障碍相一致。我们建议将 PathTS 作为实际应用中的默认选择，同时也提醒用户注意对抗性实例的存在。
