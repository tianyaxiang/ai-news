# An Empirical Study of Handcrafted Feature Learning and Convolutional Neural Networks for Facial Expression Recognition
# 手工特征学习与卷积神经网络在面部表情识别中的实证研究

**Abstract:** Facial expression recognition is an important computer vision task with applications in human--computer interaction, mental health monitoring, driver alert systems, and behavioral analysis. 
**摘要：** 面部表情识别是一项重要的计算机视觉任务，在人机交互、心理健康监测、驾驶员预警系统和行为分析等领域有着广泛的应用。

While convolutional neural networks (CNNs) dominate modern facial expression recognition, handcrafted feature descriptors such as Histogram of Oriented Gradients (HOG) and Local Binary Patterns (LBP) remain useful classical baselines. 
尽管卷积神经网络（CNN）在现代面部表情识别中占据主导地位，但诸如方向梯度直方图（HOG）和局部二值模式（LBP）等手工特征描述符仍然是实用的经典基准。

This study compares HOG with Support Vector Machine (SVM), LBP with Logistic Regression, and a lightweight CNN across three facial expression datasets: FER-2013, CK+, and KDEF. 
本研究在 FER-2013、CK+ 和 KDEF 三个面部表情数据集上，对比了“HOG + 支持向量机（SVM）”、“LBP + 逻辑回归”以及轻量级 CNN 的表现。

The results show that CNNs achieve the best overall performance, particularly on more complex data, while HOG performs strongly in controlled environments. LBP performs poorly across all datasets. 
结果表明，CNN 在整体性能上表现最佳，尤其是在处理复杂数据时；而 HOG 在受控环境下表现强劲。LBP 在所有数据集上的表现均不理想。

The study highlights that dataset complexity significantly affects performance and that robust feature learning is essential for real-world facial expression recognition.
该研究强调，数据集的复杂性会显著影响模型性能，且稳健的特征学习对于现实世界中的面部表情识别至关重要。
