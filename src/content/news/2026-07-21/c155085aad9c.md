# Training-Free Open-Vocabulary 3D Point-Cloud Segmentation on the Generalized Few-Shot Benchmark
# 基于广义少样本基准的免训练开放词汇 3D 点云分割

**Abstract:** Generalized few-shot 3D point-cloud segmentation (GFS-PCS) asks a model to segment a scene into many base classes seen at training time and a set of novel classes. The state of the art reaches novel classes by reconciling a dense but noisy 3D vision-language prior with the few-shot support, but it pays for this with base 3D labels, per-episode training, and the support annotations themselves.

**摘要：** 广义少样本 3D 点云分割（GFS-PCS）要求模型将场景分割为训练时见过的多个基类以及一组新类。目前的最先进方法通过协调密集但带有噪声的 3D 视觉-语言先验与少样本支持来实现对新类的识别，但其代价是需要基类 3D 标签、每回合（per-episode）训练以及支持集标注。

We ask how far the same reconciliation can go with none of these: no training, no 3D labels, and not even the few-shot support. We pair a frozen 3D vision-language model (RegionPLC) as a dense prior with a frozen promptable concept segmenter (SAM3), prompted by the bare novel class names and lifted from posed RGB views, and reconcile the two by cross-view consistency: a point becomes novel only when enough of the views that see it agree.

我们探讨了在完全不使用这些资源的情况下，这种协调机制能达到什么程度：即无训练、无 3D 标签，甚至无需少样本支持。我们将一个冻结的 3D 视觉-语言模型（RegionPLC）作为密集先验，与一个冻结的可提示概念分割器（SAM3）配对；通过原始的新类名称进行提示，并从具有位姿的 RGB 视图中进行提升（lifted），通过跨视图一致性来协调两者：只有当足够多的观测视图达成一致时，一个点才会被判定为新类。

On the ScanNet200 GFS-PCS benchmark this fully training-free, open-vocabulary pipeline improves novel mIoU by +2.6 over the training-free dense prior while holding base accuracy within 0.5, and recovers a third (33%) of the novel-class gap to the trained state of the art that uses far more supervision. We further show that injecting the few-shot support into the pipeline, as a fusion gate and as a prototypical dense classifier, adds nothing over consistency alone and in fact degrades it through the classifier, which is why the method needs no support at all.

在 ScanNet200 GFS-PCS 基准测试中，这一完全免训练的开放词汇流水线将新类的 mIoU 提高了 +2.6，同时将基类准确率保持在 0.5 以内，并弥补了与使用更多监督信息的训练型最先进方法之间三分之一（33%）的新类性能差距。我们进一步证明，将少样本支持作为融合门控或原型密集分类器注入流水线，并不能比单纯的一致性带来任何提升，反而会因分类器导致性能下降，这就是该方法完全不需要支持集的原因。

On the harder ScanNet++ benchmark, where the dense prior is far weaker on novel classes, the same pipeline nearly doubles novel mIoU (+15.7, from 16.2 to 31.9) at a 1.7 base cost, lifting the harmonic mean from 21.5 to 31.1.

在难度更高的 ScanNet++ 基准测试中（该测试中密集先验在新类上的表现较弱），同样的流水线在 1.7 的基类成本下，使新类 mIoU 几乎翻倍（+15.7，从 16.2 提升至 31.9），并将调和平均值从 21.5 提升至 31.1。
