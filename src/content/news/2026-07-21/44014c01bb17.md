### Backdrop

**Launching today**
今天正式发布

**AI Coworkers that run your projects and operations**
能够运行您的项目与运营的 AI 同事

**AI made execution faster. The bottleneck has shifted to deciding what to build while the knowledge behind those decisions is scattered across people, tools, and AI chats.**
人工智能加快了执行速度。现在的瓶颈已转移到“决定构建什么”上，而支撑这些决策的知识却分散在不同的人员、工具和 AI 对话中。

**Backdrop provides AI coworkers for projects and operations that understand your company, work with your team, and build shared company context.**
Backdrop 为项目和运营提供 AI 同事，它们能够了解您的公司，与您的团队协作，并构建共享的公司背景知识。

**Across Slack, GitHub, Linear, Notion, Asana, Google Workspace and more, they synthesize customer feedback, create plans and specs, manage tickets, draft documents, and keep work moving.**
通过整合 Slack、GitHub、Linear、Notion、Asana、Google Workspace 等平台，它们能够综合客户反馈、创建计划与规范、管理工单、起草文档，并确保工作持续推进。
