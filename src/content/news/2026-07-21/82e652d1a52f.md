# GS-RealBlur: A Flexible Data Acquisition Framework for Real-World Image Deblurring
# GS-RealBlur：一种用于真实世界图像去模糊的灵活数据采集框架

**Abstract:** High-quality, large-scale paired data is essential for training learning-based image deblurring models. However, synthetic blurry images generally lack realism, while real-world captured images require complex and inflexible camera systems. In this work, we propose GS-RealBlur, a data acquisition framework for real-world image deblurring, achieving both blur realism and acquisition flexibility.

**摘要：** 高质量、大规模的成对数据对于训练基于学习的图像去模糊模型至关重要。然而，合成的模糊图像通常缺乏真实感，而真实世界中拍摄的图像则需要复杂且不灵活的相机系统。在这项工作中，我们提出了 GS-RealBlur，这是一种用于真实世界图像去模糊的数据采集框架，同时实现了模糊的真实感和采集的灵活性。

Specifically, we use a handheld camera to capture blurry images, and deploy a gimbal to densely capture sharp images of the same scene. We reconstruct the 3D representation of sharp images and calibrate the camera pose of each blurry frame within this 3D. The image rendered from this 3D according to the pose serves as the sharp counterpart.

具体而言，我们使用手持相机拍摄模糊图像，并部署云台（gimbal）对同一场景进行密集拍摄以获取清晰图像。我们重建了清晰图像的 3D 表示，并在该 3D 空间内校准了每一帧模糊图像的相机位姿。根据位姿从该 3D 模型中渲染出的图像即作为对应的清晰图像。

To better align the rendered image with the blurry image, we introduce a Blur-aware Pose Refinement (BPR) module that refines the pose using appearance consistency and centroid alignment constraints. Leveraging GS-RealBlur, we construct a high-quality and diverse dataset. Extensive experiments demonstrate that a deblurring model trained on our dataset achieves superior generalization performance across various real-world deblurring benchmarks, consistently outperforming models trained on existing synthetic and real-world datasets. The code and dataset will be made publicly available.

为了更好地将渲染图像与模糊图像对齐，我们引入了一个模糊感知位姿细化（Blur-aware Pose Refinement, BPR）模块，该模块利用外观一致性和质心对齐约束来优化位姿。利用 GS-RealBlur，我们构建了一个高质量且多样化的数据集。大量实验表明，在我们数据集上训练的去模糊模型在各种真实世界去模糊基准测试中表现出卓越的泛化能力，始终优于在现有合成和真实世界数据集上训练的模型。代码和数据集将公开提供。
