# LG’s glossy OLED gaming monitor is rare to find under $400
# LG 的镜面 OLED 游戏显示器罕见跌破 400 美元

If you’ve been thinking about upgrading your gaming monitor, LG’s 27-inch 27GX704A-B pairs a glossy WOLED panel with a fast refresh rate, and it’s currently on sale for $379.99 (about $70 off) at Amazon and directly from LG, marking a new low price. It originally launched at $799.99, but has been available for under $500 since May.

如果您一直在考虑升级游戏显示器，LG 的 27 英寸 27GX704A-B 将镜面 WOLED 面板与高刷新率相结合，目前在亚马逊和 LG 官网的售价为 379.99 美元（优惠约 70 美元），创下历史新低。该产品最初发布时售价为 799.99 美元，但自 5 月以来价格已降至 500 美元以下。

The monitor offers a 1440p resolution and a glossy coating, which isn’t a combination of specs we often see at this price. Most OLED monitors under $400 use QD-OLED panels, which can give blacks a slight bluish tint. LG’s WOLED monitor delivers truer-looking blacks while the glossy coating helps colors appear richer and more vibrant.

这款显示器提供 1440p 分辨率和镜面涂层，这种规格组合在这一价位并不常见。大多数 400 美元以下的 OLED 显示器使用 QD-OLED 面板，这可能会使黑色呈现出轻微的蓝调。而 LG 的 WOLED 显示器能提供更真实的黑色，同时镜面涂层有助于让色彩看起来更丰富、更鲜艳。

It doesn’t sacrifice speed, either. The monitor boasts a fast 240Hz refresh rate with variable refresh rate support (Nvidia G-Sync compatible and AMD FreeSync Premium Pro), along with a fast 0.03ms response time to keep games looking sharp in motion.

它在速度方面也毫不妥协。该显示器拥有 240Hz 的高刷新率，支持可变刷新率（兼容 Nvidia G-Sync 和 AMD FreeSync Premium Pro），并具备 0.03ms 的极速响应时间，确保游戏在动态画面下依然清晰锐利。

It comes with a solid array of ports for the price, with two HDMI 2.1 ports, DisplayPort 1.4, and USB-A ports, making it easy to connect a PC, consoles, and accessories. Rounding things out, the stand is fully adjustable, too, allowing you to raise, lower, tilt, swivel, or rotate the display into portrait mode for a more comfortable setup. Or, because it has a VESA mount, you can yank it off and toss it onto a monitor arm of your choice.

以这个价格来说，它配备了相当不错的接口阵列，包括两个 HDMI 2.1 接口、一个 DisplayPort 1.4 接口和多个 USB-A 接口，方便连接电脑、游戏主机和外设。此外，其支架支持全方位调节，您可以升降、倾斜、左右旋转或将显示器旋转至纵向模式，以获得更舒适的使用体验。或者，由于它支持 VESA 壁挂安装，您也可以将其拆下并安装在您选择的显示器支架臂上。
