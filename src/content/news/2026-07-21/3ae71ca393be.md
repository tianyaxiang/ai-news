### Kogvio

**Launching today**
今天正式发布。

**Understand anything online without leaving the page.**
无需离开当前页面，即可理解网络上的任何内容。

**Every time I didn't understand something online, I had the same workflow: Copy. Open ChatGPT. Paste. Come back. It completely broke my focus. So I built Kogvio.**
每当我在网上遇到不懂的内容时，我总是重复同样的流程：复制、打开 ChatGPT、粘贴、再切回原页面。这完全打断了我的思路。因此，我开发了 Kogvio。

**Highlight anything on a webpage or PDF, ask questions instantly in context, and continue learning without leaving the page. Whether it's documentation, research papers, medical textbooks, or technical diagrams, Kogvio helps you understand—not just search.**
只需高亮网页或 PDF 中的任何内容，即可在语境中即时提问，并在不离开页面的情况下继续学习。无论是技术文档、研究论文、医学教材还是技术图表，Kogvio 都能帮助你真正理解内容，而不仅仅是进行搜索。
