# Structure of the Circular-Dyadic Convolution Error
# 循环-二进卷积误差的结构

**Abstract:** Dyadic and circular convolution can both be computed in $O(N\log N)$ time using the Hadamard transform and the FFT-computed discrete Fourier transform (DFT), respectively. The Hadamard transform is preferable for its real-valued sign flips, yet its substitution for the DFT introduces algebraic error.

**摘要：** 二进卷积（Dyadic convolution）和循环卷积（Circular convolution）分别可以通过哈达玛变换（Hadamard transform）和基于快速傅里叶变换（FFT）的离散傅里叶变换（DFT）在 $O(N\log N)$ 时间内完成计算。哈达玛变换因其仅涉及实数符号翻转而更具优势，但用它替代 DFT 会引入代数误差。

We present three complementary results that characterize this error. First, we identify exact error cancellation: two input and two output positions are universally error-free, and no reordering of the output can eliminate this error.

我们提出了三个互补的研究结果来刻画这种误差。首先，我们确定了精确的误差抵消现象：两个输入位置和两个输出位置在任何情况下均无误差，且通过对输出进行重排序也无法消除该误差。

Second, the error operator is nearly full rank, while its null space has only logarithmic dimension. Third, the expected error is governed by a single alignment scalar, with a closed-form expression obtained by averaging over random filters.

其次，误差算子几乎是满秩的，而其零空间仅具有对数维数。第三，期望误差由单一的对齐标量（alignment scalar）控制，通过对随机滤波器进行平均，可以获得该误差的闭式表达式。

In general, the substitution error asymptotically doubles the output energy, except for filters in the universal zero-error subspace, which incur no error. Collectively, these results show that the substitution error is structured, predictable, and governed by alignment.

总体而言，这种替代误差在渐近意义上会使输出能量翻倍，但处于通用零误差子空间中的滤波器除外，这些滤波器不会产生误差。综上所述，这些结果表明，这种替代误差具有结构性、可预测性，并受对齐方式的支配。
