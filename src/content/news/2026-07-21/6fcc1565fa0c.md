### Reasoning-Guided Part-Level Visual Grounding via Reinforcement Learning
### 基于强化学习的推理引导式部件级视觉定位

**Abstract:** Multimodal large language models (MLLMs) ground whole objects well from free-form language queries, but they struggle when the query names a part rather than the object. We trace this to a missing object-part hierarchy, since parts are localized in the same single step used for objects. 

**摘要：** 多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）能够很好地根据自由形式的语言查询定位完整物体，但当查询涉及物体的“部件”而非整个物体时，它们往往表现不佳。我们认为这归因于模型缺乏“物体-部件”的层级结构，因为目前的定位方法将部件与物体视为同一层级，在单一步骤中进行定位。

We propose Object-Part Hierarchical Reflective Grounding (OP-HRG), a coarse-to-fine reasoning-guided grounding strategy that first localizes the parent object and then the part within it. A self-check then reflects on the result, with an extension to re-encode the predicted crop to inspect the region it is correcting. 

我们提出了“物体-部件层级反射定位”（OP-HRG），这是一种由推理引导的从粗到细的定位策略：首先定位父级物体，随后在其内部定位具体部件。随后，系统会进行自我检查以反思定位结果，并通过扩展功能重新编码预测的裁剪区域，从而对正在修正的区域进行审查。

We introduce a part-aware GRPO framework to train our pipeline with stage-wise rewards. A 4B model trained this way outperforms 7B grounding LLMs and SAM3 across PascalPart, PartImageNet, and InstructPart, and transfers to reasoning segmentation.

我们引入了一个具备部件感知能力的 GRPO 框架，通过分阶段奖励来训练我们的流水线。实验证明，以此方式训练的 4B 参数模型在 PascalPart、PartImageNet 和 InstructPart 数据集上的表现均优于 7B 参数的定位大模型及 SAM3，并可迁移应用于推理分割任务。
