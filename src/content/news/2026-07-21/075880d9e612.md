# Get Borderlands 3, Risk of Rain 2 and 13 other great PC games for $15
# 以 15 美元入手《无主之地 3》、《雨中冒险 2》及其他 13 款精选 PC 游戏

The aptly-named “2K Megahits 2026 Bundle” from Humble includes 15 Steam games for $15. It’s a smattering of the publisher 2K’s biggest games of the 2010s, including BioShock Infinite, Risk of Rain 2, Borderlands 3, and XCOM: Enemy Unknown. The bundle will be available until August 7th, though the bundle page notes that keys for some games are already starting to run low. All of the games work on Windows, while some support MacOS and SteamOS.

Humble 推出的“2K Megahits 2026 Bundle”正如其名，仅需 15 美元即可获得 15 款 Steam 游戏。该合集汇集了发行商 2K 在 2010 年代最重磅的作品，包括《生化奇兵：无限》、《雨中冒险 2》、《无主之地 3》和《幽浮：未知敌人》。该合集将持续发售至 8 月 7 日，不过页面显示部分游戏的激活码已开始告急。所有游戏均支持 Windows 系统，部分游戏也支持 MacOS 和 SteamOS。

One of my personal favorite games, Risk of Rain 2, is included in the bundle. In the third-person roguelike, you’ll face off against increasingly difficult waves of enemies, fighting to stay alive as long as you can and collecting power-ups and useful perks before extracting to the next zone to do it all again. It’s just challenging enough to make victory satisfying, and fun to play alone or with friends. You also get 2012’s XCOM: Enemy Unknown, a legendary turn-based strategy game that’s usually $15 on its own, to give you a sense of the bundle’s value.

我个人最喜欢的游戏之一《雨中冒险 2》也包含在内。在这款第三人称 Roguelike 游戏中，你将面对一波又一波难度递增的敌人，在努力求生的同时收集强化道具和实用技能，随后撤离到下一个区域重复挑战。游戏的难度恰到好处，让胜利充满成就感，无论是独自游玩还是与好友联机都乐趣十足。此外，你还能获得 2012 年的《幽浮：未知敌人》，这款传奇的回合制策略游戏单买通常就需要 15 美元，由此可见该合集的超高性价比。

The bundle also notably includes three games from the Borderlands universe. Borderlands 3 Special Edition is a traditional looter-shooter FPS, Tiny Tina’s Wonderlands adds RPG elements like an overworld map and spellcasting, and Tales from the Borderlands is an interactive episodic visual novel from Telltale Games. You’ll also get a variety of shooters, including BioShock Infinite, Duke Nukem 3D, Bulletstorm, plus Hidden and Dangerous from the turn of the millenium.

该合集还特别收录了三款“无主之地”系列游戏。《无主之地 3 特别版》是一款传统的刷宝射击 FPS 游戏；《小缇娜的奇幻之地》加入了大地图和施法系统等 RPG 元素；而《无主之地传说》则是 Telltale Games 开发的互动式章节视觉小说。你还将获得多款射击游戏，包括《生化奇兵：无限》、《毁灭公爵 3D》、《子弹风暴》，以及千禧年之交的经典之作《隐秘与危险》。

Part of the proceeds from the bundle benefit Covenant House, an organization that benefits young people facing homelessness, as well as the publisher and Humble. You can adjust how you pledge is split between the beneficiaries by clicking the button that says “adjust donation” under the checkout button.

该合集的部分收益将捐赠给 Covenant House（一家帮助面临无家可归问题的年轻人的组织），其余部分则归发行商和 Humble 所有。你可以通过点击结账按钮下方的“调整捐款”按钮，自行分配捐款在各受益方之间的比例。
