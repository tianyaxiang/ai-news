The robovac is one of our top picks if you’re on a budget, with good AI-powered obstacle detection and navigation that helps it clean around clutter without getting stuck. It also boasts automatic carpet detection and a mop that can lift itself over rugs, so you don’t need to worry about carpets getting wet. The flat vibrating mop pad also impressed us in testing and is particularly gentle on delicate floors, able to scrub away dirt effectively without leaving them soaked. When it finishes cleaning, the included dock automatically empties the robovac’s dustbin and recharges the bot, reducing the amount of maintenance required.

对于预算有限的用户来说，这款扫地机器人是我们首选的推荐产品之一。它具备出色的 AI 避障和导航功能，能够绕过杂物进行清洁，避免被困。它还拥有自动地毯识别功能，拖布在经过地毯时可以自动抬升，因此您无需担心地毯被弄湿。在测试中，其平板震动拖布的表现也令我们印象深刻，它对娇贵的地面非常友好，能够有效擦除污垢而不会让地面过度潮湿。清洁完成后，配套的基站会自动清空尘盒并为机器人充电，大大减少了日常维护的工作量。

Of course, there are some compromises Roborock made to keep costs down. Unlike Roborock’s much pricier $1,600 Saros 20, the Q10 S5 Plus can’t climb taller thresholds, and its 10,000Pa suction isn’t nearly as powerful as the Saros’ 36,000Pa system. The dock also lacks conveniences like automatic mop washing and water-tank refilling, meaning you’ll still need to handle those tasks yourself. Still, for under $300, it’s hard to complain given everything else the Q10 S5 Plus brings to the table.

当然，为了控制成本，石头科技在 Q10 S5 Plus 上做出了一些妥协。与售价高达 1600 美元的更高端机型 Saros 20 不同，Q10 S5 Plus 无法越过较高的门槛，其 10,000Pa 的吸力也远不及 Saros 的 36,000Pa 系统。此外，其基站也缺少自动洗拖布和自动补水等便捷功能，这意味着您仍需手动处理这些任务。不过，考虑到 Q10 S5 Plus 提供的其他功能，在 300 美元以下的价位段，它依然是一款无可挑剔的产品。
