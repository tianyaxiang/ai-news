# i1: A Simple and Fully Open Recipe for Strong Text-to-Image Models
# i1：构建强大文生图模型的简单且完全开源方案

**Abstract:** Diffusion models have consistently driven progress in text-to-image generation. However, it is challenging to attribute recent progress to specific modeling and data choices: state-of-the-art open-weight models provide limited ablations, and do not disclose their training data and full training details. The research community needs fully open (weights, data, and code) models as a foundation for further research; yet existing fully open models still fall significantly short of leading models in performance.

**摘要：** 扩散模型持续推动着文生图生成领域的进步。然而，目前很难将近期的进展归因于特定的模型架构或数据选择：最先进的开源权重模型往往缺乏充分的消融实验，且未公开其训练数据及完整的训练细节。研究界需要完全开源（包括权重、数据和代码）的模型作为后续研究的基础；然而，现有的完全开源模型在性能上仍远落后于领先模型。

In this project, we conduct a systematic investigation of the modeling and data design choices in text-to-image diffusion training and inference with 300+ controlled experiments totaling 700K+ TPU v6e hours. Our experiments highlight several empirical findings (e.g., equal weighting is a strong default for mixing curated datasets) and simple design decisions (e.g., larger text encoder adapters improve performance with minimal added parameters) for training strong models.

本项目通过 300 多项受控实验（总计耗时超过 70 万个 TPU v6e 小时），对文生图扩散模型的训练与推理中的模型架构及数据设计选择进行了系统性研究。我们的实验总结出了一些实证发现（例如，在混合精选数据集时，等权重分配是一个强有力的默认策略）以及简单的设计决策（例如，使用更大的文本编码器适配器可以在增加极少参数的情况下提升性能），为训练强大模型提供了指导。

Guided by these insights, we train i1, a 3B-parameter text-to-image diffusion model using only publicly available datasets. i1 is competitive with leading models on five representative benchmarks (GenEval, DPG, PRISM, CVTG-2K, and LongText), and outperforms the best existing fully open model by 29.5 absolute percentage points on average. We provide the i1 checkpoints, training and inference code, and the data processing pipeline. Together, our findings and the i1 recipe establish a practical foundation for future open research in text-to-image diffusion models.

在这些洞察的指导下，我们仅使用公开数据集训练了 i1——一个拥有 30 亿参数的文生图扩散模型。i1 在五个代表性基准测试（GenEval、DPG、PRISM、CVTG-2K 和 LongText）中表现出与领先模型相当的竞争力，且平均性能比现有的最佳完全开源模型高出 29.5 个百分点。我们提供了 i1 的检查点（checkpoints）、训练与推理代码以及数据处理流水线。总而言之，我们的研究发现和 i1 方案为未来文生图扩散模型的开放研究奠定了实践基础。
