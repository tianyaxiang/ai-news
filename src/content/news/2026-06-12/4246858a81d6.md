# Semantic Segmentation of Node and Edge Diagrams for Assistive Technology
# 用于辅助技术的节点与边图语义分割

**Abstract:** In this paper, we present a novel set of related models for semantic segmentation of node-link diagrams. These diagrams are frequently used to represent mathematical graphs, relationships between concepts, and flowcharts. 

**摘要：** 在本文中，我们提出了一套用于节点-链接图（node-link diagrams）语义分割的新型相关模型。这类图表常用于表示数学图形、概念间的关系以及流程图。

Such diagrams are difficult to access non-visually; while some assistive interfaces have been designed for node-link diagrams, they rely upon a machine-readable representation of the diagram, whereas such diagrams will generally be made available as bitmap images. 

对于非视觉用户而言，此类图表难以获取；尽管目前已有一些针对节点-链接图设计的辅助界面，但它们通常依赖于图表的机器可读格式，而现实中这些图表大多以位图（bitmap images）形式存在。

Our compact deep learning models show excellent quantitative and qualitative performance on a large synthetic dataset of node-link diagrams, reaching per-pixel accuracy over 93%.

我们的轻量级深度学习模型在一个大型节点-链接图合成数据集上表现出了优异的定量和定性性能，像素级准确率超过了 93%。
