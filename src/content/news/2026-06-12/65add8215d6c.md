# Amazon’s Echo Hub gets a customizable new look and Ring’s AI features
# 亚马逊 Echo Hub 迎来全新可定制界面及 Ring AI 功能

Resize and rearrange sections on the smart display however you like.
你可以随心所欲地调整智能显示屏上各个板块的大小和布局。

Amazon’s rolling out a free software update for Echo Hub devices that gives the home screen a much-needed update to the interface it launched with in 2024. It had already added Alexa Plus AI support, but the new interface has a cleaner, fully customizable layout that fits more smart home info and controls on the screen than the previous version.
亚马逊正在为 Echo Hub 设备推送免费软件更新，对其 2024 年发布时的初始主屏幕界面进行了急需的升级。此前该设备已增加了 Alexa Plus AI 支持，而此次的新界面布局更加简洁且完全可定制，相比旧版本，它能在屏幕上容纳更多的智能家居信息和控制选项。

The Echo Hub is also getting access to Ring AI’s Video Search feature that lets you use natural language to search through your smart home camera footage, as well as Alexa Plus summaries of detected camera events.
Echo Hub 还将接入 Ring AI 的视频搜索（Video Search）功能，允许用户使用自然语言搜索智能家居摄像头的录像，并支持通过 Alexa Plus 对检测到的摄像头事件进行摘要总结。

These are the five new features Amazon highlighted for the Echo Hub:
亚马逊为 Echo Hub 重点介绍了以下五项新功能：

**Organize by room** - Organize your dashboard by room or function (e.g., “Bedroom,” “Downstairs”, “Climate”). Navigate to your existing groups using the bottom bar, select a group and long press to edit. Add or remove devices, drag to reorder them, and control all devices in a group simultaneously with a single tap.
**按房间整理** - 按房间或功能（例如“卧室”、“楼下”、“气候”）来组织你的仪表盘。通过底部栏导航至现有群组，选择一个群组并长按即可进行编辑。你可以添加或移除设备、拖动以重新排序，并能通过单次点击同时控制群组内的所有设备。

**Create groups** - Navigate to the “Add group” button along the bottom bar, pick a name and add devices to that group. Your new group is created and will be accessible via voice control and app as well.
**创建群组** - 点击底部栏的“添加群组”按钮，选择名称并添加设备。新群组创建完成后，即可通过语音控制和应用程序进行访问。

**Arrange and resize sections and tiles** – Easily add, remove and arrange sections with simple drag-and-drop actions, allowing you to create a layout that matches how you use your smart home. You can also resize the device tiles to make commonly used ones bigger.
**排列并调整板块与磁贴大小** – 通过简单的拖放操作即可轻松添加、移除和排列板块，让你能够创建符合个人使用习惯的智能家居布局。你还可以调整设备磁贴的大小，将常用的设备磁贴放大。

**Access granular device settings** – Tap the 3 dots on any connected device to access more detailed controls. Try tapping your lights to access precise dimming (0-100%) and color adjustment or use the color wheel on compatible bulbs to select from a variety of colors. Hit the power button on each tile for instant on/off control.
**访问精细的设备设置** – 点击任何已连接设备上的三个点，即可访问更详细的控制选项。例如，点击灯光可以进行精确的亮度调节（0-100%）和色彩调整，或者在兼容的灯泡上使用色轮选择多种颜色。点击每个磁贴上的电源按钮，即可实现即时的开关控制。

**Quick access to common Routines** - Access your most frequently used Routines directly from the home screen using the automations section or the automations tab and trigger them with a single tap.
**快速访问常用例程（Routines）** - 通过主屏幕的自动化板块或自动化标签页，直接访问你最常用的例程，并实现一键触发。
