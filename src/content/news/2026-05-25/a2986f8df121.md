### What’s Changing with Vivado Tool Licensing
### Vivado 工具许可的变更

AMD Vivado™ Design Suite is evolving to a new tiered licensing model starting with the 2026.1 release. This gives more licensing options at more price points, providing flexibility to pay only for the device families and features that you need.
AMD Vivado™ 设计套件将从 2026.1 版本开始演进为全新的分级许可模式。这将提供更多价格区间的许可选项，让您能够灵活地仅为您所需的器件系列和功能付费。

### OS SUPPORT (Key Change)
### 操作系统支持（关键变更）

According to the official comparison table, the **BASIC** tier (the free version) now only supports **Windows®**, while **Linux®** support is restricted to the **CORE, PRO, ENTERPRISE, and GOLD** tiers.
根据官方对比表显示，**BASIC** 层级（即免费版）现在仅支持 **Windows®**，而 **Linux®** 支持仅限于 **CORE、PRO、ENTERPRISE 和 GOLD** 层级。

### FAQ: What You Need to Know
### 常见问题解答：您需要了解的内容

**Will the Vivado Design Suite still be free?**
Yes. The BASIC tier remains free with annual renewal.
**Vivado 设计套件还会免费吗？**
是的。BASIC 层级在年度续期后依然免费。

**Do I need to switch to a subscription?**
No. Perpetual options are still available, and existing Enterprise licenses remain valid.
**我需要转向订阅制吗？**
不需要。永久许可选项依然可用，现有的企业版许可证仍然有效。

**What happens if a subscription expires?**
Active tool use will be suspended until renewal. AMD will send multiple reminders about renewal in advance.
**如果订阅过期会怎样？**
工具的使用将被暂停，直到完成续期。AMD 会提前发送多次续期提醒。
