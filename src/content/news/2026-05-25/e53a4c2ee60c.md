### What are AI Agents?
### 什么是 AI 智能体？

But first, what exactly are AI Agents? AI Agents are software programs that are able to not only answer specific questions like simple chatbots, but they go a step further. They are able to answer questions and make autonomous decisions, as well as create things and get tasks done! They can observe, think, decide, and act to complete tasks with minimal human input.
首先，AI 智能体到底是什么？AI 智能体不仅能像简单的聊天机器人那样回答特定问题，而且更进一步。它们能够回答问题、做出自主决策，还能创造事物并完成任务！它们可以在极少的人工干预下，通过观察、思考、决策和行动来完成任务。

Suppose we want to buy a new laptop for heavy programming. We can ask the same question to both a chatbot and an AI Agent. The chatbot approach will be to suggest laptops for heavy programming and then respond to specific questions one by one. It waits for user input, has limited memory, and works mostly as a text generator.
假设我们想买一台用于重度编程的新笔记本电脑。我们可以向聊天机器人和 AI 智能体提出同样的问题。聊天机器人的做法是建议几款适合重度编程的笔记本电脑，然后逐一回答具体问题。它等待用户输入，内存有限，主要作为文本生成器工作。

An AI Agent, on the other hand, takes goals and performs tasks automatically without the need to explicitly ask/direct to a specific feature. It researches, compares, plans, and analyzes requirements to make research-backed decisions. For our heavy programming laptop question, the chatbot will just answer in a single line, but the AI Agent will give us a comparison table, mention different products, their pricing, and pros and cons, and aid us in making the decision.
另一方面，AI 智能体接收目标并自动执行任务，无需明确要求或指向特定功能。它会研究、比较、规划和分析需求，从而做出有据可查的决策。对于我们关于重度编程笔记本电脑的问题，聊天机器人只会给出一行回答，而 AI 智能体会为我们提供对比表，列出不同产品、价格、优缺点，并辅助我们做出决定。

### How does an AI Agent work?
### AI 智能体是如何工作的？

The AI Agent is a smart program that is coded to fulfill a goal. Once we give it a task, the AI Agent first receives the request, breaks it down into smaller problems to address, and takes further input from the user if required through questions to properly understand and meet all requirements. It then uses appropriate tools like web searching, calculators, and its own memory to collect additional information, and analyzes this information carefully. It compares different options and curates the answer to the user’s needs.
AI 智能体是一个为了实现目标而编写的智能程序。一旦我们给它一个任务，AI 智能体首先会接收请求，将其分解为更小的待解决问题，并在必要时通过提问获取用户的进一步输入，以正确理解并满足所有需求。然后，它会使用网络搜索、计算器及其自身内存等适当工具来收集额外信息，并仔细分析这些信息。它会比较不同的选项，并根据用户的需求整理出答案。
