# Record Club is trying to be Letterboxd for music nerds
# Record Club 致力于成为音乐爱好者的 Letterboxd

There isn’t really a solid equivalent to Goodreads or Letterboxd for music lovers, but Record Club is aiming to change that. Yes, we have Rate Your Music, but its interface is crowded, and it feels more geared towards longer-form reviews than cataloging your listening habits and connecting with other fans. Record Club is clean and modern, with a streamlined interface that’s quite similar to Letterboxd.

对于音乐爱好者来说，目前还没有一个真正能与 Goodreads 或 Letterboxd 相媲美的平台，但 Record Club 正试图改变这一现状。虽然我们有 Rate Your Music，但它的界面过于拥挤，且更倾向于长篇评论，而非记录听歌习惯或与乐迷互动。Record Club 则简洁现代，其精简的界面与 Letterboxd 非常相似。

The basic features you’d expect from such a site are all there. You can rate and review records or mark them as listened to. You can also see what your friends are listening to and see what albums are trending with other users. There’s a spot on your profile to list your five favorite albums, plus five records you have in heavy rotation.

这类网站应有的基本功能它一应俱全。你可以对唱片进行评分和评论，或者将其标记为“已听”。你还可以查看朋友们正在听什么，以及其他用户中哪些专辑正流行。在个人资料页面，你可以列出自己最喜欢的五张专辑，以及五张近期循环播放的唱片。

You can also create custom lists (ranked or unranked) and share them — handy for tracking your top albums of the year, or putting together genre-specific crash courses. You can also add records to your queue, so you can keep track of albums you want to listen to, but haven’t gotten around to yet. (I’ll probably be making extensive use of that.)

你还可以创建自定义列表（可排序或无序）并进行分享——这对于追踪年度最佳专辑或整理特定流派的入门指南非常方便。此外，你还可以将唱片添加到队列中，以便记录那些你想听但还没来得及听的专辑。（我大概会频繁使用这个功能。）

You can follow your favorite artists as well as entire record labels. That makes it easy to stay on top of new artists on labels like 4AD, AD 93, Fire Talk, and Warp. Record Club pulls all of its data from the open-source music encyclopedia MusicBrainz. If you sign up, give me a follow, and see what I’m spinning on repeat this week.

你不仅可以关注喜爱的艺人，还能关注整个唱片厂牌。这让你能轻松掌握 4AD、AD 93、Fire Talk 和 Warp 等厂牌旗下新艺人的动态。Record Club 的所有数据均来自开源音乐百科全书 MusicBrainz。如果你注册了，记得关注我，看看我这周都在循环播放什么音乐。
