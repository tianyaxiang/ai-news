### Danish Pension Blacklists SpaceX over 'Catastrophic Governance'
### 丹麦养老基金将 SpaceX 列入黑名单，理由是“灾难性的公司治理”

A $25 billion Danish pension fund that earlier this year made headlines by ditching Treasuries as Donald Trump was threatening to seize Greenland now says it won’t touch SpaceX.
一家规模达 250 亿美元的丹麦养老基金曾因在唐纳德·特朗普威胁要“购买”格陵兰岛时抛售美国国债而登上头条，如今该基金表示，将不会投资 SpaceX。

The company, which is targeting a valuation of at least $1.8 trillion in its initial public offering, is not only “grossly overvalued” but also marred by a “catastrophic governance structure,” according to Anders Schelde, the chief investment officer of AkademikerPension.
AkademikerPension 首席投资官安德斯·谢尔德（Anders Schelde）表示，这家目标在首次公开募股（IPO）中实现至少 1.8 万亿美元估值的公司，不仅“估值严重过高”，而且还存在“灾难性的公司治理结构”问题。
