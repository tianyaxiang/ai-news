# Embodied3DBench: Benchmarking Low-Level Embodied Spatial Intelligence of Vision Language Models
# Embodied3DBench：视觉语言模型底层具身空间智能基准测试

**Abstract:** Are current Vision Language Models (VLMs) ready to comprehend and reason about complex embodied interactions in 3D environments? We introduce Embodied3DBench, a robot-centric benchmark targeting low-level spatial intelligence in embodied 3D environments. 

**摘要：** 现有的视觉语言模型（VLM）是否已经准备好理解并推理 3D 环境中复杂的具身交互了？我们推出了 Embodied3DBench，这是一个以机器人为中心的基准测试，旨在评估具身 3D 环境中的底层空间智能。

To systematically evaluate these foundational perceptual capabilities, the benchmark includes 6 task categories divided into two core groups: Spatial Structural Understanding (Grounding, Spatial Relation Prediction, and Multi-view Correspondence) and Interaction-Oriented Perception (Affordance Prediction, Grasp Point Prediction, and Trajectory Prediction). The benchmark spans 12 subcategories and contains over 21k high-quality question-answer pairs. 

为了系统地评估这些基础感知能力，该基准测试包含了 6 个任务类别，分为两个核心组：空间结构理解（定位、空间关系预测和多视图对应）以及交互导向感知（可供性预测、抓取点预测和轨迹预测）。该基准测试涵盖 12 个子类别，包含超过 2.1 万个高质量的问答对。

We evaluate 13 state-of-the-art models, and the results show that while current models exhibit relatively strong high-level spatial reasoning, such as understanding object-to-object positional relations, they remain fragile in interaction-oriented perception, highlighting a significant lack of robust 3D-aware interaction priors. 

我们评估了 13 个最先进的模型，结果显示，虽然当前模型在理解物体间位置关系等高层空间推理方面表现出相对较强的能力，但在交互导向感知方面仍然脆弱，这凸显了模型在稳健的 3D 感知交互先验方面的严重缺失。

To actively bridge this capability gap revealed by our benchmark, we further synthesize a large-scale training dataset comprising 1.3M QA pairs. Notably, fine-tuning on this dataset yields significant improvements in low-level spatial intelligence. 

为了积极弥补基准测试所揭示的能力差距，我们进一步合成了一个包含 130 万个问答对的大规模训练数据集。值得注意的是，在该数据集上进行微调，能够显著提升模型的底层空间智能。

Ultimately, Embodied3DBench fills a critical gap by providing both a systematic evaluation framework and a scalable data solution, setting a clear target for the development of interaction-aware multimodal systems.

最终，Embodied3DBench 通过提供系统化的评估框架和可扩展的数据解决方案，填补了关键空白，为开发具备交互感知能力的多模态系统设定了明确的目标。
