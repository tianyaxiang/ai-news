# Today is the last day to apply to speak at TechCrunch Disrupt 2026
# 今天是申请成为 TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 演讲嘉宾的最后一天

TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 returns October 13–15 to Moscone West in San Francisco — and applications to speak are open for just a few more hours. We’re inviting founders, investors, operators, and technology experts to apply for a chance to take the stage at one of the most influential tech events of the year.
TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 将于 10 月 13 日至 15 日重返旧金山 Moscone West 会展中心，距离演讲申请截止仅剩最后几个小时。我们诚邀创始人、投资者、运营专家和技术专家踊跃申请，争取在年度最具影响力的科技盛会之一上登台亮相。

More than 10,000 startup and VC leaders will gather at Disrupt 2026 to explore what’s next in AI, scaling, fintech, infrastructure, robotics, and the future of innovation. Applications close tonight at 11:59 p.m. PT. Apply now to share your expertise and help shape the conversations defining the tech industry.
超过 10,000 名初创企业和风险投资领域的领袖将齐聚 Disrupt 2026，共同探讨人工智能、规模化扩张、金融科技、基础设施、机器人技术以及创新未来的发展趋势。申请将于今晚太平洋时间 11:59 截止。立即申请，分享您的专业见解，并参与塑造定义科技行业的关键议题。

Pick your session format
选择您的会议形式

We’re looking for high-impact speakers to lead one of two session types:
我们正在寻找具有影响力的演讲者来主持以下两种会议形式之一：

Breakout Sessions: A 30-minute talk (up to 4 speakers, including a moderator) with a 20-minute audience Q&A. Capacity: 100 attendees.
分组会议（Breakout Sessions）：30 分钟的演讲（最多 4 位演讲者，含主持人），外加 20 分钟的观众问答环节。容纳人数：100 人。

Roundtables: A 30-minute speaker-led group discussion, designed for up to 40 participants. No slides or AV — just insight and conversation.
圆桌会议（Roundtables）：由演讲者引导的 30 分钟小组讨论，专为最多 40 名参与者设计。无需幻灯片或视听设备，仅专注于深度见解与交流。

How the application process works
申请流程说明

Each application will be carefully reviewed by our editorial team. Finalists will be selected for the Audience Choice vote — where TechCrunch readers choose which sessions make it to the Disrupt Stage. Learn more about speaking on Disrupt’s Call for Content page.
每一份申请都将由我们的编辑团队进行仔细审核。入围者将进入“观众选择”投票环节，由 TechCrunch 的读者决定哪些议题能够登上 Disrupt 的主舞台。如需了解更多关于演讲申请的信息，请访问 Disrupt 的“内容征集”（Call for Content）页面。

Lead the conversation at Disrupt 2026
在 Disrupt 2026 引领对话

If you have actionable insights, real-world experience, and a desire to contribute meaningfully to the tech ecosystem, we want to hear from you. Submit your application before today’s deadline.
如果您拥有可落地的见解、丰富的实战经验，并渴望为科技生态系统做出有意义的贡献，我们期待听到您的声音。请在今日截止日期前提交您的申请。
