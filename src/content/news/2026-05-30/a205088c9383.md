# Kiwibit’s AI-powered bird feeder is my new backyard buddy
# Kiwibit 的 AI 智能喂鸟器成了我的后院新伙伴

Earlier this month, I got my hands on the Kiwibit Bird Feeder Pro 4K AI Camera, and it has become my favorite backyard accessory. Setting it up is pretty straightforward. Multiple mounting options allow you to place the feeder on a pole, window ledge, or tree. Its dual seed compartments are designed for easy refills and cleaning. The solar panel on top ensures you don’t have to worry about batteries running low. Durability and camera quality are also strong points. Other specs include support for 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi, cloud storage, built-in two-way audio with a microphone and speaker, and a 130-degree wide-angle lens.

本月初，我入手了 Kiwibit Bird Feeder Pro 4K AI 摄像头喂鸟器，它已经成为我最喜欢的后院装备。它的安装非常简单，多种安装方式让你能将其固定在杆子、窗台或树上。双料仓设计方便补给和清洁，顶部的太阳能电池板让你无需担心电量不足。耐用性和摄像头画质也是它的亮点。其他规格包括支持 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi、云存储、内置麦克风和扬声器的双向音频，以及 130 度广角镜头。

As soon as I installed it in the backyard, I connected the feeder to the companion Kiwibit app on my phone. This is where you can be notified when a bird stops by, watch recordings, and track all the visits. A few weeks into testing is when the real fun started. My phone buzzed with a notification every time a new visitor showed up, and I found myself eagerly waiting for updates. Even on extremely rainy days, I managed to entice a few birds, including a stunning northern cardinal that I’ve now come to anticipate seeing every morning. As of this writing, the device has successfully recorded visits from six species.

安装好后，我立即将喂鸟器连接到了手机上的 Kiwibit 配套 App。通过这个 App，当有鸟儿造访时，我会收到通知，还可以观看录像并追踪所有的访问记录。测试几周后，真正的乐趣才开始。每当有新访客出现，我的手机就会震动提醒，我发现自己开始热切地等待更新。即使在雨天，我也能吸引到几只鸟，包括一只令人惊艳的北美红雀，现在我每天早上都期待着见到它。截至本文撰写时，该设备已成功记录了六个物种的造访。

I’ve been addicted ever since. I find myself eagerly checking the app every morning to see which feathered little guy stopped by. I show off the videos to almost everyone I know as if they’re my own pets. One amusing notification I keep receiving is “a nuisance animal detected” when squirrels raid my birdseed stash (which happens as often as you’d expect). The app uses Kiwibit’s proprietary bird-identification algorithm to identify over 10,000 bird species, such as blue jays, ravens, and mourning doves. The Activity tab is particularly useful, as it tracks the number of “visits” captured, videos recorded, and total species observed. You can also navigate through the calendar to view specific days. The Birds tab offers in-depth information on each species, featuring detailed descriptions from Wikipedia.

从那以后我就上瘾了。我每天早上都会迫不及待地查看 App，看看是哪位“羽毛小家伙”光顾了。我还会像展示自己的宠物一样，向身边的每个人炫耀这些视频。一个有趣的通知是，每当松鼠来偷吃鸟食时（这种情况发生的频率正如你预料的那样），我就会收到“检测到滋扰动物”的提醒。该 App 使用 Kiwibit 专有的鸟类识别算法，可以识别超过 10,000 种鸟类，如蓝松鸦、渡鸦和哀鸠。“活动”选项卡特别有用，它会追踪捕捉到的“访问”次数、录制的视频以及观察到的物种总数。你还可以通过日历查看特定日期的记录。“鸟类”选项卡则提供了每个物种的深入信息，并附有来自维基百科的详细描述。

However, I did notice that the system — which typically costs between $179.99 to $249.99, depending on the model — occasionally has trouble accurately counting “visits.” For example, if a house sparrow is feeding in front of the camera for several minutes, the AI might record it as multiple visits, even if the bird hasn’t moved that much.

不过，我也注意到该系统（根据型号不同，价格通常在 179.99 美元到 249.99 美元之间）偶尔在准确统计“访问次数”时会出问题。例如，如果一只家麻雀在镜头前停留进食几分钟，即使它并没有怎么移动，AI 也可能会将其记录为多次访问。

Overall, testing the Kiwibit Bird Feeder Pro has been delightful. If you’re looking for a way to connect with nature while having some fun collecting bird species like Pokémon, give this smart feeder a try. Just be prepared for all the squirrels to visit, too.

总的来说，测试 Kiwibit Bird Feeder Pro 的过程非常愉快。如果你想在亲近自然的同时，体验像收集宝可梦一样收集鸟类品种的乐趣，不妨试试这款智能喂鸟器。只是要做好准备，松鼠们也会来凑热闹。
