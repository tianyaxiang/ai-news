# Representation Signatures and Risk-Feedback Alignment in LLM Trading Agents
## 大语言模型交易智能体中的表征特征与风险反馈对齐

**Abstract:** We study behavioral alignment and representation dynamics of large language model (LLM) agents in financial decision environments. Using TradeArena, an auditable trading-agent testbed with risk reports, execution simulation, memory, and replayable trajectories, we analyze how rationales, positions, and interventions evolve under market stress.

**摘要：** 我们研究了大语言模型（LLM）智能体在金融决策环境中的行为对齐与表征动态。通过使用 TradeArena（一个包含风险报告、执行模拟、记忆功能及可回放轨迹的可审计交易智能体测试平台），我们分析了在市场压力下，智能体的决策依据（rationales）、持仓情况及干预措施是如何演变的。

We find measurable pre-failure signatures: planning embeddings drift from normal-state centroids, fused plan-risk representations separate normal from pre-drawdown states, and manifold diagnostics show effective-rank contraction before failures. To address small-sample and embedding-choice concerns, we use 80 rolling failure anchors across eight LLM trajectories and show that contraction persists across hash, LSA, Transformer, and white-box hidden-state probes.

我们发现了可测量的故障前特征：规划嵌入（planning embeddings）偏离了正常状态的中心点，融合后的规划-风险表征将正常状态与回撤前状态区分开来，且流形诊断显示在故障发生前存在有效秩收缩（effective-rank contraction）。为了解决小样本和嵌入选择的顾虑，我们跨越八个 LLM 轨迹使用了 80 个滚动故障锚点，结果表明这种收缩在哈希、LSA、Transformer 以及白盒隐藏状态探测中均持续存在。

Stress tests with CoT-free target weights, lexical controls, OHLCV noise, and false-audit reports indicate that rationale-level contraction can vanish without rationales, while intent-space contraction may remain; lexical diversity does not collapse; and fused signatures remain informative under noise.

通过对无思维链（CoT-free）目标权重、词汇控制、OHLCV 噪声以及虚假审计报告进行的压力测试表明，在没有决策依据的情况下，决策依据层面的收缩可能会消失，但意图空间的收缩可能依然存在；词汇多样性并未崩溃；且融合后的特征在噪声环境下依然具有信息价值。

We also find that structured risk feedback can act as an external alignment signal without fine-tuning, but not as a universal performance enhancer: true audit feedback improves calibration for some models, return and drawdown for others, and reveals cases where hidden or placebo feedback has higher short-horizon return but weaker alignment diagnostics.

我们还发现，结构化的风险反馈无需微调即可作为外部对齐信号，但它并非通用的性能增强器：真实的审计反馈改善了部分模型的校准能力，提升了另一些模型的收益与回撤表现，同时也揭示了某些情况下，隐藏反馈或安慰剂反馈虽然能带来更高的短期收益，但其对齐诊断效果较弱。

Finally, a 51-stock intraday experiment reveals a correlation blind spot: LLM rationales often justify concentrated exposure to coupled assets that the risk layer repeatedly clips, with a rolling Markowitz baseline as a covariance reference. These results support a research claim rather than a profitability claim: auditable risk feedback and representation trajectories reveal when LLM financial reasoning is aligning, drifting, or failing.

最后，一项针对 51 只股票的日内实验揭示了一个相关性盲点：LLM 的决策依据往往为那些被风险层反复削减的耦合资产提供集中敞口辩护，实验以滚动马科维茨（Markowitz）基准作为协方差参考。这些结果支持的是一项研究结论而非盈利性声明：可审计的风险反馈和表征轨迹能够揭示 LLM 的金融推理是在对齐、漂移还是失效。
