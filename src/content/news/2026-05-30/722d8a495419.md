# The Verge’s 2026 high school graduation gift guide
## The Verge 2026 年高中毕业礼物指南

These 32 gift ideas will help your high school grad find their footing, whether they’re off to college or plan to travel.
这 32 个礼物点子将帮助你的高中毕业生站稳脚跟，无论他们是准备上大学还是计划去旅行。

High school graduation is a time of change that might be felt more deeply by family members than by the grads themselves. While some grads may immediately embark on a career path, many continue their education and delve deeper into their studies at college. Either way, they’ll be taking on more responsibility, meaning it’s up to friends and relatives to figure out how to support them.
高中毕业是一个充满变化的时刻，家人们感受到的冲击可能比毕业生本人还要强烈。虽然一些毕业生可能会立即投身职场，但许多人会选择继续深造，在大学里钻研学业。无论哪种情况，他们都将承担更多的责任，这意味着亲朋好友需要思考如何为他们提供支持。

Support comes in many forms, but a gift or two certainly won’t hurt. Luckily, we’ve compiled a host of Verge-approved gift ideas spanning a wide array of interests and price points. They’ll think of you when they grab their AirPods 4 to tune out distractions while studying, or when they pack a fabulous homemade lunch into their newfangled bento jar. We also have recommendations for travelers, tinkerers, and even soon-to-be caffeine fiends. Late-night study sessions are a thing, after all.
支持的形式多种多样，但送上一两件礼物绝对不会出错。幸运的是，我们整理了一系列经 The Verge 认可的礼物点子，涵盖了广泛的兴趣爱好和价格区间。当他们戴上 AirPods 4 隔绝干扰专心学习时，或者将美味的自制午餐装进新潮的便当盒时，他们一定会想到你。我们还为旅行者、动手达人，甚至是即将成为“咖啡因成瘾者”的学生们准备了推荐。毕竟，深夜学习可是常有的事。

### Zojirushi Classic Bento Lunch Jar ($53)
### 象印经典便当盒 ($53)

Packing a meal from home for work or school shouldn’t be cumbersome or messy. Zojirushi’s bento jar makes it easy to tote a multi-part meal, and it’s equipped with a spork and chopsticks. It features three food bowls of different sizes, one of which is vacuum-sealed to keep its contents hot or cold throughout the day until it’s time to munch.
从家里带午餐去学校或工作不应该是一件麻烦或脏乱的事情。象印的这款便当盒让你轻松携带多道菜肴，并配有叉勺和筷子。它包含三个不同尺寸的餐盒，其中一个采用真空密封设计，能确保食物在一天内保持冷热，直到享用时刻。

### Pebblebee Halo ($50)
### Pebblebee Halo 追踪器 ($50)

The Pebblebee Halo is a personal safety tool featuring a flashlight, Apple Find My tracking support, and a silent alert mode that notifies trusted individuals without making any noise. The keyring-ready, water-resistant device can last up to a year on a single charge, and it includes a one-year trial of Alert Live for live emergency location sharing (a $25 value).
Pebblebee Halo 是一款个人安全工具，具备手电筒功能、支持 Apple Find My 追踪，以及一种无需发出声音即可通知信任之人的静音警报模式。这款防水设备可挂在钥匙扣上，单次充电续航长达一年，并包含价值 25 美元的 Alert Live 一年试用期，用于实时紧急位置共享。

### Fiio Echo Mini lossless music player ($60)
### Fiio Echo Mini 无损音乐播放器 ($60)

Chances are, your grad has never had the pleasure of owning their own drag-and-drop MP3 player. Well, it’s time to change that with Fiio’s Echo Mini, which was designed to look like a cassette player. Its hardware is much nicer than the low-cost MP3 players from the early aughts, especially since you can use it with either wired or wireless headphones (over Bluetooth).
你的毕业生可能从未体验过拥有属于自己的拖拽式 MP3 播放器的乐趣。现在，是时候用外观设计酷似磁带机的 Fiio Echo Mini 来改变这一点了。它的硬件比 21 世纪初的廉价 MP3 播放器好得多，而且你可以通过有线或无线（蓝牙）耳机使用它。

### Anker Nano Power Strip ($70)
### Anker Nano 电源排插 ($70)

Many modern desks offer a few outlets and USB ports to power your electronics, but Anker’s 10-in-1 Nano Power Strip is packed with them. It clamps to your desk, neatly hugging its surface with a strip containing two plugs, two USB-C ports, and two USB-A ports. The hidden section beneath also boasts four plugs, allowing you to power even more devices.
许多现代书桌提供了一些插座和 USB 接口来为电子设备供电，但 Anker 的 10 合 1 Nano 排插功能更为强大。它可以夹在书桌边缘，整齐地贴合桌面，提供两个插孔、两个 USB-C 接口和两个 USB-A 接口。下方的隐藏区域还额外提供了四个插孔，让你能为更多设备供电。

### Mechanism Gaming Pillow ($64)
### Mechanism 游戏靠枕 ($64)

A pillow for gaming may not be as vital as some of the other gift ideas here, but Mechanism’s accessory is worth considering for its ergonomic benefits. It can alleviate arm strain, and with its wide ecosystem of mounts, you can attach devices like a Nintendo Switch 2 or an Amazon Kindle for effortless entertainment.
游戏靠枕可能不像这里提到的其他礼物那样必不可少，但 Mechanism 的这款配件因其人体工学优势而值得考虑。它可以缓解手臂疲劳，并且凭借其广泛的支架生态系统，你可以安装 Nintendo Switch 2 或 Amazon Kindle 等设备，享受轻松的娱乐体验。

### Cuisinart Chef’s Convection Toaster Oven ($300)
### Cuisinart 主厨对流烤箱 ($300)

A proper toaster oven is the gateway to some good old-fashioned home cooking. Cuisinart’s big, powerful convection oven is easy to use, heats faster than a conventional oven, and, unlike a microwave, cooks food evenly. You can quickly brown the cheese on your pizza or reheat leftovers to taste just like they did at the restaurant, whichever strikes your fancy.
一台像样的烤箱是开启传统家庭烹饪的大门。Cuisinart 这款功能强大的大型对流烤箱易于使用，加热速度比传统烤箱更快，而且与微波炉不同，它能让食物受热均匀。你可以快速烤化披萨上的奶酪，或者加热剩菜，让它们尝起来像刚从餐厅端出来一样，随你心意。

### Remarkable Paper Pure ($399)
### Remarkable Paper Pure 电子纸 ($399)

Whether your grad’s path takes them to college or straight into a career, a device made exclusively for note-taking is always nice to have. To that end, Remarkable’s Paper Pure is a great pick, as its textured finish feels similar to writing on paper. The Pure is also great for marking up required reading for school or tailoring a cover letter and résumé.
无论你的毕业生是去上大学还是直接步入职场，拥有一台专门用于笔记的设备总是件好事。为此，Remarkable 的 Paper Pure 是一个绝佳选择，其纹理表面书写起来感觉就像在纸上一样。Pure 也非常适合标注学校的必读书目，或修改求职信和简历。

AirPods 4 是娱乐生活的生命线。它们能让家务活变得不再枯燥，为你的学习或创作提供放松的背景音乐，还能让你尽情享受电影和游戏。当你准备隔绝外界干扰时，只需开启主动降噪功能，就能更专注于你最喜爱的播客、歌曲或有声书。
