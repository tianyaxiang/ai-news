# GeRaF: Neural Geometry Reconstruction from Radio Frequency Signals
# GeRaF：基于射频信号的神经几何重建

**Abstract:** GeRaF is the first method to use neural implicit learning for near-range 3D geometry reconstruction from radio frequency (RF) signals. Unlike RGB or LiDAR-based methods, RF sensing can see through occlusion but suffers from low resolution and noise due to its lensless imaging nature. While lenses in RGB imaging constrain sampling to 1D rays, RF signals propagate through the entire space, introducing significant noise and leading to cubic complexity in volumetric rendering. Moreover, RF signals interact with surfaces via specular reflections, requiring fundamentally different modeling.

**摘要：** GeRaF 是首个利用神经隐式学习（neural implicit learning）从射频（RF）信号进行近距离 3D 几何重建的方法。与基于 RGB 或激光雷达（LiDAR）的方法不同，射频传感能够穿透遮挡物，但由于其无透镜成像的特性，往往面临分辨率低和噪声大的问题。RGB 成像中的透镜将采样限制在 1D 射线内，而射频信号则在整个空间中传播，这不仅引入了显著的噪声，还导致了体积渲染中立方级的计算复杂度。此外，射频信号通过镜面反射与表面相互作用，这需要从根本上采用不同的建模方式。

To address these challenges, GeRaF (1) introduces filter-based rendering to suppress irrelevant signals, (2) implements a physics-based RF volumetric rendering pipeline, and (3) proposes a novel lensless sampling and lensless alpha blending strategy that makes full-space sampling feasible during training. By learning signed distance functions, reflectiveness, and signal power through MLPs and trainable parameters, GeRaF takes the first step towards reconstructing millimeter-level geometry from RF signals in real-world settings.

为了应对这些挑战，GeRaF (1) 引入了基于滤波的渲染技术以抑制无关信号；(2) 实现了一个基于物理的射频体积渲染流水线；(3) 提出了一种新颖的无透镜采样和无透镜 Alpha 混合策略，使得训练过程中的全空间采样成为可能。通过利用多层感知机（MLP）和可训练参数来学习符号距离函数（SDF）、反射率和信号功率，GeRaF 在现实场景中从射频信号重建毫米级几何结构方面迈出了第一步。
