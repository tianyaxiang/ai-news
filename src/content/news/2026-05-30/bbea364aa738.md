# The Best Robotic Pool Cleaners of 2026: Beatbot, iGarden, Dreame
# 2026年最佳泳池清洁机器人：Beatbot、iGarden、Dreame

Swimming pools are fun. Cleaning swimming pools is not fun. I learned this simple logic as a kid growing up in and around pools—it’s the only way to survive summer in Houston. Four years ago, I became a pool owner myself, and I found that the rule still holds. Jumping into the pool on a hot day remains a rare treat, but if the pool is filled with leaves and dirt, that treat becomes a lot less delightful. And when the thermometer is reading over 100 degrees Fahrenheit, the thought of laboring on the pool deck, scooping out debris with a net, is downright cruel.

游泳池很有趣，但清洁泳池却不然。我在休斯顿长大，从小就在泳池边玩耍，我学到了这个简单的逻辑——这是在休斯顿度过夏天的唯一生存之道。四年前，我自己也成了泳池主人，我发现这条规则依然适用。在炎热的天气跳进泳池依然是一种难得的享受，但如果泳池里满是落叶和污垢，这种享受就会大打折扣。当温度计显示超过华氏100度（约38摄氏度）时，还要在泳池边辛苦地用网兜打捞杂物，简直是一种折磨。

Robotic pool cleaners were a thing when I was growing up in the ’80s, but there was really only one technology available: pressure-side cleaners that attach to a pump on the surface and use water pressure, not electricity, to move around the pool. These types of cleaners are still around; you’ll recognize them by the length of tubing that floats in the water and connects to a jet set into the wall of the pool.

在我80年代成长的时候，泳池清洁机器人就已经存在了，但当时实际上只有一种技术：压力式清洁器。它们连接到地面的水泵上，利用水压而非电力在泳池中移动。这类清洁器至今仍在使用；你可以通过漂浮在水面上并连接到泳池壁喷嘴的长管来识别它们。

Today, the market is gravitating to electronic cleaners that don’t require a pump or that big, ugly hose in the water. And while corded cleaners are still an option—essentially running off of a long extension cord connected to wall power—battery-powered robotic cleaners are the future of the industry.

如今，市场正转向无需水泵或水面长管的电子清洁器。虽然有线清洁器仍然是一种选择（本质上是通过连接墙壁电源的长延长线运行），但电池供电的机器人清洁器才是该行业的未来。

Battery-powered cleaners have many benefits, including the lack of hoses or cords and the ability to be removed from the water on demand, leaving your pool unencumbered by obstacles when you want to swim in it. Of the ones I've tested, the Beatbot AquaSense 2 Ultra is the best all-around, but I've also found options for other budgets and preferences.

电池供电的清洁器有很多优点，包括没有软管或电线，并且可以随时从水中取出，当你想要游泳时，泳池里不会有任何障碍物。在我测试过的产品中，Beatbot AquaSense 2 Ultra 是综合表现最好的，但我也为其他预算和偏好找到了合适的选择。

Updated May 2026: I added the iGarden’s M1 A1 90 and Beatbot Sora 100, and checked and updated prices throughout.

2026年5月更新：我增加了 iGarden 的 M1 A1 90 和 Beatbot Sora 100，并核对更新了全文价格。

### The Best Pool-Cleaning Robot: Beatbot AquaSense 2 Ultra
### 最佳泳池清洁机器人：Beatbot AquaSense 2 Ultra

After four years of reviewing pool robots professionally and hundreds of test runs, the one I go back to the most is Beatbot’s top-shelf bot, the AquaSense 2 Ultra. This robot has everything: near-perfect cleaning capabilities (including floors, walls, and waterline), a powerful battery with six hours of charge under the water, AI-powered debris detection, and a solid mobile app. It also has the ability to skim the surface of the pool. When finished cleaning, the AquaSense 2 Ultra floats, so collecting it is just a matter of grabbing it from the comfort of the deck. After a quick cleanup, drop the robot on the included charging stand to juice it back up, no cables required.

在专业评测泳池机器人四年并进行了数百次测试后，我最常使用的还是 Beatbot 的顶级产品 AquaSense 2 Ultra。这款机器人应有尽有：近乎完美的清洁能力（包括池底、池壁和水线）、水下续航六小时的强力电池、AI 驱动的杂物检测功能以及功能强大的移动应用程序。它还具备清理水面的能力。清洁完成后，AquaSense 2 Ultra 会浮出水面，因此你只需在泳池边轻松将其捞起即可。简单清理后，将其放在附带的充电座上即可充电，无需任何线缆。

What’s not to like? Only two things, really. Monstrous cleaning ability requires a monstrous chassis, and to say the 29-pound Beatbot AquaSense 2 Ultra is unwieldy would be an understatement. Hauling this robot out of the water can be a chore, so work on your forearm curls if you plan to purchase one. There’s also the price point: At around $3,000 it’s pretty much the most expensive battery-powered pool robot on the market.

有什么缺点吗？其实只有两点。强大的清洁能力需要庞大的机身，说29磅（约13公斤）重的 Beatbot AquaSense 2 Ultra 笨重都算是轻描淡写了。把这个机器人从水里捞出来是一项体力活，所以如果你打算买它，最好先练练前臂力量。此外还有价格问题：它售价约3000美元，几乎是市场上最昂贵的电池供电泳池机器人。

### Pool-Cleaning Robot With the Best Battery Life: iGarden Robotic Pool Cleaner M1-AI 90
### 最佳电池续航泳池清洁机器人：iGarden M1-AI 90

The traditional way to use a pool robot is to keep it dry-docked and charging, then drop it into the pool only when you need it. An alternative may appeal to lazier pool owners: Drop the robot in the pool and leave it there for a week or two, let it run on a repeating schedule, then clean it out only when the battery is dead. The trick with this strategy is that few pool robots have a battery big enough to allow for more than one or two thorough cleanings. But with its new M1-AI series, iGarden drops a massive 12,500 mAh battery into its sleek pool bot, allowing up to nine hours of running time in floor-only operation.

使用泳池机器人的传统方式是将其存放在干燥处充电，仅在需要时才放入泳池。对于更懒的泳池主人来说，还有另一种选择：将机器人放入泳池并放置一两周，让它按预定计划循环运行，直到电池耗尽后再清理。这种策略的难点在于，很少有泳池机器人的电池大到足以支持一两次以上的彻底清洁。但 iGarden 在其全新的 M1-AI 系列中，为其时尚的泳池机器人配备了巨大的 12,500 mAh 电池，使其在仅清洁池底模式下可运行长达九小时。

### Best Budget Pool-Cleaning Robot: Dreame Z1 Pro
### 最佳预算泳池清洁机器人：Dreame Z1 Pro

Buying a pool robot for under $800 generally means having to make a lot of compromises: poor performance, limited battery life, iffy design. Dreame’s Z1 Pro breaks those expectations with a quirky but capable pool robot that’s full of intelligence: Its onboard sensors can even map your pool graphically in its effective mobile app as it trudges along. The robot is quite slow in the water, but it’s an effective cleaner. You won’t get flawless scrubbing performance like you would with more luxe poolbots, but it’s good enough for casual cleanups.

以低于800美元的价格购买泳池机器人通常意味着必须做出很多妥协：性能差、电池寿命有限、设计不可靠。Dreame 的 Z1 Pro 打破了这些预期，它是一款独特但功能强大的机器人，且非常智能：其内置传感器甚至可以在它缓慢移动时，在移动应用程序中以图形方式绘制出你的泳池地图。该机器人在水中的速度较慢，但清洁效果不错。你可能无法获得像高端机型那样完美的刷洗性能，但对于日常清洁来说已经足够了。
