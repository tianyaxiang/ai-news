# What happens when companies become too AI-pilled?
# 当企业对人工智能“上瘾”后会发生什么？

The people deciding that AI can replace your job are also the ones least likely to understand what your job truly involves, according to Box founder Aaron Levie, who pointed to this as an example of “AI psychosis.”
Box 创始人 Aaron Levie 指出，那些决定用人工智能取代你工作的人，往往是最不了解你工作内容的人。他将这种现象称为“人工智能精神错乱”（AI psychosis）。

Indeed, ClickUp recently cut 22% of its workforce for AI agents, tech layoffs in 2026 are already nearly matching all of 2025, and DuckDuckGo installs are climbing from users who want Google to stop forcing AI into search and just give them links.
事实上，ClickUp 最近裁减了 22% 的员工以引入人工智能代理；2026 年的科技行业裁员人数已经几乎赶上了 2025 年全年的总和；与此同时，DuckDuckGo 的安装量也在攀升，因为用户希望谷歌停止在搜索中强行植入人工智能，转而提供纯粹的搜索链接。
