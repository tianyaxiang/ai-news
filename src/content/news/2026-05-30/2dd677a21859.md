# 15 Best Wireless Chargers, All Tested and Reviewed (2026)
# 2026年15款最佳无线充电器：全面测试与评测

Wireless charging may not be as cool as it sounds. You still need a cord to plug the charger in, and it’s slower than wired charging, but once you free yourself from fishing for and fumbling with cables in the dark, you never go back. Dropping your phone on a pad or stand at the end of the day and waking up to a fully charged battery in the morning is easy and convenient.
无线充电听起来可能没那么酷。你仍然需要一根线来连接充电器，而且它的充电速度比有线充电慢。但一旦你摆脱了在黑暗中摸索和摆弄线缆的烦恼，你就再也回不去了。每天结束时把手机往充电板或支架上一放，第二天早上醒来就能拥有满电的电池，这种体验既轻松又便捷。

After testing more than 100 wireless chargers over the past decade, sorting the good from the bad (there are certainly bad ones), I have identified the best wireless chargers. You will find many pads and stands in different styles, shapes, and build materials with spots for other devices, alongside your phone.
在过去十年测试了超过100款无线充电器，并剔除劣质产品（市面上确实存在不少劣质品）后，我选出了这些最佳无线充电器。你将看到许多不同风格、形状和材质的充电板与支架，它们不仅能为手机充电，还能同时为其他设备提供充电位。

Updated May 2026: I reorganized this guide, slimmed down my picks, added some further thoughts on my top pick, and updated prices.
2026年5月更新：我重新整理了本指南，精简了推荐产品，增加了对首选产品的进一步思考，并更新了价格。

### Best Wireless Charging Stand: Nomad Stand One (4th Gen, Qi2 25W)
### 最佳无线充电支架：Nomad Stand One（第四代，Qi2 25W）

Practical and good-looking, this MagSafe and Qi2 wireless charging stand from Nomad will work with iPhones and Android phones (you can use a magnetic case if they don't support Qi2 or MagSafe). It’s a weighty mix of metal and glass in black or white, with padding in all the right places. You can pick up or place your phone one-handed (even in the dark), and the stand doesn’t slide around or topple easily. It has a ring of powerful magnets to hold your phone in portrait or landscape orientation (handy for StandBy mode) and can charge at up to 25 watts. The pad tucked away around back is for wireless earbuds. There are no superfluous lights or sounds, so it's perfect for the nightstand. You get a nice braided cable in the box (USB-C to USB-C), but you will need a 40-watt charger ($25), which is a bit cheeky at this price.
这款来自Nomad的MagSafe和Qi2无线充电支架既实用又美观，适用于iPhone和安卓手机（如果手机不支持Qi2或MagSafe，可以使用磁吸手机壳）。它由金属和玻璃混合制成，质感厚重，有黑白两色可选，并在关键部位配有衬垫。你可以单手取放手机（即使在黑暗中），支架也不会轻易滑动或倾倒。它配有一圈强力磁铁，可将手机固定在纵向或横向（方便使用StandBy模式），充电功率最高可达25瓦。背面的充电板专为无线耳机设计。它没有多余的灯光或噪音，非常适合放在床头柜上。包装内附赠一根优质编织线（USB-C转USB-C），但你需要额外购买一个40瓦充电器（25美元），在这个价位下，这显得有些不够厚道。

### The Best Budget Wireless Chargers: Anker PowerWave Pad and Stand
### 最佳入门级无线充电器：Anker PowerWave 充电板与支架

Anker’s pad and stand prove you don't need to spend much for wireless charging. They're both made from plastic with a rubbery finish on the bottom to prevent slips and slides, though it's not too grippy. A tiny LED lights up blue when it’s charging and flashes to indicate there’s a problem. We prefer stands over pads, because you can easily see your phone notifications, but Anker’s pad is so cheap you could grab a few to scatter around your home. Both come with a 4-foot MicroUSB cable, but you have to supply the power adapter. That’s not a big surprise at this price. Most importantly, they'll charge your phone every bit as capably as many other options in this guide. These chargers can hit up to 10 watts for some Samsung phones, 7.5 watts for iPhones, and 5 watts for other devices.
Anker的充电板和支架证明了无线充电无需高昂成本。它们均由塑料制成，底部带有橡胶涂层以防滑动，虽然抓地力一般。充电时，一个小LED灯会亮起蓝光，闪烁则表示出现故障。我们更推荐支架而非充电板，因为支架能让你轻松查看手机通知，但Anker的充电板非常便宜，你可以多买几个放在家里各处。两者均附带一根4英尺长的MicroUSB线，但你需要自备电源适配器。在这个价位下，这并不令人意外。最重要的是，它们的充电能力与本指南中的许多其他选项不相上下。这些充电器对部分三星手机最高可达10瓦，iPhone为7.5瓦，其他设备为5瓦。

### Best Cooling Wireless Charger: Spigen OneTap Pro 3 Cryomax
### 最佳散热无线充电器：Spigen OneTap Pro 3 Cryomax

Spigen’s stand can charge your iPhone or Qi2-certified phone at up to 15 watts. The built-in fan is effective at keeping your device cool, with a subtle blue light to emphasize its powers of chill. This would ordinarily annoy me on the nightstand, but Spigen has included a button to turn the fan and light off so it doesn’t disturb your slumber. With a strong magnetic pad you can tilt and a stable base, this pad works great with the iPhone’s StandBy mode. You get a USB-C cable to plug into the back, but you need your own power adapter (25 watts and up). I have tried a few chargers with cooling capabilities, but my iPhone has never felt cooler than when I plucked it from Spigen’s stand, so this could be a solid buy if you're worried about heat.
Spigen的这款支架能为你的iPhone或Qi2认证手机提供最高15瓦的充电功率。内置风扇能有效保持设备凉爽，并配有柔和的蓝光来强调其散热能力。通常这会让我觉得放在床头柜上很烦人，但Spigen贴心地加入了一个按钮，可以关闭风扇和灯光，以免打扰睡眠。凭借可倾斜的强力磁吸板和稳固的底座，它与iPhone的StandBy模式配合得非常好。包装内附带一根USB-C线，但你需要自备电源适配器（25瓦及以上）。我尝试过几款带散热功能的充电器，但我的iPhone从Spigen支架上取下时感觉最凉爽，所以如果你担心发热问题，这绝对是一个稳妥的选择。

### A Functional and Fast 2-in-1 Charger: Belkin UltraCharge 2-in-1 Foldable Magnetic Wireless Charger (Qi2 25W)
### 功能强大且快速的二合一充电器：Belkin UltraCharge 二合一可折叠磁吸无线充电器（Qi2 25W）

Want something to quickly juice up your phone and earbuds? Belkin's UltraCharge 2-in-1 supports the latest Qi2 25W standard for the main magnetic charging coil, so this charger won't feel out of date even in a few years. It looks nice enough to be a permanent fixture on the nightstand or desk, but you also have the option to fold it up and take it on the go. Over on the back, when unfolded, there's a 5-watt wireless charger for earbuds (or a second phone in a pinch), plus there's a spare USB-C port to plug in another device. Best of all, Belkin includes a 45-watt power adapter and cable, which is an incredible value at this price.
想要快速为手机和耳机充电？Belkin的UltraCharge二合一充电器主磁吸线圈支持最新的Qi2 25W标准，因此这款充电器几年内都不会过时。它的外观足够美观，适合长期放在床头柜或桌面上，同时你也可以将其折叠起来随身携带。展开后，背面有一个5瓦的无线充电位供耳机使用（紧急时也可为第二部手机充电），此外还有一个额外的USB-C接口可连接其他设备。最棒的是，Belkin附赠了45瓦电源适配器和线缆，在这个价位下性价比极高。
