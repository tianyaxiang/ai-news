# PrismFlow: Residual Dynamics for Flow Matching in Time-Series Generation
# PrismFlow：用于时间序列生成的残差动力学流匹配

**Abstract:** Generating high-quality time-series data is challenging because real-world signals often exhibit multimodal patterns and multiscale dynamics, including oscillations and high-frequency variations. Flow Matching (FM) offers an efficient alternative to diffusion models, but practical implementations typically rely on a single finite-capacity global vector-field estimator.

**摘要：** 生成高质量的时间序列数据极具挑战性，因为现实世界中的信号通常表现出多模态模式和多尺度动力学特征，包括振荡和高频变化。流匹配（Flow Matching, FM）为扩散模型提供了一种高效的替代方案，但目前的实际应用通常依赖于单一的、有限容量的全局向量场估计器。

In such heterogeneous temporal distributions, distinct regimes may pass through nearby flow states while requiring incompatible conditional velocities. A monolithic estimator trained with the standard $\ell_2$ velocity-matching objective may therefore learn an overly smoothed approximation of the local transport field. This estimator-level smoothing can attenuate branch-specific dynamics, leading to spectral distortion and poor mode coverage.

在如此异构的时间分布中，不同的状态区域可能会经过相近的流状态，但却需要互不兼容的条件速度。因此，使用标准 $\ell_2$ 速度匹配目标训练的单体估计器可能会学习到局部传输场过于平滑的近似值。这种估计器层面的平滑处理会削弱特定分支的动力学特征，从而导致频谱失真和模态覆盖不足。

To address this, we propose PrismFlow, a new FM method with Koopman-inspired dynamical experts. Each expert learns residual corrections in a latent space where local nonlinear temporal evolution can be approximated by linear transitions. We further propose a confidence-aware Winner-Take-All (WTA) objective that updates only the expert best aligned with each sample while masking gradients to the others, encouraging mode-specific specialization.

为了解决这一问题，我们提出了 PrismFlow，这是一种结合了 Koopman 启发式动力学专家的新型 FM 方法。每个专家在潜在空间中学习残差修正，在该空间中，局部非线性时间演化可以通过线性转换来近似。我们进一步提出了一种置信度感知的“赢家通吃”（Winner-Take-All, WTA）目标函数，该函数仅更新与每个样本最匹配的专家，同时屏蔽其他专家的梯度，从而促进模态的专门化。

During sampling, the selected expert adds a residual dynamical correction to the global transport field, preserving FM stability while recovering fine-grained and high-frequency temporal structures. Across various benchmarks, PrismFlow effectively mitigates the spectral contraction in standard FM and achieves state-of-the-art performance, with a 15.6% gain in Context-FID and a 38.6% improvement in Discriminative Score, while remaining robust in low-data settings and effective for forecasting and imputation.

在采样过程中，选定的专家会向全局传输场添加残差动力学修正，在保持 FM 稳定性的同时恢复细粒度和高频的时间结构。在各项基准测试中，PrismFlow 有效缓解了标准 FM 中的频谱收缩问题，并达到了最先进的性能水平，Context-FID 提升了 15.6%，判别得分（Discriminative Score）提升了 38.6%，同时在低数据环境下保持了鲁棒性，并在预测和插补任务中表现出色。
