# Auditing Training-Free 3D Shape Retrieval with Diffused Geodesic Moments
# 使用扩散测地线矩审计无需训练的 3D 形状检索

**Abstract:** Reported retrieval scores for training-free shape descriptors conflate local signal design, normalization, aggregation, codebook fitting, and metric choices, making isolated component evaluation difficult. This paper reframes descriptor evaluation as a {\em protocol audit}. We introduce Diffused Geodesic Moments (DGM), a seed-conditioned descriptor that computes sparse implicit heat responses, converts them to distance-like fields, and summarizes each vertex by low-order moments across seeds and scales.

**摘要：** 目前针对无需训练（training-free）形状描述符的检索评分报告，往往将局部信号设计、归一化、聚合、码本拟合以及度量选择混为一谈，导致难以对单一组件进行独立评估。本文将描述符评估重新定义为一种“协议审计”（protocol audit）。我们引入了扩散测地线矩（Diffused Geodesic Moments, DGM），这是一种基于种子条件的描述符，它通过计算稀疏隐式热响应，将其转换为类距离场，并利用跨种子和尺度的低阶矩来总结每个顶点。

DGM is used both as a practical non-spectral baseline and as an instrument for isolating protocol effects. On the registered FAUST benchmark split (FAUST-Reg) and the TOSCA shape collection, aggregation-matched experiments show that an independent Geometric Moment Shape Descriptor baseline built on Heat Kernel Signature features (GMSD-HKS) obtains the highest scores in this implementation ($0.621/0.820$ and $0.865/0.963$ mean average precision (mAP)/top-1), Wave Kernel Signature (WKS) remains a strong classical signal, and DGM is useful mainly when sparse solves, non-spectral deployment, or symmetry-informative seed frames are priorities.

DGM 既可作为一种实用的非谱（non-spectral）基准，也可作为隔离协议效应的工具。在已配准的 FAUST 基准测试集（FAUST-Reg）和 TOSCA 形状集合上的聚合匹配实验表明，基于热核特征（HKS）构建的独立几何矩形状描述符基准（GMSD-HKS）在此实现中获得了最高分（平均精度均值 mAP/top-1 分别为 $0.621/0.820$ 和 $0.865/0.963$）；波核特征（WKS）依然是一种强大的经典信号；而 DGM 主要在需要稀疏求解、非谱部署或对称信息种子框架时具有显著优势。

The broader finding is methodological: the input field and aggregation protocol can dominate the moment formula. The paper contributes a reproducible protocol-cascade analysis, a cross-shape alignment diagnostic for functional-map compatibility, and concrete recommendations for designing and reporting training-free shape descriptors.

更广泛的研究发现具有方法论意义：输入场和聚合协议往往比矩公式本身更能决定最终效果。本文贡献了一种可复现的协议级联分析方法、一种用于函数映射兼容性的跨形状对齐诊断工具，并为设计和报告无需训练的形状描述符提供了具体建议。
