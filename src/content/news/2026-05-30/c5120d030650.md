# Lightweight Complementary-Cue Fusion for Robust Video Face Forgery Detection
# 用于鲁棒视频人脸伪造检测的轻量级互补线索融合技术

**Abstract:** Current face video forgery detectors use wide or dual-stream backbones. We show that a single, lightweight fusion of two handcrafted cues can achieve higher accuracy with a much smaller model. 

**摘要：** 当前的人脸视频伪造检测器通常使用宽体或双流骨干网络。我们证明，通过对两种手工设计的线索进行单一且轻量级的融合，可以在模型规模显著减小的情况下实现更高的准确率。

Based on the Xception baseline model (21.9 million parameters), we build two detectors: LFWS, which adds a 1x1 convolution to combine a low-frequency Wavelet-Denoised Feature (WDF) with a phase-spectrum channel derived from Spatial-Phase Shallow Learning (SPSL), and LFWL, which merges WDF with Local Binary Patterns (LBP) in the same way. 

基于 Xception 基准模型（2190 万参数），我们构建了两个检测器：LFWS 和 LFWL。LFWS 通过添加一个 1x1 卷积，将低频小波去噪特征（WDF）与源自空间相位浅层学习（SPSL）的相位谱通道相结合；LFWL 则以同样的方式将 WDF 与局部二值模式（LBP）进行融合。

This extra module adds only 292 parameters, keeping the total at 21.9 million, smaller than F3Net (22.5 million) and less than half the size of SRM (55.3 million). 

这一额外模块仅增加了 292 个参数，使总参数量保持在 2190 万，小于 F3Net（2250 万），且不到 SRM（5530 万）规模的一半。

Even with this minimal overhead, the fused models increase the average area under the curve (AUC) from 74.8% to 78.6% on FaceForensics++ and from 70.5% to 74.9% on DFDC-Preview, gains of 3.8% and 4.4% over the Xception baseline. 

即便开销如此之小，融合模型在 FaceForensics++ 数据集上的平均曲线下面积（AUC）仍从 74.8% 提升至 78.6%，在 DFDC-Preview 数据集上从 70.5% 提升至 74.9%，较 Xception 基准分别提升了 3.8% 和 4.4%。

They also consistently outperform F3Net, SRM, and SPSL in eight public benchmarks, without extra data or test-time augmentation. 

此外，在无需额外数据或测试时增强的情况下，这些模型在八个公开基准测试中均持续优于 F3Net、SRM 和 SPSL。

These results show that carefully paired, handcrafted features, combined through the lightweight fusion block, can provide competitive robustness at a significantly lower cost than comparable frequency-based detectors. 

这些结果表明，通过轻量级融合模块结合精心配对的手工特征，可以在显著降低成本的同时，提供与同类基于频率的检测器相媲美的鲁棒性。

Our findings suggest a need to reevaluate scale-driven design choices in face video forgery detection.

我们的研究结果表明，有必要重新评估人脸视频伪造检测中以规模为导向的设计选择。
