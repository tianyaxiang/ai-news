# Neural Architecture Search for Generative Adversarial Networks: A Comprehensive Review and Critical Analysis
# 生成对抗网络（GAN）的神经架构搜索：全面综述与批判性分析

**Abstract:** Neural Architecture Search (NAS) has emerged as a pivotal technique in optimizing the design of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), automating the search for effective architectures while addressing the challenges inherent in manual design. 

**摘要：** 神经架构搜索（NAS）已成为优化生成对抗网络（GAN）设计的关键技术，它通过自动化搜索高效架构，解决了人工设计中固有的挑战。

This paper provides a comprehensive review of NAS methods applied to GANs, categorizing and comparing various approaches based on criteria such as search strategies, evaluation metrics, and performance outcomes. 

本文对应用于 GAN 的 NAS 方法进行了全面综述，并根据搜索策略、评估指标和性能结果等标准对各种方法进行了分类和比较。

The review highlights the benefits of NAS in improving GAN performance, stability, and efficiency, while also identifying limitations and areas for future research. 

本综述强调了 NAS 在提升 GAN 性能、稳定性和效率方面的优势，同时也指出了其局限性及未来的研究方向。

Key findings include the superiority of evolutionary algorithms and gradient-based methods in certain contexts, the importance of robust evaluation metrics beyond traditional scores like Inception Score (IS) and Fréchet Inception Distance (FID), and the need for diverse datasets in assessing GAN performance. 

主要研究发现包括：进化算法和基于梯度的方法在特定场景下的优越性；除了传统的 Inception Score (IS) 和 Fréchet Inception Distance (FID) 等评分外，建立稳健评估指标的重要性；以及在评估 GAN 性能时使用多样化数据集的必要性。

By presenting a structured comparison of existing NAS-GAN techniques, this paper aims to guide researchers in developing more effective NAS methods and advancing the field of GANs.

通过对现有 NAS-GAN 技术进行结构化的比较，本文旨在引导研究人员开发更有效的 NAS 方法，并推动 GAN 领域的发展。
