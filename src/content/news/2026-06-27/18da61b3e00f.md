# grafana / grafana

The open-source platform for monitoring and observability Grafana allows you to query, visualize, alert on and understand your metrics no matter where they are stored. Create, explore, and share dashboards with your team and foster a data-driven culture:
开源监控与可观测性平台 Grafana 允许您查询、可视化、设置警报并理解您的指标，无论它们存储在何处。您可以与团队创建、探索和共享仪表板，并培养数据驱动的文化：

Visualizations: Fast and flexible client side graphs with a multitude of options. Panel plugins offer many different ways to visualize metrics and logs.
可视化：快速且灵活的客户端图表，提供多种选项。面板插件提供了多种可视化指标和日志的方法。

Dynamic Dashboards: Create dynamic & reusable dashboards with template variables that appear as dropdowns at the top of the dashboard.
动态仪表板：创建动态且可重用的仪表板，并使用模板变量，这些变量会以下拉菜单的形式显示在仪表板顶部。

Explore Metrics: Explore your data through ad-hoc queries and dynamic drilldown. Split view and compare different time ranges, queries and data sources side by side.
探索指标：通过即席查询和动态下钻来探索您的数据。使用分屏视图并排比较不同的时间范围、查询和数据源。

Explore Logs: Experience the magic of switching from metrics to logs with preserved label filters. Quickly search through all your logs or streaming them live.
探索日志：体验从指标切换到日志的神奇过程，同时保留标签过滤器。快速搜索所有日志或实时流式传输它们。

Alerting: Visually define alert rules for your most important metrics. Grafana will continuously evaluate and send notifications to systems like Slack, PagerDuty, VictorOps, OpsGenie.
警报：为最重要的指标可视化定义警报规则。Grafana 将持续评估并向 Slack、PagerDuty、VictorOps、OpsGenie 等系统发送通知。

Mixed Data Sources: Mix different data sources in the same graph! You can specify a data source on a per-query basis. This works for even custom datasources.
混合数据源：在同一个图表中混合使用不同的数据源！您可以按查询指定数据源。这甚至适用于自定义数据源。

Get started: Get Grafana. Installation guides. Unsure if Grafana is for you? Watch Grafana in action on play.grafana.org!
开始使用：获取 Grafana。安装指南。不确定 Grafana 是否适合您？请在 play.grafana.org 上观看 Grafana 的实际演示！

Documentation: The Grafana documentation is available at grafana.com/docs.
文档：Grafana 文档可在 grafana.com/docs 获取。

Contributing: If you're interested in contributing to the Grafana project: Start by reading the Contributing guide. Learn how to set up your local environment, in our Developer guide. Explore our beginner-friendly issues. Look through our style guide and Storybook. Share your contributor experience in our feedback survey to help us improve.
贡献：如果您有兴趣为 Grafana 项目做出贡献：请从阅读贡献指南开始。在我们的开发者指南中了解如何设置本地环境。探索我们对初学者友好的议题。查看我们的风格指南和 Storybook。在我们的反馈调查中分享您的贡献体验，以帮助我们改进。

Get involved: Follow @grafana on X (formerly Twitter). Read and subscribe to the Grafana blog. If you have a specific question, check out our discussion forums. For general discussions, join us on the official Slack team. This project is tested with BrowserStack.
参与其中：在 X（前身为 Twitter）上关注 @grafana。阅读并订阅 Grafana 博客。如果您有具体问题，请查看我们的讨论论坛。如需进行一般性讨论，请加入我们的官方 Slack 团队。本项目使用 BrowserStack 进行测试。
