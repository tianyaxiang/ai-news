# DocArena: Turning Raw Documents into Controllable Training Environments for Document Search Agents
# DocArena：将原始文档转化为文档搜索智能体的可控训练环境

Recent methods train search agents via reinforcement learning from (question, answer, evidence) tuples without requiring expert trajectories. The tuples serve as the training environment, and whose properties directly shape what search strategies and generalization abilities the agent can develop.
近期的研究方法通过（问题、答案、证据）元组进行强化学习来训练搜索智能体，而无需专家轨迹。这些元组充当了训练环境，其属性直接决定了智能体能够发展的搜索策略和泛化能力。

While prior works have made encouraging progress in improving training data quality, existing environments remain predominantly text-based and existing approaches can struggle to construct training environments that are controllable, scalable, and account for multimodal data.
尽管先前的工作在提高训练数据质量方面取得了令人鼓舞的进展，但现有的环境仍然以文本为主，且现有方法在构建可控、可扩展并兼顾多模态数据的训练环境方面仍面临挑战。

Given this, we propose DocArena, a fully automated data curation pipeline building on the practical need for multimodal document search and question-answering. It transforms raw document collections into training environments for search agents without any human annotation.
鉴于此，我们提出了 DocArena，这是一个基于多模态文档搜索和问答实际需求的全自动数据整理流水线。它无需任何人工标注，即可将原始文档集合转化为搜索智能体的训练环境。

The pipeline first structures and indexes documents through MLLM-based visual perception, then profiles and leverage the cross-page information distribution to construct reasoning-intensive QA pairs, as well as performs cascaded quality assurance operations via MLLM.
该流水线首先通过基于多模态大模型（MLLM）的视觉感知对文档进行结构化和索引，然后分析并利用跨页面的信息分布来构建推理密集型的问答对，并通过 MLLM 执行级联质量保证操作。

We introduce DocArena-79K with QA pairs from 8,336 documents spanning 16 domains and 49 languages. We further design a Doc-Search agent infrastructure that decouples visual perception from the policy model, allowing text-based LLMs to serve as the reasoning backbone for multimodal document retrieval and QA.
我们推出了 DocArena-79K 数据集，其中包含来自 16 个领域和 49 种语言的 8,336 份文档的问答对。我们进一步设计了一种文档搜索智能体架构，将视觉感知与策略模型解耦，使基于文本的大语言模型（LLM）能够作为多模态文档检索和问答的推理核心。

Under a unified evaluation framework where only the policy model differs, experiments on six multimodal document scenarios and seven text-based QA benchmarks show that agents trained on DocArena data achieve the best performance on both retrieval accuracy and QA quality. Further analysis on agent search behaviors confirms the effectiveness and controllability of the constructed training environment.
在仅策略模型不同的统一评估框架下，针对六个多模态文档场景和七个基于文本的问答基准测试的实验表明，在 DocArena 数据上训练的智能体在检索准确率和问答质量方面均达到了最佳性能。对智能体搜索行为的进一步分析证实了所构建训练环境的有效性和可控性。
