# Beyond Aesthetics: Quantifying Information Loss in Turbid Scenes
# 超越美学：量化浑浊场景中的信息丢失

**Abstract:** Visibility in underwater environments degrades rapidly under turbid conditions, yet the effects on computer-vision models remain unclear. This issue is compounded by reliance on synthetic turbidity datasets, which may misrepresent real-world information loss. 

**摘要：** 在浑浊环境下，水下环境的能见度会迅速下降，但这对计算机视觉模型的影响尚不明确。由于过度依赖合成的浑浊数据集，这一问题变得更加复杂，因为这些数据集可能无法准确反映现实世界中的信息丢失情况。

To address this gap, we introduce the Turbid Underwater Baseline (TUB) dataset, comprising 1,320 images captured under extreme turbidity and over 16,000 high-confidence ground-truth segmentation masks. 

为了填补这一空白，我们引入了浑浊水下基准（Turbid Underwater Baseline, TUB）数据集，其中包含 1,320 张在极端浑浊条件下拍摄的图像，以及超过 16,000 个高置信度的真实分割掩码（ground-truth segmentation masks）。

We additionally propose PCD, a metric derived from phase congruency maps that is invariant to contrast and aims to capture the loss of structural information in real turbidity. 

此外，我们提出了一种名为 PCD 的度量指标。该指标源自相位一致性图（phase congruency maps），具有对比度不变性，旨在捕捉真实浑浊环境下的结构信息丢失。

We show that PCD correlates strongly with the performance of instance segmentation models on both real and synthetic turbid images, whereas common metrics in the field show weak to no correlation at all. The dataset and relevant code can be found on the project page.

研究表明，PCD 与实例分割模型在真实和合成浑浊图像上的表现具有很强的相关性，而该领域常用的其他指标则表现出微弱甚至完全没有相关性。数据集及相关代码可在项目页面找到。
