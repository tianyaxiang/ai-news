# LCG: Long-Context Consistent Image Generation with Sparse Relational Attention
# LCG：基于稀疏关系注意力的长上下文一致性图像生成

**Abstract:** Recent image generation models achieve impressive quality in single-image synthesis, but often fail to maintain consistency across sequential outputs, as required in comics, storyboards, and visual narratives. We propose Long-Context Generation (LCG), a framework for long-context multi-image text-to-image generation, to improve consistency and scalability in long-context multi-image generation.

**摘要：** 近期的图像生成模型在单图合成方面取得了令人瞩目的质量，但在漫画、分镜脚本和视觉叙事等场景所需的连续输出中，往往难以保持一致性。我们提出了长上下文生成（Long-Context Generation, LCG）框架，这是一种用于长上下文多图像文本生成图像的框架，旨在提高长上下文多图生成的一致性和可扩展性。

LCG employs the Sparse Relational Attention (SRA) mechanism to selectively attend to core features across extended visual contexts, ensuring that the propagation of semantic and layout information remains computationally tractable.

LCG 采用了稀疏关系注意力（Sparse Relational Attention, SRA）机制，能够跨扩展的视觉上下文选择性地关注核心特征，从而确保语义和布局信息的传播在计算上保持可控。

To enforce semantic alignment, we introduce the Routing Consistency Constraint (RCC), which leverages identity-aware masks to align structural patterns across generation branches, effectively mitigating drift in appearance even in complex multi-character scenes.

为了强化语义对齐，我们引入了路由一致性约束（Routing Consistency Constraint, RCC），利用身份感知掩码（identity-aware masks）来对齐不同生成分支间的结构模式，即使在复杂的多角色场景中，也能有效缓解外观漂移问题。

To support training and evaluation in this setting, we construct the Long-Context Consistency Dataset (LCCD), a large-scale synthetic dataset comprising character-centric multi-image sequences spanning varied situational contexts. LCCD contains 600K training sequences and a separate 1K test set, with each sequence containing 6 to 20 images.

为了支持在此设置下的训练与评估，我们构建了长上下文一致性数据集（Long-Context Consistency Dataset, LCCD）。这是一个大规模合成数据集，包含跨越多种情境、以角色为中心的多图像序列。LCCD 包含 60 万个训练序列和 1000 个独立的测试集，每个序列包含 6 到 20 张图像。

The experiments demonstrate that LCG outperforms the compared baselines in prompt alignment and character consistency for long-context image generation, including multi-character scenes.

实验表明，在长上下文图像生成（包括多角色场景）中，LCG 在提示词对齐和角色一致性方面均优于对比基准模型。
