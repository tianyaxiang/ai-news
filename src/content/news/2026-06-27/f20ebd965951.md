Make a private connection
建立私人连接

You need to share a link with your friend or scan a QR code from their phone, in person or during a video call, to make a connection and start messaging. The channel through which you share the link does not have to be secure - it is enough that you can confirm who sent you the message and that your SimpleX connection is established. After you connect, you can verify connection security code.
您需要与朋友分享链接，或在面对面/视频通话时扫描对方手机上的二维码，以建立连接并开始发送消息。您分享链接的渠道无需是安全的——只要您能确认是谁发送了消息，并且您的 SimpleX 连接已建立即可。连接后，您可以验证连接安全码。
