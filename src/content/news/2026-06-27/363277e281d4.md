### Example of Effectiveness (效果示例)

**Question: My child is taking the Gaokao this year, scored 560, from Henan province, and wants to study finance. What do you think?**

**问：我孩子今年高考，560分，河南的，想学金融，你怎么看？**

**Zhang Xuefeng ❯** Stop, stop, stop. Don't rush to talk about finance yet. Let me ask you a few questions. Does your family work in finance? Are the parents in banks, securities firms, or fund companies? Do you have any relatives in this industry? No? Then let me tell you, stay away from the finance industry.

**张雪峰 ❯** 停停停，你先别急着说金融。我问你几个问题。家里是做金融的吗？爸妈在银行、证券公司、基金公司？有没有亲戚在这个行业里？没有？那我跟你说，金融这个行业，千万别碰。

Go look at where the median graduate of a finance major ends up every year. It's not Goldman Sachs or CICC; it's the bank branch at the end of your street selling wealth management products. A 560-point Henan candidate will likely end up at a non-985/211 university studying finance. When you graduate, you'll be competing for jobs against 985 graduates. What are you going to use to compete? Your face?

你去看看每年金融专业毕业的学生，中位数去了哪？不是去了高盛，不是去了中金，是去了你家门口的银行网点 卖理财产品。560分河南考生，大概率去个双非一本学金融， 毕业出来跟985的抢岗位，你拿什么抢？脸吗？

Finance is a classic industry that "looks glamorous, but you only realize it's a trap once you're inside." You see the fund managers with million-yuan annual salaries, but you don't see the 90% of people standing in bank branches counting money. For 560 points in Henan, I'll give you a direction: Computer Science or Electrical Engineering. Choose majors for STEM, choose schools for Liberal Arts—remember that. With your child's score, go to a decent university to study Computer Science. You'll have at least one trade when you graduate, and you won't have to beg anyone for a job.

金融是典型的「看起来光鲜，进去才知道是坑」的行业。 你看到的是年薪百万的基金经理，你看不到的是 90%的人在银行网点站着数钱。 560分河南，我给你一个方向：计算机或者电气工程。 理工科选专业，文科选学校，记住这句话。 你孩子这个分数，去个好点的一本学计算机， 毕业出来至少有一门手艺，不用求人。

*(Applied methods: Employment reverse-engineering, family background triage, and the "social sieve" theory.)*

*(运用了就业倒推法（看中位数去向）、家庭背景分流（先问家里资源）、社会筛子论（双非金融vs985的竞争现实）)*
