# Implementation of reinforcement learning in chemical reaction networks: application to phototaxis as curiosity-driven exploration
# 在化学反应网络中实现强化学习：作为好奇心驱动探索的趋光性应用

**Abstract:** Living systems navigate environments using noisy and incomplete sensory signals. In unicellular algae, phototaxis is often modeled as a mechanistic run--tumble process driven by stimulus--response rules. However, such descriptions overlook how organisms actively sample their environment to reduce sensory ambiguity. From a minimal cognition perspective, we reframe this navigation as a subjective, information-driven sensorimotor process.

**摘要：** 生命系统利用嘈杂且不完整的感官信号在环境中导航。在单细胞藻类中，趋光性通常被建模为由刺激-反应规则驱动的机械式“游动-翻滚”（run-tumble）过程。然而，此类描述忽略了生物体如何主动采样环境以减少感官模糊性。从最小认知（minimal cognition）的角度出发，我们将这种导航重新定义为一个主观的、信息驱动的感知运动过程。

To this end, we propose a framework linking a Partially Observable Markov Decision Process (POMDP) with biochemical reaction dynamics. Environmental variables are hidden, while the cell updates a minimal internal state from each observation through a memoryless Bayesian step. These internal dynamics balance orienting toward light with exploratory reorientation and can be implemented through Chemical-Reaction-Network Ordinary Differential Equations (CRN--ODEs).

为此，我们提出了一个将部分可观测马尔可夫决策过程（POMDP）与生化反应动力学联系起来的框架。环境变量是隐藏的，而细胞通过无记忆的贝叶斯步骤，根据每次观测更新其最小内部状态。这些内部动力学在朝向光源的定向与探索性的重新定向之间取得平衡，并可以通过化学反应网络常微分方程（CRN-ODEs）来实现。

Our model includes a biophysical observation process for photoreception and a chemically computable polynomial bound on information gain. Using Inverse Reinforcement Learning (IRL) on 30 experimentally recorded Chlamydomonas trajectories, we infer the behavioral objective consistent with observed phototactic motion and benchmark the resulting dynamics with standard Stochastic Simulation Algorithm (SSA) baselines.

我们的模型包含了一个用于光感受的生物物理观测过程，以及一个可化学计算的信息增益多项式界限。通过对 30 条实验记录的衣藻（Chlamydomonas）轨迹应用逆强化学习（IRL），我们推断出与观察到的趋光运动一致的行为目标，并利用标准的随机模拟算法（SSA）基准对所得动力学进行了评估。

Our model reproduces the empirical alignment-to-light distribution, comparable to objective SSA baselines on this dataset. Within this framework, run--tumble alternation emerges as an information-acquisition strategy: tumbling reorients the cell to sample new sensory configurations and resolve sensor ambiguity, demonstrating how intracellular biochemical networks can support adaptive information-seeking behavior in cellular navigation.

我们的模型重现了经验性的趋光对齐分布，在该数据集上与客观的 SSA 基准相当。在此框架内，“游动-翻滚”的交替出现作为一种信息获取策略：翻滚使细胞重新定向以采样新的感官配置并解决传感器模糊性，这证明了细胞内生化网络如何支持细胞导航中的自适应信息寻求行为。
