# Blurry Window Attention

**Abstract:** The Softmax Attention operation in Transformer language models has a quadratic complexity in the sequence length and a growing state size in the form of KV cache, which becomes a bottleneck in long context scenarios.

**摘要：** Transformer 语言模型中的 Softmax 注意力操作在序列长度上具有二次复杂度，且以 KV 缓存的形式存在不断增长的状态大小，这在长上下文场景中成为了瓶颈。

To overcome this limitation, alternative architectures with linear complexity and finite state size have been introduced, such as State-Space Models (SSMs), Linear Attention (LA), and Attention with Bounded-memory Control (ABC).

为了克服这一限制，研究人员引入了具有线性复杂度和有限状态大小的替代架构，例如状态空间模型 (SSMs)、线性注意力 (LA) 和有界内存控制注意力 (ABC)。

Though linear models achieve similar language perplexity as Transformers, they are still behind in tasks which require retrieval or recall of specific information.

尽管线性模型在语言困惑度上能达到与 Transformer 相似的水平，但在需要检索或回忆特定信息的任务中，它们仍处于劣势。

In this work, we introduce Blurry Window Attention (BLA) a novel ABC method inspired by SSMs. BLA stores a frequency window from which a blurry KV history is reconstructed via interpolation using Dirichlet kernels.

在这项工作中，我们引入了模糊窗口注意力 (Blurry Window Attention, BLA)，这是一种受 SSM 启发的新型 ABC 方法。BLA 存储一个频率窗口，并利用狄利克雷核 (Dirichlet kernels) 通过插值重建模糊的 KV 历史记录。

BLA can be understood as a generalization of Sliding Window Attention (SWA) depending on the Dirichlet kernels resolution or as a special case of the Gated Slot Attention (GSA), where the decay factor is implemented with Dirichlet kernels.

BLA 可以被理解为滑动窗口注意力 (SWA) 的一种泛化形式（取决于狄利克雷核的分辨率），也可以看作门控槽注意力 (GSA) 的一种特殊情况，其中衰减因子是通过狄利克雷核实现的。

We describe in details the theory and efficient implementation of BLA.

我们详细描述了 BLA 的理论及其高效实现方式。

On the Multi-Query Associate Recall (MQAR) synthetic task, we show that the state efficiency of BLA is 8$\times$ better than SWA and is competitive with popular linear attention models, and in the RegBench synthetic task, only BLA and SWA improve their performance as the state size grows among the linear models we tested.

在多查询关联回忆 (MQAR) 合成任务中，我们证明了 BLA 的状态效率比 SWA 高出 8 倍，并与主流的线性注意力模型具有竞争力；而在 RegBench 合成任务中，在我们测试的线性模型中，只有 BLA 和 SWA 的性能会随着状态大小的增加而提升。
