### Interpretable Temporal Facial-Region Motion Analysis for In-the-Wild Parkinson's Disease Video Classification
### 基于可解释性面部区域时序运动分析的野外帕金森病视频分类研究

**Abstract:** Reduced facial expressivity is a common motor manifestation of Parkinson's disease (PD), often described as hypomimia or facial bradykinesia. This paper examines whether temporal motion descriptors extracted from facial-region keypoints can support in-the-wild PD-related video classification on the YouTubePD benchmark.
**摘要：** 面部表情减少是帕金森病（PD）常见的运动表现，通常被称为面具脸（hypomimia）或面部运动迟缓。本文探讨了从面部区域关键点提取的时序运动描述符是否能够支持 YouTubePD 基准测试中野外（in-the-wild）帕金森病相关视频的分类。

Each video is represented using geometric descriptors from 14 predefined facial regions. Static geometry, normalized geometry, velocity-based descriptors, relative-velocity descriptors, and a GRU sequence baseline are compared under the same binary classification protocol.
每段视频均使用来自 14 个预定义面部区域的几何描述符进行表示。在相同的二分类协议下，研究对比了静态几何、归一化几何、基于速度的描述符、相对速度描述符以及 GRU 序列基线模型。

To assess stability and interpretability, the study includes seed-robustness analysis, region-level ablation, and permutation importance. The best result is obtained with normalized velocity descriptors and a Random Forest classifier, reaching a balanced accuracy of 0.826 and an AUROC of 0.855 on the held-out test split.
为了评估稳定性和可解释性，本研究包含了随机种子稳健性分析、区域级消融实验以及排列重要性分析。实验结果表明，使用归一化速度描述符结合随机森林分类器效果最佳，在留出测试集上达到了 0.826 的平衡准确率和 0.855 的 AUROC 值。

Across 10 random seeds, this representation remains stable, with balanced accuracy of 0.810 +/- 0.018 and AUROC of 0.855 +/- 0.005. Overall, the results suggest that normalized facial-region motion is a lightweight and interpretable representation for YouTubePD video classification.
在 10 次随机种子测试中，该表示方法保持稳定，平衡准确率为 0.810 ± 0.018，AUROC 为 0.855 ± 0.005。总体而言，研究结果表明，归一化面部区域运动是一种用于 YouTubePD 视频分类的轻量级且具有可解释性的表示方法。

The study is framed as a benchmark-level analysis and does not claim clinical severity assessment or MDS-UPDRS facial-expression scoring.
本研究被定位为基准测试级别的分析，并不声称能够进行临床严重程度评估或 MDS-UPDRS 面部表情评分。
