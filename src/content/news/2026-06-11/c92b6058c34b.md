# Physical AI: What It Is and What It Is Not
# 物理人工智能：它是什么，又不是什么

A quick guide to separating Physical AI from world models, embodied AI, physics AI, and digital twins.
一份将物理人工智能（Physical AI）与世界模型、具身智能（Embodied AI）、物理学人工智能（Physics AI）以及数字孪生区分开来的快速指南。

Physical AI is the new buzzword. NVIDIA is talking about it, consulting firms are talking about it, and so are the investors and robotics startups. They are all talking about Physical AI. Suddenly, the term is everywhere.
“物理人工智能”是目前最新的流行语。英伟达在谈论它，咨询公司在谈论它，投资者和机器人初创公司也在谈论它。大家都在谈论物理人工智能。突然之间，这个词无处不在。

But what exactly is Physical AI? And just as importantly, what is it not? In this post, let’s demystify the term. We’ll not only explain the concept itself but also try to separate it from nearby terms it’s often confused with — world models, embodied AI, physics AI, and digital twins.
但物理人工智能究竟是什么？同样重要的是，它又不是什么？在这篇文章中，让我们揭开这个词的神秘面纱。我们不仅会解释这一概念本身，还会尝试将其与经常混淆的相关术语——世界模型、具身智能、物理学人工智能和数字孪生——区分开来。

### 1. What Physical AI Is
### 1. 什么是物理人工智能

A working definition I find useful is this: Physical AI is AI that closes the loop between perception and action in the real physical world.
我发现一个实用的定义是：物理人工智能是指在真实物理世界中闭合感知与行动循环的人工智能。

Most AIs today live on screens. They do tasks such as classifying images, summarizing documents, drafting emails, or recommending which movie to watch next. All of them are certainly useful works, but they all happen in the digital world.
当今大多数人工智能都存在于屏幕上。它们执行诸如图像分类、文档摘要、起草电子邮件或推荐下一部电影等任务。这些当然都是有用的工作，但它们都发生在数字世界中。

A physical AI is different than that. It breaks out of the digital world and actually interacts with the real world. It takes in what’s happening around it through sensors, works out what needs to be done, and finally actually does it.
物理人工智能则不同。它突破了数字世界的界限，真正与现实世界进行交互。它通过传感器获取周围发生的情况，计算出需要完成的任务，并最终将其付诸实践。

The action might be achieved through a robot arm, a humanoid, a drone, a self-driving car, or an industrial machine on a factory floor. The output of Physical AI is no longer just text on a screen; it’s an actual movement in the world.
这种行动可以通过机械臂、人形机器人、无人机、自动驾驶汽车或工厂车间的工业机器来实现。物理人工智能的输出不再仅仅是屏幕上的文字，而是现实世界中的实际动作。

Think about picking up a cup. A chatbot that simply explains how to do it is not Physical AI. But a robot that can see the cup, and adjust its gripper to move the cup to where you want, that’s much closer to a Physical AI.
以拿起杯子为例。仅仅解释如何拿起杯子的聊天机器人不是物理人工智能。但如果是一个能看到杯子，并调整抓取器将杯子移动到你想要位置的机器人，那就非常接近物理人工智能了。

So, Physical AI isn’t really a single model doing one thing. It’s a whole system that has to: sense, understand, predict, plan, act, and receive feedback.
因此，物理人工智能并不是指执行单一任务的某个模型，而是一个必须具备感知、理解、预测、规划、行动并接收反馈的完整系统。

### 2. Physical AI vs World Model
### 2. 物理人工智能 vs 世界模型

The term World Models often appears in the same conversations of robots, autonomous agents, simulation, synthetic environments, etc. If we are not careful, it’s very easy to slide into treating it as just another way of saying Physical AI. But that’s not true.
“世界模型”一词经常出现在关于机器人、自主智能体、仿真和合成环境等的讨论中。如果不加注意，很容易将其视为物理人工智能的另一种说法。但事实并非如此。

World Model is, as the name suggests, a model. More concretely, this model is an internal representation of how an environment changes. What naturally comes out of it is the prediction capability. It lets an agent in that environment anticipate what comes next.
顾名思义，世界模型就是一个模型。更具体地说，这个模型是对环境如何变化的一种内部表征。它自然而然地产生了预测能力，使环境中的智能体能够预见接下来会发生什么。

If I move forward, what do I run into? If I push this object, does it slide or topple? If that car changes lanes, where will it be in two seconds? All those questions can be answered with that prediction capability.
如果我向前移动，会撞到什么？如果我推这个物体，它是会滑动还是倾倒？如果那辆车变道，两秒后它会在哪里？所有这些问题都可以通过这种预测能力来回答。

A robot might use a world model to imagine what could happen if it approaches the cup from different angles. But the key realization here is that the world model itself is not the robot, not the gripper, not the motor controller, and not the full system that actually moves the cup.
机器人可能会利用世界模型来想象从不同角度接近杯子时会发生什么。但关键在于，世界模型本身并不是机器人，不是抓取器，不是电机控制器，也不是真正移动杯子的完整系统。

That’s actually the key relationship here: A world model can live inside of Physical AI, especially for simulation, training, and planning. But on its own, it only predicts possible futures; it doesn’t act. Without sensors/controls/actuators that connect to the real world, it can only imagine.
这正是两者关系的关键所在：世界模型可以存在于物理人工智能内部，特别是在仿真、训练和规划方面。但就其本身而言，它只能预测可能的未来，而不能采取行动。如果没有连接到现实世界的传感器、控制器或执行器，它只能进行“想象”。

To summarize: world models predict what could happen. Physical AI acts in the real world.
总结：世界模型预测可能发生的事情，而物理人工智能在现实世界中采取行动。

### 3. Physical AI vs Embodied AI
### 3. 物理人工智能 vs 具身智能

This is the pair people mix up most. You can see that people and many companies use the two terms interchangeably. In fact, in a recent post from Cambridge Consultants, Capgemini’s innovation chief literally called it “physical AI, or embodied AI as some call it.”
这是最容易混淆的一对概念。你可以看到人们和许多公司将这两个术语互换使用。事实上，在剑桥咨询公司（Cambridge Consultants）最近的一篇文章中，凯捷（Capgemini）的创新主管直接称其为“物理人工智能，或者有些人称之为具身智能”。

But I believe there’s a useful distinction we can keep in mind, and the key is about what they emphasize. Physical AI emphasizes what a system does, and this includes perceiving, deciding, and acting in the real world.
但我认为我们可以记住一个有用的区别，关键在于它们各自强调的重点。物理人工智能强调系统“做什么”，包括在现实世界中感知、决策和行动。

Embodied AI, on the other hand, is more about what shapes the intelligence. The “embodied” in its name comes from the embodiment hypothesis. That hypothesis believes that intelligence doesn’t just live in software you load onto a machine. It’s growing out of having a body. With the body, it can sense, try out things, and learn from what happens.
另一方面，具身智能更多地关注“是什么塑造了智能”。其名称中的“具身”（Embodied）源于具身假设。该假设认为，智能不仅仅存在于加载到机器上的软件中，它源于拥有一个身体。有了身体，它才能感知、尝试事物并从发生的事情中学习。

The body here isn’t the output device at the end of the process; it’s effectively part of how it learns to think in the first place. In practice, however, Physical AI and Embodied AI usually go hand in hand. This is especially true in practical robotics discussions, as many real robots are both embodied AI and Physical AI.
这里的身体并不是流程末端的输出设备；它实际上是智能体最初学习思考方式的一部分。然而在实践中，物理人工智能和具身智能通常是相辅相成的。在实际的机器人讨论中尤其如此，因为许多真实的机器人既是具身智能，也是物理人工智能。

To summarize: Physical AI is about acting in the world. Embodied AI is about intelligence being shaped by a body and its interaction with the environment.
总结：物理人工智能关注在世界中行动；具身智能关注智能如何通过身体及其与环境的交互来塑造。

### 4. Physical AI vs Physics AI
### 4. 物理人工智能 vs 物理学人工智能

This pair is easy to confuse if you are not careful in reading their names. One is Physical, the other is Physics. When people say Physics AI, what they usually refer to is AI methods that incorporate the laws of physics.
如果不仔细阅读名称，这一对也很容易混淆。一个是“物理的”（Physical），另一个是“物理学”（Physics）。当人们说“物理学人工智能”时，他们通常指的是结合了物理定律的人工智能方法。

A good example is the physics-informed neural network, or PINN. Technically, PINN is just an ordinary neural network. What makes them different is the loss formulation.
