### Wait. Which iPad Do You Own?
### 等等，你用的是哪款 iPad？

There are so many iPads out there, it can be hard to identify the exact model you own, and therefore know which accessories are compatible. Do you have the iPad Pro from 2017 with the 10.5-inch screen? Or the 9.7-inch one from 2016? It doesn't help that Apple avoids the sequential naming structure it uses for the iPhone. But no matter.

市面上的 iPad 型号繁多，很难准确辨认你手头的是哪一款，从而确定哪些配件兼容。你用的是 2017 年 10.5 英寸屏幕的 iPad Pro 吗？还是 2016 年的 9.7 英寸版本？苹果不像 iPhone 那样采用连续的命名规则，这让辨认变得更困难。不过没关系。

Here's how to find your model: Look at the back of your iPad. Etched on the back cover, near where it says "Designed by Apple in California," should be a model number starting with the letter A. Head to this website from Apple. Look for your corresponding model number, and that's it! You found your iPad model. (Pro tip: Use your browser's "Find in Page" function to type in your model number and locate it quickly.)

以下是查找型号的方法：查看 iPad 背面。在后盖上靠近“Designed by Apple in California”字样的地方，应该刻有一个以字母 A 开头的型号代码。前往苹果官网的对应页面，查找你的型号代码，就这么简单！你已经找到了你的 iPad 型号。（专业提示：使用浏览器的“在页面上查找”功能输入型号代码，可以快速定位。）

### Best Stylus: Apple Pencil Pro
### 最佳触控笔：Apple Pencil Pro

The Apple Pencil is one of the most useful tools you can add to the iPad. Specific models work with certain iPads. It's excellent for drawing, with an imperceptible delay as the Pencil moves across the glass display. Like a normal pencil, your lines get thicker as you press down harder. The Pencil is also great for navigating iPadOS, which has handwriting support in various search fields so you don't need to switch to the keyboard to type—and it's handy for signing or marking up documents.

Apple Pencil 是你可以为 iPad 添加的最实用工具之一。特定型号仅适用于特定的 iPad。它非常适合绘画，当笔尖在玻璃屏幕上移动时，几乎感觉不到延迟。就像普通铅笔一样，用力越重，线条越粗。这支笔也非常适合操作 iPadOS，系统在各种搜索栏中支持手写输入，因此你无需切换到键盘打字——它在签署或标注文档时也非常方便。

Often available for not much more, the Apple Pencil Pro is the stylus to buy, though it's compatible with far fewer iPads. It has a “squeeze” capability that triggers the tool palette (you can still double-tap) along with a haptic engine inside that delivers feedback as you squeeze. There's a built-in gyroscope sensor, which allows you to change the orientation of the tools as you twist it (for finer control), and it has support for Apple's Find My Network in case you accidentally lose it.

Apple Pencil Pro 通常价格相差不大，是目前最值得购买的触控笔，尽管它兼容的 iPad 型号较少。它具备“挤压”功能，可以调出工具面板（你仍然可以使用双击操作），内部还配有触觉引擎，在挤压时提供反馈。它内置了陀螺仪传感器，当你旋转笔身时可以改变工具的方向（实现更精细的控制），并且支持苹果的“查找”网络，以防你不慎丢失。
