# Uncertainty-aware Multi-fidelity Closure via Conditional Normalizing Flows
# 基于条件归一化流的具有不确定性感知能力的多保真度闭合模型

**Abstract:** Reduced-order models (ROMs) provide an efficient surrogate for complex multiscale systems, but their predictive accuracy is often compromised by truncation errors and the inadequate representation of interactions between resolved and unresolved scales. The missing effect of truncated (unresolved) scales on ROM (resolved) scales is often denoted as the closure problem.

**摘要：** 降阶模型（ROMs）为复杂的多尺度系统提供了高效的替代方案，但其预测精度往往受到截断误差以及对已解析尺度与未解析尺度之间相互作用描述不足的影响。截断（未解析）尺度对 ROM（已解析）尺度的缺失影响通常被称为闭合问题。

In this work, we formulate ROM closure modeling as a multi-fidelity (MF) learning problem and propose an uncertainty-aware MF framework based on conditional normalizing flow to enhance ROM predictive accuracy. The proposed approach learns a probabilistic mapping from low-fidelity (LF) ROM coefficients to high-fidelity (HF) coefficients, thereby improving predictive fidelity while quantifying the uncertainty associated with the learned closure.

在这项工作中，我们将 ROM 闭合建模表述为一个多保真度（MF）学习问题，并提出了一种基于条件归一化流的具有不确定性感知能力的 MF 框架，以提高 ROM 的预测精度。该方法学习从低保真度（LF）ROM 系数到高保真度（HF）系数的概率映射，从而在提高预测保真度的同时，量化与所学闭合模型相关的不确定性。

Two correction strategies are investigated: direct learning, in which HF coefficients are predicted directly from LF inputs, and residual learning, which learns the discrepancy between LF and HF coefficients and uses it to recover the corrected HF solution. The framework is demonstrated on a vortex merging problem governed by the two-dimensional Navier Stokes equations.

本文研究了两种校正策略：直接学习（Direct Learning），即直接从 LF 输入预测 HF 系数；以及残差学习（Residual Learning），即学习 LF 和 HF 系数之间的差异，并利用该差异来恢复校正后的 HF 解。该框架在由二维纳维-斯托克斯方程控制的涡旋合并问题上进行了验证。

Results show that both correction strategies improve ROM accuracy over uncorrected ROM, with residual learning achieving consistently better performance than direct learning. Moreover, the two proposed deep generative model-based strategies provide uncertainty quantification for the corrected ROM coefficients, which is critical for assessing prediction confidence and supporting the reliable use of ROMs in practical applications.

结果表明，两种校正策略均提高了 ROM 相对于未校正 ROM 的精度，其中残差学习的表现始终优于直接学习。此外，这两种基于深度生成模型的策略为校正后的 ROM 系数提供了不确定性量化，这对于评估预测置信度以及支持 ROM 在实际应用中的可靠使用至关重要。
