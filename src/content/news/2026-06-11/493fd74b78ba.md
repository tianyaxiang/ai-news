# A Controlled Audit of Pretraining Contamination in Public Medical Vision-Language Benchmarks
# 公共医学视觉语言基准测试中预训练数据污染的受控审计

**Abstract:** Medical vision-language models (VLMs) are evaluated on public benchmarks whose images and question-answer pairs have been freely downloadable for years, yet reported accuracy assumes these examples were absent from pretraining. We audit open VLMs on SLAKE-En, PathVQA, VQA-RAD, and an auxiliary public OmniMedVQA mirror using four detector families: image-side near-neighbour overlap against PMC-OA-beta, canonical-order exchangeability, cohort-relative Min-K%++ tail enrichment, and cross-model top-K overlap.

**摘要：** 医学视觉语言模型（VLM）通常在公共基准测试集上进行评估，而这些基准测试中的图像和问答对多年来一直可以免费下载，但模型报告的准确率却假设这些样本并未出现在预训练数据中。我们使用四类检测方法对 SLAKE-En、PathVQA、VQA-RAD 以及辅助公共镜像 OmniMedVQA 上的开源 VLM 进行了审计，这些方法包括：针对 PMC-OA-beta 的图像侧近邻重叠检测、规范顺序可交换性检测、队列相对 Min-K%++ 尾部富集检测以及跨模型 Top-K 重叠检测。

We find measurable image-side source overlap on SLAKE-En: 19.8% of images are flagged under SigLIP-B-16 and 4.2% under SigLIP-SO400M, while out-of-domain controls produce 0/2000 flags. Manual adjudication shows same-modality, same-projection matches to different patients rather than verified pixel-level duplicates, so we interpret this as source or distributional overlap rather than confirmed per-image memorization.

我们发现 SLAKE-En 上存在可测量的图像侧源重叠：在 SigLIP-B-16 模型下有 19.8% 的图像被标记，在 SigLIP-SO400M 下有 4.2% 被标记，而域外对照组的标记率为 0/2000。人工核查显示，这些匹配属于相同模态、相同投影但针对不同患者的图像，而非像素级的重复，因此我们将其解释为源数据或分布上的重叠，而非确凿的单图记忆现象。

On the text side, Qwen2.5-VL on SLAKE-En shows a canonical-order exchangeability signal that survives ordering ablation and external non-medical baselines. On the OmniMedVQA mirror, exchangeability fires for five medical and general VLMs while BLIP-2 remains clean. In contrast, cohort-relative Min-K%++ tail enrichment and cross-model top-K overlap collapse under an external pre-domain baseline: BLIP-2 reproduces the apparent positive signals despite lacking plausible medical-VQA exposure. We conclude that these cohort-relative detectors are unreliable as standalone membership-inference signals on small medical-VLM cohorts.

在文本方面，Qwen2.5-VL 在 SLAKE-En 上表现出规范顺序可交换性信号，该信号在顺序消融实验和外部非医学基准测试中依然存在。在 OmniMedVQA 镜像上，五个医学和通用 VLM 均触发了可交换性信号，而 BLIP-2 则保持“干净”。相比之下，队列相对的 Min-K%++ 尾部富集和跨模型 Top-K 重叠检测在外部预域基准下失效：尽管 BLIP-2 缺乏合理的医学 VQA 训练，但它仍重现了明显的阳性信号。我们得出结论，这些队列相对的检测器在小型医学 VLM 队列中作为独立的成员推理信号是不可靠的。
