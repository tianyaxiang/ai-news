# Conformal Risk Prediction for Non-Alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease Using Gradient Boosting with Distribution-Free Coverages
# 基于分布无关覆盖的梯度提升算法在非酒精性脂肪肝风险预测中的应用

**Abstract:** Non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD) affects roughly 25% of global adults, posing substantial hepatic and cardiovascular risks. Yet, population-level screening tools remain inadequate. We present Method, a machine-learning framework for NAFLD risk prediction coupling gradient-boosted decision trees with conformal prediction to yield calibrated, distribution-free coverage guarantees on individual risk estimates.

**摘要：** 非酒精性脂肪肝（NAFLD）影响着全球约 25% 的成年人，带来了巨大的肝脏和心血管健康风险。然而，目前针对人群层面的筛查工具仍然不足。我们提出了一种名为 Method 的机器学习框架，用于 NAFLD 风险预测。该框架将梯度提升决策树与共形预测（Conformal Prediction）相结合，从而为个体风险评估提供经过校准且分布无关的覆盖保证。

It integrates a mutual-information-based stability selection procedure to identify a compact, clinically interpretable feature subset via bootstrap resampling, constructing prediction sets whose marginal coverage provably exceeds a user-specified confidence level.

该框架集成了一种基于互信息的稳定性选择程序，通过自助重采样（bootstrap resampling）识别出一个紧凑且具有临床可解释性的特征子集，并构建出其边际覆盖率可证明超过用户指定置信水平的预测集。

We evaluated Method on a multicenter cohort from Guangzhou, China (primary n=2,187; external validation n=412) using 78 candidate features across demographics, metabolic biomarkers, and lifestyle factors. Method achieves an AUROC of 0.912 internally and 0.891 externally, outperforming deep neural networks, TabNet, support vector machines, and logistic regression.

我们在来自中国广州的多中心队列（主要队列 n=2,187；外部验证队列 n=412）上评估了 Method，使用了涵盖人口统计学、代谢生物标志物和生活方式因素的 78 个候选特征。Method 在内部验证中达到了 0.912 的 AUROC，在外部验证中达到了 0.891，表现优于深度神经网络、TabNet、支持向量机和逻辑回归。

Conformal prediction sets achieve 91.3% empirical coverage at the 90% nominal level. A three-tier risk stratification derived from these scores separates the population into distinct groups, with the high-risk subgroup showing a 12-month progression rate 4.7 times that of the low-risk tier.

在 90% 的标称置信水平下，共形预测集实现了 91.3% 的经验覆盖率。基于这些评分得出的三级风险分层将人群划分为不同的组别，其中高风险亚组的 12 个月进展率是低风险组的 4.7 倍。

The selected features -- notably waist circumference, ALT, GGT, triglycerides, fasting glucose, and BMI -- align with established metabolic risk factors, providing biological plausibility.

所选特征（特别是腰围、谷丙转氨酶 ALT、谷氨酰转肽酶 GGT、甘油三酯、空腹血糖和体重指数 BMI）与既定的代谢风险因素相吻合，具有生物学合理性。
