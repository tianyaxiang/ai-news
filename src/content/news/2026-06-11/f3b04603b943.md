### The Best Kindle: Amazon Kindle Paperwhite (2024, 12th Generation)
### 最佳 Kindle：亚马逊 Kindle Paperwhite（2024 年，第 12 代）

You came here to find out one thing: What is the best Kindle? The Kindle Paperwhite is Amazon's most popular Kindle and our favorite after testing every model, thanks to its great range of features. You'll get a large display with high resolution and warm lighting, and the technology inside has a fast and smooth performance. Amazon launched the 12th-generation Paperwhite in late 2024, and it's been our new favorite ever since.
你来到这里是为了弄清楚一件事：最好的 Kindle 是哪款？Kindle Paperwhite 是亚马逊最受欢迎的 Kindle，也是我们在测试了所有型号后最心仪的一款，这得益于它出色的功能配置。你将获得一块高分辨率的大屏幕和暖光阅读灯，其内部技术也带来了快速流畅的性能表现。亚马逊于 2024 年底推出了第 12 代 Paperwhite，自那以后它就成了我们的新宠。

Whether you want to read more or are tired of carrying physical books, all the extra features are worth the price. The basic Kindle may seem enticing, especially with the fun new colors it offers, but the Paperwhite will serve you better in the long run, especially if you're a nighttime reader like I am. The warm lighting is a great option on the eyes before bed.
无论你是想多读书，还是厌倦了携带实体书，它所有的额外功能都物有所值。入门款 Kindle 可能看起来很诱人，尤其是它提供的新颖配色，但从长远来看，Paperwhite 会更适合你，特别是如果你像我一样喜欢在晚上阅读的话。暖光阅读灯是睡前保护眼睛的绝佳选择。

If you already have a Paperwhite, the update isn't so major that you need to upgrade to the newest model, but it's the best to choose if you're shopping for a brand-new Kindle. You'll get a slightly larger screen and the highest contrast ratio on any Kindle, not just of the Paperwhites. The battery life also increased from two months to three months, and it has the thinnest form factor yet.
如果你已经拥有了一台 Paperwhite，这次更新的幅度并不大，没必要非得升级到最新款；但如果你正在选购全新的 Kindle，它是最佳选择。你将获得一块稍大的屏幕，以及所有 Kindle（不仅仅是 Paperwhite 系列）中最高的对比度。电池续航也从两个月提升到了三个月，并且它拥有迄今为止最轻薄的外观设计。

### The Best Kindle for Note-Taking: Kindle Scribe (2nd Gen, 2024)
### 最佳笔记型 Kindle：Kindle Scribe（第 2 代，2024 年）

If you're looking for a Kindle for note-taking and reading, you’ll want the second-generation Kindle Scribe. The 2024 version has the same 10.2-inch (300 ppi) paperlike display as the first iteration, but it has a white border for a more notebook-style look, and the new Premium Pen is not only included but also has a soft-tipped eraser at the end. Personally, this is my most-used Kindle because I love to scribble notes and ideas all day long.
如果你正在寻找一款既能记笔记又能阅读的 Kindle，那么你需要第二代 Kindle Scribe。2024 年版本拥有与第一代相同的 10.2 英寸（300 ppi）类纸显示屏，但它采用了白色边框，看起来更像笔记本，且附赠的新款高级触控笔（Premium Pen）末端还配有一个软头橡皮擦。就我个人而言，这是我使用频率最高的 Kindle，因为我喜欢整天随手涂写笔记和想法。

The second-gen Scribe also packs new software capabilities. Active Canvas lets you write directly in the book you're reading, though it won't work like true annotation—instead, the notes will appear in a small square on the screen like a Post-It note. That means it'll always be there if you resize the screen, but it didn't feel as natural as our testers would've liked. There's now Expandable Margins as well that let you write notes in the side panel of books, which feels a little more intuitive.
第二代 Scribe 还具备了新的软件功能。“Active Canvas”功能让你能直接在阅读的书籍上书写，虽然它不像真正的批注那样工作——笔记会像便利贴一样出现在屏幕上的一个小方框里。这意味着即使你调整屏幕大小，它也会一直存在，但我们的测试人员觉得这种体验不够自然。现在还有“可扩展页边距”功能，让你可以在书籍的侧边栏写笔记，这感觉稍微直观一些。
