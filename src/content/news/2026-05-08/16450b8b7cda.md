# Lookahead Drifting Model

**Abstract:** Recently, a new paradigm named *drifting model* has been proposed for mapping distributions, which achieves the SOTA image generation performance over ImageNet via one-step neural functional evaluation (NFE). The basic idea is to compute a drifting term at each training iteration and then push the output of the model towards the direction of the drifting term.

**摘要：** 最近，一种名为“漂移模型”（drifting model）的新范式被提出用于分布映射，该模型通过单步神经函数评估（NFE）在 ImageNet 上实现了最先进（SOTA）的图像生成性能。其基本思想是在每次训练迭代中计算一个漂移项，然后将模型的输出推向漂移项的方向。

In this paper, we propose a *lookahead drifting model*. At each training iteration, we compute a set of drifting terms sequentially. Each drifting term is calculated by making use of previously computed ones as well as the positive samples and the output of the model.

在本文中，我们提出了一种“前瞻漂移模型”（lookahead drifting model）。在每次训练迭代中，我们按顺序计算一组漂移项。每个漂移项的计算都利用了先前计算出的漂移项，以及正样本和模型的输出。

In principle, the drifting terms obtained at a later stage capture higher order gradient information towards the positive samples. At each training iteration, the model is optimized by pushing its output towards the direction of the (weighted) summation of the drifting terms. Experimental results on toy examples and CIFAR10 demonstrate the better performance of the new method than the baseline.

原则上，在后期阶段获得的漂移项能够捕获朝向正样本的高阶梯度信息。在每次训练迭代中，模型通过将其输出推向漂移项（加权）总和的方向来进行优化。在玩具示例和 CIFAR10 上的实验结果表明，该新方法比基线方法具有更好的性能。
