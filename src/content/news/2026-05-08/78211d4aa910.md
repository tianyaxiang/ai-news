# Ploopy Bean - 外置指点杆 (Trackpoint)

The most sensitive Bean there is. The Bean is a high-performance pointing stick, providing a precise and complete cursor experience in a small, convenient package.
这是目前最灵敏的“豆子”（Bean）。Bean 是一款高性能指点杆，在小巧便捷的机身中提供了精准且完整的鼠标指针操控体验。

Senses every single touch. Extreme sensitivity. The Bean is able to detect touches as small as 3 microns, so even the slightest touches of the Bean are easily detected.
感知每一次触碰。极致灵敏度。Bean 能够检测到小至 3 微米的触碰，因此即使是最轻微的触碰也能被轻松捕捉。

High-resolution drag scrolling. The Bean features high-resolution drag scrolling out of the box, delivering the most precise scrolling solution available.
高分辨率拖拽滚动。Bean 开箱即支持高分辨率拖拽滚动，提供目前市面上最精准的滚动解决方案。

Premium switches. The Bean has four Omron D2LS-21 switches, featuring a crisp actuation for superior feel.
优质微动开关。Bean 配备了四个欧姆龙（Omron）D2LS-21 微动开关，按压清脆，手感极佳。

Prime polling rate. A 1,000Hz polling rate ensures that the Bean feels snappy and responsive, especially when paired with high-frequency monitors.
顶级轮询率。1,000Hz 的轮询率确保了 Bean 的操作灵敏且响应迅速，特别是在搭配高刷新率显示器使用时表现尤为出色。

Make it your own. The Bean’s four buttons are easy to customize using VIA, a free online web tool. Macros, extended functionality, key combos and more are all possible. And best of all, your customizations travel with your Bean for ultimate portability.
打造专属你的设备。通过免费的在线网页工具 VIA，可以轻松自定义 Bean 的四个按键。宏命令、扩展功能、组合键等应有尽有。最棒的是，你的自定义设置会随设备存储，实现极致的便携性。

3D-printed and ready to go. Great surface finish. The Bean is designed so that the button surface has a great finish, a stylish texture that feels great.
3D 打印，即刻可用。出色的表面处理。Bean 的设计确保了按键表面拥有极佳的质感，时尚的纹理触感舒适。

Easy to repair and customize. 3D-printing means that new parts can always be made with your own 3D-printer. You can fix broken parts or print in new colours and make your Bean completely unique.
易于维修与定制。得益于 3D 打印技术，你可以随时用自己的 3D 打印机制作新零件。你可以修复损坏的部件，或打印不同颜色的外壳，让你的 Bean 独一无二。

Modifiable. Dozens of our community members have created incredible mods for Ploopy products, and more are being tweaked and released every day. The Bean’s 3D-printed design means you can modify the design files as much as you want.
可改装性。我们的社区成员已经为 Ploopy 产品创作了数十种令人惊叹的改装方案，并且每天都有新的改进和发布。Bean 的 3D 打印设计意味着你可以随心所欲地修改设计文件。

Open source, now and forever. Just like all of our kits, we’ve released all of the design files for the Bean: electrical, mechanical, and firmware designs. Everything is available to everyone, for free. We’ve also released thorough documentation, so you can understand and modify your Bean.
开源，现在及永远。正如我们所有的套件一样，我们已经发布了 Bean 的所有设计文件：包括电子、机械和固件设计。所有人都可以免费获取这些资源。我们还发布了详尽的文档，以便你理解并改装你的 Bean。

### 技术规格 (Tech Specs)

*   **Case Dimensions:** 84mm x 64mm x 16mm (3.3″ x 2.5″ x 0.63″)
    **外壳尺寸：** 84mm x 64mm x 16mm
*   **Supported Operating Systems:** Windows, Mac, Linux
    **支持操作系统：** Windows, Mac, Linux
*   **Weight:** 52g (1.8oz)
    **重量：** 52克
*   **Magnetic Sensor Chip:** TMAG5273, >1,000Hz Polling Rate, 20,000 samples per second, 12-bit resolution
    **磁传感器芯片：** TMAG5273，轮询率 >1,000Hz，每秒 20,000 次采样，12 位分辨率
*   **Microcontroller:** Raspberry Pi RP2040, Cortex M0+, 125MHz
    **微控制器：** 树莓派 RP2040, Cortex M0+, 125MHz
