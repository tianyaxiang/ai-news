# Topology-Constrained Quantized nnUNet for Efficient and Anatomically Accurate 3D Tooth Segmentation
# 用于高效且解剖结构精确的 3D 牙齿分割的拓扑约束量化 nnUNet

We propose a topology-constrained quantized nnUNet framework for efficient and anatomically accurate 3D tooth segmentation, addressing the challenges of spatial distortion introduced by quantization in deep learning models.
我们提出了一种用于高效且解剖结构精确的 3D 牙齿分割的拓扑约束量化 nnUNet 框架，旨在解决深度学习模型中因量化而引入的空间失真挑战。

The proposed method integrates a novel tooth-specific topological loss into quantization-aware training, preserving critical anatomical structures such as tooth count, adjacency relationships, and cavity integrity while maintaining computational efficiency.
该方法将一种新颖的牙齿专用拓扑损失函数集成到量化感知训练中，在保持计算效率的同时，保留了牙齿数量、邻接关系和空腔完整性等关键解剖结构。

The system employs an 8-bit quantized nnUNet backbone, where weights and activations are dynamically calibrated to minimize precision loss during inference.
该系统采用 8 位量化 nnUNet 主干网络，通过动态校准权重和激活值，以最大限度地减少推理过程中的精度损失。

Furthermore, the topological loss combines connected-component analysis, adjacency consistency, and hole detection penalties, ensuring anatomical fidelity without modifying the underlying network architecture.
此外，拓扑损失函数结合了连通分量分析、邻接一致性和空洞检测惩罚，在不修改底层网络架构的情况下确保了解剖结构的保真度。

The joint optimization objective harmonizes cross-entropy loss, quantization regularization, and topological constraints, enabling end-to-end training with gradient approximations for persistent homology terms.
联合优化目标协调了交叉熵损失、量化正则化和拓扑约束，通过对持久同调项进行梯度近似，实现了端到端的训练。

Experiments demonstrate that our approach significantly reduces topological errors compared to conventional quantized models, achieving clinically plausible segmentations on dental CBCT scans.
实验表明，与传统的量化模型相比，我们的方法显著降低了拓扑错误，在牙科 CBCT 扫描中实现了临床上可用的分割效果。

The method retains the hardware efficiency of integer-only inference, making it suitable for deployment in resource-constrained clinical environments.
该方法保留了仅使用整数推理的硬件效率，使其适用于资源受限的临床环境部署。

This work bridges the gap between computational efficiency and anatomical precision in medical image segmentation, offering a practical solution for real-world dental applications.
这项工作弥合了医学图像分割中计算效率与解剖精度之间的差距，为现实世界的牙科应用提供了一种实用的解决方案。
