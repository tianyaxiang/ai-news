### Lingo.dev v1

**Lingo.dev: The Localization Engineering Platform.**
Lingo.dev：本地化工程平台。

**Lingo.dev is a localization engineering platform where teams create localization engines: Stateful translation APIs configured with glossaries, brand voice rules, per-locale model chains, and AI quality scoring. Integrate via API, CLI, CI/CD, or MCP.**
Lingo.dev 是一个本地化工程平台，团队可以在此创建本地化引擎：通过配置术语表、品牌语调规则、针对特定语言区域的模型链以及 AI 质量评分，构建有状态的翻译 API。支持通过 API、CLI、CI/CD 或 MCP 进行集成。

**Launching today: Localization engineering platform for consistent translation.**
今日发布：用于实现一致性翻译的本地化工程平台。

**On Lingo.dev, teams configure localization engines: Stateful translation APIs with glossaries, brand voice rules, per-locale model chains, and AI quality scoring, and then call them via API, CLI, CI/CD, or MCP.**
在 Lingo.dev 上，团队可以配置本地化引擎：即包含术语表、品牌语调规则、特定区域模型链和 AI 质量评分的有状态翻译 API，并通过 API、CLI、CI/CD 或 MCP 调用它们。
