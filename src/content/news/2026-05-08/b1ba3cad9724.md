# Endogenous Regime Switching Driven by Scalar-Irreducible Learning Dynamics
# 由标量不可约学习动力学驱动的内源性机制切换

**Abstract:** Achieving endogenous regime switching is crucial for the emergence of autonomous intelligence, yet remains a central challenge for existing machine learning frameworks, where such transitions are typically externally imposed.

**摘要：** 实现内源性机制切换对于自主智能的涌现至关重要，但这仍然是现有机器学习框架面临的核心挑战，因为此类转换通常是外部强加的。

In this work, we introduce a classification that distinguishes scalar-reducible dynamics, which can be expressed as gradient flows driven by a scalar objective, from scalar-irreducible dynamics that cannot be reduced to such a form.

在这项工作中，我们引入了一种分类方法，用以区分“标量可约动力学”（可以表示为由标量目标驱动的梯度流）与“标量不可约动力学”（无法简化为上述形式的动力学）。

While most existing machine learning systems operate within the scalar-reducible class, we demonstrate that scalar-irreducible dynamics naturally enable internally generated regime switching through feedback between fast dynamical variables and slow structural adaptation.

虽然大多数现有的机器学习系统都在标量可约的范畴内运行，但我们证明了标量不可约动力学可以通过快速动力学变量与缓慢结构适应之间的反馈，自然地实现内部生成的机制切换。

Using a minimal dynamical model, we illustrate how this mechanism produces sustained endogenous regime transitions without external scheduling.

通过一个极简的动力学模型，我们展示了该机制如何在没有外部调度的情况下产生持续的内源性机制转换。

Our results suggest a new dynamical paradigm for regime exploration and provide a potential route toward autonomous learning systems whose adaptive behavior is organized internally rather than externally prescribed.

我们的研究结果提出了一种用于机制探索的新动力学范式，并为通向自主学习系统提供了一条潜在路径，使系统的适应性行为能够由内部组织，而非由外部预设。
