### Imagery Dataset for Remaining Useful Life Estimation of Synthetic Fibre Ropes
### 用于合成纤维绳剩余使用寿命评估的图像数据集

**Abstract:** Remaining useful life (RUL) estimation of synthetic fibre ropes (SFRs) is critical for safe operation in offshore-crane, wind turbine installation, and heavy-load handling applications, where rope failure can result in catastrophic safety incidents and costly downtime. Despite growing research interest in data-driven condition monitoring, there is no publicly available image dataset that captures the complete degradation lifecycle of SFRs under controlled cyclic fatigue loading.

**摘要：** 合成纤维绳（SFRs）的剩余使用寿命（RUL）评估对于海上起重机、风力涡轮机安装及重载搬运应用的安全运行至关重要，因为绳索失效可能导致灾难性的安全事故和昂贵的停机成本。尽管数据驱动的状态监测研究兴趣日益浓厚，但目前尚无公开的图像数据集能够捕捉合成纤维绳在受控循环疲劳载荷下的完整退化生命周期。

To address this gap, we present a novel image dataset comprising approximately 34,700 high-resolution images of eleven Dyneema SK75/78 high-modulus polyethylene (HMPE) rope samples subjected to cyclic fatigue on a sheave-bend test stand at seven distinct axial load levels ranging from 60 kN to 280 kN. Ropes were loaded until mechanical failure, with fatigue lifetimes ranging from 695 cycles to 8,340 cycles.

为了填补这一空白，我们提出了一个新的图像数据集，包含约 34,700 张高分辨率图像。这些图像来自 11 个 Dyneema SK75/78 高模量聚乙烯（HMPE）绳索样本，它们在滑轮弯曲试验台上经历了 60 kN 至 280 kN 七个不同轴向载荷水平的循环疲劳测试。绳索被加载直至机械失效，其疲劳寿命在 695 次循环到 8,340 次循环之间。

After every fixed number of sheave cycles (an inspection burst), ten images were captured at different cross-sectional positions along the rope, providing spatially representative sampling of surface degradation throughout the rope's entire service life. The images obtained from each load are annotated with the corresponding elapsed cycle count, enabling a direct computation of RUL for any rope in the sequence.

在每完成固定次数的滑轮循环（检查爆发期）后，我们会在绳索沿线的不同横截面位置拍摄十张图像，从而在绳索的整个使用寿命期间提供具有空间代表性的表面退化采样。从每个载荷下获得的图像都标注了相应的已用循环次数，使得能够直接计算序列中任何绳索的剩余使用寿命（RUL）。

This dataset aims to support a broad range of machine learning (ML) tasks including RUL regression, damage progression modelling, anomaly detection, and load-conditioned prognostics. The dataset is intended to serve as a benchmark resource for the development and comparison of vision-based condition monitoring (CM) and prognostics algorithms for SFRs.

该数据集旨在支持广泛的机器学习（ML）任务，包括 RUL 回归、损伤演变建模、异常检测以及基于载荷的预测。该数据集旨在作为开发和比较合成纤维绳基于视觉的状态监测（CM）和预测算法的基准资源。
