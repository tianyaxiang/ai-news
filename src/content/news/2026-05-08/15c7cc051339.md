### Physics-Guided Regime Unmixing

**Abstract:** The Linear Mixing Model (LMM) dominates spectral unmixing for its simplicity, but fails under multiple scattering; existing nonlinear models compensate by applying a fixed regime uniformly across entire scenes. 

**摘要：** 线性混合模型（LMM）因其简洁性在光谱解混领域占据主导地位，但在多次散射情况下表现不佳；现有的非线性模型通常通过在整个场景中统一应用固定的机制来进行补偿。

We propose Physics-Guided Regime Unmixing (PGRU), which estimates a pixel-wise scalar $\xi_i \in [0,1]$ from observable physical features to activate nonlinear mixing only where justified. 

我们提出了物理引导的机制解混（PGRU），该方法通过可观测的物理特征估计像素级的标量 $\xi_i \in [0,1]$，从而仅在必要时激活非线性混合。

Residuals from the Generalized Bilinear Model (GBM), the Post-Nonlinear Mixing Model (PPNM), and Hapke are combined via learned attention, yielding interpretable regime maps. 

通过学习到的注意力机制，将广义双线性模型（GBM）、后非线性混合模型（PPNM）以及 Hapke 模型的残差进行结合，从而生成可解释的机制图。

Experiments on Samson, Jasper Ridge, and Urban show consistent improvements over baselines, with physical coherence $\rho > 0.90$.

在 Samson、Jasper Ridge 和 Urban 数据集上的实验表明，该方法相比基准模型具有持续的性能提升，且物理一致性 $\rho > 0.90$。
