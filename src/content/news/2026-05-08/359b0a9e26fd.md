# Continual Distillation of Teachers from Different Domains
# 不同领域教师模型的持续蒸馏

**Abstract:** Deep learning models continue to scale, with some requiring more storage than many large-scale datasets. Thus, we introduce a new paradigm: Continual Distillation (CD), where a student learns sequentially from a stream of teacher models without retaining access to earlier teachers.

**摘要：** 深度学习模型的规模持续扩大，其中一些模型所需的存储空间甚至超过了许多大规模数据集。因此，我们引入了一种新的范式：持续蒸馏（Continual Distillation, CD）。在这种范式下，学生模型从一系列教师模型流中进行顺序学习，且无需保留对早期教师模型的访问权限。

CD faces two challenges: teacher training data is unavailable, and teachers have varying expertise. We show that external unlabeled data enables Unseen Knowledge Transfer (UKT), allowing the student to acquire information from domains not present in the training data, while known to the teacher.

CD 面临两个挑战：教师模型的训练数据不可用，且不同教师模型具备不同的专业知识。我们证明，外部无标签数据能够实现“未见知识迁移”（Unseen Knowledge Transfer, UKT），使学生模型能够从训练数据中不存在、但教师模型已知的领域中获取信息。

We also show that sequential distillation causes Unseen Knowledge Forgetting (UKF) when transferred knowledge is lost after training on later teachers. To better trade off between UKT and UKF, we propose Self External Data Distillation (SE2D), a method that preserves logits on external data to stabilize learning across heterogeneous teachers.

我们还发现，顺序蒸馏会导致“未见知识遗忘”（Unseen Knowledge Forgetting, UKF），即在后续教师模型上进行训练后，之前迁移的知识会发生丢失。为了在 UKT 和 UKF 之间取得更好的平衡，我们提出了自外部数据蒸馏（Self External Data Distillation, SE2D）方法，该方法通过保留外部数据上的逻辑值（logits）来稳定跨异构教师模型的学习过程。

Experiments on multiple benchmarks show that SE2D reduces UKF and improves cross-domain generalization. The code and implementation for this work are publicly available at: this https URL.

在多个基准测试上的实验表明，SE2D 能够减少 UKF 并提高跨领域泛化能力。该工作的代码和实现已公开，地址为：[此链接](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04059)。
