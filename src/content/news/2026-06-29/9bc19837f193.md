# Every Dream Has a Limit

Using an agent feels like a dream. The limit is what wakes you up. Everyone's talking about loops, goals, agents, subagents, yada, yada, yada. But barely anyone mentions what to do when the hand of time slaps you in the face and the dream you were dreaming vanishes like an island cloud.
使用 AI Agent 的感觉就像一场梦，而限制则是让你清醒的闹钟。每个人都在谈论循环、目标、智能体、子智能体等等，但几乎没人提到，当时间的巴掌扇在你脸上，你所编织的梦境像海岛上的云雾般消散时，该怎么办。

### Then You Hit the Wall
### 然后，你撞上了墙

It feels really nice to have that kind of power on your side — to command multiple entities working for you. One agent is fun. Five agents, all running at once? That's an army. But here's the thing nobody tells you: the bigger your army, the faster it burns through everything. And then, bam! You hit the wall. A wall made of tokens. A wall made of money. A wall made of time. And this wall doesn't move.
拥有这种力量的感觉确实很棒——指挥多个实体为你工作。一个智能体很有趣，但五个智能体同时运行？那就是一支军队了。但没人告诉你的是：你的军队规模越大，资源消耗得就越快。然后，砰！你撞上了一堵墙。一堵由 Token 组成的墙，一堵由金钱组成的墙，一堵由时间组成的墙。而且这堵墙纹丝不动。

If you can't — or won't — throw more money at it, you're locked out. A few hours of waiting before the limit resets. No agents. No army. Just you. You're sitting in a dark room, lit only by your monitor, asking yourself — what now? "Should I put more money in so I can finish what I started?" Or maybe… "Should I use that cool plugin that optimizes token usage so I can save some cash in the process?" Yep, you could do both. But you're already awake, and it's not that easy to drift back instantly.
如果你不能——或者不愿——继续砸钱，你就会被拒之门外。在限额重置前，你得等上几个小时。没有智能体，没有军队，只有你自己。你坐在昏暗的房间里，只有显示器的光照亮四周，问自己：现在该怎么办？“我是该充值以完成手头的工作？”还是……“我是该用那个能优化 Token 使用率的插件来省点钱？”没错，你可以两者都做。但你已经清醒了，想要立刻回到梦境并不容易。

### The Silence in Your Head
### 脑海中的宁静

So what do you do? You cherish the moment. That moment of silence. I know you're alone in the room and it's quiet anyway — but not that silence. The silence in your head. While your sleepy agent waits to be summoned — to have their cage rattled with prompts — you, my friend, could take a look at what was written for you. "The LLM can only be as senior as you are" — and by that I mean you need the skills, knowledge, and mindset of a senior engineer to be able to lead. I said engineer, not developer, intentionally. But that's a rabbit hole for another day.
那么你该做什么？珍惜这一刻。那份宁静。我知道你独自在房间里，本来就很安静——但不是那种安静。而是你脑海中的宁静。当你的智能体在沉睡中等待被召唤——等待被提示词惊醒时——我的朋友，你可以审视一下它为你写下的代码。“大语言模型的能力上限取决于你的资历”——我的意思是，你需要具备资深工程师的技能、知识和思维方式才能驾驭它。我特意用了“工程师”而不是“开发者”。但这又是另一个深坑了，改天再聊。

### Babysit Your Agent
### 像带新人一样带你的智能体

So how do you build that fusion of knowledge, skills, and mindset to become truly great at what you do — a real engineer? Simple: babysit your agent the same way you used to babysit your long-forgotten colleagues. You remember them, right? Those little humans with hearts and souls — the ones you replaced with a machine that can read and write. Statistically. Review the code together with your agent like you did with your junior mentee. Discover what decisions they made for you, and open a discussion — ask them to elaborate, the same way you'd ask a junior to explain their reasoning. And in that moment, even if they didn't show their best technical chops, you see the idea — the angle they attacked the problem from. That's what helps you develop the mindset I mentioned earlier.
那么，如何构建这种知识、技能和思维的融合，让自己成为一名真正优秀的工程师？很简单：像对待你那些早已被遗忘的同事一样，去“带”你的智能体。你还记得他们吧？那些有血有肉、有灵魂的人类——那些被你用一台只会进行概率性读写的机器所取代的人。像指导初级员工一样，和你的智能体一起审查代码。发现它为你做出的决策，并展开讨论——要求它详细说明，就像你要求初级员工解释他们的逻辑一样。在那一刻，即使它没有展现出最顶尖的技术水平，你也能看到它的思路——它解决问题的切入点。这正是帮助你培养我之前提到的那种思维方式的关键。

### From Larva to Butterfly
### 从幼虫到蝴蝶

In short: build a mentor–mentee relationship with your agent. Mentorship, if you want the common term for it. That's what makes you grow faster — just like it did back when you mentored humans — and it's what lets you grow from junior larva into the engineer butterfly, the kind who saves money, time, and tokens. You are the best optimizer of all three. No plugin or agent skill can replace that. So next time you wake up, don't just throw a pile of cash at the problem and panic-buy more tokens. Write some code. Change something. Play with it. Read. Mentor. Become better. And use that time to remember — there are still humans around you.
简而言之：与你的智能体建立导师与学员的关系。如果你喜欢通俗的说法，那就是“指导”。这会让你成长得更快——就像你当年指导人类同事一样——这能让你从初级的“幼虫”蜕变成工程师的“蝴蝶”，成为那种能节省金钱、时间和 Token 的人。你是这三者最好的优化器，没有任何插件或智能体技能可以取代这一点。所以下次当你从梦中醒来时，不要只是一味地砸钱去解决问题，也不要恐慌性地购买更多 Token。去写代码，去修改，去尝试，去阅读，去指导。让自己变得更好。并利用这段时间记住——你的身边，依然还有人类。
