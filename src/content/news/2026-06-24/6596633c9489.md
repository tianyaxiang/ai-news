# Prime Day has served up several great deals on 4K TVs
# Prime Day 为 4K 电视带来了多项超值优惠

With the quality of TVs from each year getting so close, it’s a better idea to save big on last year’s models.
随着每年电视质量的差距不断缩小，购买去年的机型以节省大笔开支是一个更明智的选择。

There are three times of year that are best for buying a new TV: leading up to the Super Bowl, Black Friday, and of course now, during Amazon Prime Day. Many of the new 2026 models have been released, and while some will be seeing discounts, the majority of the best deals are going to be on last year’s TVs.
一年中有三个购买新电视的最佳时机：超级碗前夕、黑色星期五，当然还有现在的亚马逊 Prime Day。许多 2026 年的新款机型已经发布，虽然其中一些会有折扣，但大部分最划算的交易还是集中在去年的机型上。

While you might feel FOMO setting in, I don’t think there’s much to worry about. The quality of TVs — from budget models up to flagships — is closer than it’s ever been. And that goes for year-to-year improvements, as well. There’s been so much focus in the TV industry on new high-end RGB LED TVs coming out, like the Sony Bravia 7 II. Sure, the tech is interesting, but its performance is not drastically different than the LED TVs from 2025. Even OLEDs from last year can outperform some of the new RGB LED TVs.
虽然你可能会感到“错失恐惧症”（FOMO），但我认为不必担心。从入门级到旗舰级，电视的质量比以往任何时候都更接近。每年的技术改进也是如此。电视行业目前非常关注新款高端 RGB LED 电视（如索尼 Bravia 7 II）的发布。诚然，这些技术很有趣，但其性能与 2025 年的 LED 电视相比并没有本质区别。甚至去年的 OLED 电视在某些方面也能胜过一些新款 RGB LED 电视。

We’re in a heyday of TV technology, and during the Prime Day sale, there sure are lots of options. These are the ones I think are the best right now. I’ve also included discounts on streaming devices.
我们正处于电视技术的全盛时期，在 Prime Day 促销期间，确实有很多选择。以下是我认为目前最好的几款产品。我还整理了流媒体设备的折扣信息。

### The best deals on 4K TVs
### 4K 电视的最佳优惠

65 inches is a sweet spot for TVs, and getting one as good as the TCL QM6K for only $527.99 at Amazon and Walmart is worth it. It’s particularly good for someone looking for a lower-priced gaming TV, as it supports high refresh rates up to 4K/144Hz with a PC (or 1080p at 288Hz) with VRR and has low input lag. It’s not the light cannon that more expensive TCL TVs are, but in a light-controlled room there’s enough brightness for watching or playing anything.
65 英寸是电视的黄金尺寸，能以 527.99 美元的价格在亚马逊和沃尔玛买到像 TCL QM6K 这样出色的电视非常值得。它特别适合寻找低价游戏电视的用户，因为它支持高达 4K/144Hz（或 1080p/288Hz）的 PC 高刷新率，具备 VRR（可变刷新率）功能且输入延迟极低。虽然它不像更昂贵的 TCL 电视那样亮度惊人，但在光线可控的房间里，它的亮度足以满足观看或游戏需求。

You and The Verge editor-in-chief Nilay Patel will own the same TV if you buy the Sony A95L, for 23% off. This 77-inch QD-OLED has incredible picture quality, thanks in part to Sony’s industry-leading processing. It’s still $3,498 during this Walmart sale and $3,499 at Best Buy, but if you want one of the best TVs ever (and a multi-year winner of the Value Electronics TV Shootout) this is the lowest price we’ve seen on it.
如果你以 23% 的折扣购买索尼 A95L，你将拥有与 The Verge 主编 Nilay Patel 同款的电视。这款 77 英寸 QD-OLED 电视拥有令人难以置信的画质，这在很大程度上归功于索尼行业领先的图像处理技术。在沃尔玛促销期间，它的价格为 3,498 美元（百思买为 3,499 美元），但如果你想要一台史上最好的电视（且是 Value Electronics 电视评测大赛的多年冠军），这是我们见过的最低价格。

If you do need or want a bigger high-brightness TV, there’s the 75-inch Hisense U8QG for $1,299 at Amazon and Best Buy. And if you want to go really big, Best Buy has the 100-inch for $2,220, which is ridiculous pricing for a TV so big. When I reviewed the 65-inch model last year, it was the brightest TV I had tested, with over 5,000 nits from a small window. Because of that, its HDR performance is excellent, with small specular highlights popping off the screen.
如果你需要或想要一台更大、亮度更高的电视，亚马逊和百思买有 75 英寸的海信 U8QG，售价 1,299 美元。如果你想要更大的尺寸，百思买还有 100 英寸的版本，售价 2,220 美元，对于这么大的电视来说，这个价格简直不可思议。去年我评测 65 英寸型号时，它是当时我测试过最亮的电视，小窗口峰值亮度超过 5,000 尼特。因此，它的 HDR 表现非常出色，屏幕上的微小高光细节非常突出。

### The best deals on streaming devices
### 流媒体设备的最佳优惠

One thing you can be certain of during Amazon Prime Day is that Amazon products are going to be on sale, even newly released updates like the Amazon Fire TV Stick HD, now available for $15.99 (54% off). This new version of the 1080p streaming stick has a smaller design — both in terms of width and volume — so it’s less obtrusive in a TV’s HDMI port. The Fire TV Stick HD can now be powered by a TV’s USB port instead of needing to be plugged into an outlet or power strip.
在亚马逊 Prime Day 期间，你可以确定的一点是亚马逊产品肯定会打折，即使是像 Amazon Fire TV Stick HD 这样刚发布的新品，现在也只需 15.99 美元（54% 折扣）。这款 1080p 流媒体棒的新版本在宽度和体积上都更小，因此插在电视的 HDMI 接口上时不会显得突兀。Fire TV Stick HD 现在可以通过电视的 USB 接口供电，无需再插在墙上插座或排插上。

Google TV OS has emerged as one of the best options for a smart TV OS. If it’s not included on your TV and you need to get away from the baked-in Tizen OS on your Samsung TV, then the Google TV Streamer 4K is exactly what you need, especially if you’re already in the Google smart home ecosystem. During Prime Day it’s available for $74.99, which is the lowest we’ve ever seen it for.
Google TV OS 已成为智能电视系统的最佳选择之一。如果你的电视没有内置该系统，且你想摆脱三星电视自带的 Tizen OS，那么 Google TV Streamer 4K 正是你所需要的，特别是如果你已经在使用 Google 智能家居生态系统的话。在 Prime Day 期间，它的售价为 74.99 美元，这是我们见过的最低价。
