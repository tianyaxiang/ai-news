### Logitech MX Master 3S
Logitech has a very specific market in mind for the MX Master 3S. This is a mouse that’s meant for office and productivity work, where practical considerations are more important than 360 no scopes. Used for that purpose, it works quietly and feels great, thanks to its amazing scroll wheel, sculpted design, and ever-helpful secondary scroll wheel. Just be prepared to pick up an inexpensive USB extender if you run into any latency issues. — Jon Porter, former senior reporter
罗技为 MX Master 3S 设定了非常明确的市场定位。这是一款专为办公和生产力工作设计的鼠标，在这些场景下，实用性远比“360度无死角盲狙”更重要。用于此类工作时，它运行安静且手感极佳，这得益于其出色的滚轮、人体工学设计以及非常实用的侧滚轮。如果你遇到任何延迟问题，只需准备一个便宜的 USB 延长线即可。—— Jon Porter，前高级记者

### Eero Pro 6E tri-band router
Eero’s Pro 6E mesh router is the Amazon-owned company’s last-gen model. It supports the 6GHz Wi-Fi band with speeds of up to 1.3Gbps and covers up to 2,000 square feet with one unit. Wired speeds can go up to 2.5Gbps. The tri-band Eero Pro 6E is another leap forward in home Wi-Fi for the company that popularized mesh Wi-Fi systems. And the Pro 6E certainly delivers. I saw significantly faster speeds over the previous generation Eero Pro 6 in my tests. But I also saw some network drop-offs, which Eero says is an issue around the Thread implementation — a key part of support for the coming smart home standard Matter that Eero has committed to. — Jennifer Pattison Tuohy, senior reviewer
Eero 的 Pro 6E 网状路由器是这家亚马逊旗下公司上一代的产品。它支持 6GHz Wi-Fi 频段，速度高达 1.3Gbps，单台设备覆盖面积可达 2,000 平方英尺。有线速度最高可达 2.5Gbps。对于普及了网状 Wi-Fi 系统的 Eero 来说，三频 Eero Pro 6E 是其家庭 Wi-Fi 技术的又一次飞跃。Pro 6E 的表现确实出色。在我的测试中，它的速度比上一代 Eero Pro 6 快得多。但我也遇到了一些网络掉线情况，Eero 表示这是 Thread 实现方面的问题——这是 Eero 承诺支持的未来智能家居标准 Matter 的关键部分。—— Jennifer Pattison Tuohy，高级评测员

### Instant Pot Duo
For the past few years, the Instant Pot has been a staple in my kitchen. From making rice to slow-cooking stews and even frying up chicken, it can do just about anything. The best part is that the Instant Pot can cook most recipes in a fraction of the time it would take in the oven or on the stove. Rice, for example, takes just six minutes to cook (not counting the amount of time it takes to build pressure), and there’s no fussing with covering the pot or adjusting the heat. Sometimes, it’s just easier — and much less stressful — to let the Instant Pot take the wheel. — Emma Roth, news writer
过去几年里，Instant Pot 一直是我厨房里的必备品。从煮饭到慢炖，甚至炸鸡，它几乎无所不能。最棒的是，Instant Pot 烹饪大多数食谱所需的时间仅为烤箱或炉灶的一小部分。例如，煮饭只需六分钟（不计入加压时间），而且无需担心盖锅盖或调节火候。有时候，让 Instant Pot 来接管烹饪确实更简单，也更省心。—— Emma Roth，新闻撰稿人

### Fractal Scape gaming headset
I’ve used the Scape at work and while playing games, and it’s among the best that I’ve ever tested in this price range. The fit and finish are impressive for a first-gen device from a company with no lineage in audio hardware. Its sleek design comes in gray or black, with minimal LEDs that add a subtle glow around the bottom edge of the ear cups (these are the first LEDs in a headset I haven’t wanted to immediately switch off). It has a chunkier version of Apple’s Digital Crown on the back of its left ear cup. The knob controls volume and can be clicked to pause and resume content. — Cameron Faulkner, commerce editor
我在工作和玩游戏时都使用过 Scape，它是我在这个价位段测试过的最好的耳机之一。对于一家在音频硬件领域没有背景的公司来说，其第一代产品的做工和质感令人印象深刻。它外观时尚，有灰色或黑色可选，耳罩底部边缘带有极简的 LED 灯，散发出柔和的光芒（这是我第一次不想立即关掉耳机上的 LED 灯）。左耳罩背面有一个类似 Apple Digital Crown 的加厚版旋钮。该旋钮用于控制音量，点击即可暂停或恢复播放内容。—— Cameron Faulkner，商务编辑

### Noco Boost GB40
The gist is this: It’s a small battery pack with jumper cables, which means you can use its power to jump-start your car instead of having to connect a jumper cable from another car’s battery to yours, then start the other car, wait for a few minutes, and hope your car starts… It’s supereasy to use and, thankfully, comes with a partial charge… The device has a flashlight, too, in case you ever need to jump a car at night, and it can charge a phone over USB-C. — Todd Haselton, Deputy Editor Reviews and Commerce
简单来说：这是一个带有跨接电缆的小型电池组，这意味着你可以用它的电量来启动汽车，而不必从另一辆车的电池连接电缆到你的车上，然后启动另一辆车，等待几分钟，再祈祷你的车能发动……它使用起来超级简单，而且很庆幸它自带部分电量……该设备还有一个手电筒，以防你需要夜间启动汽车，它还可以通过 USB-C 为手机充电。—— Todd Haselton，评测与商务副主编
