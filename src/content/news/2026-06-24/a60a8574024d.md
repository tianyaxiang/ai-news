# Best Prime Day Deals on Yoto Players and Accessories
# Yoto Player 及配件 Prime Day 最佳优惠

There's a lot of toys in my house, and most of them I would (lovingly) refer to as crap. But one of the coolest toys I have for my almost four-year-old is easily the Yoto.
我家里的玩具堆积如山，其中大部分我（带着爱意地）称之为“垃圾”。但在我快四岁的孩子拥有的所有玩具中，Yoto 绝对是最酷的玩具之一。

The Yoto reminds me of a Walkman, but if a Walkman was cute and square and used audio cards instead of CDs, and had a built-in speaker if you didn't want to use headphones. It makes for a great screen-free way for kids to listen to age appropriate content, and you can even make your own card like you'd make a mixtape back in the day. It's a great device and travels well, whether it's just keeping your kid entertained for a long drive or coming on a flight with the family, and both versions—the larger Yoto Player and the petite Yoto Mini—are on sale for Prime Day.
Yoto 让我想起了随身听（Walkman），但它更可爱、呈方形，且使用音频卡而非 CD，如果不戴耳机，它还有内置扬声器。它是孩子们收听适龄内容的绝佳无屏幕设备，你甚至可以像以前制作混音带一样制作自己的专属卡片。这是一款非常棒的设备，非常适合旅行携带，无论是长途驾驶时让孩子保持安静，还是全家飞行时使用，都非常方便。目前，大尺寸的 Yoto Player 和小巧的 Yoto Mini 两个版本都在 Prime Day 促销中。
