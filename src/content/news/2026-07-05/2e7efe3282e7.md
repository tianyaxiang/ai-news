# Who's Hiring? - Support Edition - Q3 2026
# 谁在招人？—— 支持岗位专刊 - 2026年第三季度

Welcome to the trial post of a "Who's Hiring?" call for non-software engineer positions! As we try to kick this off, I would love community input on things like; What other details would you like employers to provide in their initial reply? What frequency do we want? Quarterly? Monthly? Every other week? Are there any other roles that should be considered for these posts?

欢迎来到“谁在招人？”非软件工程师岗位的试运行帖！在启动这项计划之际，我非常希望能听取社区的建议，例如：您希望雇主在初步回复中提供哪些其他详细信息？我们希望多久发布一次？每季度？每月？还是每两周？还有哪些其他职位应该纳入这些招聘帖的考虑范围？

Any job postings should be geared towards needed company roles other than Software Engineer, such as customer support, data entry, field technician, marketing, purchasers, etc...that list can get quite long, depending on the size of the company and type of business. We'll keep this initial post conversational for the sake of getting community input about what we want to see and refining the template. Should this garner enough interest and stick, future posts should result in only/mostly job postings in the comments.

任何招聘信息都应针对除软件工程师以外的公司所需岗位，例如客户支持、数据录入、现场技术员、市场营销、采购等……根据公司规模和业务类型的不同，这个列表可能会很长。为了收集社区反馈并完善模板，我们将保持这篇初始帖的交流性质。如果该活动能获得足够的关注并持续下去，未来的帖子评论区将仅限或主要用于发布招聘信息。
