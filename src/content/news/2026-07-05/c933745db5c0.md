# 🚀 Introducing Career Agents: An Open-Source Collection of AI-Powered Career Assistants
# 🚀 隆重介绍 Career Agents：一套开源的 AI 职业助手合集

I'm pleased to share Career Agents, an open-source initiative designed to help students, developers, and professionals navigate key stages of their career journey through AI-powered guidance.
很高兴向大家分享 Career Agents，这是一个旨在通过 AI 驱动的指导，帮助学生、开发者和专业人士应对职业生涯关键阶段的开源项目。

Career development often involves multiple challenges — crafting an effective resume, preparing for interviews, optimizing professional profiles, and identifying growth opportunities. Career Agents aims to simplify these processes by providing specialized AI assistants focused on career advancement.
职业发展往往伴随着多重挑战——撰写有效的简历、准备面试、优化职业档案以及寻找成长机会。Career Agents 旨在通过提供专注于职业晋升的专业 AI 助手，来简化这些流程。

**Key Areas of Focus**
**核心关注领域**

*   ATS Resume Analysis (ATS 简历分析)
*   Resume Strategy & Optimization (简历策略与优化)
*   Technical Interview Preparation (技术面试准备)
*   HR & Behavioral Interview Coaching (HR 与行为面试辅导)
*   LinkedIn Profile Enhancement (领英档案提升)
*   Career Planning & Guidance (职业规划与指导)

**Why I Built This**
**我为何构建此项目**

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I observed that many talented individuals struggle not because of a lack of ability, but because they lack access to structured guidance and resources. Career Agents was created to bridge that gap by combining AI, open-source collaboration, and practical career support.
在我的学术和职业生涯中，我观察到许多有才华的人之所以挣扎，并非因为能力不足，而是因为缺乏结构化的指导和资源。Career Agents 的创建旨在通过结合 AI、开源协作和实用的职业支持来弥补这一差距。
