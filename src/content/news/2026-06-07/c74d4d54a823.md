### If LLMs Have Human-Like Attributes, Then So Does Age of Empires II

**Original:**
The recent surge in research claiming that Large Language Models (LLMs) possess human-like attributes—such as theory of mind, reasoning, or consciousness—has sparked significant debate. This paper argues that the methodologies used to attribute these qualities to LLMs are often flawed. By applying similar evaluative frameworks to the classic real-time strategy game *Age of Empires II*, we demonstrate that one could just as easily "prove" that the game possesses human-like cognition, thereby highlighting the absurdity of current anthropomorphic benchmarks in AI research.

**Translation:**
