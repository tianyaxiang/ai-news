# My SciPy ODE Solver Was Killing My Bayesian Inference: A Cosmologist’s Honest Account of Discovering Diffrax
# 我的 SciPy 常微分方程求解器正在扼杀我的贝叶斯推断：一位宇宙学家发现 Diffrax 的真实记录

A theoretical cosmologist’s journey from SciPy’s traditional ODE solvers to JAX-powered Diffrax, achieving dramatically faster likelihood evaluations, exact automatic differentiation, and scalable Bayesian inference for modern cosmological models.
这是一位理论宇宙学家从 SciPy 传统常微分方程（ODE）求解器转向基于 JAX 的 Diffrax 的历程，实现了更快的似然评估、精确的自动微分，以及针对现代宇宙学模型的可扩展贝叶斯推断。

### The problem that made me look for an alternative
### 促使我寻找替代方案的问题

I am a theoretical cosmologist. My work involves taking models of the Universe – dark energy equations of state, modified gravity, tachyonic fields – and asking: what do the data actually say about the parameters? The tool for that question is Bayesian inference. I usually run dynesty nested sampling for a few thousand to a few hundred thousand likelihood evaluations depending upon the complexity of the model. For most of my PhD, I did not think much about the ODE solver inside the likelihood as `solve_ivp` worked. It was reliable. Hence I used it and moved on.
我是一名理论宇宙学家。我的工作涉及构建宇宙模型——如暗能量状态方程、修正引力、快子场——并探究：数据对这些参数究竟意味着什么？解决这个问题的工具是贝叶斯推断。根据模型的复杂程度，我通常会运行 `dynesty` 嵌套采样，进行几千到几十万次似然评估。在我的博士生涯大部分时间里，我并没有过多考虑似然函数内部的 ODE 求解器，因为 `solve_ivp` 运行良好且可靠。所以我一直使用它，没有深究。

Then I started working on a tachyonic DBI dark energy model where the dark energy field is governed by a non-standard kinetic term, and the background and perturbation equations are a coupled stiff-ish system. Each likelihood call solved those ODEs, computed the comoving distance, and evaluated the distance modulus at the redshifts of 30 supernovae. I profiled it. The ODE solve alone was taking 0.4 ms per call. In a nested sampling run with 10⁵ evaluations, that is 40 seconds — just in ODE calls, before you count any bookkeeping. And for a 10-parameter model, getting a gradient via central finite differences costs 20 extra forward solves, turning those 0.4 ms into 8 ms per gradient. That is 300 seconds, or about 5 minutes, just for the gradients. For a single nested sampling run. Something had to change.
后来，我开始研究一种快子 DBI 暗能量模型，其中暗能量场由非标准动能项控制，背景和扰动方程是一个耦合的“刚性”系统。每次似然调用都需要求解这些 ODE，计算共动距离，并评估 30 个超新星红移处的距离模数。我对其进行了性能分析：仅 ODE 求解一项，每次调用就需要 0.4 毫秒。在进行 10⁵ 次评估的嵌套采样运行中，仅 ODE 调用就耗时 40 秒，这还不包括任何簿记开销。对于一个 10 参数模型，通过中心有限差分获取梯度需要额外 20 次前向求解，将 0.4 毫秒变成了每个梯度 8 毫秒。这意味着仅梯度计算就需要 300 秒（约 5 分钟）。对于单次嵌套采样运行来说，这太慢了，必须做出改变。

### What I found: diffrax
### 我的发现：Diffrax

After a day of searching, I landed on diffrax [1], a library of numerical ODE solvers written entirely in JAX. Not a neural surrogate. Not an approximation. The same embedded Runge–Kutta algorithms I already use in scipy — Tsit5 instead of RK45, but the same family of methods — just compiled, differentiable, and vectorisable.
经过一天的搜索，我发现了 `diffrax` [1]，这是一个完全用 JAX 编写的数值 ODE 求解器库。它不是神经代理模型，也不是近似值。它使用的是我在 SciPy 中已经使用的相同嵌入式龙格-库塔算法（Tsit5 代替 RK45，但属于同一类方法），只是它经过了编译，支持自动微分和向量化。

Three properties come from the “written entirely in JAX” design:
“完全用 JAX 编写”的设计带来了三个特性：

*   **JIT compilation** – The entire adaptive-stepping loop compiles to a single XLA kernel. Zero Python overhead after the first call.
    **JIT 编译** – 整个自适应步长循环被编译为一个单一的 XLA 内核。首次调用后，Python 开销为零。
*   **Autodiff** – Because every operation inside the solver is a JAX primitive, `jax.grad` propagates gradients through the solve. Exact gradients. One backward pass. Regardless of how many parameters.
    **自动微分** – 由于求解器内部的每个操作都是 JAX 原语，`jax.grad` 可以通过求解过程传播梯度。这是精确的梯度，只需一次反向传播，无论参数有多少。
*   **vmap** – An entire batch of parameter vectors can be solved in parallel with `jax.vmap`. Critical for nested sampling.
    **vmap** – 可以使用 `jax.vmap` 并行求解整批参数向量。这对嵌套采样至关重要。

### Surprise 1: the speed
### 惊喜 1：速度

The adaptive while-loop goes through the Python interpreter asking: “is the local error too large? reject; else grow the step; repeat.” For a 12-step solve, that is 12 rounds of Python dispatch, 12 allocations, 12 error-estimate computations sitting behind the interpreter lock.
