# 82-0 is the best basketball game, to hell with NBA 2K
# 《82-0》才是最棒的篮球游戏，去他的《NBA 2K》

82-0 marries the stat nerd fun of fantasy basketball with instant gratification and a bit of dumb luck. The goal is to draft a team of players that could (theoretically) have a perfect 82-0 season.
《82-0》将梦幻篮球中数据狂热的乐趣与即时满足感以及一点点运气完美结合。游戏的目标是组建一支（理论上）能够打出 82 胜 0 负完美赛季的球队。

Obviously, if you just had free rein to pick whoever you wanted from throughout history, there would be little challenge. The twist is that the site randomly selects a team and an era, and then you pick a single player. So, for example, if you pulled the Knicks and 2020s, you might pick Jalen Brunson as your point guard, then you’d get a new team and era to select your center from.
显然，如果你可以随心所欲地从历史长河中挑选任何球员，那将毫无挑战性可言。游戏的精妙之处在于，系统会随机选定一支球队和一个时代，然后让你从中挑选一名球员。举个例子，如果你抽到了尼克斯队和 2020 年代，你可能会选择杰伦·布伦森（Jalen Brunson）作为你的控球后卫，接着系统会为你提供一个新的球队和时代，让你从中挑选你的中锋。

You can reshuffle the team once, and the era once, if you don’t like your options. But that’s it. Part of the challenge is also figuring out exactly when to settle for what’s in front of you, and when to hold out hope for the very best. Domantas Sabonis is an excellent player, but do you reshuffle or pick someone else from the Kings in hopes of maybe drawing Wilt Chamberlain in a later round?
如果你对当前的选项不满意，可以有一次重置球队和一次重置时代的机会。但仅此而已。挑战的一部分在于你需要权衡：何时该满足于眼前的选择，何时又该为了追求顶级球员而孤注一掷。多曼塔斯·萨博尼斯（Domantas Sabonis）是一位出色的球员，但你是该选择他，还是重置并从国王队挑选其他人，寄希望于在后续轮次中抽到威尔特·张伯伦（Wilt Chamberlain）呢？

There’s also a HoopIQ mode that hides all the stats and basically requires you to have an encyclopedic knowledge of basketball to score a perfect season. Honestly, though, even with the stats right in front of you, it’s pretty hard. The best I could get was 78-4. There are also already clones cropping up like SixRings from databallr, though that has been pretty unstable in my experience.
游戏中还有一个“HoopIQ”模式，它会隐藏所有数据，基本上要求你拥有百科全书般的篮球知识才能打出完美赛季。老实说，即便数据摆在眼前，这依然非常困难。我目前取得的最好成绩是 78 胜 4 负。市面上也已经出现了类似的作品，比如 databallr 开发的《SixRings》，但根据我的体验，它的稳定性还有待提高。

If you’re a basketball fan at all, definitely go check out 82-0. And then drop your best score in the comments.
如果你是一位篮球迷，一定要去试试《82-0》。然后，别忘了在评论区晒出你的最高分。
