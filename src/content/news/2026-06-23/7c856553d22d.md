# The Apple Watch SE 3 is just $199 for Prime Day
# Apple Watch SE 3 在 Prime Day 期间仅售 199 美元

The Apple Watch SE 3 is at an all-time low of $199, making the sleeper hit of last year’s Apple Watches an even better value. While the Series 11 and Ultra 3 were iterative updates, the SE 3 was a wide-ranging glow-up that added an always-on display, gestures like double tap and wrist flick, a wrist-temperature sensor, 5G connectivity, fast charging, a speaker, and an upgraded processor that’ll support watchOS 27 when it arrives in the fall.

Apple Watch SE 3 的价格降至 199 美元的历史最低点，使得这款去年 Apple Watch 系列中的“黑马”产品性价比进一步提升。虽然 Series 11 和 Ultra 3 属于迭代更新，但 SE 3 却是一次全方位的升级，增加了全天候显示屏、双指互点和手腕轻弹等手势操作、腕部温度传感器、5G 连接、快速充电、扬声器，以及一颗能够支持秋季发布的 watchOS 27 的升级版处理器。

This is the Apple Watch that I recommend for most people, unless you really want a watch with thinner bezels and EKG and atrial fibrillation detection. The 40mm size is also great if you have a smaller wrist. When I was testing the watch, I really didn’t feel any FOMO. But if advanced health features are a sticking point, the Apple Watch Series 11 is also $50 off for an all-time low price of $279.

这是我向大多数人推荐的 Apple Watch，除非你非常执着于更窄的边框、心电图（EKG）以及房颤检测功能。如果你手腕较细，40mm 的尺寸也非常合适。在测试这款手表时，我完全没有感到任何功能缺失的遗憾。但如果进阶健康功能对你来说至关重要，Apple Watch Series 11 目前也降价 50 美元，达到了 279 美元的历史最低价。

Just a note for bargain hunters: keep in mind that we’re expecting to see a Series 12 in a few months, while we won’t likely see an update to the SE line for at least another year.

给“捡漏党”的一个小贴士：请记住，我们预计几个月后就会看到 Series 12 发布，而 SE 系列在至少一年内不太可能会有更新。
