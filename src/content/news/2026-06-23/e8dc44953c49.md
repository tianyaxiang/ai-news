### MediaSeg

**MediaSeg**

Launching today, MediaSeg splits large media files into upload-ready chunks on macOS.
今天正式发布，MediaSeg 是一款可以将大型媒体文件分割成适合上传的小块的 macOS 工具。

MediaSeg is a local macOS utility that splits large media files into upload-ready chunks while preserving quality.
MediaSeg 是一款本地 macOS 实用工具，能够在保持画质/音质的前提下，将大型媒体文件分割成适合上传的片段。

It was produced and directed with full AI assistance, and shipped in 2 days from idea to public release.
该产品在全程 AI 辅助下完成构思与开发，从创意萌芽到正式发布仅用了 2 天时间。

Originally created to streamline long-recording upload prep, MediaSeg is useful for NotebookLM and other size-limited upload destinations.
MediaSeg 最初是为了简化长录音文件的上传准备工作而开发的，对于 NotebookLM 以及其他有文件大小限制的上传平台非常实用。
