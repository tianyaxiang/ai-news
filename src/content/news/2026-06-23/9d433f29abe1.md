## What the ADHD app actually is
## 这款 ADHD 应用到底是什么

Five features. No more.
1. A body-doubling companion — a quiet on-screen presence that stays with you for the session. Working alongside a presence (even simulated) activates the ADHD brain differently than working alone. It's not magic, it's neuroscience.
2. A visual depleting timer — a ring that shrinks as you work. Not a number in the corner. You look up and you see how much is left.
3. One-tap task capture — before the thought disappears.
4. "You showed up N days" — a counter that only goes up. No streaks. No resets. No shame.
5. High-contrast and reduce-motion from day one.

五个功能，仅此而已。
1. 镜像工作伙伴——一个安静的屏幕伴侣，在会话期间陪伴你。与一个存在（即使是模拟的）一起工作，比独自工作更能以不同方式激活 ADHD 大脑。这不是魔法，这是神经科学。
2. 视觉递减计时器——一个随着工作进行而缩小的圆环。不是角落里的数字。你抬头就能看到还剩多少时间。
3. 一键任务捕捉——在想法消失之前记录下来。
4. “你已坚持 N 天”——一个只会增加的计数器。没有连胜，没有重置，没有羞耻感。
5. 从第一天起就支持高对比度和减少动画。

No account. Nothing leaves your phone. No ads. The codebase is public at github.com/Costder/FocusKit under PolyForm Noncommercial (personal copies fine, no commercial competing apps).

无需账号。没有任何数据会离开你的手机。没有广告。代码库已在 github.com/Costder/FocusKit 公开，采用 PolyForm 非商业许可（允许个人复制，禁止商业竞争性应用）。
