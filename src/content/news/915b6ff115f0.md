# GPT-5.5 System Card

**1. Introduction**

GPT‑5.5 is a new model designed for complex, real-world work, including writing code, researching online, analyzing information, creating documents and spreadsheets, and moving across tools to get things done. Relative to earlier models, GPT‑5.5 understands the task earlier, asks for less guidance, uses tools more effectively, checks its work, and keeps going until it’s done.

GPT-5.5 是一款专为处理复杂现实工作而设计的新模型，包括编写代码、在线研究、分析信息、创建文档和电子表格，以及跨工具协作以完成任务。与早期模型相比，GPT-5.5 能更早理解任务需求，减少对引导的依赖，更有效地使用工具，并能自我检查工作成果，直至任务完成。

We subjected the model to our full suite of predeployment safety evaluations and our Preparedness Framework, including targeted red-teaming for advanced cybersecurity and biology capabilities, and collected feedback on real use cases from nearly 200 early-access partners before release. We are releasing GPT‑5.5 with our strongest set of safeguards to date, designed to reduce misuse while preserving legitimate, beneficial uses of advanced capabilities.

在发布之前，我们对该模型进行了全套的部署前安全评估，并遵循了我们的“准备框架”（Preparedness Framework），包括针对高级网络安全和生物学能力的定向红队测试，并收集了近 200 家早期访问合作伙伴在实际用例中的反馈。我们发布 GPT-5.5 时配备了迄今为止最强大的安全防护措施，旨在减少滥用行为，同时保留高级功能在合法且有益场景下的使用。

We generally treat GPT‑5.5’s safety results as strong proxies for GPT‑5.5 Pro, which is the same underlying model using a setting that makes use of parallel test time compute. As noted below, we separately evaluate GPT‑5.5 Pro in certain cases because we judge that the setting could materially impact the relevant risks or appropriate safeguards posture. Except where noted, the results in system cards describe evaluations we ran in an offline setting.

我们通常将 GPT-5.5 的安全评估结果视为 GPT-5.5 Pro 的有力参考，后者是基于相同底层模型，但采用了并行测试时计算（parallel test time compute）设置的版本。如下文所述，我们在某些情况下会对 GPT-5.5 Pro 进行单独评估，因为我们认为该设置可能会对相关风险或适当的安全防护态势产生实质性影响。除非另有说明，系统卡中的结果均描述了我们在离线环境下进行的评估。

本卡片于 2026 年 4 月 24 日更新，增加了关于 GPT-5.5 和 GPT-5.5 Pro 在 API 中部署的安全防护措施的相关信息。
