# A Systematic Failure Analysis of Vision Foundation Models for Open Set Iris Presentation Attack Detection
# 视觉基础模型在开放集虹膜呈现攻击检测中的系统性失效分析

**Abstract:** Vision foundation models have demonstrated strong transferability across diverse visual recognition tasks and are increasingly considered for biometric applications. Their suitability for iris Presentation Attack Detection (PAD), particularly under realistic open-set operating conditions, remains insufficiently examined.

**摘要：** 视觉基础模型在各种视觉识别任务中展现出了强大的迁移能力，并日益受到生物识别应用领域的关注。然而，它们在虹膜呈现攻击检测（PAD）中的适用性，特别是在现实的开放集操作条件下，尚未得到充分的研究。

This work presents a systematic failure analysis of general-purpose vision foundation models for open-set iris PAD using periocular imagery. Five representative foundation models are evaluated under three open-set protocols that explicitly separate different sources of distribution shift: unseen Presentation Attack Instruments (PAIs), unseen datasets captured with different sensors and cross-spectral transfer from near-infrared (NIR) to visible spectrum (VIS) imagery.

本研究针对使用眼周图像进行开放集虹膜 PAD 的通用视觉基础模型进行了系统性的失效分析。我们在三个明确区分不同分布偏移来源的开放集协议下，评估了五种具有代表性的基础模型：未见的呈现攻击工具（PAIs）、使用不同传感器捕获的未见数据集，以及从近红外（NIR）到可见光（VIS）图像的跨光谱迁移。

Both frozen feature representations and parameter-efficient task adaptation using Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) are assessed within a unified experimental framework. The results indicate that foundation models can transfer across datasets with similar sensing characteristics, but fail to generalise reliably to unseen attack instruments and degrade sharply under cross-spectral evaluation.

我们在一个统一的实验框架内，评估了冻结特征表示以及使用低秩自适应（LoRA）进行的参数高效任务适配。结果表明，基础模型可以在具有相似传感特性的数据集之间进行迁移，但在面对未见的攻击工具时无法可靠地泛化，且在跨光谱评估下性能急剧下降。

While LoRA improves performance in certain cross-dataset settings, it frequently amplifies failure under attack-level and spectral shifts. Additional validation experiments using segmented iris inputs, full backbone fine-tuning, joint cross-dataset and cross-PAI shifts, and reverse VIS to NIR transfer further confirm that these failures are not simply artefacts of periocular input, weak adaptation, or one-directional spectral evaluation.

虽然 LoRA 在某些跨数据集设置中提高了性能，但它往往会放大攻击级别和光谱偏移下的失效情况。通过使用分割后的虹膜输入、全骨干网络微调、联合跨数据集与跨 PAI 偏移，以及反向 VIS 到 NIR 迁移进行的额外验证实验进一步证实，这些失效并非仅仅是眼周输入、弱适配或单向光谱评估所导致的伪影。

These findings show that strong closed-set or cross-dataset performance should not be treated as evidence of robust open-set security, and highlight the need for PAD representations that maintain sensitivity to presentation artefacts while remaining stable under realistic deployment variation.

这些发现表明，强大的闭集或跨数据集性能不应被视为稳健的开放集安全性的证据，并强调了开发 PAD 表示法的必要性——即在保持对呈现伪影敏感的同时，在现实部署的变动下仍能保持稳定。
