### Create and build with our most advanced models
### 使用我们最先进的模型进行创作与构建

**Gemini 3.5**
**Gemini 3.5**

1. We launched Gemini 3.5 Flash: the first in our latest series of models combining frontier intelligence with action.
1. 我们发布了 Gemini 3.5 Flash：这是我们最新系列模型中的首款产品，它将前沿智能与行动能力相结合。

2. Gemini 3.5 Flash is generally available today via our agent-first development platform Google Antigravity, the Gemini API in Google AI Studio and Android Studio.
2. Gemini 3.5 Flash 即日起通过我们以智能体为先的开发平台 Google Antigravity、Google AI Studio 中的 Gemini API 以及 Android Studio 全面开放使用。

3. Gemini 3.5 Flash delivers intelligence that rivals large flagship models at speeds you expect from the Flash series. It outperforms Gemini 3.1 Pro on challenging coding and agentic benchmarks like Terminal-Bench 2.1 (76.2%), GDPval-AA (1656 Elo) and MCP Atlas (83.6%).
3. Gemini 3.5 Flash 提供了足以媲美大型旗舰模型的智能水平，同时保持了 Flash 系列应有的速度。在 Terminal-Bench 2.1 (76.2%)、GDPval-AA (1656 Elo) 和 MCP Atlas (83.6%) 等极具挑战性的编程和智能体基准测试中，它的表现均优于 Gemini 3.1 Pro。

4. Landing in the top-right quadrant of the Artificial Analysis index, 3.5 Flash delivers frontier-level intelligence at exceptional speed — proving you no longer have to trade quality for latency.
4. 在 Artificial Analysis 指数中位居右上角象限，3.5 Flash 以极高的速度提供了前沿级别的智能，证明了你无需再为了降低延迟而牺牲质量。

5. Gemini 3.5 Flash is ideal for tackling long-horizon agentic tasks. What used to take a developer days or an auditor weeks, 3.5 Flash can now help complete in a fraction of the time, often at less than half the cost of other frontier models. It rapidly plans, builds and iterates to solve real-world problems, whether it’s developing new applications, maintaining codebases or helping to prepare financial documents.
5. Gemini 3.5 Flash 非常适合处理长周期的智能体任务。过去需要开发者花费数天或审计员数周才能完成的工作，现在 3.5 Flash 可以在极短时间内协助完成，且成本通常不到其他前沿模型的一半。它能快速规划、构建并迭代以解决现实世界的问题，无论是开发新应用、维护代码库还是协助准备财务文档。

6. Building on the strong multimodal foundation of Gemini 3, 3.5 Flash generates richer, more interactive web UIs and graphics.
6. 基于 Gemini 3 强大的多模态基础，3.5 Flash 能够生成更丰富、更具交互性的网页 UI 和图形。

7. We’re also hard at work on Gemini 3.5 Pro. It’s already being used internally and we look forward to rolling it out next month.
7. 我们也在全力开发 Gemini 3.5 Pro。它目前已在内部使用，我们期待下个月将其推出。

**Gemini Omni**
**Gemini Omni**

8. Gemini Omni is our new model that can create anything from any input — starting with video. It combines Gemini’s intelligence with the best of our generative media models for a new level of world understanding, multimodality and editing. We’re starting with video outputs now, but over time, Gemini Omni will be able to generate any output from any input.
8. Gemini Omni 是我们的新模型，它能够通过任何输入创建任何内容——首先从视频开始。它将 Gemini 的智能与我们最优秀的生成式媒体模型相结合，在世界理解、多模态和编辑方面达到了新的高度。我们目前从视频输出开始，但随着时间的推移，Gemini Omni 将能够通过任何输入生成任何输出。

9. Gemini Omni combines an intuitive understanding of physics with Gemini's knowledge of history, science and culture, bridging the gap from photorealism to meaningful storytelling. It has an improved understanding of forces like gravity, kinetic energy and fluid dynamics, allowing you to create more realistic scenes.
9. Gemini Omni 将对物理学的直观理解与 Gemini 在历史、科学和文化方面的知识相结合，弥合了从照片级真实感（photorealism）到有意义的故事叙述之间的鸿沟。它对重力、动能和流体动力学等力的理解得到了提升，使你能够创作出更逼真的场景。

10. Videos created with Omni include our imperceptible SynthID digital watermark. You can easily verify content through the Gemini app, Gemini in Chrome and Search.
10. 使用 Omni 创建的视频包含我们不可见的 SynthID 数字水印。你可以通过 Gemini 应用、Chrome 中的 Gemini 以及搜索功能轻松验证内容。

11. You can reference anything. Gemini Omni turns any reference — image, text, video or audio — into a single, cohesive output. While only voice references will be supported for audio to start, we’ll roll out other types of audio inputs soon.
11. 你可以引用任何内容。Gemini Omni 将任何参考资料（图像、文本、视频或音频）转化为单一、连贯的输出。虽然起初音频仅支持语音参考，但我们很快会推出其他类型的音频输入支持。

**Gemini Omni in the Gemini app, Google Flow and YouTube**
**Gemini 应用、Google Flow 和 YouTube 中的 Gemini Omni**

12. Gemini Omni Flash is rolling out now to all Google AI Plus, Pro and Ultra subscribers globally through the Gemini app and Google Flow. It’s also available today in YouTube Shorts Remix and the YouTube Create app to users (18+) at no cost.
12. Gemini Omni Flash 现已通过 Gemini 应用和 Google Flow 向全球所有 Google AI Plus、Pro 和 Ultra 订阅者推出。它今天也已在 YouTube Shorts Remix 和 YouTube Create 应用中向用户（18 岁以上）免费开放。

13. Creating, remixing and editing a video is easier than ever. Gemini Omni in the Gemini app offers a fluid, conversational way to create and edit videos — like applying cinematic zooms or changing backgrounds with a simple prompt.
13. 创作、混剪和编辑视频比以往任何时候都更容易。Gemini 应用中的 Gemini Omni 提供了一种流畅的对话式方式来创建和编辑视频，例如通过简单的提示词应用电影级缩放或更换背景。

14. You can upload any photo or video from your camera roll, apply built-in templates with a single click and experience the magic without needing expensive equipment or technical jargon. You can even drop yourself into the action by creating a custom AI avatar that looks and sounds like you.
14. 你可以上传相册中的任何照片或视频，一键应用内置模板，无需昂贵的设备或专业术语即可体验神奇效果。你甚至可以通过创建看起来和听起来都像你的自定义 AI 虚拟形象，将自己置身于视频内容中。

15. For creatives using Google Flow, Omni Flash allows you to blend real-world inspiration with generated content and iterate conversationally. Gemini Omni Flash also improves character consistency, meaning identity and voice are preserved across every scene.
15. 对于使用 Google Flow 的创作者，Omni Flash 允许你将现实世界的灵感与生成的内容融合，并进行对话式迭代。Gemini Omni Flash 还提高了角色一致性，这意味着身份和声音在每个场景中都能保持统一。

16. And you can try the new Gemini Omni model at no cost in YouTube Shorts Remix, with an exciting new upgrade that lets you step directly into your favorite Shorts. Just select an eligible Short, prompt what you want changed — like adding yourself or any visual reference — and get a fresh new version with your edits.
16. 你还可以在 YouTube Shorts Remix 中免费试用全新的 Gemini Omni 模型，并体验一项令人兴奋的升级：让你直接进入你最喜欢的 Shorts 短视频中。只需选择一个符合条件的短视频，输入你想要更改的内容（例如添加你自己或任何视觉参考），即可获得包含你编辑内容的全新版本。

**AI 搜索**

17. AI 模式是我们最强大的 AI 搜索功能，其月活跃用户已超过 10 亿。今天，我们通过将 Gemini 3.5 Flash 作为全球默认模型，对这一体验进行了升级。

19. Today we’re launching the biggest upgrade to our Search box in over 25 years — a new, intelligent Search box, now completely reimagined with AI. You can search using text, images, files, videos and Chrome tabs and Search reasons across them all. You’ll continue to get a range of results from Search, just like you do today.
19. 今天，我们推出了 25 年来对搜索框最大的升级——一个全新的、智能的搜索框，现在已通过 AI 完全重构。你可以使用文本、图像、文件、视频和 Chrome 标签页进行搜索，并跨越所有这些内容进行搜索推理。你将继续像今天一样从搜索中获得一系列结果。

20. We’re also making it even easier to continue the conversation with Search, bringing AI Overviews and AI Mode into one, seamless AI Search experience. You can flow effortlessly from your question, to a search results page with an AI Overview, to a follow-up in AI Mode, all with links to learn more. The new seamless AI Search experience is live today across desktop and mobile, worldwide.
20. 我们还让通过搜索进行对话变得更加容易，将 AI 概览（AI Overviews）和 AI 模式整合为一个无缝的 AI 搜索体验。你可以轻松地从你的问题跳转到带有 AI 概览的搜索结果页面，再到 AI 模式下的后续追问，所有这些都附带了深入了解的链接。全新的无缝 AI 搜索体验即日起在全球桌面端和移动端上线。
