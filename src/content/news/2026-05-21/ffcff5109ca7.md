# MedFM-Robust: Benchmarking Robustness of Medical Foundation Models
# MedFM-Robust：医疗基础模型鲁棒性基准测试

**Abstract:** Medical foundation models (MedFMs) have emerged as transformative tools in healthcare, demonstrating capabilities across diverse clinical applications. These models can be broadly categorized into two paradigms: Medical Vision-Language Models (Med-VLMs) and segmentation foundation models.

**摘要：** 医疗基础模型（MedFMs）已成为医疗保健领域的变革性工具，在多种临床应用中展现出强大的能力。这些模型大致可分为两种范式：医疗视觉-语言模型（Med-VLMs）和分割基础模型。

Med-VLMs range from medical-specialized models such as LLaVA-Med and MedGemma, to general-purpose models like GPT-4o and Gemini, all capable of medical image understanding tasks including visual question answering (VQA), report generation, and visual grounding.

Med-VLMs 的范围涵盖了 LLaVA-Med 和 MedGemma 等医疗专用模型，以及 GPT-4o 和 Gemini 等通用模型，它们均具备医疗图像理解任务的能力，包括视觉问答（VQA）、报告生成和视觉定位。

Concurrently, the Segment Anything Model (SAM) has catalyzed a new generation of medical segmentation models, with adaptations like SAM-Med2D and MedSAM. The widespread clinical deployment of these models thus necessitates rigorous evaluation of their reliability under real-world conditions.

与此同时，分割一切模型（SAM）催生了新一代医疗分割模型，例如 SAM-Med2D 和 MedSAM 等适配版本。因此，这些模型在临床的广泛部署，迫切需要对其在真实世界条件下的可靠性进行严格评估。
