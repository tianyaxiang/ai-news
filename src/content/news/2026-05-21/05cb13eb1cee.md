# Masters of the Universe final trailer brings the '80s nostalgia
# 《宇宙的巨人希曼》最终预告片唤醒 80 年代怀旧情怀

No doubt buoyed by all the positive advance reviews on social media, Amazon MGM Studios has released one last trailer for *Masters of the Universe*, and it comes in strong with the 1980s nostalgia, chock-full of Easter eggs to delight Ars readers of a certain age.
毫无疑问，在社交媒体上众多正面先期评价的推动下，亚马逊米高梅影业发布了《宇宙的巨人希曼》（Masters of the Universe）的最后一支预告片。预告片充满了 80 年代的怀旧气息，并塞满了足以让特定年龄段的 Ars 读者感到惊喜的彩蛋。

Directed by Travis Knight (*Bumblebee*, *Kubo and the Two Strings*), the film is a reboot of sorts of the *He-Man and the Masters of the Universe* series, its spinoff, *She-Ra: Princess of Power*, and the many, many other offshoots of this hugely popular Mattel franchise.
该片由特拉维斯·奈特（代表作《大黄蜂》、《魔弦传说》）执导，是对《宇宙的巨人希曼》系列及其衍生剧《非凡的公主希瑞》以及美泰公司这一广受欢迎的系列作品中众多分支的某种程度上的重启。

Per the official premise: In *Masters of the Universe*, Director Travis Knight brings the legendary franchise back to the big screen in this epic live-action adventure. After being separated for 15 years, the Sword of Power leads Prince Adam (Nicholas Galitzine) back to Eternia where he discovers his home shattered under the fiendish rule of Skeletor (Jared Leto). To save his family and his world, Adam must join forces with his closest allies, Teela (Camila Mendes) and Duncan/Man-At-Arms (Idris Elba), and embrace his true destiny as He-Man — the most powerful man in the universe.
根据官方剧情简介：在《宇宙的巨人希曼》中，导演特拉维斯·奈特通过这部史诗般的真人冒险电影，将这一传奇系列带回了大银幕。在与家乡分离 15 年后，力量之剑指引亚当王子（尼古拉斯·加利齐纳饰）回到了埃特尼亚，却发现他的家园在骷髅王（杰瑞德·莱托饰）的邪恶统治下已支离破碎。为了拯救家人和世界，亚当必须与他最亲密的盟友——蒂拉（卡米拉·门德斯饰）和邓肯/武士（伊德里斯·艾尔巴饰）联手，并拥抱他作为“希曼”——宇宙中最强男人的真正命运。

In addition to Galitzine, Mendes, Elba, and Leto, the cast includes Alison Brie as Professor Evelyn Powers (aka Evil-Lyn); Morena Baccarin as the Sorceress of Castle Grayskull; Jóhannes Haukur Jóhannesson as Malcolm/Fisto; James Purefoy and Charlotte Riley as King Randor and Queen Marlena; Sasheer Zamata as Suzie, Adam/He-Man’s BFF on Earth; Kristen Wiig as Roboto; James Wilkinson as Mekaneck; Jon Xue Zhang as Ram-Man; Kojo Attah as the bounty hunter Tri-Klops; Sam C. Wilson as cyborg/weapons expert Kronis/Trap-Jaw; Hafþór Júlíus Björnsson as Goat Man; Robert Towers as Karg; and Christian Vunipola as Adam’s roommate on Earth, Hussein.
除了加利齐纳、门德斯、艾尔巴和莱托外，演员阵容还包括：爱丽森·布里饰演伊芙琳·鲍尔斯教授（即邪恶林）；莫蕾娜·巴卡琳饰演灰骷髅堡的女巫；约翰内斯·豪克·约翰内森饰演马尔科姆/拳击手；詹姆斯·鲍弗和夏洛特·莱利饰演兰多国王和玛琳娜王后；萨希尔·扎马塔饰演苏茜（亚当/希曼在地球上的闺蜜）；克里斯汀·韦格饰演机器人；詹姆斯·威尔金森饰演伸缩人；张学（Jon Xue Zhang）饰演撞击人；科乔·阿塔饰演赏金猎人三眼怪；萨姆·C·威尔逊饰演半机械人/武器专家克罗尼斯/陷阱颚；哈弗波·朱利尔斯·比昂森饰演山羊人；罗伯特·托尔斯饰演卡格；以及克里斯蒂安·武尼波拉饰演亚当在地球上的室友侯赛因。

The first trailer dropped in January and gave us He-Man’s origin story. When he was 10 years old, Adam Glenn, prince of Eternia and our future He-Man, crash-landed on Earth—the home planet of his ex-astronaut mother, Queen Marlena, who sent him there for protection from the forces of Skeletor. The now-grown Adam is an office drone with a penchant for nerd culture who has been searching online for his magic sword, convinced it will show him “the way home” so that he can finally confront Skeletor.
首支预告片于一月发布，讲述了希曼的起源故事。10 岁时，埃特尼亚王子、未来的希曼亚当·格伦坠落地球——这是他曾为宇航员的母亲玛琳娜王后居住的星球，她将他送往地球是为了保护他免受骷髅王势力的伤害。如今已成年的亚当是一名沉迷于极客文化的办公室职员，他一直在网上寻找那把魔法剑，坚信它能指引他“回家的路”，从而让他最终能够对抗骷髅王。

In March, a second trailer brought He-Man back to Eternia and reunited him with Man-at-Arms and Teela—as well as with the talking tiger, Cringer (who can transform into Battlecat).
三月份发布的第二支预告片将希曼带回了埃特尼亚，并让他与武士和蒂拉重聚，同时也让那只会说话的老虎——胆小虎（可以变身为战斗猫）回归。

This final trailer goes right for the heartstrings, opening with vintage footage of the original 1980s series and segueing into Adam grasping the sword and declaring, “I have the power!” Good thing, too, since Skeletor has declared that, while he might be a devil, “I mean to be a god,” and he’s not about to let Eternia’s prodigal son stand in his way. Mekaneck, Ram-Man, and Fisto make cameos but the reunions are short-lived because with Skeletor on the rampage, “The time for talk is over.”
这支最终预告片直击人心，开篇使用了 80 年代原版剧集的复古镜头，随后转入亚当紧握宝剑并高呼“赐予我力量吧！”的经典桥段。这很及时，因为骷髅王已经宣称，虽然他可能是一个恶魔，但他“意欲成为神”，他绝不会让埃特尼亚的浪子挡住他的去路。伸缩人、撞击人和拳击手在片中客串亮相，但重聚时光短暂，因为随着骷髅王的肆虐，“谈话的时间已经结束了。”

*Masters of the Universe* hits theaters on June 5, 2026.
《宇宙的巨人希曼》将于 2026 年 6 月 5 日在影院上映。
