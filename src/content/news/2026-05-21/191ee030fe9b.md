# Artifact-Bench: Evaluating MLLMs on Detecting and Assessing the Artifacts of AI-Generated Videos
# Artifact-Bench：评估多模态大模型（MLLMs）在检测与评估 AI 生成视频伪影方面的能力

Recent video generative models have greatly improved the realism of AI-generated videos, yet their outputs still exhibit artifacts such as temporal inconsistencies, structural distortions, and semantic incoherence.
近期的视频生成模型极大地提升了 AI 生成视频的逼真度，但其输出结果仍存在诸如时间不一致、结构畸变和语义不连贯等伪影问题。

While Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) show strong visual understanding capabilities, their ability to perceive and reason about such artifacts remains unclear.
尽管多模态大模型（MLLMs）展现出了强大的视觉理解能力，但它们感知和推理此类伪影的能力尚不明确。

Existing benchmarks often lack systematic evaluation of artifact-aware perception and fine-grained diagnostic reasoning, especially across diverse AI-generated video domains beyond photorealistic content.
现有的基准测试往往缺乏对伪影感知和细粒度诊断推理的系统性评估，尤其是在超越照片级真实感内容、涵盖更多样化 AI 生成视频领域方面存在不足。

To address this gap, we introduce Artifact-Bench, a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating MLLMs on AI-generated video artifact detection and analysis.
为了填补这一空白，我们推出了 Artifact-Bench，这是一个用于评估 MLLMs 在 AI 生成视频伪影检测与分析能力的综合性基准测试。

We first establish a three-level hierarchical taxonomy of realism artifacts, covering photorealistic, animated, and CG-style videos.
我们首先建立了一个三级层次化的真实感伪影分类体系，涵盖了照片级真实感、动画和 CG 风格的视频。

Based on this taxonomy, Artifact-Bench defines three complementary tasks: real vs. AI-generated video classification, pairwise realism comparison, and fine-grained artifact identification.
基于该分类体系，Artifact-Bench 定义了三项互补的任务：真实视频与 AI 生成视频的分类、成对真实感比较，以及细粒度的伪影识别。

Experiments on 19 leading MLLMs reveal substantial limitations in artifact perception and reasoning, with many models approaching random or even below-random performance in challenging settings.
对 19 个主流 MLLMs 的实验揭示了它们在伪影感知和推理方面存在显著局限性，许多模型在挑战性场景下的表现接近甚至低于随机水平。

We further observe significant misalignment between MLLM judgments and human perceptual preferences, highlighting their limited reliability as general evaluators for AI-generated video realism.
我们进一步观察到 MLLMs 的判断与人类感知偏好之间存在显著偏差，这凸显了它们作为 AI 生成视频真实感通用评估器的可靠性有限。
