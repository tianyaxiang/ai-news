**What is Optimization in Operations Research?**
**什么是运筹学中的优化？**

Operations research leverages mathematical models and optimization to find the best decision under practical constraints. It is the backbone of prescriptive analytics, which transforms predictive insights to actions that are critical to decision making. Framing a real-world situation into an abstract mathematical model is the key to solving any operations research problem.
运筹学利用数学模型和优化方法，在实际约束条件下寻找最佳决策。它是规范性分析（Prescriptive Analytics）的基石，能够将预测性洞察转化为对决策至关重要的行动。将现实世界的情况转化为抽象的数学模型，是解决任何运筹学问题的关键。

**How to Optimize Agent Cost and Resource Allocation**
**如何优化智能体成本与资源分配**

AI agent planning involves making resource allocation decisions within a company’s budget while still achieving the best possible outcomes, which makes it a strong fit for operations research scenarios. We can map the core components above to agent cost optimization and resource planning use cases.
AI 智能体规划涉及在公司预算范围内做出资源分配决策，同时实现最佳结果，这使其非常适合运筹学场景。我们可以将上述核心要素映射到智能体成本优化和资源规划的用例中。

**We will use 4 most standard optimization patterns to address common agent planning scenarios:**
**我们将使用 4 种最标准的优化模式来解决常见的智能体规划场景：**

1. **Set-Covering Problem:** Choose the smallest set of agents to cover all required tasks, skills, or business functions, with the objective of minimizing cost without leaving gaps in capability.
1. **集合覆盖问题：** 选择最少数量的智能体来覆盖所有必需的任务、技能或业务功能，目标是在不留能力缺口的前提下最小化成本。

2. **Assignment Problem:** Decide which agent should handle each project, with the goal of maximizing overall output value.
2. **分配问题：** 决定每个项目应由哪个智能体处理，目标是最大化整体产出价值。

3. **Knapsack Problem:** Select the best bundle of agents under a fixed budget, to maximize total output tokens.
3. **背包问题：** 在固定预算下选择最佳的智能体组合，以最大化总产出 Token。

4. **Network Problem:** Design workflows across a network of agents to meet department and user demands at the lowest cost under capacity constraints.
4. **网络问题：** 在容量约束下，设计跨智能体网络的流程，以最低成本满足部门和用户的需求。
