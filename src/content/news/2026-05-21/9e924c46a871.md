# A new experiment brings better group meetings to Google Beam
# 一项旨在提升 Google Beam 群组会议体验的新实验

A new experiment brings better group meetings to Google Beam. See and hear your colleagues in true-to-life size and sound, making hybrid meetings feel more inclusive and connected.
一项旨在提升 Google Beam 群组会议体验的新实验现已推出。通过逼真的尺寸和音效来观看和聆听同事，让混合办公会议变得更具包容性和连接感。

Mohamed Abdelgany, Google Beam, Group Product Manager.
Mohamed Abdelgany，Google Beam 产品组经理。

While video conferencing tools keep us connected, users can still struggle to feel included in the conversation. Trying to read subtle emotions in a sea of tiny boxes may leave remote participants feeling like observers rather than engaged participants.
尽管视频会议工具让我们保持联系，但用户在对话中仍难以产生参与感。在密密麻麻的小方框中试图捕捉细微的情绪，往往会让远程参与者感觉自己更像是旁观者，而非积极的参与者。

Google Beam, our true-to-life video communication platform, helps you solve this problem by turning your meetings into a more active exchange. Today, we’re sharing a new experiment that brings better group meetings to Google Beam across more devices.
Google Beam 是我们推出的逼真视频通信平台，它能将会议转化为更积极的交流，从而帮助您解决这一问题。今天，我们分享了一项新实验，旨在让更多设备上的 Google Beam 群组会议体验得到提升。

Using HP Dimension’s immersive display, Google Beam renders participants joining from non-Beam devices in their true size, positioned as if they were sitting around a table with you. Paired with spatial audio that anchors each voice directly to the person speaking, colleagues look, sound, and feel more like they are in the same room.
通过使用 HP Dimension 的沉浸式显示屏，Google Beam 可以将从非 Beam 设备加入的参与者以真实比例呈现，其位置就像坐在您身边的桌子旁一样。配合将每个声音直接定位到发言者的空间音频技术，同事们看起来、听起来都更像是身处同一个房间，带来更真实的临场感。

This optimization happens automatically, whether you join a meeting from home or the office. Our research suggests approaches like these help close the hybrid 'inclusion gap' by facilitating a 50% stronger sense of social connection and a 21% increase in reported ability to contribute to conversations.
无论您是在家中还是办公室参加会议，这种优化都会自动进行。我们的研究表明，此类方法有助于缩小混合办公中的“包容性差距”，使社交连接感增强 50%，并使参与者在对话中的贡献能力提升 21%。

We’re also continuing to work with Google Workspace and Zoom to help elevate the meetings you have today on Google Beam. Stay tuned for more at beam.google.
我们还将继续与 Google Workspace 和 Zoom 合作，帮助提升您目前在 Google Beam 上进行的会议体验。更多信息，请持续关注 beam.google。
