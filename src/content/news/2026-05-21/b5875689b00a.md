# MotionMERGE: A Multi-granular Framework for Human Motion Editing, Reasoning, Generation, and Explanation

**MotionMERGE：用于人体动作编辑、推理、生成与解释的多粒度框架**

---

Recent motion-language models unify tasks like comprehension and generation but operate at a coarse granularity, lacking fine-grained understanding and nuanced control over body parts needed for animation or interaction. This stems from fundamental issues in both the model and the data, in which the model can't focus on motion's localized pattern, and the training data lacks fine-grained supervision.

近期的动作-语言模型虽然统一了理解和生成等任务，但其操作粒度较粗，缺乏动画或交互所需的对身体部位的细粒度理解和精细控制。这源于模型和数据两方面的根本性问题：模型无法聚焦于动作的局部模式，且训练数据缺乏细粒度的监督信息。

To tackle this, we propose MotionMERGE, a unified framework that bridges the granularity gap. First, we pioneer the study of fine-grained language-guided motion control, including detailed understanding and localized editing, by explicitly modeling motion at part and temporal levels within a single LLM, thereby endowing the model with robust priors for precise control.

为了解决这一问题，我们提出了 MotionMERGE，这是一个弥合粒度差距的统一框架。首先，我们开创了细粒度语言引导动作控制的研究，包括详细的理解和局部编辑；通过在单一大型语言模型（LLM）内显式地对身体部位和时间维度进行动作建模，从而赋予模型精确控制所需的稳健先验。

Second, we design Reasoning-Aware Granularity-Synergy pre-training, a novel strategy that employs joint supervision for cross-granularity alignment, temporal grounding, localized alignment, motion coherency, and motion-grounded chain-of-thought (CoT) reasoning. This equips the model with fine-grained motion-language alignment, cross-granularity synergy, and explicit reasoning ability.

其次，我们设计了“推理感知粒度协同”（Reasoning-Aware Granularity-Synergy）预训练策略，这是一种采用联合监督的新颖方法，用于实现跨粒度对齐、时间定位、局部对齐、动作连贯性以及基于动作的思维链（CoT）推理。这使模型具备了细粒度的动作-语言对齐能力、跨粒度协同能力以及显式的推理能力。

Third, we curate MotionFineEdit, a large-scale dataset (837K atomic + 144K complex triplets) with the first fine-grained spatio-temporal corrective instructions and motion-grounded CoT annotations, establishing a new benchmark for fine-grained text-driven motion editing and motion-grounded reasoning.

第三，我们构建了 MotionFineEdit，这是一个大规模数据集（包含 837K 个原子级和 144K 个复杂三元组），提供了首批细粒度时空修正指令和基于动作的思维链标注，为细粒度文本驱动的动作编辑和基于动作的推理建立了新的基准。

Extensive experiments demonstrate the capability of MotionMERGE for more precise motion generation, understanding, and editing, and compelling zero-shot generalization to other complex motion tasks. This work represents a significant step toward models that interact with motion in finer granularity and human-like reasoning.

大量实验证明，MotionMERGE 在更精确的动作生成、理解和编辑方面表现出色，并在其他复杂动作任务上展现了令人信服的零样本泛化能力。这项工作标志着模型在以更细粒度与动作交互以及实现类人推理方面迈出了重要一步。
