# LiFT: Lifted Inter-slice Feature Trajectories for 3D Image Generation from 2D Generators

**Title: LiFT: Lifted Inter-slice Feature Trajectories for 3D Image Generation from 2D Generators**
**标题：LiFT：用于二维生成器三维图像生成的切片间特征轨迹提升方法**

**Abstract:** High-resolution 3D medical image generation remains challenging because fully volumetric models are computationally expensive, while efficient 2D slice generators often fail to preserve anatomical consistency across the third dimension.
**摘要：** 高分辨率三维医学图像生成仍然是一项挑战，因为全容积模型计算成本高昂，而高效的二维切片生成器往往难以在第三维度上保持解剖结构的一致性。

We propose LiFT, a framework for Lifted inter-slice Feature Trajectories that factorizes 3D volume synthesis into per-slice image generation and inter-slice trajectory learning. Rather than modeling the volumetric distribution end-to-end, LiFT treats a volume as an ordered trajectory in feature space, capturing how anatomical structures appear, transform, and disappear across depth.
我们提出了 LiFT，这是一个用于“切片间特征轨迹提升”的框架，它将三维容积合成分解为逐切片图像生成和切片间轨迹学习。LiFT 不再端到端地建模容积分布，而是将容积视为特征空间中的有序轨迹，捕捉解剖结构如何随深度出现、变换和消失。

A tri-planar drifting loss aligns the trajectory of generated slices with the trajectories of real volumes, enabling distributional learning over inter-slice progressions in unconditional generation; in paired translation, a bidirectional $z$-context mixer trained against the registered target supplies through-plane coherence while preserving per-slice fidelity.
三平面漂移损失（tri-planar drifting loss）将生成切片的轨迹与真实容积的轨迹对齐，从而在无条件生成中实现对切片间演进的分布学习；在成对转换任务中，针对配准目标训练的双向 $z$-上下文混合器（bidirectional $z$-context mixer）在保持逐切片保真度的同时，提供了跨平面的一致性。

We evaluate LiFT on BraTS 2023 (unconditional and missing-modality MR) and SynthRAD2023 (MR-to-CT). Across these settings, LiFT preserves per-slice quality, approaches the reported cWDM missing-MR reconstruction quality at $\sim$$135\times$ lower inference cost (without formal equivalence testing), and improves through-plane coherence on MR-to-CT relative to a no-mapper ablation, demonstrating that lightweight inter-slice trajectory learning is a viable route to high-resolution 3D medical synthesis.
我们在 BraTS 2023（无条件和缺失模态 MR）和 SynthRAD2023（MR 转 CT）上评估了 LiFT。在这些设置中，LiFT 保持了逐切片的质量，以约 135 倍的更低推理成本接近了已报道的 cWDM 缺失 MR 重建质量（未经正式等效性测试），并在 MR 转 CT 任务中相对于无映射器消融实验提高了跨平面一致性，证明了轻量级切片间轨迹学习是实现高分辨率三维医学合成的可行途径。
