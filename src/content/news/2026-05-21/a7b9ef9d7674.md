# Personalized Face Privacy Protection From a Single Image
# 基于单张图像的个性化人脸隐私保护

**Abstract:** Photos of faces uploaded online are vulnerable to malicious actors who can scrape facial images from online sources and intrude on personal privacy via unauthorized use of facial recognition models. This paper presents FaceCloak, a novel personalized face privacy protection system, which can generate defensive identity-specific universal face privacy masks from a single image of a user, causing facial recognition to fail.

**摘要：** 上传到网络上的人脸照片极易受到恶意攻击者的威胁，他们可以从网络资源中抓取人脸图像，并通过未经授权使用人脸识别模型来侵犯个人隐私。本文提出了 FaceCloak，这是一种新颖的个性化人脸隐私保护系统。它仅需用户的一张照片，即可生成具有防御性的、针对特定身份的通用人脸隐私掩码，从而导致人脸识别失效。

FaceCloak introduces a three-stage personalized face perturbation learning methodology: (1) It generates a small set of high-variety synthetic face images of a person based on a single image of the person. (2) It learns face cloaking by adding more protection to key facial-identity leakage regions through iterative perturbation generation over the small set of synthetic images, effectively shifting a user's identity embedding towards a distant anchor identity and away from a similar one. (3) It generates a personalized identity-protective mask in the form of pixel-wise cloaking, which is light-weight and can be efficiently applied to any facial image of a user while maintaining good perceptual quality.

FaceCloak 引入了一种三阶段的个性化人脸扰动学习方法：(1) 基于用户的单张图像，生成一组具有高度多样性的合成人脸图像。(2) 通过在这一小部分合成图像上进行迭代扰动生成，对关键的人脸身份泄露区域增加更多保护，从而学习人脸遮蔽技术，有效地将用户的身份嵌入（identity embedding）向远离原始身份的锚点移动，并远离相似身份。(3) 以像素级遮蔽的形式生成个性化的身份保护掩码。该掩码轻量化且能高效应用于用户的任何面部图像，同时保持良好的视觉质量。

Extensive experiments on three popular face datasets across ten recognition models show the effectiveness of FaceCloak compared to 29 other existing representative methods. Code is available at this https URL.

在三个流行的人脸数据集上，针对十种识别模型进行的广泛实验表明，与现有的 29 种代表性方法相比，FaceCloak 具有显著的有效性。代码已在相关链接中提供。
