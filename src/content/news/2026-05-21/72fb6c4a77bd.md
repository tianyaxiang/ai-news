# HELLoRA: Hot Experts Layer-Level Low-Rank Adaptation for Mixture-of-Experts Models
# HELLoRA：面向混合专家模型的层级热点专家低秩自适应

**Abstract:** Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) dominates parameter-efficient fine-tuning of large language models, yet most variants target dense architectures. Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) models scale parameters at near-constant per-token compute, and their sparse activation patterns create untapped opportunities for more efficient adaptation.

**摘要：** 低秩自适应（LoRA）在大语言模型的参数高效微调（PEFT）领域占据主导地位，但其大多数变体主要针对稠密架构。混合专家（MoE）模型在保持每个 Token 计算量近乎恒定的前提下扩展了参数规模，其稀疏激活模式为实现更高效的自适应提供了尚未开发的潜力。

We propose Hot-Experts Layer-level Low-Rank Adaptation (HELLoRA), which attaches LoRA modules only to the most frequently activated experts at each layer. This simple mechanism reduces trainable parameters and adapter-induced FLOPs while improving downstream performance, an effect we attribute to a form of structured regularization that preserves pretrained expert specialization.

我们提出了“层级热点专家低秩自适应”（HELLoRA），该方法仅在每一层中激活频率最高的专家模块上附加 LoRA 模块。这种简单的机制在减少可训练参数和适配器引入的浮点运算（FLOPs）的同时，提升了下游任务的性能。我们将此效果归因于一种结构化正则化，它能够有效保留预训练专家模型的专业化能力。

To stress-test HELLoRA under extreme parameter budgets, we further compose it with LoRI to form HELLoRI, which freezes the up-projection and sparsifies the down-projection. Across three MoE backbones, namely OlMoE-1B-7B, Mixtral-8x7B, and DeepSeekMoE, and three task families covering mathematical reasoning, code generation, and safety alignment, HELLoRA consistently outperforms strong PEFT baselines.

为了在极端参数预算下对 HELLoRA 进行压力测试，我们将其与 LoRI 结合形成了 HELLoRI，该变体通过冻结上投影（up-projection）并稀疏化下投影（down-projection）来进一步优化。在 OlMoE-1B-7B、Mixtral-8x7B 和 DeepSeekMoE 三种 MoE 主干网络，以及涵盖数学推理、代码生成和安全对齐的三类任务中，HELLoRA 的表现始终优于强力的 PEFT 基准模型。

Relative to vanilla LoRA on OlMoE, HELLoRA uses 15.7% of the trainable parameters, reduces adapter FLOPs by 38.7%, achieves 1.9x the training throughput, and improves accuracy by 9.2%. On DeepSeekMoE, HELLoRA outperforms LoRA while using only 23.2% of its trainable parameters. These results demonstrate that activation-aware adapter placement is an effective and practical route to scaling PEFT for MoE language models.

与 OlMoE 上的原生 LoRA 相比，HELLoRA 仅使用了 15.7% 的可训练参数，将适配器 FLOPs 降低了 38.7%，训练吞吐量提升至 1.9 倍，并将准确率提高了 9.2%。在 DeepSeekMoE 上，HELLoRA 在仅使用 LoRA 23.2% 可训练参数的情况下，性能依然超越了 LoRA。这些结果表明，基于激活感知的适配器放置策略是扩展 MoE 语言模型 PEFT 的一种有效且实用的途径。
