# Can LLMs Replace Survey Respondents?
# 大语言模型能取代调查受访者吗？

Large Language Models Can LLMs Replace Survey Respondents? How unlearning fixes mode collapse in synthetic survey replies.
大语言模型能取代调查受访者吗？“去学习”（Unlearning）如何修复合成调查回复中的模式坍缩问题。

What happens when you ask an LLM to simulate 6,000 American households answering questions about inflation? Recent papers find that large language models can replicate the average responses of major household surveys to within a percentage point (Zarifhonarvar, 2026). In 2020, the Survey of Consumer Expectations (SCE) reported a one-year-ahead median inflation rate of about 3%. The median produced by a prompted LLM with realistic personas and a knowledge-cutoff instruction: also about 3%. Close enough that LLMs have been pitched as a low-cost, high-frequency complement to the SCE, Michigan, and Survey of Professional Forecasters surveys.
当你要求大语言模型（LLM）模拟 6,000 个美国家庭回答关于通货膨胀的问题时，会发生什么？近期的论文发现，大语言模型可以将主要家庭调查的平均回复误差控制在 1 个百分点以内（Zarifhonarvar, 2026）。2020 年，“消费者预期调查”（SCE）报告的未来一年通胀率中位数为 3% 左右。而通过提示词设定真实角色并加入知识截止指令后，LLM 产生的中位数同样约为 3%。由于结果非常接近，LLM 已被提议作为 SCE、密歇根大学调查以及专业预测者调查的一种低成本、高频的补充手段。

In a recent paper, *Can LLMs Mimic Household Surveys?*, co-authored with Ami Dalloul from the University of Duisburg-Essen, we look at the second moment, the part of a probability distribution that tells you whether the model represents one opinion or a thousand. It is here that the apparent success of LLM-based surveys disappears. The same Llama-3 model that hits the SCE median to within a percentage point places 95% of its simulated respondents inside a two-percentage-point window. The real 2020 SCE responses range from roughly minus 25 to plus 27 percent. In short, the average is right, but the population behind it does not exist. So running a simulation with several thousand LLM personas boils down to one representative agent.
在近期与杜伊斯堡-埃森大学的 Ami Dalloul 合著的论文《大语言模型能模仿家庭调查吗？》中，我们研究了概率分布的二阶矩——即衡量模型是代表一种观点还是上千种观点的指标。正是在这里，基于 LLM 的调查所表现出的成功“幻象”消失了。同一个能将 SCE 中位数误差控制在 1 个百分点以内的 Llama-3 模型，却将其 95% 的模拟受访者置于一个仅 2 个百分点的区间内。而 2020 年真实的 SCE 回复范围大约在负 25% 到正 27% 之间。简而言之，平均值是对的，但其背后的群体并不存在。因此，用数千个 LLM 角色进行模拟，最终其实只相当于一个代表性代理人。

### Mode collapse
### 模式坍缩

We benchmarked five LLMs (Llama-3-8B, Llama-3-70B, Claude-3.7-Sonnet, DeepSeek-V3, GPT-4o) against the SCE, Michigan Survey, and Survey of Professional Forecasters. In the human surveys, 44 to 70% of respondents give answers more than 3 percentage points away from the modal reply; in the LLM samples, that share is essentially zero. The standard remedies from the survey-simulation literature do not improve this problem. Census-derived personas with complex and varying characteristics, zero-shot knowledge-cutoff instructions (“you do not know events after June 2018”), and explicit “do not look up statistics” prompts all default to the same narrow distribution.
我们针对 SCE、密歇根调查和专业预测者调查，对五种 LLM（Llama-3-8B、Llama-3-70B、Claude-3.7-Sonnet、DeepSeek-V3、GPT-4o）进行了基准测试。在人类调查中，44% 到 70% 的受访者给出的答案与众数回复相差超过 3 个百分点；而在 LLM 样本中，这一比例几乎为零。调查模拟文献中的标准补救措施无法改善这一问题。无论是基于人口普查数据构建的复杂多变的角色，还是零样本知识截止指令（“你不知道 2018 年 6 月之后的事件”），亦或是明确的“不要查找统计数据”提示，最终都默认回到了同样狭窄的分布中。

The likely cause is that the LLMs see CPI tables, news coverage of FRBNY survey releases, and academic replications in their training corpora. Asked for the median 2020 inflation expectation, the model is doing retrieval against memorized data. The weight of that training data overpowers whatever the prompt instructions ask it to do.
可能的原因是，LLM 在其训练语料库中见过 CPI 表格、纽约联储调查发布的新闻报道以及学术复现数据。当被问及 2020 年通胀预期中位数时，模型实际上是在对记忆中的数据进行检索。这些训练数据的权重压倒了提示词指令所要求的任何行为。

### Unlearning the LLMs
### 对 LLM 进行“去学习”

If memorized statistics are the problem, a potential fix is to remove them from the weights rather than ask the model to look away. We applied two unlearning methods to Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct, an open-source model that allows us to modify its weights: Gradient Ascent (GA) maximizes prediction loss on a forget set of CPI series and survey aggregates, with a retain loss on micro-survey reasoning so general capability survives. Negative Preference Optimization (NPO) treats the forget set as dispreferred completions and minimizes a bounded preference loss against a reference model. The data we ask the model to forget is the official inflation record itself: monthly CPI series and published mean inflation expectations from the FRBNY SCE and Michigan surveys.
如果记忆中的统计数据是问题的根源，那么一个潜在的解决方法是从权重中移除它们，而不是要求模型“视而不见”。我们对 Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct（一个允许修改权重的开源模型）应用了两种去学习方法：梯度上升（GA）通过最大化 CPI 系列和调查汇总数据的预测损失来“遗忘”这些信息，同时保留微观调查推理的损失，以确保通用能力得以留存。负偏好优化（NPO）则将遗忘集视为不被偏好的补全内容，并针对参考模型最小化有界偏好损失。我们要求模型遗忘的数据正是官方通胀记录本身：月度 CPI 系列以及纽约联储 SCE 和密歇根调查发布的平均通胀预期。
