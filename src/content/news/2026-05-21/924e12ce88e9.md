# Theory-optimal Quantization Based on Flatness
# 基于平坦度的理论最优量化

**Abstract:** Post-training quantization has emerged as a widely adopted technique for compressing and accelerating the inference of Large Language Models (LLMs). The primary challenges in LLMs quantization stem from activation outliers, which significantly degrade model performance especially at lower bit precision.
**摘要：** 训练后量化（Post-training quantization）已成为压缩和加速大语言模型（LLM）推理的广泛采用的技术。LLM 量化的主要挑战源于激活值中的离群点（outliers），这些离群点在低比特精度下会显著降低模型性能。

While recent approaches attempt to mitigate outliers through linear transformations across feature dimensions, our analysis reveals that the transformed weights and activations still exhibit persistent outlier patterns with concentrated magnitude distributions.
尽管最近的一些方法试图通过跨特征维度的线性变换来缓解离群点问题，但我们的分析表明，变换后的权重和激活值仍然表现出持续的离群模式，且其量级分布依然高度集中。

In this paper, we first model the mathematical relationship between quantization error and outliers, and then introduce a new metric Flatness to quantify the distribution of outliers. Based on this, we derive the theoretical optimal solution with respect to Flatness.
在本文中，我们首先对量化误差与离群点之间的数学关系进行了建模，随后引入了一种新的度量指标——“平坦度”（Flatness），用于量化离群点的分布。基于此，我们推导出了关于平坦度的理论最优解。

Building on these insights, we propose Bidirectional Diagonal Quantization (BDQ), a novel post-training quantization framework that effectively disperses outlier patterns through optimized matrix transformations. BDQ strategically distributes outlier magnitudes across matrix dimensions via learned diagonal operations.
基于这些见解，我们提出了双向对角量化（Bidirectional Diagonal Quantization, BDQ），这是一种新颖的训练后量化框架，通过优化的矩阵变换有效地分散了离群模式。BDQ 通过学习到的对角操作，策略性地将离群点的量级分布到矩阵的各个维度上。

Extensive experiments demonstrate that BDQ establishes a new quantization benchmark. It achieves less than 1% accuracy drop in W4A4 quantization on the LLaMA-3-8B model. In the more challenging W2A4KV16 experiment, compared to state-of-the-art approaches, BDQ reduces the performance gap by 39.1% on the DeepSeek-R1-Distill-LLaMA-70B model.
大量实验表明，BDQ 确立了量化领域的新基准。在 LLaMA-3-8B 模型上进行 W4A4 量化时，其精度下降不到 1%。在更具挑战性的 W2A4KV16 实验中，与当前最先进的方法相比，BDQ 在 DeepSeek-R1-Distill-LLaMA-70B 模型上将性能差距缩小了 39.1%。
