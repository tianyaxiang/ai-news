# Meta cuts contractors who reported seeing Ray-Ban Meta users have sex
# Meta 解雇曾举报目睹 Ray-Ban Meta 用户性行为的承包商

In February, numerous workers from a company that Meta contracted to perform data annotation for Ray-Ban Meta reported viewing sensitive, embarrassing, and seemingly private footage recorded by the smart glasses. About two months later, Meta ended its contract with the firm.
今年 2 月，多家受 Meta 委托为 Ray-Ban Meta 智能眼镜进行数据标注的公司员工报告称，他们看到了由这些智能眼镜录制的敏感、尴尬且看似私密的视频片段。大约两个月后，Meta 终止了与该公司的合同。

According to a BBC report today, “less than two months” after a report from Swedish newspapers Svenska Dagbladet and Göteborgs-Posten and Kenya-based freelance journalist Naipanoi Lepapa came out featuring Sama workers complaining about watching explicit footage shot from Ray-Ban Metas, “Meta ended its contract with Sama.” Sama is a Kenya-headquartered firm that Meta contracted to perform data annotation work, including working with video, image, and speech annotation for Meta’s AI systems for Ray-Ban Metas.
据 BBC 今日报道，在瑞典报纸《瑞典日报》(Svenska Dagbladet)、《哥德堡邮报》(Göteborgs-Posten) 以及肯尼亚自由撰稿人 Naipanoi Lepapa 发布报道，披露 Sama 公司员工抱怨观看了由 Ray-Ban Meta 拍摄的露骨视频后，“不到两个月”，Meta 就终止了与 Sama 的合同。Sama 是一家总部位于肯尼亚的公司，Meta 曾委托其进行数据标注工作，包括为 Ray-Ban Meta 的 AI 系统处理视频、图像和语音标注。

Sama claims that Meta’s cancellation of the contract affected 1,108 workers. A Meta spokesperson told BBC that Meta “decided to end our work with Sama because they don’t meet our standards.” Ars Technica reached out to Meta asking how, specifically, Sama failed to meet Meta’s expectations and will update this article if we hear back. Ars has also reached out to Sama.
Sama 声称，Meta 取消合同影响了 1,108 名员工。Meta 发言人告诉 BBC，Meta “决定结束与 Sama 的合作，因为他们不符合我们的标准。”Ars Technica 已联系 Meta，询问 Sama 具体在哪些方面未能达到 Meta 的期望，如有回复将更新本文。Ars 也已联系了 Sama。

In a statement shared with BBC, Sama claimed that it was never notified of any failure to meet Meta’s standards. BBC reported that Sama workers believe Meta ended the contract because workers spoke out about seeing Ray-Ban Meta-shot footage of people performing personal acts, like changing their clothes, having sex, and using the toilet.
在提供给 BBC 的一份声明中，Sama 声称从未收到任何关于其未达到 Meta 标准的通知。BBC 报道称，Sama 员工认为 Meta 终止合同是因为员工公开谈论了他们看到 Ray-Ban Meta 拍摄的视频，内容涉及人们进行私人行为，如更衣、性行为和上厕所。

In a statement shared with Ars, Sama said: We do not comment on specific client processes or decisions, however, we can confirm that the engagement with Meta is ending. Sama has consistently met the operational, security, and quality standards required across all of our client engagements, and we stand behind the integrity of our work. Our focus is on supporting our employees during this transition while continuing to deliver for our clients.
在提供给 Ars 的一份声明中，Sama 表示：“我们不对特定客户的流程或决策发表评论，但我们可以确认与 Meta 的合作即将结束。Sama 一直以来都满足我们所有客户合作中要求的运营、安全和质量标准，我们对工作的完整性负责。我们的重点是在此过渡期间支持我们的员工，同时继续为我们的客户提供服务。”

In February’s report, an anonymous Sama employee was quoted as saying, per a machine translation, they “are just expected to carry out the work” even when viewing private footage. After Sama workers told journalists that they had watched private footage that appeared to be recorded unbeknownst to glasses owners, Meta responded by halting business with Sama, a spokesperson said, per BBC’s report today.
在 2 月份的报道中，一位匿名 Sama 员工表示（根据机器翻译），即使在查看私人视频时，他们也“只是被要求执行工作”。据 BBC 今日报道，在 Sama 员工告诉记者他们观看了似乎是在眼镜所有者不知情的情况下录制的私人视频后，Meta 作出了回应，暂停了与 Sama 的业务。

“Last month, we paused our work with Sama while we looked into these claims,” the spokesperson said. “We take them seriously. Photos and videos are private to users. Humans review AI content to improve product performance, for which we get clear user consent.” BBC said that Meta has not responded to allegations that it cut ties with Sama because the workers spoke out.
“上个月，我们在调查这些指控时暂停了与 Sama 的工作，”该发言人说。“我们非常重视这些指控。照片和视频对用户来说是私密的。人类审核 AI 内容是为了提高产品性能，我们为此获得了明确的用户同意。”BBC 表示，对于因员工公开谈论此事而切断与 Sama 联系的指控，Meta 尚未作出回应。

In response to the February report, Meta confirmed that it sometimes shares content that glasses owners provide to the Meta AI generative AI chatbot with contractors so that the contractors can review data with “the purpose of improving people’s experience.” The company said that such “data is first filtered to protect people’s privacy,” such as by blurring out faces in pictures.
针对 2 月份的报道，Meta 证实，它有时会与承包商共享眼镜所有者提供给 Meta AI 生成式聊天机器人的内容，以便承包商能够以“改善用户体验为目的”审查数据。该公司表示，此类“数据会先经过过滤以保护个人隐私”，例如模糊图片中的人脸。

Ray-Ban Metas show a light when taking photos or recording a video; however, Sama workers said in February that it appeared that some users remained unaware that their glasses were recording sometimes. “People can record themselves in the wrong way and not even know what they are recording,” an anonymous employee was quoted as saying, per a machine translation of the Swedish newspapers.
Ray-Ban Meta 在拍照或录像时会亮起指示灯；然而，Sama 员工在 2 月份表示，似乎有些用户在眼镜录制时仍然不知情。根据瑞典报纸的机器翻译，一位匿名员工表示：“人们可能会以错误的方式录制自己，甚至不知道自己在录制什么。”

Since Sama workers’ claims became public, Ray-Ban Meta glasses have faced extra scrutiny. In March, a class-action complaint was filed in the US District Court for the Northern District of California, San Francisco Division [PDF] against Meta and Luxottica of America, a subsidiary of EssilorLuxottica, which is Ray-Ban’s parent company.
自 Sama 员工的指控公开以来，Ray-Ban Meta 眼镜面临了额外的审查。3 月份，美国加利福尼亚北区联邦地区法院旧金山分院受理了一起针对 Meta 和 Luxottica of America（Ray-Ban 母公司依视路陆逊梯卡旗下的子公司）的集体诉讼。

The complaint accuses Meta of breaking state consumer protection laws and seeks damages, punitive penalties, and an injunction requiring Meta to make changes in order “to prevent or mitigate the risk of the consumer deception and violations of law.” That same month, the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) said it would send Meta a letter about the Sama workers’ “concerning” reports.
该诉讼指控 Meta 违反了州消费者保护法，并寻求损害赔偿、惩罚性罚款以及一项禁令，要求 Meta 进行整改，以“防止或减轻消费者受欺骗和违法行为的风险”。同月，英国信息专员办公室 (ICO) 表示将就 Sama 员工的“令人担忧”的报告向 Meta 发送一封信函。

The data watchdog told BBC at the time that “devices processing personal data, including smart glasses, should put users in control and provide appropriate transparency.” “Service providers must clearly explain what data is collected and how it is used,” the ICO’s statement said. The office of Kenya’s Data Protection Commissioner said in March that it was investigating “privacy concerns raised in relation to the Ray-Ban Meta glasses and the processing of personally identifiable information for the training of Meta AI.”
该数据监管机构当时告诉 BBC，“包括智能眼镜在内的处理个人数据的设备，应让用户拥有控制权并提供适当的透明度。”ICO 的声明称：“服务提供商必须清楚地解释收集了哪些数据以及如何使用这些数据。”肯尼亚数据保护专员办公室在 3 月份表示，正在调查“有关 Ray-Ban Meta 眼镜以及为训练 Meta AI 而处理个人身份信息所引发的隐私担忧。”
